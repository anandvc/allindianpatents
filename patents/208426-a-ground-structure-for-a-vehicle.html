Title of Invention

A GROUND STRUCTURE FOR A VEHICLE
Abstract To make an earth cable between an engine and a body frame advantageous against vibrations and increase the universality in use and further improve the flexibility in coupling to a vehicle body. [Solving Means] An engine, particularly an engine case 12A, and a vehicle body,particularly a body frame 3, are connected to each other by a cable C1 to ground the engine. The cable C1 has a diameter set substantially equal to that one of the other wriing lines, that is, a wiring line cable C4 between a battery and the engine 1 and wiring line cables C2 and C3 between the battery 4 and a starter motor 6, which cable has the greatest wire diameter.
