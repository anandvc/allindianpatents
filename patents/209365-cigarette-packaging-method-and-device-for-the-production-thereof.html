Title of Invention

CIGARETTE PACKAGING, METHOD AND DEVICE FOR THE PRODUCTION THEREOF
Abstract A pack, in particular cigarette pack, having a blank which encloses the pack contents, is made of foldable packaging material, such as paper, film, cardboard or the like, is of multi-layered design in a sub-region and, as a result of folding, has a border- side overlap, an inner folding tab being connected to an outer folding tab by glue and the glue passing through a cutout in an inter mediate tab, characterized in that the cutout in the blank or in the intermediate tab is bounded by a punch cut which is rounded overall.
Full Text Patent claims:
1. Pack, in particular cigarette pack, having a blank (10) which encloses the pack contents, is made of foldable packaging material, such as paper, film, cardboard or the like, is of multi-layered design in a sub-region and, as a result of folding, has a border-side overlap, an inner folding tab being connected to an outer folding tab by glue and the glue passing through a cutout (29) in an intermediate tab, characterized in that the cutout (29) in the blank (10) or in the intermediate tab is bounded by a punch cut (30) which is rounded overall.
2. Pack according to Claim 1,. characterized in that the cutout (29) is elongate, in particular of more or less oval design, in the vertical direction of the pack.
3. Pack according to Claim 1 or 2, characterized in that a piece of material or punched segment (31), which has been punched out of the blank (10) by the punch cut (30) in order to form the cutout
(29) , is connected to the blank (10) via a residual connection (32) and is folded. out of the region of the cutout (29) .

region of the cutout (29) .
5. Process for producing packs in the case of which pack contents are enclosed by at least one blank (10) made of foidable material, in particular paper, film or cardboard, the blank being severed from a continuous material web (33) and having at least one multi-layered folding strip (21). which is directed transversely and/or extends in the direction of the material web (33). and at least one cutout (29) in the region of the folding strip (21). characterized by the following features:
a) the circuit ;29) is made in the blank (10) by punching into the continuous material web (33) in accordance with the position of the cutout (29),
b) the material web (33) is then folded continuously in the longitudinal direction in order to form the folding strip (21),
c) the blanks (10) provided with the folding strip (21) are then severed from the continuous material web (33) in their correct position by a transversely directed severing cut (34).
d) the severing cut (34) runs through the cutout (29) with open sub-cutouts of different sizes being formed at one border of the blank (10) in the process,
e) the blank (10) is then folded about the pack contents.
