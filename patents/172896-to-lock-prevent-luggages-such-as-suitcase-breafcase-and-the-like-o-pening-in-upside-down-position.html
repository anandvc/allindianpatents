Title of Invention

TO LOCK PREVENT LUGGAGES, SUCH AS, SUITCASE, BREAFCASE AND THE LIKE,O PENING IN UPSIDE DOWN POSITION.
Abstract A lock which prevent luggages, such as, suitcases, briefcases and the like, opening in upside down positions, which includes : a base plate having a primary and a secondary pairs of extensions at predetermined locations, at both sides, having holes, which are bent to approximately 90Â° to form "U" shape; an outer plate having a primary, a secondary and an auxiliary extensions at predetermined locations, at both sides, having holes, which are bent to approximately 90 to form "U" shape and, are adapted to rest within said "U" shaped extensions of the base plate; said primary extensions of base plate and outer plate being pivotted, with the help of a pivot pin and, is spring loaded; said secondary extensions of base plate and outer plate are pivotally connected with the help of an extension member, such that when said primary pivotted end of outer plate is pressed towards the base plate, the other end of it moves away frorn the base plate to the extent of length of said extension member and is held in that position, due to expanding of the said spring;
