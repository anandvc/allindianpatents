Title of Invention

A PORTABLE DEVICE FOR AUTOMATIC NON DESTRUCTIVE TESTING OF PANELS
Abstract This invention relates to a portable device for non-destructively testing flat panels particularly sandwich panels. Debonding or improper bonding of panels may be noticed by processing signals obtained on tapping the panel. The device consists of a drivable plotter connected to a computer .Signal sensing means, signal processing means, signal storing and displaying means are also provided for collecting test data. The pen plotter has a force guage movable up and down on the panel on receiving commands from the computer to generate signals which are processed, stored and displayed.

WE CLAIM :
1. A portable device for automatic non destructive testing of panels
comprising a flat bed plotter consisting of a drivable pen plotter
provided with force guage, control means commanding up and down
movements of the said pen plotter, drive means to drive the said
plotter in the X-Y axis of the said panel, fixing means to fix the said plotter at any desired location on the said panel, the said plotter having connected thereto signal sensing means, signal processing means, signal storage and signal display means to sense, process store and display the signals received therefrom.
2. The portable device as claimed in claim 1, wherein the force gauge of the pen plotter is piezo electric force gauge.
3. The portable device as claimed in claim 2, wherein the piezo electric force gauge is a transducer.
4. The portable device as claimed in any one of the preceding claims, wherein the said pen plotter is provided with a solenoid for imparting up and down movement thereto.
5. The portable device as claimed in claims 1 to 4, wherein the pen plotter is mounted on a movable arm and the drive means is a stepper motor connected to a computer.

6. The portable device as claimed in any one of the preceding claims, wherein the fixing means are vacuum cups located on a frame member.
7. The portable device as claimed in any one of the preceding claims,
wherein the signal sensing means and signal processing means are signal conditioners and analog digital converters connected to a computer.
8. The portable device as claimed in any one of the preceding claims,
wherein the signal storage means is a computer and the signal display means is a cathode ray oscillograph.
9. The portable device as claimed in any one of the preceding claims,
wherein the control means commanding up and down movement of the pen plotter is a digital analog converter connected to a computer.
10. A portable device for automatic non destructive testing of panels,
substantially as hereinabove described and illustrated with reference to the accompanying drawing.
