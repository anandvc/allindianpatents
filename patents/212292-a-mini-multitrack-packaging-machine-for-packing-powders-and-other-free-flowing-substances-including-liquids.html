Title of Invention

A MINI MULTITRACK PACKAGING MACHINE FOR PACKING POWDERS AND OTHER FREE FLOWING SUBSTANCES INCLUDING LIQUIDS.
Abstract The invention describes a mini multi track packaging machine for packing powders and other free flowing substances including liquids means for unwinding a roll of film/laminate, means for splitting the said plastic or laminate into two equal parts, means for pulling the film/laminate to the front of the system from the unwinding means and splitting means, means for sealing the vertical sides and horizontal sides of the said laminate/film means for filling products in said pouches and sealing the end horizontally and punch and die means for giving the desired contour to the pouch.
Full Text The present invention relates to a mini multi-track pouch making and packaging
machine for packing powder and flowing substances including liquids.
BACKGROUND
Conventional machine available in the market are single track, continuous type as
compared to mini multi track and intermittent type of the mentioned machine. Only
rectangular pouches can be formed on conventional machines.
The object of the present invention is to provide a packaging machine which can give
high production (more than one track) and unique different shape pouches, which
are not available oh present machines in the market.
To achieve the said object, the present invention provides A mini multi-track pouch
making and packaging machine for packing powder and flowing substances
including liquids comprising:
unwinding means (1) for unwinding a roll of film /laminate,
splitting means (S) for splitting said film /laminate into two equal parts,
pulling means (2) for pulling the split films/laminates from splitting
means,
sealing means (3) for sealing said split films/laminates vertically and
horizontally to form pouches,
filling means (4) as herein described for filling the powder and flowing
substances including liquids in said pouches and thereafter sealing
said pouches,
punch and dye means (5) having shapes in accordance with the shape
contour to be punched on the pouch, said punch and dye means
comprises a set of punches (41) and dyes (42) of required shape
depending upon the number of tracks on the machine,
logic controller means (6) for controlling the operation of above means
(1), (S), (2), (3) and (5).
The programmable logic controller having sufficient input/output capacity is provided for
controlling the various operations of the system.
The means for unwinding comprises an unwinding frame onto which a reel shaft is
mounted.
The unwinding frame comprises braking means for applying brakes on moving reel shaft in
order to maintain a uniform speed of the film/laminate.
The said means for unwinding comprises dancing roller means which is
connected to braking means for ensuring uniform tension in the rolling film.
Said means for splitting comprises turn bar means having sharp edge spiltter
at its center.
Said pulling comprises various silicon rubber rollers driven by
electromagnetic clutch and having break means.
The sealing means is a vertical sealing means and horizontal sealing means,
Said vertical sealing means comprises vertical sealers mounted on a sealing
plate.
Said vertical sealers have at least one pencil heater inserted in it.
Sealing means comprises cartridge heaters to seal to heat the sealers
Horizontal sealing means is mounted on sealing plates which moves
intermittently through two levers controlled by a cam and a motor.
Means for filling is a rotary disc adjustable filling means, rotary valve
adjustable filling means, sliding plate filling means or a liquid filling means.
The rotary disc adjustable filling means comprises rotary discs mounted on a
plate, said discs having of volumetric cups for determining the amount of
product to be packed, a feeding hopper on the upper side the said disc to
feed the desired product and a funnel at its lower side for directing the
product into the pouch.
The rotary valve adjustable filling means comprises a feeding hopper
mounted on the main rotary valve, the lower end of the rotary valve having a
number of volumetric pockets for filling pouches.
The sliding plate filling means comprises of a set of horizontal plates and a
special hopper is mounted over it, said hopper having more than one
compartments.
The liquid filling means comprises hopper, filling tubes, valve, valve
body, mechanical cylinders and piston
The punch and die means comprises a set of punches and dies, depending on
the number of tracks on the machine, the designs of which depends on
the shape contour to be punched on the pouch.
The present invention also comprises a process for manufacturing a
packaging pouch comprising the steps:
loading a roll of plastic film/laminate on the reel shaft of the said
unwinding means,
pulling and passing the said film/laminate through the aluminum alloy
rollers of the turn bar means so as to bring it between the silicon
rubber rollers
passing the said film/laminate through the turn bar for splitting the
laminate into two equal parts,
simultaneously pulling both the parts of the laminate on the either side
of the pulling means
sealing the vertical and the lower ends of the said laminate by means
of vertical and horizontal sealers
the said open ended pouch is again pulled to the filling means where
the desired product is filled in the pouch
heat sealing the said end to form a pouch and
passing the pouch to punch and dies means to give the desired
contour.

Referring to Figure 1, the unwinding means (1) for unwinding the roll of
plastic/ laminate is connected to pulling means (2). The pulling means (1)
pulls the film/laminate to the front of the machine from the unwinding
means. The filling means (4) is connected the sealing means (3) to seals the
sides of the laminate and also to the punch and die means (5). The control of
this machine is connected to an electronic means having sufficient
input/output capacity is provided for controlling the various operations of
the system.
Figure 2 illustrates the unwinding means. The unwinding means comprises a
reel shaft (7) mounted on the unwinding frame (8) at the back of the
machine. The roll of film/laminate (6) is loaded onto the Reel shaft (7). The
Unwinding Frame (8) also contains Braking means (9) for applying brakes
on moving Reel Shaft (7) in order to maintain a uniform speed of the film.
Dancing Roller means (10) is also provided in the unwinding means for
ensuring uniform tension in the moving film which is connected to the
braking means (9). The Reel Shaft (7) rotates freely on bearings (not shown)
mounted on the sides of Unwinding Frame (8). As the film moves, the
Dancing Roller means also moves which in turn is connected to the braking
means. As the web tension of the film/ laminate reduces the Dancing Roller
means makes the braking means operate. The brakes applied on Reel Shaft
reduce the unwinding speed of the film roll resulting in increased tension in
film. This ensures uniform tension in the film essential for smooth pouch
formation.
In Figure 3, the means for splitting the laminate/film in two equal parts comprises
a turn bar (13) having a sharp edge splitter (13") at its center for splitting the
laminate/film and aluminum rollers (13") for rolling the split film/laminate.
In Figure 4 the pulling means have a plurality of silicon rubber rollers (11).
These rollers are driven by Electromagnetic Clutch and Break means (12).
The film passes through various aluminum alloy rollers
In Figure 5, the sealing means comprises a plurality of vertical sealers (14)
and horizontal sealers(16) which are mounted on the Sealing Plate (15) and
horizontal sealers (16). The vertical sealers are provided with pencil heaters.
Horizontal sealers are provided with cartridge heaters to heat mem. Between
the horizontal sealing means, perforation blade (16") is provided for
perforation and batch cutting of pouches. The Sealing means moves
intermittently through two Levers (18). The lever movement is controlled
through a Cam (19) and motorized drive. As per the cam contour the levers
bring the sealing jaws closer so as to meet together under pressure for a
particular time required for sufficient sealing. The pouch so formed is then
filled with product through a filling system and released from the machine.
In Figure 6a, the rotary disc adjustable filling means comprises rotary discs
on a plate (20). The discs (24) consists of volumetric cups (21) for
determining the amount of product to be packed. On the upper side of the
rotary disc, a feeding hopper (22) is provided to feed the desired product and
at its lower side a funnel (23) is provided to directing the product into the
pouch.
In Figure 6b, the rotary valve adjustable filling means comprises a feeding
hopper (26) mounted on the main rotary valve (25). The lower end of the
rotary valve has a number of volumetric pockets (27) for filling pouches.
The product gets filled inside the pockets. On rotation, material which is
filled inside the pockets gets deposited in the feeding funnels. The number of
pockets on the valve spool and valve depends upon the number of tracks on
the machine. This filling system is suitable for free flowing products such as
tea, sugar, mourn refresher pan masala etc.
In Figure 6c, the sliding plate filling means comprises of a set of horizontal
plates (28) and a special hopper (29) is mounted over it. This hopper (29)
may have more than one compartments (not shown) for filling different
types of product at the same time. These products gets filled in volumetric
cups (30) mounted on said plate. The plate may be a stainless steel plate.
The top plate of the said plates contains at least two sets volumetric cups. As
the mechanism operates, the plate reciprocates between two extreme
positions. When one set of cups is being filled through one leg of the hopper,
the other set of cups drops the product into the feeding funnels. The position
is changed in next cycle when the filled cups comes in the centre and drops
material while the earlier set of cups gets filled in another arm of the hopper.
Cup volume can be changed by increasing/ decreasing the bore diameter.
Adjustable plates can also be made by moving the fixed plates up and down.
Fixed plate is having a acrylic covering for smooth movement. Special
separators are provided in the hoppers to avoid product mixing.
In Figure 6d liquid filling means comprises hopper (33), filling tubes (34),
Valve (35), valve body (36), mechanical cylinders (37) and piston (38).
Individual eccentric slide (39) are provided with each cylinder (37). A piston
(38) is used to adjust volume to be filled in each pouch. Number of cylinder,
piston and slide will depend on number of tracks on the machine.
The required volume of material is sucked through valve into the cylinder
and delivered to the filling tube again through valve. Product to be packed is
stored in hopper (33 ) installed at the top of rotary valve (35). The rotary
valve is actuated through a pneumatic cylinder (40) which is connected to a
solenoid valve. Solenoid valve gets signal from proximity switch mounted
on the cam. Piston in the mechanical cylinder moves mechanically through
individual eccentric slide mechanism (39) connected to the main drive of the
machine. The cycle consists of suction and delivery strokes.
This system is used to pack free flowing liquids viz. shampoo, oil etc.
Figure 7 the punch and die means comprises a set of punches and dies,
depending on the number of tracks on the machine. The designs of punch
(41) and die (42) depends on the shape contour to be punched on the pouch.
Punch can be pneumatically or mechanically operated through a mechanism
connected to the machine drive. Punching is done intermittently after the
vertical sealing is completed. Alignment of punch and die is of prime
importance.
Once the film is secured in between sealing jaws, punch becomes
operational to get a clean cut of the shape designed. Once punching is
completed and punches are withdrawn, film is allowed to move further. Off
cuts of the punched area can be removed pneumatically.
A Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) having sufficient input/output
(I/O) capacity is used to control various machine operations. Input signals
are received from various proximity sensors mounted on cams, photocell etc.
while output signals are fed to solenoid coil, clutch/brake etc. Logic is
programmed in advance and stored in the PLC.
A suitable operator interface comprising of a screen display mounted on
instrument panel is used to display various faults and parameters.
Adjustments are possible on the screen itself. It is capable to display
parameters like speed, batch cutting timing, number of pouches produced
etc. Faults like low level of hopper, gears open etc. can be shown along with
buzzer warning sound.
According to the present invention the capability and efficiency of Form,
Fill and Seal Packaging Machines will be improved significantly.
