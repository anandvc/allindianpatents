Title of Invention

DEVICE FOR EXPANSION OF HAEMOPOIETIC STEM CELLS FOR THERAPEUTIC USE
Abstract The present invention is related to a device which is a three dimensional bio-reactor for expansion of haemopoietic stem cells derived from human Umbilical Cord Blood for therapeutic use,without compromising are self-renewal property of the cells.The process of self -renewal and differentiation are regulated by haemopoietic growth factors and haemopoietic microenvironment,which the device of the present invention has regulated,in a manner that the expansion system used retains and expands stem cells with long term repopulating potential.
