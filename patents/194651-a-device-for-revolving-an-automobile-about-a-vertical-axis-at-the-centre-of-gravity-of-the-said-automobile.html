A DEVICE FOR REVOLVING AN AUTOMOBILE ABOUT A VERTICAL AXIS AT THE CENTRE OF GRAVITY OF THE SAID AUTOMOBILE
Abstract The invention relates to a device for revolving an automobile about a vertical axis. The device is operable only when the vehicle is stationary. The device comprises a pneumatic compressor (2) connected to the engine (1 ), the said compressor (2) supplies pneumatic air to the systems selected out of a. remote system of revolving arrangement of base plate (11) mounted pivotably at bottom of the automobile through a hydraulic jack (10) and two pairs of wheels (23,24) rotatably mounted on to the said base plate (11) at 90° to each other, the said jack (10) is connected to the compressed air supply (9) and the base plate (11) with two pairs of wheels (23,24) extended and supporting the automobile to rotate about the crank pin (13) and bearing (12) being connected to a piston (19) and cylinder (20) through crank pin (15,16) and piston rod (14,18); b. a hydraulic jack system (25) mounted pivotably on to the bottom of an automobile through a base plate (26) to lift the automobile upward and in that upward position can be rotated with pressurised air to a desired, the said pressurised air is supplied by a compressor (2) connected to the engine, and reservoir (4,5) and brake valve (9) to hydraulic jack (10) and the hydraulic revolving base plate (11); and c. a four section hydraulic jack (27) mounted centrally at the base plate (29) of the automobile, the said jack (27) is a closed cylinder (28) with a tension spring (29), and expands alongwith the four section hydraulic jack to support and lift the car for rotation and two bevel gears (30,32) mounted on to the base plate (29) and drivingly connected to the engine (1) through a belt pulley (33) for the rotation of the car.
Full Text The invention relates to a device for revolving an automobile about a vertical axis. This device is operable only when the vehicle is stationary.
At present, all automobiles can steer left or right up to a certain angle by steering the front wheels. There are special vehicles made experimentally where all the four wheels can be steered to obtain a tighter turning circle.
But upto date, no vehicle has been produced which can rotate about its axis 360° while stationary.
This facility of revolving about its axis is advantageous when the road is very narrow or the vehicle is caught in a conjested traffic.
Thus, the main object of the present invention is to produce a device which can be used for revolving a vehicle at any angle upto 360° while it is stationary.
According to this invention,, there is provided a device for revolving an automobile around a vertical axis at the centre of gravity of the said automobile having a hydro/pneumatic compressor connected to the engine of the automobile, said compressor supplying pneumatic air to the device, the device comprising a revolving means constituting of a first base plate mounted ptvotably at bottom of the automobile through a hydraulic jack and two pairs of wheels rotatably mounted on to said base plate at 90° to each other, said jack being connected to a compressed air supply line and the revolving means such that the automobile can be rotated around a crank pin and a bearing connected to a piston and a cylinder of trie engine via a crank pin and a piston rod.
