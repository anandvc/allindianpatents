Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED BRACKET ASSEMBLY FOR VEHICLES
Abstract The mounting bracket assembly of a long member for vehicles, in accordance with the present invention, comprises; first flange member (A) with central portion having first leg portion and second leg portion, second flange member (B) with central portion having opposed leg portion and leg portion, flat member (C) having left edge portion and right edge portion and support member (D) having top edge and bottom edge. Said first flange member (A), second flange member (B) and flat member (C) comprises plurality of aperture or openings provided therein for mounting of said bracket to the long members and cross member of vehicles. For making the present bracket first leg portion (1) of said first flange member (A) is welded longitudinally at the front surface of the said flat member (C), and left edge portion of said flat member (C) is welded to the top surface of the first leg portion of the said first flange member (A). now edge of leg portion of second flange member (B) and front surface of said flat member (C) are welded together after aligning respective aperture or openings of said members, and bend line of the said second flange member (B) is welded with the front surface of the said flat member. Further said support member (D) comprising first and second width placed between aligned apertures or openings of first leg portion (1) of said first flange member (A) and said flat member (C), and then both side of said support member (D) is welded to the front surface of the said flat member (C).

PREAMBLE TO THE DESCRIPTION
The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed.

FIELD OF INVENTION
The present invention is related to the bracket system of vehicles and more particularly it relates to improved bracket assembly for vehicles and method of mounting thereof.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Generally conventional brackets are used in most of the HCV vehicles. Which are usually made up of a single-plate bracket, in some cases, with a reinforcements-like stiffener provided at the location of the holes to avoid the effect of excessive stresses at the holes are used. These brackets (RH and LH components) are mounted onto the long member directly. Then, the cross member assembly is mounted onto these brackets .These RH and LH brackets, along with the cross-member, are used for the front mounting of the engine of a vehicle. For rear mounting engine, conventional mounting brackets are not suitable, thus new brackets is developed.
OBJECTS OF INVENTION
The main object of the present invention is to provide improved bracket assembly for vehicles.
Another object of the present invention is to provide method of mounting improved bracket assembly for vehicles.
