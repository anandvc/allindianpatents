A SYNERGISTIC PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION COMPRISING BETA-LACTUM ANTIBIOTIC AND POLYSACCHARIDE USEFUL IN THE TREATMENT OF MICROBIAL INFECTION
Abstract The present invention relates to a synergistkic pharmaceutical composition comprising Beta-lactum antibiotic and polysaccharide useful in the treatment of microbial infections having improved thrapeutic efficacy.
