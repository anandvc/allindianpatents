Title of Invention

APPARATUS FOR AIDING THE GAME OF CRICKET
Abstract Apparatus for aiding bowling in the game of cricket, comprising a set of flat, planar main bands and inner bands for removably forming a grid of at least six quadrilaterals at a predetermined distance and of predetermined sizes from the stumps; said bands having eyelets at least at their ends; a set of naols adapted to pass through the eyelets to removably fix the bands in their operative configuration on a cricket pitch; a locator band for locating the formed grid of bands, at a predetermined distance from a set of stumps on a cricket pitch, said locator band having attachment means at one end for removably fixing the locator band to any one of the stumps.
