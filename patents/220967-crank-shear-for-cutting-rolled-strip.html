Title of Invention

CRANK SHEAR FOR CUTTING ROLLED STRIP
Abstract Crank shear for cutting rolled strip (22), which comprises two pairs of blades (3, 4) which are mounted on blade holders (1, 2), wherein the blade holders (1, 2) are supported opposite each other in a vertical plane (x-x) in a pair of eccentric drive shafts (5, 6) and are pivoted on torque-supporting levers (7, 8) in double-joint mechanisms (9, 10) in interaction with two hydraulic control units (11, 12) that act on these double-joint mechanisms (9, 10) and that the upper pair of blades (3) are located in a curved recess (20) of the upper blade holder (1) at the bearing surfaces (16, 17), characterized in that the lower pair of blades (4) are located at the outer bearing surfaces (18, 19) of a projection (15) that is positioned .against the recess (20) and that the projection (15) is constructed as a single piece together with the lower blade holder (2).
