Title of Invention

APPARATUS FOR MIXING LIQUIDS OR LIQUID WITH PARTICLES
Abstract "A rotor (1) to establish a swirling flow in liquid(s) contained in a vessel having an upper end, a lower end and a containing wall extending between the upper and lower ends by being submerged in the liquid adjacent the upper end, said swirling flow being chracterised by an outer annular region of moderate rotational flow adjacent the containing wall moving from the upper end of the vessel toward the lower end, an inward flow adjacent the lower end of the vessel, and an inner core region of rapid rotational flow about a central region of the vessel moving from the lower end toward the upper end and extending from substantially adjacent the lower end of the vessel to the rotor, said rotor including two substantially parallel spaced apart generally planar discs (2)extending perpendicular to an axis of rotation, an array of vanes (4) interposed between the discs (2), one of said discs being annular to define an inlet (3) to said array of vanes, said inlet controlling the size of said inner core region of said swirling flow.
