Title of Invention

METHOD OF MAKING A RINSE CONDITIONER
Abstract A composition for use in the rinsing of laundry contains particles with perfume located or absorbed in or on the particles, characterised in that the particles are formed of cross-linked organic polymeric material that have a mean particle size not greater than 1 micron. The composition is preferably a fabric softener. A method of treating laundry with the composition is also provided.
