Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PREPARING MODIFIED BIOCOMPATIBLE SPONGE
Abstract This invention relates to a process for preparing modified biocompatible sponge comprising in the steps of: a. preparing a solution of the polyvinyl alcohol of high-visco- sity; b. adding to the solution of the polyvinyl a surfactant such as herein described; c. mixing solution of PVA and surfactant with an acidic solution chitosan followed by frothing the mixture obtained; d. adding an aldehyde crosslinker such an acid catalyst to crosslink the PVA and the chitosan to obtain the biocompatible sponge.
