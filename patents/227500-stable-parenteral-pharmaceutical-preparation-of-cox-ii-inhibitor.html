Title of Invention

STABLE PARENTERAL PHARMACEUTICAL PREPARATION OF COX - II INHIBITOR
Abstract A parenteral formulation comprising a pharmaceutically active ingredient such as Cox II ingibitor, in particular Rofecoxib or its analogues, in a lyophilized and stabilized powder for injection, where the clear solution for lyophillisation is stabilized by using Sodium hydroxide solution in the presence of complexing agent and without use of buffering agent.
