Title of Invention

SKIN CARE COMPOSITION
Abstract A topical composition comprising: a) pretroselinic acid and/or derivatives thereof; b) a phenolic compound and/or mixtures thereof; and c) a dermatologically acceptable vehicle. The product is particularly useful for treating wrinkles and soothing sensitive skin.
