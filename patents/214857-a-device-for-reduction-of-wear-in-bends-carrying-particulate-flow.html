Full Text This invention relates to a device for reduction of wear in bends carrying particulate flow.
In a coal based thermal power station the particulatrd air with the pulverised coal is carried from the pulverizers to the boiler t furnace through a long network of piping to various coal burners. On entering the bend the solid particles separate from the carrier air and impinge on the inner surface of the outer wall due to effect of inertia and centrifugal force and lead to wear of the outer wall. With the curvature of the bend continuing, the impingement and the drag of the particles continue on the rest of the inner surface of the outer wall of the bend and causing the wear. The wear is negligible in the straight pipes as the particles travel with air in a stream-lined fashion parallel to the pipe wall.
There are disadvantages associated with the existing system of particulate flow such, coal and air to the boiler.
One of the main disadvantages with the existing system is that. The coal, particularly the Indian coal is rich in abrassive alfa-quartr This not only leads to excessive wear of pulveriser components but also the bends in the piping lying between the pulverizers and the coal burners.
Another disadvantage is with the existing system is that due to excessive wear the pipe bends are subjected to
frequent puncture in the large radius side walls leading to loss of pressure, air, coal dust and energy in addition to causing air pollution in the neighbourhood.
Yet another disadvantage with the exihting system is that frequent repairs/replacements of such worn out pipes bends has always been a major constraints to tfficlent functioning of the Power Plant.
A further disadvantage with the existing system is that to minimise the problem of repair or replacements, ceramic lining is provided on the inner surface of the bends. Providing ceramic lining inside the bend is expensive. These also wear out little time later than bare pipe exposing the metal of the bends to wear faster.
Therefore, the main object of the present invention is to provide a simple and effective device to minimise the wear of the bend pipes due to particulate flow.
Yet a further disadvantage with the existing system is that the bends are repaired at the damaged parts by welding plates in patches and replace it when no further repair can done*
An object of the present invention/is to provide a device comprising multifunctional bend saver plates so that the wear of the bend is minimum.
Another object of the present invention is to provide bend saver plates which could be sacrificial by using ordinary mild steel or made to last for a longer period by using right type of wear resistance steel.
