Title of Invention

CRIMPING TOOL AND METHOD OF CRIMPING
Abstract Title : Method Of Progressive Insertion Assembly The present invention relates to a progressive insertion assembly comprising locator means comprising top locator (2) and bottom locator (1) , tubular profile (5) operatively placed on the bottom locator ( 1) and a strip ( 6) with predetermined rigidity and width greater than the tubular profile ( 5) and placed on the top locator (2) in a manner forming an axial alignment with each other. Guide means (4) are operatively located in the top locator (2) so as to guide the strip (6) into the tubular profile (5) and plurality of rollers ( 3) are connected to the roller guides ( 4) providing deformation of the tubular profile (5) to a predetermined shape. The present invention also relates to a method of progressive insertion of strip inside tubular profile. The method comprises locating strip on top locator and tubular profile on bottom locator, moving down and co-operative guiding of the strip, disposing it inside the tubular profile and crimping of the tubular profile so as to cause its deformation to a shape equal to that of the strip.
