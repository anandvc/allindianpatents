AN IMPROVED WATER FILTER
The invention relates an improved water filter for the purification of drinking water. More particularly the invention relates to an improved water filter for removing physical impurities as well as micro biological impurities for prevention cf water bone diseases.

In tne water filter having double container one above the other EO as to receive unfiltered water in the top stainless steel container fitted with ceramic cartridge for purification of physical impurities and at the bottom of the cartridge is fitted with iodine resin cup for microbiological purification and silver impregnated activated carbon cup of chemical purification. During long use the brass plate fitted inside the container give away and therefore the cont mated water starts coming which is detrimental for public tealth.


The inventors therefore thoughts of improving upon the existing devices by providing a copper coating through electroplating process inside the stainless steel container thereby avoiding the possible chances of contimination of filtered water.
The principal object of the invention is therefore to provide an pure copper coating through electroplating process inside stainless steel container.
The another object of this inventionis to provide a pure drinking water though the water filtered having copper coating inside the stainless steel contianer so as to avoid further contimination of filtered water.
Accordingly there is provided an improved water filter (1) having a stainless steel upper container (2) which is copper coated through the electro plating process inside; said upper copper coated stainless steel plate (2) is fitted at the base to the high grade ceramic cartridge (3) for purification of physical impurities; the said ceramic cartridge at its base is fitted with iodine resin cup for microbiological purification and silver impregnated activated carbon cup (5) for chemical purification (bacteria, gems) which are placed in an inverted position on the bottom stainless steel cover coated with copper through electroplating process inside for collecting filtered water & having an outlet (tap) (6) for the dispensing filter water; the upper plate containing unfiltered water is position on the lower stainless steel plate through a hollow bracket (7) ; the said upper stainless steel contianer is provided with lid (8).
