Title of Invention

A LIQUID TRANSPARENT CLEANSING COMPOSITION
Abstract The present invention provides for a liquid cleansing composition comprising: 5 to 25% by weight of soap, 5 to 20% by weight humectants, balance water; wherein the soap comprises 0.05 to 3% by weight of the composition, salt of unsaturated fatty acid.
