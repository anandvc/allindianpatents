Title of Invention

AN INFORMATION PROVIDING SYSTEM
Abstract An information providing system that can provide information in a shorter downloading period. The information providing system includes a terminal device capable of reading information data recorded in a storage medium that has recorded incomplete information data. The incomplete information data from prepared by omitting some data in complete information data which provide complete information. The information providing system also includes an information administration device for storing complementary information data which is transmittable between the terminal device and the information administration device to complement the incomplete information data thereby reconstructing an original information data. The terminal device includes a reception module for receiving the complementary information data by way of data transmission from the information administration device, a readout module for reading the incomplete information data from the storage medium, and a combine module for combining the received complementary information data with the read incomplete information data.

BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to an information providing system for providing information over lines, circuits and networks such as the internet. Description of the Related Art
A so-called e-commerce is now prevailing. It allows a client terminal device such as a personal computer to download an information file such as a music informatlon file from a Web server over the internet, whereby various information including music (or musical pieces), images, pictures, programs, novels, etc. is bought and sold over the internet.
It is advantageous because a purchaser can immediately obtain desired information such as music without going to a store.
The size of the information file such as music and pictures is, however, as large as megabyte even after the file is compressed. A terminal device, therefore, requires a considerable amount of time to download the file. An additional time is further needed when the internet is crowded and/or a great amount of data or people access a server. As a result, a user may feel a stress during downloading or hesitate to purchase something by the time-consuming downloading.
