Title of Invention

EARTH POTENTIAL UTILIZER
Abstract A method of utilizing the Earths magnetic field & earths state of spin for charging capacitors and chemical battery. The presented invention offers a very simple static arrangement (called as "earth potential utilizer " ) to tap height wise variations of earth's magnetic field intensity, which manifests as elevation dependent electromotive force, to store electricity free of cost and supply power using these cells at a price. Number of cell can be charged in parallel and discharged in series depending on the load requirements.
