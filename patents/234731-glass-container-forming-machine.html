Title of Invention

GLASS CONTAINER FORMING MACHINE
Abstract A parison is blown in the blow mold of an I. S. machine and as soon as the parison is blown, the blow head is raised to an exhaust location increasing the flow of cooling air to the interior of the bloen parison. When the blown parison has been cooled sufficiently so that the blow mold can be opened, the formed bottle is removed.
