MOUNTING STRUCTURE FOR A FUEL INJECTION APPARATUS FOR AN ENGINE OF A SMALL SIZE VECHICLE
Abstract To further enhance the3 durability of a fuel injection apparatus to protect the fuel injection apparatus from external force by a simple perform an assembling operation or a maintenance or inspection operation of an engine on which the fiel injection apparatus is mounted. A small size vehicle a cylinder extending forwardly and substantially horizontally. An intake port5 122 extends substantially rearwardly from an upper portion of za cylinder head 115 of the engine and a fuel injection apparatuse 140 for intake port into a combustion chember through the intake port is disposed in the proximity of the cylinder head. A mounting boss 124 is provided on an face of the intake port rearwardly of the fuel injection apparatus. The fuel injection apparatus is mounted on the mounting boss.
