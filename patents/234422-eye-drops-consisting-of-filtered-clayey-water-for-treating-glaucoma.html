The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed.
1

The invention concerns eye drops consisting of clayey water filtered and purified from all its particles, used for treating Glaucoma.
MANUFACTURING PROCESS:
Said eye drops are obtained from a blend, in a thick glass recipient, of one quarter of very fine powder of green clay and three quarters of ordinary cold water. Air tightly cover the recipient, never use any metallic object and let it settle one week at less before lengthy filtering this water taking care not to move the silt settled in the bottom, in order to eliminate all the particles and obtain a perfectly purified water. Said eye drops are ready for use.
THERAPEUTIC INDICATIONS OF THE PRODUCT:
Said eye drops intend to Glaucoma treatment in order to decline the hypertension until restoring a normal intraocular pressure.
It eases the evacuation of aqueous humor which, when is in excess, exerts an unfortunate, even dangerous pressure on the optical nerve.
Other advantage: as this clayey water does not include contraindication, nothing opposes to several instillations (3 to 4 instillations each 24 hours) if needed.
ACTIVITIES:
Activities of this clayey water are essentially sedative, antiseptic, tonic, as well as a determining radioactivity as for the balance of the eye vital functions. What confers it an efficiency which in the usage, turns out unmistakable and it without any contraindication.
2

Admittedly, the virtues of the clay are well known, but the argillaceous water never has been used as eye drops.
Still, it was dared to instill it in a sick eye and moreover when you are blind in one eye. And from the first days, I felt benefactions. I was on the right way, with the confirmation on the long term.
And last important point, considering the simplicity of this natural product, its cost price would be much lower than those currently present on the market.
