Title of Invention

"A HERBAL COMPOSITION FOR THE PREVENTION AND MANAGEMENT OF LOSS OF COGNITIVE DECLINE AMONG THE AGED POPULATION AND A PROCESS FOR PREPARATION THEREOF"
Abstract This invention relates to a herbal composition and a process for preparation thereof wherein the herbal composition is useful for the prevention and management of senile dementia which involves loss of cognitive function among the ageds. The composition comprises mixture of organic extracts of Centella asiatica (Mandukparni), Hippophae salicifolia (Badriphal) and Convolvulus pluricaulis (Shankhapushpi), with or without additive. The process for preparation of the composition comprises mixing the organic extracts of the above herbs with optional addition.
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a herbal composition and a process for preparation thereof wherein the composition is useful for the prevention and management of senile dementia which involves loss of cognitive functions among the ageds. The preparation of the present invention may also be advantageously used in arresting the further decline of cognitive function among the ageds particularly the present invention will help in slowing down the decline of memory performance among the ageds. BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Degenerative changes occur throughout the body with increasing age, which are sufficient to constitute various pathological manifestations. Decline in cognitive function, loss of adaptive response to stress, decreased humoral as well as cell mediated immunity, slower metabolic clearance of drugs, increased susceptibility of infection, psychological involvement like cognitive impairment and depression all are observed most frequently among all ageds. Dependency due to physical and mental disabilities are the major factors responsible to adverse the quality of life and health care. Due to the above reasons the elderly population is the heavy user of medical and personal care services.
The decline in cognitive functions is an inevitably consequence of ageing. A broad range of modern nootropic and psychotropic agents are being used to manage the age related cognitive impairment and associated functional disorders. But the long-term intervention of such agents has there own limitations due to adverse effects on various biological system.

OBJECTS OF THE PRESENT INVENTION
An object of the present invention is to propose a herbal formulation and a process for preparation thereof useful for the prevention and management of senile dementia involving loss of cognitive function among the aged cases.
Another object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation to check the further loss of cognitive functions among the ageds.
Further object of the present invention is to propose a herbal formulation to improve the quality of life among the ageds by improving the memory performance and concentration ability.
Still further object of the present invention is to propose a herbal preparation effective in maintenance of cognitive function by checking the further loss of memory and other mental functions.
Yet further object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation for the prevention and management of anxiety and depression among the ageds which hamper the cognitive functions significantly.
