Title of Invention

"IRON BASED/COPER BASED CERMET FRICTION MATERIALS/COMPOSITES/BRAKE ELEMENTS WITH BUILTIN BACKING PLATES"
Abstract This invention relates to iron based/copper based cermet friction materials/composites/brake elements with builtin backing plates. This is particularly suitable for use as brake element of the brake assembly in light to heavy duty automobiles, rails and aircrafts.
