Title of Invention

A DEVICE FOR WELDING A NUT OR THE LIKE
Abstract A device for welding a nut or the like, comprising a tubular electrode holder (2), a lower electrode (3) coupled to an upper end of the electrode holder and having a through hole (3h) defined centrally therein, an air cylinder (5) coupled to a lower end of said electrode holder (2) by a coupling block, a coupling rod (7) disposed in a tube of said electrode holder (2) and vertically movable when said air cylinder (5) is operated, a positioning pin (9) disposed on an upper portion of the coupling rod (7) and having a tip end movable into and out of the through hole (3h) in said lower electrode (3) when the coupling rod (7) is vertically moved, and an air blowing mechanism for delivering air into an upper portion of the tube from a lower portion of said electrode holder (2), wherein said coupling block comprises a magnetic member, an insulation is provided between the electrode holder and the air cylinder, said air cylinder has a cylinder rod with magnetic graduations, and said air cylinder has a magnetic detecting element for detecting said magnetic graduations and an amplifier for amplifying a signal from said magnetic detecting element.

The detector used in this arrangement comprisÂ¬es magnetic graduations formed on the cylinder rod 5a, a magnetic detecting element primarily in the form of a magnetoresistance element, and an amplifier 26 for ampliÂ¬fying a signal detected by the magnetic detecting eleÂ¬ment.
