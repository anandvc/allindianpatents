Title of Invention

NOVEL 1,3-DIOXANE CARBOXYLIC ACIDS
Abstract The present invention relates to novel 1,3-dioxane carboxylic acid of the general formula (I), their tautomeric forms, their stereoisomers, their pharmaceutically acceptable salts, pharmaceutical compositions containing them, method for their preparation, use of these compounds in medicine and the intermediates involved in their preparation.
