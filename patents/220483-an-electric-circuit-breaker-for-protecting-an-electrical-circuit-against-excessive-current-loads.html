Title of Invention

AN ELECTRIC CIRCUIT BREAKER FOR PROTECTING AN ELECTRICAL CIRCUIT AGAINST EXCESSIVE CURRENT LOADS
Abstract An electric circuit breaker (l) comprises a switch (11) to- be arranged in said electrical circuit (3); means (13) for causing said-switch (11) to break, said electrical circuit (3) in response to A tripping signal.(14); means (l7) for receiving (IF) and strong (MEM) a programmable current threshold command (CC); means (15) for detecting a current level (CL) in said electrical circuit (3); and processing means (16) for generating-said tripping signal (14)'-depending on said stored programmable current threshold command (CC) and said detected current level (CL).
