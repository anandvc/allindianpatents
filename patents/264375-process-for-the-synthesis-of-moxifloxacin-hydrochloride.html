Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR THE SYNTHESIS OF MOXIFLOXACIN HYDROCHLORIDE
Abstract A new polymorph of moxifloxacin hydrochloride is described, together with a method for making the polymorph. In addition, new intermediates in the formation of moxifloxacin hydrochloride are described, having formulas (I) and (II).
