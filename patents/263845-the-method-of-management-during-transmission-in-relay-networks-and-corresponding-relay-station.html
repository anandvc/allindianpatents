Title of Invention

THE METHOD OF MANAGEMENT DURING TRANSMISSION IN RELAY NETWORKS AND CORRESPONDING RELAY STATION
Abstract ABSTRACT Various example embodiments are disclosed relating to sending and processing messages in relay networks. In an example embodiment, a first unicast message indicating a plurality of wireless nodes included in a communication path between a base station and a path endpoint node in a wireless network may be received (1310) at a first receiving node, wherein the first unicast message may be received from a sending wireless node located neighboring to the first receiving node in the communication path. The first unicast message may be processed (1320) at the first receiving node. A second message may be sent (1330) based on a success/failure status of the processing from the first receiving node to a second receiving node included in the communication path.
