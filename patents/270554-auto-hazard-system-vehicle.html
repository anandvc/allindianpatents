Title of Invention

AUTO HAZARD SYSTEM - VEHICLE
Abstract An auto hazard system for vehicle comprises an ECU (Electronic Controller Unit), various doors switches, vehicle speed sensor, parking lamp, brake switch, and various hazard indicating lamps provided on the vehicle; wherein an inbuilt ECU of vehicle is electrically connected to various switches and hazard indicating lamps; wherein said switches send signals to ECU, and on receipt of such signals, ECU turns on all hazard lamps providing hazard signal to the upcoming vehicles, following vehicles and personnel in the surrounding. The system is also provided with inbuilt manual over ride witching, enabling the user to disable the auto hazard function.

Title

Field of invention
The present invention relates to the field of motor vehicles and
more particularly, to a system for automatically controlling all
hazard indication systems (lights, a voice messaging system,
etc.) on a motor vehicle so as to avoid motor vehicle accidents.
Prior art
To the best of the knowledge of applicant, no prior art
references are available releavent to the present invention.
Object of the invention
The objective of this invention is to provide additional safety to
the upcoming or to following vehicles with help of a system for
switching ON all the lamps and a voice messaging system
connected to the hazard system.
Further to provide a system which indicate a hazrdous situation
like
1) Night time if the vehicle speed is zero and bonnet is open
2) In a moving vehicle, one or any of the doors or bonnet is open
3) If sudden braking is applied
Futher more objective is to provide a system for automatically
controlling the hazard lights and a voice messaging system of a
motor vehicle under said conditions

System for switching ON all the lamps and a voice messaging system connected to the auto hazard system under any of the following conditions
1) Night time if the vehicle speed is zero and bonnet is open
2) In a moving vehicle, one or any of the doors or bonnet is open
3) If sudden braking is applied
The system according to the present invention comprises ECU (Electronic Controller Unit), various doors switches viz. hazard switch, vehicle speed sensor, parking lamp, brake switch, and various signal lamps provided on the vehicle. All the above said switches are connected to ECU to its input port. All lamps are connected to out put port of ECU. On receiving signals form all or any one of switches ECU sends signals to all connected lamps and lamps start blinking giving hazard signal to the personnel present in the surroundings. Operation and function
The enclosed block diagram shows an auto hazard system of
the vehicle.
Auto hazard system will start working under the following
conditions:
1). If the park lamp is ON for less than few seconds OR if the
light sensor detects low illumination AND vehicle speed is zero
AND bonnet is open the bonnet switch, light sensor, park
lamp gives the signal to ECU and on receiving these signal ECU
sends the signal to the all connected hazard lamps and all
hazard lamps (16) start blinking.
2) In a moving vehicle, if any of the doors OR bonnet is open,
the door switch OR bonnet switch will give signal to ECU and on
receiving this signal, ECU sends the signal to the all connected
lamps and all connected lamps start blinking.
3) If the de-acceleration of the vehicle is more than the
specified limits, vehicle speed sensor switch will send signal to
ECU and on receiving this signal, ECU sends the signal to the
all connected lamps and all connected lamps start blinking.
4) In all the cases, manual over ride by switching ON and OFF, hazard switch, the auto hazard function will get disable.
5) Normal working of hazard switch and lamps connected to the same will remain as it is.
