FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a bracket assembly for mounting fiber reinforced plastic (FRP) trims on the sheet metal panels of vehicles. Further this invention relates to bracket assembly for mounting FRP trims on the sheet metal panels of prototype vehicles where using injection molded trims are costly and time consuming.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Trims are used to improve the aesthetic looks of the vehicle interior. Various kinds of trims are used in vehicles to improve the aesthetic look such as FRP, plastic etc. Plastic components are used in production vehicles where as FRP trims are used in prototype vehicles. In mass production of vehicles plastic trims with provision for fixing it to sheet metal panel is also made by injection mold. By using injection molded plastic trims in prototype vehicles, the cost for making such trims will be high and also it will be time consuming to make such trims. In prototype vehicle the design of the vehicle will change to incorporate new design and making injection molded plastic trims accordingly will be time consuming and involved high cost. By using FRP trims in prototype vehicles cost and time for making trims saved and also it will be easy to modify the design if required. Trim panels will help to hide the sheet metal panels to give more aesthetic appearance. It will also improve the noise vibration harshness (NVH) of the vehicle. There are holes provided on the sheet metal panels for mounting trims, however there is no mounting available on the inner surface of the FRP trims which are available on the plastic trims made from production tools by injection molding method. So, to assemble the FRP trims, sheet metal brackets are required to fill the gap between the sheet metal panel & the trim. These brackets are screwed to the panel at places convenient for mounting the trim. The actual mountings on the panel, as per design cannot be utilized to mount these brackets. To mount the FRP trims on the panel by screwing them on these brackets with the help of sheet metal screws.

Disadvantages in conventional methods:
The appearance of the complete assembly is poor and shabby. The method of fixing trims is troublesome and time consuming job. It will result in loss of NVH properties due to creating extra holes on body panels.
SUMMERY OF THE INVENTION
The objective of present method is to develop bracket assembly for mounting FRP trims that will be:
a) easy to assemble.
b) create a good appearance of the assembly
c) improve NVH properties
d) close to the design intent assembly to validate the design.
The above objective is achieved by developing a sheet metal support member with a hole on one side for fixing the tree clip and a slot on the other side for fitment of the push button. This support member is mounted on the sheet metal panel using the same holes provided for mounting trims as per design. This support member is mounted by press fitting the push button in the panel hole. The FRP trim is then mounted on these support members with the help of tree clips.
According to first aspect the invention provides a bracket assembly for mounting trim on the sheet panel of the prototype vehicle comprising a support member having a slot at one end and a hole at the other end, a push button for fixing said support member on to the panel and a tree clip for fixing said trim to the support member.
Another aspect of the present invention is a method of mounting trim on the sheet metal panel of the prototype vehicle comprising steps of inserting collar of a push button in to the slot at one end of the support member to fix the push button on the support member, press fit the push button into the holes provided on the panel to fix the push button and support member assembly into the panel, providing a hole on the

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
Referring now to the drawings wherein the showings are for the purpose of illustrating a preferred embodiment of the invention only, and not for the purpose of limiting the same.
Reference to the figures 1, 2 & 3, a bracket assembly (10) is used for mounting FRP trims (5) on sheet metal panel (4). The assembly is having a support member (1) formed from a metal plate. A slot for fixing a push button (2) and a hole for inserting a tree clip (3) are made on the metal plate at two ends. The metal plate then turns in desired shape such as 'C, 'Z' to form as a bracket. The height of the support member (1) will be equal to the gap between the panel and inner surface of the trim as per the design. The push button (2) having a male component (shaft) with thick part and a base part (flange). A collar arrangement is provided in the Flange of the push button to slide fit into the slot in the support member. Male component of the push button having thick part at the middle and used to connect panel and the support member. Push button is made from plastic. Another element of the bracket assembly is a Tree clip having ribbed shaft and a head to press fit. This is used to connect the FRP with support member. The collar of the push button slides into the slot at one end of the
4

support member to fix it. So that, the male component of the push button is projected outside. The thick part in the male component of the push button press fit in the holes provided on the panels of the vehicle to fix the push button and support member to the panel. Said holes are design intent holes used for mounting trims during production. Holes are provided in the FRP trims at place where mountings are provided as per design. Mount FRP trims at the respective position on the support members and tree clips are inserted through the FRP trim into the hole provided on the support member.
The bracket assembly is very simple in arrangement and it can be fixed very easily. The bracket assembly can be reused. The arrangement facilitates easy assembly of trims on the panel. Any design change can be easily accommodated. The arrangements avoid drilling additional holes on the panels to fix the trims which will improve the NVH properties. It will provide good appearance to the complete assembly. By using such brackets design intent assembly can be achieved.
