Title of Invention

A PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION FOR JOINT LUBRICATION
Abstract The present invention concerns the use of liposomes having membranes with at least one phospholipid (PL) of the group consisting of a glycerophospholipid (GPL) having two, being the same or different, C12-C16 hydrocarbon chain and a sphingolipid (SPL) having a C12-C18 hydrocarbon chain, the one or more membranes having a phase transition temperature in which solid ordered (SO) to liquid disordered (LD) phase transition occurs, the phase transition temperature being within a temperature of about 20°C to about 39°C for lubrication of joints.
