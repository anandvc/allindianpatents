Title of Invention

FOLDABLE NON BREAKABLE SOLAR CHARGER
Abstract According to the invention there is provided a Foidable portable and non-fragile solar charger .The Foldable Charger can be folded like a bag and being low weight can be taken to remote places comfortably to charge the batteries at remote places where conventional power is not available. Also, the cut pieces of solar cells can be used to give required power for charging the batteries of lights and the communication systems of the Personnel working in remote and border areas. The charger is suitable to charge a 12 Volt Lead Acid Battery.
