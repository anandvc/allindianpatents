Title of Invention

ROLLER TAPPET WITH COLD ADVANCE
Abstract This invention in general relates to a Roller tappet device for warm start and adapted for cold start use on single and multi-cylinder diesel engine with PF pumps. The Roller Tappet device with cold advance provide quick warm up and thereby achieve the emission requirements with reference to cold start and white smoke. The roller tappet includes a movable piston which advance is the plunger position, a plate valve connected to the lubricating oil supply pump such that lubricating oil flowing through the said plate valve provides the upward thrust on the movable piston which in turn lifts and thereby advances the plunger towards the top, a stopper means to limit the stroke of the piston; a cut off means to cut off the lubricating oil supply and a return means of throttle hole on said plate valve to facilitate the collapse of the plunger position via the lubricating cut off means in warm condition. This invention thus decides the position of the plunger depending on the start up warm to cold condition and advance position is created.

OBJECT OF THE INVENTION
This Invention relates to a Roller Tappet device for advancing the Injection beginning in cold condition (- 25°C to + 40°C) In single and multi-cylinder diesel

engine with PF pumps, for easy startability and quick warm up and to achieve the emission requirements with reference to cold start and white smoke.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
The invention relates to a Roller Tappet device for advancing the injection beginning in cold condition (- 25°C to + 40°C), in single and multi-cylinder diesel engine with PF pumps, by means of which the injectbn onset is adjusted in cold condition and also in normal condition; and comprising of :-
a. A piston so provided in the Roller Tappet, such that it moves up and down the said Roller Tappet, due to flow of lubricating oil from lubricating oil supply pump which is at a pressure of 2.5 - 3 bar, through a plate valve. Said lubricating oil which flows through the said plate valve, lifts the said hydro tappet piston by 1.5 mm. The said 1.5 mm lift enables the plunger to be 1.5 mm in advance position. The said 1.5 mm lift of the said plunger corresponds to 8° to 10° crank angle depending upon the cam profile.
The stroke of the said piston is limited by a stopper. The said stroke of the Roller Tappet device is so designed that under the existing cold condition, the piston is advanced to the extent desired by creating an equivalent pressure in the lubricating oil and the stopper such that the piston is held in desired advance position. The said plate valve connected to lubricating oil pump provides a hydraulic lock and therein prevents the said lubricating oil leakage due to Injection pressure. The said plate valve is provided with a throttle hole. A return

means is provided wherein the said throttle hole facilitates the collapse of the said plunger to the normal position as soon as the said lubricating oil supply is cut-off by cut-ff means, due to the leakage of the said lubricating oil through the said throttle hole on the said plate valve.
The size of the said throttle hole on the said plate valve, is relative to the selected diameter of the said plunger and the peak injection pressure of the pump.
b. Application of the Invention :
This Roller Tappet device is recommended for use on single and multi cylinder diesel engine with PF pumps.
c. Function of the Invention :
The function of the Roller Tappet device is to advance the injection beginning In cold condition (- 25°C to + 40°C).
d. Working principle of the invention :
When the Roller Tappet is in Bottom Dead Centre position, lubricating oil, which is at a pressure of 2.5 to 3.0 bar, flows through the plate valve and lifts the hydro tappet piston by 1.5 mm. With this, the plunger is 1.5 mm in advance position as compared to normal operating position. This 1.5 mm lift corresponds to 8° to 10° crank angle depending upon the cam profile. The stopper limits the stroke of the piston. The stroke of the device is relative to the cold advance, the piston advanced to an extent decide by creating an equalent pressure in the

lubricating oil and limiting the advance by the stopper means such that the piston is held in a forward position at a decide location as decide which is further relative to the requirement of the engine. The plate valve connected to lubricating oil supply pump provides a hydraulic tockand prevents the lubricating oil leakage due to injection pressure. When the engine is in cold condition (-25°C to + 40°C), the plunger is in advance position. In the warm condition ( >40°C) the lubricating oil is cut off. Due to the lubricating oil leakage through the throttle hole on the plate valve, the plunger collapses back to the normal operating position.
In cold condition, the lubricating oil inlet pressure prevents the lubricating oil leakage through the throttle hole. The size of the throttle hole is selected depending upon the plunger diameter and the peak injection pressure of the pump.
e. Advantages of the invention :-
Easy start ability and quick warm up ;
To achieve the emission requirements with reference to cold start and white smoke.
The invention is related to the Roller Tappet device consisting of a piston which moves up and down in the Roller Tappet against plunger spring force and lifts the plunger due to the lubricating oil pressure, a plate valve which provides hydraulic lock and throttle hole on the plate valve which facilitates the collapse

of the plunger to the normal position as soon as the lubricating oil supply is cut off.
