Title of Invention

A DEVICE FOR AUTOMATICALLY DISCONNECTING POWER SUPPLY WHEN OVERHEAD LINES OF A POWER SYSTEM BREAK
Abstract A device for automatically disconnecting power supply when overhead lines of a power system break, which comprises a non-conductive triangular frame (3) fitted onto a phase line between two adjacent posts of the power supply system. A non-conductive hollow cylinder (5) is provided with a closed end nearer to the post that comes first in the direction of the current flow. A metallic ball (2) is provided, which is freely movable inside the cylinder (S). A pair of metallic strips (1) is disposed between the cylinder at an end away from the closed end and connected respective Iy to the phase line and to a neutral line. In the event of the phase line breaking or going down, the metallic ball (2) rollos down the cylinder short circuiting the pair of metallic strips (1) and disconnecting the power supply through the broken line by blowing of a fuse.
