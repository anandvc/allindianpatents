Title of Invention

BAR PULLING ARM
Abstract The innovated bar pulling arm assembly is used for industrial mass production purpose. Necessity of new bar pulling arm innovation came because of continuous production to reduce the schedule time and to increase the productivity. Bar pulling arm can be used on all CNC turning center and auto lathes. Which has a hollow spindle to facilitate lengthy bars and auto clamping, unclamping facility. (chuck or collect). There is no need of a bar feed unit. When working with bar feed unit to control the length of the component a stopper is fixed in one of the station in the turret, instead of that stopper a bar pulling arm can be fixed and bar can be pulled to the required length by grooving or slit bush method which is convenient. The CNC center of small scale industries they don't have the existing costly bar feeding unit. The innovated bar pulling assembly is very cheap and very simple and it serves the same purpose of bar feeding unit for industrial mass production. The innovation bar pulling assembly is designed is such a way that it can directly mounted on the turret as a tool and fastened as a tool here no (separate motor) electrical power consumption and no risk of accident, where as in bar feeding unit there is a chances of accident. The space for bar feeding unit is more where as no more space required for the innovated one, with this we can pull the bar as required length in this method and no raw material wastage. Cycle time reduces also bar feed setup time reduced drastically. No operator risk easy program. To support length bar behind of the spindle a simple designed support is required.

This invention relates to a bar pulling arm used in all CNC turning centers.
This invention has for its object to provide a holder, for the said arm assembly, having a recess or pocket for the holding of a arm at one end. The said recess or pocket has parallel of the machine spindle face.
A clamping element is provided on the holder to hold the pulling arm in place of said recess or pocket. This clamping element is provided with means to prevent displacement of the arm once it is placed in position in the holder.
The bar pulling arm of this invention can be used on all types of CNC turning center, which having auto clamping and unclamping facility and other special purpose auto machines. The following are the different bar pulling method of this invention.
1. Grooving method
2. Slit bush method and
3. Magnetic method
The arm may have a cross-section such as spanner shaped of rectangular with locating slots on the face, top and bottom sides for appropriate positioning of the same in the holder. The locking profile of the arm have the required profile for bar pulling. The locking profile is CC shape, and straight is provided by holding the bar in the arm.

This invention relates to a bar pulling arm in all CNC turning center.
Bar pulling is a technique which is ever evolving into better and still better methods calling for specialized skill and precise technical knowledge. Previously bar feeder unit is required (electromechanical and hydraulic bar feeder is available) in that for bar feeding continuously motor is running so electrical power is consumed. Hydraulic oil required in case of hydraulic bar feed unit. Machining bar and bar feeder bar, both occupied more (space) length so gives more vibration, we can't give high rpm, So cycle time is more. While working change the insert or some small work by mistake open the collect or chuck bar will be comeout seriously accident occurs. Bar feeder unit occupied more area, stopper and bar feeder is different and cost is too high.
Another novel method is to use bar pulling arm suitably located and clamped on the holder for a variety of methods. In this arrangement the cost of the bar pulling arm comparatively very less and after usage, only the bar suitable arm have to be replaced, whereas the arm and holder is not damaged or worn out as in the case mentioned above.
The present invention has for the object to provide a Bar pulling arm assembly to be used on all CNC Turning Centers and other auto special purpose machines. The bar pulling arm is clamp on the turret like normal tools it is very simple 'C shape profile arm. An arm we can change easily for different size bar like parting blade, extra power is not required for pulling the bar.
Necessity of new bar pulling arm innovation came because of continuous production to reduce the schedule time and to increase the productivity. Bar pulling arm can be used on all CNC turning centers and auto lathes which having auto clamping and unclamping facility, (chuck or collet)
There is no need of a bar feed unit. When working with bar feed unit to control the length of the component a stopper is fixed in one of the station in the turret. Instead of that stopper a bar pulling arm can be fixed and bar can be pulled from the front and clamped the bar, which is to be machined as per drawing.
For different size of rod, different arm has to be used. This can be easily changed like a parting blade and cycle time can be reduced.

To pull the bar, groove is made at the end of bar and pull the bar to the required length, carryout all the operations as per dimension, then part off the component and made groove for next job pulling. Same steps is followed for Mass Production till the bar completion.
Another object of the invention is to provide a Bar pulling assembly which can, easily be adapted to CNC turning centers and hold the bar firmly during the bar pulling of the CNC lathe or like machine to which the bar pulling arms may be fixed on turret.
A still further object of the invention is to provide a simple and economical bar pulling assembly.
With these and other objectives in view, this invention provides different bar pulling arm comprising of a pulling arm and a slit bush, the said arm holder having a shank, one end of which is to be fitted in a machine turret and the other end is provided for sitting on groove of bar, the said arm having identical profile at one end thereof depending upon the nature of the pulling for which it is required, the pulling portions of the arm having a locking on bar groove predetermined value, means provided at the said other end of the shank for holding the said arm and to be clamped by the said clamping device, the said means comprising a recess or pocket the base of which is perpendicular in a direction opposition to the machine axis, the said arm also being provided with recess on the face of holder being located in the recess or pocket on the arm holder, and to be held in position by the clamping device, and wherein the said clamping device is provided with means such as an extension, on the bar pulling arm holder.
The bar pulling arm are 'C shaped and has a straight form on its bar groove at one end. The clamping device comprises of a pivotally mounted clamping element having a projection at one and to hold the bar resting on the said bar groove.
The clamping device may also comprise a clamping element having a projection at one end thereof to be seated over the arm to hold the same lock on bar and pull the required length and come back normal position and an extension at the other end thereof, the said extension being disposed in a allot, recess, or the like on the tool holder.

The clamping element is held on the tool holder by means, such as a screw. The axis of the arm may be perpendicular to the longitudinal axis of the tool
holder.

DIFFERENT BAR PULLING METHODS
(1) Grooving (2) Slit bush and (3) Magnet
In these three methods, only two methods are more useful. They are grooving and slit bush. 1. GROOVING METHOD:
This method can be used widely when the supplied bar outside diameter (OD) is rough and normally OD operation is common in all machining components. So we can make groove on 3D, for pulling the bar. Bar can be pull with help of bar pulling arm groove depth and width has to maintain as per arm size. When the Bar pulling arm is locked on the groove (X-axis) a lengthy job can be pulled easily. Required length is pulled and after the operations has been done again a groove is made (space has to be left for making groove) again required length is pulled and supported by

tailstock and remaining operations can be carried out. Similarly making required number of grooves should pull any amount of length. Any material (ferrous or non ferrous) can be pull & there is no limit for pulling the bar length.
2. SLIT BUSH METHOD :
When the supplied bar is smooth or polished, slit bush is used for pulling the bar to the required length to carry out the operation without any additional operation (Groove).
With this method any amount of length can be pulled, instead of groove on the job there will be a groove on the slit bush. Slit bush is fixed on the bar tightly, the arm sit on the groove and pull the slit bush along with the bar to required length (when the chuck is unlocked), then the bar is clamped and arm pushes the slit bush back to its original position and carry out all the operation as per dimensions. The part off the component and same above steps followed for mass production till the bar completion.
3. MAGNETIC METHOD:
By using magnetic method any dia and possible length of ferrous material bar can be pulled to the required length for machining component.
In place of a stopper used in the turret a magnet has to be fixed and bar can be pulled. Like bar feed unit in this method, length of bar that can be pulled depends on maximum 'Z' axis length. It also depends on distance from rod face to maximum distance of turret can travel away from the rod face (limited Length).
To machine a maximum dia rod a powerful magnet is enough. This is useful for all smaller dia rods. Length of a rod should be 1 meter or 2 meters which ever is convenient.
Bars pulled in this method will not effect the magnetic properties of component because the face is machined. Magnet should be fixed in the turret opposite to the tools which remove chips, care has been taken to see that chips do not get struck to the Magnet.

BAR PULLING PARTS AND EXPLANATION: Holder, Arm, and Slit Bush.
HOLDER: It is common to both grooving as well as slit bush method.
Holder is fixed in the turret like a tool, and the arm is fixed in the holder with help of bolts. The arm holder is fabricated in such a way that the arm is exactly in the center height of the M/c Spindle and & perpendicular to the Z.-axis.
ARM: It is mainly used to pull the bar. Arm is fixed in the holder with the help of bolts. Different size arms are required for different dia bars. The ends of the CC clamp (half circle) arm is projected straightly.
This arm after sitting in the groove in half circle is slightly projected. It will be holding the bar to machines center axis, then it pulls the bar to required length as per the program.
The bar pulling arm is designed such a way that it pulls the bar and care has been taken to maintain center height of spindle axis.
Which is the bars used in the bar feed unit, which can be pulled by arm. As collet varies for different dia of bars similarly arm also varies.
SLIT BUSH: Slit bush outer dia (OD) has a groove for the arm to sit and there will be a slit on one side. Internal Dia (ID) of slit bush is less than the OD of the bar. This will help the slit bush to sit tightly on the bar. There is a radius on one side of the ID. This radius helps the slit bush to enter the rod and move on it easily
Different dia rods different slit bush with arm is required. When seating the slit bush, internal radius side should be enter on the bar. Then it should be tapped slowly, so that the slit bush sits on the rod tightly. When the slit bush seats on the bar tightly then to pull the bar as required length along with pulling arm.
To pull the bar or pushes slit bush, machine cutting force is enough. If bar size various machine size and capacity of cutting force also varies.

THE MAIN OBJECTIVE OF THE INVENTION :
1. Expensive bar feeding unit not required.
2. M/cs without bar feeding unit can also be used for mass production.
3. Floor area saved.
4. Hydraulic oil saved.
5. Electricity saved.
6. Bar feed setting time avoided.
7. High rpm can be used for machining.
8. Noise from bar feeder unit avoided & no noise pollution
9. Increase in productivity.
10. Bar feeding also becomes a part of CNC m/c resulting in closed loop system.
11. Accidents resulting from bar feeding unit are avoided.
12. Loads spindle bearing and bar feed stopper of turret are avoided.
Note: 1) For lengthy bars support is to be provided on backside of spindle by designing a suitable support 2) For easy movement of bar use suitable silent bushes in the spindle.
