Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A FERTILIZER
Abstract This invention relates to a process for producing a fertilizer which comprises in providing a first layer of pith covered by a second layer of a fungus which is capable of feeding on lignin present in the pith, a third layer of pith covered by a fourth layer of urea, repeating said first to fourth layers around height of 1m, water is sprinkled on such layer to get moisture content.

FIELD OF INVENTION

PRIOR ART
It is generally known that a coconut comprises a nut protected by a fleshy material to consist of fibre, pith and peel. The peel and the nut is removed and the remainder constituents consisting of the pith and fibre is subjected to a retting process. The retting process causes a separation of the fibre from the pith as the f ibre or coir has commercial value and the coir yarn can be woven into coir products such as floor coverings. The pith separated from the husk constitutes a waste. The pith can not be employed

as a fertilizer as it contains lignin. No process has hitherto been described or suggested in the art to convert the pith of a useful application.
