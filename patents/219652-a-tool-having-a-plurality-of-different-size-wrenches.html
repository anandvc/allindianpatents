Title of Invention

"A TOOL HAVING A PLURALITY OF DIFFERENT SIZE WRENCHES"
Abstract This invention relates to a tool having a plurality of different size wrenches comprising insert or handle (2 and 11 a-11c)characterized in that a head (3a and 3b, 13a-13c) at each end of the handle securedly held thereto wherein the central longitudinal axis of said head being coincident with the central longitudinal axis of the handle and said heads having a plurality of wrenches of different sizes provided integrally therewith.
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
The present invention relates to a tool having a plurality of different size wrenches.
PRIOR ART
Wrenches are known in the art and which comprise an insert or a lever with a hex wrench of a first size provided with a head member only at one end or a hex wrench of a differnet size provided with a head member at the opposite end. Several distinct disadvantages are assocaited is that as each tool had only two sizes of a wrench, it was necessary to provide a plurality of such tools to constitute a set of wrenches. Yet another disadvantage was that such tools had a wrench either in a millimieter or inch size, and not a combination thereof. A further disadvantage is that such tools were easily misplaced.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose a novel construction of tool having a plurality of different size wrenches.
Another object of this invention is to propose a tool having a plurality of different size wrenches at one or both ends.

Yet another object of this invention is to propose a tool which is versatile.
DESCRIPTION OF THIS INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided a tool having a plurality of different size wrenches comprising insert or handle characterized in that a head at each end of the handle securedly held thereto wherein the central longitudinal axis of said head being coincident with the central longitudinal axis of the handle and said heads having a plurality of wrenches of different sizes provided integrally therewith.
Specifically, in accordance with this invention but without implying any limitation thereto, the tool comprises an insert or handle having a head at opposite ends thereof. A head member is integrally provided at either end of said handle or insert. Each head member has wrenches of different sizes. Thus, the head member has a set of different size wrenches in accordance with a first measurement, such as millimeters. The opposite head member has wrenches of different sizes in accordance with a second measurement, such as inches.
In accordance with another embodiment of this invention, a head member with different size wrenches is provided only at one end.
In accordance with yet another embodiment of this invention the tool comprises an insert to consist
of three handle disposed at an inclination to each other. A head is provided at the free end of each
handle. The head member is integrally or securedly provided at said end .of each handle. Each head member
has wrenches of different sizes. Thus, the head member has a set of different size wrenches in accordance with
a first measurement, such as millimeters. The other head member has wrenches of different sizes in
accordance with a second measurement, such as inches. The third head member has a wrench of yet another
different size.
The handles are disposed at an inclination of 120° to each other.
