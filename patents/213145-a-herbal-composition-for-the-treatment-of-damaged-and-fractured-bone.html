There is used GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE) tree ends in powder form, and ‘charoli (buchanania latifolia)’ powder with same proportion against 70% of GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE). This composition is the best for use. This composition can be used in powder and liquid forms both.

The following specification describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed: -


FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The invention relates generally to a herbal composition, can be used for the treatment of damaged and fractured bone of human being .This composition can be used in powder and liquid forms both.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION:
During my own accident, l was supposed to use root of GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE); as a freshener, to brush my teeth, and surprisingly 1 found ft amazing. l tried it often and got back to my normal condition in less than required time. Then l continuously worked on it, applied it to others as well.
OBJECTIVE OF THE INVENTION:
It is therefore a primary object of the present invention to provide a treatment of damaged and fractured bone of human being
To learn it"s working and how it can produce excellent results for human being.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
The present invention provides excellent results on bone damages. It recovers to normal condition in minimum time without any side effects, cost saving.
DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION:
There used GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE)" tree ends in powder form, and then added "ashwagandha (withania somnifera)" powder and "charoli (buchanania
latifolia)" powder with same proportion against 70 % of GADHAM (GREWIA
DAMINE). This composition is the best for use.
l have used root of GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE) tree, to make the herbal composition.
It is very easy to prepare the composition. I put roots of "GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE)" in natural heat available from sun, under sunlight keep it for two days, then simple grind it with grinding machine, add ashwagandha (withania somnifera) powder and charoli (buchanania latifolia) power .which are available in open market, then mix GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE) - 70 %, ashwagandha (withania somnifera) 15 % and charoli (buchanania latifolia) powder 15 %, mix all three properly and the composition is ready to use. you can take this composition directly in powder form orally, with milk or with water.

The composition may further comprise a pharmaceutically acceptable excipient for an oral dosage form.
The excipient may be selected from starch, pre-gelatinized starch, dicalcium phosphate or a mixture thereof.
The composition may also contain a preservative selected from propyl paraben sodium, methyl paraben sodium or bronopol or a mixture thereof
It is necessary to take /use this composition in early morning time preferably, between 5 am to 7 am, with empty stomach.
Method of preparation -
Take root of GADHAM (GREWIA DAMiNE) tree, dry it for two days in sunlight, a natural heat, grind it , now, add ashwagandha (withania somnifera) powder and charoli (buchanania latifolia) powder, which are available in open market, or, we can grind it at home, the ratio is 70 % GADHAM (GREWIA DAMINE), 15 % ashwagandha (withania somnifera) and 15% charoli (buchanania latifolia) powder, mix all three properly and with the mentioned proportion .we can apply/use for bone damage/fracture 20 gm per day in early morning with empty stomach with water or milk., and as per our personal experience and observation we assure patient will recover his/her injury within 60% less time than doctor prescribes., but we can say not more than 15 days is required to take this composition. For general /minor injuries and damage. For major damage it will surely help a lot ,but one has to continue for month for better result
For the medical trail the present novel herbal composition, which is used either in powder form or in liquid or semi solid form specifically for damages, has given a good result without any side effect. The patient recovers to normal condition in minimum time with minimum dosage i.e. one teaspoon in water or milk.
EXAMPLE:
I met with an accident and got my bone fractured At that time I used to brush my teeth with gadham stick as freshner.but later with the same herb in combination of other herbs made formulation using water/milk and applied to my fracture.lt gave me a surprising effect as to recover completely from the bone fracture without any side effects within a short time with minimum dosage.
I have applied the composition to my self and to other friends and familiars. We all have got excellent results without any side effect

PRESCRIBED DOSE:
20 gm for all age human being, with water or milk and 10 gm for child less than 10 years age. As explained earlier the composition can be in liquid or powder forms only. This can be taken with water or milk.
The composition is very effective on bone damage and having no side effect, environment friendly.
ADVANTAGES OF THE PRESENT INVENTION
The composition is very effective as RECOVERY agent particularly in BONE DAMAGE, cost effective too.
The composition is effective even in small dose and can also be administered in
any dosage form.
The composition is very safe and has NO side effects
