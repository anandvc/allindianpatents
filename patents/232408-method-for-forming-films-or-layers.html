Title of Invention

METHOD FOR FORMING FILMS OR LAYERS
Abstract An improved method for forming films of organo-metallic complexes in which an organic complex and a labile metal salt are vaporised together or sequentially and the vapour condensed on to a substrate to form a film or layer of the organo-metallic complex on the substrate. The method produces electroluminescent films with improved performance.
