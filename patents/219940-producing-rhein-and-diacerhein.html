Title of Invention

PRODUCING RHEIN AND DIACERHEIN
Abstract The following description sets forth a process for producing rhein. diacerhein and other diacyl derivatives thereof, which comprises the following steps : treatment of a diphenylketone in which R1 is -OR', -NR'R"", -SR' , where R' and R"" are H, an alkyl or aromatic group : R2 is H or a protective group, R3 is -OH or C1-C4 alkyl with an acid or superacid to give a 1-aminoanthraquinone derivative. diazotisation, replacement of the -NH2 group by -OH . optional removal of the protective group, and acylation.
