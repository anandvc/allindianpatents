Title of Invention

ALGINATE MATRIX PARTICLES
Abstract The present invention relates to matrix particles comprising a pluality of inclusions of oil, preferably flavor or fragrance oil, dispersed within a matrix, the matrix comprising a crosslinked polymer and filler as microcrystalline cellulose, wherein the cross-linked polymer is selected from the group consisting of alginate and pectin, derivatives thereof, or combinations thereof and a process for preparing the same.
