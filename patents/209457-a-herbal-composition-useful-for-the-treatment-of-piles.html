Title of Invention

A HERBAL COMPOSITION USEFUL FOR THE TREATMENT OF PILES.
Abstract The present invention discloses a novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of Piles, comprising the following ingredients in a ratio as mentioned therewith: 10 % Kattha powder of Acacia Catechu plant,90 % of Ash after burning of seed of Sapindus Trifoliatus; this invention also discloses a process for the preparation of the novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of Piles, comprising the steps: Collecting the seeds of Sapindus Trifoliatus, Washing with pure water for 5 mins,Keeping the seeds in an earthen pot in which a hole is there in the center keeping the earthen pot on a mild fire for burning the seeds,Collecting the Ash after conversion of the seeds to ash ,Grinding of the ash into a fine powder,Mixing the said ash with Kattha powder of Acacia Catechu in a rartio of 10 & 90% respectively to get the desired herbal composition.

Background & Prior Art for the invention
So far in the prior art neither any ayurvedic nor any allopathic or any homeopathic medicines are available which can treat piles effectively and permanently. The allopathic final remedy is said to be the surgical exercise to remove the piles, however it has been seen in most of the instances that the piles reappear. Therefore allopathic treatment is very costly . If the allopathic treatment is carried out by medication only the remedy is never permanent. The allopathic medicines exert side effects also. The homeopathic & ayurvedic systems provides some medicines as given hereunder but these also can not treat piles permanently and they reappear. Abovesaid medicines are:
1. Pilax Tablet & ointment ( Sold by Himalaya Ayurvedic Drugs )
2. Arshonet Normal and Forte Tab. & Ointment (Charak )
3. AbhyaSin Tab. ( Zhandu )
4. Arshohar Tab. ( RajyaVaidya Shital Prasad)
5. Arshantak Capsule (Garg Phamaceticals)
6. Saini Line Syrup ( Dabur)
These medicines give good results as long as the use of these medicines are not closed. On closure the piles reappear and then final remedy is only the surgical removal of the piles.
Objects of the present invention:
The object of the present invention is to provide an effective treatment for Piles. Further objective of the invention is to provide a process for the preparation of herbal composition useful in the treatment of the piles. Still further objective of the invention is to provide a novel herbal composition for the treatment of piles. Disclosure of the invention:
The present invention discloses a novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of Piles, comprising the following ingredients in a ratio as mentioned therewith:
(i) 10 % Kattha powder of Acacia Catechu plant
(ii) 90 % of Ash after burning of seed of Sapindus Trifoliatus
This invention also discloses a process for the preparation of the novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of Piles, comprising the following steps:
Collecting the seeds of Sapindus Trifoliatus,
Washing with pure water for 5 mins.
Keeping the seeds in an earthen pot in which a hole is there in the center
keeping the earthen pot on a mild fire for burning the seeds
Collecting the Ash after conversion of the seeds to ash
Grinding of the ash into a fine powder
Mixing the said ash with Kattha powder of Acacia Catechu in a rartio of 10
& 90% respectively to get the desired herbal composition.
