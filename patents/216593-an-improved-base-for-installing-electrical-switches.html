AN IMPROVED BASE FOR INSTALLING ELECTRICAL SWITCHES
The invention relates to an improved base for installing electrical switches. More particularly the invention relates to an improved base for installing electrical switches which is a base for snuggly fitting at least one switch in the block.
The prior art switches are themselves fitted on the block their was no base for fitting. Therefore there was little chance for decorating and smooth working of the switches. In the advent of Improve switches with better installation facilities, the aesthetic look is also taken care off. The base is made from any shape & size made from non conductor mouldable material the contour of the base is made attractive while keeping view for easy installation.
The primary object is therefore to provide an improved base for installation electrical switches wherein the installation of switches has been made easier as well as disposable while the outlook of the electrical installation will be attractive.
Still further object is to provide an improved base for installation electrical switches which will be simple shock proof with a better aesthetic look.
Accordingly there is provided an improved base for installation of Electrical Switches made from mouldable material comprising a box type construction

open at one end with outwardly extended flanges having at least one central groove for installing at least one switch, said groove having extended flange with plurality of holes on the either side for installing the switches which have matching grooves for fixing the individual switches by fastening means; the said base having grooves on extreme left and right side on the face side for fixing the base on the electrical blocks .
The invention will now be described reference to figure 1 to 8 in the accompanying drawing.
Figures 1 to 8: Front view, side view and top view of an improved base for installing electrical switches with different number of grooves for installing switches.
Referring to figures 1 to 8 an improved base (1) for installation of Electrical Switches made from mouldable material comprising a box type construction open at one end with outwardly extended flanges (3). Improved base consists of at least one central groove (4) for installing at least one switch. This groove is having extended flange (6) with plurality of holes (7) on the either side for installing the switches which have matching grooves for fixing the individual switches by fastening means. The base contains grooves (10) on extreme right and left on the face side for fixing the base on the electrical blocks.

I CLAIM:
1. An improved base for installation of Electrical Switches made from mouldable material comprising a box type construction open at one end with outwardly extended flanges having at least one central groove for installing at least one switch, said groove having extended flange with plurality of holes on the either side for installing the switches which have matching grooves for fixing the individual switches by fastening means; the said base having grooves on extreme left and right side on the face side for fixing the base on the electrical blocks.
2. An improved base for installing electrical switches as claimed in claim 1 and substantially herein describe with reference to figure 1 to 8 in the accompanying drawings.
