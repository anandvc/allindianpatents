"MORPHOLINE DERIVATIVES AS NOREPINEPHRINE REUPTAKE INHIBITORS"
Abstract Compounds of the general formula (I) are inhibitors of the reuptake of norepinephrine. As such, they may be useful for the treatment of disorders of the central and/or peripheral nervous system.
Full Text MORPHOLINE DERIVATIVES AS NOREPINEPHRINE REUFTAKE INHIBITORS
This invention relates to novel morpholine compounds, and to their use in selectively inhibiting norepinephrine reuptake.Selective inhibition of norepinephrine reuptake is a relatively new mode of action for the treatment of affective disorders. Norepinephrine appears to play an important role in the disturbances of vegetative function associated with affective, anxiety and cognitive disorders. Atomoxetine hydrochloride is a selective inhibitor of norepinephrine reuptake, and is marketed for the treatment of attention deficit hyperactrvity disorder (ADHD). Reboxetine is also a selective norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor, and is marketed for the treatment of depression. WO99/15177 discloses the use of reboxetine to treat ADHD and WO01/01973 discloses the use of S,S-reboxetinc to treat inter alia ADHD. WO2004/018441 discloses certain morpholine derivatives as selective inhibitors of the reuptake of norepinephrine.
