Title of Invention

A SAFE DEVICE FOR DELIVERING MEDIA
Abstract DEVICE FOR DISPENSING OF MEDIA, WITH A DISPENSING CONTAINER (1() WITH CLOSURE UNIT (3) RECEIVING THE RELAVANT MEDIUM, INCORPORATING A CANNULA (11) FOR THE MEDIUM TO BE DISPENSED, THEOUTERMOST END (15) OF WHICH PROJECTS FROM THE CLOSURE UNIT (3), AND WITH ASAFETY MEANS (5,17) WHICH CAN BNE TRANSFORMED FORM AND EFFECTIVE PROTECTIVE POSITION IN WHICH IT COVERS THE OUTERMOST END (15) OF THE CANNULA (11) INTO AN INEFFECTIVE OPERATING POSITION ENVISED FOR TRHE DISPENSING OF THE MEDIUM FOR THE CANNULA (11) AND INCIRPORATING A SAFETY BODY (19) WHICH CAN BE PUSHED BACK INTO AN OPERATING POSITION AND FORWARDS INTO THE PROTEDCTIVE POSITION FROM A PROJECTING PROTECTIVE POSITION AT THE END (15) OF THE CANNULA (11), CHARACTERIZED IN THAT THE SAFETY MEANS (5,17) INCORPORATES A PROTECTIVE HOOD (5) ENCLOSING THE PROJECTING END (15) OF THE CANNULA (11), WHICH CAN BE REMOVED FORM THE DISPENSING CONTAINER (1), THE SAME BEING CONNECTED WITH THE DISPENSING CONTAINER VIA A PERFORATION (7), AND IN THAT THE PROTECTIVE HOOD (5) INCORPORATES A TUR4NING KNOB (9) AS A HANDLE FOR TWISTING OFF THE PROTECTIVE HOOD (5) FROM THE DISPENSING CONTAINER (1).
