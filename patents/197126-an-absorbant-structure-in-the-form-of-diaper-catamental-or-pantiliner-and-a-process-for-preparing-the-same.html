Title of Invention

"AN ABSORBANT STRUCTURE IN THE FORM OF DIAPER, CATAMENTAL OR PANTILINER AND A PROCESS FOR PREPARING THE SAME"
Abstract A composition of matter, comprising an absorbent gelling material, a water-soluble or water-dispersible binder material and a water-insoluble odor-absorbing agent. The odor-absorbing agent typically comprises greater than 10% by weight of the final composition and 20% by weight of a particulate odor-absorbing agent selected from zeolite and activated carbon odor-absorbing agents.

We claim:
1. An absorbent structure in the form of diaper, catamenial or pentiliner comprising
a fluid permeable topsheet material, an absorbent core and a fluid permeable backsheet
material, characterized in that said absorbent artical contains an odor-absorbing amount
of a composition comprising an absorbent gelling material, a water soluble or water dispersible binder material and a water-insoluble odor-absorbing agent wherein the composition has:
(a) at least 20% by weight of a particulate odor-absorbing agent selected from
zeolite and activated carbon odor-absorbing agents, and mixture thereof;
(b) at least 40% by weight of an absorbent gelling material of the kind herein
described; and
(c) a water soluble or water-dispersible binder material of the kind herein
described forming the balance of the composition.
2. A process for preparing the absorbent artical as claimed in claim 1 comprising
the step of contacting said absorbent gelling material and said odor-absorbing agents with the water-soluble or water-dispersible binder material, said process step being carried out in a fluidized bed apparatus.
3. An absorbent artical substantially as herein described in any one of the
foregoing examples.
4. A process for preparing an absorbent artical substantially as hereinbefore
described in any one of the foregoing examples.
