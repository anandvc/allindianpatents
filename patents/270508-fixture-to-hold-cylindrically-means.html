Title of Invention

FIXTURE TO HOLD CYLINDRICALLY MEANS
Abstract A fixture to hold cylindrical means comprising a hollow cube, said cube being opened at two opposite vertical sides, an eye-bolt being provided to hold the cylindrical means, head of said eye bolt being provided at front open side of the said cube, tail end of the said eye bolt being secured with rectangular plate movably at back open end of the said cube, a v-slot being provided at front of the vertical sides of the cube, a hollow pipe being secured at the center of the horizontal sides of the cube, a stopper being fitted with right side of the cube.

Objects and advantages of the present invention
The main object of the present invention is to provide a Fixture to hold cylindrical means ' which produces the keyway at center of the shaft, rotor of submersible pump, hollow pipe etc. more precisely and efficiently with neither any index marking left on shaft nor collapsing of pipe during gripping of shaft by eye bolt.
Another object of the present invention is to provide Fixture to hold cylindrical means, (which produces the keyway on larger diameter shaft as compare to the conventional mean of tool post on lathe bed.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide Fixture to hold cylindrical means, which is low in cost, easy in operation.

Brief description of invention
In accordance with this invention a fixture to hold cylindrical means comprising a hollow
cube. The cube is made through two "L" shaped block. The cube is opened through front
vertical side and back vertical side of the cube, which are opposite two each other. An

eye-bolt is provided inside the cube horizontally. The head of the eye bolt is provided at the front side of the cube to hold the cylindrical means and provide the tight grip to the different size of cylindrical means. Tail end of the eye bolt is secured with rectangular plate movably at back open end of the cube. A v-slot of same size and shape is provided at front vertical end of the right vertical side and left vertical side of the cube. V-slot is provided at the center of the vertical end of the vertical sides. A hole is provided at the center of the horizontal sides. Horizontal sides are connected through a hollow pipe at the center inside the cube. A vertical support is provided between the right and left vertical sides and connected with horizontals sides inside the cube to give grip to the cylindrical means. A stopper is provided at the right vertical side of the cube which is connected ■' through a connector.

This arrangement makes the centers of the cutter blade and cylindrical means match with each other.
Manufacturing of gear is also possible by this fixture by putting blank on which tooth is required to cut on metal shaft at one end towards the cutter blade and a master gear on other end of metal shaft towards the stopper bolt. This metal shaft, blank and master gear are placed on the fixture such that blank remains in front of the cutter blade of lathe and indexing is done by putting stopper bolt on tooth of master gear.
