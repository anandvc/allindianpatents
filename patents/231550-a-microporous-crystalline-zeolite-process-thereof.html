Title of Invention

"A MICROPOROUS CRYSTALLINE ZEOLITE & PROCESS THEREOF"
Abstract The present invention relates to synthesis of a new aluminosilicate zeolite identified as USM-4.This new zeolite has the BPH morphology and is structurally related to zeolite Q. UZM-4 has an empirical formula of Mmn+Rrp+Al!.KExSiyO2 where M is an alkali or alkaline earth metal ion, R can be quaternary ammonium ion and E can be galluim, iron, boron, chromium, indium and mixtures thereof. The Si/AI ratio can range from 1.5 to 4.0. The zeolite can be used in various hydrocarbon conversion processes such as isomerization of alkylaromatic hydrocarbons.

As will be shown in detail in the examples, the UZM-4 material is thermally stable up to a temperature of at least 400°C and preferably up to 600°C. The UZM-4 material has also been found to have a smaller unit cell size than zeolite Q, indicative of a higher Si/AI ratio. That is, a representative UZM-4 has a unit cell of a=13.269 Å, c= 13.209 Å, versus a unit cell for zeolite Q of a=13.501 Å and c=13.403Å..
As synthesized, the UZM-4 material will contain some of the exchangeable or charge balancing cations in its pores. These exchangeable cations can be exchanged for other cations, or in the case of organic cations, they can be removed by heating under controlled conditions. Because UZM-4 is a large pore zeolite, it is also possible to remove some organic cations directly by ion exchange.
