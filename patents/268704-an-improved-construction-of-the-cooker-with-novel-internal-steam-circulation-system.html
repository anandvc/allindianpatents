The following Specification particularly describes and ascertains the nature of this invention and the manner in which it is to be performed:

This invention is about the "cooker in which food is cooked. This cooker has the novel system of circulation of steam. The steam is circulated by internal circulation.
The- novel Construction involves plurality of vessels or containers, one-above-the-other, as is seen in normal., vertical tiffin carrier, except the change, that is novelty, in our cooker, the novelty being a small metallic tube fitted inside each vessel except the lowest vessel at the bottom
For each vessel, the bottom-end or lower-end of die tube is attached in "flush" mode, & the lower-end of the tube is "coning-out‚Äù from the bottom of the vessel. The top-end or the upper-end of the tube is exposed or opened within that vessel itself at & near the top-end-rim-of-the-vessel. The tube can be positioned vertical or inclined. A single tube in a vessel serves the purpose, but, optionally, plurality of the tubes in each vessel can also be put.
This system helps to circulate the steam uniformly inside & through all the vessels. The heat-transfer is bettered. The heating & the cooking of the stuff that is substances inside the vessels is better, quicker, uniform, near-perfect.
The Cooker is to be mounted on top of chulhhaa, kerosene stove, LPG stove, electrical device & the like. The cooker can also be placed at the focal point of Parabolic Solar Cooker.
The Cooker consumes lesser energy The cost is less.
