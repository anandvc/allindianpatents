Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF PREPARING PHYSIOLOGICALLY ACTIVE EXTRACT USEFUL AS A FOLIAR APPLICATION ON PLANTS FROM BLUE GREEN ALGAE AND SEA-WEEDS
Abstract A process of preparing physiologically active extract useful as a foliar application on plants from blue green algae and sea-weeds have been devised which finds use as a stimulant for growth and yield of agricultural products. The stimulant formed is of natural origin and can substitute the synthetic or organic based stimulants, which retains the residual toxins adversely affecting the overall exports of horticulture crops and vegetables.
