Title of Invention

IMIDAZOQUINOLINES COMPOUNDS
Abstract Imidazoquinoline and retrahydroimidazoquinoline compounds that contain thioethr functionality at the 1 - position are useful as immune response modifiers. The compounds and compositions of the invention can induce the biosynthesis of various cytokines and are useful in the treatment of a variery of conditions including viral diseases and neoplastic diseases.
