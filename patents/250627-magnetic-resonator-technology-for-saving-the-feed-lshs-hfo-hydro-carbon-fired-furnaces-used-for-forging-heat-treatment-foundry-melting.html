Title of Invention

MAGNETIC RESONATOR TECHNOLOGY FOR SAVING THE FEED LSHS/HFO (HYDRO-CARBON) FIRED FURNACES USED FOR FORGING/HEAT TREATMENT/FOUNDRY MELTING.
Abstract A brief description of drawing, there are two types of magnetic resonators to be installed on the individual fuel line leading to the burners. 2. As per the designed requirement, Double resonance Type already available repulsive pair was chosen with the following dimensions. It consists of two halves having the Strontium ferrite magnetic composition and housed in a thermo plastic container. Length x Breadth x Height is 80 mm x 26 mm x 20 mm as per FIG Al and it consists of two halves. The two halves are clamped as shown in the respective to the pipe line.
