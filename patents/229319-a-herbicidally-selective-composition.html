Title of Invention

A HERBICIDALLY SELECTIVE COMPOSITION
Abstract This invention relates to a herbicidal composition that, in addition to comprising customary inert formulation adjuvants, comprises: a) a compound of formula (I), wherein the substituents are as defined in claim 1; and b) a synergistically effective amount of one or more compounds of formulae (2.1 to 2.51). The compositions according to the invention may also comprise a safener.
