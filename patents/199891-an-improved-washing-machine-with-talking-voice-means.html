Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED WASHING MACHINE WITH TALKING VOICE MEANS
Abstract This invention relates to an improved washing machine with talking voice means. The machine comprises an outer tub disposed in an outer casing, a spin basket for spinning clothes, a pulsator for pulsating action during washing of clothes, a motor for rotating the means supporting the outer casing, a top cover to close the open end of the machine and talking voice means. The talking voice means comprises a main controller which receives signals from the different sensors secured to different components such as drain motor, safety valve, motor, etc. The main controller processes the signals and feeds to a sound box having a memory chip, a decoder to convert voice signals to talking signal, a speaker and a power cercuit.
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to an improved washing machine with talking voice means.
PRIOR ART
Washing means, whether it be front or top loading, have video and audio means for providing a signal in the event of an error or fault in the operation of the machine. Thus, and as by way of example, if the lid of the machine is open though it is intended to be in a closed status, means are provided to provide a visual and audio signal of the presence of such an error. The visual signal means normally comprises an error detection circuit connected to visual means, such as light emitting diodes and to audio signal means such as buzzer.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose an improved washing machine having talking
voice signal means to provide talking instructions of the presence of an error.
Another object of this invention is to propose a washing machine having talking voice signal means to direct an operator for the operations of the washing machine.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a washing machine which is efficient.
Further objects and advantages of this invention will be more apparent from the ensuing description.
BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided an improved washing machine with talking voice means comprising:
(a) an outer tub disposed in an outer casing of the washing machine;
(b) spin basket disposed in the said outer tub which is adapted to provide spin action to the clothes during the washing operation of machine;
(c) a pulsator provided at the bottom of said spin basket which is adapted to provide pulsating action during washing of clothes;
(d) a motor provided at the bottom end of said outer casing which is adapted to rotate the mounting means which support the said outer casing;
(e) a top cover assembly provided at the top end of the said outer casing which is adapted to cover the open end thereof;
charcterised in that:-
(f) talking voice means are provided which comprise:-
a main controller adapted to receive signals from the sensors secured with different components such as drain motor, feed valve, safety switch, motor pressure sensor; a circuit means to receive the signals and to process and feed the same to a sound box comprising a memory chip connected to a decoder which is adapted to convert voice signals into the talking signals which are fed to a speaker through an amplifier; a power circuit to energise the circuit of the said sound box.
Specifically, in accordance with this invention, the washing machine has a voice circuit which is activated in the event of an error present in the operation of the machine. Thus, as by way of example if the cover or door of the machine is in an open status when intended to be in a closed status, such an error is detected by the door sensor which activates the first and second circuit and the talking voice circuit. Reference is made herein only to the door or cover sensor. However, a plurality of such sensors are provided to detect various errors which then activate its respective voice circuit.
Besides, the error indication the talking voice circuit is provided to render directions in the form of talking voice for operation of the machine. Thus, and by way of example, if the door is to be closed, the voice circuit for directions is activated to provide talking directions "please close the lid".
In accordance with another embodiment audio/visual display means are also provided to provided an audio/visual display of the error simultaneously or separately.
