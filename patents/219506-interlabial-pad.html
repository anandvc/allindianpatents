Title of Invention

"INTERLABIAL PAD"
Abstract An interlabial pad capable of being closely mounted between the labia, having high adhesion to the body of the user and usable irrespective of differences in labial shape among users, and constructed so that it can be mounted reliably and sanitarily. The body-side surface of an interlabial pad (1) is provided with a long convex region (3) formed by folding an absorption layer sheet (2) so that it can be interlabially mounted, while the opposite-to-body-side surface of the interlabial pad has a ready-made minisheet (14) attached thereto to control the spreading of the folded portion of the absorption layer sheet (2), so as to make it possible to insert a finger into a space defined between the ready-made minisheet (14) and a back sheet (12).
