Title of Invention

''SELECTION OF INFORMATION UNITS FOR MOBILE CLIENT COMPUTERS''
Abstract Facility for selecting information units for a mobile client computer, comprising a server computer having means for selecting information units by preset criteria, characterized in that, the present criteria have geographical locational information in the form of a geographical longitude and latitude, each information unit has geographical locational information in the form of a geographical longitude and latitude assigned to it, and this locational information and a geographical position for the client computer, in the form of a geographical longitude and latitude, are input variables for the selecting means.

The database is generated automatically, semi-manually or only manually on the basis of websites and other information about the facilities. If websites do not exhibit standardised geographic location information, some manual work is essential. Such work is carried out, for example, by a specialised information service and financed by the relevant facilities and/or by charging for user enquiries.
The server 3 transmits to the client 4 brief descriptions of one or more facilities found together advantageously with hyperlinks, by means of which the client 4 may directly retrieve information about the facilities found.
Such information relates, for example, to prices, timetables, programmes, menus, wine lists, occupancy or quality of the facility. In another variant of the invention, criteria relating to this information may be set by the user and already taken into account during the search for facilities. For example, a user searching for restaurants may stipulate that a table for ten must be free. If a restaurant is found which fits the criteria, the table is reserved via the restaurant's website and for example the wine brought to room temperature. If the user so wishes, the server 3 may transmit to the client 4 a plan of the client's surrounding area, on which the restaurant is marked.
