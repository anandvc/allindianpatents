STRAIGHT SHACKLE TWIN KEY PADLOCK
This invention relates to the Twin Key Padlock and more particularly Straight Shackle Twin Key Padlock.
This invention relates, to two key operated padlock having different combinations and specifically used for Access Control. Heretofore , it was known to make padlock having single key which, was operated by a single person for locking and unlocking. Due to the single key / person control, these locks are susceptible for key duplication and to avoid this usually demands for very high technology , this has made the inventor to split the control / restrict the access to open lock by single person. This will enhance the security of the lock irrespective of the lock technology. This is a very useful feature , generally required in banks (safe deposit vaults), post offices, government offices, hotels , shops, departmental stores, joint custodian areas where the permission of the supervisor / common controller is required in addition with the individual to open the lock.
In the existing single actuated key locks , there is also a possibility of key loss or key not returned by the customer in case at the hotel ,. wherein the whole lock is required to be replaced to avoid the access to unknown person or to the old customer. This has made the inventor to invent a lock, which can be rekeyed and will avoid replacement of the whole lock as well as access with old key. The same invention can

be offered with single key system as an another embodiment of the invention for normal application were the security requirement is not very high.
Now in the present invention there is provided dual key operating system , which does not allow any person to operate the lock with single key. The lock is to be operated with two keys simultaneously by two different persons having agreement on opening the lock with each other, thereby providing the access control and security feature of dual control•
Accordingly it is an object of the invention to provide a split control / restricts the.access to padlock opening , which will enhance tUe padlock security and avoid the key duplication by a single person.
It is further object of the invention to provide a lock with rekeying facility in which the same lock will be rekeyed to avoid the access by a person with old key.
It is still further object of the present invention is to provide a padlock which has a higher safety and security as compared with the known padlock and at the same time is of simple construction and ease to manufacture.
It is still further object of the invention is to provide a straight shackle fully enclosed inside the body thereby making it difficult to cut by a hack - saw and to open by a crow bar or similar object.

It is another object of the invention to provide a lock with two key operating system for requirements of Access Control or to avoid the opening of the lock by a single person with single key.
