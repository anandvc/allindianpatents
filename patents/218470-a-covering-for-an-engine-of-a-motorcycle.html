Title of Invention

A COVERING FOR AN ENGINE OF A MOTORCYCLE
Abstract A COVERING FOR AN ENGINE OF A MOTORCYCLE A covering for an engine of a motorcycle comprising, dummy covers (42A, 43A; 42B, 43B) for covering at least a part of cylinder heads (34A, 34B) from the sides, and overhead covers (64A, 65A, 64b, 65B) for covering at least part of head covers (35A, 35B) coupled to said cylinder heads (34A, 34B) from above and the sides, characterized in that, at least one spot of said dummy covers (42A, 43A, 42B, 43B) is fastened to an engine main unit (32) including said cylinder heads (34A, 34B) and head covers (35A, 35B) with bolts (49, 60), and a plurality of engaging protrusions (66, 67, 70, 71) provided in said overhead covers (64A, 65A; 64B, 65B) formed to cover said bolts (49, 60) are resiliently engaged with said engine main unit (32) or said dummy covers (42A, 43A, 42B, 43B) so as to detachable or attachable.

The present invention relates to an engine for motorcycle.
[DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION]
[0001]
[Technical Field of the Invention]
The present invention relates to an engine for a motorcycle having dummy covers for covering at least part of cylinder heads from the sides and overhead covers for covering at least part of head covers coupled to said cylinder heads from above and the sides.
[0002]
[Prior Art]
Japanese Published Unexamined Patent Application No. 2000-234562 discloses a construction in which at least part of head covers is covered by overhead covers from above and the sides in order to enhance the functional beauty of an engine for a motorcycle and to improve noiseproofing. Japanese Published Unexamined Patent Application No. Hei 11-82051 discloses a construction in which at least part of cylinder heads is covered by dummy covers having dummy cooling fins from the sides in order to enhance the functional beauty of an engine for a motorcycle. [0003]
2

[Problems to be Solved by the Invention]
In the construction disclosed in Japanese Published Unexamined Patent Application No. 2000-234562, protrusions provided in the head covers are engaged with the overhead covers, the overhead covers are fastened to the head covers with bolts, and the bolt heads are arranged to face the outer surface of the overhead covers . The bolts are seen from outside . To enhance the appearance functional beauty, bolt heads of a special shape must be employed and an expensive surface treatment with chromeplating must be done to the bolt heads.
[0004]
In the construction disclosed in Japanese Published Unexamined Patent Application No. Hei 11-82051, the dummy covers are attached to stays mounted on the cylinder heads in snap fit and are fastened to the stays and a crankcase with bolts in order to increase vibration endurance. The bolt heads are exposed to outside. There may arise the same problem as Japanese Published Unexamined Patent Application No. 2000-234562.
[0005]
The present invention has been made in view of such circumstances and an object of the present invention is to provide an engine for a motorcycle which requires no parts of a special shape and no surface treatment and enhances the functional beauty.
[0006]
[Means to Solve the Problems]
To achieve the foregoing object, in the invention

aWording to claim 1, an engine for a motorcycle having dummy covers for covering at least part of cylinder heads from the sides; and overhead covers for covering at least part of head covers coupled to the cylinder heads from above and the sides, wherein: at least one spot of the dummy covers is fastened to an engine main unit including the cylinder heads and head covers with bolts, and a plurality of engaging protrusions provided in the overhead covers formed in a shape to cover the bolts are resiliently engaged with the engine main unit or the dummy covers so as to be detachable or attachable. Such a construction of the invention according to claim 1 can require no parts of a special shape and no surface treatment and enhance the functional beauty. In other words, in many cases, the dummy covers for covering at least part of the cylinder heads from the sides are provided with dummy cooling fins for enhancing the functional beauty and are made of metal to eliminate heat and enhance the functional beauty. Strong support is thus necessary. Also in many cases, the overhead covers require no dummy cooling fins and are formed of lightweight material such as synthetic resin since requirements to eliminate heat and to enhance the functional beauty are relatively low. It is thus unnecessary to support the overhead covers very strongly. At least one spot of the dummy covers is fastened to the engine main unit and a plurality of engaging protrusions provided in the overhead covers are resiliently engaged with the engine main unit or the dummy covers so as to be detachable or attachable.
