Title of Invention

MODULAR APPARATUS FOR THE PRODUCTION OF MOLTEN METAL
Abstract modular apparatus for the production of molten metal by self reduction of agglomerates of metal oxide or of prereduced metal, which may be iron. The apparatus is typically a shaft furnace of modular construction and includes a plurality of connected cells of identical size and construction. Each apparatus is connected to equipment for supplying the agglomerates for reduction or melting and refining within a reduction chamber or melting chamber, respectively, of each cell.
