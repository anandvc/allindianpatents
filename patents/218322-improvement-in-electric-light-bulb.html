Title of Invention

IMPROVEMENT IN ELECTRIC LIGHT BULB
Abstract An improvement in or relating to electric light bulb which consist of two filaments, a selector switch, a sealed glass envelope and a base fixed to the glass envelope in the same manner as a conventional electric light bulb. The two filaments work independently of each other and one at a time When the selector switch is electrically connected to the phase terminal of the bulb then the first filament will be shorted and the second filament will give service and this filament will stay in service until it is damaged or taken out of selection When we want to use the other filament then the selector switch is electrically connected to the neutral terminal of the bulb then the second filament will be shorted and the first filament will give service.
Full Text BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
1. Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to the improvement in electric Light bulb wherein the novel inventions include two filaments and one selector switch; each filament works independently of the other and one at a time; Any of the two filament can be selected with the help of the selector switch and after the chosen filament completes its life, is burnt, fused or damaged, the other filament is selected through the selector switch; in this way life of a single electric bulb is doubled at almost the same cost as that of die single filament bulb.
2. Description of the Related Art
hi general lighting electric bulbs available in the market consist of a single filament and when this filament completes its life, is burnt, fused or damaged then the whole bulb is replaced instead of changing the filament thereby increasing the cost. In rural areas people have to travel large distances in order to buy an electric bulb, whereas in urban areas due to voltage fluctuations the electric bulb gets damaged, burnt or fused.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
To solve the above-described problems, it is an objective of the present invention to make the present invention more advantageous and technically sound than the conventional electric bulb and the defect has been removed by adding two filaments in place of one

filament in the electric bulb assembly. The two filament electric light bulb includes a sealed glass envelope and a base fixed to the glass envelope in the same manner as a conventional electric light bulb. The two filament electric light bulb is lightable one filament at a time. A filament is selected with the help of a selector switch. A selector switch is a simple mechanism made up of brass sheet folded into T-shape and revetted to a brass metal base. T- shaped switch is slightly bent into a bow to give it a spring action so that the contact with the phase or neutral terminal of the bulb is firm. The selector switch can be swung through a small angle as the revetting point acts as a pivot. By moving the selector switch left or right, the switch can be electrically connected to phase or neutral terminal of the bulb.

As described above, the electric light bulb with two filaments and a selector switch according to the present invention has the following advantages
First, the life cycle of the electric light bulb is doubled due to the presence of two filaments and the price of the bulb is almost the same
Secondly, in rural part of the country where somehow power has reached but the poor masses can not afford to buy a light bulb comfortably, bulbs with double the life and with price almost the same as the conventional bulbs, shall be of reasonable relief
Thirdly, even in urban areas and industrial areas lightning bulbs are often damaged due to fluctuations in the power supply voltage and a reserve bulb may not be in stock at that time so by using an electric light bulb with two filaments and a selector switch this problem can be solved
