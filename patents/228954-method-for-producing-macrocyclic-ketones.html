Title of Invention

METHOD FOR PRODUCING MACROCYCLIC KETONES
Abstract The present invention relates to a novel thermo-isomerization method for rapidly and simply producing macrocyclic ketones of the formula la or lb. The macrocyclic ketones are prepared in the gas phase at temperatures above 500°C rapidly and in a simple manner from alcohols of the formula IIa and IIb directly with a high yield. In the formulae la, lb, Ila and lib, Rl - R5, and n have the meanings given in the description.
