Title of Invention

A TIME INDICATING DEVICE HAVING A INNER MECHANISM WHICH RESULTS IN THE HANDS TO ROTATE POINTING TOWARDS CENTER OF THE DIAL FROM THE CIRCUMFERENCE
Abstract ABSTRACT: - This invention relates to Time Indicating Device. In the existing known technology, the Seconds Hand, Minuits Hand, Hours Hand of the time indicating device are pointing towards the circumference of the dial, where as in the present invention the hands of the time indicating device of the .present invention are rotating in a different manner. Like the Seconds, Minuits, Hours hands are rotating along the circumference of the dial and are pointing towards the centre of the dial. In order to achieve the above objects, some changes are made to the inner mechanism, that is to the Seconds wheel. Seconds Hand, Minuits Hand, Hours Hand and to the dial of the existing known technology. And also introduced a thin tempered metal rod. The Seconds Hand, Minuits Hand, Hours Hand of the present invention(time indicating device) are of C-shaped and there is no hole at the centre of the dial of the present invention.
Full Text

This invention relates to Time indicating device. A main object of the present invention is to make the rotation of hands of the Time indicating device in a different manner, like the hands are pointing towards the center of the Dial (as shown in fig-4).
In order to achieve the above objects some changes are made to the seconds wheel, Seconds hand, Minuit Hand, Hours Hand and to the Dial of the existing known technology. And also introduced a thin tempered metal rod.
The total number of wheels and the number of teeth of each wheel are as it is in the existing technology.
