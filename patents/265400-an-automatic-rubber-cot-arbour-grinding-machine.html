Title of Invention

AN AUTOMATIC RUBBER COT ARBOUR GRINDING MACHINE
Abstract An automatic rubber cot arbour grinding / buffing machine comprising an unfinished arbour storage unit / loading machine for safe storing of a large number of unfinished arbour in minimum floor space, an unifinished arbour conveyer unit provided adjacent to the said loading magazine for taking the arbour from the said loading magazine and smooth conveying to a measuring unit, or a carriage one by one, a rubber cot diameter measuring unit, provided adjacent to the said unfinished arbour conveyer unit, for measuring the cot diameter one by one and comparing with the predetermined set diameter, as preprogrammed for rejecting of the arbours, not suitable for grinding in the rejection tray, a loading – unloading carriage provided adjacent to the said measuring unit, for loading unfinished arbours to an arbour holder unit for grinding and simultaneously unloading finished arbour after grinding into a finished arbour conveyer unit, a center less rubber cot arbour holding and guiding the device provided substantially perpendicularly to the said arbour loading – unloading carriage, for one by one holding and precisely guiding the unfinished arbour for grinding and pushing out the finished arbour after grinding, on to the said carriage for unloading, a grinding wheel head unit, provided adjacent to the said arbour holder unit, movable along X-axis and Y-axis for grinding / buffing the rubber cot arbours as per presetting of fix diameter or fix cut, a dressing tool unit, provided adjacent to the said grinding wheelhead unit, for dressing the grinding wheel after grinding whel after grinding a predetermined number of arbours, a finished arbour conveyer unit, provided adjacent to the said carriage on its unloading end for smoothly conveying the finished arbour one by one to a finished arbour storage unit / unloading magazine provided adjacent to the finished arbur storage unit and adjacent and parallel to the said unfinished arbour storage unit / loading magazine. A control panel for controlling all the operations of arbour grinding machine in a sequence as per desired program, all the units being mounted on a main frame and enclosed in a body having suitable doors, windows and covers.

The following specification describes the invention:

2
This invention relates to an automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine.
More particularly, this invention relates to an automatic rubber cot grinding machine in which the finished/unfinished rubber cot arbour, storage magazines are provided to form a compact 'U' shape path. The unit conveyor system is provided for conveying the rubber cot arbour to the machine system which is first measures the desired diameter of the rubber cot, and if rejected, is removed without feeding to the grinding machine head. When found suitable for grinding I is carried to the grinding head thereby, continuing the measuring as well as the grinding operation simultaneously for increasing the production rate.
In a prior art rubber cot arbour grinding machine measuring the rubber cot diameter is done manually and loading and unloading is also carried out manually, which operation is prone to accident. Also stone dress is manually resulting into time consuming process leading to a very low production.
An automatic rubber cot armour grinding machine is also known in the art, but then the machine is also having a very low storage capacity. Unfinished rubber cot arbours are stored on one side of the machine towards the grinding head unit and the finished rubber cot arbour storage magazine are provided to the other end of the machine thereby, making long path for loading and unloading the rubber cot arbours. The floor space required for the machine is comparatively very low. Various operations are quite complicated i.e. the arbour is lifted by robot type device which first picks the arbour and then feeds it to the grinding head through the diameter of the rubber cot machine and if the diameter is not found suitable, the arbour is rejected at the robot arm turning away and releasing the rejected arbour in the tray and then picking another arbour repeating the same process. During all these process the actual time for grinding operation remains unperformed, as such the production of the machine is very low.
In the prior art machine various process steps are not sequentially performed and the system is quite complicated. The machine is not having ergonomic design concept and is not suitable for easy maintenance, not easy for operation, visibility of the operator is very poor. It is having fixed type of operation panel and the machine is not compact. Further, in the grinding head unit, the job of holding the unit is traversing, while grinding the head remains stationery, which also a complicated system.

The main object of this invention is to provide automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine, which is quite compact having ergonomic design concept, is having large storage capacity for unfinished as well as finished rubber cot arbours.
The rubber cot arbour path is in 'U' shape and quite compact. Cot diameter
measuring is carried out before feeding the arbour to the grinding head and the grinding as well as the measuring operations are carried simultaneously thereby, providing sequential operation resulting into increased production of the machine. An automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine mainly comprises of a storage magazine for storing large quantity of unfinished rubber cot arbours in
number of inclined racks mounted vertically one above the other, which stores very
large number of arbours and feeds to the conveyer which moves vertically, picking arbours in the hooks prided in the conveyor and which are fed one by one to the cod diameter measuring unit mounted on the carriage. The said carriage is provided to the T bracket arbour holding device which feeds the arbour to the grinding head and after grinding it is transferred to another conveyor and from i9t it is fed to the finished arbour storage magazine. Holding operations are made fully automatic with the help of a CPO, through the control panel. The measuring unit will measure the diameter, which is fed to the servo motor of the centerless unit through the CPU. The grinding head is set either according to the fixed diameter or according to the fixed cot for grinding. Measuring system may be provided before grinding and/or after grinding.
