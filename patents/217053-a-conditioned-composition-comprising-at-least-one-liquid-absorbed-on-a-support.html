Title of Invention

A CONDITIONED COMPOSITION COMPRISING AT LEAST ONE LIQUID ABSORBED ON A SUPPORT
Abstract The present invention relates to a conditioned composition comprising at least one liquid absorbed on a support containing a precipitated silica, characterized in that the said silica is in the form of substantially spherical beads and has: - an average bead size in excess of 150 µm, - a packed filling density (PFD) in excess of 0.29, - a 75 µm screen oversize of at least 92% by weight, - an oil absorption value (DOP) of at least 250 ml/100 g, wherein the said composition has a liquid content of at least 50% by weight, in particular between 50 and 65% by weight.
