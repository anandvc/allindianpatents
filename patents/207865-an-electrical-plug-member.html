AN ELECTRICAL PLUG MEMBER
The invention relates to an electrical plug member. More particularly, the invention relates to an electrical plug made from mouldable plastic material having a flap member which is detachably fixed with the projections of flap member with grooves provided in body portion by which the body portion is fixed with the panel so as to conceal the fixing screw.
The conventional installation of electrical plug are individually fixed with the panel and the base screws are visible. Today's interior decorations has changed the concept all together and wants to change frequently the door panel thereby there is need to develop electrical plug which will conceal those visualable screw and to make the product more attractive by means of colourful flap to match with the panel.
The conventional plugs are single piece having at least two holes for fasterung the screws with the panel/switch board. The plug member made of plastic having grooves at least two for installing the brass pieces from the back. Since the screws are visible there is a chance for getting shock due to manipulation of the plug member. Usually they are of mono colour.
In the modern days the inventor wants to conceal these visible screws by means of colourful flap members by means of grooves & projections or any interlocking means with the body of the plug. This not or\ly avoids accidental shock but also changes the look and the colour as required with the switch board or panel for a better aesthetic look.
The primary object of the invention is to provide inter changeable flap of any color to suit the panel.

The another object of the invention is to conceal the fixing screw to avoid shock at the corners of the body.
Accordingly there is provided an improved electrical plug member (1) comprising a front flat surface (2) with or without flanges having projections (3) on the edges of the flat surface; said flat surface is pressed inside the groove (4) of the body portion (5) having two opposing holes (6) for fastening the screws with the door panel; said body is provided with grooves (7) in the inner flange portion so as to push fit the front surface into the grooved body portion; said front portion may be of any colour for aesthetic look with matching colour with panel thereby connecting the screw members; the said plug member is having at least two holes (8) in the body portion on the projected central portion of body member is having at least two holes (8) in the body portion on the projected central portion of body member flash with the front surface when press fit.
