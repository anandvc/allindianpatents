Title of Invention

DYNAMIC SCOLIOSIS BRACE FOR SCOLIOTIC CURVES IN THE SPINE
Abstract The invention in general relates to a Dynamic Scoliosis Brace for the scoliotic curves in the spine. The DYNAMIC SCOLIOSIS BRACE" is an adjustable 'tailor-made' brace for daily wear under the clothes for non surgical treatment of scoliosis with dynamic correction achieved on a day to day basis by simple manipulation of the device. It is made of 4/2 pieces of high density polypropylene sheets as per individual moulds and divided into 2 components for easy wearing and maneuverability. The pieces are interconnected with threaded rods moving on slotted fixtures both at the anterior and posterior.
