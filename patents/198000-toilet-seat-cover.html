Full Text The present invention relates to a toilet seat cover used as a hygienic and disease preventing disposable device for the European model closets, Combination closets and wallvit pan model closets.
In public toilets, hotels, lodges, trains, hospitals and wherever European and wallvit pan model closets are used, one has to sit on the same seat others had used. Even though it is loathsome and unhygienic there is no other alternative. This may lead to a chance of getting infectious diseases such as skin diseases, venereal diseases and even AIDS. This invention relates to a device which prevents and protects from such diseases.
This simple cheap and disposable device which is very easy for installation, gives adequate protection from all above problems,
This kind of protector toilet seat cover generally made of materials like paper, Cellulose, Cotton, Rayon, Plastic or thin metallic foils or combination of materials like plastic coated paper or metallic coated paper. Generally biodegradable material like paper is preferred.
This protector toilet seat cover is placed over the toilet seat in order to avoid direct contact of the anatomy of the user w/ith the toilet seat or to the back of the toilet lid and thereby prevent the chance of getting any diseases described earlier.
The size, shape and design of the protector toilet seat cover make it suitable for most of the normally used closet seats.
