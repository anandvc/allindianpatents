Title of Invention

A NOVEL DENTAL AND GUM CARE HERBAL COMPOSITION
Abstract The present invention discloses a novel dental and Gum care herbal composition , comprising Dried powders or Aq. Extracts of flower of Nelumbo Nucifera, wood of Santalum Album, seeds of Piper Longum, roots of Zingiber Officinale, plant of Symplocos Race Mosa, fruit of Operculina Terpethum, wood of Pterocarpus Santalinus, Root of Saussurea Lappa, plant of Anacyclus Pyrethrum, fruit of Emblica Officinalis, Seeds of Brassica Campastris,froth of sea Water,fruits of Emblica Officinalis+Terminaliachebula + Terminalia Bellirca (all in equal amount )mixed all thoroughly in equal quantity and optionally mixing with one or more auxiliary substances selected from CaCo3, Precipitated Chalk, bathing soap, glycerin, menthol, Thymol, and Gum in a ratio of 0.5:1:1:0.5:0.5:.005:.01:.005:.05 respectively .
Full Text Field of the invention
This invention relates to novel dental and gum care herbal composition and is useful in treatment and prevention of Pyorrhea, improvement of general gum and teeth conditions along with prevention and treatment of various other diseases. The composition of the present invention is generally used in the form of toothpaste or tooth powders.
Background & Prior Art for the invention
Various compositions in the form of tooth powders and pastes, such as G-32 Tab AlarSin,
Pyorrhea Dental care powder, dental care powder produced by Dabur,, Pyorrhea
produced by GuruKul Kangri, Pyro Dental powder produced by Jwala Ayurveda and
tooth pastes like Forhans, Colgate, Binaca, Neem, Babool, Pepsodent etc. are available in
the market.
All the abovesaid toothpastes and powders are very effective in cleaning of teeth and
making them free of germs. However none of them can treat permanently the pyorrheated
gums. The doctors suggest repeated brushing of the teeth during infected conditions
which may sometimes result wakening or loosening of the gums.
Various ayurvedic and allopathic toothpastes and toothpowders are also available for
treatment of the infected gums. However the side effects due to chemicals are inherent
problems which the body has to face..
In cases of Pyorrhea the patient either has to extract out whole set of teeth or has to take
treatment using antibiotics and pain killers and if not relieved the final treatment is the
extraction of full dentures..
The Ayurvedic toothpastes are also available but are not so effective which can
effectively and completely relieve or prevent the disease.
Objects of the present invention:
The object of the present invention is to provide a comprehensive and effective herbal composition with no side effects for care of the gums and teeth as well as for effective treatment of Pyorrhea. Further objective of the invention is to provide a herbal composition which can keep the teeth powerful and sparkling white. Still further objective of the invention is to provide a herbal composition which can restore the sparkling whiteness of the teeth which have got discolored due to some disease.

Statement of Synergism
The herbal composition of the present invention alone can be used for
the treatment of pyorrhea, discoloration of the tooth/teeth, Repairing of
the teeth damaged due to an accident ( No requirement of any
antibiotic), Strengthening of the gums & teeth in old age. This herbal
composition if used once a day on teeth it prevents discoloration and
pyorrhea of the teeth. In this way the composition of the present
invention is not a mere admixture but is synergistic composition and
having unexpected & enhanced sum total of the properties as compared
to the properties of the individual ingredients.
