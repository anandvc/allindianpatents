Title of Invention

SWITCH OPERATOR
Abstract In places, where we use electrical switches, we have the problem of, electrical shock when the switch is operated with wet hand or in wet condition. The problem is solved by adopting a new switch operator on the switch which will prevent the electrical shock. During the operation of switch, electrical spark is produced by the switch leads to gas (LPG) fire explosion in home or industries or where it is applicable. The new switch operator reduce the spark energy and hence the electrical fire or gas fire are prevented.
Full Text DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
The switch operator has novel features for operating the switches. The operator is essential for our modem life to protect ourself from electrical problems in home or where it is applicable. The conventional electrical switches don"t have any protection from the electrical shock when we operate it by wet hands or it is being in wet condition , this leads to the electrical shock. Moreover , the conventional switches producing electrical spark during the operation leads to the LPG fire or explosion in home. Hence the Switch Operator is introduced for avoiding those problems.
A switch operator comprising an operator (4), operator body (5), ON (1) and OFF (2) rods, and screw (3), The said switch operator preventing the electrical shock and electrical fire, when operated the switch by using wet hand and during the LPG leakage.

STRUCTURE OF SWITCH OPERATOR DESCRIBED AS FOLLOWS :
It looks like an electrical switch, but it does not have electrical points. It is named as switch operator (fig.l). In the (fig.l) the switch operator has two non-conducting rods (1 & 2) which helps to operate the switch. The non-conducting rods are acting as a switch ON and switch OFF rod.
The (fig.2) shows the existing electrical switch. The electrical switch has two electrical points (+ and - ). The (fig.3) shows that switch operator is fixed over the switch body (6). The operator body (5) cover the entire area of electrical switch. The operator (4) and operator body (5) made of no conducting materials and the operator and operator body are fixed by metal screw (3). The operator body (5) is fixed over the switch body (6) by pasting or screwing method. If we operate the operator by wet hand, the water will not reach the electrical switch hence the electrical shock is prevented.
During the function of the switch, it produce electrical sparks. This spark energy is reduced by the switch operator, this means that the spark enter into the large space between the switch and its operator, spark energy automatically fall down and spark temperature will be dropped to lower level. Hence the spark will not be able to produce electrical fire accidents.


I CLAIM

2. A switch operator as claimed in Claim 1 where in the operator rod (ON (1) and OFF (2)) is fixed with the operator.
3. A switch operator claimed in Claim 1 where in the operator is fixed with the operator body by metal screw (3)
4. A switch operator claimed in Claim 1 where in a non conducting operator body (5) is fixed over the switch body (6) by metal screw or
pasting method.
5. A switch operator as herein above described and illustrated with the reference to the accompanying drawings.
