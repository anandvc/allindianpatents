Title of Invention

AN AUTOMATIC DEVICE FOR SWITCHING OFF THE IGNITION SYSTEM OF AN AUTOMOBILE WHENEVER IT COMES TO A HALT OVER A CONTINUOUS PREDETERMINED PERIOD OF TIME
Abstract An automatic device for switching off the ignition system of an automobile whenever it comes to a halt over a continuous predetermined period of time, comprising a sensor for sensing the wheel movement of the automobile; a microcontroller based circuit connected on its input side, to the sensor and on its output side, to the ignition system of the automobile, for receiving, the output signal from the sensor, and switching off the ignition system whenever the sensor senses no wheel movement over a continuous predetermined interval of time; a throttle switch, provided for the throttle and connected to the input side of the microcontroller, said throttle switch being actuated, on opening the throttle, to cause the microcontroller to switch on the ignition system and activate the starter system to re-start the engine, a neutral switch connected to the gear system and to the starer system, the neutral switch disenabling the starter system from re-starting the engine unless, while opening the throttle, the gears are in neutral position, to enable the starter system to re-start the engine.

brought to a halt, with the engine running. When such

the ignition system and, thus, the engine, to save
fuel .
If the engine is to be re-started, a simple procedure

A safety feature of the said device is that re-starting
is made possible only when the vehicle gearing is in
neutral position; not otherwise. This feature ia to
guard against the possibility of the automobile

instance, when the automobile gears are in engaged
condition.
The said device can be de-activated, whenever
necessary, by a manual override switch which, when

operated, enables the automobile to work normally.
