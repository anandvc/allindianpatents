Title of Invention

A COATING COMPOSITION AND A COATED ABRASIVE
Abstract The present invention relates to a coating composition comprising an ultraviolet-polyrnerizable formulation and from 5 to 50% by volume of an aluminum trihydrate filler with a particle size from 1 to 10 micrometers, wherein said composition can allow a cure of greater thickness compared to a '" composition that is the same except that the filler is formed of calcium carbonate or silica. The present invention also relates to a coated abrasive .comprising a layer formed from the coating composition.
