Title of Invention

APPAPATUS TO DETECT ABNORMAL CELLS IN EPITHELIAL TISSUE OF THE BODY
Abstract A sample of an epithelial lesion which may be keratinized is disclosed in which an analytical system including an imaging system is provided to detect precancerous and cancerous cells. A transepithelial non-lacerational brush produces sufficient cells from all three layers of the epithelium so that an analytical system comprising a programmed computer can detect which cells exhibit abnormal keratinization and require further examination because of a likely suspicion of said pre-cancerous and cancerous conditions. The method and system can apply to the diagnosis of non-cancerous conditions as well.
