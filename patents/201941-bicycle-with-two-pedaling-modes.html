Title of Invention

BICYCLE WITH TWO PEDALING MODES
Abstract A bicycle with two pedaling modes used for increasing more pedaling interest comprises of a bicycle body(62), a rotating shaft tube (61),two cranks (7) and two pedals (8);the bicycle body has two handlebars(10) respectively disposed on right and left sides thereof,two wheels(11)respectively disposed on front and rear sides thereof and a seat disposed between the two wheels;the said two cranks respectively are connected to two ends of the rotating shaft structure and the two pedals respectively are connected to the two cranks;the rotating shaft tube has a rotating shaft structure(3)and a mode change apparatus(9);the mode change apparatus is used for changing pedaling modes.
