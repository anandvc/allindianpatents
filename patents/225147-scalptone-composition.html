Title of Invention

"SCALPTONE COMPOSITION"
Abstract Scalpton composition for a wider spectrum of symptoms of hair falling, dandruff, premature graying itching scalp, irritation and dryness of scalp comprising Acidum Fluoricum, Acidum Phosphoricum, Arsenicum Album, Kali Muriaticum, Kali Sulphuricum, Natrum Muriaticum, Phosphorus, Vinca Minor in equal proportion having potencies of items 1,2,3 & 6 to 8 ranging from 6 to 200 on centesimal scale and for items 4 & 5 ranging from 6 to 30 on centesimal scale wherein the ingredients are potentized in 90% alcohol or distilled water individually before mixing in equal proportion.
Full Text This invention relates to Scalptone composition in Homoeopathy.BACKGROUNDHead contains between 1,00,000 to 1,50,000 hairs of which 100 are shed;
everyday. When number of hair lost exceeds this, hair fall or thining of hair is noticed. Scalp dryness leads to various problems like, dandruff, hair dullness and ultimately hair loss.
The colour of the hair depends upon the pigment melanin present in the hair. Greying starts when melanogenic activity is reduced. Early greying is usually hereditary but cause of premature greying is not known in many cases. Various reasons can lead to loss of hair, like, rubbing or pulling hair, severe or sudden stress, serious illness, high fever, major surgery, thyroid and iron deficiency. Dandruff is a common condition in which the scalp is covered with small (lakes of dead skin. The ilakes come away when hair is brushed
or combed which represent an increase in the normal loss of outermost skin. Some type of dandruffs are accompanied by inflammation of scalp. If sebum produced by the sebacious gland is reduced, the hair becomes dry and brittle.
An Homoeopathic doctor will prescribe Phosphorus for a patient whose hair falls out in bunches (Alopecia) and for a person who has started losing hair after mental shock may be prescribed Acidum Phosphoricum. .In case, a patient has alopecia along with intense itching of scalp, Acid Fluoricum and
Vinca Minor may be prescribed together.The object of this invention is to simplify the prescription by doctor for a. large group of patients and to cover wider-spectrum symptoms in hair falling, dandruff, premature greying, itching of scalp, irritation,-dryness of scalp in a single Homoeopathic composition consisting of herb and chemical salts.
To achieve the said objective, this invention provides Scalptone composition for a wider spectrum of symptoms of hair falling, dandruff, premature greying, itching scalp, irritation and dryness of scalp which comprises.
