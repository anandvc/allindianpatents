Title of Invention

OPTICAL CONVERTER AND BINOCLE INCORPORATING THE SAME
Abstract In a optical converter, objects are focused simultaneously with object lenses being separate apart, and they created two figures of the object. One figure contains more of the right and other contains more of the left. This two figures of the object are focused simultaneously with the screen lenses being separate apart. The intersected figures as seen on the screen. Thus the over all effect of the figures as seen on the screen through monovision is three dimension.

ACCORDING TO KNOWN
MONOVISION : Monovision is nothing but 2 D. A Photograph represents a two dimensional recording of a three dimensional scene, what is recorded is the intensity distribution that prevailed at the plane Qf the photograph, when it was exposed. The light sensitive medium is sensitive. Only to the intensity variations and hence while recording a photograph, the phase distribution which prevailed at the plane of the photograph is lost. Since only the intensity pattern has been recorded, the three dimensional character of the object scence is lost.
Monovision device is nothing but 2D Camera. A photographic camera consists of a light - tight box - light can enter the box only through a small opening in front of the box. A convex lens is mounted on the front surface and a photo of the lens from the film can be altered. The leng system forms a real inverted image of the objects placed in front of the camera, on the film.
STEREOVISION : Stereovision is nothing but 3D. Three dimensions relates to length, width and; height that any solid object has, hence the 3D Photograph represents amplitude and phase distribution at any place.
Stereovision device is nothing but Stereoscope Camera. In a stereoscope camera two photographs of the object are taken simultaneously with the two lenses being separate apart. One picture contains more of the right and the other contains more of the left when two photograph's are fixed in a stereoscope and viewed with both the eyes with the help of lenses fixed in the stereoscope, a three dimensional picture is seen.
A man who is living with single eye, he cannot able to know the effect of stereovision.
The present invention gives us optical converter which is used for converting the stereovision into monovision.
In a optical converter, objects are focused simultaneously with convex lenses being separate a part, and they created two figures of the object. One figure contains more of the right and other contains more of the left. This two figures of the object are focused simultaneously with the convex lenses being separate apart. The intersected figures of object as seen on the screen. Thus the over all effect of the figures as seen on the screen through monovision is three dimension."
