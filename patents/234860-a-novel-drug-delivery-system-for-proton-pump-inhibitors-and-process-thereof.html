Title of Invention

A NOVEL DRUG DELIVERY SYSTEM FOR PROTON PUMP INHIBITORS AND PROCESS THEREOF
Abstract The present invention discloses a novel drug delivery system for proton pump inhibitors comprising benzimidazole compounds or their salts,preferably Rabeprazole or its and pharmaceutically acceptable excipients in powder form which is reconstitutable ina parenterally acceptable solvent to form an injectable solution.
