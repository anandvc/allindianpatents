Title of Invention

PROJECTOR FOR MICROSCOPIC SLIDE
Abstract The present invention is disclosed to solve the aforementioned problems and its object of the present invention to provide lightweight Microscopic Slide Projector and so the easy mobility of this projector can be possible. Another object of the present invention is to provide a cost-effective device, which is useful for the students and teachers in schools, colleges even universities. Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a device, which is useful in field. It is another object of the present invention is to provide powerful magnifier attached with this projector as compared to the conventional light microscope. Another object of the present invention is to provide a device by which microscopic slide can be projected directly on the screen. In accordance with another object of the present invention, there is provided Microscopic Slide Projector, by which microscopic slide projection can be done using an electrical lamp as a light source in night and as well as by sun light in day. Further object are to achive the above with the Microscopic Slide Projector which is compact, light weight, simple, safe, durable, efficient, versatile, eye strain free, inexpensive and easy to manufacture, maintain and use.
Full Text TITLE
MICROSCOPIC SLIDE PROJECTOR
FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to improvements in light microscopes which
needed essentially for biological/microscopic examination and observation for
students and teachers during practical work. It has been noticed that simply for
observing a microscopic specimen under the microscope, not only much time is
spent but also a number of microscopes my be needed for the satisfaction and
benefit of a group of students in the study room at the same time naturally the
overall cost will be increased. More particularly it is a Microscopic Slide
Projector with the powerful magnification than the conventional light
microscope along with a fine adjustment which may be readily operated, and
which is of simple construction, easy of manufacture, capable of being readily
assembled and optically strain free. By this Projector microscopic slide can be
projected directly on the screen
