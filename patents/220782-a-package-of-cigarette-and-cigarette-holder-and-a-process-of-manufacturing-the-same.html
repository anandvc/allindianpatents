Title of Invention

"A PACKAGE OF CIGARETTE AND CIGARETTE HOLDER AND A PROCESS OF MANUFACTURING THE SAME"
Abstract The present invention relates to a composite and integral cigarette cum cigarette holder packet as well as convenient and a continuous process for packaging cigarette cum cigarette holder. The invention provides for a cost-effective process that avoids the repackaging costs for packaging cigarette holder with cigarettes, besides ensuring that the cigarette holder is available to the consumer in a hygienic and sealed condition and that the same holder can be reused for the same pack of cigarettes.
Full Text The present invention relates to a cigarette cum cigarette holder packet and a process for preparing the same
BACKGROUND
Cigarettes are marketed either loose or in the form of cigarette packets. The pack is a composite packing containing cigarettes only. Cigarette holders are also available in the market. These cigarette holders are sold separately and therefore may not be readily available to all consumers. In our co-pending application 1192/Del/2000 filed on 21st December 2000, we have filed an application for a novel cigarette holder.
Till date there is no composite packaging available for cigarette and cigarette holder.
Further, devices as well as process for packaging normal packet of cigarettes to are well known to the persons skilled in the art. Such devices and process suffer from a drawback that a holder with a pack of cigarettes cannot be packed simultaneously.
Accordingly the object to the present invention is to overcome the aforementioned drawbacks and provide a composite and integral cigarette cum cigarette holder packet as well as convenient and a continuous process for packaging cigarette cum cigarette holder.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a cost-effective process such that repackaging cost is avoided.
Yet another object of the present invention is to ensure that the cigarette holder is available to the consumer in a hygienic and sealed condition and ensures that the same holder can be reused.
To achieve the said object the present invention provides a cigarette cum cigarette holder packet comprising:a predetermined number of cigarettes arranged in a predetermined manner wrapped in a foil,said wrapped cigarettes being placed at one end of the shell slide and said holder being placed at the opposite end of the said shell slide and
an outer rectangular shaped shell of predetermined dimensions to accommodate the cigarette and cigarette holder open at top and bottom end, said slide shell having the wrapped cigarettes and holder being positioned into the said outer rectangular shaped shell and the slide.
The said cigarette cum cigarette holder is wrapped in a Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene film (hereinafter called as BOPP film for short) with two tear off ribbon (TOR) strips, said strips being positioned close to the proximal and distal end respectively of the cigarette packet to provide easy accessibility to the cigarette from one end and the cigarette holder at the other end.
The cigarette holder is present either at proximal or the distal end of the said outer rectangular shaped shell.
The cigarette holder is present distal end of the said outer rectangular shaped shell.

WORKING OF THE INVENTION
Tobacco is supplied in nopper of Cigarclie manufacturing machine, witch is opened up by carding drum and sucked on Nylon band by vacuum suction. This gives cylindrical profile to the tobacco, which is then taken to Garniture tape carrying cigarette paper, and the cylindrical profile tobacco is placed above the cigarette paper. This tobacco and Cigarette paper passes below the rod heaters. This is the place where tobacco rod is completely formed. This rod is then cut by cutter knife. The machine produces cut cigarette as per desired length, circumference and diameter at various tobacco densities.
This cut and rolled cigarette then enters filter assembler cum tipping machine. The function of filter assembler is to roll the incoming cigarette with filter & cigarette tipping paper. The mechanism involves wrapping of two plain cigarettes with a predetermined width of cigarette tipping paper. The absence of filter rod resulted in modification to filter assembler to feed only the CT paper on plain cigarette. Only CT paper is cut into two after wrapping. Thus the over tipped cigarette is manufactured without filter media or plug in it.
The over tipped cigarettes are then transferred to a modified packer which has been designed to incorporate an innovative design of a "Bowl Feeder" The bowl feeder stores the plastic cigarette holder. The Feed Mechanism (6) of the plastic filter is synchronized to match the feed rate of the cigarette bundle, slide & shell. There is flexibility to position the plastic holder either at the bottom of the bundle i.e rear side preference or at the top of the bundle i.e. top side preference.The two spools of the Tear Off Ribbon (TOR) 1 and 2 are fitted on the TOR bobbin holders above the BOPP film. TORs are then passed over the guide rollers and stuck on the BOPP film, 'flic BOPP film with two TOR strips stuck to it are passed into the cutting unit and cut into the required length of the film before it is wrapped over the packet.
The finished packets are then fed to a unique designed wrapper which has the two TOR strips. One TOR is used to open the pack, whereas the bottom TOR helps to remove the plastic holder at the rear end of the foil bundle containing cigarettes.
The machine designed has flexibility to insert the plastic cigarette holder to pack plain or filter cigarettes.

the two OK advantage, in the sense that the cigutLuc holder can be used, kept back and again used for the remaining cigarettes i.e. it provides storage \ pocket for the holder to ensure its usability again and again for the same pack.
the two TOR advantage also increases the accessibility of the cigarette holder in every pack.
The two TOR strip has another advantage. Cigarette can be pulled out from one end of the pack while holder can be removed and placed back from another side of the pack. It allows for double-sided accessibility, such that the removing of one commodity does not disturb the placement of the other.
