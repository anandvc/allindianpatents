Title of Invention

A DETERGENT BAR FOR PERSONAL USE OR FABRIC WASH.
Abstract A detergent bar for personal use or fabric wash having improved properties in use such as improved crack resistance, improved lather and less mush*. The bar composition comprising : i) w25 to 9096 b/w of detergent of which at Least half, preferably three quarters is soap; ii) 0.1 to 60# b/w of the bar of solid structurant at least part of which amounting "to at least 0*1%, preferably at least 0.5%, more preferably at least \% b/w of the bar of modified starch as herein described; and iii) 8 to 4Q% b/w of the bar of water, the modified starch having been incorporated in the dry state to the detergent material of the bar. The modified starch can be derived from any source of starch such as rice, potato, maize, tapioca, jowar, millets, waxy maise, high amylose maize, wheat and the like. The modified starch may be used in granular or gelatinized form.
