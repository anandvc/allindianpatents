Title of Invention

OFFSHORE PLATFORM FOR DRILLING AFTER OR PRODUCTION OF HYDROCARBONS
Abstract Platform for drilling after or production of hydrocarbons at sea, consisting of a semi-submersible platform body (2) which supports drilling and/or production equipment on its upper surface. The platform body (2) is designed as a vertical mainly flat bottomed cylinder which is provided with at least one peripheral circular cut-out (5, 30, 35, 36) in the lower section of the cylinder since the center of buoyancy for the submerged section of the platform (1) is positioned lower than the center of gravity of the platform.
