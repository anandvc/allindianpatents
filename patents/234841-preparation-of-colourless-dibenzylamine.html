Title of Invention

PREPARATION OF COLOURLESS DIBENZYLAMINE
Abstract PREPARATION OF COLOURLESS DIBENZYLAMINE The invention relates to a process for preparing colourless dibenzylamine by adding ammonium chlorides or amines to the industrially obtained dibenzylamine and subsequent distillation.
