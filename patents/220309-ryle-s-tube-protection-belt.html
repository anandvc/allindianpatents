Title of Invention

"RYLE'S TUBE PROTECTION BELT"
Abstract Instant invention relates to a device (belt) useful for the protection of Ryle's Tube from possibilities of pulling out accidentally by the patient. The belt is made up of plastic material.
Full Text Ryle's Tube Protection Belt (R.T.P.B.)
Field of Invention
This invention relates to a Ryle's Tube Protection Belt which is concerned with Medical Science. It is used for safe guarding of Ryle's Tube. Ryle's tube is used for feeding patients.
Background of Invention
Patients used to encounter various difficulties before this invention. Ryle's Tube was to be fixed on the nose of patients with the help of tape or micropore, which was getting removed due to sweating, water or soaking. Tape or micropore generally used, to leave ill effect on the skin of the body which resulted in itching, dis colouring of the skin or even scars.
The insertion of Ryle's Tube is painful process and it is generally accompanies by sever coughing and bleeding from the mouth and nose. Also there is a possibility of contacting infectious diseases.
Besides this, Ryle's Tube is generally taken out by patients and which is to be put again. Patients under sub conscious stage usually remove Ryle's Tube because of nasal irritation hence it was only option to tie their hands. Putting of Ryle's Tube, again used to cause infection and wound in Trachea which resulted in bleeding, coughing. In such circumstances the condition of the patients used to become bad to worse.

Prior Art - In the past there was no device available to keep the Ryle's Tube in place which was generally coming out. Patients and attendants undergone a lot of trauma and pain.

Statement of Invention ;-
A Ryle's Tube Protection Belt comprising : a upper part (1) made up as a long and thin plastic strip having Ryle's Tube hanging system(s); a connector (3) connects upper part (1) & lower part (2) ; a lower part (2) made up as a long & thin strip having a nose shaped front side (8) ; a longitudinally cut PVC tube (4) which protects Ryle's Tube is attached to said lower part (2) at one end and another end of said tube (4) is kept open; valcros(6) are attached at both ends of upper part (1) & lower part(2).
Brief Description of the accompanying drawings ;-
Further feature and advantages of the invention become clear from the following description and drawing .
Drawing Detail :- Fig. No.l shows the shape and various parts of belt, various labelled parts are as.
Part No.l : This is upper part of belt, inner side of this belt is cover with sofa eva (eva is a kind of foam), it has slots on the both side of belt for tying valcro. This part of belt is plastic.

Part No. 2 : This is lower part of belt, inner side of this belt is cover with eva, it has two holes on the both side of belt for tie valcro. This part of belt is made of plastic.
Part No. 3 : This is a connector between upper and lower portion of the belt. Inner side of this connector is also covered with foam.
Part No. 4 : This is PVC tube which slot on inner side of tube, whole tube is cut in longitudinal way for covering and protecting the Ryle's Tube. One end of this tube is tight with lower part of belt and other end is kept open. This tube is made on non toxic grade of PVC by extrusion method.
Part No.5 : This is connected with the part no. 1. This provides Ryle's Tube hanging system. There are two cuts in the upper portion. These cuts used to hang the Ryle's Tube. Inner portion of this is covered with foam.
Part No. 6 : These are the valcro, which are at both ends of upper and lower portion of belt. These help to tie the belt with head of patient. Material for these valcro is non toxic and allergic free.
Part No. 7 : This is the joint of belt and valcro both.
Part No. 8 : This is the joint of PVC Tube and lower middle portion of the belt which is curved in nose shape.

Detailed Description of the Invention :-
The nature of the invention, its objective and further advantages residing in the same will be apparent from the following description made with reference to the non limiting exemplary embodiment of the invention, represented in the accompanying drawings.
After applied Ryle's Tube tape or micropore was used to fix on the body of patient till now, which used to get removed in case of sweating or soaking. Hence its replacement became necessary again and again. Patients used to pull it out easily by hands. Hence to put Ryle's Tube in sub-conscious patient it was necessary to tied their hands, so that patients are not able to remove it easily. In cases of patients suffering from carcinoma throat and mouth, it was an up hill task to put Ryle's Tube safely however this exercise was carried out by the expert doctors, and patients had to bear burden of charging heavy fees. Ryle's Tube used to result in infection due to repeated application which resulted in bleeding, coughing and it became prime cause for the seriousness of the patients. Patients faced difficulties living country side since no such facilities available there. This resulted in wastage of money and labour, even they had to face difficulties for getting the tape or micropore changed.
Attendants used to incur expenditure again and again for purchasing of syringe, gloves and jelly etc. Inspite this they had to bear the burden of doctor fee and all these exercise took place due to non-availability of such device.

Ryle's Tube Protection Belt is only the best option available in such circumstances. Ryle's Tube Protection Belt is tied with the head of the patient. It is fixed as per necessity with the valcro put on both the ends of plastic belt. Middle part of lower belt is of nose shape which proves untrouble some for the patients. A tube having length of 46 cm cut in centre lengthwise, is fixed with lower margin of lower belt, which makes it difficult for the patients to remove it until it is broken or knowingly taken out.
After applying this Ryle's Tube protection belt. Ryle's Tube will not come out by any means itself until and unless some body will knowingly take it out.
This Ryle's Tube protection belt has proved to be boon for the patients, foam (eva) is used for protecting the skin and its application is very simple.
Method :
For preparation of this Ryle's Tube Protection Belt, a tube having longitude cut with 46 cm length approx, and two thin plastic belt of same size, are used. Lower belt is given shape of nose in middle part and, these two plastic belts joined together with a thin plastic strip. Hanging system is made in upper side towards both side of forehead. In case of excess length it can be removed by scissors after putting Ryle's Tube. Ryle's Tube & tube remain of equal size, for the comfort of the patients Rubber Foam (eva) is used in inner side of the belt which gives soothing effect to the patients and it has no side effect on the body of the patient.
