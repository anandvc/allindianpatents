Full Text We Claim:
1. A razor blade unit comprising at least one blade (6) with an
elongate edge, a skin engagement surface for engaging the skin
ahead of the blade edge during shaving, the skin engaging surface
having slots (10) for hairs to pass through, the skin engaging
surface being formed by a series of elements (9) between which the
slots (10) are formed, characterised in that the elements (9) have
slot edge profiles such that the slot edges (16) notch into the hairs
as they pass through the slots (10), and in the direction towards
the blade (6), the slot edge formations being inclined so as to guide
the notched hairs to follow a path causing the extension of the
hairs from the skin to be increased before the hairs are cut by the
blade edge.
2. A blade unit as claimed in claim l, wherein the inclination of the
edge formations is in the range of 30째 to 70째 to the skin
engagement surface.
3. A blade unit as claimed in claim 2, wherein the inclination of the
edge formations is in the range of 40째 to 60째 to the skin
engagement surface.
4. A blade unit as claimed in claims l, 2 or 3, wherein the elements
(9) have downwardly inclined parts (14) extending from the skin
engaging surface defining parts in the direction towards the blade.
5. A blade unit as claimed in claim 4, wherein the width of the slots
(10) tapers from the leading edge of the skin engagement surface to
a minimum value less than the diameter of a hair (H), and the
adjacent elements (9) are resiliently movable relative to each other
substantially parallel to the plane of the skin engaging surface.
6. A blade unit as claimed in claims 4 or 5, wherein the slot sections
defined between the inclined parts (14) are of substantially
constant width.
7. A blade unit as claimed in any one of claims 4 to 6, wherein the
elements (9) are provided by respective teeth of a comb-like
member.
8. A blade unit as claimed in any one of claims 4 to 7, wherein the
slot edge formations have edge portions (16) of the elements (9)
having reduced thickness.
9. A blade unit as claimed in claim 8, wherein the reduced thickness
edges (16) are formed by rebates on the undersides of the elements
(9).
10. A blade unit as claimed in claim 9, wherein the rebates are etched
surface portions.
11. A blade unit as claimed in claims 8, 9 or 10, wherein the edge
thickness is not greater than 20 microns.
12. A blade unit as claimed in any one of claims l to 11, wherein the
surface elements (9) are supported by respective springs.
13. A blade unit as claimed in claim 12, wherein the springs are
torsion springs (12) connected to the elements (9) at the forward
ends thereof.
14. A blade unit substantially as herein described with reference to
and as illustrated in the accompanying drawings.
