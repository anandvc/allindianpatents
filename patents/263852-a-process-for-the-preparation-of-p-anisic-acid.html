Title of Invention

“A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF P-ANISIC ACID”
Abstract The present invention relates to a process for the preparation of 4-Methoxy benzoic acid of formula (I) which is also known as a p-Anisic acid. p-Anisic acid is useful as an intermediate for pharmaceutical agents, dyes and fragrances.
Full Text The present invention is in the field of chemistry and more particularly the invention deals with a process for the preparation of 4-Methoxy benzoic acid (I) which is also known as a p-Anisic acid. p-Anisic acid is useful as an intermediate for pharmaceutical agents, dyes and fragrances..............................................
