AN IMPROVED PILFER PROOF CONTAINER
Abstract An improved pilfer proof container (1) consisting of receptacle (2) and a lid portion (3) having flange with matching grooves; the said receptacle being provided with a plurality of ribs (4) at a space apart relationship at the outer circumference and with or without a means (5) for holding the said container; the said lid portion having inwardly directed matching grooved to fit into the circumference ribs of the receptacle for locking and sealing; with or without a spout (10) for dispensing liquid characterized in that the lid when pressed fit on the container the grooves in the lid fits in at least two rib portions of the container thereby giving a double locking arrangement.

AN IMPROVED PILFER PROOF CONTAINER
The invention relates to an improved pilfer proof container. More particularly an improved pilfer proof container which is used for packaging solid and semi-solid or liquid substances avoiding pilferage of material or adulteration of contents therein for goodwill of the manufacturer.
The packaging of material in containers for supplying bulk material are provided are containers of varying dimensions. The problem arises for the storage of materials due to the property of the material themselves, heaviness of the material, difficulty in transporting the material, difficulty arises out of stacking of material.
During the transportation the materials are altered or adulterated by mixing the lower grade of material which ultimately tail upon the goodwill of the manufacturer & customers satisfaction. There are pilfer proof containers which are used for transporting solid, semi-solid or liquid substances. There they got their inherent problems apart from pilferage. Most of the pilfer proof packages are destructive in nature once the lid cut open cannot be reused therefore subsequent locking the container is difficult. The inventor has carried out lot of research over the matter and tried to overcome the aforesaid disadvantages of pilfer proof containers and come out with an
