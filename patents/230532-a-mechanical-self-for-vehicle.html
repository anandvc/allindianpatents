Title of Invention

"A MECHANICAL SELF FOR VEHICLE"
Abstract This invention relates to a mechanical self for use with the vehicles. The self has a plurality of strip coils mounted on a main shaft is disposed in a main casing rotatably. A free-wheel disposed in said casing mounted on said main shaft such that to allow the shaft to be rotated in one direction. A gear adapted to be rotated by a gear train is mounted on said main shaft so as to rotate said shaft for contracting said strip coils over said shaft. A starting gear is provided on a ratchet gear mounted rotatably on said main shaft towards the outer end of said shaft is extended outwardly of said casing. A starting ratchet mounted at the outer end of said shaft slidably is provided to engage said starting gear with said main shaft so as to provide rotational movement to said starting gear upon unlocking said free wheel by means of a start lever. A hand lever is provided with said casing such that to engage said starting gear with said gear mounted on said main shaft and for rotating said main shaft.

provided at either ends of said casing to support the main shaft.
3. A mechanical self as claimed in claim 2 wherein the inner ends of
said strip coils are secured with main shaft and the outer end of
strip coils are secured with casing.
4. A mechanical self as claimed in claim 1 wherein said gear train
having a gear mounted rotatably on a shaft secured with the said
casing to receive the drive from a gear mounted on a screw
conveyer shaft disposed rotatably in a second casing secured with
said main casing, another gear is mounted at the outer end of
screw conveyer shaft to receive the drive from a driving
gear rotated by starting gear.
5. A mechanical self as claimed in claim 4 wherein a screw conveyer
nut is mounted rotatably on said screw conveyor shaft to displace
said hand lever to disengage the driving gear from the starting gear
when the said strip coils are contracted completely.
6. A mechanical self substantially as herein described and illustrated with the help of accompanying drawings.
