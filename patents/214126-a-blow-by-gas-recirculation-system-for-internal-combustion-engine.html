Title of Invention

A BLOW-BY GAS RECIRCULATION SYSTEM FOR INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE
Abstract [Object] In an intake system of an internal combustion engine comprising a carburetor and an air cleaner disposed on the upstream side thereof which are connected to each other by a connecting tube, to simplify the structure of supporting a gas-liquid separation chamber of a blow-by gas recirculation means by the connecting tube and the structure of a communication passage between the connecting tube and the gas-liquid separation chamber. [Solving Means] Two support portiuons 25 and 26 are provided integrally with a connecting tube TC for connection between an air cleaner AC and a carburetor CA, and one 25 of them is made to funtion also as a communication passage 25a for communication between the connecting tube TC abd the pas- liquid separation chamber 29.
