Title of Invention

SWIM RING FOR INFANT
Abstract This invention describes a swim ring for infant which is an aerated circle ring, wherein the circle ring comprises inter ring and outer ring, and the outer ring is thicker than that of the inter ring. A cut is mounted on the inner ring and the outer ring separately. A fastener movably connects the inner ring and the outer ring. The outer ring is two-double structure and two air holes are arranged in the outer ring, and the inner air chamber is communicated with one of the chamber of the outer ring. A groove used for holding infant chin is mounted on the inner ring, and the outer ring has also a groove. This structure can fix the aerated ring around the neck of the infant, and make the infant float in water perpendicularly, without any influence on movement of infant's four limbs and breath. Thus, it can achieve effectiveness in infant's nonage exercise. (Figure 1)

Technical Field
The present invention relates to a swim ring for infant.
Background of the Art
At present, swim rings are used to secure the safety of people who are learning to swim. However, the conventional swim rings are generally suitable for the persons who have subjective sense and distinguishing ability. Generally, the conventional swim ring is to be put around the middle portion of the body of a person and below his or her arms. Such swim rings are not suitable for infant.
Contents of the Invention
An object of the present invention is to provide a swim ring for infant, with which the infant can exercise himself/herself.
In order to achieve the above object, the present invention provides a swim ring for infant, which is an inflatable circular ring, being characterized in that the circular ring comprises an inner ring and an outer ring being thicker than the inner ring; a cutout is formed on the inner ring and the outer ring; a fastener is provided at the cutout so as to movably connect the inner ring with the outer ring; the outer ring is of a double layer structure and accordingly two air taps are formed on the outer ring; an air chamber of the inner ring is communicated with one of air chambers of the outer ring; a recess is formed on the inner ring and the recess has a size being suitable for accommodating a chin of the infant.
According to further improved solution of the present invention, color balls are provided in the outer ring.

According to another improved solution of the present invention, a water-proof music player is provided on the outer ring.
According to additional improved solution of the present invention, two handles are provided at a periphery of the outer ring.
With the above mentioned structure of the swim ring according the present invention , the inflatable circular ring can be put at the two sides of the chin and neck of the infant and the chin of the infant would be hold in the recess so that the infant can float in water erectly or obliquely, without any influence on the movement of limbs and the breath of the infant. Thereby, the swim ring according to the present invention would have the effect of benefiting the early exercise of the infant.
Statement of Invention
Accordingly, the present invention relates to a swim ring for infant comprising, an inflatable circular ring (1) comprises an inner ring (2) and an outer ring (3) being thicker than the inner ring (2); a cutout (4) is formed on the inner ring (2) and the outer ring (3); a fastener (5) is provided at the cutout (4) so as to movably connect the inner ring (2) with the outer ring (3); the outer ring (3) is of a double layer structure and accordingly two air taps (31) are provided on the outer ring (3); an air chamber of the inner ring (2) is communicated with one of air chambers of the outer ring (3); a recess (6) is formed on the inner ring (2) and the access (6) has a size being capable of accommodating a chin of the infant.

Preferred Embodiments of the Invention
As shown in figure 1 and figure 2, the swim ring for infant according to the present invention is an inflatable circular ring, which is characterized in that the circular ring 1 comprises an inner ring 2 and an outer ring 3 being thicker than the inner ring 2; a cutout 4 is formed on the inner ring 2 and the outer ring 3; a fastener 5 is provided at the cutout 4 so as to movably connect the inner ring 2 with the outer ring 3; the outer ring 3 is of a double layer structure and accordingly two air taps 31 are provided on the outer ring 3; an air chamber of the inner ring 2 is communicated with one of air chambers of the outer ring 3; a recess 6 is formed on the inner ring 2 and the recess 6 has a size being capable of accommodating a chin of the infant.
Additionally, color balls 7 may be provided in the outer ring. A waterproof music player
9 may be provided on the outer ring 3. Two handles 8 are provided of the outer ring 3.
When in use, the inflatable circular ring 1 can be fixed at the neck of the infant, and the chin of the infant would be hold in the recess so that the infant can float in water erectly or obliquely, without any influence on the movement of limbs and the breath of the infant. Thereby, the swim ring according to the present invention would have the effect of benefiting the early exercise of the infant. The air contained in the swim ring of the present invention may be replaced by any other buoyancy materials.
