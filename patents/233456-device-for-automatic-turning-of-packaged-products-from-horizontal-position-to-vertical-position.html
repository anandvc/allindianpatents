DEVICE FOR AUTOMATIC TURNING OF SACHET STRINGS FROM HORIZONTAL POSITION TO VERTICAL POSITION
FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to a device for automatic turning of packaged products from horizontal position to a vertical position. More particularly, the present invention relates to a device for turning sachets arriving in horizontal position on a flat conveyor belt to a vertical position for being placed on an in-feed conveyor of flow wrap machine.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
To the best of the Applicants knowledge, there is no automatic device developed so far for the purpose of turning sachets strings arriving in horizontal position on a flat conveyor belt to a vertical position.
As present, the turning of the sachet strings is done manually. The existing mechanism of manual turning of sachet strings is depicted in Figure 1 and the working of the device is described here below.
Multiple rows of sachet strings in horizontal position arrive on single flat conveyor belt. Workmen standing along the flow direction of the sachet strings manually turn the sachet strings and place them in a vertical position on the conveyor belt. Due to involvement of human labor, the process of packaging the product is slow. Thus is a need to automate the process of turning the sachet strings from horizontal position to vertical position.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
The main object of the present invention is to provide a device for automatic turning of packaged products from horizontal position to a vertical position.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a device for transferring sachets arriving in horizontal position on a flat conveyor belt to a vertical position for being placed on an in-feed conveyor of flow wrap machine.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
Accordingly, the present invention provides a device for turning packaged products arriving in-a horizontal position on a flat conveyor belt to a vertical position for being placed on an in-feed conveyor of flow wrap machine.

STATEMENT OF THE INVENTION
Accordingly, the present invention provides a device for automatic turning packaged products from horizontal position to a vertical position, said device comprising a flat conveyor belt carrying the packaged products in a horizontal position and one or more twist product guides mounted on the flat conveyor belt, such that the twist product guides progressively turns the sachet strings from horizontal to vertical position, wherein the twist product guide comprises a means for guiding an incoming sachet string to a sachet mounting portion, wherein the sachet mounting portion is preferably in the form of a flat platform for facilitating an end of the sachet string to mount on to a ramp portion, said ramp portion being suffixed by a tilting portion to tilt / turn the sachet coming on the ramp progressively from horizontal position to vertical position such that at the end of the tilting portion, the sachet string acquires right vertical position and a stabilizing portion optionally being provided after the turning portion to prevent falling down of the sachet.

WORKING OF THE NEWLY DEVELOPED MECHANISM FOR AUTOMATIC TURNING OF SACHET STRINGS
The device of the present invention works as follows:
Multiple rows of sachet strings arrive from single belt conveyor onto automatic sachet strings turning device. There are multiple rows of strip belts on the turning device. Each belt carries individual sachet string and transfers the same from one end of the device to the other.
The Applicants have now designed special profile of twist product guides, which are mounted above strip belts on turning device. The sachets strings which are transferred by strip belts are guided by these guides.
The design of the profile of guides is made such that during travel of sachets strings, the guides progressively turn them from the horizontal position to vertical position. At the end of the guide, the strings acquire right vertical position.
The workman standing near the turning device then manually places the strings on the in feed conveyor of flow wrap machine.
