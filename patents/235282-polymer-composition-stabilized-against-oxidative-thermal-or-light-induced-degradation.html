Stabilization of Synthetic Polymers
The present invention relates to compositions comprising a synthetic polymer subject to oxi¬dative, thermal or light-induced degradation, especially polyolefins, polyether polyol or poly-urethane, and as stabilizer a specific group of compounds of the benzofuran-2-on6 type.
The use of compounds of the benzofuran-2-one type as stabilizers for synthetic polymers is known, for example, from WO-A-80/01566 or US 5,516,920.
The known stabilizers do not satisfy in every respect the high requirements which a stabilizer is required to meet, especially with regard to shelf life, water absorption, sensitivity to hydro¬lysis, in-process stabilization, color properties, volatility, migration behaviour, compatibility apd improvement in protection against light. As a result there continues to be a need for effective stabilizers for synthetic polymers that are sensitive to oxidative, thermal and/or light-induced degradation.
