According to the first aspect of the Invention, since the metallic connector 1 has teeth 4 on the inner surface of each of upper and lower jaws 3 and a connecting ring 5 attached to the outer surface of a cormon base of the upper and lower jaws 3 while the belt 2 has on its ends thermoplastic resin ridges 8 to be caught by the teeth 4, it is possible to attach the belt 2, v*iich serves as a handle string or a slide fastener slider pull string, to the connector 1 sinply and neatly with adequate firmness and to lirprove the rate of production and hence make it suitable for mass production.
