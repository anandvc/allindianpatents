Title of Invention

A METHOD FOR INSERTING A DOWNLINK COMMON PILOT
Abstract An inserting method of downlink common pilot, which includes: A, a pending transmission data is divided into several consecutive time segments, each time segment contains several time slot, in which have at least one downlink time slot; B, a common pilot is inserted into one downlink time slot of said time segment. Therefore, the present invention can realize dispatchment upon frequency domain in virtue of channel quality measurement for common pilot.
Full Text The present invention relates to mobile communication technology and specifically to a method for inserting a downlink common pilot in a radio frame, which is adaptive to a Time division Duplex (TDD) system based on orthogonal frequency division multiple access (OFDMA) so that a Scheduling based on frequency domain can be achived by a measurement on a common pilot channel...........................
