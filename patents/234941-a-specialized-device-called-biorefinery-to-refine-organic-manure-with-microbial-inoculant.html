Title of Invention

A SPECIALIZED DEVICE CALLED BIOREFINERY TO REFINE ORGANIC MANURE WITH MICROBIAL INOCULANT
Abstract ABSTRACT A novel device used to refine organic manures with microbial bioinnoculant in controlled conditions. The device consists of a vessel with accessories for inoculation, aeration, mixing, harvesting, refining and the like. The vessel can also be used for brewing cultures of agriculturally useful organisms with fungicidal activity, insecticidal activity, nematicidal activity, compost accelerating activity, nitrogen fixing, phosphate solubilizing activity, plant growth promoting activity and probiotic activity for producing cattle feed. The biorefinery is a sturdy tool to produce microbial cultures and refine compost on farm without any loss of activity of microbes due to environmental or storage stress.

Present invention relates to a device for, an economical way of producing agriculturally useful microbes on field or refining composts.
It is a well known fact that, excessive and obsessive use of chemicals has disturbed the biological cycle, thereby destroying the soul of soil. The biological cycle comprises predominantly of microbes, like fungi, algae, protozoa, bacteria, viruses etc. These microbes are responsible for recycling nutrients form dead and decaying matter back to the soil. Organic farming methods like addition of composts, mulches and crop residues are not sufficient. The nutrients locked up inside the compost have to be acted upon and mineralized by microbes. Due to excessive usage of toxic chemicals, the biological activity in many soils has deteriorated. Thus, supplementation of agriculturally useful microbes to composts and soils is necessary for realizing the benefits in a shortest possible time period. The benefits of refining compost have already been explained in our co-pending patent application no. 864/MUM/2004 dated 11.8.2004.
Present invention aims at developing a special purpose device to aerobically refine compost for producing an organic refined extract. As the application involves setting up of this system on farm, the operation and design has to be suitably robust.
Object of the present invention, therefore aims at developing a special
purpose device i.e. specialized refining equipment referred to as a bio-refinery for carrying out an improved process to brew extracted and added microbes, which refine any organic manure such as compost or vermicompost.
The specialized refinery equipment referred to as "Biorefinery" consists of a non-reactive plastic or stainless steel tank rested on a metallic or plastic stand. The tank has a height diameter ratio of 1-3:1, wall thickness of 2 to 4mm and a man-hole size of 0.5 to 0.75 times the diameter, with a close fitting lid. The tank
has four openings fitted with 0.5" to 1" ball valves, two of which are used for

The tank rests on a stand with a box in the front which serves as a control panel.
