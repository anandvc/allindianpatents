EXAMPLE The nan0-composite material is shaped into form of a rod, sheet or fihn. In the Example, it is shaped mto the form a film F fixed to a glass slide S which is then exposed to sunlight for 30 minutes, when reduction takes place as can be seen from the fact that the colour of the film now changes from transparent to blue. It is then dipped in a solution B containing carbon (support material) and the active component hexachloroplatinic acid, while stirring the same vigorously, to reoxidise the said composite. The re-oxidised composite is then dried at 70 deg. Centigrade to obtain the support metal catalyst R, that is, Pt on C.
