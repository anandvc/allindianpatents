The present invention also relates to novel intermediate of the formula 20 and a process for its preparation. The novel intermediate of formula 20 is used in preparation of Dorzolamide hydrochloride having formula 1. Dorzolamide hydrochloride is useful in the treatment of ocular hypertension by inhibiting carbonic anhydrase enzyme.
