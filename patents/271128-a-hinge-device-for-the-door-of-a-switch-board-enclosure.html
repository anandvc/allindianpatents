Title of Invention

A HINGE DEVICE FOR THE DOOR OF A SWITCH BOARD ENCLOSURE
Abstract For rotatably mountinq a door (40) to a switchboard enclosure (30), a hinge device (10) comprises a body (11) fixable to the enclosure (30), a fixed pintle (15) integral with the body (11) and extending in a vertical direction, and a mobile pintle (16) vertically slidably in the body (11) The pintle (16) is movable between an extended position for rotatably engaging the door (40), and a retracted position towards the inside of the body (11) for disengaging from the door.
