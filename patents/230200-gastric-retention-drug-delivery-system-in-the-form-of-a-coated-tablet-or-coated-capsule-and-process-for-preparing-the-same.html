Title of Invention

GASTRIC RETENTION DRUG DELIVERY SYSTEM IN THE FORM OF A COATED TABLET OR COATED CAPSULE AND PROCESS FOR PREPARING THE SAME.
Abstract The present invention provides a novel gastric retention system in the form of a tablet or a capsule coated with an expandable coating, more particularly, with an expandable coating comprising a film-forming polymer and expandable component.
