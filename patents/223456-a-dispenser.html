Title of Invention

A DISPENSER
Abstract An inhalant dispenser has a cover (3) pivotally carried on a body (6). The cover (3) has a cam arrangement for lifting a junction member (41) against the action of the spring in an inhalant can C. Lifting of the junction member (41) cocks a breath actuated dose release mechanisms. The latter includes a flap (61) in the breath path. The flap (61) is carried on the junction member (41) and is arranged to release a hinged member (48) by action of latches (70) on the flap (61) and sears (591, 592) on the hinged member (48).
