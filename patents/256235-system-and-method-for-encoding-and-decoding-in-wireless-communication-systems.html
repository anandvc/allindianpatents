Title of Invention

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR ENCODING AND DECODING IN WIRELESS COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS
Abstract A control encoder,e.g.,in a UMB system,uses a channel structure that can efficiently transmit more information bits,yet achieve sufficient detection and false alarm performancea.A control channel encoder can use a fixed encoder packetsize,tail-biting convolutional coding,and Cyclical Redundancy Check(CRC).A control channel decoder can use Viterbi Decoding and a circular trellis check.
