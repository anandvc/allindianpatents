Title of Invention

PROTECTION CONTROL MEASUREMENT SYSTEM
Abstract This invention provides a protection control measurement system, with excellent economy and 5 maintainability, which collects protection control measurement data via a communication network from a plurality of terminals which acquire electric quantities. In the protection control measurement terminal 1, electric quantity acquisition and 10 transmission means 14-1 determines synchro-phasor quantities from electric quantity data and transmits the synchro-phasor quantities to arrival time-guaranteed transmission means 15-1. The arrival time-guaranteed transmission means 15-1 transmits in real- 15 time the synchro-phasor quantities to the parent station device 3. Setting value transmission means 14-2 transmits setting value data to the message transmission means 15-2, which performs message transmission to the parent station device 3 over the 20 communication network 2. In the parent station device 3, electric quantity arithmetic operation means 32-1 calculates electric quantities as close as possible to true values by multiplying received synchro-phasor quantities by setting value data. The protection 40 25 control measurement arithmetic operation means 32-2 performs arithmetic operations for accident judgment for protection control measurement terminals 1 from the magnitudes of electric quantities and voltage phase differences.

1. Field of the Invention
This invention relates to a protection control measurement system which performs protection, control and measurement of an electric power system, and relates to a system with excellent economy and maintainability, which collects protection control measurement data using a communication network.
