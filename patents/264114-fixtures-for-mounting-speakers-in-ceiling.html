Title of Invention

FIXTURES FOR MOUNTING SPEAKERS IN CEILING
Abstract Fixtures for mounting speakers in ceiling consisting of: a perforated grill; at least two clamps fitted on the operative inner surface of the grill; flanges extending from the clamp for securely holding a speaker to the inner surface of the grill; spring loaded swivcllable arms fitted in the clamps on an angularly displaccablc support accessible from a particular perforation leading lo the operative outer surface of the grill such that in the operative configuration of the mounted speaker in the aperture, the grill with a speaker fitted thereto is inserted in the aperture and the support is accessed from the outer surface of the grill by a suitable tool by which it is angularly displaccablc to swivel the arms under spring bias and hold the inner surface of the wall defining Ihe aperture in a removable manner.
