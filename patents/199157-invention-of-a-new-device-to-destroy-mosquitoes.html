Title of Invention

INVENTION OF A NEW DEVICE TO DESTROY MOSQUITOES
Abstract An equipment invented to control and annihilate mosquito genus. The main parts of this equipment are plastic containers having two doors at top part and net. Pockets fixed on doors. The meshes of net pocket are small that only eggs of mosquitoes can pass through and not a mosquito. Water filled in the container attracts female mosquitoes to lag eggs. The mosquito eggs reach at the bottom of containers through mesh of met pockets and become new mosquito but because of they are under net pocket they cannot fly out and die.

Specification
THIS INVENTION is new and related to an equipment and method intended to control and annihilate mosquito genus from the world, where human being are living. Mosquitoes are insects can carry and spread many types of diseases that are caused by parasites, bacteria etc. The diseases spreading by mosquitoes included malaria, filarisis. Dengue Fever, Yellow Fever, Encephalitis etc. Cores of rupees are spending for the protection from the disease and for controlling the spreading growth of mosquitoes. Commonly mosquitoes are controlled by spraying pesticides and by using mosquito repellants, and by personal protection.These are not probable methods to control or destroy the mosquito genus as a whole, which is a menace to the mankind.
Kerosene spray and Ignition may use to destroy mosquitoes in waste water channels and heap of impurities lightening banquet is also usefril to destroy some mosquitoes in waste water channels and the continues use of it destroys mosquito genus as a whole within a prescribed time. The invention is the result of long- term experiment. The equipment and method invented by me is really a remedy against tfie growth and spread of mosquito menace. As like any other insects mosquitoes with also hatches from eggs. The adult mosquitoes seeks mate and then feed. The female mosquitoes with long proboscis bite animals and humans and feed on their blood before each time they lay eggs. In the life cycle of mosquitoes ,the female lay their eggs in water only and the larva and pupa live entirely in water pupa change in to adults and leave the water in to a free flying adult. The equipment attracts the mosquitoes to lay eggs and hatches and breeds the eggs, but it prevents the adult mosquitoes flying away on the land, but it extinct with in the equipment. That is, after the completion of the cycle, tiie adult cannot fly away and the survival of the mosquitoes is checked. Extinction of the newly hatched mosquitoes and the continuous repetition of the process destroys adult mosquitoes and annihilates growtfi and spread of the mosquitoes with in a prescribed time.
