Title of Invention

METHOD AND DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING COMMON MODE ELECTRODE VOLTAGE IN A LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY.
Abstract TITLE: METHOD AND DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING COMMON MODE ELECTRODE VOLTAGE IN A LIQUID CRYSTAL DISPLAY. A method of controlling common mode electrode voltage in a liquid crystal display, comprising the steps of applying a video signal with a predetermined color and drive level to an imager, determining a difference between a positive field detector voltage and a negative field detector voltage and feeding back the difference to a controller to adjust the common mode electrode voltage.
