Title of Invention

BEADED CROSSLINKED POLYMERS CONTAINING TERT-AMINO FUNCTIONAL GROUP MOIETIES
Abstract The present invention relates to synthesis of beaded crosslinked porous polymers comprising of tert-amino functionality. The surface properties and morphology of the polymers can be designed by variation in reaction parameters.
