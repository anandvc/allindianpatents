Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR THE PRODUCTION OF TEREPHTHALIC ACID"
Abstract A process for the production of terephthalic acid comprising: a first stage in which paraxylene is oxidised in a reaction medium containing acetic acid to produce a slurry of crude terephthalic acid crystals in acetic acid-based mother liquor, the acetic acid-based mother liquor is separated from the crude terephthalic acid by means of an integrated separation and multistage countercurrent washing filter operation as Herein described to produce a filter cake comprising the crude terephthalic acid crystals and residual mother liquor, characterised in that the filter cake is washed with a wash liquor of the kind such as herein described, and following removal of the acetic based mother liquor and the wash liquor the crude terephthalic acid is dissolved in water to produce an aqueous solution of crude terephthalic acid;and a second stage in which the terephthalic acid is purified by contacting the said aqueous solution with hydrogen in the presence of a heterogeneous catalyst of the kind* such as herein described to reduce impurities associated with the crude terephthalic acid, and the purified terephthalic acid is separated from the aqueous mother liquor; washing of the said terephthalic acid using water and followed by the dissolution step using said aqueous mother liquor obtained from the second stage as a source of water thereof.
