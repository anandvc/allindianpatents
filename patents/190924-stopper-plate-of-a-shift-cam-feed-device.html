Title of Invention

STOPPER PLATE OF A SHIFT CAM FEED DEVICE.
Abstract A STOPPER PLATE (17) OF A SHIFT CAM FEED DEVICE FITTED FIXEDLY TO ONE END OF A SHIFT AM (13) CONSTITUTING A GEAR SHIFT MECHNISM AS HEREIN DESCRIBED FOR TURNING SAID SHIFT CAM BY A PREDETERMINED ANGLE AS HEREIN DESCRIBED AT A TINE THROUGH SHIFTING OPERATIONS, CHARACTERIZED IN THAT THE STOPPER PLATE (17) IS FORMED INTO A PLATE LIKE SHAPE, AND A PLURALITY OF CAM FACES (17a-0 TO 17a5) ARE FORMED ON THE CIRCUMFERENTIAL SURFACE THEREOF BY PRESS FORMING OF A METAL PLATE, AND A PLURALITY OF PINS (17c) FORMED BY PRESS EXTRUSION PROJECTING FROM THE END FACE THEREOF ARE PROVIDED INTEGRALLY DISPOSED IN A CIRCLE WHICH IS CONCENTRIC TO THE CIRCLE OF THE ROUND HOLE (17b) FORMED ON THE CENTRAL PORTION OF THE STOPPER PLATE (17) AND THE DIAMETER (d1) OF RECESSES (17d) formed on the SIDE OPPOSITE TO THAT ON WHICH SAID PINS (17c) are formed, is set to a value larger than the diameter (d2)OF SAID PINS.
