Title of Invention

'' AN IMPROVED INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE"
Abstract This invention concerns an improved internal combustion engine having a crankshaft, an accessory, a motor/generator, and a belt-drive system having a crankshaft pulley, an accessory pulley, a mo tor/generator pulley, a belt tensioner, a belt tensioner pulley, and a power transmission belt trained about said crankshaft pulley, said accessory pulley, said mo tor/generator pulley, and said belt tensioner pulley said belt tensioner pulley contacting said belt at a starting-slack-side span, the improvement comprising: said tensioner being asymmetrically biased in a direction tending to cause said power transmission belt to be under tension.
