Title of Invention

a process for the preparation of a novel 3-hydroxy-5-(tert.butyldimethyl silyloxy)-(3S,5R)-cyclohexan-1-one useful as an intermediate for Beta-hydroxy Game-lactone
Abstract A process for the preparation of a novel 3-hydroxy-5-(tert.butyldimethyl silyloxy)-(3S,5R)-cyclohexan-1-one useful as an intermediate for ß-hydroxy &#947;-lactone by reacting a compound 3-tetrahydro-2H-2-pyranyloxy-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy)-(1S.3R, 5R) -cyclohexan-1-one with magnesium bromide in an aliphatic organic solvent at a temperature ranging from 5 to 30°C for a period from 1 to 12 hours, quenching the above reaction with ammonium chloride, separating organic layer, drying and on evaporating to obtain 3-hydroxy-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy)-(3S,5R)-cyclohexan-1-one.

The prior art processes have following drawbacks:
1. The processes use chemicals such as butyl lithium, lithium aluminum hydride, methoxy-diethylborane which are costly and difficult to handle and therefore make the process difficult.
All known process are however involves large number of synthetic steps resulting in low over all yields.
The main object of the present invention is to provide a process for the preparation of a novel 3-tetrahydro-2H-2-pyranyloxy-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy)-(1S,3R,5R)-cyclohexan-1-ol useful as an intermediate for 6-hydroxymethyl-4-(tert-butyldimethylsilyloxy)-(4R,6S)-tetrahydro-2H-2-pyranone.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a process for the
preparation of 6-hydroxymethyl-4-(tert.butyldimethyl silyloxy)-(4R, 6S)-tetrahydro-
2H-2-pyranone(1) which obviates the drawbacks of the prior art proceses and use
cheaper and easily accessible chemicals.
Accordingly, the present invention provides a process for the preparation of a
novel 3-hydroxy-5-(tert.butyldimethyl silyloxy)-(3S,5R)-cyclohexan-1-one having
formula 9

5R) -cyclohexan-1-one with magnesium bromide in an aliphatic organic solvent such as herein described at a temperature ranging from 5 to 30°C for a period from 1 to 12 horrs, quenching the above reaction with ammonium chloride, separating organic layer, drying and on evaporating to obtain 3-hydroxy-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy)-(3S,5R)-cyclohexan-1-one.
In an embodiment of the present invention the organic solvent used for the reaction is selected from the group consisting of ethyl acetate, chloroform, dichloromethane, methanol and diethyl ether.

Example 1 Preparation of cis, cis-3-hydroxy-5-methylcarbonyioxy-cyclohexylacetate
Finely powdered cis, cis-3,5-di(methylcarbonyloxy)cyclohexylacetate (6.5 parts, 25.19 mmol parts) was suspended in 0.1 M sodium phosphate buffer (pH 7) (135 parts) and stirred vigorously. To the stirred suspension Porcine Liver Esterase (0.110 parts) was added and reaction mixture was stirred vigorously at 30°C for 20 hr. pH of the reaction mixture was monitored at every 2 hours and was maintained at pH 7 using IN NaOH solution. Example 2
Preparation of cis,cis-3-(methylcarbonyloxy)-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy) cyclo-
hexylacetate
Cis, cis-3-hydroxy-5-methylcarbonyloxy-cyclohexylacetate (2 parts, 9.26 mmol) and DMAP (0.113 parts, 0.926 mmol) were placed in 100 ml two-necked round bottom flask equipped with dropping funnel and two-way stopcock. It was evacuated and flushed with argon. To it, dry dichloromethane (10 parts) and dry HMPA (2 part) was added and stirred to dissolve. The solution was cooled to -10°C with stirring. To it, solution of tert-butyldimethylsilyl chloride in 10 part dry dichloromethane was added dropwise while maintaining temperature below 0°C. Reaction mixture was stirred for 15 min and to it dry triethylamine (2.02 g, 20 mmol) was added dropwise. Reaction mixture was stirred at room temperature for 12 hr. It was then transferred to a separating funnel and washed successively with cold, dil. HC1 water, aq. NaHCO3 and then brine. Organic layer was dried on anhydrous sodium sulfate and solvent was removed under vacuum. Residue was purified by flash column chromatography, (eluent 2-4% ethyl acetate in petroleum ether) to yield Cis,cis-3-
(methylcarbonyloxy)-5-(tert.butyldimethylsilyloxy)cyclohexylacetate ( yield 2.85 parts, 90%).
