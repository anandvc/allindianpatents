Title of Invention

A MODULAR DISPLAY UNIT FOR ADVERTISING
Abstract ABSTRACT A modular display unit for advertising comprising of number of boxes (1,3 & 4) mounted one over the other, each boxes comprising advertising message / pictures printed front, back and sides of the boxes, the boxes are connected with the help of molded side connectors or support (2) so that each support enters the lower and upper boxes up to projection level, upper box is supported on the said projection to make seamless unit, the bottom box fitted with molded 'T' stands (5) to form modular unit.

FEILD OF INVENTION
The present invention relates to an externally multi-divided display to enable printing and branding on it on all sides. This means printing on the front, the back, and the sides of the display. This display is made out of art card, duplex board, or other printable plastic material.
PRIOR-ART
A number of other displays are known to be available, which allows a customer of consumer to view the display. Typically these displays are oval shaped, and are foldable in nature. These displays are mainly constructed of art card, to allow the print to be viewed on the front and back. Typically this folded displays automatically springs to shape when opened, resembles a column, is double sided and elliptical (oval) in shape.
However, a number of disadvantages exist with these structures. For example, since the sides of an oval is nonexistent, there cannot be any printing. Secondly, the unit is non-flexible in nature That is, there cannot be any extensions in height if required in an already manufactured display. Likewise, there are other disadvantages too, in these available units.
Thus a need for an improved and advanced multi-divided display exists.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
A modular display unit for advertising comprising of number of boxes, mounted one over the other each boxes comprising advertising message / pictures, as desired by the client are printed front, back and sides of the boxes, the boxes are connected with the help of molded side connectors or support (2) so that each support enters the lower and upper boxes up to projection level and upper box is supported on the said projection to make seamless unit, the bottom box fitted with molded'T' stands to form modular unit.


Detail Description with reference to the accompanying Drawing
Figs 1-4 are a cross sectional view of modular advertising display according to the present invention.
Fig 5 is a rectangular box made of art cardboard or plastic.
Fig 6 is an Injection molded supports to fit top & bottom of the rectangular boxes.
Fig-7 is an injection molded Stand for the base (T).
Fig-8 is the complete form of modular display unit.
The modular advertising display chosen to illustrate the present invention comprises the following steps as illustrated in Fig D-G;
Firstly, the T-Type base stand (5) is placed at the bottom of the rectangular box (1,3,4) and placed on the ground (As illustrated in Fig. D)
Secondly the supports (2) are then placed on top of the rectangular box. (As illustrated in Fig. E)
Third step is another rectangular box is placed on top of these supports (As illustrated in Fig. F)
Likewise similar levels are placed one upon the other, based on the height required (As illustrated in Fig G)
A modular display unit wherein the side supports (2) have center projection in perpendicular direction to its surface so that each support enters the lower and upper boxes up to projection level and the upper box is supported on the said projection, the side supports (2) inter-connects any two adjoining boxes.
A modular display unit wherein the width of the side support (2) is same as or less than thickness of the box it supports, the supports are made of plastics, fiber, wood or metal.
A modular display unit wherein the stand is made of plastic, fiber wood or metal.
Each stand comprises a group contacting piece and a vertical piece, which enters into the
lowest box (4).
A modular display unit wherein the width of vertical piece is same as or less than the width of the display box; each display box is connected with the adjoining display box by two side supports (2), display message and or pictures are printed on display boxes.

A modular display unit wherein the bottom most display box is supported on two 'T-stands (5), each stand has a small projection at the center in perpendicular direction to its surface for supporting the bottom most display box (4)
