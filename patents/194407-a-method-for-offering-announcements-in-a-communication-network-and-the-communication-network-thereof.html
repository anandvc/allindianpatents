Title of Invention

"A METHOD FOR OFFERING ANNOUNCEMENTS IN A COMMUNICATION NETWORK AND THE COMMUNICATION NETWORK THEREOF"
Abstract A method and communication network wherein one or more announcement texts (ANT) are established in a service control point (8CP). A message (IDP) initiated on the basis of a call of the subscriber (A) wherein information about the supportabi1ity of announcements by an announcement unit (IP) are contained is received and interpreted by the service control point (SCP). Given support of an announcement by an announcement unit (IP-A-IP ) , a message (PAP) in which the announcement text (ANT) is contained is sent from the service control point (SCP). The announcement unit(IP-A-IP) converts a received announcement text (ANT) into an announcement that is then transmitted to the calling subscriber (A) on a speed channel (SPCH).
