Title of Invention

TWIN SHEET LOCKING JOINT SYSTEM FOR PUFF FILLED PRODUCTS
Abstract Twin Sheet Locking Joint System For Puff Filled Products consists inner vessel (1) and outer vessel (2). Both vessel are jointed by bending the inner vessel in a 'Z' shape angle of different size and shape fixing it with outer vessel on round bedding and locking it from the top in round and flat shape sea mining joint. Twin sheet locking joint system for puff filled product storing the food, milk and the drugs in safe condition for a long period of time. Top lid is fitted directly in the inner vessel to make it airtight. Inner vessel is totally open from the top, cleaning become very easy. TWIN SHEET LOCKING JOINT SYSTEM FOR PUFF FILLED PRODUCTS is available in different capacity.

The following specification particularly describes the nature of the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed.



The present invention relates to TWIN SHEET LOCKING JOINT FOR PUFF FILLED PRODUCTS. This invention relates to the field of the protection of food such as filled snakes, milk, drugs and the like. Protection of this type consists of a base, such as a casing or envelope made of puff to which a filling or top layer must be added.
The filling operation must be performed which complies with the requirements laid down in the food act on food hygiene. These conditions relate to use correct raw materials, refrigeration for freezing, heating the foods to the right temperatures, storage, the cleaning of equipment etc. All these requirements are needed for maintaining 'he desired standards of hygiene. The object of the invention is to enable to carry out the manufacture of such products in more efficient manner, for complicate of the said requirements concerning hygiene.
In the conventional puff filled product, top lid is not airtight as the inner vessel is hanged with the small height round pipe, top cover is kept loose on this and the puff material could not be provided on the lid due to this heat loss is there and these it's low efficient. The mouth of this vessel is very small so it becomes difficult to clean it and is not convenient to put thick food material. Inner vessel could not provide the big size puff product due to its small mouth opening. Small puff filled vessel with two inner vessels and outer vessel has limitation of heights so, it is not used by the hotels, canteen and caterers etc.. Water filter is not available in
