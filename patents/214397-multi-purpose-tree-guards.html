Title of Invention

"MULTI-PURPOSE TREE GUARDS"
Abstract This invention relates to a tree guard. The tree guard has a panel/frame made of hollow horizontal and vertical members secured with each other. Openings are provided in the top horizontal member for providing water in the said structure. Pickets are provided with the bottom horizontal member for fixing said panel into the ground. A hollow drive down pipe is provided with the bottom end of said panel such that to provide water near the roots of the tree. Connectors are provided removably at the ends of horizontal members for connecting said panels with the other panels so as to form the tree guard.
Full Text This invention relates to a multi purpose tree guard.
In the known art the small. 1 trees are saved from cattle's by providing fencing made of bricks or by placing a drum having the holes therein. Further a metallic structure having side walls made of wiremesh which also used -for guarding the small trees from cattle's.
There are disadvantages associated with the tree guards known in the art* One of main disadvantage is that these tree guards, do not have means to provide drip feeding of the water near the roots of the tree.
Another disadvantage is that the size of the tree guards is fixed during the manufacturing of the tree guard and the size cannot be changed as par the requirements of the user.
Another disadvantage is that transportation of the tree guards is difficult.
Therefore the main object of this invention is to provide a navel construction of the tree guard which obviates the disarivctntagea associated in the known art.
Another object of this invention is to propose a novel tree guard having means for drip •feeding a-f the water near the roots of the tree and wastage of the water is avoided.
Yet another object o-f this invention is to propose a tree guard whose size can be adjusted as per the requirements cvf the user.
Still another object of this invention is to propose a navel tree guard in the form of a panels to make it easily transportable.
