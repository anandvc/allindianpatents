Title of Invention

AN ATTACHMENT FOR AUTOMATICALLY STOPPING THE ENGINE (ENGINE KA CHOWKIDAR)
Abstract An attachment for automatically stopping the engine characterized by the fact that it comprises ; (a) a body made up of suitable material which can be removeably fixed near the cooling block of the engine; the said body being made up of two parts, lower part (I) and Upper Part (2). (b) a bladder (Bl) having an opening at one end, the said opening being matching with the opening at the lower part of the body; (c) a spring loaded plunger (SI) being placed over the said bladder (Bl). (d) a spring loaded plunger (S2) placed in an housing being integrally fixed with the upper part (2) of the body. (e) a nipple pipe (PI) which is inserted in the bladder at one end and at the other end is secured with the lower part (1) of the body through a check nut (Cl). /
Full Text Field of Invention :
The present invention relates to an attachment for automatically stopping the engine. The engine specifically can have an attachment which will control the speed of the engine in all adverse conditions when used in farms or fields.
Prior Art:
For irrigation purposes, the farmer requires water and for that the farmer depends on number of sources like rains, rivers, canals can solve the purpose. Now a days centrifugal pumps, motors, motor with engine can be used for supply water. In the agricultural fields where there is abundance of water due to water table in ground or due to canals or river flowing nearby, engine used with pumps is a remarkable achievement of the past days. These engines are mainly diesel engines, which run a tubewell and ofcourse which is having a fan. Now as fan rotates due to suction created water can be sucked from the ground and can be supplied to the field.
Disadvantages of the prior art:
(1) Sometimes pipes which contain water to cool engine parts get worn out or sometimes
this pipe is thrown away from engine due to back pressure, however engine
continuously runs in adverse condition with out being cooled. This makes engine hot
and it ceases.
(2) Sometimes belt connecting engine and tube well gets disconnected due to slippage
and other factors. This causes the load on the engine to reduce and makes the engine
to run at very high speeds causing unbalanced force which makes the engine loose on
the foundation.
(3) Sometimes the fan, which is used, for sucking the water does not rotates properly and
therefore the amount of water to cool the engine is reduced thereby causing the
heating of engine.

Because of above said dangers the farmer must Sit near the engine, some times the farmer also was accidented because of stopping the overspeed of engine. It was a big problem to the farmer which has no complete solution yet

WORKING SYSTEM OF ATTACHMENT -
To keep cool the running Engine, it is linked with an Input Tube through a Pipe, which keeps the Engine Block cool regularly. The pressure of Blowing Water is joined with the Attachment to body of the Engine on T-Pipe (4). This water pressure is going inside the Bladder (Bl) through flexible Plastic Pipe (3), Nipple (P1) and Check Nut (Cl). When Bladder (Bl) swells with water pressure, then iron Plate (S1) use up and this works as a signal force. When Control of On/Off Race (5) is pulled back, then iron rode (S2) also pulled with it and after this the signal force/iron Plate (S1) stands in front of Power Force. The control of On/Off Race (5) is connected with fuel pump of the Engine. Whenever any problem/danger occurs then after the water pressure become low. Due to the low water pressure, the bladder (Bl) fell down in opposition because of signal force/iron Plate (S1) and at the same time the power force stopped the Engine 100% in 1-2 Second in short time and the farmer saved from the heavy loss, because all danger/problems of Tubewell Engine depends on this blowing water of Block which is controlled by this attachment for Automatically stopping the Engine.

8 An attachment as claimed in Claim 1 wherein the spring tension of the plunger (S1) can be adjusted by the two screws (6,7) which are provided on the upper portion of the said body.
9. An attachment as claimed in claim 1 wherein the two parts of the body, lower

suitable material.
