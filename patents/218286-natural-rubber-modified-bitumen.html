Title of Invention

NATURAL RUBBER MODIFIED BITUMEN
Abstract The present invention relates to Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen using crumb rubber powder obtained from ammoniated field latex, heat stabilizing agents, anti-oxidants, curing agents and rubber chemical dispersions.
Full Text FIELD OF THE INVENTION
This invention relates to a Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) prepared from Crumb Rubber Powder produced from ammoniated field latex. The Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) described herein is useful in industrial applications such as road paving.
More specifically the subject invention relates to Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) prepared from ammoniated field latex, heat stabilizing agents, antioxidants, curing agents and rubber chemical dispersions.
Natural rubber has been widely employed in industrial articles such as automobile tires, belts and adhesives and domestic articles such as gloves.
In addition to its excellent mechanical properties as vulcanized rubber, natural rubber is a much superior in the raw rubber strength (green strength) to synthetic rubbers. Accordingly, natural rubber is excellent in processing characteristics in kneading, sheeting and various molding procedures. In the form of a latex, natural rubber has a high gel strength of solidification and thus can be easily formulated into a film, which makes natural rubber applicable to various products including condoms, surgical gloves and diverse catheters.

For some time there have been discussions on the use of free-flowing rubber powders which allow rubber mixtures to be processed as simply and rapidly as thermoplastic powders or pallets.
The preparation of the rubbers usually used in the rubber industry, via polymerization of various monomers, is generally divided into two fundamentally different processes:
a) Polymerization in water (Emulsion Polymerization)
Known rubbers present in or prepared in water are natural rubber (NR), emulsion styrene-butadiene rubber (ESBR), nitrite-butadiene rubber (NBR) and chloroprene rubber (CR).
b) Polymerization in Organic Solvents
Examples of important rubbers prepared in organic solvents are styrene-butadiene rubber based on solution polymerization (SSBR), butadiene rubber (BR), butyl rubbers, halobutyl rubbers, and also ethylene-propylene rubbers with (EPDM) or without (EPM) a copolymerized tercomponent.
It was observed that natural rubber can not compete successfully with synthetic rubber which have specific characteristics e.g. nitrite rubber being excellent in oil resistance.
The importance for using modified bitumen was realized in the mid-90s for commercial application and to use it for improving quality of roads. Guidelines have been laid down for specifications of various types of modified bitumen

namely (1) Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB), (2) Polymer Modified Bitumen (PMB), and (3) Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB).
Conventionally bitumen used as road paving material and other commercial applications are manufactured using the synthetic or crumb rubber mainly due to the fact that the available natural rubber latex is highly unstable for storage and production purpose. Also the water content in the natural rubber latex is quite high. It is therefore essential that natural rubber latex should be stabilized and water contents should be removed before the same can be mixed with bitumen.
Number of attempts have been made to modify natural latex so as to impart the characteristics of other rubbers thereto so that the same can be blended with bitumen. The present invention helps in manufacturing Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) as per the specification of Indian Road Congress vide their special publication no. IRC:SP:53:2002.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
The subject invention relates to Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) prepared from ammoniated field latex, heat stabilizing agents , antioxidants, curing agents and rubber chemical dispersions.
These heat stabilizing agents are KOH solution , TDQ (Polymerised 2,2, 4-trimethyl-1,2 dilhydroquinoline ) dispersion or a mixture thereof , the curing agents are sulphur dispersions, ZDC (Zinc diethyl dithiocarbamate) dispersions, ZMBT (Zinc Salt of 2-mercapto-benzothiazole) dispersion or a mixture thereof and antioxidant is SP(Styrenated Phenol (SPH) Predominantly 2, 4-di-(alpha-phenylethyl)-phenol.) emulsion.

The process for the manufacture of a crumb rubber powder from ammoniated natural rubber latex involves the following steps:-
The material is transferred into aluminum trays (normally used for making natural rubber sheets) and put in heated oven/smoke house at the temperature between 100-110°C for curing for 5-20 hours. The semicured rubber sheets free from water are removed from the oven and are ready for grinding/powdering which again involves two steps: In first step the sheets are broken into smaller pieces in cracker grinder/rubber shredder and in the second step the small pieces of rubber are put into roller grinder/disc grinder for fine powdering of 30 Mesh to 50 Mesh size
The grinded crumb rubber can be used either directly for modification of bitumen or by making a blend of latex crumb rubber powder with natural asphalt powder The natural rubber latex crumb powder singularly or along with natural asphalt is tnen packed in HOPE bags for storage.
To Manufacture Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen (NRMB) the natural rubber latex crumb powder is blended along with the bitumen.
The bitumen of specific grades i.e.80/100 or 60/70 is taken into the mixing tank through a direct pipeline or bitumen tankers. The quantity of bitumen taken is checked through a caliberated dipping rod. The total quantity of bitumen taken is only 2/3rd of the capacity of the tank. Each tank is fitted with at least two agitators of a specific design and a pair of circulation pumps for the mixing.

The bitumen taken in the tank is heated to a temperature 150°C - 170°C under controlled conditions by means of indirect heating with steam/ thermic oil
The crumb rubber powder along with the natural asphalt is fed through a rubber conveyor at the controlled speed of 1 MT per 30 minutes approximately . The ratio of crumb rubber modifier and natural asphalt to bitumen is 6:4:90/3:2:95. The different proportions are used for making different grades of the end product i.e. Natural Rubber Modified Bitumen as per Indian Road Congress Publication
Three stirrers attached with each tank are started simultaneously while mixing the modifier and the blend is allowed to mix for a period of 1-2 hours from the time of feeding of the modifier. The stirring is done at a controlled speed of 140-195 RPM per minute to obtain the crumb rubber modified bitumen.
