Title of Invention

"BI-CENTRIC HIP DEVICE"
Abstract The invention relates to a bi-centric hip system having two components viz. a cup being made from metal and inner lining of cup being made up from ultra high molecular weight polyethylene or ceramic and second component called prosthesis which has got a head and a stem ; head being of predetermined size ,being made up from steel or ceramic and a stem with collar and neck , stem being made of steel or titanium, coated with or without hydroxyappetite ;can be articulated at the time of surgery with the help of a jig or it can come as a single piece. System can be disarticulated with disarticulated jig. Fig: F1G1

The greatest advantage of this system is that after serving for a couple of years in the human body when the system fails usually the cup compo-nent then it can be converted into total hip by reoperating. In the second surgery the cup will be dismantled and thrown out instead a new cup of the total hip system will be fitted into the ascetabu-lum with the help of cement, and the already exist-ing prosthesis with 22mm diameter head will be married to the new cup, thus reducing the time of surgery as well as the cost to a great extent.
The presently designed system has got certain advantages over the already existing bipolar hip prostheses,
The two component of the bipolar already existing are not dismantable.
The seating angle of the prosthesis is unac-ceptably low so, the surgeon have to cut a lot of calcarbone which is so disadvantageous to the patient as far as the long term results are con-cerned .
The long term results are not good because of the stiffness that develops probably due to abnor-mally high stress generated at. the acetabular
cartliage and the metallic cup level as well as the
metallic head and the polyethylene cup.
The inner ball of the presently existing
bipolar has got a diameter of 28mm which is diffi¬cult to marry with the commonly existing cup of the total hip system which has got a 22.5mm inner diameter.
The present existing bipolar system cannot be converted into a total hip system.

Accordingly the present invention describes about A Bi-centric hip device comprising
(a) A stem portion (endoprosthesis) having a distal end (1) of a predetermined
length which is straight and an opposed proximal end(2) which is
arcuately shaped; the said proximal end (2) having a collar portion (3);
said collar portion having a neck of reducing diameter; a head portion (4)
which is an extension of the said neck portion; the said head portion being
in the shape of a spheroid having a predetermined diameter; the said collar
[portion having a groove of a predetermined radius for inserting the said
stem into the hollow bone structure by way of slight hammering; the said
arcuated potion (2) having at least two through holes of predetermined
radius at predetermined positions for removing the said stem from the
hollow bone structure; the said proximal end (2) being so arcuated such
that the angle formed between the straight distal end(l) of the stem and the
opposed proximal end(2) is about 138° and the angle between the collar
(3) and the straight distal end(l) is about 40° -50° preferably 45°;
(b) A bi-centric hemispherical hollow cup (6) having a predetermined
diameter and predetermined thickness; the inner portion of the
hemispherical hollow cup having a layer of a suitable material of a
predetermined thickness; the said layer(5) being arcuately shaped so that
the resulting obstruction between the said layer and the portion between
the said neck and head portion is minimal; the said cup being adapted to
be inserted on to the head portion (4) of the stem in a manner wherein the
center of the said head portion (4) and the center of the said hollow cup(6)
is offset by about 2mm providing greater movement of the said head
portion(4) in the said cup (6) as compared to the movement of the hollow
cup(6) itself.
Accordingly the present invention also describes about a disarticulating jig for dismantling the bi-centric hip device comprising a rod portion (8) of a predetermined length; an arcuated frame portion (11) shaped to accommodate the head portion of the said stem; a threaded spindle (10) attached to a screw (9); the said spindle having soft pads (12) wherein the said frame portion (11) is adjusted over the head portion of the stem and the spindle is moved through screw(9) to hold the said head portion.
Also the present invention describes about an articulating Jig for fixing the Bi-centric hip device comprising two leg portions (14,15) having a straight distal end(17) and proximal ends(18, 19) which are arcuately shaped; the said legs (14, 15) being pivoted at a point (16) closer to the proximal end; the said proximal end (18,19) being so shaped so as to properly hold the Bi-Centric hip device on the soft pads(12).
