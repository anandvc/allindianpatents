Title of Invention

A STRUCTURAL SPHEROID FOR SYNCHRONOUSLY ROTATING HOTEL SUITS IN TWO PARALLEL VERTICAL PLANES
Abstract A structural spheroid and a method for synchronously rotating hotel suits in two parallel vertical planes Restaurants on top of skyscrapers and multi-storeyed buildings are revolved in horizontal planes to offer panoramic view of surroundings. They suffer from drawbacks such zero horizontal mobility beyond the premise and denial of access to other amenities during the stay. The present invention relates to rotating in two vertical planes hotel suits, allowing simultaneous access to other amenities of the complex. The suits are synchronously rotated by transfer of rotational power of 2 pairs of 18 small output a.c. motors – through gear wheels on the motors – to the 2 pairs of rotating structural rings. The plurality of 10 KW motors performs the required task.
