INTRODUCTION :
Poppy Capsules are cultivated in states of U.P. Rajasthan and M.P. They are also cultivated in some other countries Generally poppy seeds and extracted opium are only used. Till now there is no specific use of poppy capsules and they are treated as waste by farmers.
I have observed some people to use it as medicine. However they were not clear about exact effects, side effects, proper dosage etc. I observed this, studies further and prepared a drug.
INVENTION HISTORY ;
I am in business of poppy seeds since 1987. Based pn my initial observation, I stared my work to make more useful poppy capsules since 1998. During this period I interviewed people who were using poppy capsules for various purposes. I studied many books about possible use of poppy capsules along with, other natural herbs. In book Bhavprakash Nighantu, I found more usage of poppy capsules.
I tried and tested formula depicted in Bhavprakash Nighantu but find it not the best due to following reasons:
a. It makes the person addict to use of poppy capsule juice.
b. It tests bitter.
c. It gives quick excitation but it doesn't stay longer.

I targeted my research to over come these problems. I consulted many vaidyas, ayurvedic doctors researchers to get more information on similar herbs and herbs to reduce the addictions of poppy capsules. I prepared my own formula and consulted some people possessing vaidya visharad degree, Further.I asked some people, who were already taking capsules as per bhavprakash Nighantu's formula. They found my formula much better. It has been liked due to its delicious taste. Manglam Powder has been approved by F.D.A. Bhopal (M.P.) vide Drug license No.

5. DESCRIPTION:
All the medicines used in preparation are natural and safe. Manglam Powder is an oral therapy for pain, Cough, diarrhea, sexual deficiency, tension anxiety etc. It is yellow colored powder with a specific color of cardamoms.


6. INDICATION:
For the treatment of sexual deficiency, cold, cough, pain, diarrhea, tension, anxiety.
7. USES & PROPERTIES :
It is very useful for a person one who has lost his sexual activity or less enjoyed in sex or time decrease in sexual activity. It increases excitation, Sexual Power & provide vitality. Person enjoyed more time in sexual activity. It is useful in treatment of pain, early stage of cold, cough, tension, anxiety and more useful for diarrhea in a child at a stage of teeth raise.
8. DOSAGES & ADMINISTRATION ;
I. FOR INCREASE SEXUAL ACTIVITY :
The recommended dose is take 3 tea spoonful "Manglam Powder" and pour it in a glass containing 250 ml warm water & leave it for half an hour or more, then shake well 10 minutes. After this filter with a clear cloth piece. Draining the solid material & take filtrate orally before 1 hour of sexual activity. Based on effectiveness & toleration the dose may be increased to maximum recommended dose 5 tea spoonful in 250 ml warm water.
II. FOR OTHER TREATMENT ;
The recommended dose is take 3 spoon full powder in 100ml warm water and lave it 2-3 hours shake it 15 minutes interval and then filter with clean cloth. Use filtrate solution. Tae 3-4 tea spoon full for adult and 1-2 spoonful for child before going to bed.
