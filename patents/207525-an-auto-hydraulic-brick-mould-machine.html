This description explains and specifies the nature of this new machine, its performance and working.
This machine is used to fix the pressed material in to shaped bricks which is made up with the help of clay mixture or fly ash or stone powder and similar materials.
At present, as known in the state of art, bricks of cutting with the help of a wire the existing machine is know as wire cut brick machine. In the existing system, the material is pressed into a narrow shaped wooden die while the material coming out like a cake form that is cut with the help of wire and preserved under a roof to dry.

Another type of existing hydraulic machines being used in this fields or hallow blocks field or press the semi - solid material with high pressure at a sudden movement. The bricks prepared by this way is mechanically operated and there have been not time limit. In some cases the sudden pressure is given by a hydraulic cylinder in between fabricated frames (or) with in graded materials. This way large quantity of material pressed and shaped into small brick from the output is almost dry and suitable because of dry tight pressure packing.
The main drawback of these machines is that there are not suitable for all type of clay. It can only be used for a particular soil, which are available in particular districts in India.
In these machines one must use only semi - solid material (i.e. lightly water mixed) if the material is over dried or diluted there will be no result.
Further, in the machines oil is used to polish the bricks at the same time used as lubricant for free flow of materials.

The sliding box has two plates horizontally one plate is thin (15) another is thick (16). These two plates are welded with one vertical heavy plate (17) on the center of this

INJECTION BOX:
Injection box is a fabricated hollow shaped welded box is to feed the materials in to the main box. It has a door gap (22) and a plate shutter. The door gap have a gap to allow the clay mixed material to be filled in the box. A plate (20) press the material with help of cylinder (SI) and the limit switches LI and L2 control the to and from motion of plate 20. A lubrication system (23) is placed in the front edge of the box.
A rectangular slot is made on the side of injection box for positioning the limit switch at the desired locations as per density of clay used.
WORKING SYSTEM:
STAGE ONE:
In the initial stage the main box auxiliary box and sliding box are over lapped with one another. In this initial stage two brick shaped hollow space provided in between the three boxes.
At first grinded clay material is pour in to the door gap (22). Then the gap closed manually now electronic control panel system gives on signal to limit switch (LI). Now plate (20) injects material.
Into Al, A2 side of the main and auxiliary box which results the Material to form brick shape in the lower portion of auxiliary box. After feeding material control panel gives ON signal to limit switch (L2) which pulls plate (20) backward by SI cylinder.

STAGE TWO
Now control panel gives ON signal two limit switch (L3) which pulls ram and declamp the sliding box by cylinder (S2) now control panel gives ON signal to limit switch (L4) which operate cylinder (S3) and pull the auxiliary box upwards. Now the bricks are release from all arrested surfaces and get down to a conveyor surface. While moving the conveyor shift the bricks to drying shed for drying purpose.
STAGE THREE:
Now the control panel gives ON singal to limit switch (L5) which press the auxiliary box downwards by cylinder S3 after auxiliary box set in its original position control panel give ON signal to limit switch (L6) which moves the sliding box into its original position. By this way a same process carry over continuously by automatic electronic switches and electronic timers the inner surface of auxiliary box and inner surface of injecting box are lubricated by oil or water continuously special tubes and circulation system (23) is used to spray this liquid in the inner surface. Some holes and key ways are provided in the particular boxes for lubrication.
