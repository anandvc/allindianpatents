Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED WEAR PROTECTION DEVICE AND A METHOD FOR HANGER TUBES IN BUBBLING FLUIDIZED BED COMBUSTION (BFBC) BOILERS
Abstract Accordingly, there is provided an improved method of strap support in hanger tube for wear protection in BFBC boilers comprising a hot air distributor plate disposed at the bottom boundary of the combustor enclosed by a membrane, said plate having a plurality of air nozzles, a hot air source for continuous supply of hot air through said nozzles into the fluidized bed, a first super heater coil immersed in the bed of the boiler; a second super heater coil above the bed supported by a plurality of hanger tubes; and a tube assembly comprising a tube, straps and vertical sleeves fitted over the tube.
