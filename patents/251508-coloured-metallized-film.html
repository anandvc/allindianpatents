Title of Invention

COLOURED METALLIZED FILM
Abstract This invention relates to a composition and the processes for the preparation of Coloured plastic film laminates, characterized in that the composition comprises color lacquer coated laminates with a structure consisting of 3 layers namely polymer Film, Aluminium Foil and color lacquer coating.
