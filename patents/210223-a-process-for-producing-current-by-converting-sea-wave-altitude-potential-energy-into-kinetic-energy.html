Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING CURRENT BY CONVERTING SEA WAVE ALTITUDE POTENTIAL ENERGY INTO KINETIC ENERGY
Abstract In sea, wave occurs due to raise of water against gravitation, that is raising of heavy mass alive a few seconds of time and moving towards seashore. The energy require to rise ofseawater in sea wave is named as Sea wave altitude potential energy. That energy is available due to various atmosphere pressure is produce, that energy is executed as sea wave at edges of ocean/at sea shore. That Potential energy can be converted into kinetic energy and used to rotate the axle. There is no wave which come across the ball, the energy is zero. The energy in this state is ground state. There is a wave which come across the ball, the energy in this state is not zero. The sea wave come across the ball and the ball get moved upward to maintain the ground state / to maintain the low energy state. Hence the ball moves up and the corresponding rope and chain also moves so that the axle also rotates.

3. CONVERSION OF ENERGY IN LAB EXPERIMENT
The apparatus are minimum weight, chain, free wheel, main axle, dynamo, rope, wheel and ball are setup in a water tank as shown in fig.no.3.
The^water pumping device pumped in through the inlet and after some time the same quantity of water is pumped out through the out let. So when pumping device work the water surfer is oscillated vertically, from L^ to L^. (Other wise the whole apparatus are setup on pool connected with water wave producing machine.)
The free wheel is not allow to rotate temporarily, that is ball is fixed position. Now the pump device is working, water level is increased from point L^ to L^, the ball immerced in water, Now the ball has potential energy. The free wheel is allow to rotate, the ball is moving towards upward direction. So the ball travels high potential energy state to low potential energy state, the decreased potential energy is converted to kinetic energy, the kinetic energy is converted to electric energy.
