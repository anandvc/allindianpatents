Title of Invention

ROLLING MILL FINISHING SECTION
Abstract A single strand hot rolling mill finishing section comprising: a plurality of multi-stand prefinishing rolling units (10, 12) positioned along a first pass line segment (P1); transfer means (14) for selectively shifting said prefinishing rolling units into and out of operative positions on said first pass line segment; a multi-stand finishing rolling unit (16) and a multi-stand post finishing rolling unit (18) positioned sequentially along a second pass line segment(Pl); and switch means (26) and associated delivery guides (28, 30) for directing products from said first pass line segment to said second pass line segment either at a location (32) upstream of said finishing rolling unit, or alternatively at a location (34) between said finishing rolling unit and said post finishing rolling unit, said transfer means and said switch means and associated delivery guides being selectively operable to precede final rolling in said post finishing rolling unit with or without rolling in said finishing rolling unit and with or without rolling in one or more of said prefinishing rolling units.

BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
1. Field of the Invention
10 This invention relates to continuous single strand hot rolling mills of the type producing
bar and rod products, and is concerned in particular with an improved layout of the rolling equipment in the finishing section of the mill.
2. Description of the Prior Art
15 In a typical continuous hot rolling mill of the type producing bar and rod products,
billets are heated in a furnace and are then rolled in roughing and intermediate sections of the mill before being directed to the mill finishing section. The finishing section does what its name suggests, namely, to finish roll products in various sizes and to prescribed tolerances.
Typical mill finishing sections include one or more multi-stand rolling units with 20 associated water boxes, shears, pinch rolls, etc. Conventional arrangements of such equipment impose undue restrictions on the range of finished products that can be efficiently produced firom a selected size of feed stock supplied by the intermediate null section.

D-1175
The finishing mill layout of the present invention is largely free of the such restrictions, thus providing the mill operator with the ability to efficiently roll a wide range of finished products from a selected feed stock size.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
In accordance with the present invention, multi-stand rolling units are aligned on
different pass line segments. At least some of the rolling units are shiftable onto and off of
10 their respective pass Ime segments, and switch mechanisms are operable in concert with the
selective positioning of the shiftable rolling units to accommodate the rolling of a broad range
of finished product sizes from a single feed stock.
