CENTRE LESS RUBBER COT ARBOUR HOLDING & GUIDING DEVICE
Abstract Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device for automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine in which arbour holding and guiding unit/head provided multiple such as two, three, four, six, eight and even more numbers on a rotary table for simultaneously receiving unfinished rubber cot arbour form the measuring unit, feeding the unfinished arbour to the grinding unit and unloading the finished rubber cot arbour to the finished rubber cot arbour storage unit of Automatic rubber cot arbour Grinding/buffing machine.

The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed:



This invention relates to Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device for automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine and an automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine comprising the same.
More particularly this invention provides centre less rubber cot arbour holding and guiding device, in which multiple units/head have been provided for holding and guiding the rubber cot arbour to the grinding head, for grinding as well as for unloading the finished rubber cot arbour into the finished rubber cot arbour storage unit of the rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine. While one of the unit hold measured rubber cot arbour received from the measuring unit, second will wait for feeding the rubber cot arbour to the grinding unit, the third will be engaged with the grinding unit for grinding the rubber cot arbour and the forth will unload the finished rubber cot arbour storage unit.
Rubber cot arbour mainly comprises a steel spindle or axle provided with one or two rubber cots used as top rollers in Ring Frame, Speed Frame, Draw Frame, Combers, etc. of cotton/synthetic yarn spinning mills, in the textile industries.
2

The arbours or top rollers used in Draw Frames or Combers are provided with single long cot upto 400 mm in length in the middle of the axle portion, while the arbours or top rollers used in Ring Frames or Speed Frames are provided with a pair of cots, one each at the two ends of the axle. The cots are preferably made of rubber. A very large number of top rollers or rubber cot arbours are used in a Spinning mill/machine, which work continuously at a high speed.
Regular grinding of the rubber cot arbours is required for ensuring quality of yarn by reducing thread breaks and minimizing lap formation. Generally centreless grinding is used for rubber cot arbours of Ring Frames and Speed Frames and 'with centre grinding' is used for Draw Frames and Comber Cots.
Manual rubber cot arbour loading and unloading device for manual rubber cot arbour grinding/ buffing machines are known in the prior art. This manual rubber cot arbour loading and unloading device is prone to accidents during loading and unloading of the rubber cot arbour in the grinding unit.
3

Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device are also known and used in the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machines sold in the marked under the brand name 'BERKOL' Â® manufactured by 'HUBER + SUHNER'. The existing Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device is having low performance as it contains the single unit for receiving unfinished rubber cot arbour from the measuring unit, and the same unit is used for feeding the unfinished to the grinding head/unit and then for unloading the finished rubber cot arbour to the finished rubber cot arbour storage unit of the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine. Thus resulting into lower performance of the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine.
The main object of this invention is to provide Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device for automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine in which multiple arbour holding and guiding unit/head have been provided on a rotary table for simultaneously receiving unfinished rubber cot arbour form the measuring unit, feeding the unfinished arbour to the grinding unit and unloading the finished rubber cot arbour to the finished

A further object of this invention to provide Centre Less Rubber cot arbour holding & guiding device which provides a smooth automatic function while increasing the performance of the Automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine.
