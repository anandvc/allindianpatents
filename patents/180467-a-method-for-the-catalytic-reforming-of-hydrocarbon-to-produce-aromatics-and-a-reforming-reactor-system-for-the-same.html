Title of Invention

A METHOD FOR THE CATALYTIC REFORMING OF HYDROCARBON TO PRODUCE AROMATICS AND A REFORMING REACTOR SYSTEM FOR THE SAME
Abstract Disclosed is a method for reforming hydrocarbons comprising contacting the hydrocarbons with a catalyst in a reator system of improved resistance to carburization and metal dusting under conditions of low sulfur.
