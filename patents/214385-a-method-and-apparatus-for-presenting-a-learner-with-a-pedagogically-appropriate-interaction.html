Title of Invention

A METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR PRESENTING A LEARNER WITH A PEDAGOGICALLY APPROPRIATE INTERACTION
Abstract Computer-aided education systems and methods provide a learner with a pedagogically appropriate interaction, including determining learner information . A state for the interaction is determined based on the determined learner information . A concept pattern is created based on the determined state . The appropriate interaction is then accessed based on the concept pattern and the determined state.
