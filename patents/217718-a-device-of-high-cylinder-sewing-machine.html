Title of Invention

"A DEVICE OF HIGH-CYLINDER SEWING MACHINE"
Abstract A device of high-cylinder sewing machine comprising: a first high-cylinder fixed on the assembly base connecting with a second high- cylinder on one end, and fitted with an alignment shaft and acentric shaft; a second high-cylinder fitted with a groove and fork plate at upper and lower portion respectively, wherein the groove is opposite to alignment washer, and fork plate is opposite to acentric shaft; said first high cylinder and second high cylinder is fastened by means of the combination of alignment washer and groove, acentric shaft and fork plate,
Full Text Description:
Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to a device of high-cylinder sewing
machine, especially refer to a high cylinder which can be alternated adequately and conveniently in accord with the size of sewing materials.
Background of the Invention
A conventional high-cylinder type sewing machine uses proper type of cylinder (shown as Fig.1) to stitch various sizes of bag. The needle base 13 contains spindle with needle plate 14 at side, fixed on the high-cylinder 12, and form a free rotate needle base by the axis of spindle
shaft.
Assembly base 10 fixed on the shaft center holds the "C" shape high-cylinder 12 in order to rotate the "C" shape high-cylinder 12 freely, and arrange wheel 27 in the inner side to translate the rotate movement (not shown) into vertical movement and finally reach spindle; whereby the "C" shape part of high-cylinder of various brand of high-cylinder sewing machine is built in a solid structure and form into an independent sewing machine; therefore, various kinds of sewing machine has to be prepared to meet the requirements of stitching processing or different stitching materials, and cause idle machines and spending the alternating time.
Accordingly, changing the high-cylinder part of conventional high-cylinder sewing machine including assembly base 10 into another type has to be replaced in whole.
In addition, the replacement will cause time-consuming, labor losing, and inefficiency due to the clumsy body of these rotating high-cylinder type sewing machine bigger than usual high-cylinder type sewing machine.
Summary of the invention
It is therefore a primary object of the present invention to provide a high-cylinder sewing machine by which replacement of the part of high-cylinder without pushing down the sewing machine. It is further object of present invention to provide a high-cylinder sewing machine by which the rotating high-cylinder designed into separable parts of high-cylinder A and high-cylinder B; whereas high-cylinder A used to transmit the rotation movement from lower shaft and translate into vertical rotation by means of right angle translation mechanism forms the horizontal high-cylinder unit; whereby high cylinder B support the rotation transmission mechanism to reach the spindle.
The designation of unitary high cylinder mainly arranges an alignment washer and fork plate on the driving shaft to assemble two high-cylinder units into correct position.
Therefore, the further object of the present invention of rotating high-cylinder sewing machine is to make high-cylinder replacement in more convenient and economic efficiency.
The present invention therefore provides a device of high-cylinder sewing machine comprising:
a first high-cylinder fixed on the assembly base connecting with a second high-cylinder on one end, and fitted with an alignment shaft and acentric shaft;
a second high-cylinder fitted with a groove and fork plate at upper and lower portion respectively, wherein the groove is opposite to alignment washer, and fork plate is opposite to acentric shaft; said first high cylinder and second high cylinder is fastened by means of the combination of alignment washer and groove, acentric shaft and fork plate.
