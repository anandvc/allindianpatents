Title of Invention

A COMPACT PARALLEL GRIPPER
Abstract The parallel griper according to the invention comprising a housing (4) and two adjustable gripper base jaws (10, 12) which are displacably guided in a jaw guiding recess (8). The base jaws (10, 12) are moved relative to one another by wedge hook gearing (28) that is adjusted by a piston (30) guided in the housing (4). The jaw guiding recess (8) is provided with complementary configured guiding grooves (18). The ratio of the wall thickness (E) of the housing (4) between the base of the guiding grooves (18) and the outside of the housing (4) to the width (A) of the housing (4) measured vertically in relation to the direction of sliding (6) of the base jaws (10,12) is at least 0.2. The ratio of the width of angular power transmission of the wedge hook gearing (20) to the external width (B) of the base jaws (18) is at least 0.4.
