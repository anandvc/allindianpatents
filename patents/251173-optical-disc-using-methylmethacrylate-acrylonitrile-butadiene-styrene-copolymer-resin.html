Title of Invention

OPTICAL DISC USING METHYLMETHACRYLATE-ACRYLONITRILE-BUTADIENE-STYRENE COPOLYMER RESIN
Abstract An optical disc comprises an active disc in which information is recorded and a dummy disc for protecting the active disc, wherein the active disc and the dummy disc are bonded to form a single body, wherein the dummy disc is made of methylmethacrylate-acry-lonitrile-butadiene-styrene copolymer (MABS) resin.
