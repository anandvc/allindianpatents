Title of Invention

STEAM TURBINE WITH A PLURALITY OF STAGE SECTIONS AND A DEVICE FOR OUTPUT CONTROL OF THE STEAM TURBINE
Abstract The invention relates to a multistage steam turbine (1) having a second MD control wheel (27), which is situated behind the first stage part (7) of the turbine and which can be subjected to the action of additional steam (31) during overload operation. The inventive steam turbine (1) can be operated both in the partial load range as well as at the nominal point and which a high overload with a uniformly high efficiency.
