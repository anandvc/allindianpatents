Title of Invention

PRINTING UNIT AND INKING UNIT
Abstract The invention relates to a printing unit having at least one press unit, the or each press unit having at least one forme cylinder, a transfer cylinder, an inking unit and, preferably, a damping unit, the inking unit of each press unit applying printing ink to a forme cylinder of the respective press unit in such a way that a ductor roll of an inking unit picks up printing ink kept ready in an ink fountain and applies it to the forme cylinder of the respective press unit via a film roll interacting with the ductor roll, via inking unit rolls arranged downstream of the film roll and via at least one ink applicator roll rolling on the forme cylinder. According to the invention, within each inking unit, inking unit rolls (17) driven in rotation and inking unit rolls (18, 19, 31, 32, 33) drive side.
