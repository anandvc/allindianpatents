STORAGE VESSEL SYSTEM
Abstract (57) Abstract: A storage vessel system comprising an external drum having a perforated cover and snuggly fitted lid and an inner drum, the said inner drum being adapted to hold a storage pot, the said ceramic storage pot, having a ceramic cover housing inside the inner dram and rests over it or. its rim, a water pump and pipe connection for circulation of watar within the system, an exhaust fan fitted with top cover of the outer vessel drawing air contained inside and throwing it to open atmosphere the system being such that the clay pot being porous, the heat is drawn from the product is stored and the temperature is thereby reduced and maintained at a fairly low level.

PRIORART :
This Invention relates to the field of mechanlcal and fabrication technology. In particular, this Invention relates to the construction of a storage vessel system which is very effective In preserving the products to be stored.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION :
The primary object of the invention is to construct a storage vessel system which is very effective in preserving the product.
It Is another object of the Invention Is to construct a storage vessel system which has bw capital investment and low working cost
BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION :
A storage vessel system comprising an external drum having a perforated cover and snuggly fitted lid, and an inner drum, the said inner drum being adapted to hold a storage pot; the said ceramic storage pot; having a ceramic cover housing inside the inner drum and nests over it on Its rim, a water pump and pipe connection for circulation of water within the system, an exhaust fan fitted with top cover of the outer vessel drawing air contained Inside and throwing It to open atmosphere the system being such that the clay pot being porous, the heat is drawn from the product is started and the temperature is thereby reduced and maintained at a fairly low level. The said storage pot is the subject of copending application no. 1290/MAS/96.

DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION :
The storage vessel system according to this Invention Includes the storage vessel alongwith the storage pot which contains the product that needs to be stored. Though the description Includes the storage pot as part of the Invention, the storage pot Is subject of application no. 1290/M/AS/96.
The storage vessel system comprises an external drum(4), made out of 18 SWG Galvanised M.S Sheet of sl2se 750 mm dia, 900 mm long, with provision for cold water lntet(5). A perforated lid (3) having central top(l) portion fits tightly to this drum at Its top end. An exhaust Fan (2) is fitted on to central top. Concentric to the external drum(4), an inner drum(7) made out of Galvanised Iron Sheet of size 450 mm dia x 525 mm long Is suspended using tie supports(9). This Inner drum (7) has provision for water Inlet(lO) at one end and an outlet pipe(ll) is brazed at 180 (degree) apart to this inlet port which extends approximately 75 mm from the outer surface of the external drum(4). The joints between the outlet pipe and the drums are brazed to have a leak tight arrangement The outer drum has pair of a handles (6) riveted to It A storage pot /vessel(8), made out of clay, with a clay cover Is housed Inside the Inner drum(7) and rests over It on Its rim(13). An annular clearance (14) of about 15 mm between the Inner drum(7) and the storage pot/vessel(8) Is maintained for free flow of cold circulating water. A water pump (Monobloc) of 0.25 HP rating Is used for forced circulation of cooling water and flow Is regulated by using gate valve (12) In its delivery pipe line.

The product to be stored is kept inside the safd storage pot (8) and the perforated lid (15) of the storage pot is closed. The top lid (3) and the cover top (1) of storage vessel ane fitted In position and water pipe connections from pump to the inlet and from outlet to the sump are completed. When the pump is energized, the cold water flows through the Inlet port (5) Into the external drum filing the annular space between the concentric drum and when the level reaches the Inlet port (10) of the Inner drum, the water flows Inside the Inner drum and get filled between the annular cavity of Inner drum and storage pot When the level raises and reach the outlet port (11) opening, the excess water flows out and falls Into the sump. A continuous flow of cooling water is thus established as long as the pump is on.
Meanwhile, the exhaust fen on the top cover of storage vessel is energized which draws the air contained Inside the system which Is almost saturated with water vapour above the water level and throws it out to the open atmosphere. Fresh air enters inside the vessel through the pores provided in the top lid and hence the pressure balance and the air current Is malntalned Inside the system. Due to this air current; the rate of evaporation Is Increased and the latent heat Is drawn from the storage day vessel. Storage pot being a day vessel is porous, and thereby the heat Is drawn from the product stored and the temperature is reduced and maintained at a fairly low level.
We have thus achieved as per the Invention a eco-friendly Storage vessel system which Is double layered drum, capable of holding a storage pot within,
