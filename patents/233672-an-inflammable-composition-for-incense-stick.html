Title of Invention

AN INFLAMMABLE COMPOSITION FOR INCENSE STICK
Abstract A self igniting incense stick and insect repellant stick comprising: a stick selected from incense stick and a insect repellant stick or coil and an inflammable composition coated at the tip of the said stick for ignition and a container package having both exterior sides coated with the screening composition.
Full Text The invention relates to an inflammable composition for self-igniting incense
stick.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION;
At present in order to light an incense stick or insect repellant stick we need a
match stick. Whereas, according to the present invention the incense stick or the
insect repellant stick or coil per se can be ignited without the use of a match
stick. It becomes sometimes inconvenient to light an incense stick or a insect
repellant stick/coil as we need to procure a match stick for the same. Further, the
match stick often gets extinguished before lighting a incense stick and therefore
several match sticks are sometimes required to ignite a single incense stick.
Whereas, the present invention is cost effective as no match stick is required to
light the stick.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION:
An object of this invention is to propose an inflammable formulation for .self
igniting-incense stick.
Another object of this invention is to propose an inflammable formulation for self
igniting-insect repellant stick/coil.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a process of manufacturing
inflammable formulation for a solf igniting incense stick and insert repellant stick.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a stick which is cheap and cost
effective.
BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION:
According to this invention there is provided An inflammable composition for self
igniting incense stick, wherein said glue formulation and casein formulation, said
glue formulation comprises:
Potassium chlorate 2 to 10 kg
Glue 400 to 2 kg
Glass powder 200 to 1 kg
Sulphur 280 to 1.5 kg
Rasin 20gms to 200gms
Potassium Bicromate 80gms to 400gms
Black powder 1 to 5 kg
and said casein formulation comprises of:
Potassium chlorate 2 to 12 kg
Caesin 300gms to 2 kg
Glass powder 1 to 6 kg
Potassium dichlorate 85gms to 500gms
Raisin 85gms to 500gms
Sulphur 300gms to 2 kg
Zinc white 3.5gms to 20gms
Liquid ammonia 1.5 lit. to 8 lit.
Further, according to this invention there is also provided a process of
manufacturing an inflammable formulation self igniting incense stick and an
insect repellant stick comprising:
Subjecting the stick to the step of dipping into an inflammable solution such that
only the tip of the stick is coated with the said solution, and then drying the
coated stick at room temperature.
In one of the embodiments of this invention there is provided a method for
producing caesin formulation. The method comprises the step of diluting
ammonia using water;
adding caesin and sulphur and then rasin to the said diluted ammonia to form a
mixture; and
mixing potassium dichlorate and the mixing ingredients such as glass powder,
potassium chlorate and zinc white to the said mixture;
grinding the said mixture after 1 to 2 hours for producing the formulation for
coating.
In accordance to another embodiment of this invention there is provided a
method for producing glue formulation. The method comprises the step of boiling
the glue in water;
adding potassium chlorate to the said glue solution after cooling the same to form
a mixture, and
adding the remaining ingredients such as glass powder, sulphur, rasin,
potassium dichlorate and black powder in powder form to the said mixture;
grinding the mixture to produce the formulation for coating.
The various components of the inflammable composition has a systematic effect
amongst themselves which results in quick ignition of the self igniting incense
stick and a insect repellant stick.
Moreover, due to synergism the amount of the inflammable composition required
for ignite is also very little.
The invention will now be explained in greater details with the help of the
following non-limiting examples:
Example-1
An incense and insect repellant stick is prepared by dipping the stick into
inflammable caesin solution such that only the tip of the stick is being coated with
the said solution. The caesin solution contains:
Potassium chlorate 6 kg
Caesin 1 kg
Glass powder 3 kg
Potassium Bicromate 250 gms
Rasin 250 gms
Sulphur 1 kg
Zinc white 10 gms
Ammonia liquid 4 lits
After the step of coating the stick is dried at room temperature and then it is
packed into the boxes which have the screening solution coated at the sides for
lighting the sticks.
Example 2:
An incense and insect repellant stick is prepared by dipping the stick into
inflammable glue solution such that only the tip of the stick is being coated with
the said solution. The glue solution contains:
Potassium chlorate 5 kg
Glue 1 kg
Glass powder 500 gms
Sulphur 700 gms
Raisin 100 gms
Potassium Bicromate 200 gms
Black powder 21/2 kg
After the step of coating the stick is dried at room temperature and then it is
packed into the boxes which have the screening solution coated at the sides for
lighting the sticks.
The screening composition comprises of red phosphorus, varnish, aluminium
hydrate, potassium dichlorate, black carbon and turpentine oil.
