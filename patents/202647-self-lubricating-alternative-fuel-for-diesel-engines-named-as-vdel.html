Title of Invention

SELF LUBRICATING ALTERNATIVE FUEL FOR DIESEL ENGINES' NAMED AS VDEL.
Abstract In these days of advanced technology and science there are many fuels for internal combustion engines such as Petrol, Diesel, Propane, B100, etc. There are also some mixed fuels such as Power Alcohol, B20, etc. Generally we need such a type of fuel which is cheap, powerful, fast-igniting, low pollutant, less explosive etc. But none of the fuels mentioned above i.e. which are commonly used has all the above properties. As for example, Diesel creates a lot of CO2, CO, toxic Carbon particles, NOx gases etc. Petrol, power alcohol, Propane & B100 are high cost fuels and contain comparatively low energy. B100 creates a lot of NOx gases. So, if we want to get such a fuel that is cheaper than diesel, fast-igniting than diesel, produces low pollution, powerful like diesel and not explosive like Petrol, also it can be used in diesel engines with out any modification of the engine, then we are to use VDel, the fuel for the modern days demand. This fuel is a homogeneous mixture of a chemical (Kerosene) & a bio-fat (Mustard oil) in a specific ratio. Kerosene (Paraffin) + Mustard oil (Bio-fat) VDel (Mechanical Energy) This fuel VDel has been invented by us for the first time and also the fuel has effectively been experimented by us on diesel vehicle engines / pump engines for the first time. And we are the pioneer in this field. The fuel, VDel is of high energy level as good as diesel, cheaper than diesel, self lubricating, easy to produce, low pollutant, non-toxic, fast-igniting and non-explosive. Industrially this fuel can be produced at large scale by the process that we have also designed (drawing attached with complete specification). The significance of our invention is that we have found out the synergistic effect that arises when two such types of oil (Kerosene & Mustard oil), those are totally unfit for use as fuel in diesel engine, are mixed in a particular ratio and thus produce a very efficient fuel for diesel engine.

Kerosene only is not a suitable futl to be used in diesel engines and it has been observed that some sort of difficulties such as -

Surprisingly, when Kerosene and Mustard oil are mixed together with jerk / stirring a homogeneous mixture is produced and a unique, ideal & very efficient fuel for diesel engines is produced due to the synergistic effect of the components. It gives fine lubrication to the engine at the time of burning and emission of carbon particles, soot reduces remarkably. But the most Important factor associated with this synergistic effect is the particular ratio (Kerosene : Mustard oil Â¦ 17 : 3 ) at which these two components are mixed. With the increase in the quantity of Mustard oil above 15% the problem In starting the angina and disruption in proper burning increase. On the contrary, with the decrease in the quantity of Mustard oil below 15% the energy level of fuel and the lubrication of angina decrease rapidly. The most interesting point is that with the increase in mustard oil above 15% the energy level of the fuel diminishes practically, though it must increase mathematically. This is due to improper / partial burning of the fuel.
The key point behind this invention lies with determining the proper ratio to utilize the optimum level of synergistic effect of two oils (those can not be used separately as fuel for diesel engine) to give birth to an ideal, effective and efficient fuel for diesel engine.
