Title of Invention

"ELECTRO-MECHANICAL POWERED WHEEL CHAIR"
Abstract The electro mechanical powered wheelchair was developed to make bed ridden groups of severely physically challenged persons whose mobility has been extremely limited even to two fingers only. The present invention consists of two batteries, a main drive motor, a gear box, a turning system with a separate motor and a reduction gear box, a clutch system, a control panel box having fitted with a joystick switch a turning indicator switch a horn button, battery status indicator and a key lock switch, fro and aft movement turning system, speed control and electronic braking are all controlled by a single joystick switch, a potentiometer for sensing of the turning angle of the rear wheel. These form the unique features of this exceptional invention.
Full Text 4. DESCRIPTION
The object of this invention is to help daily living activities usefulness, self esteem and social activity of severely physically challenged persons and chronically all cases. Who find difficult to propel manual wheel chairs because of weak and paralyzed muscles or chromic arthritis deformed cases. The aim is to provide enhance easy transport to and from place of vocation and promote the activities inside and out side his provides.
Large number of sophisticated electrical wheel chairs are available in United states, European Country but the prohibitive prices problem of repair makes it unreachable for average disabled persons. In this innovation of electro mechanical powered wheel chair all has been made to keep the mechanism as less expensive and easily available and reparable as possible. The disadvantages of using separate knobs for various movements of the electrically operated mobility systems available have been overcome in this invention.
The invention of electro-mechanical powered wheelchair is a system of mobility for severely handicapped person who can negotiate on both even indoor rooms and verandahs as well as on rough roads and mild slopes. It is built on steel pipe frame manual wheel chair with sturdier larger wheels shifted to the front and the small castor replaced by wheels shifted to the rear electrical drive unit assembly is fitted to the front wheels. The angular movement of the rear wheels is powered by separate small motor and gear box connected to the rear wheel by levers.
BACKGROUND QF THE INVENTION
All the wheelchairs that are being manufactured in India at present are operated manually, with the user using his hands to push the hand rim on the wheels of the wheelchair, or some assistant of the user pushing the wheelchair from behind for transportation of the user.
This has its disadvantages because many a times the user may be an old and / or infirm person who may not be able to put in the effort for propelling the wheelchair and also because a suitable help or assistant may not be available to the user for moving the wheelchair.
SUMMARY OF INVENTION
in the present invention, there is a main drive motor, a gear box, a steering mechanism (which has its own separate motor and reduction gear), two 12 volt batteries, clutch system, and an electronic joystick control which are fitted on to an appropriately designed wheelchair for it to be operated by the user by simple
movements of the joystick of the joystick control unit placed on one of the arm rests of the wheelchair. Thus the disadvantages of a manually operated wheelchair are eliminated in the present invention.
STATEMENT OF INVENTION
The electro-mechanical powered wheelchair comprising:
A frame designed and fabricated with mild steel pipe and its chassis fabricated with mild steel pipes to locate motor, redicon, batteries, charger and electronic circuit plates;
Front wheels comprising rim, spokes, hub (including brake drum), tyre, tube which are mounted on blocks fixed in the frame with the help of an axle;
Rear wheels or castors the rims of which are made of tough plastic, having pneumatic tubes and tyres and mounted with the help of axle in the fork made of mild steel pipe;
Well cushioned seat, back and arm rest, with the arm rest being detachable;
A worm and worm wheel type horizontal Redicon gear to reduce the high speed of revolutions of the motor, which is mounted on a platform made of mild steel plate fixed on the chassis;
An electronic braking device in the joystick switch which is operable during movement of the wheelchair;
A turning system which is controlled electronically by the joystick switch, the functioning of which is controlled by a gearbox and a small motor;
A motor which is used for the main drive and is mounted on base brackets fixed on the chassis, the motor can be adjusted by an adjusting bolt and a "V" pulley mounted on its spindle to transmit the power to the reduction gearbox through a "V" belt;
Electronic circuits designed to control and regulate the speed, forward and backward movements, and the electronic PCB, field control circuits, armature control circuits, etc are fixed on polypropylene sheets mounted on mild steel strip base and a circuit breaker provided to meet the circumstances arising as a result of short circuiting;
An operating control panel box made of acrylic sheets and aluminum angles which contains the joystick switch which in turn controls forward, reverse and
steering movements as well as the speed of the wheelchair, the electric horn and its push button switch are also mounted on it, and the ignition key switch at the rear of the box, with the battery charge status indicating light fitted on to the box behind the horn switch;
A face type clutch system with shifting bracket mounted on outlet shaft of the gearbox with clutch boss and the 'female' part is made in the matching sprocket mounted on the main wheel;
A built-in battery charger is provided and every time after using the wheelchair the battery charger plug is to be connected to AC power socket to get the battery fully charged again;
Two maintenance free batteries are provided as source of power;
Head lights and side indicating lights for operating the wheelchair at night.
