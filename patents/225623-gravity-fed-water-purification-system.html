Title of Invention

GRAVITY FED WATER PURIFICATION SYSTEM
Abstract The invention provides for a gravity fed water purification system comprising a filtration unit adapted to separate particulate and soluble material from the input water,which is in fluid communication with - a chemical dispensing unit such that flow rate of water exiting the filtration unit is controlled by - a flow control means before the water encounters a biocide dispensed by the chemical dispensing unit,the water thereafter being retained in - a retention chamber for a predetermined period of time before exiting the water purification system through - a scavenger means adapted to separate the dispensed biocide from the exit water.
