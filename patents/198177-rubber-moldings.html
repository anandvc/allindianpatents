Title of Invention

RUBBER MOLDINGS
Abstract DIENE RUBBER COMPOUNDS FRO IMPROVED RUBBER MOLDINGS This invention relates to vulcanizable diene rubber compounds with a high capacity for the addition of sulfur to be varied whilst processing safety is maintained, for the production of improved rubbber moldings, particularly tire components.
