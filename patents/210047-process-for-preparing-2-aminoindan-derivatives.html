Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR PREPARING 2-AMINOINDAN DERIVATIVES
Abstract A process for preparing 5,6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine and acid addition salts thereof from 2-aminoindan. The process comprises protecting the amino group of 2-aminoindan, acetylating the ring in the protected compound, reducing the acetyl group to ethyl to form a monoethyl derivative, acetylating the monoethyl derivative, reducing the acetyl group to form a diethyl derivative, deprotecting the latter by hydrolysis and recovering the product in free or salt form.

ORGANIC COMPOUNDS This invention relates to the preparation of organic compounds, 5,6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine hydrochloride has Formula (VIII)

and has been used as an important intermediate in the synthesis of p2-adrenoceptor agonist for such indications as asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Previous processes for preparing 5,6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine hydrochloride involve the use of Grignard reagents, poor regioselectivity during Friedel-Craft cyciization, use of isoamyl nitrite and poor yield.
EP 1018514 A1 describes indane derivatives for use as an inhibitor of NF-KB, The indane derivatives of EP 1018514 A1 are prepared using isoamyl nitrite. WO 00/75114 describes indane derivatives for use as p2-adrenoceptor agonists.
The present invention provides a process for preparing 5.6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine comprising;
(i) protecting the amino group of 2-aminoindan with an acyl group in the presence of an organic solvent to form a compound of Formula (I)

(ii) reacting the compound of Formula (1) with acetyl halide in the presence of a Lewis acid to form a compound of Formula (II)


(iii) treating the compound of Formula (II) with a reducing agent in the presence of a catalyst and an organic solvent to form a compound of Formula (III)

(iv) reacting the compound of Formula (III) with acetyl halide in the presence of a Lewis acid to form a compound of Formula (IV)

(v) treating the compound of Formula (IV) with a reducing agent in the presence of a catalyst and an organic solvent to form a compound of Formula (V)

(vi) hydrolyzing the compound of Formula (V) in the presence of a base to form 5,6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine of Formula (VI)

wherein R is selected from the group consisting of an alkyl, aryl, alkoxy, alkenyl, cycloalkyi, benzocycloalkyi, cycloalkylalkyi, aralkyl. heterocyclic, heteroaralkyi and haloalkyl.
The process of the invention is environmentally clean and safe due to the absence of Grignard reagents and isoamyl nitrite. In addition, acetyl halide can be used as both a reactant and a solvent in Steps (ii) and (iv). The process also has high regioselectivity and high yield of 5,6-diethyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2-amine in free or salt form.

Example 1
Preparation of N-(2,3-dihydro-1H-inden-2'-yl)-2.2,2-trifIuoroacetamide. according to Step (i) of the process of the invention.
