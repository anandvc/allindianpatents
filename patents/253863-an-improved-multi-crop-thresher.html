Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED MULTI CROP THRESHER."
Abstract The present invention relates to an multi crop thresher: - feeding unit (32) provided at the inlet side of the threshing unit (14); - the feeding unit is mounted on the chassis (21) of the thresher along with cleaning aspirator means; - power transmission means is provided to transmit the rotational movement of different parts of the thresher, characterized in that - a cutting unit (28) connected to threshing unit being provided for variation in air flow as per the requirement of crops - adjusting means for adjusting beater depth as per the requirement of the particular crop - the whole unit is mounted on a wheeled (29) chassis which can be transferred from one farm to the other for threshing purpose. Full Text Field of the Invention
This invention relates to an improved multi crop thresher used for threshing different crops like wheat, barley, jwar, chana, mustered, pulses, zera, menthe, groundnut, soyabean, etc.
BACKGROUND
The use of the threshers is well established in the prior art for threshing the different crops. One of the conventional threshers comprises a shaft mounted on frame and disposed in a cashing secured with the same frame. A plurality of the rods having cutters at the free ends thereof is secured in the middle of the shaft by means of a bracket secured with the shaft. A pulley is mounted on one side of the shaft for facilitating the rotation of the shaft. The pulley is connected with the prime mover by means of belt(s)! A wire mesh in the form of an arc is provided at the bottom side of the casing. A hoper is secured at one side of the casing for feeding the crop to be threshed. An out let is provided at the front end for the exit of the fodder. A fan is provided at opposite end of the outlet for separating the grains from the fodder.
Another thresher known in the prior art comprises almost all the components arranged in the same manner as mentioned herein above. Additionally winnowing means are provided below the bottom wire mesh for separating the grains and chaff so as to fill the grains into the gunny begs.
As disclosed in the Indian patent no. 175344, a multi purpose threshers known in the art for threshing the different crops. This kind of the thresher has a threshing unit mounted on a frame. A pulley is mounted on one side of the main shaft for providing the rotational movement to the threshing drum of the threshing unit and also other units. A wire mesh is secured to the bottom end of the threshing unit for converting the thresher for threshing the different kind of the crops. Disadvantages associated with the prior art
There are disadvantages associated with the prier art. One of these disadvantages is that the conventional threshers do not produce good quality of fodder.
Another disadvantage is that conventional threshers exert heavy load on the drives by which it is run and therefore the farmers find it difficult to run the machine.
Yet another disadvantage is that the conventional threshers do not separate the chaff and grains completely and the farmers have to separate it again either by using the fan assembly of the same thresher or by using a separate fan like device and in the process a lot of grains are wasted. Still another disadvantage is that the same thresher can not be used for threshing the different crops as mentioned herein above and there fore more number of the thresher are required by one farmer.
Advantages and Objects of the Invention
Therefore the main object of the present invention is to provide an improved multi crop thresher, which can be used for threshing the different kind of crops.
Another object of the present invention is to provide an improved multi crop thresher, which does not exert more loads on the drive which runs it.
Yet another object of this invention is to provide an improved multi crop thresher, which produces good quality of fodder/chaff.
Yet another object of this invention is to provide an improved multi crop thresher, which provides the chaff/fodder and grains separately and the farmers do not have to separate it again.

Statement of the Invention
According to this invention there is provided an improved multi crop thresher for threshing the different kind of the crops comprising feeding unit provided at the inlet side of the threshing unit mounted on the chassis of the thresher, cleaning/aspirator means being provided on the other side of the threshing unit, power transmission means being provided to transmit the rotational movement to the different parts of the thresher, characterized in that a cutting unit adapted to be connected to said threshing unit being provided between the feeding unit and said threshing unit, means being provided for providing the variation in the air flow as per the requirement of the crops, adjusting means being provided for adjusting the beater depth as per the requirement of the particular crop and specific threshing means being provided for making the thresher suitable for threshing different crops.
Brief Description of the Invention
In accordance with this invention the improved multi crop thresher has a power transfer unit provided to transmit the power from the shaft of the tractor to the main shaft of the thresher through the coupling pulley. A aspirator unit comprising a plurality of fans secured with an annular axle being mounted on the main shaft such to have independent flow at the required rate. A threshing unit is provided between the aspirator unit and the cutter unit, for threshing the chopped crop fed by the cutter unit. Though some little improvements have been made on the above construction however these features may not be treated as the inventive features.
Specifically according to this invention, a cutter assembly is provided towards the feeding unit for cutting the crop in to the small pieces so to reduce the load on the cutter unit. The cutter assembly has a fly wheel having a plurality of cutters provided therewith for providing the cutting action to the crop before feeding the same to the threshing assembly. A leaver mechanism is provided with the bottom of the thresher so as to increase and decrease the beater depth as per the requirement of the crops. A mechanism is provided to give air flow of the different pressure and as per the requirement to suck the cut crop from the cutter assembly. The. mechanism comprises a plurality of pulleys of the different sizes mounted on the main shaft of the thresher. Also a typical bolted blade assembly is provided on the main shaft of the thresher so as get the straw of the required fineness. This assembly is disposed in the threshing unit of the thresher.
