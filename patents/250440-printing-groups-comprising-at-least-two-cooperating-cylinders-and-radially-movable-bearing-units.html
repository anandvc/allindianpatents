Title of Invention

PRINTING GROUPS COMPRISING AT LEAST TWO COOPERATING CYLINDERS AND RADIALLY MOVABLE BEARING UNITS
Abstract The invention relates to printing groups (04) comprising at least two cooperating cylinders (06, 07, 312), each of which is mounted in a bearing unit (14) that radially displaces the respective cylinder. At least one bearing unit is provided with at least one actuator (82, 84) which is controlled or regulated by a control unit. An inking unit (68, 302) encompassing at least one ink applying roll (28, 306, 307) is provided, at least one of the cylinders and an ink applying roll of the inking unit being optionally placed against each other, and/or a dampening unit (09, 303) comprising at least one moisture applying roll (41, 304) is provided, at least one of the cylinders and a moisture applying roll of the dampening unit being optionally placed against each other. The at least one respective actuator of the bearing units is remote controlled. The at least one actuator is embodied as a hydraulic actuator or pneumatic actuator.
