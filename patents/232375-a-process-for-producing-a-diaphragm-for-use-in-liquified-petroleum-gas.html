Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING A DIAPHRAGM FOR USE IN LIQUIFIED PETROLEUM GAS"
Abstract This invention relates to a process for producing a diaphragm for use in liquified petroleum gas or methane vaporizer of an internal combustion engine comprising treating the polyester-cotton fabric having 30-40% of polyester and 60-65% of cotton with a bonding agent such as herein described by dip coating, drying said treated fabric in air at atmospheric temperature under stretch condition, subjecting the dried fabric to the step of lamination with an elastomeric compound, press curing the laminated fabric in a mould at a temperature of 150-160°C and pressure of 1-1.5 tonn for 20-30 minutes followed by cutting the same and fixing metallic washers therewith with rivets, said elastomeric compound comprises 100 parts by weight of copolymer, 10-20 parts by weight of magnesium oxide, 15-25 parts by weight of carbon black, 3-6 parts by weight of calcium hydroxide mixed with each other homogeneously.

BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided a process for producing a diaphragm for use in liquified petroleum gas or methane vaporizer of an internal combustion engine comprising treating the polyester-cotton fabric having 30-40% of polyester and 60-65% of cotton with a bonding agent such as herein described by dip coating, drying said treated fabric in air at atmospheric temperature under stretch condition, subjecting the dried fabric to the step of lamination with an elastomeric compound, press curing the laminated fabric in a mould at a

temperature of 150-160°C and pressure of 1-1.5 tonn for 20-30 minutes followed by cutting the same and fixing metallic washers therewith with rivets, said elastomeric compound comprises 100 parts by weight of copolymer, 10-20 parts by weight of magnesium oxide, 15-25 parts by weight of carbon black, 3-6 parts by weight of calcium hydroxide mixed with each other homogeneously.
In accordance with this invention the polyester-cotton fabric is treated with a bonding, agent such as isocyanate adhesive mixed with a solvent. The treated polyester-cotton fabric is subjected to the step of drying in a dust and dirt free atmosphere. The dried fabric is then subjected to the step of lamination with an elastomeric compound and press curing. The laminated cured fabric is cut into pieces of the required size in order to form the diaphragm which is subsequently fitted with the washers in the central portion of the diaphragm. The elastomeric compound according to this invention comprises 100 parts copolymer, 10-20 parts magnesium oxide, 15-25 parts carbon black, 3-6 parts calcium hydroxide mixed with each other homogeneously.
