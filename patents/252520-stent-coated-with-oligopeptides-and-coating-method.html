Title of Invention

STENT COATED WITH OLIGOPEPTIDES AND COATING METHOD
Abstract The present invention relates to a pharmaceutical composition comprising a caspase inhibitor and/or a compound of the general formula R-Lys-X, methods for coating medical products using said caspase inhibitors and/or said compounds of general formula R-Lys-X and medical products coated with said caspase inhibitors and/or said compounds of general formula R-Lys-X, wherein the coating inhibits restenosis.
