Specific gravity separation is one of the principles for the sorting various commodities. This type of sorting method is generally used for food grains.
There is much specific gravity separator available in the market, but as food grains are free flowing, arrangement of the hopper or feeding arrangement for such machine is according to free flowing food grain and which is not suitable for sticky material like resins. The traditional method used for the resins is manual. It takes much time and energy.
In the Patent US 6631799 and the present Patent application there is difference in construction.
In the Patent US 6631799 there are three parts (a) Hopper, (b) Tray, (c) Vibrator; whereas in the present Patent application there is only the Tray which is called the Feeding Hopper Assembly. In the Patent US 6631799 there is the Hopper, which is disposed above the Tray, and the Vibrator is mounted to the Tray; whereas in the present Patent application there is no separate vibrator used to vibrate the Feeding Hopper Assembly. In the Patent US 6631799 there are V groove and step; whereas in the present Patent application, these V groove and step are not present. In the Patent US 6631799 there is Singulation of components; whereas in the present Patent application, the Feeding Hopper Assembly gives the continuos flow of resins. In the Patent US 6631799 there is no provision for sticking and jamming components (commodities); whereas in the present Patent application, the Feeding Hopper Assembly is made only for the sticking and jamming commodities: resins.
The object of the invention is to reduce the wastage and to minimize efforts of labour, and give maximum degree of efficiency of separation of resins. This invented special feeding hopper assembly is suitable for feeding resins in such specific gravity separator. With the invented development of this type of hopper, resins can be feed in to machine without sticking or jamming. This type of hopper assembly is very easy to load without spillage of resins, also minimize efforts of labour for loading and give maximum degree of efficiency of separation. This is eco friendly.
It is also the object of the invention to have a feeding hopper assembly suitable for feeding resins in specific gravity separator machine, which is very simple in construction and easy for operation.

Summary of the Invention:-
This Invention relates to a feeding hopper assembly suitable for feeding resins in specific gravity separator machine consist of holding bracket (1) for hopper, the rear support (2), reinforcement plate (3), feed control plate (4) wherein comprising
2a
a) the holding bracket (1) for hopper, fastening to supporting lower deck of the machine and hopper by using standard nut bolts;
b) the rear support (2) is used to hold hopper with vibrating deck, connected by using standard nut bolts; consists the slot made for the adjusting the slope of the hopper;
c) reinforcement plate (3) welded to hopper base to avoid slacking of the hopper base and used to stiffen hopper base;
d) feed control plate (4) located at discharge end of the hopper for the flow; adjusts the flow by moving up and down by the provision of slots.



Thus according to this invention a feeding hopper assembly suitable for feeding resins in specific gravity separator machine consists of hopper assembly. The vibrating hopper assembly feeds material continuously and uniformly to the machine. There are two supports given to the hopper, support located at discharge end is a holding bracket and another located at backside is a rear support convey vibrations to hopper. Slot to the rear support is made for adjusting the slope of the hopper. The reinforcement plate is used to stiffen hopper base. The feed control plate located at discharge end of the hopper for the flow. Flow can be adjusted by moving feed control plate up and down. A provision of slots is made on feed control plate to move the plate up and down.
