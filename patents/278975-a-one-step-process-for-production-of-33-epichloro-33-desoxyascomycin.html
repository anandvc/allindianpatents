"A ONE STEP PROCESS FOR PRODUCTION OF 33-EPICHLORO-33-DESOXYASCOMYCIN""
Abstract A process for the production of 33-Epi-33-chloro-FR 520 in one step from FR520 wherein protecting groups are avoided.

Example 1 33-Epi-33-chloro-FR 520
9.69 g of s-collidine (2,4,6-trimethylpyridine) and 15.84 g of FR520 are added to a solution of 24.8 mMol of dichlorotriphenylphosphine in 160 ml of toluene. The mixture obtained is stirred at 60° for 1 nour, H2O is added, two phases obtained are separated and the organic layer obtained is washed with aqueous, saturated NaCI solution and dried. From the mixture obtained solvent is evaporated and the evaporation residue obtained is subjected to chromatography over silicagel. 33-epi-33-chloro-FR 520 is obtained. Yield: 51.4%. From chromatography a fraction containing unreacted FR520 is obtained.
