Full Text This invention relates to electrically conducting coated kapton film.
Kapton is a trade mark of Dupont, USA, and pertains to a polyimide film. It possesses a unique combination of properties that makes it ideal for a variety of applications in many different industries, such as, electrical and electronic insulation applications.
One of the very important applications of these films is their use in satellites. For this specific application, the films are to be electrically conducting on the outer surface for protecting against static discharge. These surface coatings have also to withstand harsh environments like extremely low temperature and exposure to hard radiations.
