Title of Invention

"A METHOD AND SYSTEM FOR PROVIDING A VIEWER PARTICIPATION PROGRAM"
Abstract There is disclosed a method and a system for enabling viewers to participate in using a program in real time, without any limitation on the number of participants. According to the present invention, TMS 3 obtains broadcast data from broadcast station 2, converts the broadcast data into mobile unit broadcast data, and distributes the mobile unit broadcast data to mobile phones 1. Each mobile phone 1 displays an image corresponding to the mobile unit broadcast data and sends input data to TMS 3. TMS 3 generates update data on the basis of the input data and distributes the update data to mobile phones 1. Mobile phone 1 displays the image updated on the basis of the updated data. On the other hand, TMS 3 forwards the input data to broadcast station 2, and broadcast station 2 broadcasts the viewer participation program updated on the basis of the input data .

TECHNICAL FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to a method and system for providing a viewer participation program, and, in particular, for provision of a program for a broadcast station to provide a program to viewers and a broadcast system and server optimal for use in that method.
PRIOR ART
An auction program has been broadcast as a viewer participation television program. Participation of viewers in such an auction program has required the viewers to send in postcards applying for participation to the television station in advance. Then drawing viewers from the application postcards sent from the viewers is made, and only the selected viewers can participate in the program. These selected participants were then asked to come in to the broadcast station on the day of recording the program to stage the viewer participation type auction program.
Oh the other hand, in quiz shows and other television programs, sometimes telephone numbers are announced during the television program for viewer response to questions in the quiz and the viewers call in answers over those telephone numbers.
In such conventional viewer participation type auction programs, the viewers had to personally come into the recording location to participate. Therefore, there often could be cases where some viewers could not participate due to geographic constraints or time constraints. Further, even viewers meeting the right conditions could not obtain the chance of participating in the program if not being selected in the drawing. Therefore,

while they are called viewer participation type programs, there has been the problem that only a very limited number of viewers were able to actually participate.
As opposed to this, when using the method of having viewers call in answers over telephone numbers announced during the program, participation in the program of viewers spread over a wide region becomes possible. When using this method for a television program involving commercial transactions such as an auction program, however, because it takes time to verify the identity of the viewers to ensure the legitimacy of the transactions, it was extremely difficult to secure a real time feeling in the programs.
