Title of Invention

"A BINDER RESIN FOR A TONER AND A TONER THEREOF"
Abstract A binder resin for a toner obtained from a polyethylene terephthalate (PET) and/or a polybutylene terephthalate (PBT), a polycarboxylic acid, a polyhydric alcohol, a wax (cl) having a group selected from a substituent of aromatic structure having 6 to 750 carbon atoms a hydroxyl group and a carboxyl group, and a polyisocyanate (d).
