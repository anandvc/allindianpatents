Title of Invention

ORAL TOOTH CLEANING COMPOSITION
Abstract The present invention relates to oral care compositions with an improved teeth whitening effect. This effect is achieved by inclusion in the oral care compositions of certain peroxyamido-phthalamides as teeth whitening/bleaching agents, together with hydrogen peroxide.
