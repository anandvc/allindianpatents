Title of Invention

LEVER TYPE RECLINER ASSEMBLY FOR A VEHICLE SEAT .
Abstract ABSTRACT Lever Type Recliner Assembly for a Vehicle Seat According to the present invention there is provided a vehicle reclining seat with lever type recliner, for a seat occupant who in particular is the driver and/or co-driver of the vehicle, comprising of a seat frame which includes a seat rest (1), a back rest (2) and a lever type recliner assembly (3), arm recliner (4) of which is mounted to the said backrest and plate base (5) is mounted to the cushion frame of said seat rest, the entire seat frame is mounted on the vehicle under body wherein said lever type recliner assembly (3) consists of a arm recliner (4) held in between the plate base (5) and plate holder (6) by a pin stopper (7) and is engaged with lower part with the help of teeth parts provided on each of them, said lower part in turn is held in a desired position by a cam lever (9) held in between plate base (5) and plate holder (6) along with lever rod (10) by a shaft cam (11), being activated by a lever release (13) held with lever rod (10) by a pin lever rod (14). and operating against the spring lever (15) tension.
Full Text This invention relates to a lever type recliner assembly for a vehicle seat and
for example a vehicle reclining seat for a seat occupant who, in particular, is the
driver and/or co-driver of the vehicle.
The seat recliners are basically used for giving maximum comfort to the
driver and co-driver and to ensure that there is minimum fatigue levels even after
prolonged hours of driving. The two types of recliners used in the art are Rotary
type and lever type. The rotary type has an infinite adjustment in steps of 1 degree,
2 degrees, or 3 degrees. The backward and forward movements in the rotary type is
slower compared to in the lever type recliners. However it is observed that most of
the manufacturers of cars in the world prefer the lever type recliners. Moreover it is
a purpose of this invention to provide avehicle reclining seat with lever type
recliner assembly.
It may here now be mentioned that at present, all conventional vehicle
reclining seat fitted with lever type recliner assembly, are all associated with
various defects. In particular, occurrence of the squeaking noise in the recliner
during its operations, lower life of coil spring, plate holder bending during staking
operations thereby restricting the free movement of the arm recliner during
operation, thus occupants tend to become disinclined to take advantage of the
reclining arrangements.
Accordingly, an object of the present invention is to provide a lever type
recliner assembly for a vehicle seat, for a seat occupant who in particular is the
driver and/or co-driver of the vehicle, is novel in its constructions and obviates all
disadvantages and drawbacks associated with the conventional vehicle reclining
seat fitted with lever type recliner assembly.

A further object of the present invention is to provide a
vehicle reclining seat with lever type recliner, for a seat
occupant who in particular is the driver and/or co-driver of the
vehicle, which is easy to operate without any squeaking noise in
the recliner during its operation.
Another object of the present invention is . to provide a
vehicle reclining "seat with lever type recliner, for a seat
occupant who in particular is the driver and/or co-driver of the
vehicle, where the life span of coil spring is much longer thereby
increasing the reliability of the system.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a
vehicle reclining seat with lever type recliner, for a seat
occupant who in particular is the driver and/or co-driver of the
vehicle which is stronger in its construction, light in its weight
and cheaper in original costs.
Keeping the above objects in mind, the present invention thus
provides a vehicle reclining seat with lever type recliner, for a
seat occupant who in particular is the driver and/or co-driver of
the vehicle, comprising of a seat frame which includes a seat rest
a back rest and a lever type recliner assembly arm recliner of
which is mounted to the said backrest and plate base is mounted to
the cushion frame of said seat rest, the entire seat frame is
mounted on the vehicle under body wherein said lever type recliner
assembly consists of a arm recliner held in between the plate base
and plate holder by a pin stopper and is engaged with lower part
with the help of teeth parts provided on each of them, said lower
part in turn is held in a desired position by a cam lever held in
between plate base and plate holder along with lever rod by a
shaft cam, being activated by a lever release held with lever rod
by a pin lever rod and operating against the spring lever tension.

The said lever type recliner assembly of the present
invention is made of special metal or the like non-rigid and
resilient material, heat treatment cycle of which is established
to achieve the required case depth, resulting in having soft core,
tough teeth and ability to withstand impact load without failure.
Moreover the assembly may be configured into any shape depending
upon requirement and/or choice, but preferably in the shape of the
seat frame.
Unlike other known vehicle reclining seats, the vehicle
reclining seat of the present invention can be operated far more
easily and reliably than the existing ones.

The description of the' invention made here under which now
follows is made in relations to figures 1 to 4 wherein a vehicle
reclining seat with lever type recliner, comprises of seat frame
which includes a seat rest (1), a back rest (2) and a lever type
recliner assembly (3) connecting both of them by arm recliner and
plate base respectively. The said lever type recliner assembly
consists of arm recliner (4), one end of which mounted to the said
back rest and the other end is held in between the plate base (5)
and plate holder (6) by a pin stopper (7) provided with a step (8)
on it so as to eliminate the problem of plate holder bending
during staking operation. The teeth of arm recliner and lower part
(not shown in figure) are engaged wherein the first tooth on the
arm recliner form the dwell profile and same is 0.2 mm higher than
the maximum tooth height (not shown in figure) thereby eliminating
the possible squeaking noise in the recliner during operation.
Said lower part is held in a desired position (engagement or
disengagement of lower tooth with the tooth of arm recliner) by a
cam lever (9) held in between plate base (5) and plate holder (6)
along with leyer rod_ (10) with the help of a shaft cam (11) with a
step down (12) portion for lever rod. The said cam lever is
actuable by lever release (13) held with lever rod (10) by a pin
lever rod (14) spin rivetted and is operable against the spring
lever (15) tension. The return spiral spring (16) is of size
3.4 x 10 mm for increased life span of the spring.
Now to show how the vehicle reclining seat with lever type
recliner for a seat occupant who in particular is the driver

and/or co-driver of the vehicle of the present invention may be
carried into effect or function, reference is again made to figure
1 and 2 wherein in normal condition teeth of arm recliner and
lower part are engaged thereby keeping the backrest in locked
condition. For angular adjustment of. the backrest for giving
maximum comfort to the driver and co-driver after a long driving,
the lever release is operated against the spring lever thereby
allowing the cam lever to slide down along with the lower part,
causing disengagement of lower tooth with arm recliner. Now the
arm recliner could be adjusted to the desired position and by
releasing lever release, the backrest gets locked at desired angle
of comfort.
Here it may be noted that the said arm recliner, lower tooth
and cam lever are fine blanked so as to achieve flatness, higher
dimensional accuracy on tooth profile area of arm recliner and
lower tooth and profile area of cam lever.
