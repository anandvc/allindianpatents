Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR OBTAINING A PURIFIED ANHYDROSUGAR ALCOHOL
Abstract A process for the formation of a anhyrosugar alcohol is disclosed, whereby the resultant purified anhydrosugar alcohol has a purity of at least 99.8% and is substantially colorless. The anhydrosugar alcohol is purified is purified by distillation, recrystallization from methanol, ethanol or ethylene glycol, melt recrystallization, or a combination thereof, preferably, the purification is by distillation followed by recrystallization from methanol, ethanol or ethylene glycol. At test for determining the purity of the anhydrosugar alcohol is disclosed, wherein the color of the anhydrosugar alcohol after annealing at a temperature of at least 260&#730; C for at least four hours is examined. Alternatively, purity can be determined by examination of ultraviolet (UV) transmittance of the purified abhydrosugar alcohol at various wavelengths. The purified anhydrosugar alcohol at various wavelengths. The purified anhyrosugar alcohol may be incorporated into a polyester, which may in turn be used to form such articles of manufacturing as , optical, disk, fibers, sheets, and films.
