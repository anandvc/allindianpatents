Full Text [DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION]
[0001]
[Technical Field OF the Invention] The present invention relates to an assist step for two passengers of a motorcycle.
[0002]
In general, a motorcycle for two passengers has a double-seat for a drive and a fellow passenger and moreover driver's steps on which a drive can put his legs thereon at the former part of body and assist steps on which a fellow passenger can put her legs thereon at the rear part of body.
The assist step has been modified many times so that a fellow passenger can take a seat in comfortable attitude.. For example, such modifications are realized by the technique described in the refe rences, (1) Japanese Patent Publication No. Hei 6~ 13307, "Assist Foot Step of A Scooter Type Motorcycle" and by the technique (2) that an assist foot step is further provided at the rear part of the bar type assist step. [0003]
According to Fig. 1 and Fig. 2 of the official gazette, the technique (1) teaches that a front stage 16A corresponding to the height of the upper edge of the rear wheel 32 is provided to the rear frame pipe 16 (corresponding to the same reference ntimeral in the official gazette, it is also true hereinafter), and the falling type assist foot steps 66a, 66b (corresponding to the assist step) are fixed to the area near the rear part of this front stage 16A via the brackets 68a, 68b. The assist foot steps 66a, 66b are formed in the shape of a bar (pirion step) and it can be fallen along the lower edge of the rear cover 48 for the accommodation when no fellow passenger is taking a seat. [0004]
The technique (2) teaches a motorcycle for two passengers in such a structure that an assist foot step which is almost rectangular in the plan view is provided at the rear part of the bar type assist step on which a fellow passenger can put her leg and moreover the side elevation of body is covered up to the area in the vicinity of the assist foot step with a cover member-[0005]
[Problems to be Solved by the Invention]
The techniques (1) and (2) suggest that the bar type assist steps and assist foot steps are provided to a body frame made of steel pipe material.
In these years, many kinds of motorcycle are manufactured, in which so-called a monocock type frame is introduced as the body frame. In such a monocock frame, the steep plate is pressed so that the major cross-section of the body frame becomes hollow section formed by a thin plate.
However, when the bar type assist step, assist foot step and cover member formed by the techniques (1) and (2) are fitted to the monocock type frame, it is not so easy to widely cover the side elevation of the body with a cover member while assuring rigidity of the fitting portions and thereby the fitting structure is considerably complicated.
[0006] ;
It is therefore an object of the present invention to provide an assist step for passenger of motorcycle which can attach the bar type assist,step, foot step and cover member to the monocock type frame with a simplified structure and also can cover the side elevation of the body in a wide area with the cover member.
[0007]
[Means for Solving the Problems] In view of achieving the object explained above, the invention of the claim 1 relates to an assist step for passenger of motorcycle which utilizes, as a body frame, a monocock type frame which is extending to the rear lower part up to a swing arm supporting part from a head pipe, rising to the rear upper part from said swing arm supporting part and is being provided with the upper part of a rear cushion at the upper end part of the rising part characterized in that a bracket for step which is extended to the rear part to become thinner and is formed in almost the triangular shape in the side view is attached to the swing arm supporting part, a bar type assist step is fitted to the rear part of the bracket for step, meanwhile a cover member covering the side elevation of the body is dropped from the upper end part of the rising part and an assist foot step which is formed almost in the rectangular shape in the plan view is attached between the lower end .part of the cover member and the rear end part of said bracket for step, in view of covering the side elevation of the body up to the vicinity of the bar type assist step and

the vicinity of the assist foot step with the cover member,
Moreover, the bar type assist step and assist foot step can be attached by making use of the swing arm supporting part and upper end part of the rising part having a higher rigidity among the monocbck type frame and the side elevation of the body can be covered with the cover member in a wide area. [0008]

In the rear frame 7 completed as the monocock type frame, the swing arm supporting part 8 for supporting the swing arm 23 and the upper end part 9 of the rising part for fitting the rear cushion 26 have higher rigidity than that of the other part because heavier loads are applied thereon.
The assist step 40 provides, in coupling with each other, the bracket 41 for step, assist foot step 44 and cover member 43 by making use of the swing arm supporting part 8 and upper end part 9 of the rising part having a higher rigidity.
Therefore, the bar type assist step 42 and assist foot step 44 may be attached without providing a complicated structure to the rear frame 7 to give a higher rigidity and moreover the side elevation of the body can be covered with the cover member 43 in the wider area.
[0019]
In the embodiment of the present invention, the body frame 2 is characterized in the monocock type frame, which comprehends a structure, for example.

formed only with a steel plate, in addition to the structure completed by steel plate and steel pipe.
Moreover, the bracket 41 for step, bar type assist step 42, cover member 43 and assist foot step 44 are not limited only to the structure where these are arranged in the left side of the body of a motorcycle 1 and these may also be arranged, for example, to the right side of the body or both sides of the body.
When the bracket 41 for step, cover member 43 and assist foot step 44 are arranged only in the right or left side of the body, a fellow passenger can take the seat 25 from the sideway and put her leg on the assist foot step 44,
Moreover, when the bracket 41 for step, bar type assist step 42, cover member 43 and assist foot step 44 are provided in both sides of the body, a fellow passenger can take the seat 25 by crossing over the body and puts her legs on the bar type assist steps 42 and assist foot steps 44.
[0020]
Since the bracket 41 for step is coupled, at its rear end part, with the upper end part 9 of the rising part of the rear frame 7 via the cover member 43 and assist foot step 44, it is not limited to the
structure that the base end part is fixed at two positions of the swing arm supporting part 8 and the assist supporting part 12 and it sufficiently plays the role of the bracket even when it is fixed only to the swing arm supporting part 8.
In addition, the swing arm supporting part 8 and the bracket 41 for step are not limited to the structure that these are fitted with only one bolt and may be fitted with a plurality of bolts and moreover these may also be fitted by riveting or welding, in place of using a bolt.
[0021]
Moreover, it is desirable that the bracket 41 for step is completed almost in the triangular shape in its side view because a load is centralized to the base part, but it is not limited to this shape when consideration is taken for the rigidity. The bracket 41 for step is not limited to a plate material and may be formed, for example, with angled steel. [0022]
