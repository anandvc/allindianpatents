DISTINGUISHING FEATURES OF THE INVENTION WITH PRIOR ART;
Object of this invention is to improve the general health by removing digestive disorder and normalizing the functions of the stomach and intestines where as there is no medicine available whether ayurvedic or synthetic available at present which have long corrective nature it no only cure the digestive disorder but also improve the general health by purifying blood and giving sound sleep, it is delicious and flavorous.
Accordingly the invention provides a process for the preparation of synergistic herbal pharmaceutical composition (SWASTHYA CHURNA) useful for treatment of digestive disorder, comprising
a) Cleaning, washing, drying and roasting the ingredients of Group A as herein described
b) Washing drying, grinding, roasting the ingredients of Group B as herein described,
c) Mixing by course grinding the salts of Group C as herein described
d) Mixing all the ingredients of Group A, B and C in a rotary mixer drum for 1 hour in ratio 40:47:13.
e) Final grinding of the mixture obtained in step d to get the desired product.
