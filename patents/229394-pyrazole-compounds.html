Title of Invention

PYRAZOLE COMPOUNDS
Abstract This invention relates to a compound of the formula: wherein each of Ar<sub>1</sub>, Ar<sub>2</sub>, and Ar<sub>3</sub>, independently, is phenyl, thienyl, furyl, pyrrolyl, pyridinyl, or pyrimidinyl; each of R<sub>1</sub>, R<sub>2</sub>, R<sub>3</sub>, R<sub>4</sub>, R<sub>5</sub>, and R<sub>6</sub>, independently, is R, nitro, halogen, C(O)OR, C(O)SR, C(O)NRR', (CH<sub>2</sub>)mOR, (CH<sub>2</sub>)mSR, (CH<sub>2</sub>)mNRR', (CH<sub>2</sub>)mCN, (CH<sub>2</sub>)mC(O)OR, (CH<sub>2</sub>)mCHO, (CH<sub>2</sub>)mCH=NOR, or R<sub>1</sub> and R<sub>2</sub> together, R<sub>3</sub> and R<sub>4</sub> together, or R<sub>5</sub> and R<sub>6</sub> together are O(CH<sub>2</sub>)mO, in which each of R and R', independently, is H or C<sub>1</sub>~C<sub>6</sub> alkyl; and m is 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, and n is 0, 1, 2, or 3.
