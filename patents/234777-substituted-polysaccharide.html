Title of Invention

SUBSTITUTED POLYSACCHARIDE
Abstract The present invention relates t substituted polysaccharides, useful as macroinitiator intermediates for the preparation of the polysaccharide graft polymers. The polysaccharide graft polymers may be used, for example, to impart soil release and/or fabric care benefit to laundry detergent of fabric treatment compositions.
