Title of Invention

A PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION CONTAINING DIPEPTIDYL PEPTIDASE IV INHIBITOR ISOLEUCINE THIAZOLIDIDE AND A PROCESS FOR PREPARATION THEREOF
Abstract A method for the treatment of diabetes mellitus, especially Type 2 diabetes and conditions associated with diabetes mellitus in a mammal such as a human, which method comprises administering an effective, non-toxic and pharmaceutically acceptable amount of a dipeptidyl peptidae IV inhibitor and another antidiabetic agent to a mammal in need thereof.
