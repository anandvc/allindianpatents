Title of Invention

A DEVICE WITH BRUSH FOR CONTROLLED DISPENSING OF LIQUID/PASTE FROM REPLACEABLE TUBE CARTRIDGE
Abstract The invention consists of a novel combination construction (s) of the Pastetube & Brush, of plastics e.g. Polypropelene & the like material/s, in any conceivable size & shape, conical, cylindrical, squarish & or any combination/s thereof, where the combination unit is formed by parts like holder, brush,lock,slider & pastetube. The pasttube is fitted into the cavity of the holder. The slider is inserted into the cavity of the holder after fitting the pastetube so that the tip rests on the sealed end of the pastetube. This end is sealed by putting a lock. The brush is fitted at the other end of the holder so as to form a single unit. During usage, first the brush is taken out, then by moving the slider forward; the paste is taken out on the brush. The brush is again fitted on the holder so as to form a single unit again. Now, combination can be used as brush and after usage, the brush can be cleaned by water for further use. And this novel combination construction of Pastetube & Brush includes all other applications, machines, apparatus, equipments & the like as described in Patent Application / Specifications with reference to the accompanying drawing/s.

"A NOVEL COMBINATION CONSTRUCTION OF PASTETUBE & BRUSH SO AS TO FORM A SINGLE ENTITY & THE LIKE" Introduction :
There are various applications in our day to day life where a liquid /paste is
applied or used by a brush. One of the common example is TOOTHPASTE &
TOOTHBRUSH.
Such example(s) are also evident in industrial applications. More particularly, this
invention provides a Novel Combined Construction(s) of Pastetube & Brush, & thus
also provides improvised products for all the applications involving application of
any paste & brush.
The invention is described herein by referring to TOOTHPASTE &
TOOTHBRUSH as a representative example for illustration purpose,
however, this Patent/Application/Specification also covers & includes many other
applications as well. Some such examples are: Rust removing paste, thread sealant,
lock-tight-threaded joints, nail-paint application and the like. This invention provides
economy in the consumption of packing material. The paste consumed is also
controlled. These achievements are possible without sacrificing any of the desired
technical property(s).
The invention offers the improvised product for all applications where a paste/ liquid
is applied by a brush; e.g. cleaning of machine parts by applying oil. This invention
provides convenience & economy in usage of both the Paste & brush.
Prior Art :

Both paste(s) & brush(s) are being sold as two different products in the market. If a person is travelling for a few days, he has to carry both Toothpaste & Toothbrush alongwith.

Drawings consist of 5 figures.
Referring to Fig.-[1 ]; a unique product consisting of a combination of a toothbrush & a toothpaste in & as one single unit is designed. The combination consists of following 5 parts.
Holder(l); Brush(2); Lock(3); Shder(4), Pastetube (5) All the parts are shown separately & also in assembled state. The four parts, namely, holder(l), brush(2), lock(3) &slider(4) are moulded or formed separately. The toothpaste material is filled m a tube (5). This tube (5) is sealed from & at one end (6). The tube (5) is fitted into the cavity (7) of the holder (1). The cavity (7) is shown in the cross-sectional drawing "A-A". The Slider (4) is inserted into the cavity after fitting the tube. The slider (4) is fitted in such a way so that the tip (8) rests above the sealed end (6) of the tube (5). When the slider is moved forward; the paste comes out from the other end (9). Thus the tube (5) has two ends (6) & (9).
This assembly of holder (1), slider (4) & pastetube (5) is sealed by putting a lock (3) at one end(6). The brush (2) is fitted on holder (1) by any known method. One such method is of thread arrangement (10). The other end (9) of the holder (1) has threads & a brush (2) with threading is fitted onto it.
The top face (11) of the holder & the top face (12) of the brush are in 180 degree out of phase m assembled condition so as to avoid the pressure of user's thumb on the slider (4) & on the tube. The fixing position, however, can be any conceivable as well.
During use of the combination product, first, the brush (2) is to be separated or disengaged from the holder assembly. Then by moving the slider (4) forward, toothpaste is taken out on the brush that is already disengaged from the assembly. After taking paste, the brush is to be again assembled with the holder (1). Now the unit is ready to be used as a brush for cleaning the teeth. After usage, The brush is washed & cleaned by water for further usage.
