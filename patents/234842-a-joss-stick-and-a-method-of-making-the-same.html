Title of Invention

A JOSS STICK AND A METHOD OF MAKING THE SAME
Abstract A joss stick, being a rod having an arrow shape, for use between sunrise of Wednesday and sunrise of Thursday, comprising an intimate mixture, prepared in a bass vessel on a Wednesday, in demineralized water of a base or fumigant made from Sawdust (wood powder) 20-35%, Coconut shell powder 5-15%Chlorophytum Borivilianum (White chipps)15-25%,Vateria indica, Linn(White ral) 5-10%as combustible materials, Litsea chinensis (Meda lakdi)powder 15-30%as a binder, a special aromatic fuel mixture comprising dry powders of bark of Achyranthes aspera (Apamarg) as samidha 40-55%,Vateria indica, Linn (Ral) as dhoop 15-20%,Saraca indica (Ashoka) petals as pushp 15-20%,Pterocarpus santalinus (Rakta-Chandan) as chandan 15-20% and Vateria indica, Linn (Ral) fragrance
