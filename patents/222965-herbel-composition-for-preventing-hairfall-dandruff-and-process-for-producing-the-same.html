Title of Invention

HERBEL COMPOSITION FOR PREVENTING HAIRFALL & DANDRUFF AND PROCESS FOR PRODUCING THE SAME
Abstract A herbal composition for preventing hair fall and drandruff in very short time i.e. within 2 to 3 days and without having any adverse effect. The herbal composition can be prepared either water based or oil bound to be used as hair oil which comprises Amla / Euphorbicaea (7 to 35%), Bhrangraj / Compositae (7 to 35%), Shikakai / Bixa Orellana (12 to 55%), Sajji mitti / Crude Carbonate of Soda (2 to 12%), Lime Juice / Rutaceae (7 to 35%) and Camphore / Comphora (0.5 to 2.5%).

This invention relates to a herbal composition for Preventing hair fall and dandruff and the process of producing the same.
More particularly this invention relates to a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff which is not only a purely ayurvedic product free from any harm full effects or side effects but which gives very quick noticeable effect by completely removing dandruff and preventing hair fall within a very short period, that is just with in two, three days of its daily use, further all the herbal ingredients used are quite cheap and readily available in local markets.
Many antidandruff or dandruff removal products as well as hair fall preventing products are available in the market but mostly, these known products are chemical based, thereby having harmful effects or side effects as well as time taking to show the results. Most of them have been found totally un-effective. Further two separate products are used for preventing hair fall and dandruff. The common problems faced by the users of known anti-dandruff products are, itching headache, increase in hair fall, besides these products are costly and non effective.
The main object of this invention is to obviate the above mentioned problems of the existing products and to provide a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff with the help of a single product, which is very effective showing results almost instantly, that is just within two to three days of its use.
Another object of this invention is to provide a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff which is purely an ayurvedic product free from any harmful effects or side effects.
A further object of this invention is to provide a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff in which all the ingredients used are easily available locally or indigenously.


A further object of this invention is to provide a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff which is quite cheap.
A further object of this invention is to provide a process of producing the herbal composition for preventing hair fall and drandruff which is quite simple, easy and environment friendly.
Accordingly this invention provides a herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dranduff comprising of Embiic Myrobalans (Amia) 7-35% by Wt., Compositae / Bhrangraj 7 - 35% by Wt.,/ Bixaorellana /Shikakai 10-50% by wt./ Rutaceac/Lime juice 7-35 by wt., Crude Carbonate of soda / Sajji mitti - 2-12% by wt. and Camphora Comphor 0.5-3%) by wt. prepared as water based or oil bound herbal composition / solution.
Typically the herbal composition in which the said Amia, Bhrangraj & Lime juice is preferably 15-25% by wt. and more preferably 18-22% by wt.
Typically the herbal composition in which the said Shikakai is preferably 25-40% by wt. and more preferably 30-35% by wt.
Typically the herbal composition in which the said Sajji mitti is preferably 5-8% by wt. and more preferably 6-7% by wt.
Typically the herbal composition in which the said Camphor is preferably 1.5-2.5% by wt. and more preferably 1.5-2%) by wt.
This invention also provides a process of producing herbal composition for pre¬venting hair fall and dranduff comprising the following steps :
(i) mixing dry powder of the Amia, Bhrangraj, Shikakai and Sajji mitti in desired proportion and soaking in water taken four times in volume of the total weight of the mixture, for two to three days;
(ii) boiling the mass till it reduces to half of its volume and filtering out the solution;

(iii) boiling the filtered solution of the step (ii) to reduce the same to its half volume and then cooling for mixing herbal solution;
(iv) mixing lime juice and camphor in desired proportion to the herbal solution of step (iii) forming a water based herbal composition of this invention.
This invention also provides a process of producing herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff comprising the following steps :
(i) mixing Amla extract, Bhrangraj extract and Shikakai extract indesired
proportion, (ii) mixing dry Sajji miltti powder and Citricacid in desired proportion and soaking in water taken two times in volume of the total weight of the mixture for about 24 hours, (iii) adding Paraffin oil in the mixture of the step (ii) in desired proportion and shaking for two to three hours and then separating out the paraffin oil; (iv) mixing the mixture of step (iii) into the mixture of step (i) forming herbal solution; (v) dissolving Camphor in desired amount in warm paraffin oil and mixing it with the solution of step (iv) forming the oil bound herbal composition.
The said extract of Amla, Bhrangraj and Sikakai used are any readily available extracts such as of 20:1 or 15:1 of 10:1.


The invention will be clear from the following description with reference to examples. The herbal composition for preventing hair fall and dandruff according to this invention can be prepared as water based or oil bound as per user requirement. The main ingredients used for water based as well as oil bound herbal composition, according to this invention are :
1) Euphorbicaeal / Emblic myroba lans commonly known as Amla,
2) Compositae / Eclipta alba, commonly known as Bhrang raj,
3) Bixa. Orellana / Acacia concinna, commonly known as Shikakai,
4) Crude carbonate of sada commonly known as sajju mitti,
5) Rutaceae / citrus autantifolia, commonly known as lime juice,
6) Comphora commonly known as camphor.
The source of all these herbal / Biological ingredients used in the invention is local market Harda, M^dhya Pradesh, India and these ingredients are very common and available every where.
The process for preparing water based herbal composition of this invention comprises the following steps.
i) Procuring dry powder of Amla, Bhrangraj Shikakai and Sajjimitti;
ii) Mixing in desired proportion the ingredients of step (i) such as for a batch of
100 % herbal composition Amla & Bhrangraj - 7 to 35 % preferably 15 to 25
% each;
Shikakai -12 to 55 kgs preferably 25 to 35 kgs, Sajjimitti - 2 to 12 Kgs
preferably - 5 to 8 kg ans soaking the mixture in water in a ratio 1 : 4, weight
by volume for two to three days; iii) boiling the soaked mass of step (ii) till mixture to water ratio becomes 1:1
forming a herbal solution; iv) further boiling the herbal solution of step iii till it remains 50% by volume; v) Cooling the solution to normal temperature and mixing lime juice 7 to 35 kg
preferably 15 to 25 kgs and Camphor 0.5 kg to 2.5 kgs preferably. The
solution to form water based herbal composition for preventing hair fall and
dandruff;

The process for preparing oil bound herbal composition of this invention comprises the following steps:
i) procuring Amla extract (20:1) Bhrangraj extract (20:1), and Shikakai extract
(20:1) mixing in a desired proportion e.g. 10-30% : 10:30%:15:50% by
weight; ii) procuring sajjimitti dry powder and citric acid dry powder mixing the same in
a desired proportion e.g. 10-30% : 3 - 10% by weight and soaking in water in
a ratio of 1:2 w/v, for about 24 hours; iii) adding paraffin oil 10-30% by volume in the mass of step (ii) and shaking for
two to three hours and then separating out the oil and mixing into the mixture
of step (informing herbal solutions; iv) dissolving camphor 2.5 to 7.5% by wt. into hot oil and mixing into the
solution of step (iii), forming oil bound herbal composition. Invention will become more clear from the following examples which are to be considered as illustrations and not to limit the scope of invention,
Example 1.
For preparing a batch of 55 ltr. Water based herbal composition of this invention dry powder of Amla lOkgs, Bhrangraj - 12kgs Shikakail8 kgs., Sajjimitti - 4 kgs are taken in a vessel and soaked in 176 ltr. water for two to three days then the mass is boiled till the water reduces to 88 ltr, it is then filtered forming herbal solution. Further heating the solution till it remains 44 ltr. Lime juice 10 ltr. and camphor 1 kg. is added into the solution forming water based herbal composition of about 55 ltr.
Example - 2.
For a batch of 21 ltr. Oil bound herbal composition of this invention mix (20:1) extract of, Amla 5 kg., Bhrangraj 5 kg. and shikakai - 8.5 kgs. in a vessel, Sajjimitti - 2 kg. and Citric acid - 0.7 kg. are mixed and soaked in 4 ltrs. of water for 24 hrs, then adding Paraffin oil 2 ltr. and shaking for two hours and then separating out the Paraffin


oil, and mixing in the mixture of Amla, Bhrangraj and Shikakai extracts, dissolving Camphor 350gms in 1 Itr. Paraffin oil, little warmed and mixed into the above mixture forming oil bound herbal composition of this invention.
The use of herbal composition of this invention is quite simple. The water based herbal compo¬sition is applied on the hair roots and left for at least 12 hrs. and then washed gently. Preferably hot water is used for washing the hair for better results in case of dandruff removal. When just applied for two to three days daily results are apparent both in preventing hair fall as well as dandruff.
The oil bound herbal composition is used just like a hair oil. The results are apparent with in two to three days just like in ease of water based herbal composition.
