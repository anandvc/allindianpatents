Insects trappinq on the ocean is known in the art,, One of the devices used for trappinq airborne
insects comprises trappinq sheats having adhesive coatinq/wet paint on the front side thereof. The main

Another device used for trappinq airborne insects on the ocean comprises a plurality of nets secured with a complicated system of small ringlets which are secured to a metallic chain. Another chain is provided for installing the device on the ship used for trappinq insects,, The main disadvantage associated with this device is that skilled persons are required to install the device on the ship.
Still another disadvantage is that the device is very heavy in weight and a number of persons are required for installing and operating the device,,

Still another disadvantage is that the plurality of ropes are required for installing the device on the ship. As a result the direction of the nets remains fixed and insects are not trapped when wind direction is not parallel to that of the device.
Therefore the main object of this invention is to propose a novel multinet insect trapping device which obviates the disadvantages associated with the known devices.
Another object of this invention is to propose a multinet insect trapping device wherein a single rope is required.
Still another object of this invention is to propose a multinet insects trapping device which is light in weight and can be installed and operated easily by a single person.
