Title of Invention

AN AYURVEDIC COMPOSITION FOR JOINING FRACTURED BONE & AS ANTI-INFLAMMATORY AND PROCESS FOR PREPARATION THEREOF
Abstract The present invention relates to an ayurvedic composition useful in the treatments of the fractured bones and as an anti-inflammatory medicine which comprises : (v) Cissus Quadrangularis powder (vi) Pterocarpus Marsupium heartwood powder (vii) Buffalo/ Cow Milk (viii) Chenopodium Murale (kurund) In a ratio of 1 to 2 : 0.4 to 0.8 : 40 : 0.4 to 0.8 respectively; the invention also relates to the process for the preparation of the same.
Full Text Field of the invention
This invention relates to an ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of bone fractures and body pains and also for the process for the preparation of the same. More particularly the composition of the present invention is very effective in joining the fractured bone when used in the manner as herein after described. The invented composition is also useful in relieving the body pains due to inflammation. The composition is useful in relieving the pain due to the arthritis.
Prior Art for the invention
Various medical techniques so far are available for joining of the fractured bone which
includes lengthy procedures like plastering of the fractured area for 3 to 10 weeks with
subsequent medication which include pain killer allopathic medicine which may develop
side effects, some of them may be very severe and may be life threatening. Thus such
treatments involves a higher amount of patience, money and side effects by the medicines
developed by the medicines ingested..
In ayurveda also there is no unidose medication is available which can treat the patient in
less irritating manner and may treat the patient in faster and effective way. Almost all the
known medical methods takeout to ten weeks in full repairing depending on the kind of
the fracture.
Object of the invention
The object of this invention is to provide an ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of bone fractures and body pains and also for the process for the preparation of the same. Further object of the invention is to provide a method for the treatment of the patient using the composition of the present invention for joining of the fractured bone and relieving the patient from the pain. Still further object of the invention is to provide a composition for the treatment of the arthritic pains and any other kind of the pains due to inflammation.

Clinical trial:

The patient with fracture of Radio Ulna was given the treatment with the
composition in the following quantities:
Cissus Quadrangularis linn powder 5 Gm
Buffalo milk 200 ml
Pterocarpus Marsupium 2 Gm
Chenopodium Murale (kurund) linn dried powder 2 Gm
This composition was given 2 times a day for 15 days with binding a crepe bandage around the fracture . the fracture was repaired fully and patient not needed to administer any anti-inflammatory separately.
The composition of the present invention as described above is an unique
composition , having unexpected properties as compared to the properties of it's
ingredients and therefore it is not a mere admixture.
