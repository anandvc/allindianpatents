SPIROPIPERIDINE DERIVATIVES FOR CONTROLLING PESTS
The present invention relates to hetero-spiroindoline derivatives to processes for preparing them to insecticidal acaricidal molluscicidal and nematicidal compositions comprising them and to methods of using them to combat and control insect acarine mollusc and nematode pests.

It has now surprisingly been found that certain hetero-spiroindolines have insecticidal properties.
