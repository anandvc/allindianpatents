Title of Invention

NOVEL PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS FOR CONTROLLED RELEASE DRUG DELIVERY SYSTEM BASED ON NOVEL POLYSACCHARIDE EXCIPIENT (HUPU GUM)
Abstract Novel compositions comprising Hupu gum as a novel excipient in pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals and cosmeceuticals wherein the said pharmaceuticals are in the form of in general and controlled release tablets of freely soluble drugs like Diltiazem hydrochloride, partially soluble drugs like Rifampicin, sparingly soluble drugs like Diclofenac Sodium in particular are disclosed herein.
