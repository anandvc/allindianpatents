The compositions of the present invention comprise a fatty 5 complexing agent.

10 fabric softening material thereby providing a composition which has relatively higher, levels of di-ester linked and tri-ester linked components. The di-ester and tri-ester linked components are more stable and do not affect initial viscosity as detrimentally as the mono-ester component.
15 Therefore, compositions already free of the mono- and tri-ester linked components do not fall within the scope of the invention.
The applicants also believe that that the complexing of the 20 mono-ester linked component (which does not contribute to softening) with the* fatty complexing material thereby provides a material which does contribute to softening.
