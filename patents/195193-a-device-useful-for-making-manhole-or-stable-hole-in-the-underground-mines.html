Title of Invention

A device useful for making manhole or stable hole in the underground mines
Abstract AA device useful for making manhole or stable hole in the underground mines, which comprises a cutter head coupled with a motor, the said head being connected with an advancing hydraulic ram , the said advancing ram being mounted upon a vertical frame ,the said frame is provided with a rack and pinion , the said rack and pinion is coupled with the said cutter motor through the transmission , the said vertical frame for cutter boom being supported by a multi telescopic hydraulic jack and a freely moving bottom support , the said hydraulic jack and bottom support being hinged upon a chassis , four wheels , an electric motor , a power pack , and electrical trans-switch being installed upon the said chassis , the said motor has power transmission for the said power pack and also for the movement of wheels , the said chassis also provided with one steering which is connected with front wheels and two mechanical breaks at both sides.
Full Text This invention relates to a device useful for cutting stable noie or manhole on the walls of underground mines required for mining and safety purpose.
In underground coal mines or even in non-coal mines, it is required to make man holes on the walls of a traveling road for miners. The height of such man holes are maintained generally as the common height of a man and about 1 m inside the rock of the mining wall. This type of holes are made as the emergency shelter for the mine workers traveling in the roads of underground mines es. The man holes provide temporary shelter and protection against many unseen dangers which may occur any time in the mines.
These manholes are generally made by the picking method i.e. by the help of labourer's picks. It is necessary to make hundreds of man holes to fulfill the requirement in a mine and at the same time the process is time taking and tedious job for the workers. Thus the safety process in the mines gets delayed.
Further, in the process of extraction of coal or minerals, sometimes it becomes necessary to cut stable holes on the wall of .mineral deposit or in the pillars. Sometimes, for making ditch on the side walls which is necessary for isolation stopping, generally pickminers are employed. Again, the process becomes very slow by the manual labourers and the purpose of safety of isolation stopping is not really fulfilled.
Till date there no such machine which can quickly cut a man hole or a stable hole or a ditch on the side walls of mineral deposits in underground mines.
The object of the present invention is to provide a suitable machine which can quickly cut man hole or stable hole or ditches on the side walls in underground mines and easily operable, maintainable, repairable and economically viable for the common Indian mines.

At the time of operation, the machine is anchored by mechanical brakes on both sides. Then the multi-telescopic ram (7) is operated through the valve box of power pack (8).
