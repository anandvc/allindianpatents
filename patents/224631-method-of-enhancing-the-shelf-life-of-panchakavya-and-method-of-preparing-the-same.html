Title of Invention

METHOD OF ENHANCING THE SHELF LIFE OF PANCHAKAVYA AND METHOD OF PREPARING THE SAME
Abstract A preservative hemitomus indicus (or) hemidesmus indicus or wallichi to enhance panchakavya shelf life to two years to make it marketable as packaged commodity.

This invention pertains to patient search in the herbal kingdom to get a product to enhance shelf life of panchakavya.

The ingredients buttermilk, curd in the products which are to be tackled for shelf life. Hemidermus indicus gave an amazing good shelf life upto 2 years. The experiment started in 1997 ended with good result by 2003 and confirmed our requirement.
