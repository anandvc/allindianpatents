Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF PREPARING A PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION FOR THE TREATMENT OF CHRONIC RENAL FAILURE AND HEART FAILURE
Abstract This invention provides a process of preparing a pharmaceutical composition for the treatment of chronic renal failure and also for heart failure comprising mixing the following ingredients in the proportions indicated there against: Coenzyme Q10 - 24.6% by wt. Vitamin E - 73.2-75.8% by wt. Lovastatin - 1.5-2.4 % by wt. and the balance if any adding a conventional filler for preparing the tablet or capsule.
