"A COMPOSITION CAPABLE OF ADMINISTRATING AN ACTIVE AGENT"
Abstract A composition capable of administering an active agent comprising 10-50% solvent as herein described mixed with 10-40% glycols and 10-40% of penetration enhancing agent as herein described being mixed therewith.
Full Text This invention relates to a composition capable of carrying/administering an active agent for example proteins, protein hormones and other proteindrugs through skin. Reference is made to copending patent application no. 1051/Del/96 relating to a composition comprising a vehicle and an active agent for treatment of pigmentary disorders of the skin and as a cosmetic preparation for tanning of the skin surface without exposure to sunlight. However, such a composition of the aforesaid patent application could only be employed for the aforesaid application. In distinction, the present invention relates to a vehicle for active agents for use in applications other than tanning of a skin surface.
An object of this invention is to propose a composition for active agents for applications other than tanning of skin surface or pigmentary disorder.

In accordance with this invention the composition has 1θ-5θ% volume by volume of solvent mixed with 1θ-4θ% of glycols and 1θ—4θ% weight by weight of penetration enhancing agent at an ambient temperature, preferably at a temperature around 25°C.
