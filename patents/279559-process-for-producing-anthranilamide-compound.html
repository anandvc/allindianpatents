Preparation of 1-cyclopropylethylamine (preparation using PtO2)

cyclopropyl methyl ketone, and 33.6 g of sodium cabonate was gradually added with vigorous stirring, followed

was washed with water and with a saturated sodium chloride aqueous solution and dried over anhydrous sodium
