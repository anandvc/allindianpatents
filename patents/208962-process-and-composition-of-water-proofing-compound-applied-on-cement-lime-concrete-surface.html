Title of Invention

PROCESS AND COMPOSITION OF WATER PROOFING COMPOUND APPLIED ON CEMENT/LIME CONCRETE SURFACE
Abstract A waterproofing compund comprising organic & inorganic base per each batch need as like Sodium Silicate - 50% Copper Sulphate - 0.999%, Detergent Powder - 0.999% . Molases - 0.999%, Formaline - 0.999% and water - 46.62%.
Full Text This invention related to water proofing compound is applicable on a cement / Lime concrete based new/old roof and wall surface.
A) Development Background : In a tropical country like India, intensity of rainfall varies greatly from one place to the other. This necessitates suitable water proofing methods as far as longevity of the roof is concerned. In view of this, Govt. organisations CPWD & CRBT conducted extensive trials on the efficiency & specification of various waterproofing methods available in the market and finally observed the following limitations in the specification. :
(a) Excessive high cost, (b) Additional Deed load,
(c) Requires considerations for buildings at constrction stage.
Present day use of bitumen felt as roof waterproofing is Prohibitive as :
(i) It's life is maximum 4 to 5 years, (ii) renders the roof incessible, (iii) absorbe heat, (iv)costly when compared to life.
In an effort to provide with a best alternative in this field, IRC RAINPROOF is came out with a highly effective as well as economical product. This product IRC RAINPROOF effectively reduces percolation of water through roofs and brick walls to zero.
B) The product : IRC RAINROOF is a complex chemical solution with organic base with
pore fillers and hardeners as admixtures. When applied, it fills the pores of the surfaces to a depth of 12mm and sets and hardens in contract with air thus leaves an impermeable top surface. This treatment is equally effective in old surface also.
Application is very simple and easy and even an unskilled labourer can perform the operation under proper supervision.
