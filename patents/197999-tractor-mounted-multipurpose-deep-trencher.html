Title of Invention

TRACTOR MOUNTED MULTIPURPOSE DEEP TRENCHER
Abstract Abstract This invention is in respect of tractor mounted multipurpose deep trencher which can be attached to any tractor and used to make deep and wide trapezoidal trenches for agriculture, horticulture and forest development activities. The said invention envisages tractor mounted multipurpose deep trencher compatible to attach on any tractor above 35 HP for digging trenches and comprises of a body having cutting tools mounted on either side of body comprising of adjustable slot fastened by nut and bolts such that the trencher size is at least 24 inches width on top, 12 inches width bottom and 24 inches height. The mould board and curved shank is disposed to hold body and chassis.The said chassis is being fitted in three hitch point to tractor .the cutting tool is disposed at an angle of 8-10 degrees from horizontal axis and the mould board is inclined at an angle of 30 degrees to vertical axis of trencher for lifting soil and throwing it out.
Full Text This invention relates to multi purpose trencher which is compatible as an attachment to tractor in order to dig trenches, these trenchers finds advantageous application in Agricu¬ltures Horticulture, forest land development activities in an easy, economical manner.
It is the object of invention to device trencher with this equipment it is possible to dig tenure of at least size 24 inches Top, 24 inches deep and 12 inches bottom for agricultural land waste land, dry land, Horticultural land and forest development activities, In Horticulture, it can be used to form trenches to plant saplings of Areca nut papaya, Banana, Apple Sapporo, sugarcane, and ginger, mango est.’s per recommended spacing It can also be used to form trenches to lay pipelines for drip irrigation. It can also be used in forming trenches to eliminate perennial weeds, social forestry for planting trees, furrows and ridges, seepage drains in submerged soil, side drain in roads etc.
It is still another object of invention to provide a trenches simple in construction with easy interchangeability compatible to tractors above 3511P,
Accordingly the present invention provides a tractor mounted multipurpose deep trencher comprising of body having at least two cutting tools, mould board and curved shank ' disposed to hold body and chassis and chassis being fitted in three hitch point to the tractor where in cutting tool is disposed at an angle of 8-10 degrees from horizontal and mould board inclined at an angle of at least 3 0 degrees to vertical axis for lifting soil and throwing it out.
