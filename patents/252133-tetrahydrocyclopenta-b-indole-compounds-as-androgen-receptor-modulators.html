Title of Invention

TETRAHYDROCYCLOPENTA[B] INDOLE COMPOUNDS AS ANDROGEN RECEPTOR MODULATORS
Abstract The present invention provides a compound of the formula: Formula (I) or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof; pharmaceutical compositions comprising aa compound of Formula (I) in combination with a suitable carrier, diluent, or excipient; and methods for treating physiological disorders, particularly reduced bones mass, osteoporosis, osteopenia, or reduced muscle mass or strength, comprising administering a compound of Formula (I), or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof.
