Title of Invention

"A SEALED PLASTIC PACKAGE WITH PLASTIC LAMINATE FILM"
Abstract A sealed package for an article of personal use. The package includes a plastic container that defines a storage region and has a sealing surface (22) around an entrance to the region. The package also includes a plastic, multi-layer laminate film (14) sealed to the sealing surface of the container. The film is removable away from the sealing surface in one piece and has deadfold characteristics.
