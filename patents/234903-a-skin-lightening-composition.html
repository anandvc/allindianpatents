Title of Invention

A SKIN LIGHTENING COMPOSITION
Abstract The invention provides for a skin lightening composition comprising: (i)0.05-10%by weight vitamin B6 or derivative thereof (ii)0.05-10%by weight vitamin b3 or derivative thereof and (iii)0.05-20%by weight at least one organic acid selected from mono,di or poly carboxylic or phenolic acids/esters/salts or derivatives thereof.
