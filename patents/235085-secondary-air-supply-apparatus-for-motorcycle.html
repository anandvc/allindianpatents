SECONDARY AIR SUPPLY APPARATUS FOR MOTORCYCLE
Abstract [Object] To facilitate, in a secondary air supply apparatus for a motorcycle wherein part of a passage for sypplying secondary air to an exhaust system for an engine carried on a body frame including a frame pipe is formed in the frame pipe, maintencace of a secondary air cfleaner, and to eliminate the necesity for attachment of a suplus part in conneciton of an intake tube to a frame pipe therby to reduce the cost. [Sloving Means] An intake tube 64 is connected at a downstream end thereof to an intake opening of a secondary air cleaner 63 disposed outwardly of the body frame 25 and supporeted on the body frame 25, and an upstream end of the intake tube 64 is inserted in an end opeing of the frame pipe 30 with a sound insulating material 65 interposed between the upsteram end of the intake tube 64 and an inner face of the frame pipe 30.
