Title of Invention

STERILIZING EQUIPMENT FOR THE CAPS OF DRINK-CONTAINERS
Abstract A sterilizing equipment for the caps (10) of drink-containers which is treated and guided at least one trail with a closed gas flowing casing (1), that is run through tracks (11, 6.1-6.4, 13.1-13.3; 14) where by the track inside the casing (1) is arranged downwards many times around a vertical axis (3). It is characterized by that for every turn of a carrying ring (6.1-6.4) between radial inside as well as outside the track (6.1-6.4) limited lateral guides (5.9) are arranged and that a sector-section lying one above the other of the outside lateral guide (9) stationary overloading slides (13.1-13.3) are arranged, which take over the caps from the upper carrying ring 6.1) and transfer to outside as well as towards bottom tilted carrying ring (6.2), where by a supply conducts the caps to the highest carrying ring (6.1) and a purging (14) removes the caps (10) from the bottom-most carrying ring (6.4).
