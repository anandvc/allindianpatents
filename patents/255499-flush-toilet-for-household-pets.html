This invention relates to house pet toilets and more particularly household flush toilets having attachments to facilitate their use by pets indoors.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
In practice it has been observed that there is no proper means for providing the litter box for maintaining the cleanliness of the house to remove the feces and to eliminate the smell coming out of the excrement of the pets like cats and dogs as they relieve themselves at any place they like. Thus, in order to provide with a proper means for relieving the household pets and with the purpose of maintaining the sanitary and clean environment in the house the present Flush toilet ahs been provided in the present invention.

DETAILED DESCRPITION OF THE PREFERRED EMBODIMENTS
The toilet has a top standing board 1 surrounded at the side with an upright fencing wall 2 to provide privacy for the pet. The top of the toilet has a flat platform 3 provided with a stepped standing board 4 having various dimensions in order to accommodate pets of various body sizes. The stepped standing board 4 has a small inner step 5 designed for a kitten to stand on. The excrement passes through the center opening 6 of the standing board into a conventional toilet 7 which has a water tank 8. A sloping board 9 is provided for the pet to get on or off the toilet. A training ring 10 is located at the rear of the standing board, and the sloping board 9 is mounted to the platform by hinges 11. The toilet has a flushing ducts 13 and a drain 14. The excrement falls into the water pool 15 in the toilet when it is flushed. An inside standing board 16 is positioned within the toilet, which may be lifted with a handle rod 17. The water tank is provided with an operating pivot handle18. The toilet has water pipes 19 and 20. The water pipe 20 is for conducting water to the outlet opening 21 for cleaning the side walls of the toilet with flushing water.
The present invention provides a flush toilet or accessories which may be incorporated in a conventional flush toilet for removing excrements indoors for household pets such as cats and dogs.
