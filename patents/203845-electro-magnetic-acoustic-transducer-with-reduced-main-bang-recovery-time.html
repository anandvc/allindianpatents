Title of Invention

"ELECTRO-MAGNETIC ACOUSTIC TRANSDUCER WITH REDUCED MAIN BANG RECOVERY TIME"
Abstract An electro-magnatic acoustic transducer with reduced main bang recovery time,comprising: a transmitter electro-magnetic acoustic transducer coil(12)adjacent the transmitting coil(10),the receiving coil(12)having a plurality of sections which are separated by a pitch distance;and means(14)for resistively shielding the transmitter coil from the receiver coil. The transducer of this invention can be used for testing a work piece.
