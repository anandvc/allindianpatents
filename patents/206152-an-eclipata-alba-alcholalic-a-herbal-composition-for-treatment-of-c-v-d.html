Description: -
The present invention relates to an Eclipta alba extractant containg herbal composition for treatment of coronary vascular diseases hyperlipidemia and hyper cholesterolemia comprising an extract of Eclipta alba from the raw material of pieces, dried in shade for more than 4 days, and grinded to to make fine powder. The powder dried material was taken for extraction mixed with methanol or hot percolation method for a period of more than three days .The solvent was removed by distillation oyer boiling water bath at

atmospheric pressure and remaining under reduced pressure in a boiling water bath and finely dried in vacuum desiccators. The resultant alcoholic Eclipta Alba shall be used for the preparation of herbal composition for the treatment of coronary vascular diseases. The composition comprising Eclipta alba alcoholic extract ant in the range of 1 mg/kg to 2 gm/kg , and Terminalia arjina in the range of 10 mg /kg to 25 mg /kg was initially suspended in water and added to a composition commiphora mukul was extracted with 95% alcohol subsequently filtered and distilled under reduce pressure to obtain alcohol free extract was added to the composition in the range of 10 mg /kg to 20 mg /kg further emblica officinalis fresh fruit was obtained and the juice was taken out initially analyzed for Vitamin -C approximately 100 gm content was added to the above composition in the range of 5 mg /kg to 15 mg/kg further embodiment of the present invention is Azadiracta Indica its alcoholic extract of 5 mg /kg was added to the composition.
Example;-
Taken 1.5 mg /kg of alcoholic extractant of Eclipta alba was mixed with 20 mg of Terminalia arjina in mixer further 15 mg of Commiphora mukul was taken and added to the above composition. 10 mg of Emblica officinalis added along with 10 mg of Azadiracta Indica to make a herbal composition, which could ready for CVD,s treatment.
