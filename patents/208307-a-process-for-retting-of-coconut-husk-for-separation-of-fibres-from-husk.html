Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR RETTING OF COCONUT HUSK FOR SEPARATION OF FIBRES FROM HUSK
Abstract This invention relates to a process of retting for causing a seperation of fibres from husk. According to the process a micro organism medium is applied in atleast two steps to the husk. The micro organism chosen from a species capable of consuming the phenolic compounds and pectins is used so as to losen the bond of the fibres.
