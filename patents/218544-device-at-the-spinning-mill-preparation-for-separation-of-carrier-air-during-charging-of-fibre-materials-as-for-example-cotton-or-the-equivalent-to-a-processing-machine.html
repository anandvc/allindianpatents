Title of Invention

DEVICE AT THE SPINNING MILL PREPARATION FOR SEPARATION OF CARRIER AIR DURING CHARGING OF FIBRE MATERIALS AS FOR EXAMPLE COTTON OR THE EQUIVALENT TO A PROCESSING MACHINE.
Abstract IN A DEVICE AT THE SPINNING MILL PREPARATION FOR SEPARATION OF CARRIER AIR DURING CHARGING OF FIBRE MATERIALS, AS FOR EXAMPLE COTTON AND THE EQUIVALENT, TO A PROCESSING MACHINE (1) IN WHICH A MAINLY VERTICAL FILING CHUTE (16) FOR THE MATERIAL (D) IS PROVIDED TO WHOSE UPPER ENTRY OPENING (a)AN EQUIPMENT WITH PNEUMATIC FIBRE SUPPLY AND TO WHOSE LOWER EXIT OPENING AN EQUOPMENT (4d) FOR FLOCCULATION WITH A TAKING-IN EQUIPMENT (4a to 4d) AND A RAPID RUNNING OPENING ROLLER (4e) ARE ALLOCATED FOR SEPARATION (elimination) OF FIBRE MATERIALS (D) FROM THE CARRIER AIR STREAM (E) AIR PRESENT, THE CARRIER AIR STREAM (E) IS LED ALONG THE OPENING ROLLER (4e). IN ORDDR TO MAKE POSSIBLE THE PNEUMATIC CARRY AWAY OF THE RELEASED FIBRE FLOCKS (5) TO A RECONNECT EQUIPMENT (7) AND FOR THIS THE FORMATION OF A CARRIER AIR STREAM (E) OF CONSTANT OR MAINLY CONSTANT QUANTITY, A PART AIR STREAM (F) OF SEPARATED CARRIER AIR STREAM (E) IS LED TO AN EXHAUST EQUIPMENT, (11) IN WHICH AN AIR STREAM REGULATING EQUIPMENT (14) IS PRESENT, WHICH STANDS IN COMBINATION WITH A CONTROLLER REGULATING EQUIPMENT.
Full Text FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The invention refers to the device in the spinning mill preparation for separation
of carrier air during charging of fibre materials, as for example cotton and the
equivalent to a processing machine in which a filing chute mainly vertical is
provided for the fibre material where an equipment with pneumatic fibre supply
is allocated to the upper entry opening and an equipment for flocculation with a
taking-in equipment and a rapid running opening roller allocated to the lower exit
and at which least one air permeable surface for separation (elimination) of fibre
materials from carrier air stream and an equipment for discharge of carrier air
stream are present which in the carrier air stream is guided along the opening
roller.
PRIOR ART
In a known device (DE-OS 3217668) the entire carrier air stream flows along the
opening roller and gets into post laid flock chute with an air permeable wall
surface. The carrier air stream is separated from the fibre flocks and followingly
discharged while the fibre flocks are transported further through a roller. The
carrier air stream is drawn in for compression of fibre flocks deposited in the
flock chute. It is disadvantageous that in practice the carrier air stream which
transports the fibre flocks to upper reserve chute fluctuates, so that also the
separated carrier air stream is irregular in considerable quantity.
EP.0563700 discloses an apparatus for controlling the air flow volume in an air
canal wherein a fibre shaft with three air impermeable walls and with an air-
permeable wall has an airfibre.
The present invention discloses a device at the spinning mill peration for
seperation of carrier air during charging of fibre materials as for example cotton
or the equivalent to a processing machine, the seperation takes place due to
creation of differenical air pressure in the seperation chamber.
The assignment therefore lies at the base of the invention to procure a device
described at entry which avoids the mentioned disadvantages which specially
makes possible the pneumatic carrying away of the released fibre flocks to a
reconnected equipment and for this permits the formation of a carrier air
stream of constant or mainly constant quantity.
The solution of this assignment takes place through the characteristic
features as described in the embiment of the invention.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
Through it that a part air stream is separated from the eliminated carrier air
stream and the quantity of the part air stream is adjustable, a carrier air
stream of constant or mainly constant quantity is generated. In this way it is
possible to use the changing air stream for disposal of the separator and
consequently to reduce the energy and processing cost of the pneumatic
material transport. A further advantage exists therein that in the application
of machine couplings in which the charging air stream is greater than the
disposing air stream, the charging air flow is to be distributed with the help of
the regulating system such that the air stream under disposal receives a
constant quantity.

For the pneumatic flock conveying between the separator 1 and the material

last roller la of the four roller separators 1 through an air stream B, which is

The fibre flock-air-mixture C is flung on the inside wall surface of a perforated
plate 10 of material separator over distribution gate 9, wherein the fibre
flocks D are separated from the carrier air E. The carrier air E gets into an
exhaust chamber 11, to which an exhaust channel 12 and an exhaust pipe
line 13 are connected in each case with their one end. The other end of the

Below the perforated plate 10 a vertical flock chute 16 with air permeable
wall surfaces are assemble at whose lower end a metering separator 4 is

In this way a device for elimination of the carrier air E during charging in a

The device as per invention removes the disadvantage according to which -
