Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PRODUCTION OF GAS DIFFUSION ELECTRODE AND THE ELECTRODE PRODUCED THEREBY
Abstract Summary The invention relates to a process for production of gas diffusion electrodes from a silver catalyzer based on PTFE substrate, where the pore system of the silvery catalyzer is filled with a wetting filling material, a dimensions table solid body with a grain size upper half of that of the silver catalyst is mixed under the silvery catalyst, this compression table mass so obtained is formed in a first calendar to a homogenous catalyst tape, in a second calendar in an electrically conductible charge eliminating material is embedded in the catalyzer tape, and between the first and second calendar a heating with the help of a heating device is carried out, in which at least part of the wetting filling material is stripped off, as well as the gas diffusion electrode produced after completion of the process (Figure 1).
Full Text Patent Claims
