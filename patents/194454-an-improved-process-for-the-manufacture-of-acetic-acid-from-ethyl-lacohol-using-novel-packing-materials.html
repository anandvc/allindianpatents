Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED PROCESS FOR THE MANUFACTURE OF ACETIC ACID FROM ETHYL LACOHOL USING NOVEL PACKING MATERIALS"
Abstract There is provided an improved method for the manufacture of acetic actd by Oxidation of ethyl alcohol in the presence of bacteria of the type in a counter-current contact in a packed column characterized by the improved that the counter current contact of liquid and air/oxygen is carried out using trough shaped packing materials, made of conventional high alumina ceramic.
Full Text Field of the invention
This invention relates to an Improved process for the Manufacture of Acetic Acid from Ethyl Alcohol using a novel packing material
Prior art and draw backs
It is already known to produce Vinegar by bacterial oxidation of dilute Ethyl Alcohol This process is the traditional process of slow fermentation through the alcohol state to the acid stage It is also known to trickle the fermented liquid mixed with some vinegar over shavings In order to quicker the process The drawbacks of this known process are many of which mention may be made of the following
a) This is a slow process taking about 12 hours for fermentation.
b) It is usually earned out in a packed tower where dilute alcohol containing bacteria is fed from the top and air is injected from the bottom It is necessary to circulate the alcohol solution several times within the tower in the presence of air
c) The packing material used in the tower is of conventional shape Bearl Saddles, Raschig Rings and other shaped material and in the packed column channeling takes place.
d) In the conventional towers the flow pattern of liquid is mostly laminar and less significant turbulence In the invented process the flow path of the liquid is mostly turbulent and hence resistance to diffusion by liquid layer is significantly reduced which results in higher rate of mass transfer.

Brief Statement of the invention
Thus, according to this invention, there is provided an improved method for the manufacture of acetic acid (Vinegar) by Oxidation of ethyl alcohol in the presence of bacteria of the type bacterium aceta in a counter-current contact in a packed column characterized by the improvement, that the counter current of liquids and air/oxygen is carried out using trough shaped packing materials made of conventional high alumina ceramic
In carrying out the above method plurality of the trough shaped packing materials are loosely dumped in the packing area of the tower
The trough shaped packing materials are of uniform shape and size and are made of conventional ceramic material
Each packing material comprises a solid bottom part and a surrounding uprising wall part
The bottom part is of circular elliptical, square, rectangular or polygonal shape with corresponding surrounding uprising walls divergent from the base
3

The liquid air/oxygen contact is earned out preferably by providing vibratory motion during the contact of liquid and oxygen
Further, according to the invention, there is provided a novel packing material for gas-liquid counter Gurrent contact in a packed column wherein the liquid is fed from the top and the gas Is admitted from the lower part of the tower comprising a solid bottom wall or part and corresponding surrounding uprising wall/walls, divergent from the base,
As stated before the bottom wall is of circular, elliptical square, rectangular or of any regular or irregular polygonal shape
It is also possible to have combination of these troughs shaped materials wherein a pair of the packing materials are joined at their bases with the uprising wall portions contra located

Detailed description of the accompanying drawings
Referring to improved packing material, this is made of as a single trough shaped material or a double trough shaped material or as a plurality of troughs made as a unitary item in any required Geometrical shape such as a polygon
The trough comprises a base (1) and four side walls (2)
The base can be circular or elliptical or of any other suitable size or shape The side wall is-divergent from the base and is of a required short height When plurality of such packing materials are dumped in the tower, and alcoholic liquor is admitted from the top, the liquid fills the trough and over flows continuously There is continuous filling and over flowing There is therefore, better contact between alcohol and Oxygen The rate of conversion is improved and conversion time is considerably reduced
Further additional advantages can be obtained if the base of the trough, instead of being flat, is provided with an up riser section
The novel packing material is made in the conventional manner from conventional high alumina ceramic material using suitable moulds to the required shape and size
5

Further improved results can be obtained by providing usual vibrating means at the bottom of the packed tower
Vibration diminishes channeling in the tower Also rate of conversion is enhanced since each trough acts Tike a stirred tank reactor and eliminates diffusional transfer of Oxygen
