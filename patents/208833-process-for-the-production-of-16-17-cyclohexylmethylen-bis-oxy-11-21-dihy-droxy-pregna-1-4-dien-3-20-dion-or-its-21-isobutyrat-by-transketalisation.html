Technical field
The invention relates to a novel process for the preparation of a known glucocorticoid, which is used in the pharmaceutical industry for the production of medicaments.
Prior art
The International patent application WO 9422899 describes novel prednisolone derivatives and a proc¬ess for their preparation. In this process, 16-hydroxyprednisolone Is reacted with cyclohexanealdehyde, German patent application DE 41 29 535 discloses novel glucocorticoids and a process for their prepa¬ration. The process comprises reacting pregna-1,4-diene-3,20-dione-l6,17-dihydroxy compounds, in the form of their 16,17-diester derivatives, with aldehydes (e.g. with cyclohexanealdehyde) to give the desired final products.
