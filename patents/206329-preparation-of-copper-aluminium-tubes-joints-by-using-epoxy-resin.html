Title of Invention

PREPARATION OF COPPER ALUMINIUM TUBES JOINTS BY USING EPOXY RESIN
Abstract A process for "Preparation of Copper-Aluminum Tubes Joints by Using Epoxy Resin" comprises of: a) aluminum and Copper tube are straightened and cut in specified sizes for inlet & outlet after ensuring constant supply of coolant on cutting wheel damp; b) deburring on the said aluminum & copper tubes is done on a bench grinder by rotating both the said of tube on a grinding wheel; c) the said aluminum tube flairing is carried out at one end for specified size; d) the said flaked end of aluminum tube is provided with internal threading and external threading is done on copper tube; e) epoxy Resin is applied on the said copper tube then inserted in flared aluminum tube to form threaded joint and said joint is crimped after inserting mandrill there in; f) the Said crimped joint is cured in an oven at a temperature of 180° C for fifteen minutes, then leak test is carried out at specified pressure for specified time

In the present process, first of all aluminium tube is straightened and then cut for inlet and outlet. The aluminium and copper tube cutting is done after ensuring constant supply of coolant on cutting wheel clamp straightens copper tube in the cutting fixture. Then, copper tube deburring is carried out.
Copper tube threading is done. Then aluminium tube deburring is done Deburring is done on a bench grinder by rotating both the sides of tube on a grinding wheel. Aluminium tube straightening is done and checked on surface plate. Aluminium tube flaring is carried out. Internal threading on flared portion of aluminium tube is done.
