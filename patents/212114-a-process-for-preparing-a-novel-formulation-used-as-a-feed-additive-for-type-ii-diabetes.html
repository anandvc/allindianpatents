Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PREPARING A NOVEL FORMULATION USED AS A FEED ADDITIVE FOR TYPE II DIABETES
Abstract This invention relates to a process for preparing novel formulation used as a feed additive for type II diabetes comprising mixing 5% to 25% of bean, 2.5% to 15% of trigonella foenum, 10% to 40% of guar gum, 2.5% to 20% of momordica charantia, 1% to 15% of piper nigrum, 5% to 35% of soybean defatted, 5% to 35% of cicer arietinum (bengal gram) and other pulses, 2.5% to 20% of hordeum vulgare (barely powder) and 0.1% to 5% of anti-oxidant and anti-caking agent selected from vita-C, sodium methyl paraben, calcium carbonate.
Full Text -1A-
This invention relates to a novel formulation used as a food additive for type II diabetes.
Substantial efforts have been extended to find new food additives capable of substituting the carbohydrate part of the principal meals of a patient suffering of diabetes. However, the inventor of the present invention has been successful in formulating such a diet additive.
An object of this invention is to propose a formulation for patients suffering of diabeties.
Another object of this invention is to propose a process for preparing a formulation for diabetic patients.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a formulation and a process of preparing the formulation useful as an additive to carbohydrate part of the principal meal for type II diabetics.
According to this invention there is provided a formulation used as a feed additive for type II diabetics comprising 5% to 25% beans, 2.5% to 15% of trigonella foenum, 10% to 40% guar gum, 2.5% to 20% of momordica charantia, 1% to 15% of piper nigrum, 5% to 35% of soyabean defatted, 5% to 35% cicer arietinum and other pulses, 2.5% to 20% of hordeum vulgare and 0.1% to 5% of antioxidant and anticaking agent.
Further, according to this invention there is also provided a process of preparing a formulation comprising mixing 5 to 25% beans, 2.5% to 15% trigonella foenum, 10% to 40% of guar gum, 2.5% to 20% of momordica charantia, 1% to 15% of piper nigrum, 5% to 35% of soyabean defatted, 5% to 35% of cicer arietinum and other pulses, 2.5% to 20% of hordeum vulgare and 0.1% to 5% of antioxidant and anticaking agent.
