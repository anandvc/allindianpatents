As described above, a primary objective of the present invention is to increase the internal volume available in a battery for containing the electrochemically active materials to volumes previously not obtained. To achieve this objective without detrimentally decreasing the reliability of the pressure relief mechanism provided in the
battery and without increasing the likelihood that the battery would otherwise leak, various novel modifications are suggested below to the construction of batteries of various sizes.
