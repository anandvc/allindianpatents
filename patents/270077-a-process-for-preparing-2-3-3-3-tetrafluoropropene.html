Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR PREPARING 2,3,3,3 TETRAFLUOROPROPENE"
Abstract The present invention provides a process for its preparation comprising (a) converting 1,1,1-trifluoro-2,3-dichloropropane (243db) to 3,3,3-trifluoro-2-chloro-prop-1-ene (CF3CCI=CH2) in the presence of a first catalyst in a first reactor, (b) contacting CF3CCI=CH2 with a fluorinating agent in the presence of a second catalyst in a second reactor to produce a compound of formula CF3CFXCH3, wherein X = CI or F, and (c) dehydrohalogenating the compound of formula CF3CFXCH3 to produce 2,3,3,3-tetrafluoropropene (1234yf).
