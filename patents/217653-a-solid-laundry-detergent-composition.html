Title of Invention

"A SOLID LAUNDRY DETERGENT COMPOSITION "
Abstract A Schiff Base reaction product is formed by providing a perfume component, providing a primary amine, providing an acid catalyst, providing a solvent, and providing a mild base. The perfume component, the methyl anthranilate and the acid catalyst are combined in the solvent to form a reaction mixture. The reaction mixture is agitated, and then neutralized with the mild base to form a Schiff Base reaction product. A solid laundry detergent composition, especially a solid shaped laundry detergent composition, may also include therein from about 0.05% to about 5% of a Schiff Base reaction product, from about 5% to about 60% surfactant, from about 1% to about 60% builder, and the balance adjunct materials.
