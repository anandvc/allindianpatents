The present invention relates to an improved process for preparation of Triazophos.
More particularly, the present invention relates to a process for manufacturing of Triazophos using 0.02% to 2% one-phase transfer catalysts in high yield and at least 92% purity.
Triazophos having insecticidal and acericidal properties used for fighting against different type of plant and animal pest.

It is the object of present invention to manufacture triazophos using phase transfer catalysts in high yield and purity.
