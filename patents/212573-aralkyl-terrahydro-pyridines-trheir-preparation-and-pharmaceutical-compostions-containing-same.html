Title of Invention

ARALKYL-TERRAHYDRO-PYRIDINES, TRHEIR PREPARATION AND PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSTIONS CONTAINING SAME
Abstract The invention concerns compounds of formula (I), wherein: X represents N or CH; R1 represents a hydrogen or halogen atom of a CF3 group; n is an integer from 1 to 5; A represents a parity saturated bicyclic or tricyclic carbon compound, as well as their salts or solvates, their N-oxides, pharmaceutical compositions containing them, a method for preparing them and synthesis intermediates in said method.

The compounds of the invention have
advantageous properties with respect to the inhibition of TNF- .

17
These properties were demonstrated with the aid of a test aimed at measuring the effect of molecules on the synthesis of TNF- induced in Balb/c mice by lipopolvsaccharide (LPS) from Escherichia Coli (055;B5, Sigma, St. Louis, Mo).
The test products are administered orally to groups of 5 female 7- to 8-week old Balb/c mice (Charles River, Prance). One hour later, the LPS is administered intravenously (10 g/mouse). The blood of each animal is taken 1.5 hours after the administration of the LPS. The samples are centrifuged and the plasma is recovered and frozen at -80°C. The TNF-a is measured using commercial kits (R and D, Abingdon, UK).
In this test, representative compounds of the invention were found to be very active, by inhibiting the synthesis of TNF-a even at very low doses.
