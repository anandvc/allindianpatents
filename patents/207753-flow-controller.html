Title of Invention

FLOW CONTROLLER
Abstract A flow controller for controlling the outlet flow of a fluid comprising a pipe separated into two chambers, one being the inlet chamber and the other being the outlet chamber, a device chamber in fluid communication with the said outlet chamber, a flexible hose biased with the tension mechanism being contained in the said device chamber, a plate being provided at the partition of the chamber thus forming a openable gate and the said plate being connected to the said flexible hose by means of a rod such that when the pressure drops in the outlet chamber the flexible hose moves down and so the plate and the gate opens.

WORKING OF THE INVENTIONS :
The working of the flow controller can be better understood with the following situation. When the water tank is on the top floor of the apartment and delivery lines are there to all the flats. Pressure in the line increases or decreases depending upon the headTo control required flow controller is provided in each line. When cock of outlet side is opened pressure within the outlet chamber goes down little and the gate opens slightly. When the second cock is opened the pressure will go further down and the gate will open further. Similarly when the third cock is opened the pressure will further reduce and the gate will be opened further. This way one by one cocks will be opened, gate will be opened more and more. When a cock is closed gate will be closed a little. Similarly when we close cocks one by one the gate closes accordingly. In another situation when water is distributed in sub lines from the mainline, flow controller controls the flow in sub mains up to" the end of distributary line. When water is pumped then in thisJine vacuum; is created and more water flow goes out. To prevent more water flow going out, a double gate flow controller can be provided. In this controller the second gate is closed dueto the vacuum created.
