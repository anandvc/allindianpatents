Title of Invention

APPARATUS FOR PREVENTING PASSING OFF OF A BREWED BEVERAGE
Abstract This invention is directed to an apparatus for brewing a beverage. The apparatus has a brew funnel (10), a key (38) and an elevation device (26) so that only one brand of beverage can be brewed to thereby prevent harm and confusion to consumers.
