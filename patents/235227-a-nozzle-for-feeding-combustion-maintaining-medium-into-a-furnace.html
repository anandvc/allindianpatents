Title of Invention

A NOZZLE FOR FEEDING COMBUSTION MAINTAINING MEDIUM INTO A FURNACE
Abstract A NOZZLE FOR FEEDING COMBUSTION MAINTAINING MEDIUM INTO A FURNACE A nozzle for feeding combustion maintaining medium into a furnace at high temperature conditions, said nozzle comprising: a nozzle tip, at least partly protruding into said furnace, and feeding means, for feeding said combustion medium from a source of said medium to said nozzle tip, said nozzle tip having a mainly open ended outer shell, having a first end wall portion in flow connection with said feeding means and a second end wall portion protruding into the furnace, an air cooling zone formed on the interior side of at least a portion of said second e3nd wall portion of the outer shell, by providing an air flow along said interior side, characterized in that said nozzle tip has a shroud means having a shroud wall portion disposed to cover at least a portion of said first end wall portion of the outer shell, an air shroud channel formed between said shroud wall portion of the shroud means and said first end wall portion of the outer shell, for discharging from said air shroud channel an air shroud flow along the exterior side second end wall portion of the outer shell.
