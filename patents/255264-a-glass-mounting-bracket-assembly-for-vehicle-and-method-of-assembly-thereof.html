Title of Invention

A GLASS MOUNTING BRACKET ASSEMBLY FOR VEHICLE AND METHOD OF ASSEMBLY THEREOF
Abstract This invention relates to glass mounting system of vehicles and more particularly this invention relates to improved glass mounting bracket assembly and method of assembly thereof. In accordance with this invention, improved glass mounting bracket assembly comprises; Upright leg portion (1),Central base portion (3) for supporting said upright leg member. First and second pillar member (2, 9) extended perpendicularly from the said central base portion (3). Hinge member (4) provided on the bottom portion of said first and second pillar member (2, 9) which rotates about its axis to provide flexibility to insert the side of the said bracket, Central flat member (5) which is hingedly connected at the bottom to the said first and second pillar members (2, 9),Plastic molded metallic insert (6) provided on said central flat member (5),Insert hole (7) with thread provided on the said plastic molded metallic insert (6),Snap member (8) provided on the edge portion of the said central flat member for locking nut plate with said first and second pillar members (2. 9).Boss member (10) provided on the surface of the said upright leg member (1). Hole (11) provided on the said boss member for mounting, Extended hanger member (12) extending preferably perpendicular to the surface of the said upright leg member, Flat surface (13) of said extended hanger member. The present improved glass mounting bracket assembly is positive mounting and permanent arrangement.

FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to glass mounting system of vehicles and more particularly this invention relates to improved glass mounting bracket assembly and method of assembly thereof.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Generally glass mounting clips are used in window glass of vehicles and existing glass mounting clips are self tapping one and that self tapping clip arrangement has drawback of loosening with the time and also it has to be thrown away after one fitment.
The same clip cannot be used again. There is a risk of damaging the clip with slight over tightening. In order to address this issue, positive mounting clips means are required, so new improved glass mounting bracket assembly has been developed which addresses the above limitations.
OBJECTS OF INVENTION
The main object of the present invention is to provide an improved glass mounting bracket assembly for vehicles.
Another object of the present invention is to provide method of assembling improved glass bracket assembly for vehicles.


Yet another object of the present invention is to provide an improved glass mounting bracket assembly for vehicles which is a positive mounting arrangement.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide and improved glass mounting bracket assembly for vehicles which is a permanent arrangement.

improved glass bracket assembly comprises;
Upright leg portion (1),
Central base portion (3) for supporting said upright leg member,

central base portion (3),
Hinge member (4) provided on the bottom portion of said first and second pillar

Said hinge member (4) provided on the first and second pillar member (2, 9) extended perpendicularly from the said central base portion (3) which rotates about its axis to provide flexibility to insert the side of the said bracket. This facilitates easy insertion of the window winding glass in the said bracket.
Plastic molded metallic insert (6) which is M6 screw insert. Said M6 screw insert use to get engaged in the said plastic molded metallic insert (6) which clamps said glass with window winding regulator. The said plastic molded metallic insert (6) provides positive clamping arrangement of the window winding glass and the regulator.
Said first and second pillar member (2,9) use to flex during engagement of the said snap member (8) provided on the insert side wall of said first and second pillar members (2,9).
Extended hanger member (12) helps to hang the said glass on the said regulator temporarily. Wherein said hanger extending from said upright leg portion preferably perpendicular to the surface of the said leg portion is L- shape hanger. This engages with the said regulator glass lifting plate. This saves assembling time of said regulator with the said glass.
Boss member (10) is the projection on the surface of the said upright leg portion (1). The said boss member is inserted in the said glass hole. The said boss member (10) also acts as guider for the M6 bolt and to locate precisely the window winding glass. It prevents the relative movement between said glass and said mounting bracket after clamping with the said bolt. Wherein said boss member is


the projection inserted in the glass hole which acts as guider to locate precisely the winding glass.
Snap member (8) provided on the side edge of the said central flat member (5). The said snap member (8) is used to engage with the said first and second pillar members (2, 9). Said snap member (8) holds insert side wall with said first and second pillar members (2,9) during the engagement of the M6 bolt with insert. Wherein said snap member is press fitted arrangement to engage with the said first and second pillar members.
In accordance with the present invention, the method of assembly comprises;
Providing the holes in the winding glasses at desired location and desired pitch,
Preparing a suitable boss member (10) corresponding to said glass hole,
Inserting glass between the said bracket by flexing said first and second pillar
members (2,9) up to the thickness of glass in order to fit the glass on said boss
member (10), during assembly of a said glass with the said bracket,
Tilting of the said glass with respect to mounting clip in order to reduce the
flexing of the said first and second pillar members (2,9),
Pressing the said snap member (8) against the said first and second pillar members
(2,9) till the locking of nut plate portion, after inserting the glass on the said boss
member (10).
