The invention relates to a strip member for locking plurality of outlets by means of single padlock. More particularly the invention relates to a strip member for locking plurality of outlets by means of single padlock for transporting inflammable liquid material to the destination securely.
The oil tankers are provided with multiple outlets with valve members with wheels for opening and closing the valves, for loading and unloading inflammable materials during transportation. This inflammable materials are tampered and the genuine material does not reach to the customer. Therefore there is a need to device a pilferage proof locking system which can prevent the above defects.
The conventional locking system used for transporting inflammable, hazardous material which are very sensitive to spark are kept in partitions in the tanker where there are several outlets which require to be locked. The conventional system of locking can be tampered open & closed and therefore not safe.
By research and development the inventor has invented a strip member for locking plurality of outlets by means of single padlock.
The primary object of this invention is to provide a strip member for locking plurality of outlets by means of single padlock to prevent pilferage and malpractice's during transportation of inflammable material.
The another object of this invention is to provide a strip member for locking plurality of -outlets by means of single padlock so that genuine material and its correct weight reach to the destination.

The material used for making the strips are from metal, composites or from antispark material.
The bottom and top strips are hinged at one end and the other end is provided with the matching hole so that when they overlap a padlock can be used for locking.
There is a provision that the master key is provided to the filling source and different common key to the individual user at the outlets so that filling and unloading is possible by authorized people only.
An ultra locking system of padlock with/without restricted key way with master key with this improved locking strip effectively prevents such malpractice's.
