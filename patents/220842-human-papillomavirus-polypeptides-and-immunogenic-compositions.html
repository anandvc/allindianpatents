Title of Invention

HUMAN PAPILLOMAVIRUS POLYPEPTIDES AND IMMUNOGENIC COMPOSITIONS
Abstract The present invention provides immunogenic and pharmaceutical compositions for the treatment and prevention of human papillomavirus (HPV)-associaled cancers and in particular, cervical cancer. In particular, this invention relates to fusion proteins, and the nucleic acids encoding these fusion proteins used to generate immune responses against HPV Specifically, this invention provides Tor fusions of HPV E6 and E7 in which the E6 and/or E7 contains one or more mutations .These mulations abrogate the transformation activity of these oncogenic proteins and, thus, confer safety to the E6/E7 fusions In addition, these fusions maintain or increase the immunogenic efficacy of E6 and E7 Any gene or protein delivery method can be used to deliver or package the immunogenic compositions of the present invention.
