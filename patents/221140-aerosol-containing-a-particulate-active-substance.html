Title of Invention

AEROSOL CONTAINING A PARTICULATE ACTIVE SUBSTANCE
Abstract The invention relates to active substances in particulate form, and to formulations containing the active substances in suspension in nonsolvent fluid vehicles. The formulations exhibit improved flocculation performance and are of particular use as aerosol formulations for instance for use in metered dose inhalers. (FIG.nil)
