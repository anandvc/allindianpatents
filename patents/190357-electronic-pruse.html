Title of Invention

"ELECTRONIC PRUSE"
Abstract Electronic-Purse comprising main computer (Part-A) having two display screens neon-red (nr) and lower liquid crystal display (Id), two channels (ch-l) and (ch-ll), to enhance it's capacity, two inlets (il-1) and (il-2) provided for receiving figures, one outlet lead (ol) provided for giving figures, housed in B plastic body having 24 push-buttons.
Full Text Electronic-Purse is irelated with currency. Digital currency. E-money. Currency in figures. Figures to give ;ud figures to take, inspite of paper currency, iÂ«the main objective of this invention. Presently, paper currency and coins are in existence. It is very expensive to print notes, and to mould coins. These things also occupy space and weight and need immense security. These are to be counted for every transaction, which consume time. Fake notes and base coins is one more draw-back. Electronic-Purse (E.P.) eliminates ail these draw-backs, thus saving time and money.
This Electronic-Purse is divided into three parts. Part-A, Part-B and Part-C. Electronic-Purse comprising main computer (Part-A) having two display screens neon-red (nr) and lower liquid crystal display (Id), two channels (ch-I) and (ch-II), to enhance it's capacity, two inlets (il-1) and (il-2) provided for receiving figures, one outlet lead (ol) provided for giving figures, housed in a plastic body having 24 push-buttons.
Part-A (shown in the figure )is a computer which produces figures as well as deliver figures. The figure producer computer is ore and only one piece in the hands of the govt. It will take place of security press and mint. It will deliver e-money through Electronic-Purses (Part-B and Part-C) with the help of outlet lead 'ol'. It is housed in a plastic body. It is powered by electricity and a powerful special designed cnip-x works inside. There are 24 push type buttons (like cell-phone buttons) in front of it (as shown in the fig. Part-A). There are two in-lets 'il-l'and 'il-2'for receiving figures with the help of out-let lead 'oF. There are two channels ch-1 & ch-II in it, and a channel selector 'cs'.
Out-let lead 'ol' is the main tool which transfer figures from one E.P. to another E.P. It is equipped with two displays. Upper one is Neon-Red 'nr' display. The lower one is LCD 'Id' display .
Part-B is a small E.P. resembling a calculator. It is very simple to operate. It is designed for children, women, aged persons and illiterate people. Il is also equipped with two displays 'nr' and 'Id'. It is operated by a chip-xx. It has dual power of battery and solar cells. It has 16 push buttons. This model E.P. will be delivered by the govt. between the citizens and it will be able to transfer figures only, it is not able to produce figures.
Part-C is also a pocket-size E.P. There are two channels in it, channel ch-I will be operated with the help of code no. for greater safety. Channel ch-II will have operations like a Simple-Model E.P. operations. This model is named Lock-Model E.P. It is equipped with 20 push buttons (as shown in fig. Part-C). It has two displays, two inlets, powered by battery cells and solar-cells, main tool out-let lead 'ol' and a powerful chip-xxx inside. This model is also issued by the govt. between public, for transferring figures. This E.P. if not able to produce figures. Only Part-A computer can produce figures, which is in the hands of Govt.
