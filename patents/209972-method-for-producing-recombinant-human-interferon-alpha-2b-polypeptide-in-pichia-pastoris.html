Title of Invention

METHOD FOR PRODUCING RECOMBINANT HUMAN INTERFERON ALPHA 2B POLYPEPTIDE IN PICHIA PASTORIS
Abstract 1. A process for preparation and purification of recombinant human IFN alpha 2b which comprises of: (a) cultivating recombinant Pichia pastoris containing a hu-IFN alpha 2b gene according to SEQ ID 3 wherein said hu-IFN alpha 2b gene is cultivated by first isolating and purifying mRNA from human leucocytes, preparing a first stand of DNA from said purified mRNA to obtain said modified hu-IFN alpha 2b gene, amplifying said gene and cloning said amplified modified hu-IFN alpha 2b gene into an expression vector, amplifying and isolating said hu-IFN alpha 2b gene from said modified interferon alpha 2b clone and cloning it into an expression vector and transforming it into said Pichia pastoris; (b) culturing said recombinant Pichia pastoris in complex/defined salt culture medium to produce hu-IFN alpha 2b protein wherein: (i) the pH of complex media is in the range of 3.0 to 6.5 , preferably 6.0 to 6.5 and pH of defined salt media is in the range of 3.5 to 4.5 preferably 3.5; (ii) temperature of the medium in the range of 25 to 35°C preferably 28 to 32°C; (iii) dissolved oxygen: 20-80 % of saturation, preferably 40-50 % of saturation and said culturing is carried out for a duration of 48 to 110 hours for complex media and 90 to 110 hours for defined salt media; ($) purifying recombinant hu-IFN alpha 2b protein from said culture medium.

Another objective of the present invention is to produce interferon alfa 2b by 5 high density fermentation. Yet another objective of the present invention is to obtain IFN alpha 2b protein in a pure form. 10 acceptable formulation of recombinant human IFN alpha 2b protein or its pharmaceutically acceptable salt for their uses in various diseases as mentioned herein. SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
