The following specification particularly describes the nature of invention and the manner in which it is to be performed.



The present invention relates to the ASPHALT & WET MIX PAVER
FINISHER. The asphalt & wet mix paver finisher machine is related to the spreading or filling of paving material in a desired and ready for laying of mix materials at the road laying. This invention is related to road construction apparatus. Accordingly the general objects of the present invention are to provide novel and improved methods and apparatus of such character.
Road construction method and apparatus are well known. The equipment employed in highway construction, which may be generally described as paving machines. The conventional paving machines have a chassis, which has a skip at its front end. The skip to which the material to be applied over the roadbed is supplied, is conventionally provided with pivotal sidewall sections which define a filling funnel therefore. Such a paving machine also consists of the chassis for working the applied materials, a device known in the trade as a "beam". The skip will have a discharge opening at the bottom and a conveyor moves material, which is dumped into the skip and subsequently flows out the length of the beam by a "distributing worm". The beam of prior art paving machines is typically comprised of a primary beam and a pair of oppositely disposed, adjustable beam extensions so that the material being deposited on the roadbed can be spread across the appropriate area. The distance of the opposed beam extensions project from the main beam is usually separated control via hydraulic actuators to selective control the lane width.

During road construction or repair, trenches are often excavated alongside -the outermost travel lanes. During finishing of the road, it may be necessary to close such trenches and to form a shoulder area of breakdown lane. The conventional Paving Finisher Machine is consisted with chain and sprockets of rear wheel but during the paving operation some paving materials were comes in contact with this chain and sometime this paving material was entrapped between the space portions of the chain, which damage the chain. This paving finisher machine has number of clutch i.e. main clutch plates, fiber facing clutch plates, conveyor clutch plates so; the operator was confuses to operate the paving machine. In this paving finisher machine has single front wheel with mechanical steering so it provides less base portion to support to the whole device and it has more then two hand operated laver for gear shifting & engaging to change the speed of the paving machine. Road leveling problem was screed so; it requires more time for road laying so, it becomes very costly process. There are many joints in chassis wall of conventional paving machine so; the breakdown is occurred in this machine alternately which increase maintains cost and alternate breakdown is also time consuming. Also the conventional paving machine has low hopper capacity to store paving material so, it require to continues load the paving material in hopper. These limitations are overcome by the present invention of the ASPHALT & WET MIX PAVER FINISHER is invented with unique design.
