Title of Invention

CONTINUOUS PROCESS FOR PREPARING MONONITROTOLUENES
Abstract CONTINUOUS ISOTHERMAL PROCESS FOR PREPARING MONONITROTOLUENES The present invention relates to a process for the continuous isothermal preparation of mononitrotoluenes with concentration of the resultant waste sulphuric acid and recycling of the concentrated waste sulphuric acid to the process.
