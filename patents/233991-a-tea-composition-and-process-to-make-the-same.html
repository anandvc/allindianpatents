Title of Invention

A TEA COMPOSITION AND PROCESS TO MAKE THE SAME
Abstract A tea composition comprising 85 to 99.9% by weight tea and 0.1 to 15% by weight Withania somnifere is provided.The composition provides significant benefits against common colds and cough without affecting the organo-leptic properties of tea.
