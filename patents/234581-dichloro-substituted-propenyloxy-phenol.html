Background Art

When the present compound is used as an active ingredient of insecticidal/acaricidal agents for epidemic prevention, it is formulated into a dosage form such as emulsifiable concentrates, wettable powders and flowable concentrates, which are applied after diluted with water to a typical concentration of 0.1 to 500 ppm; or it is formulated into a dosage form such as oil sprays, aerosols, fumigants and poisonous baits, which are applied as such without any dilution.
