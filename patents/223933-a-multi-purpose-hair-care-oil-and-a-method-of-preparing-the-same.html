Title of Invention

A MULTI PURPOSE HAIR CARE OIL AND A METHOD OF PREPARING THE SAME
Abstract A hair care oil comprising effective amounts of the extract of the Hibiscus flowers, Ocimum Sanctum (Tulsi), Melia Azadirachta (Neem) .Methi, Alovera, Coconut Oil as Base of oils and Castor oil. Prepared by mixing all extracts together with the oil boiling to 80° C for 10 to 15 minutes , filtering and then cooling to normal temperature.

TECHNICAL FIELD:
The field of this invention is hair care oil containing herbal extracts. The oil has hair nourishment property and the vital capacity of hair growth even on baldheads. This invention also relates to the method of preparing the oil too.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
According to the present invention there is provided a hair oil for nourishment and growing of hair.
The oil has not only hair nourishment quality but it has the vital capacity of hair growth even on baldheads.
The basic ingredients help the body naturally to grow the hair back by normal means and not by using any harmful chemicals that may have side effects on the human body.
This is not only helpful to grow hair back but also helps in improving the quality of hair and there by promoting hair growth at a rapid rate than normal. Also giving the hair a shinier and smoother texture.
The application of the hair oil is fairly simple yet effective.
The oil has to be applied to the scalp of the person using the oil and then massaged into the scalp properly .
Then the person has to take a vibrator based massage so as to ensure that tne massaged oii bas been not only reacb'ing tbe sca'rp but aiso being absorbed by the scalp.


By doing so the nourishment that has been missing shall be provided by the oil and thus help hair grow back.
The same oil would primarily stop hair fall and then nourish the scalp and there by developing the nature for the hair to grow back.
The same oil is also used for treatment against dandruff and also would prevent hair splitting.
DESCRIPTION:-
Procedure to Make the oil.
The following ingredients are required to make the oil.
• Coconut oil,
• Hibiscus Rosa Sinensis
• Melia Azadirachta (Neem)
• Ocimum Sanctum (Tulsi)
• Aloe Vera
• Methi
• Castor
The extracts of oil and the above herbs are mixed together and boiled to a 80 degree temperature so that they mix homogenously and then the oil is filtered completely so that no granules are left in the oil extract. The oil thus made is cooled and then ready for application..
