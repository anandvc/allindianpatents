Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PROTECTING A BARE MULTI-STANDARD CABLE CONDUCTORS IN AGGRESSIVE SURROUNDINGS
Abstract "A PROCESS FOR PROTECTING A BARE MULTI-STANDARD CABLE CONDUCTORS IN AGGRESSIVE SURROUNDINGS" ------------------------------------------------ The invention relates to a process for protecting a bare multi-stranded cable conductor(7)in aggressive surroundings,the stranded cable conductors(7)being connected to atleast one electrical component(6), the process comprising the step of providing a sheating on the stranded cable conductor.After being connected to the atleast one electrical component,the lenth of the baremulti-stranded cable conductor(7) is placed together with the component(6) into an injection mold(9)and at least one region (8) of the standard cable conductor(7)is uncapsulated in fuel-resitant plastic.
