Title of Invention

AN ADDITIVE COMPOSITION FOR INCREASING THE FUEL EFFICIENCY AND REDUCE POLLUTION IN EQUIPMENTS
Abstract An additive composition for increasing the fuel efficiency and reduce pollution in equipments such as engines and furnaces using hydrocarbon fuels the said additive composition comprising 3 to 7 weight percent of anthracene powder, 3 to 7 weight percent napthalene powder, 15 to 25 weight percent of di-acetone, 3 to 7 weight percent of nitro benzene, 3 to 7 weight percent of tri-tolyl phosphate, 15 to 25 weight percent of acetone, 3 to 7 weight percent of benzoyl peroxide and 30 to 48 weight percent of alcohol.
