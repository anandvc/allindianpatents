Title of Invention

A DIVIDABLE TABLET
Abstract The present invention relates to a dividable tablet (Ta) comprising; a dividing line(Ld) formed along a center line on an upper surface(Sa) of the tablet(Ta), and ridgelines(LI,L2) located inside of the peripheral portion (E) of the tablet(Ta) so as to surround said dividing line(Ld) in two respective areas(RI,R2) defined by said dividing line(Ld) on the upper surface(Sa) of the tablet(Ta), said tablet(Ta) paving a lower surface(Sb) gradually rises from the peripheral portion(E) of the tablet (Ta) toward the center portion(C) thereof, and having areas (RLI,2) around the ridgelines(LI,L2) each formed by a convex surface.
