Title of Invention

FLYER FOR A JUTE SPINNING MACHINE
Abstract A flyer (IA) for a jute spinning machine comprising a hollow tubular body (2) provided with an open bottom and a closed top (3). The closed top is provided with an internally threaded collar (4) extending upwardly from the center thereof. The tubular body is further provided with a pair of piecing slots (5,5) at diametrically opposite sides thereof and reinforced portions (6,6) along the length thereof at the said opposite sides. The flyer is further provided with a guide passage block (7) internally at the top thereof comprising a pair diametrically opposite radially inclined guide passage (8,8) directed towards the said reinforced opposite sides of the tubular body. The outer ends of the guide passages open into the collar and the inner ends of the guide passages are provided with downwards bends that terminate shorts of the innerside of the said reinforced portions at the said opposite sides of the tubular body.
