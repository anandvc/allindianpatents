Title of Invention

"A HERBAL FORMULATION FOR THE MANAGEMENT OF OSTEO-ARTHRITIS AND A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION THEREOF"
Abstract This invention relates to a herbal formulation and a process for preparation thereof wherein the herbal formulation is useful for the management of osteo-arthritis. The herbal composition comprises mixture of organic extracts of Commiphore mukul ( guggula), Terminalia arjuna (arjun) Convolvulus pluricaulis (Shankhapushpi), with or without additive. The process for preparation of the formulation comprises mixing of the organic extracts of the above herbs with or without addition of additives.
Full Text FIELP OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a herbal formulation and a process for preparation thereof for the management of Osteo-arthritis to prevent the future complications particularly surgical interventions. The present preparation may also be advantageously used for reduction of pain, restoration of functional capability and reduction of inflammation among the cases showing clinical and radiographic evidences of osteo-arthritis.
BACKGROUND OF THE 1NVENITON
Osteo-arthritis (OA) is not a single disease rather it is the end result of a variety of problems of joint failure. This clinical condition is characterized by degeneration of particular cartilage and proliferation of new bone, cartilage and connective tissue. Males and females both are affected but Oateo arthritis is more common and severe in older women. As regard the aetiology and pathogenesis of OA is concerned, the abnormal surface contacts, weight-bearing alignments, metabolic diseases, genetically determined defect of homogentistic acid oxidase results in the accumulation of a pigmented polymer that binds to collagen rendering it brittle and prone to mechanical degradation. Crystal deposition of calcium pyrophosphate dihydrate may alter the properties of cartilage matrix directly and low-grade crystal inflammation may play a part in pathogenesis.
Pain is at first intermittent and aching is provoked by the use of the joint and relieved by rest. As the disease progresses, movement in the affected joint becomes limited because of pain, muscular spasm, capsular fibrosis, osteophyte formation and remodeling of bone. Muscular wasting is an important factor in the progress of the disease.
Although the pathological changes in OA are irreversible but efforts have been made to alleviate symptoms particularly in the early stages.
Corticosteroids, hydrotherapy, knee arthroplasty, arthrodesis are advised for its management but due to hazardous side effects such therapies have restrictions. Thus there is an urgent need of safe and useful remedial measure for the management of OA.
OBJECT OF THE PRESENT INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation and process for preparation thereof useful for the management of osteo-arthritis by over-coming the symptoms like pain, inflammation, stiffness, muscular spasm, muscular wasting etc.
Another object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation and process for preparation thereof useful for the management of osteo-arthritis which provides relief to pain and restores functional capability to minimize the risk of slipping.
Further object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation and process for preparation thereof useful for the management of osteo-arthritis to check the rapid formation of capsular fibrosis and osteophyte formation.
Still further object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation and process for preparation thereof useful for the management of anxiety and depression associated with cases suffering with cases suffering from osteo-arthritis.
Yet further object of this invention is to propose a herbal formulation and process for preparation thereof to compensate the calcium requirement which is commonly deficient in osteo-cases.

Commiphora mukul (Guggulu):
This plant commonly known as "Guggulu" belonging to family "Cucurbitaceae" whose dried Gum resin are extracted to collect the mixture of a number of steroids in the form of guggulsterone Z & E and guggulusterol I, II & III. Various studies have shown this mixture possessing potent anti-inflammatory, hypolipidemic anti-obesity properties useful in the management of Osteo-arthric symptoms.
Terminalia arjuna (Arjun):
This plant commonly known as "Arjun" belongs to the family "Combretaceae". The dried stem bark of the plant is extracted to collect the mixture of saponine and arjunoglucoside I 85 II. Various studies have shown that these compositions have potential effect on the regulation of abnormal lipid metabolism. It supplement the calcium deficiency as it contains small amount of calcium.
Convolvulus pluricaulis (Shankhapushpi):
This plant is commonly known as 'Shankhpushpi' belonging to family "Convolvulaceae" whose dried whole plant are extracted to collect the mixture of sankhapushpine and steroids. Studies conducted have proven that the leaves and flowers are having anti-anxiety anti hypertensive property and it also improves the mental function.
The known additives are excipients such as calcium sulphate.
