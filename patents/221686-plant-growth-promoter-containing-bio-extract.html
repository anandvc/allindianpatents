Title of Invention

PLANT GROWTH PROMOTER CONTAINING BIO-EXTRACT
Abstract The present invention relates to a liquid biofertilizer comprising unsaponifiable material and inorganic microonutrients. The liquid biofertilizer also contains other ingredients such as surfacants, emulsifying agents and diluents, The liquid biofertilizer promotes or enhances yield of the crop.

Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to a plant growth promoter comprising unsaponifiable material, inorganic salts and micronutrients. The plant growth promoter also contains other ingredients such as surfactants, emulsifying agents and diluents. The plant growth promoter promotes or enhances yield of the crop.

Detailed Description of the Invention
The unsaponiflabe material may be of plant origin and is prepared by procedures known in the art by extraction techniques. Sources for the unsaponiflabe material are rice bran wax. carnuba wax, alpha alpha plant.
The inorganic salts are selected from nitrogen, potassium-containing salts such as potassium nitrate, potassium chloride.
The micronutrients are selected from sodium, calcium, manganese, zinc, copper, cobalt, molybdenum and the like or mixtures thereof.
The micronutrients may be in salt form which are selected from sulphates, chlorides,
molybdates and the like or mixtures thereof.

The plant hormone used in the present invention is selected from phytohormones, auxins, gibberillins, cytokinins and the like or mixtures thereof.
The emulsifying agent used in the present invention is selected from sodium lauryl sulphate. tween 20, sodium octadecyl suphate and the like or mixtures thereof.
The diluents used in the present invention are selected from water, or other organic solvents such as chloroform, acetone.
The unsaponifiable material contained in the present invention may in the range of 0.1 to. 10 % of the total weight.
The unsaponifiable material contained in the present invention is specifically in the range
of 0.2 to 0.6 % of the total weight.
The inorganic salts and micronutrients are in the range of 0.1 to 20 % of the total weight.
The plant growth promoter is prepared by a process which comprises mixing the desired proportions of unsaponifiable material, plant hormone, emulsifying agent and diluents.
