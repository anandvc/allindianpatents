Title of Invention

AN ESTROGEN-LIKE ORGAN-SELECTIVE MEDICAMENT COMPOSITION COMPRISING EXTRACTS FROM IRIS PLANTS, CIMICIFUGA RACEMOSA AND TECTORIGENIN
Abstract N/A
Full Text The present invention relates to the use of extracts from Iridaceae and from Cimicifuga racemosa, and of tectorigenin as an estrogen-type, organoselectivemedicament for the selective treatment and/or prophylaxis of cardiovascular diseases, in particular atherosclerose, osteoporose and climacteric disorders, e.g. for preventing or alleviating hot flushes. Uterotrophic effects are practically not observed.651
