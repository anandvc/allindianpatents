A CASING CORD FOR ELECTRICAL WIRING
The invention relates to a casing for electrical wiring . More particularly the invention relates to electrical casing which is easy to install; with longer life due to twin locks , more environmentally friendly and above all with a revolutionary locking system for first time based on circles.
Electrical wirings are laid by fixing the electrical wirings on the walls by fixing
the wooden blocks with clips for anchoring the electrical wirings . This are not
only the expensive but requires high skill and are time consuming. The
conventional systems also requires environmental protection from
moisture. There are chances of electrical shocks which is dangerous and since the wire are laid bear. So there are always chance of people getting electrical shocks.
Therefore to avoid the risk of moisture and electrical shock the inventor has invented a Plastic Box - Type Panel having a lower casing and a slidable upper casing to conceal the electrical wiring in between. The material used for manufacturing the alone casing is from non-conductor of Electricity such as plastic PVC which is easily mouldable to any form. Accordingly there is provided an improved casing for electrical wiring comprises a box type construction consisting of a lower casing having base with outwardly projected plunges with inner curvature at the lower end and an upper casing having base with outwardly projected plunges; wherein plunges of upper casing slidably fitted over the plunges of lower casing to conceal the wiring disposed within the space in middle of the casing. The upper casing is made from mouldable plastic single piece with outwardly projected plunges so as to slidably fitted in the plunges of the lower casing for giving twin locks.


The invention will now be described in Figure 1 to 4 an electrical casing having a lower rectangular body (3) with outwardly projected plunges (4, 5) having inner carveture at the Lower end (8, 9) which constitute the Lower casing {1).
An upper casing having a base (2) outwardly projected plunge] s (6, 7) slidable fitted over the plungers of lower casing (1) to consealed the wiring. Therefore define a space in between the lower and upper casing through which the electrical wiring passes.
The casing which fitted together are shown in Fig (3) where the upper cases plunges overlaps on the plunges of lower casing. The lower casing is fitted on the walls and the electricals wiring is carried and finally covered by slideable upper casing to make the wiring consealed and there is no risk of getting electrical shock or chances of moisture damaging the wire.

2. An improved casing for electrical wiring as claimed in claim 1 wherein the upper casing made from mouldable plastic single piece with outwardly projected plunges so as to slidably fitted in the plunges of the lower casing for giving twin locks.
