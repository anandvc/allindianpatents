Title of Invention

FACTOR VII OR FACTOR VIIA POLYPEPTIDE VARIANT
Abstract The present invention relates to Factor VII or Factor VIIa polypeptide variant comprising at least one amino acid mdification in position 196,237 or 341 relative to hFVII or hFVIIa. The variants exhibit an increased activity, i.e. reduced clotting time, compared to rhFVIIa.
