Title of Invention

"A HOMEOPATHIC COMPOSITION TO BE USED AS A SUBSTITUTE FOR CHEMICAL PESTICIDE ESPECIALLY USEFUL IN A POTATO CULTIVATION"
Abstract Composition comprising suphuric acid and osmium Sactim in liquid forms diluted with rain water as a substitute for Chemical pesticide for treating various diseases of vegetable plants such as tomato, chilly, bitter gourd, lady finger etc to prevent death of the plant .
Full Text Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to homeopathic compositions for providing a substitute for Chemical Fertilizers and pesticides and a process for the preparation of the same.
Further the present invention relates to a composition comprising suphuric acid and osmium Sactim in liquid forms as a substitute for Chemical pesticide for treating various diseases of vegetable plants such as tomato, chilly, bitter gourd, lady finger etc to prevent death of the plant and a process for the preparation of the same.
Background of the Invention
A large population of India is directly dependent on agriculture for their livelihood. Every human being is inevitably dependent on agriculture and animals for their food sources. For healthy human civilization the food sources and land particularly agricultural land, must be free from toxicity and must have desired level of nutrients. A large proportion of the land which is used for growing food and crops is toxicated, nutrient deficient and are having poor physical condition. The indiscriminate use of chemical fertilizers is converting fertile land into barren land.


Further on account of increase in population there is a need to convert barren land into fertile land. There is a growing need to increase the cultivable area to feed the increasing population.
Due to industrial development and improper chemical waste management the toxic level in plant and in their products has been increased considerably, which is taking a toll on the health of animal kingdom including human being.
Today the considerable area of land which can be cultivated and utilized for agriculture has been left wasted and barren due to toxicity or sodic / saline conditions of land.
Homeopathic medicines have been found to be very useful and free from side effects when administered to human beings. However homeopathic medicines have not been used for increasing productivity of land.


Object of the Invention
The primary objective of the present invention is to provide a homeopathic composition for increasing fertility of agricultural land and a process for the preparation of the same.
It is further objective of the present invention to provide a safe and non toxic composition free from side effects for increasing fertility of agricultural land.
It is yet another objective of the present invention is to develop a composition that can be given easily and whose prolonged usages do not have any adverse effect.
It is still another objective of the present invention to provide a composition which can be inexpensively produced and easily employed / administered.
Yet another objective is to develop a homeopathic composition that can be stored for a longer period.

The preparation
The unique composition is prepared by mixing liquid extracts of above named
ingredients in the effective ranges shown below.


For a vial of 500 ml
1. Sulphuric acid - 125 to 375 ml (25-75% by Volume)
2. Osmium Sanctum -125 to 375 ml (25-75% by Volume)
3. Balance rain water
The best results are achieved when the key ingredients mentioned above are used as below.
For a vial of 500 ml
1. Sulphuric acid - 200 ml (40% by Volume)
2. Osmium Sanctum - 200 ml (40% by Volume)
3. Balance rain water
Unique composition
