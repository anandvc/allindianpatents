Title of Invention

SELF-STEERING THREE-AXLE BOGIE
Abstract A self-steering, three-axle bogie, in particular for a rail vehicle, comprising wheel sets and wheel set bearing housings allocated to the sets. The outer wheel sets are counter- coupled and can be displaced in a longitudinal direction and the central wheel set can be displaced in a transverse direction. The wheel set bearing housings on each side of the running gear are coupled to the wheel set bearing housings lying on the same side.
