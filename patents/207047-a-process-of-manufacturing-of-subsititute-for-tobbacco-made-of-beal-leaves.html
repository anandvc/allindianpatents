Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF MANUFACTURING OF SUBSITITUTE FOR TOBBACCO MADE OF BEAL LEAVES
Abstract A process of manufacturing of substitute for tobacco made of BAEL leaves comprising steps of:-a) firstly the pluck the green leaves or small branches of BAEL tree; b)the plucked leaves are kept in sunlight for drying up to four to five days; c)the said dried leaves are then crushed by hand; d) .the said crushed BAEL leaves contain thin sticks of BAEL LEAVES which are separated from the said crushed BAEL leaves; e)the said crushed BAEL leaves are filtered by sieve means to separate thin sticks of BAEL; f) the said thin stick of BAEL leaves are cut in equal size; g)the said cut sticks of BAEL are sprinkled with mixture of water and the dust powder which is left while crushing the BAEL leaves; h)the said thin sticks of BAEL are mixed thoroughly with the crushed and filtered BAEL leaves; i) the said mixture is kept in the jute bag for at least one month; and j) the said blended mixture of step(i) after the end of the said period is a substitute for tobacco having the taste, smell and kick of tobacco.

FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a process of manufacturing substitute of tobacco made of BAEL leaves. It is a process of manufacturing of substitute made of herbal leaves.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Tobacco is the main ingredient of bidi, cigarettes and gutaka, keni, chuning tobacco, cigar, chirut ,and chilam but it is very very harmful for the health of a person this is injurious to health due to nicotine in tobacco .tobacco causes so many diseases including cancer according to a survey about twenty lacks persons are using tobacco in different form . This invention is a substitute of tobacco. Which has no side effect and not harmful for persons who use it
OBJECTIVE OF THE INVENTION
The main objective of the invention is to give the substitute of tobacco which has no any harmful effect since it has no nicotine
The further objective of the invention is to reduce the cost of manufacturing since ingredient is herbal compound and easily available in nature.
The further advantage of the invention is to use it as medicine of hyper tension, acidity, paieies, snake poison and local medical use

DETAIL DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
In this process firstly we have to pluck the green leaves or small branches of BAEL tree, these leaves are kept in sunlight for drying up to four to five days, these dried leaves are then crushed by hand ,the crushed BAEL leaves contain s thin sticks of BAEL ALEAVES .these thin sticks are separated from BAEL leaves
Now the crushed BAEL leaves are filtered. The thin sticks of BAEL leaves are cut in equal size .the cut sticks of BAEL are sprinkled with mixture of water and the dust powder which is left while crushing the BAEL leaves, after this the thin sticks of BAEL are mixed thoroughly with the crushed and filtered BAEL leaves.

Now this mixture is kept in s jute bag for at least one month this blended mixture is can be used in cigarettes, bidis ghutkhas and chewing etc. as a substitute of tobacco, the bael leaves have the same characteristics as that of tobacco i.e. Taste smell and kick but has no ill effects on human body
The bael leaves are 100% safe substitute of tobacco products and not injurious to health
