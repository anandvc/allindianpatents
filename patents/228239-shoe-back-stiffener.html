Title of Invention

SHOE BACK STIFFENER
Abstract A shoe back stiffener, (1) for fixing into the hinder part of the shoe, comprising a curved strip, (2) having its convex surface (3) matching with the concave surface of the hinder part of the shoe, one end of the curve strip provided with "U" turn/bend (4) forming a channel guide/grip adopted to accommodate the upper edge of hinder part (6) of the shoe, a double sided adhesive tape (7), fixed adhesive over the convex surface of the said curved strip, used for easy fixing of the said shoe back stiffener into the hinder part of the shoe.
