Title of Invention

IMPROVED PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF PHENYL FLUORENOL
Abstract The present invention relates to an improved method for the preparation of Lumefantrine by converting 2,7-dichlorofluorene to a ketone; converting ketone to epoxide; and in-situ converting epoxide to Lumefantrine inclusive condensation of aminoalcohol with 4-chlorobenzaldehyde at reflux temperature of solvent.
