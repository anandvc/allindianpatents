Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED DEVICE FOR FIRE PROTECTION
Abstract Are disproved fire protective hydrant safety device in wet and dry method with protection Surrounding protection and on the way protection and is autos icily centrally linked by specially designed contraries and in short, total protection is possible Its another inherit is that where the fire/fires there the main centre or centers, so more concentration can be given on to the affected area. If is highly effective to prevent radiation and spreading of fire and the "on the way protect ion' act as a water roof path for to escape from the fire and also for to enter into the fire field for rescue operations. The switches of the working of generators, fire alarms, messages and other facilities arranged will also becomes automatically operated then.
Full Text This invention relates to an improved automatic fire protective hydrant safety device, which works with the aid of electric facilities and also even in complete failure of electricity or electric facilities it works, even though there will be a reduction in pressure occurs, in case of electric failure, and it extinguish fire at the base and prevent the spreading of fire at the base and surroundings itself. The significance of this mode of protection is that it includes simultaneous multi protections For that, protectors, surrounding protectors, additional support cum surrounding protectors and additional surrounding linking protectors are arranged in the protective portions and upper, middle and lower containers are arranged in the container portion and multi foldings are for regular water flowings and double locking folding when open, opening from main pipe and opening from the reserved water tank will formed. Here the additional surrounding linking protectors, which is connected to the neighbouring unit, will act only if the fire spread to other areas. The fire detector is connected to the air tube. By the pressure of compressed air inside the air tube the whole system stand upward in normal condition. When fire occurs, when the detector detects fire, then it works and air inside the air tube will released out and then it works automatically. Instead of air, tube can be filled by water also, then it becomes water tube and naturally water comes out first and other workings are same. By pulling the string it can be operated manually. When on pulling the string the detector, works and then other workings will be automatic. Inside the container which is made by fire resistant metal, the fixer cum water passagers (legs like arrangements) are arranged, for to close the hole on normal condition, and for to open the holes for flowing water from the upper case to the lower when on work and switch portions are used to transmit messages.
As it is a very peculiar field, field of fire, especially when it is relating to safety of life and properties, the efficiency of the protective system should be maximum. This invention can ensure perfect protection from the fire, by extinguish fire and by prevent spreading of fire at the base surroundings itself when compared to the known arts. It can protect as usual and further the object and advantages over the known art is that even in complete electric failure it can work up to an extent and more than that simultaneous automatic multi protections which includes protection with surrounding protection and additional support cum surrounding protection and additional surrounding linking protection and automatic working facilities for to work, including operation of switches to the control panel for working fire alarm, generators or additional facilities arranged, will work.

In the kown art, the hydrant systems, sprinklers, or different types of extinguishers are used for to put off fire. The disadvantages and defects of the present hydrant systems are failure in work in case of electric failure.and even it works as normally, it is not sufQcient to ensure safety, with the present available facilities.
Another method which now used for to put off fire is the extinguishers. It also have lot of defects. It is very common that when fire takes place, people will become very panic and they are not ready even to think of extinguishers or for to read its instructions to use, at such an occasion. Because panic people will give first preference for to escape or for to ran away firom the fire.
Another disadvantage is that the extinguishers, may not be available at near and even if it is available, everybody may not be aware about the proper use of it. Unless properly used it will not work and even it work,too dangerous (example: cold bum). A common method of operation is not possible for different types of extmguishers, when on work some of the extinguishers are to use in normal position but some others are to use in inverted position. Another defect of extinguishers is that it will be effective only when the fire is at the initial stage. Normally within seconds the fire will spread out and for the extinguishers it will be out of control then.
A major defect of extinguishers and other known arts is that for old peoples, children, and for handicapped or disabled persons or while in sleep all present arts becomes helpless or becomes an entirely useless things.
The object of this invention is to eliminate the above said major defects and to ensure total protection in a most effective manner.
Advantage of this invention is that fire and its very surroundings is the concentrating centre. Its object is to extinguish the fire and avoid spreadmg totally at the base itself, even in case of electric failure. Another advantage is that only affected areas and its surroundings are treated so that utilization of water in a best manner is possible.
Another advantage is that both permanent and temporary installations are possible. So that it can be used for seasonal occasions such as festivals etc.
