Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR MAKING MINERAL WOOL REINFORCED POLYMER COMPOSITE BOARD"
Abstract A process for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite board . The present invention provides a mineral wool polymer composition and a method of making composite product which consist of core making and laminating a sheet over the core. Mineral wool is reinforced in polymer matrix which acts as binder for developing the core, comprises 10-90 wt% of mineral wool (fibre) in free form or pretreated bonded form. Developed core is laminated by an adhesive to a ply sheet / metal sheet/ laminated sheet/ fibre reinforced polymer sheet to form board, panel / door shutter/ window etc. The polymer used is of thermosetting type.
Full Text This invention relates to a process for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite board using a composition useful for domestic and industrial applications in construction Sector and Furniture/Interior Decoration.
Mineral wool (fibres) are produced by melting rock or steel slag or any other similar material and by centrifuging the molten glassy rocks. These fibres are generally known as Mineral wool. These fibres have thin diameter and available commercially in different forms. These fibres can be used in development of boards / panels/ doors shutters / windows.
Development of new light weight products using mineral fibres with polymers has a potential of substitution of wood in domestic as well as Industrial applications to preserve the wood and protect Forests and Environment. These fibres can replace the carcinogenic asbestos fibres from several applications such as insulations, roofing, partitions, etc. controlling pollution problem created by asbestos fibres. Mineral fibres composites can be used in making boards, panels, table tops, furniture etc. The main problems associated with the development of these composites lies in the physical properties of mineral fibres which are fragile in nature and there fore a little higher pressure than a minimum required will crush the fibres.
Several fibres such as asbestos, glass, aramid, etc. have been used by plastic industry as reinforcement. Addition of fibres in polymers have both technological as well as economical advantages. Among technological advantages, increased modulus, high heat deflection temperature, mechinability, dimensional stability are important. From economical point of
view , these fibres reduce the cost of final product because difference in price of synthetic plastics is very high as compared to mineral fibres.
The main object of the present invention is to provide a composition for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a process for making mineral wool reinforced polymer board.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a composite product of mineral wool reinforced polymer composite for making boards, panels, door shutters, windows for domestic and industrial applications.
The composite product consist core of mineral wool, thermosetting binder, with or without coupling agents and facing of fibre reinforced polymer sheet/ laminated sheet/ ply sheet/ metal sheet for making panels, boards shutters and windows. The facing is fixed to the core with suitable adhesive.
Accordingly the present invention provides a composition for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite which comprises:
Mineral wool 10 to 90 wt%
Thermosetting polymer 10 to 80 wt%
Coupling agent 0 to 20 wt%
as defined herein.
In an embodiment of the present invention the thermosetting polymer used may be such as phenol formaldehyde, urea formaldehyde, melamine formaldehyde, polyurethane, Epoxy resin, polyester resin, vinyl esters.
In another embodiment of the present invention the coupling agent used may be such as silane, titanate, zirconate.
The composition of the present invention for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite is not a mere admixture but a synertistic mixture having distinct properties which are not a mere aggregation of the properties of the individual ingredients.
Accordingly the present invention provides a process for making mineral wool reinforced polymer composite board using a composition comprising mineral wool 10 to 90 wt%, thermosetting polymer 10 to 80 wt%, coupling agent as defined herein 0 to 20 wt%, which comprises impregnating a mineral wool slab 10-90 wt% with coupling agent solution by conventional manner, drying the resultant impregnated slab, impregnating by conventional methods the slab so obtained with thermosetting polymer solution, followed by drying, pressing the dried slab in the pressure range of 1 Kpa to 1 Mpa and a temperature in the range of 60° to 160°C, to obtained a reinforced polymer composite board.
The present invention provides a method of making composite product which consist of core making and laminating a sheet over the core. Mineral wool is reinforced in polymer matrix which acts as binder for developing the core, comprises 10-90 wt% of mineral wool (fibre) in free form or pretreated bonded form. Developed core is laminated by an adhesive to a ply sheet / metal sheet/ laminated sheet/ fibre reinforced polymer sheet to form board, panel / door shutter/ window etc. The polymer used is of thermosetting type.
