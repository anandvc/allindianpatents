Title of Invention

ELECTRONIC MILK BOILING CONTROLLER
Abstract An Electronic Milk Boiling Controller used for putting off Gas stove when boiling of milk comprising a liquid sensor mounted to a stainless steel mess utensil closure, the said sensor is connected to the input of an electronic signal conditioning circuit; the out put of the said circuit is connected to an electromechanical actuator of a valve; and the said valve having an inlet adapted to connect to gas regulator and an outlet connected to inlet of a gas stove. TECHANICAL FIELD

This invention relates to an electronic milk boiling controller to used for putting off Gas stove when milk boiling.
In present scenario life has become very fast and every body wants to atomize his daily working to avoid tensions and get himself busy in some productive work .
For example if we take up a process of boiling of milk in kitchens it is a daily routine and a simple work , but due to busy life and routine problems the persons boiling the milk normally forgets to switch off Gas when milk has boiled which results in overflow of milk , choking of gas burners . And of course gas flame quenches due to over flow of milk but supply of gas is still open and hence chances of accident are their and also loss of precious fuel.
This in turn makes a simple work of boiling of milk into a tedious job . The process of hardly 15 min turns up into a good job of half hour or 45 min . And in our very tight schedule we can not afford to loose time , Hence the product mentioned here under is a step forward in atomization of a daily routing work like boiling of milk and hence saving time and routing tension so that our precious time is saved in monitoring boiling of milk till the whole process completes and hence saving of valuable time ,

SUMMARY ON INVENTION
Claimed invention is a electronic gadget used in the process of boiling of milk which automatically turns off the gas when the milk starts boiling . The user can leave the process unmonitored and can do some other important work . The device avoids wastage of fuel and milk and also avoids the chances of accident due to leakage of gas . where as if the process is performed manually there are 40 % chances of milk overflow and gas leakages .

BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE ACCOMPANYING
DRAWING
As Seen from the Block diagram enclosed it self the milk saver consists of Stainless steel wire mesh (1), Sensors (2) Attached to it in order to provide input signal to signal conditioning Circuit (3) then followed by an Electromechanical Actuator (4) which operates a Gas Valve (5) which is fixed in between Gas cylinder and gas stove .
DETAIL DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
Milk to be Boiled is kept in the vessel on gas stove . A stainless steel wire mesh (1 ) fitter with the Sensors (2) is kept at the top of the vessel. The sensors are attached with Electronic signal Conditioning circuit ( 3 ) which senses signal from the sensors and
In turns operates Electromechanical Actuator ( 4 ) and hence the valve ( 5 ) turns off.
When the milk boils and is sensed by the probes of the sensor
A signal is passed through them . The signal conditioning circuit
Amplifies this signal and activates actuator which in turn cuts off
The supply of gas with the help of gas valve attached in between Gas stove and cylinder .
When this process is complete the gas knob has to be switched off manually and the gas valve has to be reset and now
6

The gas can be used again.
And hence eliminating continuous monitoring of the system The system is fully automatic and reliable . This causes saving of precious time and also mental tension .
WORKING OF THE INVENTION
As clearly seen from the block Diagram a Mechanical gas valve is fitted in series with the gas cylinder and gas stove . The gas valve is normally open and the triggering of the valve is electronically controlled with the help of Actuator which converts electrical signal received from the signal conditioning circuit to displacement of the valve hence turning the valve off.
A set of probes is inserted from the SS Wire mesh cover provided on the vessel on which the milk is to be boiled . the probes sense the level of milk through out the process of boiling and as soon as the milk starts boiling and reaches top a signal is sensed by the signal conditioning circuit and which in turn activates the actuator turning off Valve in the end and hence gas turns off.
After completion of the process the valve is reset again and now the gas stove can be used for other purposes .
