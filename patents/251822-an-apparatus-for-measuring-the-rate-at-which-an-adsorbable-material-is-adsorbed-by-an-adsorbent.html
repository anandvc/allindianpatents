Title of Invention

AN APPARATUS FOR MEASURING THE RATE AT WHICH AN ADSORBABLE MATERIAL IS ADSORBED BY AN ADSORBENT
Abstract An apparatus for measuring the rate at which an adsorbable material is adsorbed by an adsorbent, if apparatus comprising: an adsorbent bed; a source of oxygen and means for providing said oxygen to said adsorbent bed; a source of air or synthetic air mixture containing only N2 and O2 and means for providing said oxygen to said adsorbent bed; an oxygen analyzer downstream of said adsorbent bed for measuring the concentration of oxygen; and a flow meter downstream of said adsorbent bed for measuring the flow rate of oxygen.
