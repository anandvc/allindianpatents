Title of Invention

PURIFICATION DEVICE
Abstract The present invention relates to a water purification system adapted to faciliate simple and cost effective purification of impure water to make it suitable for human consumption. The water purification system of the invention comprises a water treatment chamber adapted for treatment of impure water with a treatment/purification composition and means operatively connected to said water treatment chamber for discharge of the treated water free of flocs and said water treatment chamber having means for discharge of flocs generated therefrom as and when required.
