Title of Invention

ANTI SABOTAGE DEVICE
Abstract An anti sabotage device used in the fish plated joints of railway track comprising at least one nut and fish plate bolt to join the rails, a key for operating the:-device and - characterized in that the bolt will go up to 45mm inside the nut after tightening with key and the said nut is provided with taper shape so that it is impossible to hold with any of the existing tools for tightening and loosening.
Full Text

The Present Invention "ANTI SABOTAGE DEVICE" relates to specially designed system which is used in Fish Plated Joints of Railway Track, which cannot be removed with any of the existing tools/devices available in the general market.
Disadvantages of Existing Systems:
Any miscreant with any spanner can remove the existing Fish Plates Bolts and Nut very easily. Due to the removal of the Bolt and Nut, many accidents are taking place frequendy in order to overcome the existing sabotages, the present invention, which is unique in its own, is made by the inventor.
ADVANTAGES OF PRESENT INVENTION:
a) The New Device can be operated with duly designed key exclusively designed for this purpose.
b) Only authorized track maintenance staff can operate the device.
c) The existing structure is not altered, but the device is fastened to fish plate bolts at the Nut End as a replacement of present Nut.

d) The Internal diameter of the device is the same of that of the existing Nut.
e) The same spanner can be used on this device.
f) Railways need not go for additional tools for operating the
device.
g) The Device is totally prevents the removal of the Fish Plates
by Un-Authorised person.
h) The Design of Each batch of the Device can be modified, revised to suit Railways requirement.

CLAIM SHEET
I claim
1. An Anti Sabotage Device used in the fish Plated
Joints of Railway Track comprising at least one nut
(fig-1) and fish plate bolt to join the rails, a Key
(fig-2) for operating the Device Characterised in
that the bolt will go up to 45mm inside the nut
after tightening with key and the said nut is
provided with taper shape so that it is impossible
to hold with any of the existing tools for tightening
and loosening.
2. An Anti Sabotage Device as claimed in claim I
where in every fishplate has four holes with bolts
and nuts.
