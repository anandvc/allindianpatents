Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF MANUFACTURE OF ABRASIVE GRAINS AT AMBIENT TEMPERATURE FOR PROVIDING A UNIFORM, HARD, SURFACE THEREON
Abstract ABSTRACT A PROCESS OF MANUFACTURE OF ABRASIVE GRAINS AT AMBIENT TEMPERATURE FOR PROVIDING A UNIFORM HARD SURFACE THEREON A process of manufacture of abrasive grains at ambient temperature for providing a uniform hard surface thereon, comprising the steps of wetting the said grains with phosphoric acid (upto 5% by weight of the of the grains); preparing a blend of any oxide of copper and aluminous compound in the ratio 30:70 to 70;30; adding tide blend to the wetted grains in the ratio of 2:1 to 6:1 to the weight of the acid and mixing the same, before allowing to cool at ambient temperature.
