Title of Invention

MULTI PURPOSE HEAVY DUTY WET GRINDER
Abstract The Multipurpose Heavy Duty Wet Grinder is designed to overcome certain difficulties we are encountering with Conventional Type Grinder. This grinder is designed to remove the batter by tilting the grinder from the Vertical position to 90 Degree Horizontal and 120 degrees horizontal to remove the batter easily. In this the Motor and Gear Box are vertically mounted one over other to save the space and the rotate able drum is mounted over that along with two cylindrical rollers. This drum is mounted over the Gear box center shaft in a detachable way for easy grinding. A separate locking system is provided for locking the drum in vertical position as well as at 90 degrees to 120-degree tilt position. The whole arrangement is mounted in a tilting mechanism to enable it to tilt the grinder in the stand. During the operation, to avoid the dust and other particles fallen into the drum, a separate lid is fixed over the drum assembly. This can be swing upward and downward. The whole assembly is mounted on a stand which is covered both the sides. In this invention the better grinding mechanism provided is helping to reduce the grinding time to half of conventional models and subsequently the power requirement.

This invention; In general relates to a novel wet grinder. In particular this invention relates to a novel multi purpose heavy duty wet grinder.
There are varieties of wet grinders known in the art. These prior art wet grinders are of low duty type and mainly used for household purposes. This invention is meant for commercial purposes such as hotels, restaurants, College mess, factory canteens, marriage halls etc:
A heavy duty wet grinder as is commonly known in the art consist of cavity in the base stone and a pestle. This pestle is placed in the base stone pit and is fastened to the post by means of chain/rope, through which lateral force is given to the pestle. When the drive is given to the base stone, it rotates and the pestle picks up the speed due to the friction created in between them by the surface contact. When the rice or similar substances are put into the grinder, it is passed through the base stone cavity and the roller. Here it is crushed in between due to the lateral load in the roller stone. This load is given by tightening the chain/rope linked to the post in the grinder. Continuous rotation of roller stone will cause the batter to form.

In this type of commercial grinder, which are known in the art, have inherent problem and the following factors affect the performance of the grinder. The first factor i.e. inefficient method of pestle and cavity arrangement i.e., in the substances meant for grinding are escaped through the outer area due to large area available in the flat portion and small gap provided in the cavity and pestle, where it matches. It consumes lot of the time to crush the substance to the required consistency. This is the second factor viz. "Loss of surface contact between the surfaces of pestle and base stone cavity". When the substance enters in between the base stone cavity and the pestle, small gap is created in between as a layer, the stones are separated temporarily resulting in loss of surface contact and friction, thereby pestle speed reduces and grinding time increases. Consequently the power consumption also increases by this arrangement. Third factor is that there is no differential speed is created in between base stone and pestle, which creates the rubbing motion. This is essential for grinding. Due to absence of this rubbing motion in the present commercial grinders, the grinding process becomes inefficient and time consuming.
In normal commercial grinders, the substances to be ground are discharged manually using hands. We have to lift huge pestle outside so as to clean and wash. Careful handling is required to place the pestle into the grinder and this entire process is labour oriented.
In a nutshell, the prior art commercial grinders are having the disadvantages to the end users.

1. Inefficient grinding method, where the substances to be ground are escaped outside the drum cavity and because of absence of rubbing motion it consumes lot of time to grind to the required consistency.
2. Batter discharge is manual.
3. Cleaning is cumbersome due to heavy weight of pestle.
4. Handling the grinder is difficult. To clean the pestle, it has to be lifted outside the grinder for thorough cleaning.
5. Prior art grinders occupies more space.
6. They consume additional power because of poor efficiency.
7. When capacity of the grinder increases, still more problems has to be handled because of size.
The invention is addressed to the problems associated with prior art grinders. This invented novel machine is completely redesigned with respect to known machines such that the technical problems associated with the prior art machine are overcome. Further the invented machine has several additional features such that the machine has been made versatile.
Accordingly this novel multi purpose heavy duty wet grinder has a efficient grinding mechanism, a detachable drum system and a tilting arrangement of drum assembly for discharging the batter. Apart from the above, a unique tilt lock system is incorporated for easy tilting of the grinder and to lock the drum assembly in vertical position. A lid is provided to cover the drum to avoid dust to fall into the batter.
Incorporation of the above systems in the commercial grinder will be the salient features of the grinder with respect to the presently available commercial grinders. Accordingly it is the primary object of the invention to invent a novel multi purpose heavy duty wet grinder.

It is another object of the invention to invent a novel multi purpose heavy duty wet grinder which is versatile by having additional features so as to execute different process apart from grinding.
It is another object of the invention to invent a novel multi purpose heavy duty wet grinder which has a novel tilting arrangement by which the material can be taken out of the grinder in a most efficient manner.
It is another object of the invention to invent a novel multipurpose heavy duty wet grinder which has a tilt lock system by which the tilting angle can be varied at will.

This cylindrical roller and flat stone system is small in size compared with big size of base stone and pestle system. Due to cylindrical configuration of rollers, which are placed over the flat stone diametrically opposite to each other at a distance away from the central axis of the drum with their grinding surface in contact with the base stone. Two cylindrical rollers are form two circles corresponding to the centre axis of the base stone i.e. at the inner edge of the rollers and outer edge of the rollers. These circles circumference varies according to the width of the cylindrical roller and creates a differential speed when the base stone starts rotating.
The inner circle will try to rotate at a lesser speed than the outer circle because of variation in their circumferential length. Finally the roller will compensate with this two speeds and rotate at one particular speed creating a difference in between the surfaces thereby rubbing motion established. This rubbing motion is very essential for grinding. When the substances are put in the drum into this system is caught in between two cylindrical rollers instead of one pestle in the normal commercial grinder. Due to small area available in the stones, the substances are continuously caught in between in quick sucession and get ground with minimum time. In this system one more factor is also helping to increase the efficiency ie the vertical (perpendicular) loading of the roller stone rather than lateral load in the pestle of commercial grinder. When the ground substance entering into the roller and flat stone, roller will be lifted temporarily and the vertical load keeps the

roller to maintain the contact with the tlat stone. Lateral load in tne pestle or commercial grinder could not maintain this because of partial distribution of the load.

Tilting arrangement of drum assembly provided in the grinder will facilitate to discharge the ground paste to the vessel without using hand. The whole drum assembly mounted in a bearing housing can be tilt forward/backward to any angle to discharge batter into the vessel kept on the floor. This drum assembly locks in position viz at 90 to 120 degree angle for effective cleaning of substance in the vessel without any residue left in the drum.
Tilt lock system enables the drum assembly to lock at the vertical position (while grinding the material) without falling of drum assembly on either side. This also helps to lock automatically for partial discharge of batter and also tilting the machine at an angle of 120 degree full and complete discharge of batter is permissible.
