A PROCTECTIVE CAPS FOR FLANGES
The invention relates to a protective caps for flanges. More particularly the invention relates to a protective caps for the flanges to protect vital areas like bevel and raised faces from any type of damages.
The conventional system a protective measures used to wrap abro tapes on raised faces and bevel ends of the flange and than it is packed in polythene bag. We found that disadvantages of the system

Flanges are mainly used in pipelines and in a flange it is necessary to protect vital areas like bevel and raised face from any type of damages therefore an appropriate protective and packing systems are needed.
The principal object of this invention is therefore to provide a
protective caps to ellaninate the damages of bevel and raised

faces of flange.
The another object of this invention is to provide a protective caps which can be made economically and fitted on the flange manually or by machine.

Referring to fig. 1 and 2 the stainless steel flange with outer circular portion and inner extended circular portion having grooves on the flange (4) spaced at right angle which has been given a protective cap for the protection of vital areas like bevel and raised face from any type of damages comprising' a single piece plastic protective cap (1) having a inner circular portion (2) and four extended portions spaced at right angle with inner extended projections (3) to fit into the matching groups provided in the flange in a snap fit manner and a circular cap which covers the circular portion of the inner extended flange which can be pressed fit inside or it can be sealed by welding

with inner and outer caps with four plug member the seal is broke open by tearing the sealed portion before use which makes the flange protective as well as pilfer proof.
The protective caps are used to avoid any damage to bevel and raised faces of the flange. The flange with central pipe section is prevented from damage by packaging the flange from both the side by plastic caps which is fitted in the snap fit or sealed manner. The pipe portion of the flange is surrounded by cap member which is fitted by push fit manner or by sealing with outer protective caps.
