"AN IMPROVED DEVICE FOR DRILLING".

Figure 2 of the drawings accompanying this specification shows the schematic diagram of the device of the present inven¬tion. The various components are a drilling unit, a power pack unit, a telescopic jack unit, a motor and a mobile transporting unit for the drill unit.
Accordingly, the present Invention provides an improved device for drilling, which comprises a telescopic, double acting hydraulic ram (14), capable of providing bidirectional movement, the ram being connected to a hydraulic power pack capable of providing bidirectional hydraulic pressure to the said ram, the said power pack being connected to a prime mover (4) with remote control (12), the said ram and power pack being enclosed in a casing (6) having removably fixed means (5) at the sides of the casing for holding and means (l) at its bottom capable of absorb-

ing thrust/shock, the said prime mover being fixed on to the outer surface of the said casing (6), the top end of the said ram being connected to known chuck (10), the said chuck having a removably fixed drill rod (11) with drill bit (16) at its top end, the chuck being provided with means(8,9) capable of provid¬ing rotation to the said chuck at variable speeds.
In an embodiment of the present invention the prime mover (4) used may be such as a flame proof electric motor.
In another embodiment of the present invention the means used for rotation of the chuck, at variable speeds may be such as an electric motor connected to the said chuck through a gear-trai n.
The present invention incorporates improvement in drilling process in underground mines/tunnels, which would significantly free the system from normal components, thereby increasing its efficiency, effectiveness and will be much safer working at the same time. The present invention is an equipment easily trans¬portable by manual labour in underground mines as a single unit on a light structure of multi wheels.
The drilling unit can be brought to the site where holes are to be drilled by the help of means such as a multi wheeled structure. A drill rod (11) is attached with the chuck (10) for drilling purpose. Then the motor and pump can be run to lift the double telescopic jack (7).

Then the pushing ram (7) Is operated by the electrical cable control unit (12) and also the motor of drill unit is operated by the control unit (13) to run the drill rod at required rotation through the reduction gear unit (9),. While in drilling operation the extension piece (1) can be extended up to the ground by mechanically or by hydraullcally. Then, the back force involved due to drilling on the pressure ram would be transferred through the extension piece (1) to the ground. The operator may be at a distance of 5m so while conducting the. drilling operation.
