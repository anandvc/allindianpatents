Title of Invention

A COLLAPSIBLE CONTAINER
Abstract A collapsible container comprising two front and rear panels with their ends dovetailing with the ends of two side panels, the dovetailing ends being fixed together by locking pins passing through matching holes in the dovetailing segments; a base panel for being slidably located in grooves cut at the base of the side panels and fixed in position by the said locking pins passing through matching holes in the said base panel; two top panels for being placed on top of the front, rear and side panels, said top panels having overhanging ridged ends abutting each other; a locking plate slidable on, engageably with, the abutting ridged ends, said locking plate having end flaps for being locked to the said panels.
Full Text

This invention relates to a collapsible container.
The container proposed herein replaces wooden boxes and crates which are now being extensively used, depleting natural resources by deforestation.
The container proposed herein has structural strength required in loading and handling, the design being such that whenever the container is not in use, it can easily be rendered collapsible and stowed away, in stacks thus saving space.
In the case of smaller sizes of the container proposed herein, handles are provided for lifting and transportation ; while in bigger sizes, hooks and eye bolts are provided.
The container proposed herein can be made of any material suitable to the application and requirement e.g. metal and plastics.

Various other features of the container proposed herein will be apparent from the following description.
The collapsible container, according to this invention, comprises two front and rear panels with their ends dovetailing with the ends of two side panels, the dovetailing ends being fixed together by locking pins passing through matching holes in the dovetailing segments; a base panel for being slidably located in grooves cut at the base of the side panels and fixed in position by the said locking pins passing through matching holes in the said base panel; two top panels for being placed on top of the front, rear and side panels, said top panels having overhanging ridged ends abutting each other; a locking plate slidable on , engageably with, the abutting ridged ends, said locking plate having end flaps for being locked to the side panels.

A base panel PB is slidably located in the grooves G cut at the base of the side panels PS1 and PS2 The base panel is fixed in position by the said locking pins passing through matching holes J in the said base panel.
Two top panels PT1 and PT 2 are provided for being placed on top
of the front, rear and side panels, said top panels having ridged
ends Rl R2 abutting each other , These ridged ends have an overhang 01 02 to enable a locking plate M to slidably move on and engage with said ridged ends, said locking plate having hinged end flaps W for being locked to the side panels.

The side panels have dovetailing slideways Y engageable with the base of the said panels to serve as supports

therefor. A pallet Q is provided to deal with heavy loads.
Any one or more of the said panels are provided with handles or eyebolts and hooks for handling and transportation.
