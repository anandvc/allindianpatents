Title of Invention

GOVINDARAJA VEHICLE HEAD LIGHT DIM BRIGHT SYSTEM
Abstract ABSTRACT The Vehicle Head light Dim and Bright system Comprising Three light Detectors in Binocular at the Front Portion of the vehicle grill, disposed to detect Head Light Flash of nearing Opposite Vehicle Communicating to through transistor no.12 and magnetic point no 5 making head light Dim Shade Relay no.18 To be bright position when vehicle overtake, wherein said system. Further Comprises change over switch making, dim and Dip through indicator relay and second relay NO. 40 to 43 magnetic point no.47, 48 while overtaking.
Full Text

This invention relates in tine filed of Electrical Electronic & Micro Waves system. The object of the invention is " Automatic Head light Dim and Bright System for Vehicles". The main object of this invention is to avoid accident of all type of vehicles during night time. When the vehicle driver drives on the road during the night time with bright head lights, the driver normally does not dimm the head lights. According to this system, the detector may be fixed in all the vehicles, i.e., 2,3,4,8 wheelers. When the driver operates the vehicle during the night time with bright head lights. If any of the vehicles come in opposite direction with bright head lights, if reflects in to the vehicle light detector, the vehicle lights will be automatically dimmed and vehicle will go automatically without any hardship.
