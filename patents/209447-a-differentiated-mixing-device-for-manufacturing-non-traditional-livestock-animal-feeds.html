The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed.






The present invention relates to "A DIFFERENTIATED MIXING DEVICE FOR MANUFACTURING NON-TRADITIONAL LIVE STOCK ANIMAL FEEDS" plant, is used for preparing animal foods and the method for preparing them and more particularly concerned with animal foods of the type which comprise a food mixture containing rice bran, ground nut, Sal seed, Soya, Soya meal, wheat bran, gram bran, all types of de-oiled cake and all types of husks meal compressed in cylindrical shape beans or pellets. A compressed bean based composite, which is economical to store and transport.
The edible bean industry has been rapidly expanding in much area. This expansion of the industry has created a surplus of bean byproducts such as splits and chips, which are basically broken. It is well known that beans can provide a great deal of protein at very low cost and the quality and digestibility of beans can be improved by consuming them with cereal grains. In the conventional process animal foods are provided in mostly in powder and cake form. But for easy transport & storage manufactures are compressed food to make this food in chips and beans form. But due to manufacturing process when the beans are broken,it creates wastage and effect the production cost. But in this food beans the contains amount of the molasses are very less. If percentage contains of molasses is increased upto 20%, to get the high amount of energy, pellet beans are not made properly and it converted into the powder form and it creates wastage.
