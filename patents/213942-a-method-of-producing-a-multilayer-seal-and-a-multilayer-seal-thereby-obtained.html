Title of Invention

A METHOD OF PRODUCING A MULTILAYER SEAL AND A MULTILAYER SEAL THEREBY OBTAINED
Abstract A method for producing multilayer seals and multilayer seals thereby obtained, wherein the pressure in the volume between the layers is brought to a preset value, at least one of the faces between the layers being provided with channels (3), said method comprising forming at least one channel (3) on at least one face of said layers by which the seal is subsequently made.
