Field of invention
This invention relates a device for making sticks which is useful for incense, ear buds, teeth stick etc., manufacturing industries.
Background of invention
There are various types of tools; cutter, machines and device are known in the art. Some of the known hand operated as well as motorised tools are bamboo splitting tools (8 & 16 blades), Bamboo splitting machine, bamboo round sticks gadget, Bamboo pole cutter, bamboo outer node remover, bamboo shaver, bamboo sizer, bamboo scraper, bamboo hand splitter, bamboo chip cutter, bamboo slicing machine, bamboo splitting machine. These tools are developed by RDTDC Bangalore (India) and INBAR (Philippines). One of the devices developed by TIFAC (India) is different in construction.
One of bamboo cutter devices disclosed in Japanese patent no. JP2004167957 is known 'as Bamboo stick processing device, and two Chinese patent disclosed in CN2115881U and CN2214938U known as Round Bamboo stick shaper and Bamboo stick conveying device of incense-making machine respectively are different in construction.
Another devices developed by INBAR known in the art comprises a table of around 3
.feet high. A horizontal slot is cut at its center longitudinally of almost 6" to 1 feet width.
A main shaft is rotatably supported by bearing at this central slot. A manual handle or a
motor shaft is attached at one end of the main shaft to give it rotation. A strip cutter unit
is supported on the table such that its two shafts one above the other get the drive from
the said main shaft through some gear drive or chain drive and rotate in opposite
direction with each other. A plurality of cutter blades is fitted on the periphery of each of t
these oppositely rotated shafts. When bamboo strips are fed in the gap in-between the cutter of these oppositely rotated shafts, strips are cut into the thin sticks. More than one strip cutter unit can be place on the table of machine such that they get the drive from the main shaft of the machine and can increase the output of the machine by utilizing the same amount of electric power. So energy can be utilized properly.

Statement of the Invention
According to the present invention there is provided a stick making device comprising a base frame, a vertical support being secured at one end of the base frame, plurality of 'cutter blades adopted to be connected with the vertical support, a spring loaded roller being connected with the vertical support, said roller used to guide the bamboo chips towards cutter blades being supported by specially design roller frame of the device, a metal strip being secured with the vertical support.
Brief description of invention
i ■ ■
In accordance with this invention a stick making device has a base frame and vertical support. Vertical support is secured at one end of the base frame. A plurality of the stick cutter blades is connected with the vertical support in different vertical plane. A spring loaded roller is connected with the vertical support pivotally. A spring loaded roller is 'used to guide the bamboo chips towards cutter blades'which is supported by specially design roller frame of the device. A spring is mounted on the frame of roller which helps to accommodate different thickness of the bamboo chips. A metal strip is secured with the bottom of the vertical support.
Detailed description of invention
The stick making device according to a preferred embodiment is herein described and illustrated in the accompanying drawings wherein;

Fig.l shows the perspective view of the stick making device Fig.2 shows the left side view of the stick making device Fig.3 shows the perspective view of the spring loaded roller ■Fig.4 shows the back view of the stick making device
Referring to the drawings particularly Fig 1 & Fig. 2, a stick making device has a rectangular base frame (1). A rectangular vertical support (2) is secured at one end of the rectangular base frame (1). A plurality of the stick cutter blades (3) is connected with the .vertical support (2) in different vertical plane (4) (see fig. 4) in such a way that the distance between the blades (3) are fixed. The pointed portions (5) of the cutter blades (3) are secured with the base frame (1) to avoid moving and vibrations. Vertical support (2) is used to provide support to stick cutter blades (3) and spring loaded roller (6). .
A spring loaded roller (6) (fig. 3) is connected with the vertical support (2) pivotally.
Spring loaded roller (6) is used to guide the bamboo chips during the cutting operation
towards cutter blades (3) which is supported by specially design roller frame (7) of the
device. A spring (8) is mounted on the frame (7) of roller (9) which helps to
accommodate different thickness of the bamboo chips. A metal strip (10) is secured with
the bottom of the vertical support (2) to minimize the friction force and reduce the wear
'and tear of the device.
During the operation of the device when bamboo chip/strip is pushed towards the spring loaded roller, it displaces the roller position slightly in upper direction as per the thickness of the strip. The strip/chip is guided toward the stick cutter blades by spring (loaded roller mechanism, which also reduces the undue, forces required for pushing. The plurality of stick cutter blades cut the strip/chip in to uniform sticks.
