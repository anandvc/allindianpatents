This invention relates to automatic day night and dim bright lighting system for motor vehicles. At present no automatic system for day-night and dim-bright operation of headlights is used in motor vehicles. This invention offers a complete solution for all problems relating to dim bright operation of the vehicle. Nowadays public roads are witnessing rising num her of road accidents. Lack of adequate dim bright operation of headlights is the main reason for all motor accidents the objectives of present invention is to provide a complete and perfect solution for problems relating driving of motor vehicles
At present one kind of automatic dim bright system is used in motor vehicles. This system using L.D.R mechanism. The main drawback of this L.D.R mechanism is that it is affected by all kinds of lights. Even by a mere tube light it will be operated. Where as the present system will be operated by the lights coming from headlight of opposite vehicle.
The Present invention introduces a complete automatic system which operates head lights and its dim bright function automatically. No remote controller or switches is required for operating this system. This system controlled by infrared rays which are included iii the headlights of the motor vehicle. Other forms of lights; tube light, mercury light, neon bulbs etc... will not affect the operation of the system as these forms of lights contain no infra red rays. This system provides day night operating function of headlights. If this system is installed in motor vehicles headlights will be operated automatically. This system is having a range of 100 meters to 500 meters (receives lights from 100 to 500 meters). When two vehicles come face to face during night the headlights of the vehicle will automatically dim and after passing the vehicle it will be automatically bright. Moreover, whenever light is required the headlight is lighted. Some time due to some climatical reasons shortage of s light may occur. To such situation also the system is very much helpful.
In addition to that usually a driver is bound to send light signals to other vehicle which are trying to overtake him. The driver may or may not be bothered about send in light signals to other vehicles behind him. Or if he is inadvertently omitted to give light signal, this system will automatically give light signals to other accompanying vehicles with the hell) of an indicator fitted on the hack of the vehicle. This light signal will be very useful to overtaking vehicle to overcome accidents during night. As it requires no manual operation of head lights.
In short once this system is installed no care or attention is required for the operation of the headlights. Thus, the present invention is having much relevance and potential in commercial application. The present invention will be explained with reference to the accompanying drawings.
