Technical Field
The present invention relates to a novel process for producing (3R, 5S)-(E)-7-t2-cyclopropyl-4-(4-fluorophenyl)-quinolin-3-yl]-3,5-dihydroxyhept-6-enic acid esters. This compound is useful as a synthetic intermediate of "3-hydroxy-3-methylglutaryl CoA-reductase inhibitor" disclosed in JP 1-279866 A as being useful for a blood-cholesterol reducer.
In addition, the present invention relates to a novel process for producing β-diketocarboxylate ester derivative, which is required for synthesis of (3R, 5S)-(E)-7-[2-cyclopropyl-4-(4-fluorophenyl)-quinolin-3-yll-3,5-dihydroxyhept-6-enic acid esters as described above.
