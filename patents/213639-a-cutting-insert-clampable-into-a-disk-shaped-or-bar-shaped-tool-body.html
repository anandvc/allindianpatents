A CUTTING INSERT CLAMPABLE INTO A DISK-SHAPED OR BAR-SHAPED TOOL BODY
Abstract The invention relates to a cutting insert which can be used to clamp into a disk-shaped or strip-shaped tool body (30, 36). Said cutting insert comprises a front surface (10,22) to which a convex front surface (11) is joined to at least one side. The are-shaped edges (12) of the front surface extend at an angular dimension of > 90 and = 18O° and are embodied as cutting edges (12). According to the invention, either a linear edge which is essentially vertical in relaton to the front surface or cuttimg edge (14) which runs counter to the vertical by a maximum 4O or a concave-shaped edge (24) are Joined to the are-shaped edge(s).
