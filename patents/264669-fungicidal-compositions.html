Full Text FUNGICIDAL COMPOSITIONS
The present invention relates to novel fungicidal compositions for the treatment of phyto-pathogenic diseases of useful plants, especially phytopathogenic fungi, to a method of controlling phytopathogenic diseases on useful plants and to a method of protecting natural substances of vegetable and/or animal origin and/or their processed forms.
It is known that certain o-cyclopropyl-carboxanilide derivatives have biological activity against phytopathogenic fungi, e.g. known from WO 03/074491 where their properties and methods of preparation are described. On the other hand various fungicidal compounds of different chemical classes are widely known as plant fungicides for application in various crops of cultivated plants. However, crop tolerance and activity against phytopathogenic plant fungi do not always satisfy the needs of agricultural practice in many incidents and aspects.
