Title of Invention

A BOOK TYPE DOCUMENT IN PARTICULAR AN IDENTIFICATION DOCUMENT HAVING A HIGH DEGREE OF SECURITY AGAINST FORGERY WITH LOW PRODUCTION COST
Abstract The invention relates to a book-type document, in particular an identification document, comprising a binding (12) or cover, which contains at least one support system (17) for at least one transponder unit (24) and internal pages (18), which are encompassed by the binding (12) or cover. According to the invention, the support system or systems (17) is or are sewn to at least one internal page (18) and said support system or systems (17) is or are configured as a pre-laminated page, which can be printed on at least one side and act or acts as a personal data page or header (14) for the binding (12) or cover.

Book-type document, in particular an identification
document
The invention relates. to a bock-type document, in
particular an identification document or document of
value, such as passports, book-type personal
identification papers, drivers' licenses, provisional
personal documents, security documents, access
authorizations or the like, comprising a book binding
and paper and/or plastic internal pages accommodated
therein.
The internal pages are fixed to the end paper or to the
inner cover page by means of a seam. The end paper or
the inner cover page is joined to the book binding. In
the case of identification documents, the internal.
pages comprise at least one personalization data page,
on which the most important personal data and the image
of the holder are arranged for identification. Further
data can contain entries relating to the holder or free
regions for subsequent entries. These personalization
data pages are provided with a laminate layer on a
front side and a rear side in order to form a laminate
layer covering.

The further internal pages are normally formed of paper
and are used for the application of stamps and visas.
The personalization data pages are stitched together
with the further internal pages and adhesively bonded
to the book binding as the book content. This
embodiment has the disadvantage that, in terms of
fabrication, a large number of operations are necessary
in order to apply the laminate layers and to form a
completely enclosed laminate layer covering.
Furthermore, the formation of the fold region of such a
laminate layer covering in the seam area of the book
content is threatened by cracking or fracture by the

flexible stressing and is relatively rigid in the fold
region.
There is in principle considerable interest in
protecting book-type documents, in particular
identification documents, against forgeries and in
making the production of forgeries technically
impossible or at least unprofitable. At the same time,
there is considerable interest in carrying out the
production of documents of value inexpensively.
The invention is therefore based on the object of
providing a book-type document, in particular an
identification document, in which a high degree of
security against forgery is provided with low
production costs.
According to the invention, this object is achieved by
a book-type document, in particular an identification
document, according to the features of the main claim.
Further advantageous refinements and developments of
the invention are specified in the further dependent
claims.
By means of the configuration according to the
invention of a book-type document, in particular an
identification document, in which the at least one
carrier system is stitched to at least one data page
and the carrier system is formed as a pre-laminate,
which can be treated by printing on at least one side
and is provided as a personalization data page or as an
end paper for the book binding or book cover, a
reduction in the production costs is achieved by the
reduction in the production steps. By using a pre-
laminate as end product which merely still has to be
printed, further processing steps, such as the
application of laminate layers, can be dispensed with
and, at the same time, the security requirements for

forestalling forgeries can be maintained. By means of
the carrier system, which can be treated by printing on
at least one side, the pre-laminate can be printed and
personalized as a personalization data page or end
paper. Thus, for example, document or passport blanks
can be produced inexpensively in which, for the purpose
of fabrication, the carrier system is treated by
printing for the purpose of personalization as required
and the transponder unit is stored a selected data set
of holders of the document. The introduction of the
carrier system into the book-type document as a pre-
larinate can be provided both for a personalization
data page and for an end paper for the book binding or
book cover, which means that various structures are
made possible in book-type documents whilst maintaining
the security requirements and inexpensive production.
According to an advantageous refinement of the
invention, provision is made for the carrier system
provided as a pre-laminate to have a carrier layer to
accommodate at least one transponder unit and at least
one layer covering the transponder unit, which are non-
detachably connected to each other. This ensures that
manipulation by separating a top or carrier layer and
removal or replacement of the transponder unit is
prevented. The non-detachable connection can be made
possible by means of cold or hot adhesive bonding,
lamination, welding and/or a chemical reaction to form
the at least partly material connection. Likewise, a
non detachable connection consists in separation of the
carrier layer or of the top layer causing fragmentation
on account of the structuring of one layer, in the
event that an attempt is made to separate the top layer
from the carrier layer or vice versa.
In order to form a seam area that can be folded well in
the book-type document, provision is advantageously
made for the carrier system provided as a pre-laminate

The carrier system provided as a pre-laminate, which is

a flap opposite the seam area or a complete data page.
As a result of the at least one surface that can be
treated by printing, the pre-laminate has the same
function as a data page and can be used to accommodate
stamps or visas.
The carrier system provided as a pre-laminate which,
according to an alternative refinement, is introduced
into the book-type document of value as an end paper,
can extend completely over both internal pages of the
book binding or book cover or can be provided at least
on one internal page and in the seam area of the book
binding or book cover.
The carrier system provided as a pre-laminate has at
least one flexible and tear-resistant layer. As a
result, removal or destruction of the layer can be
carried out only with considerable force and damage.
This flexible layer is advantageously difficult to
tear. The at least one further layer which covers the
at least one transponder unit is preferably likewise
flexible, so that the pre-laminate is formed as an
arrangement similar to paper or film, which has a layer
thickness similar to a paper or film of the data page.
