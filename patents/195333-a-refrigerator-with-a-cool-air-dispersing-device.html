Abstract A refrigerator includes a main body housing a fresh food compartment. and a coo) air duct provided in a wall of the fresh food compartment. and having cool air discharging openings opened toward the fresh food compartment to supply a cool air from an evaporator into the fresh food compartment. The refrigerator comprises a rotary shaft; a partitioning plate mounted on the rotery shaft along a rotation axis of the rotary shaft, the partitioning plate being disposed near the cooi air discharging opening; a driving means for rotating the rotary shaft', and a pair of cool air dispersing wings mounted on both surfaces of the partitioining plate with an inclined angle to the rotation axis, A cool air dispersing device equipped with such cool air dispersing wing realizes an even refrigeration of the fresh food compartmeni in the left, right, up and down directions. Furthermore a concentrated cooling of one specific region is also made possible by the fixable angular position of the cool air dispersion wings. PRICE: THIRTY RUPEES
