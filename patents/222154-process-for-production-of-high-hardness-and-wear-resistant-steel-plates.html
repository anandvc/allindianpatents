Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR PRODUCTION OF HIGH HARDNESS AND WEAR RESISTANT STEEL PLATES.
Abstract A PROCESS FOR PRODUCTION OF STEEL PLATES WITH HIGH STRENGTH , HIGH HARDNESS AND WEAR RESISTANCE, SAID PROCESS COMPRISING SELECTION OF THE ALLOYING ELEMENTS IN DEFINED PROPORTIONS, HEAT TREATMENT CYCLE AT TEMPERATURE RANGING FROM 850oC TO 950oC COMPRISING OPTIMIZATION OF QUENCHING AND TEMPERING ADAPTED FOR INCREASING THE HARDNESS OF THE STEEL PLATES AND ROLLING ADAPTED FOR PROVIDING STEEL PLATES OF THICKNESS RANGING FROM 10-32 mm.
