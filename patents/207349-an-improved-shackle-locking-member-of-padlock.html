AN IMPROVED SHACKLE (LOCKING MEMBER) OF PADLOCK
This invention relates to the Locking Member (shackle) of Padlock and more particularly to the design and construction of the components. Heretofore , it was known to manufacture the shackle from a one piece round bar fabricated to various shapes matching with the padlock construction , size and shapes.
The object of the present invention is to provide a locking member (shackle) with a novel design and construction , simultaneously retaining the strength of the padlock as a whole.
Still further object of the invention is to provide locking member (shackle) of novel construction to ease manufacture and lesser weight and simple in operation so that the lock operation i.e. locking and unlocking is easier as compared to existing shackle of solid construction. A lesser weight of the object facilitates easy rotation of key and locking / unlocking .
Accordingly there is provided an improved shackle substantially circular / cross section of pad lock comprising a flat semi-circular support plate (1) having plurality of slots (6) provided on its surface to receive guide plates (4) at various orientation. A circular plug (3) securely fitted at the end of the support plate , a round bar guide member (2) integrated with support plate (1) through the rivet (5).
