Title of Invention

AN ELECTRICAL COMPONENT PANEL FOR A MOTORCYCLE
Abstract An electrical component panel for a motorcycle, comprising electrical components of the vehicle connected to the generator of the. vehicle for deriving electric power, characterized in that the said electrical components are formed into a set, the generator and set being in juxtaposition, electrically connected to each other; and in that the said set is supported on, and fixed to, at least one rigid member, provided as a part of the vehicle itself, said member being selected from at least one of the following parts of the vehicle, namely, (1) the crank case cover of the vehicle (2) the chain case cover of the said vehicle.
