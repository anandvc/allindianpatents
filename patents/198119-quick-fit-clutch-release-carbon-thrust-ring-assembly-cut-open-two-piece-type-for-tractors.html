Title of Invention

QUICK FIT CLUTCH RELEASE CARBON THRUST RING ASSEMBLY CUT-OPEN TWO PIECE TYPE FOR TRACTORS
Abstract ABSTRACT Quick fit clutch release carbon thrust ring assembly cut-open, two-piece type for tractors comprising two-piece, cut-open clutch carbon thrust ring assembly consisting of two-piece, cut-open cast iron circular cup, two pieces carbon ring cut to accommodate correctly in the circular cup, a clip having a small folding which gets locked at the notch of the lower portion of the casting to bridge both small and big pieces such that the assembly is held by a screw which is screwed into the small piece. /

Quick fit Clutch Release Carbon Thrust Ring Assembly cut-open, two-piece type for tractors.
Field of Invention:

Baclcground of invention with regard to the draw back of associates with known art:
The Clutch carbon thrust ring assembly operates whenever the clutch pedal at the driver compartment is depressed and released. By depressing the clutch pedal, the carbon thmst ring assembly plays a role where it helps in disconnecting the clutch plate from the flywheel of the engine when a vehicle is changing geai-s. By releasing the clutch pedal (it is coming back to original position) it helps in connecting the clutch plate with the flywheel of the engine. During these operations the clutch carbon thrust ring assembly is subjected to wear & tear and is warranted replacement of the same periodically. Clutch carbon thrust ring assemblies of one-piece circular type are in vogue. A casting ring wherein a carbon ring is press fitted. After wear & tear in the operation, in the event of replacing this one-peace circular type carbon thrust ring assembly, the clutch unit of the transmission system has to be removed for fitment of the carbon thrust ring assembly over the transmission input shaft. Hence, removal of clutch unit of transmission system needs off road of vehicle for a day. This will cost loss of time, manpower and extra expenditure. This novel invention minimizes the difficulties associated in the prior art.
Object of Invention:
Object of Invention is to eliminate removal of clutch unit & Transmission input shaff from the engine so as to minimize to the least possible time of off road of the Vehicle. Object of present invention is to have cut-open, two-piece circular type clutch carbon thrust ring assembly. Object of invention is to facilitate quick and easy fitment of clutch carbon thrust ring assembly.

Single piece equipment is known in the art for a long time. Two-piece is also known in the prior art. The specific feature of the invention is in the incorporation of novel clip on top of the casting which has a small fold at bottom gets locked at the notch provided in the lower portion of the casting so as to bridge both small and big piece. The metal clip has a hole on the top through which a hexagonal screw is screwed into the small segment of the casting for firm, quick and easy fitment of carbon thrust ring assembly over the transmission input shaft.
Summary of Invention:
When a carbon ring is fixed in the cast iron circular cup is niuned as clutch carbon thrust ring assembly. The ctirbon ring is an integral part of the clutch carbon thrust ring assembly. The present invention is that the existing one-piece circular cast iron cup and a carbon ring individually as it is without any change is cut-open, into two pieces a big and small to suit to accommodate in the corresponding big and small piece in order to form a circular shape. The small segment is fixed in the big segment with the metal clip having a hole on the top, gets locked at the notching of the casting bridging small and big segments and a screw at the top of the clip screwed into the small segment forming a firm ring assembly.
