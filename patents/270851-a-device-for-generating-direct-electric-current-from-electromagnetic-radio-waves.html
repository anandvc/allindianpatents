Title of Invention

"A DEVICE FOR GENERATING DIRECT ELECTRIC CURRENT FROM ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIO-WAVES"
Abstract The invention relates to a device for generating electric energy from the radio waves comprising of a mutual induction coil adapted to be connected to antenna (2), a capacitor (C1) being connected to one of the output terminal points of the said induction coil having a pair of diodes (D2, D3) connected in series with each other, another diode (D1) connected to the common points of the said capacitor and diode being connected to the output of said induction coil in parallel, another capacitor (C2) connected to the common points of the said pair of diodes is connected to the said induction coil in parallel, another diode (D4) being connected to the said capacitor in series.
