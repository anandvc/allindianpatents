Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED CARTON"
Abstract This invention related to a carton comprising a box structure (1) with openings at either ends along the longitudinal axis closed by flaps (3), characterized in that a tearable flap member (4) is secured to the upper wall (5) through a weak line of the bow structure (1), an inlay or barrier member (6) adhered to the inner surface of said box structure (1) including said tearable flap (4) and having a weak zone tearable upon opening of said flap.
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a carton. By way of example, but without intending to imply any limitation thereto, the carton of the present invention can advantageously be employed for storage and dispensing of tea bags.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Cartons presently known in the art normally consist of an ordinary carton made of cardboard and having a flap or upper cover member, which may be opened or closed. A distinct disadvantage associated with such a known construction of a carton is that of ingress of air and moisture into the carton, and thereby detrimentally affecting the quality of tea within said bags. Yet another disadvantage is that of ingress of aroma, which results in imparting a tainted taste to tea.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a carton.

Another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a carton, which can advantageously be employed for storage of tea bags.
Still another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a carton, which prevents the ingress of air and aroma into said carton.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a carton, which is simple.
STATEMENT OF INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided a carton comprising a box structure (1) with openings at either ends along the longitudinal axis closed by flaps (3), characterized in that a tearable flap member (4) is secured to the upper wall (5) through a weak line of the bow structure (1), an inlay or barrier member (6) adhered to the inner surface of said box structure (1) including said tearable flap (4) and having a weak zone tearable upon opening of said flap.

In accordance with this invention the carton has a normal box structure made of any known board material. The ends along the longitudinal axis is closed by flaps. Such features of a carton are known in the art and do not form a concept of the present invention.
Specifically, in accordance with this invention, a tearable cover/flap member is provided on the upper wall of said box and such that the rear end of the cover member is connected to the upper wall through a weak line. The inlay or barrier member is also adhered to the cover member and which is also tearable a1ong three sides.
