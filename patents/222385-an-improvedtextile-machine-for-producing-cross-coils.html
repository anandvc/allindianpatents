Title of Invention

AN IMPROVEDTEXTILE MACHINE FOR PRODUCING CROSS COILS
Abstract The invention pertains to an improved textile machine (1) producing cross coil, which has a plurality of work positions (2) with, in each case, separate work position processor (39) and a service unit (23) for serving the work positions (2). The improved textile machine (1) additionally comprises a central control unit (37). The service unit (23) having a separate control processor (39)~ The control devices (37, 39, 39) are connected among each other through a machine bus (40). During the exchanging process between cross coil/empty pirn, for example when a cross coil (11) rigidly hangs with its pirn at the bobbin frame, the bobbin brake 50 integrated in the bobbin frame and/or th. bobbin frame elevating equipment (40) according to the proportion of the control processor (38) of the servicing unit (23) are controlled and are actuated through the. machine, bus (40).
