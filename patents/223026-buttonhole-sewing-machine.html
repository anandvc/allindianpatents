Title of Invention

BUTTONHOLE SEWING MACHINE
Abstract This invention relates to a buttonhole sewing machine, comprising a base plate (2), a top arm (1) and a standard (3) uniting the arm (1) and the base plate (2); a needle (6) movable in up and down reciprocation; at least one work piece clamp (9a,9b) displaceable in a y direction; a buttonhole cutting device (13), which is disposed downstream of the work piece clamp (9a, 9b) seen from the needle (6) in the y direction, which has a knife (36) that is disposed on the base plate (2) and has an eye cutting edge (38) on one end and a straight cutting edge (37) that extends in the y direction, a cutting block (15) which cooperates with the knife (36), and a cutting drive (16) for displacement of the cutting block (15) in a vertical z direction towards the knife (36. The knife (36) and the cutting block (15) are adjustable in parallel to each other.
Full Text FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The invention relates to a buttonhole sewing machines in
particular a buttonhole sewing machine in which the parallelism of the knife and the cutting block is accurately adjustable.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
serman Patent 291 197 teaches a buttonhole sewing machine
of the generic type, which has a buttonhole cutting device comprising knives of varying shape and size that are disposed in the base plate. Depending on the desired shape of the buttonhole, the corresponding knife can be selected from a plurality of revolverâ€”style arrangement. A device of this type is rather complicated.
Dt 197 16 631 A teaches a buttonhole sewing machine in which a knife is disposed on the base plate. A cutting block is deliverable from above towards the knife. For wear of the knife due to impact by the cutting block to be reduced, the knife is springily mounted. Under certain operational conditions that may tor example depend on the hardness and nature of the work piece that is cut, it is possible that a motion of the knife relative to the pressure face of the cutting block is occasioned by the cutting motion, this can affect the edge life of the knife.
OBJECT OF THE INVENTION
it is an abject of the invention to embody a buttonhole sewing machine of the generic type in such a way that wear of the knife and/or improper buttonhole cutting caused by the cutting block and knife not hitting each other in parallel are avoided.
