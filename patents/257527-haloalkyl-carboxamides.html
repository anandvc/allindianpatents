Title of Invention

"HALOALKYL CARBOXAMIDES"
Abstract Haloalkyl carboxamides of the formula (I) wherein R, R1, R2, R3, R4, A, M and N are as herein described as in the specification and claims and process for synthesizing the same.
Full Text The present invention pertains to new haloalkyl carboxamides, several processes for their synthesis and their use for combating undesired microorganisms.
It is already known that numerous carboxamides possess fungicidal properties (see, for example, WO 03/010149, WO 02/059086, EP-A 0 824 099, EP-A 0 737 682, EP-A 0 591 699, EP-A 0 589 301, EP-A 0 545 099, DE-A 24 09 011, DE-A 20 06 472, JP-A 2001-302605, JP-A 10-251240, JP-A 8-176112, JP-A 8-92223 and JP-A 53-72823). Thus numerous alkyl carboxamides have already become known that are not substituted in the alkyl portion, such as, for example, N-allyl-N-[2-(l,3-dimethylbutyl)phenyl]-l-methyl-3-(trifluoromethyl)-lH-pyrazole-4-carboxamide from WO 02/059086, N-[2-(l,3-dimethylbutyl)-phenyl]-2,4-dimethyl-l,3-thiazole-5-carboxamide from EP-A 0 824 099 and 5-fluoro-l,3-dimethyl-N-[2-(l,3,3-trimethylbutyl)phenyl]-lH-pyrazole-4-carboxarnide from WO 03/010149. The effectiveness of these materials is good, but they leave something to be desired in many cases, for example at low application rates.
