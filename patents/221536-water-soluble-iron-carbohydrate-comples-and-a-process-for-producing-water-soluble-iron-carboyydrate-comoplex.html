Title of Invention

WATER SOLUBLE IRON CARBOHYDRATE COMPLES AND A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING WATER SOLUBLE IRON CARBOYYDRATE COMOPLEX.
Abstract Water soluble iron carbohydrate complex obtainable from an aqueous 5 solution of from (III) salt and an aqueous solution of the oxidation product of one or more maltrodextrins using an aqueous hypochlorite solution at a pH-volue within the alkaline range, where, when one moltadexitrin Is applied. Its dextrose equivalent lies between 5 and 20, and when a mixture of several maltextrins is applid, the dextrose equivalent of the 10 mixture lies between 5 and 20 and the dextrose equivalent of each individual maltodextrin contained in the mixture lies between 2 and 40. process for its production and medicament for the treatment and prophylaxis of Iron deficiency conditions.
