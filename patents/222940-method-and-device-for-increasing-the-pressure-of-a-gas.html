Title of Invention

METHOD AND DEVICE FOR INCREASING THE PRESSURE OF A GAS
Abstract Method for increasing the pressure of a gas, in particular of the air delivered in a powder station (2) by a compressor (8), with a booster (24), the mass stream (m) of the gas being subdivided into a smaller part stream (t1) and into a larger part stream (t2), the smaller part stream (ti) being delivered to an ejector (30) via the booster (24) and being combined there with the larger part stream (t2) which is supplied to the suction nipple of the ejector (30), and the combined mass stream (m) of the gas being picked up at the out1et of the ejector (30), characterized in that the smaller part stream (t1) is cooled before delivery to the booster (24).
