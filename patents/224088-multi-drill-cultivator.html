Title of Invention

MULTI DRILL CULTIVATOR
Abstract The present invention relates to a multi drill cultivator being used for cultivating or ploughing the land for agricultural and gardening purpose. A multi drill cultivator for cultivating land as per present invention comprises: a cylindrical part having cylinder shaped body with openable top cover and being placed horizontally, the said cylinder part comprises a gearbox which is placed inside the cylindrical part, said cylinder shaped body having at least four outwardly projected portions being connected to said gear box; a chassis to facilitate mounting of said cylindrical part; a pair of rear wheels being connected at the two ends of the cylindrical part and one front wheel being connected to chassis, the combination of said rear wheels and front wheel facilitate rolling of the cylindrical part over ground; at least four drills being accommodated in the outwardly projected portions of the cylinder shaped body of said cylindrical part, said drill faces towards front wheel; a jack screw mechanism for adjusting angular movement of said drill; a towing bar being fixed near top of the front wheel for providing harnessing of driving mechanism to drive said cultivator; and a seat being provided on operable top cover of cylinder shaped body of said cylinder part for facilitating sitting of a person to drive said cultivator; wherein the motion of rear wheels and front wheel achieved by driving mechanism gets converted through said gear box and leads to an adjustable, forward, angulated and simultaneously axial rotational motion of said drills to ensure proper cultivation of the land. FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to the field of agriculture. Particularly it relates to an agricultural implement used for cultivating the land. More particularly it relates to a multi drill cultivator being used for cultivating or ploughing the land for agricultural and gardening purpose.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
Agriculture in India is one of the most prominent sectors in its economy. About 70% population of the India is directly dependent on agriculture, also agriculture is the main component for most of the state economies in India.
Since time immemorial, when human race learnt farming, the plough farming with bullocks or other animals is in use. The farmer remains occupied for the whole day. With the routine plough, currently in use, the farmer is able to plough only one furrow at a time. And even for this one furrow, the prime mover animal has to strain a lot. In addition, at every end of a furrow, the farmer has to lift up the plough and put it in a new direction of next furrow.
Then came the era of mechanized agriculture whereby, tractor became the leader with "high speed cultivation".

But all farmers cannot afford tractor, because of higher cost with all its taxes and insurance, year after year depreciating price, necessity of a trained and paid personnel to drive the vehicle, running fuel and oil cost and maintenance expenses of spares as well as labour. Consumption of diesel and oil has an impact on national economy because of hidden foreign exchange expenditure. In addition, usage of diesel in any form means some degree of pollution, adversely affecting the ecology. Leaking of diesel or oil from the engine, if any, would spoil our soil, and as with other four wheelers, fatal accidents to occur is not an exception. Till date, by alleviating the use of cattle in agriculture, this mechanized agriculture has pushed many animals to slaughter houses.
Thus for establishing a balanced ecology with qualitative growth, there was an exigency to invent an equipment to uplift the farmers and cattle force of India.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION:
The main object of the present invention is to provide a cultivator which will overcome all the deficiencies associated with the prior art cultivation mechanisms.
Another object of the present invention is to provide an eco-friendly, mainly
animal driven multi drill cultivator which promotes an economic renewable


energy source with low energy profile and which ultimately improves qualitative growth and eco development.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator which plough faster and save lot of time of farmer by producing multifold workoutput.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator, which is very economic.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator, which is eco-friendly and improves and protects environment from pollution since there is no use of fuel in such cultivator.
Still another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator, which is easy to operate. No special skill is necessary to operate such cultivator.
Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator that is easy to maintain .

Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a multi drill cultivator that reduces the physical load on the animal while cultivating the land. There by saves animal power.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
Accordingly to achieve the aforesaid objects present invention provides a multi drill cultivator for cultivating land comprising of:
a cylindrical part having cylinder shaped body with openable top cover and being placed horizontally, the said cylinder part comprises a gearbox which is placed inside the cylindrical part, said cylinder shaped body having at least four outwardly projected portions on front side of outer periphery and being connected to said gear box;
a chassis io facilitate mounting of said cylindrical part;
a pair of rear wheels being connected at the two ends of the said cylindrical part and one front wheel being connected to said chassis, the combination of said rear wheels and front wheel facilitate rolling of the cylindrical part over ground;

at least four drills being accommodated in the outwardly projected portions of the cylinder shaped body of said cylindrical part, said drill faces towards front wheel;
a jack screw mechanism for adjusting angular movement of said drill;
a towing bar being fixed near top of the front wheel for providing harnessing of driving mechanism to drive said cultivator; and
a seat being provided on openable top cover of cylinder shaped body of said cylinder part for facilitating sitting of a person to drive said cultivator;
wherein the motion of rear wheels and front wheel achieved by driving mechanism gets converted through said gear box and into an adjustable, forward, angulated and simultaneously axially rotating motion of said drills to ensure proper cultivation of the land.
According to one of the embodiment of the invention the chassis is pentagonal in shape.
According to another embodiment of the invention driving mechanism
includes human being, animal or any prime mover.

Referring to figure 1, the multi drill cultivator (1) consists of a horizontal cylinder shaped part (2) having dimensions of 914 mm horizontally and 457.4 mm vertically. The horizontal cylinder part (2) consists of a cover (19) at top which is openable. A gearbox is provided inside,the cylinder shaped part (2). On the body of said cylinder shaped part (2) minimum four projected portions (7, 8, 9, 10) are provided. These projected portions (7, 8, 9, 10) are connected internally to gearbox.
The cylinder shaped part (2) is mounted within a pentagonal chassis (3). The
cylinder shaped part (2) is rolled on ground with the help of three wheels - two
rear wheels (4, 5) being provided by the sides of said cylinder shaped part (2)
and one (6) in front. The said wheel (6) is connected with the help of wheel
bracket (20). Each outwardly projected portions (7, 8, 9, 10) is provided with
drill (11, 12, 13, 14), each of 375 mm long. These drills (11, 12, 13, 14) face

towards front wheel (6). The angulation of projected rotating drills (11, 12, 13, 14) is done by jackscrew mechanism (15), which is operated through a transverse bar (16) at the top, in front of seat (18).
The multi drill cultivator (1) of the present invention further comprises a 1305 mm long towing bar (17) fixed near the top of front wheel (6) by universal joint where on each side one bullock is harnessed with the help of a wooden cross bar, conventionally used by the farmer. The movement of the cultivator can be achieved by animals and prime mover also.
A seat (18) is provided on top of horizontal cylinder shaped part (2). The farmer rides on a seat (18) fixed on openable top cover of horizontal cylinder shaped part (2) and drives the bullocks in a direction proposed to be cultivated. With help of jack screw (15), by rotating the top mounted transverse bar (16), the farmer brings down the cultivating drills (11, 12, 13,1 4) at required depth.
As the bullocks walk further, the wheel motion through gearbox within the cylinder shaped part (2) rotates all four drills (11, 12, 13, 14) along with its long axis and start making one to four furrows at a time, according to number of drills fitted. Each furrow 22 centimeters apart and of 10 to 20 centimeters depth, depending upon land hardness and strength of prime mover. Considering 0.91 meter width being cultivated at a time with all four drills, 1200

meters walk of prime mover animal will cultivate one acre in 30 to 40 minutes time.
The cultivator (1) does cultivation using renewable energy source. As a prime mover animals are used with this cultivator (1) and the animals are fed with readily available vegetarian sources of food. Thus utilization of renewable energy source would fulfill the global demand of energy resources.
The cultivator (1) indirectly saves animals. By enhancing the use of such multi drill cultivators, farmers, will be tempted to keep more cattle and will take greater care of them, thus preventing them going to slaughter houses.
ADVANTAGES OF THE MULTI DRILL CULTIVATOR OF THE PRESNET INVENTION:
, i. Multi drill cultivator of present invention do cultivation much faster saving lot of time of the farmer. This machine does up to four furrows at a time rather than one in prior traditional ploughing. Thus, it produces four-fold work output.
ii. Being a purely mechanical machine and using cattle as a prime
mover, there are no recurrent expenses of fuel and oil. There is only
one time purchase cost and easy economical maintenance for the
farmer to bear.

iii. It does cultivation at "zero pollution". As there is no usage of any fuel there is zero pollution.
iv. It does cultivation using renewable energy source. As prime mover animals are fed with readily available vegetarian sources would fulfill the global demand of energy resources.
v. It establishes the ecological balance. The cattle while on the farm, during cultivation, will add their excreta in the soil adding bio fertilizers.
INDUSTRIAL APPLICABILITY:
The multi drill cultivator of the present invention basically relates to agricultural industries. It is used for cultivating or ploughing the land for agricultural and gardening purpose with less physical efforts on the side of animal or human being driving said cultivator.
