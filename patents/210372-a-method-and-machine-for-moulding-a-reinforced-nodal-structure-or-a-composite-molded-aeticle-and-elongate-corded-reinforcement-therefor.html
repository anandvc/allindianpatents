Title of Invention

"A METHOD AND MACHINE FOR MOULDING A REINFORCED NODAL STRUCTURE OR A COMPOSITE MOLDED AETICLE AND ELONGATE CORDED REINFORCEMENT THEREFOR"
Abstract A method of moulding a reinforced nodal structure (10)which comprises laying down an elongate cored reinforcement (38) of constant section in and along the channels (26)of a nodal mould (24)and across the nodes (13) thereof by repeated passes along the channels to at least partially fill the channels,closing the mould,and curing resin provided around the reinforcement.
