Title of Invention

AN APPARATUS FOR PURIFICATION OF BLOOD AND A METHOD OF ASSEMBLING
Abstract The present invention is relation to a medical device, dialyser or artificial kidney. The instant invention is a solution to the problem involved in various disorders of kidney. It can be placed inside the body and can bring about the purification of blood.
