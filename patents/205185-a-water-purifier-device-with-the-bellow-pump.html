Title of Invention

A WATER PURIFIER DEVICE WITH THE BELLOW PUMP
Abstract An air pressure operable water purified container comprising: An air pressure operable purifier cartridge assembly comprising an air. pressure displaceable liquid/water dispensing means comprising a bellow pump unit having an outlet and a multi stage purifier cartridge assembly comprising a multi stage purifier media cartridge operatively connected to said bellow pump at the top; said multi stage purifier cartridge comprising at its lower end a first purifier media comprising sediment, an intermediate second purifier media ,comprising disinfection media and a top third purifier media comprising carbon purifier; said bellow pump unit adapted to be releasbly secured with respect to a container mouth with said purifier cartridge housed inside said container such that the operation of the bellow pump provide for air pressure forcing the liquid/water in the container to traverse selectively through said multi stage purification cartridge and finally exit through said outlet with said bellow pump as purified liquid/water.

FIELD OF THE INVENTION [WATER PURIFIER DEVICE WITH THE BELLOW PUMP]
The present invention relates to water purifying system and in particular to a water purifier container involving the use of air pressure. The purifier container of the invention is directed to a simple and cost effective manner of purified water generation involving air pressure forces by which the water / fluid in a container is forced to flow from the bottom to a top outlet through a multistage purifier. The purifier container is very convenient for use and can be utilized by consumers very simply and readily. The invention is also directed to water purifier cartridge units such as sediment filter unit, disinfections unit, and carbon based purification unit adapted for such air pressure driven water purifier containers and the like.
BACKGROUND ART
It is well known that filtering and purifying water is a challenging process in the present day hazards concerning the wide ranging contamination of drinking water. It is found that in many parts of the world even the municipality tap water is found to the contaminated with bacteria and chemicals.Various devices / system for taking guard against such harmful contamination of water have been provided which range from simple ceramic filtration to more sophisticated purification systems like ultraviolet^ reverse osmosis, ozone treatment etc.
Moreover, the water outside home is even badly affected by problems of pollution. Bottle water or mineral water is the most common source for drinking water outside of home. It is rarely that we find any form of purifiers in bottle containing water and therefore it is also experienced that sometimes even such bottled water and mineral water if not properly treated or left unused for a long period can have contamination at a later point when used for drinking/consumption. Therefore there is a serious concern even as regards provision of packaged drinking water as to its capability to maintain the contents pure and harmless for human consumption.
The use of dispensing liquids from container using air pressure inside the container is well known.
US3955780 describes a reusable dispensing apparatus for dispensing a flowable product by pressurised air. The air pressure is created by an external air pump operated by the user.
US390520 and JA1973000043468 describe the means of pouring a liquid contained in vacuum flax without tilting the flask or opening the lid. The

Liquid is pumped up a tube by the pressure created by bellow design pump in
the lid of the flux. Such type of devices usually thermos flask, to dispense either hot and cold liquid which has a bellow type of pump In the lid is commonly known as wAir Pots".
US4113147 and US4310104 discloses further modification and easy dispensing of fluids. Further varieties of air pots wherein the water tubes are concealed in the walls of the container are further disclosed under US4359174 and US4238054. Based on the above concept numerous other products are made available that use either simple bottle to dispense fluid or use thermos flasks to dispense hot or cold fluids into a cup without opening the lid or tilting the flasks.
US4054526 discloses the manner of pressurized water flow through purifier cartridge wherein external pump is used to create the pressure.
US5733448 discloses the use of bellow pump to push water one chamber to another lower chamber having a purifier between two chambers.
US5431813 provides a water purifier cartridge in the neck of the bottle.In such system pressure is created inside the bottle by squeezing the sides of the bottle which pushes the water through the purifier cartridge.
US5545315 discloses system where the water to the purified flows up longitudinally inside the purifier cartridge. The required pressure that enable the flow of water is attained by combination of squeezing of the sides of the bottle and also sucking on the outlet mouthpieces.
It would be apparent from the above that while the use of air pressure to force fluid / water in containers and its application in purifier cartridges in isolation have been known and in use, there is no system available which could advantageously provide combination of air pressure based fluid /water displacement and integrate the same with in-house purifiers in containers thereby favouring a more reliable and ready purification of the liquid/water in such containers before consumption. Importantly also there is no such air pressure based discharge container which could facilitate immediate ready purification of water and thereby avoid the problems of insufficient and / or ineffective purification of bottled purified water or mineral water and the like. There has thus been a continuing need in the art to provide for purifying containers which would ensure that the purified discharged is effectively purified and is safe for consumption.

OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
It is thus the basic object of the present invention to provide for purifier containers with purified liquid/water discharge facilities involving ready air pressure driven multi -stage purification of liquid /water contents.
Another object of the present invention is directed to provide for a purification system which would involve the selective advantageous combination of airpots and multistage cartridge based purification in storage and purification containers.
Yet another object of the present invention is directed to provide to simple and cost effective purifier container system involving air pressure created in the container to pump the water up the multi stage purifier through an outlet spout which would be user friendly.
Yet further object of the present invention is to provide for a portable purifier unit which can be easily carried from one place to the other and can be also be used as small table top models as well as in large size units for ready and safe drinking water.
Yet further object of the present invention is directed to provide purifier container adapted with air pressure discharge and purification means which can be made of any rigid material and an any shape and size.

When all the water in the container is purified the bellow pump along with the attachment is remove from the container and the container is again filled with water to the purified. The bellow pump with the purifier assembly is then fitted back on the container and the process of bellow pumping is then resumed.

Reference is first invited to Figure 1, which illustrates an embodiment of the purifier container of the invention . As shown in the figure the purifier container basically comprises of the bellow pump (1) which is operatively connected to the multi -stage purifier cartridge system(2). The purifier cartridge system (2) is releasable secured to the bellow pump (1) and thereafter the purifier cartridge unit (2) is inserted within the container (3) .Finally to ensure the compact housing the container mouth is provided with threaded connection with respect to corresponding threads in the cap member (1A) accommodating the bellow means.
Importantly, as also illustrated in the figure, the filter cartridge unit comprises of provision for multistage purification preferable a three stage purification system as shown in said figure.
In accordance with a preferred aspect of the present invention such three stage purification comprises of a first stage purification comprising a sediment removal filter (2A), a second stage would be comprising disinfection removal means (2B) and a third stage comprising a carbon block filter means (2C).
Thus as per the embodiment illustrated the Figure 1 the first stage of the sediment filter can comprise of sediment filter made of moulded porous polymeric material, ceramic, non oven fabric. The sediment filter can involve pore sizes which can be between 3 microns to 25 microns depending upon the impurities. As shown in the figure the sediment filter is attached with an '0' ring (01) to the second stage which is the disinfection stage (2B).
The disinfection stage (2B) comprises disinfecting agents like halogenated resine such as panda iodinated resin or chlorinated beads. The disinfection stage is further adapted to be used to house hollow fiber membrane to act as the barrier against bacteria and virus. As shown in said figure the disinfection stage is further connected the third stage (2C) which comprises of carbon granules or carbon block for the final policing of the water via ring (02). The purifier cartridge unit at the top is finally connected to the bottom of the bellow pump with the '0' ring (03).The operative connection of the bellow pump and the three stage purifier cartridge is further illustrated in greater detail in accompanying figure 2 which is an exploded view of the multi stage cartridge and figure 3 which is an illustration of the operative connection of the bellow to the said multi stage purifier cartridge unit of th present purifier container.
As shown in figure 1 the entire assembly of the bellow pump is inside the container attached to cartridge which in turn is attached to the top of the container. This is achieved by way of a possible releasable connection between the threaded portion of the container with which a corresponding

thread inernally formed in the cap housing the bellow co-operate to thereby releasable connect the bellow with respect to the container with the cartridge
assembly housed inside the container.
With the pumping action of the bellow, air is pressed into the container which pump the water up the purifier cartridge assembly and the purified water is discharged from the outlet spout. With continuing pumping of the bellow, purified water continues to flow out of the outlet spout. When the entire water of the container is pumped out, the bellow pump assembly with the purifier cartridge attached is taken out of the container and the container is filled again with the water to the purified. The bellow pump assembly and the purifier cartridge is then fitted back on the container for the pumping and the purifier water.
It is thus possible by way of the present invention to provide for purifier containers with purified liquid/water discharge facilities involving ready air pressure driven multi -stage purification of liquid /water contents. The purifier containers of the invention are simple and cost effective for user friendly application and use during travel and/or at any locations. The system is portable purifier unit which can be easily carried from one place to the other and can be also be used as small table top models as well as in large size units for ready and safe drinking water.
