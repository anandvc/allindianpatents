Title of Invention

TEMPER PROOF SEALING
Abstract A temper proof seal comprising a male part and a female part made from polycarbonate material wherein a twisted double stand steel wire is mouldably connected to the base of the male part and base of the female part; said male part having double notch and V-shaped projections and the said female part having complementary groups and to arrest the projected parts and when pushed into the female part thereby permanently locking the male and female part together which have serial numbers and firm name or client logo/name on the male part or inside the female part while moulding which can be seen from outside due to the transparent character of the polycarbonate.

PREAMBLE TO THE DESCRIPTION
The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed : -

Field of Invention
The invention relates to a temper proof seal made from polycarbonate plastic material and having stainless steel twisted wire moulded itself in temper proof seal male and female part.
Prior Art
ORDINEKY PLASTIC/SEALS
Ordinary seals are made up of PP or HDP material having no UV resistance.
Plastic/Lead seals are opaque so inside tempering cannot be easily visible.
a) Easy to tamper
b) Tamper attempts are not easily evident
c) Easy to re assemble usually Single wire
d) One can cut and change seal wire easily
e) Plastic seals can withstand to 120° C so it can be easily tamper by boiling
Consecutive numbering is tough to do on lead seals. General plastic seals have printed or itching type number on male or female portion of the seals and it can be easily duplicated.
Cheaper plastics quickly deteriorate due to weather and other harsh conditions lead seal suffer from corrosion made from single plastic material. Usually low quality.
Tool require for lead seals as well as plastic seals.
Impossible with lead seals. Ordinary Plastic seals do not offer this level of customization.
Therefore there is a need to prevent such tempering of seal, with double locking system using polycarbonate male female part which are push fit, the male part and

the female part are connected by non-corosive and non- magnetic stainless steel wire which are moulded while making the seal
To manufacture the temper proof seal first of all we have to pre heat the Polycarbonate plastic materials at 110 ° C in drier for three to four hours before using. And mean time Stainless steel wire is tested first in dimension and acid test and then it is twisted in twisting machine up to desired twist and then it is cut by cutter to required length.
First we put pre heated Polycarbonate plastic materials granule in Vertical type molding machine and then mould of seal is been closed by hydraulic pressure of machine and then wire is insert in mould and then material is been injected in mould through moulds machine and hence temper proof seal is got ready.
The emboss type numbering arrangement on male and side of female part of the temper proof seal is itself in the mould and the constructive numbering is obtain -in all The temper proof seals by automatic arrangement of numerator counter inserted in mould itself.
Then these temper proof seals are tested by pullout test, Acidic test and arranging in serially, tide with the steel wire forming a loop and the same shall be packed in polythene bag with labels furnishing sr. nos. of seal, packed in corrugated box.
1. These seals are made up of plastic (Polycarbonate) which have a melting point of 270° C and have transparent property so tempering can be easily detect.
2. These seal are having Anchor type double locking arrangement with molded Stainless Steel wire in male & female part of seal so that male and female part of seal can't be separated after locking.

3. These seal have emboss type seven digit unique numbering of male and side of female part and have arrangement of providing company logo on both side of female part.
4. These seals are having properties to withstand in all whether conditions.
5. These seals are withstood in the acid test to stop the tempering of the seals.

The seals will be colorless, transparent (See through) Type, which shall give complete visualization of its fixing mechanism and shall show clear indication if tempered. Also seals can be available in different colour like Red, Blue, Yellow, Green; and Orange etc.
Design and Construction of seal.
a. Design:
The Seal will be Anchor type temper evident with double locking system as per drawing no. AP - 05 & 06 attached with the specification only.
b. Size of the Seal:
The size of the female part of the seal shall be as under:
1. Size: 18 x 14 x 7 mm ± 0.5 mm
2. Size: 20 x 20 x 8 mm ± 0.5 mm
Size of the hole of female part: The size of the hole shown in the drawing should be sufficient to insert seal wire, but in no case, more than 1.0 mm ± 0.1 mm.
Wall thickness should be 0.8 mm (no negative tolerance.)
Serial No. of the seal:
Non repeat seven digits Sr, Nos. with code no. on the seal shall be embossed and it shall not be screen printed and it should be provided on male and female portion at the place mentioned in the drawing i.e. on the top in male part and on the side in female part, Sr. No. of seal shall not be embossed after the seal is manufactured. The size of the digit shall be as under.
1. On seal size 18 x 14 x 7mm - 7 Digit size 1.5 x 2.5mm(w x h)
2. On seal size 20 x 20 x 8mm - 7 Digit size 2.0 x 3.0mm(w x h)

Monogram:
The seal have Monogram of Company on front side and month and year in figure in
on the backside as under:
1. On seal size 18 x 14 x 7 mm - Company logo - in 8 mm circle on front side and month and year in figure on backside in 8 mm circle.
2. On seal size 20 x 20 x 8 mm - in 10 mm circle on front side and month and year in figure on backside in 10 mm circle.
Seal wire.
The non corrosive non magnetic as per is 304 stainless steel twisted wire (26 SWG) shall be used as under. The seal wire shall not have affect of magnet, i.e. it should not attract to magnet.
On seal size 18x14x7 mm - open wire stainless steel twisted of 26 SWG of 160 mm length and 65 mm ± 5.0 mm.
On seal size 20 x 20 x 8 mm - open wire stainless steel twisted of 26 SWG of 160 mm length and 80 mm ± 5.0 mm molded wire as shown in the drawing.
The diameter of each individual strand wire shall be 0.45 mm (approx) (26 SWG) and over all diameters of seal wire shall be 0.9 mm ± 0.05 mm, so that it can easily insert into the female portion where the diameter of the hole shall be 1 .Omm ± O.lmm.
The seal wire used for the above size of seals shall be inbuilt in connecting male and female part of the seal, the application of the seal wire is to insert seal wire and through the hole via female part and insert the male part into female part by applying thumb pressures to lock the seal
General Construction:-

The seal shall be designed for a single use only and if Tempered with the help of puller, knife or any other sharp instruments, the seal shall be damaged and due to its see through property. The sign internal of tempering shall be easily detected. Also once opened, it cannot be re-used. The Seal shall be made in such a way that, it can be easily locked with the help of finger and thumb pressing and no tools shall be required to close the seal in the laboratory or at site. After inserting the seal wire through female pare, the cap of the male part shall be fitted in the female part in such a way that it should not leave any space to avoid insertion of any sharp tools for opening of seal body of the female part in hot or cold condition. The seal shall have also the following features.
a) Temper resistance and reliable.
b) Environmentally safe as it does not contain any lead.
c) Withstand long-term exposure to direct sunlight.
d) Tools not required for Installation.
e) Transparent and see through body reveals
f) Heat resistance.
g) Chemical resistance
TEST
The seal shall be tested at the manufacture's works for the following tests;
1. Visual Check: i.e. workmanship and other features as mentioned above.
2. Dimensional Check: as per drawing.
3. Boiling water test: the seal shall be emerged in the boiling water for more than one hour and there shall not be any affect on the seal and the seal shall remain in tact condition.
Even, with the help of any sharp instrument, pulling with puller i.e. by applying mechanical force, the seal shall not come out from the female part. In case, it comes out, the same shall damage the seal, so that it can not be re-used.

4. Pulled out test: After locking the seal, if the seal is pulled with mechanical force with the help of puller or any other instrument, sharp instrument etc, at normal condition, the seal shall not be unlocked without any damage.
5. Seal wire: In case, if someone tries to pull the seal wire and in any of the test mentioned at Sr. No. 3,4 & 5 in that case the male/female portion of the seal should be damaged and the same can be seen visually being a transparent one.
6. Chemical Test: The seal shall be kept in the concentrated acid for minimum one hour, the same shall remain in the tact condition and if try to unlock the seal, the same shall be damaged.
In sort, if the seal is tested for any of the above tests, in no condition the male and female part shall be separated without affecting damaging the seal. In case, if they are separated, the seal shall have sufficient temper evident. Also, if seal wire is pulled out from the seal in any of the above tests, it shall not come out from the seal without damaging seal.
Sampling criteria:-
Minimum 5 samples of seals shall be selected at random as pet IS 4905 for testing purpose from the each lot.
Special Feafure:-
The seals are to be manufactures as per enclosed drawing only. The size of the seal, type and dimension of the seal shall be strictly as per the drawing only.
Sample:-

The sample of seals with logo of firm on front side & month & year of manufacturing on backside of the female part of the seal. The seals shall also be provided sr. no. of 000001 to 000025 on top of the male part of the seal as well as side of the female part.
Summary of the invention
Accordingly, there is provided a temper proof seal comprising a male part (1) and a female part (2) made form polycarbonate material wherein a twisted double stand steel wire is mouldably connected to the base of the male part (3) and base of the female part (4); said male part having double notch (5) and (6) V- shaped projections and the said female part having complementary groups (7) and (8) to arrest the projected parts (5) and (6) when pushed into the female part thereby permanently locking the male and female part together which have serial numbers and firm name or client logo/name on the male part or inside the female part while moulding which can be seen from outside due to the transparent character of the polycarbonate.
