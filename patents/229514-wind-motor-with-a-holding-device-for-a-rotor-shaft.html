Title of Invention

WIND MOTOR WITH A HOLDING DEVICE FOR A ROTOR SHAFT
Abstract The invention relates to a wind motor with a gear unit (54) whose input is coupled to a rotor shaft (50) that has a separate bearing. The invention is characterized in that a holding device (10) is provided between the bearing and the gear unit (54) and serves to hold the rotor shaft (50) in a predetermined position when mounted.
