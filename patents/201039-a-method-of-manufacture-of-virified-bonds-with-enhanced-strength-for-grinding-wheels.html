Full Text This invention relates to a method of manufacture of vitrified bonds, with enhanced strength, for grinding wheels.
Xt is well known in the art that only the relative composition of frits and clay or the use of different frits are effective in controlling the strength characteristics of vitrified bonds. These changes normally result in totally different varieties of bonds.
The method proposed herein enhances bond strength and the performance of the bonds without altering their major characteristics,,
The additives to the standard bond are selected from any one of the rare earths or any refractory oxides such as any form of zirconia both stabilized and unstabilized. These substances are added to the vitrified bond up to 7.5 % by weight, more preferably, around 6%.
The additive and the bond are mixed thoroughly in a

suitable mixer depending on the uniformity achievable. The bond is sieved through fine meshes to ensure breaking of the lumps, if any.
The method of manufacture of vitrified bonds, with enhanced strength, for grinding wheels, according to this invention, comprises the steps of mixing a known bond with a substance selected from rare earth materials and refractory compositions which are nan— reactive with the said bond, such as, stabilized and unstabilized zirconia, up to 7-5% by weight; mixing the same thoroughly, before sieving through fine meshes for ensuring breaking of lumps,, if any.
