Background of the invention:
This invention relates to a composition which improves the properties of cement and white cement when mixed together. Such properties include improved gripping power, damp proofing, preventing hair cracking, desaltification of construction, minimizing heat transmission through construction, improved compatibility of the repaired portions with the rest of construction areas, prolonged strength of construction, better finish, reduced cement consumption resulting in economy of scale etc.
This invention also relates to a process of preparing the said composition. This invention further relates to a method of using the composition.
This invention still further relates to a method of enhancing the properties of cement and white cement.
This invention additionally relates to a method of damp proofing in construction.
This invention still additionally relates to a method of preventing hair cracking of white cement.
This invention still further relates to a method of desaltification of construction etc.
Almost all construction work involve the use of cement externally and internally. For better internal finish, flooring etc. white cement is also increasingly used. Needless to say that there is no better and cheap material available which can replace the mass use of cement and white cement where applicable in construction job. It is also a fact that a good civil construction from cement, mortar, steel etc, gives the building a prolong life withstanding the fury of nature, weather conditions such as summer heat, winter, monsoon, thunderstorm etc. But it is often noticed that a house gets damped, piaster loosened or cracked externally or internally sooner or later. Rainwater or water from other sources within the building penetrates, spoil the belongings and create damages to the whole building property. Life term of such buildings gets reduced unless repaired and living in such buildings also becomes hazardous.Even a good finish white cement work can show micro-crack which often requires repair for preventing further damage. Once such defects are noticed in a construction it is extremely difficult within the known art to completely repair the defect for a prolong time at least.

Description of the invention:
According to this invention there is provided a cement property enhancer composition comprising a mixture of Zn(OH)2, AI(OH)3, KCI, CaSO4 and H2SO4 as an aqueous suspension.
The relative proportions of the components are based on amount of Zinc Sulphate, Calcium Chloride and Alum used in making the composition in the proportions of 1:0.5 -2:0,5 - 2 respectively by weight.
The said composition is acidic in nature and has a pH of about 5.
The invention also provides a process for preparing the said cement property enhancer composition which comprises mixing zinc sulphate, calcium chloride, and alum, boiling the same in water sufficiently to get an emulsion as the desired composition.
The invention also provides for a method plastering/concrete roofing/pillar piling/ flooring/desaltification in concrete construction/damp proofing/repairing cracks/heat proofing/painting characterized in that the cement property enhancer composition is mixed with cement or white cement as applicable in an amount depending on the nature of work to be done such as herein described. The civil work can be construction of a new building or simply a repair job. The cement and/or white cement used for working the invention can be of any source, quality or brand.
The invention also provides for a method of enhancing property of cement or white cement comprising mixing cement or white cement with the cement property enhancer composition before use.
Generally the proportion of the cement property enhancer composition of the invention used is 7 to 14 gms of the composition per kg of cement used.
Preparation method:
The process for preparation of the cement property enhancer composition: Raw materials:-
a) Zinc Sulphate (ZnSO4)
b) Calcium Chloride (CaCI2)
c) Alum (K2SO4, AI2(SO4)3, 24H2O)
d) Water (H2O)

Equipments:
a) Heating stove or oven
b) Fuel
c) Stainless steel cauldron
d) Stirring means (stainless steel)
Procedure:
Heat water (1 Lt) to boiling in the stainless steel cauldron. Mix the raw materials zinc sulphate, calcium chloride and alum in equal proportions (totaling 1 kg). Add the mixture to the boiling water. Continue boiling the mixture with stirring with a stainless steel stirrer for one to one and half hour till the mass becomes a suspension like. Mass temperature to be within 120 - 180 degrees C. Remove the cauldron from the stove. Add 150 - 200 ml water per kg of suspension to make it a homogeneous liquid emulsion. Allow it to cool and store in natural environment in closed plastic or glass bottles.
The product composition as an aqueous suspension constitutes a mixture of Zn(OH)2, AI(OH)3, KCl, CaSO4 and H2SO4.
The raw materials are readily available in the market and are of commercial grade.
Method of using:
Using the composition of the invention is very simple. However, depending upon the nature of the use or application the proportion of the composition is determined. For example, to make brick wall, plastering, concrete roof, flooring and pillaring in a new building, ratio of the composition to the mortar is 450 gm per 1 bag (50 kg) cement. Slight deviation in proportion as prescribed would not make much difference in the end result. But in case of repair work, to increase the gripping property, the ratio of the composition to the mortar is 600 gm per 1 bag (50 kg) cement to be mixed with water.
To make desaftification in new concrete construction, simply the composition can be used as cement additive. But in case of repair of old concrete buildings, firstly the composition is needed to be spread over the affected surface before actually applying the total mix of the composition, cement, sand etc. for increasing the gripping property.

To make damp proof of new and old concrete constructions same method of application as in the case of desaltification is to be followed.
In case of repairing the crack in a building and roof, the composition of the invention acts as an adhesive. Firstly, the affected area needs to be cleaned. Then the composition of the invention is spread to have a thin layer. After the same, the mixture of the composition with cement and sand etc. be plastered on the area.
For heat proofing, a thin layer of the composition is applied on both the outer and inner surface of the walls of the existinq building. In case of new building, the plastering is done with the composition of the invention added to cement and sand in the general way. But in case of old buildings the treatment as in the case of desaltification is to be followed.
The composition of the invention will prevent hair cracking and increase the density of the piaster. So using this property it is possible to make the floor stony like, it can be used for white cement wash. It can also be used for colouring outside and inside the building with all beneficial qualities as aforesaid. It can also be applied to plaster of pans over the wet surface.
Exact mechanism as to how the composition of the invention works is not known. However, it is believed that using this composition with cement and sand etc. there occurs a kind of polymerization resulting in an increase of density of the plaster and minimization of the gap between sand and sand. This helps to increase the strength of the concrete. In the mortar the salts possibly change their character which does not absorb environment water or moisture. There are minimum pores, thus remains damp proof in comparison with the structures where such composition of the invention is not used. By the same mechanism, because of the increase in the density of the plaster using this invented composition and reducing the sand to sand gap reduction of heat transmission through the concrete surface could be possible. To repair the crack on the wall, roof in the construction, this invented composition acts as an adhesive. Similarly, when white cement is mixed with this composition, the resulting mixture prevents hair crack. The extra gripping or increase in sand to sand bond strength could be also due to polymerization and electronic bonding of the substances used.
The invented composition has long shelf life in normal temperature, light and air. It is non-toxic, non-corrosive, mildly acidic, non-odourous, white in colour and also is environment friendly.
