Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A CONDITIONED COMPOSITION BY ABSORBING AT LEAST ONE LIQUID ON A SUPPORT CONTAINING A PRECIPITATED SILICA
Abstract TITLE: PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A CONDITIONED COMPOSITION BY ABSORBING AT LEAST ONE LIQUID ON A SUPPORT CONTAINING A PRECIPITATED SILICA. The invention provides a process for the preparation of a conditioned composition by absorbing at least one liquid on a support containing a precipitated silica in the form of substantially spherical beads having an average bead size in excess of 150 um, a packed filling density lower than 0.29, a 75um screen oversize of at least 88% by weight and a pore volume made up of pores having a diameter smaller than 1um in excess of 2.0cm3/g.
