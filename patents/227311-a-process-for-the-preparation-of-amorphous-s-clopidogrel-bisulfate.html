Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF AMORPHOUS S-(+)-CLOPIDOGREL BISULFATE.
Abstract The present invetion relates to new polymorphs of S-(+)-Clopidogrel bisulfate and a process for prparing the same by racting (S)-(+) Clopidogrel free base in suitable solvents with conentrated minerls acids at elevated temperatures and subsequently slowly removing the solvents.
