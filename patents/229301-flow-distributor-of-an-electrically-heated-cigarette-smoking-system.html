Title of Invention

FLOW DISTRIBUTOR OF AN ELECTRICALLY HEATED CIGARETTE SMOKING SYSTEM
Abstract A heater fixture (39) for use in an electrically heated cigarette smoking system (21) having a source of electrical energy for heating a cigarette (23) includes an inner wall or barrel (200) having at least one opening through the inner wall (200) extending from an outer peripheral surface to an inner surface of the inner wall (200), and at least one flow distributing element (20) positioned within the at least one opening and extending inwardly from the inner surface (200). The flow-distributing element (20) or elements are provided with a plurality of openings (30a, 30b, 30c and 30d) that are in fluid communication with an interior of the heater (39).
