Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PREPARATION OF AN ANTIDEPRESSANT COMPOUND
Abstract The present invention provides optically pure (S)-(+)-N-methyl-3- (1-naphthaleneoxy)-3- (2-thienyl) propanamine, a compound of formula 1, and optically pure (S)-isomer of compound of formula 4, wherein R&#8321; and R&#8322; both are methyl or R&#8321; is methyl and R&#8322; is benzyl or substituted benzyl group and process for preparation thereof. In another aspect the present invention provides a process for preparation of an acid addition salt of compound of formula 1.
