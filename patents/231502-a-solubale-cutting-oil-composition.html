Title of Invention

A SOLUBALE CUTTING OIL COMPOSITION
Abstract A soluble cutting oil composition comprising a high viscosity index (HV1) base oil 5-90% by wt amine oleate 3-60% by wt, heavy alkyl benzene 0-8% by wt, biocid 0.5-10.8% by wt, co-solvent 0.512% by wt deodorant 0.1-3.6% by wt, corrosion inhibitor 0.8% by wt, optionally sodium oleate or potassium oleate, and water for metal working applications.
