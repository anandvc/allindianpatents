The combustion properties of a spray produced by such injectors are mainly controlled by changing relative atomizing gag and liquid fuel speeds. A combustion method according to the invention is; implemented by a burner comprising a spray-guidef atamization head 6 with external mining as shown in Figures 1 and 2. This atomization head 6 comprises:

Furthermore, the atomization head 6 has six secondary ejection channels ll oriented parallel to the fuel
ejection line 7 and to the atomizing gas ejection line 8. These six secondary ejection channels 11 are uniformly distributed around the ejection lines 7, 8 and are designed to create secondary atomizing gas, streams 1 tapped off from the atomizing gas feed 9 by bypass channels 12. Under the effect of these secondary atomizing gas streams 1, the. liquid fuel spray 10 thereby undergoes a second atomization producing a modified spray 13. Thia modified spray 13 is then contacted with an oxidizer stream (not shown), optionally divided into a primary stream and a secondary stream, to carry out the combustion of the fuel. A tertiary oxidizer feed may alao be located at a relatively long distance from the atomization head 6 and is used to inject oxygen at high spiced in order to obtain a sufficient dilution of the reagents before the main combustion zone and thereby to prevent an excessive formation of thermal NOx compounds. Experiments hav« demonstrated that such a burner, in which 50% of the total oxygen was injected at the tertiary feed level, served, all other things remaining. equal, to obtain a flame stabilization, distance equal to about four times the flame stabilization distance of a conventional injector without secondary atomizing gas stream. This longer stabilization distance serves to reduce the overheating of the atomization head 6. Furthermore, it has also been demonstrated that the flame obtained was more uniform and longer due to the delayed atomization, the spray 10 having a larger drop size than a spray issuing from a conventional burner while the modified spray 13 has a smaller drop size and; better diluted and hence more uniformly distributed
