ORIGINAL

Field of Invention :
This invention relates to invention of a machine by virtue of which Neurosurgical operative procedure for Intra ventricular Pathologies are made with extra safety and without hurting.
Background and prior art of the Invention :
Various types of lesions of 3rd or lateral ventricle of brain need surgical excision. One can enter the ventricles either through transcortical or transcallosal approach. Main problem lies in keeping this cortical or callosal incisions open. The prevalent methods are 1) using "Layla".self retaining brain retractor 2) keeping the incision open with suction canula and bipolar forceps or other instruments while using them for surgery. The 'Layla" retractor is very traumatic to brain as it is fixed to either a fixation devices attached to operation table or of the bony edge of craniotomy site. As the ventricle is opened and CSF is aspirated or tumour is excised the brain shifts and it is stretched against the retractor blade thus injuring it.,. ".
Another method where no retractor is used, it needs great deal of expertise
mastered by very few surgeons only.
It is an experience that, despite of various types of retractors available. there is difficulty in Neurosurgical operations and also injury to the patient in prevalent.
The necessity of overcoming the above difficulty of retractor blade injuring
the brain is well felt by the Neurosurgeons.
The objective of the present invention is to provide with a simple, safe and low cost device for Neurosurgical operative procedure for Intra ventricular Pathologies.


STATEMENT OF INVENTION
In accordance with the present invention, this device has provided a new assemblage of a latex balloon, plastic cylinders with a circular flange. This device i.e, Cylindrical Channel retractor is used for ventricular surgery in Brain.
' SUMMARY OF INVENTION .
In order to obviate the above mentioned draw backs of intra ventricular
operations. The present invention is a device capable of intra ventricular
"operations without hurting the brain. The device consisting of a Latex
balloon which is inflatable is capable of being mounted on a brain canula.
There are plastic cylinders-with a circular flange at outer end of the ballooh
This device keeps the incision open without any injury and provides; a safe
channel for surgery. As it is very light and moves with the brain it dies not
hurt it. It just keeps the incision open without exerting pressure, on
surrounding brain tissue. Intra third a lateral ventricular surgery requires
Transcallosal or transcortical incision and safe retraction device to keep it
open. This present device" serves the purposed much better as compared to
prevalent retraction system.
