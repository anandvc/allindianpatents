Title of Invention

"A POUCH FOR STORAGE AND DISPENSING OF LIQUIDS AND A PROCESS FOR MANUFACTURING OF THE SAME"
Abstract This invention relates to a pouch for storing and dispensing of liquids characterized in that the pouch is formed from a single sheet and to constitute a first fold (4) raised from a second (5) and third fold (6) the first fold (4) being in a direction opposite to said second (5) and third (6) folds, such folds providing a front and back side (2,9) being sealed to each other to the lower and of said pouch having inclined sides.

1. A pouch for storing and dispensing of liquids characterized in that the pouch is formed from a single sheet and to constitute a first fold (4) raised from a second (5) and third fold (6) the first fold (4) being in a direction opposite to said second (5) and third

(6) folds, such folds providing a front and back side (2,9) being sealed to each other to the lower and of said pouch having inclined sides.


2. A pouch as claimed in claim 1 having a front (7) gusset extending from the first fold (4) to the second fold (5) and a rear gusset (8) extending from the first fold (4) to the third fold (6).
