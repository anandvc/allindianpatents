Title of Invention

“Process for producing 3-chloro-N- (3-chloro-5-trifluoromethyl-2-pyridyl) -alpha, alpha, alpha-trifluoro-2, 6-dinitro-p-toluidine”
Abstract Because fluazinam is excellent as an active ingredient of pesticides and highly useful, it is desired to produce it efficiently in a proper form with simple operations at low cost in an environmentally friendly manner.
