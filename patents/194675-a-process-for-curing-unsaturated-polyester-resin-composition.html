Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR CURING UNSATURATED POLYESTER RESIN COMPOSITION
Abstract Novel peroxyoxalate compositions of Structure A, and use of the novel monoperoxyoxalates of Structure A as initiators for polymerizing ethylenically unsaturated monomers and for curing of unsaturated polyester resins are disclosed.
Full Text This invention relates to a curing unsaturated polyester resin composition.
