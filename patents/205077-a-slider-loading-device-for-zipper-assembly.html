This invention relates to a slider loading device for zipper assembly.
BACKGROUND
Normally, the sliders are loaded on zippers by hand. They can also be loaded with an escapement mechanism consisting of a slider holder, a threader or slider finger and a restraining finger. The slider holder is designed to be compatible with an assembly of sliders or individual slider. The channel of the slider holder includes an open top and open bottom. The slider holder is preferably designed to allow one slider at a time to be placed in a primary notch of the zipper. The zipper may be pushed or pulled through the escapement mechanism. The slider may be placed in the notches by gravity and driven mechanically or electro-mechanically and the zipper continues to move forward through the escapement mechanism.
The object of this invention is to provide a slider loading device for zipper assembly for loading the slider on stationary zipper assembly at any length of the zipper. The said loading device can be provided on Pouching Machine or on Form Fill and Seal Machine.
To achieve the said objectives, this invention provides a slider loading device

Referring to the drawings (1) shows the vertical magazine for storing and aligning the sliders, pneumatically operated plunger (2) is mounted close to the bottom of said vertical magazine (1) for pushing the slider at the bottom of said vertical magazine. The said plunger is operated by a pneumatic cylinder (3), which is mounted on the base plate (4) through a bracket (5). increases towards the end 6(b) for spreading the slider arms to load the slider on the zipper assembly easily. A pneumatically operated gripper (8) is mounted on a pneumatically operated rotary arm (9) for gripping the slider (S) on the rails (6) and rotating the slider (S) till the end of the rails for spreading the arms of the slider to facilitate the loading of me slider on to the zipper moving through a guide strip (10) exposed to the slider (S) at the end of the rail (6). The storing and aligning the sliders in the vertical magazine is carried out either by hand or by vibratory bowl feeder, shown in figure 2.
Vibratory bowl feeder (B) consists of a bowl having grooves (g) inside. These grooves (g) have width equal to the width of the slider shown in figures 3a, 3b. A track (T) facilitates transportation of slider (S) to the vertical magazine (1), shown in figure 1. When the vibratory bowl feeder vibrates, it accommodates sliders in the grooves (g) and pass on to the track (T) for feeding and aligning into vertical magazine (1).
The slider shown in figures 3 a and 3b has top (T) with curve arms (CS), backside (BS) and central arm (CA). The slider can be of different designs and shape.
WORKING:
Sliders (S) are stored in vertical magazine (1) by hand or by vibratory bowl feeder. The plunger (2) is pneumatically operated by the cylinder (3) and pushes the slider at the bottom of the vertical magazine (1) to the guide rails (6). The pneumatically operated gripper (8) grips the slider on to the rails (6) by actuation of rotary arm (9), the slider moves along the rails from beginning to end portion (6b) of rails (6) to spread the arms of the slider for facilitating the loading of the slider onto the zipper assembly at any length.
