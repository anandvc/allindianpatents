LEG SHIELD STRUCTURE OF MOTORCYCLE
Abstract To promote Heart radiation from an engine, particularly from a cylinder body and ro acquire airy-appearance as leg shield structure of a motorcycle. The present invention relates to the improvement of leg shield structure of a motorcycle.

While a vehicle is run, running wind enters inside a body cover via an opening provided between the right and left leg shields 38, 38, hits the engine E and cools it, and a part flows out of the body via the opening 48, howeever, as the opening 48 is located on the upside of the cylinder body, hot air in the cylinder body hardly flows our of the opening 48.
