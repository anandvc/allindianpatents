Title of Invention

INSECTICIDAL DIHYDROOXADIAZINE COMPOUNDS
Abstract Insecticidal dihydrooxadiazine compounds having the formula:(I) wherein, X is O,N, or S; R is a substituted or unsubstituted phenyl or C4-C5 heterocyclic group; R2,R3,R4 and R5 are one of the following: a) R2,R3 and R4 are, independently, hydrogen, alkyl, alkoxy, or furanyl; and R5 is nitro, cyano, alkyl, dialkylamino, alkylthio, alkoxy, haloalkoxy, phenoxy, phenylthio, or alkoxycarbonyl, wherein R3 and R5 together can form a ring: or b) R3, R4 and R5 are hydrogen; and R2 is alkylthio, alkylsulfinyl, alkylsulfonyl, nitroalkoxy, hydroxy, dialkoxyphosphinyl, cyano, acyloxy, alkoxy, haloalkoxy, cycloalkyl, or alkenyl; or R2 is a substituted or unsubstituted phenyl, phenylthio, phenylsulfinyl, phenylsulfonyl, pyrazolyl, furanyl, thienyl, phenylalkoxy or benzoyloxy; and R6 is hydrogen, alkyl, alkylthio, alkoxyalkyl, acyl, benzyl, or alkoxycarbonyl.
