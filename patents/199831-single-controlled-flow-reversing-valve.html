Title of Invention

"SINGLE-CONTROLLED FLOW REVERSING VALVE"
Abstract The present report relates to a single-controlled flow reversing valve and, more specifically, to a device to be adapted to tanks for water filtering, as well as other similar purposes
Full Text The present report relates to a single-controlled flow reversing valve and, more
specifically, to a device to be adapted to tanks for water filtering, as well as other
similar purposes
Some pieces of equipment are currently known, which make use of other technologies
for flow reversion, some of which are electronically or electrically controlled, and other
ones, mechanically Such equipment perform flow reversion horizontally, by means of
ball valves or gate valves In these cases, the operator must opmte several stopcocks
(mechanical device to open or close flow) to complete reversion. Such practice has been
proving to be disadvantageous and inconvenient, as it may induce the operator to
operate incorrectly, preventing the equipment from working well, and also, some of
these are very expensive
Thus, the object of the present innovation is to provide tanks for water filtering and
similar purposes with a specifically developed device, in order to offer the user the
possibility of operating flow variation by handling a single handle, which is external to
the module, reversing the flow by means of a shaft equipped with three duly spaced and
fixed cams, each one of those with a different profile, according to the flow reversion
order, which cams, when radially moved by the shaft connected to the handle, start
respective plungers, which open certain ways and close others, providing, thus, the
desired reversion
