Title of Invention

A SYSTEM FOR SELECTING AND CONFIRMING AN IMPULSE PURCHASE FOR PAY TELEVISION
Abstract System for selecting and confirming an impulse purchase for pay television comprising the presentation to a user, by means of a display device, of a gird of programmes, and the selection by this user of a programme of his particular choice form the grid by means know per se, this choice having to be confirmed in the system by an entilement management message (EMM), this message being specific to an impluse purchase, characterized in that the entitlement management message (EMM) specific to an impluse purchase is contained in the data (SI) tied to the presentation of the programme grid.
