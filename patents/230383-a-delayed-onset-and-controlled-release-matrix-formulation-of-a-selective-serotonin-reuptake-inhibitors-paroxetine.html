Title of Invention

A DELAYED-ONSET AND CONTROLLED RELEASE MATRIX FORMULATION OF A SELECTIVE SEROTONIN REUPTAKE INHIBITORS-PAROXETINE
Abstract Delayed-onset controlled release oral dosage from comprising paroxetine in a bioerodible reservoir device which uses combination of diffusion and dissolution matrix, having a core and a coat with outer film coat is disclosed. The coat comprising of an enteric polymer layer controls release rate of matrix device of the drug suitable for once-a-day or maximum twice-a-day administration.
