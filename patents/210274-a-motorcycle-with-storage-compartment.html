Title of Invention

A MOTORCYCLE WITH STORAGE COMPARTMENT
Abstract A motor cycle with storage compartment fixed to the space frame of the vehicle and located between the handle bar of the vehicle and the rider's seat; a hinged lid provided for the compartment, for opening and closing the compartment; a fuel tank disposed beneath the compartment and also fixed to the space frame; and an inlet for the fuel tank, with a cap for opening and closing the inlet.
Full Text

This invention relates to a motorcycle with storage compartment•
In the known motorcycle, articles are usually stored in boxes provided on one or both sides of the vehicle at the rear of the vehicle. This construction detracts from the comforts of riding, particularly, for the pillion rider to whom such construction is a hindrance.
Another kind of storage device provided for the known motorcycle is compactly integrated with the body of the vehicle, and less cumbrous; however, this device is limited to storing small articles.
None of the storage devices for the known motorcycle is
spacious enough to accommodate large size articles,
such as, a crash-helmet.
*
The known scooters, on the other hand, have adequate storage space provided under the seat, for storage of even large size articles such as crash-helmets, without marring aesthetic appeal or ergonomics. in some cases, this factor is believed to have made the customer-

choice of vehicle, between the motorcycle and scooter, tilt in favour of the latter, just because of storage space provisions being better in the case of the latter than in the case of the former -
Storage space has ofteh been one of the governihg considerations, for the customer, in choosing the scooter, instead of the motorcycle, despite the fact that persons opting for a scooter based on such considerations would otherwise have chosen the motorcycle for its proven stability, speed, highway riding qualities and comfort,
*
This invention, therefore, proposes a motorcycle which provides ample storage space and thus puts this vehicle on an equal footing with the scooter so far as storage facilities are concerned•
The motorcycle proposed herein is acceptable from the viewpoint of aesthetics or "looks" which are traditionally part of the vehicle. The storage compartment is also acceptable from the ergonomic standpoint.

In the motorcycle with storage compartment, according to this invention, the said compartment is fixed to the space frame of the vehicle and is located between the handle bar of the vehicle and the rider's seat; a hinged lid provided for the compartment, for opening and closing the compartment? a fuel tank disposed beneath the compartment and also fixed to the space frame; and an inlet for the fuel tank, with a cap for opening and closing the inlet.
