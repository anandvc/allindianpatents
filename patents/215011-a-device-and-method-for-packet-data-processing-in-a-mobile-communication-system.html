Title of Invention

A DEVICE AND METHOD FOR PACKET DATA PROCESSING IN A MOBILE COMMUNICATION SYSTEM
Abstract A packet data processing device, for maximizing throughput of packet data transmitted over forward channels for use in a base station in a code division multiple access (CDMA) mobile communication system for transmitting the packet data over the forward channels corresponding to each of a plurality of mobile stations, respectively, the device comprising: a channel status information receiver for receiving plural channel status information for the forward channels from each of the plurality of mobile stations; a supplemental channel transmission controller for allocating available maximum power in klthe lbase station based on a forward channel having a best channel condition among the forward channels corresponding to each of the plurality of mobile stations according to the channel status information and determining a bit rate of data based on the assigned maximum power; and a supplemental channel transmitter for transmitting packet data over the forward channel having the best channel condition at the determined rate.
