Title of Invention

SUBMERSIBLE WATER PUMP INSTALLED WITHOUT DELIVERY PIPE
Abstract A submersible water pump installed without delivery pipe wherein a bore is made in the ground and a water filter is placed at the bottom of the filter to filter the dust or sand particles. The pump is supported by the help of rope and hooks or by any other means. The water is then sent to the submerged pump set through the water inlet. The water comes out from the outlet of the pump. A rubber seal is attached to the side walls of the bore well pipe so that the water coming out from the pump does not go below and there is no downward leakage
Full Text BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
1. Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to submersible water pump installed without the delivery pipe wherein the water is passed through water filter having porous structure, which holds the dust and then is passed through submersible pump set wherein novei inventions include rubber seal, steel rope clamps, suspended rods, hooks, clamps, rubber pipe, cover plate
2. Description of the Related Art
In general, now the water is extracted from the ground by the procedure specified for installation of the pump by ISO 8034 A bore is made in the ground and a water filter is placed at the bottom of the filter, to filter the dust or sand particles. The water then comes to the submerged pump set through the water inlet. The water comes out from the outlet of the pump from where it is fed to the delivery pipe. The well is then covered by a cover plate. The pipe and the pumpset is hanged with the help of clamps This method is cumbersome, expensive and less efficient.
