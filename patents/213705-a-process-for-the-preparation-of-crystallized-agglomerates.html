Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF CRYSTALLIZED AGGLOMERATES
Abstract The present invention describes novel agglomerates in crystalline form of &#946; -lactam compounds. Furthermore, a process for the preparation of said agglomerates, wherein a solution or suspension of at least one &#946; -lactam compound in a solvent is mixed with one or more anti-solvents has been described.
