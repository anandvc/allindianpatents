Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED DETACHABLE WASH BASIN
Abstract An improved detachable washbasin comprising a wall bracket with projected arms at each end perpendicular to the wall and a shaped basin with flat surface towards the wall with slotted grooves at each end to match with the projections of the wall bracket which snugly enter into the slotted grooves of the basin, the basin having a sloped floor towards the wall and provided with a drain hole at the middle and bottom portion of the flat surface of the basin, and the wall bracket is fixed to the wall with screw means ,a folding plat form provided at one side of the basin to keep toiletry and a drain pipe is fitted through the wall connecting the drain hole. An improved detachable wash basin as claimed in claim 1, wherein a silicone gasket is provided between the wall and the wall bracket, An improved detachable wash basin as claimed in claim 1, wherein a lock is provided on the wall for locking the folding plat form to the wall, An improved detachable wash basin as claimed in claim 1, wherein a cold water knob and hot water knob and a flexible mixing tap are provided for water supply, An improved detachable wash basin substantially described as herein with reference to the figures of accompanying drawings.

Title - An improved detachable washbasin with bracket Field:
The present invention relates an improved detachable hand washing basin assembly to provide relatively portable device to facilitated hand washing Back ground and prior art:-
The use of hand washing basin is known in the prior art USPT No. 3722007, describes group of washing station that is connected to an out side water supply, USPT No. 4698860 describes about a multi person setup and soap dispensers incorporated into spray head,

pumping,
USPTO No. 5687434 describes a portable stand alone sinks that utilizes a two tank system for dispensing fresh water and collecting waste water.
While there device fulfill their respective particular objection and requirements, the needs remain for facilitating portable detachable wash basin.
Objects:-
The primary object of the present invention is that the detachable wash which is convenient for removal and wash the basin, and to clean the water line,
Further object of the present invention is that it doesn't requires that all attachment of the wash basin mainly the water connecting pipes are not visible which is to be connected through wall , it would not require pipeline attachments at the bottom of basin , due to which dirt accumulation around tap and in the pipeline can be avoided
Further important object of the present invention is that, it doesn't require a plat form for installation as is normally required for circular washbasin,
Further important object of the present invention is that, washbasin can be removed form the stand very easily for plumbing and maintenance.
Further very important object of the present invention is that it gives more aesthetic look for the area where washbasin is installed since no piping is seen outside,
and the cost of the installing the novel wash basin is lower that of the conventional wash basin,

First time in the World A Novel Concept in Washbasin.
overview
Normally after purchasing a goor attractive washbasin with pleasant looks and
colour one finds that all attachment of the wash basin mainly the piping is done in ar
unscientific way and leads to more usage of space around the wash basin and also
it aids the accumulation of dirt around the taps.To overcome such
problems new Novel Washbasin has been developed.

Ø Does not require a platform for installation as is normally

Ø gives more aesthetic look for the area where washbasin is installed since no piping is seen outside.

conventional washbasins.
