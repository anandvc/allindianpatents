Title of Invention

A METHOD FOR PRODUCING A CARBON ELEMENT.
Abstract Method for producing a carbon element having a honeycomb-shaped structure, using a resin-impregnated base body with a honeycomb-shaped structure that is made from paper or fleece, which is first paralyzed and then stabilized and/or compressed, characterized in that the stabilized honeycomb-shaped element is coated with a carbon-containing solution and then again paralyzed.
