Title of Invention

A PLAYBACK APPARATUS AND METHOD FOR PLAYING BACK A STREAM SEQUENCE
Abstract The invention relates to a playback apparatus for playing back a stream sequence, comprising a playback device; a package manager operable to, when storage locations for storing digital stream files which constitute the stream sequence are on separate recording media including a rewritable recording medium, set a digital stream file recorded on the rewritable recording medium to an enable status of being recognizable by the playback device or to a disable status of being unrecognizable by the playback device; and an application executor operable to execute an application to control access to any of the recording media, wherein the playback device starts playing back the stream sequence if at least one of the digital stream files is in the enable status, while one of the at least one of the digital stream files is being played back by the playback device, the package manager changes, a remaining digital stream file recorded on the rewritable recording medium, which is set to the disable status, to the enable status or a remaining digital stream file recorded on the rewritable recording medium, which is set to the enable status and is not being played back, to the disable status, according to a request from the application executor, the change to the enable status by the package manager includes changing an attribute of the remaining digital stream file recorded on the rewritable recording medium to a read-only attribute, and the change to the disable status by the package manager includes changing the attribute of the remaining digital stream file recorded on the rewritable recording medium to a rewritable attribute.

Background Art
A Virtual Package is a technique of constructing a virtual package by dynamically combining a digital stream
recorded on a read-only recording medium such as a BD-ROM
and a digital stream recorded on a rewritable recording medium
such as a hard disk, in order to develop the contents of the
read-only recording medium. Suppose the digital stream

stream recorded on the hard disk constitutes a sequel to the
movie work. This being the case, by constructing the above
Virtual Package, the movie work recorded on the BD-ROM and
the sequel recorded on the hard disk can be treated as one

Disclosure of the Invention
Problems the Invention is going to Solve

However, when playing back the digital stream recorded
on the BD-ROM and the digital stream recorded on the hard
disk as one stream sequence, there may be a case where an
unstable digital stream is contained in the stream sequence.
The unstable digital stream referred to here is such a digital
stream that has not yet been downloaded to the hard disk or
that is incompletely written to the hard disk. When playing
back the stream sequence despite the existence of such an
unstable digital stream, the playback may become unstable
or halt once a point of playback by playback means has reached
the unstable digital stream.

If playback means (such as a decoder) in a playback
apparatus is capable of checking whether a stream to be played
back is unstable or not, the above playback instability or
halt can be avoided. However, the playback means needs to
perform an operation of outputting a video signal in a unit
of a frame period of 1/24 second or 1/29.94 second, and so
is unlikely to have an enough capacity left to conduct such
check. Accordingly, the above playback instability and halt
are inevitable. In a consumer product industry for
manufacturing BD-ROM playback apparatuses, however, a strict
quality standard is required for video playback. Therefore,
even if the playback means does not have a capacity to check

digital streams, the above playback instability and halt cannot be tolerated.
The present invention aims to provide a playback apparatus that can
prevent playback instability and halt when playing back a digital stream recorded
on a BD-ROM and a digital stream recorded on a hard disk as one stream
sequence, even if playback means does not have an available capacity to check
an unstable digital stream.
Means of Solving the Problems
The stated aim can be achieved by a playback apparatus for playing back
a stream sequence, including: a playback unit; a package management unit
operable to, when storage locations for storing digital streams which constitute
the stream sequence are on separate recording media, set each digital stream to
an enable status of being recognizable by the playback unit or a disable status of
being unrecognizable by the playback unit; and an application execution unit
operable to execute an application to control access to any of the recording
media, wherein the playback unit starts playing back the stream sequence if at
least one of the digital streams is in the enable status, and while one of the at
least one of the digital streams is being played back by the playback unit, the
package management unit changes a remaining digital stream, which is set to
the disable status, to the enable status, or a remaining digital stream, which is
set to the enable status and is not being played back, to the disable status,
according to a request from the application execution unit.
Effects of the Invention
According to this construction, each digital stream is switched between the

enable status and the disable status according to the application, thereby
determining whether the digital stream is to be played back by the playback unit.
The application that realizes access to the recording medium has this right to
determine whether the digital stream is to be played back by the playback unit.
Accordingly, in the case where it cannot be confirmed that the digital stream has
reliably been written to the recording medium, the application sets the digital
stream to the disable status, so that the digital stream is treated as nonexistent.
As a result, the playback unit will not erroneously play back an unstable digital
stream. Hence unstable or intermittent video playback can be prevented.
