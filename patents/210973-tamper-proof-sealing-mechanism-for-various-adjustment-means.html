"Portable Tobacco & Lime Paste Storer Cum Charge Preparator" is a device which stores 35CC Volume of dry tobacco of any brand and 05 CC Volume of lime paste and also mixes the tobacco and lime paste uniformly. It is very handy and pocket size device made from plastic.
Generally this invented device stores tobacco and lime paste in modern style with regulation and charge preparation facility than the existing device which stores tobacco and lime paste in old style. There is no technical back up in existing device.
However the conventional methods have many disadvantages over the present invention. Some of them are mentioned below :-
In the conventional device tobacco is to be taken manually by opening cover on plan.
In the existing device lime paste have to taken by opening the cover. Manually by means finger.
In the existing device mixing of tobacco and lime to be made on palm open to atmosphere by means of fingers which causes irritation to skin of palm and dirtiness of palm.

|In existing device humidification of tobacco and dehydration of lime paste takes place while storage.
There is pollution & nuisance while preparing the mixture of tobacco in conventional device.
The present invention has its principal objection to develop a charge preparatory, which will overcome the drawbacks of existing device.
The first objective of the present device is to stores tobacco and lime paste in modern style with regulation and charge preparation facility.
The other objective is to facilitate to inject lime paste inside the chamber in required quantity just by pressing at press plate.
The present device should reduces humidification of tobacco and dehydration of lime paste while storage and use.
The other objective that it should be Environmental friendly, no pollution or nuisance while preparing the mixture of tobacco and lime paste as it takes place inside charge preparatory chamber.
The newly developed device charges tobacco automatically inside the charge preparator chamber. Besides storage of dry tobacco and lime paste the device facilitates to prepare mixture of tobacco and lime paste in desired strength. It is very simple in operation. Light weight, handy and pocket sized.

Raw materials for this device are plastic granuals of desired quality, synthetic rubber sheet of 0.5 mm thickness with high grade of elasticity and adhesive.
DETAIL DESCRIPTION ; -
Portable Tobacco & Lime Paste Storer Cum Charge Preparator has primarily three sections:
One is the section of 35 CC Volume stores dry tobacco of any brand with sliding damper facility to charge dry tobacco in charge preparator chamber. It charges in charge preparation chamber by dual combination of technique i.e. shocks caused due to body movement and gravitational force.
And it can be charged to meet immediate / frequent requirement just by shaking the device in hand.:
Second is the section of 05CC volume lime paste store which is provided with such a cover which facilitates to supply desired quantity of lime paste to charge preparator just by pressing the cover. The cover is a subassembly of 0.5 mm thick rubber sheet adhere to frame and press plate. Third section called as charge preparator. It is provided with manual rotatable blades mounted on (inside) of hinged cover to mix tobacco and lime paste uniformly.
Blades are rotated clockwise or counter clockwise by means of knob cum blade holder provided on outer side of cover. On achieving the desired mixture, it can be directly discharge on palm.

Process of using the device :
Load the tobacco in tobacco storage chamber and put the self locking sliding cover (4), keep close sliding damper (2).
Load the lime paste in lime paste storage chamber and put the sliding cover (subassembly (5,6,7).
Open the sliding damper (2), and charge the tobacco in charge preparator chamber by means of duel mechanism provided in device or just by shaking the device.
Now open the charge prepartor chamber cover to ensure quantity of dry tobacco and then press at press plate provided upon lime paste storage to take required quantity of paste lime in charge preparator chamber.
Close the charge preparator chamber cover and start rotating blades by means of knob clockwise/counterclockwise to mix tobacco and lime uniformly.

At last close the charge preparator chambers cover and keep open the sliding to get next charge of dry tobacco ready in charge preparator chamber.
Entire process takes maximum 2 or 3 minute.

It charges dry tobacco i.e. 05 CC by volume in charge preparator chamber and
