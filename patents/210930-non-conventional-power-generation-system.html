Title of Invention

NON-CONVENTIONAL POWER GENERATION SYSTEM
Abstract The non-conventional power generation system comprising a thick wall steel roller (1) made by machining and and having curling at both ends for fitting end covers (2), the said roller being supported by a shaft (3) passing through it, support ribs (4) being provided to the said shaft from inside the steel roller at fixed interval to facilitate support to the said roller and the roller and said shaft revolve together, ball bearings (6) being provided to support the said roller and the said ball bearings are fixed in a housing (5), gears (8) being fitted at the extreme ends of the said shaft, two separate generators (9) being used at both ends of the shaft for generation of power, pinions (7) being attached to the generators, these pinions are matched with the gears attached to the shaft and the gears drive the pinions attached to generators, when the vehicle passes over the roller the contact between the steel roller and the tyre creates friction and due to friction the steel roller starts rotating which further rotates the gears attached to the shaft, since the gear starts rotating, it rotates the pinion attached to the generator and thus generates power.

Statement of the invention:
Therefore, a non-conventional power generation system comprising of a Steel Roller, which is developed by a process of machining and curling at both ends of Steel Rod for fitting end covers; the steel roller is supported by a Shaft through it; this shaft is fitted with support ribs to facilitate support to the steel roller; the support ribs are provided to the shaft from inside the steel roller at fixed intervals; gears are fixed on both sides of shaft and at the extreme end to drive the pinions; these pinions are part of generators and are matched with the gears attached to the shaft; two separate generators are used at both ends for the purpose of generating power; bearings are fixed in the housing on both sides of the steel roller; this housing provides complete support for mounting the whole assembly and accordingly completes the construction of power generation system; when the vehicle passes over the steel roller, the contact between the tyre and the steel roller generates friction; due to friction the steel roller starts rotating which further rotates the gears attached to the shaft; since the gears start rotating, it rotates pinions and thereby activates generators; these generators starts rotating and subsequently generates power.
Gear ration between roller and motor generator is so maintained that if roller makes one turn, motor generator will rotate three turns. The said housing is constructed in a way to provide support for mounting the whole assembly. The outer surface of roller pipe is machined with diamond knurling finish, so as to facilitate maximum friction between the vehicle tyre and the roller.
