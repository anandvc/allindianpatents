Title of Invention

PRODUCT FROM LIGNOCELLULOSIC WASTE FOR THE REMEDIATION OF WATER CONTAMINATED WITH HEAVY METALS
Abstract This invention relates the development of product from lignocellulosic agrowaste from wheat operation for the effective removal of heavy metals such as Pb<SUP>2+</SUP>, Cd<SUP>2+</SUP>, Hg<SUP>+2</SUP>, Co<SUP>2+</SUP>, Ni<SUP>2+</SUP>, Mn<SUP>2+</SUP>, Zn<SUP>2+</SUP>, CU<SUP>2+</SUP> and detoxification of Cr(VI), and their removal mechanisms. This has an edge to other conventional methods in virtue of its large availability, very low cost and easy operational process.
