Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR PREPARING AN ECTEINASCHIDIN COMPOUND"
Abstract The present invention relates to a process for preparing an ecteinascidin compounds, particularly ecteinascidin compound (ET-743). The present invention also relates to novel intermediates compounds which are used in process for preparing an ecteinascidin compound (ET-743).
