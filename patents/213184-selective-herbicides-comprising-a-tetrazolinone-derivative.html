Title of Invention

SELECTIVE HERBICIDES COMPRISING A TETRAZOLINONE DERIVATIVE
Abstract The invention relates to novel herbicidal synergistic active compound combinations comprising a known tetrazolinone derivative andsnown herbicidally active compounds and/or safeners, which compositions can be used with particularly good results for the selective control of weeds in various crops of usefol plants.
