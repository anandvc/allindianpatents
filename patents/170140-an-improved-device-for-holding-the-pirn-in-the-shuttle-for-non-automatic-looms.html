Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED DEVICE FOR HOLDING THE PIRN IN THE SHUTTLE FOR NON AUTOMATIC LOOMS
Abstract An improved device for holding pirn in the shuttle with perfect alignment of the pirn tip with respect to shuttle eye in a non-automatic 1ooms comprising a tongue free to oscillate about X-X axis around the non-metallic peg and a jaw having spring action and gropves in the inner face to grip the pirn head having rings equally spaced similar to said grooves, the said pirn boding further provided with a blind hole in which tongue is inserted while inserting the pirn in the shuttle. Device as claimed in claim 1 wherein the said peg is made of wood/Nylon, Device as; claimed in claim 1 wherein the said jaw is held by jaw pin, jaw cov|er and, a jaw cover pin. Device as claimed in claim 1 wherein the shuttle is made of natural/artificial wood, Nylon either a lone or in combination in a predetermined ratio. Device as claimed in claim 1 wherein the pirn & shuttle has predetermined increased length to reduce the number of stoppages due to shuttle changes and thereby increase product iv i ty.
