Background of the invention:
The invention pertains to the field of solar water heater, more particularly to a high efficient water heater. Precisely this invention relates to a high efficient, compact solar water heater with interconnected fins.
The main object of the invention is to design a novel Solar water heater which is unique in constrcution The novelty designed water heater wilt be cheaper as compared to Sotar water heaters known in the art.
Further one of the design parameter of the solar water heater is such that the factor namely even when there is sudden evacuation of water from the storage tank, it will not shrink and hence the draw back of shrinkage when there is a vacuum in the storage tank is eliminated.
The present invention relates to solar water heater, and more particularly, by incorporating high efficient heat exchange made of with inter connected fins in a novel manner to get high efficiency and to make a compact system. Since the interconnected fins are incorporated around the stainless tubes, these water can be used for cooking and drinking. The use of the existing solar water heater is to heat water for bathing and/or for commercial use, but, it cannot be used for cooking and drinking. The main object of this invention is to use this invention for multipurpose including cooking and drinking. And the present invention is very compact and economical and it can be installed even on flats etc. The present solar water heater available is bigger in size, so it cannot be installed on flats and it is costlier also.


Now the inventor has developed a novel solar water heater. These novel solar water heater will heat the water speedily within a short span of time.
The object of this invention is to heat the water for multipurpose including drinking and cooking purpose.
Another object of this invention is thai it is compact and can be installed on flats.
Another object of this invention is to overcome the draw back of shrinkage of water tank
when there is vacuum in the tank.
Another main object of this invention is to provide a very cost effective solar water heater.
Summary of this invention:
The main object of the invention is to provide very economical compact solar water heater, for multipurpose including drinking and cooking.
According to this invention there is provided a solar water heater comprising of plurality of heat exchanger with interconnected metallic fins having contact with metallic pipe provided for water flow, wherein metallic pipe being surrounded by the said interconnected fins; an inside insulated outer case closed with transparent glass on the top; a vertical storage tank with insulation in the outer side, water inlet and outlet, water suction and discharge header, and gazer, the heat exchanger is transposed into an inside insulated outer case, the top of the outer case is covered with transparent glass supported by retainers; collector is connected with vertical storage tank wherein water inlet and out 1^8 Bre provided.
