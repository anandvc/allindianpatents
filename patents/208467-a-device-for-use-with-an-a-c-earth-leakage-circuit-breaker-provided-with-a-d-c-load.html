This Invention relates to a device for use with an a-c earth leakage circuit breaker provided with a d.c. load.
Circuit breakers which are used for a.c. loads for tripping the circuit and cutting off power supply, whenever an earth leakage-current occurs, as a result of a fault, including human electrocution taking place, are usually termed ELCB (earth leakage circuit breaker) or RCCB (residual current circuit breaker)• The ELCB or RCCB type of circuit breakers are highly sensitive and react rapidly to trip the circuit whenever current— leakage occurs either in the a.c. load or In the power supply 1Ines and thus in cases of human beings being subject to electric shock 1ives are saved by the sensitivity and rapidi ty in action of these circuit breakers-
The circuit breakers, ELCB or RCCB type, however, have this drawback, namely, that they do not react to a d.c current-leakage fault occurring tn a d-c. load connected to an a.c. power supply through a rectifier. For instance, a television set which requires a d.c- power supply for operation has a

built-in rectifier which is connected to an a.c. power supply. Now, if the known ELCB or RCCB is connected in the power mupply circuit, while it will operate in the event of a.c- leakage occurring, it will not react in the event of . d.c. leakage occurring at the d.c, load as, for instance, in the case of some one being electrocuted by d.c. This is so because the known ELCB or RCCB, by very nature of its construction, i« unable to react to d.c leakage-

in this specification is intended to mean and include circuit breakers of the ELCB or RCCB type refferred to above and other like circuit breakers-

In the known circult breaker, occurrence of a.c imbalance (corresponding to a.c. earth leakage) is sensed by the CBT and this results in the tripping of the power supply.
