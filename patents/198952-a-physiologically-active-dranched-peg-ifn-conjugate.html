Title of Invention

A PHYSIOLOGICALLY ACTIVE DRANCHED PEG-IFN CONJUGATE
Abstract A physiologically active branched PEG-IFN conjugate having the formula wherein Rand R' are independently lower alkyl; X is NH or 0; n and n' are integers having a sum of from 600 to 1500; and the 0 average molecular weight of the polyethylene glycol units in said conjugate is from about 26,000 daltons to about 66,000 daltons.
