Title of Invention

CONICALLY BORED HUB FOR BEARING OF A SCROLL MEMBER
Abstract A scroll member comprising: a base plate having a first side and a second side, a spiral wrap extending from said first side of base plate, and a hub extending from said second side of said base plate, said hub defining a bore, characterised in that said bore having a first diameter located adjacent said base plate and a second diameter located distal from said base plate, said first diameter being greater than said second diameter.
