Title of Invention

A GREEN GLASS COMPOSITION
Abstract Accordingly the present invention relates to a green glass composition which contains at least 14.5% by weight Na2O, at least 10.5% by weight CaO and at least 0.5% by weight total iron (measured as Fe<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>), the glass sheet being substantially magnesium-free, and having a ferrous value of at least 30% and a performance (light transmission minus direct solar heat transmission) of at least 28% at least one thickness of from 2.8 mm to 5 mm.
