Title of Invention

DOOR BOLT SYSTEM
Abstract A Product of " Special Door Bolt" with or without a built in door handle, with a specially lathed door bolt rod which is provided with a central ridge by a known method, which works as a controller of the sliding movement of the special door bolt within the guiding rings or tubular pieces, specially made guide plates as per view sheets that are welded or otherwise attached or fastened, at strategic places, to the encasing specially bent up sheet to encase the door bolt mechanism, whereas the special door bolt rod is provided with an end plate at the locking end, which is specially designed to suit the assembly of the special door bolt and wherein the plate is provided with a hole to match with another plate with a hole which is specially designed with a built in door handle and welded or riveted or otherwise fastened at the end of the encasing specially bent up sheet wherein the end plate of the special door bolt rod which is welded or otherwise fastened to the end following a strategic assembly system wherein the design process of the special door bolt is done merging the design process of the door handle and the basic purposes of the door bolt and door handle is achieved with a single Product that is called " Special Door Bolts "
Full Text

This Invention relates to a " Special Door Bolt System M, for use in the field of Hard Ware Fixtures to cater to provide a Specialty Door Bolt with many specialty features like the door bolt is provided with a built in door handle which reduces the number of visual parts of the regular door bolts reduces the number of Screws and Nut-Bolts and encases the nut bolts with a specially bent up sheet which hides even the nut bolts and their heads thereby concealing all nut bolts and improves the aesthetics of the door as a whole.
♦ The Specialty Feature of this Special door bolt system over the regular door bolt system wherein the door bolt slides along a guiding catchers and bolted with a separate Catcher at the bottom of the regular door bolt and facilitating locking with a separate Pad Lock. Where as this Specialty door bolt avoids provision of a separate Catcher at the bottom and the locking arrangement is achieved with a monolithic piece along with the door handle Plate thereby facilitating the sliding bolt which is provided with a piece of Plate with a whole arrangement that slides and bolted at the end of the door bolt itself and thereby facilitating locking arrangement with the help of a Pad Lock.
♦ The Special door bolt system provides a total Casing with a specially bent up sheet which encases the total nut bolts and special design is provided behind the sheet casing and sliding arrangement for the door bolt is achieved with the help of guides provided along with casing sheet from behind and the sliding door bolt rod, which is specially designed to serve the technical needs of the door bolt system.
The present Invention of "Special Door Bolt "is an improved Product over the present practice, in use or being followed relating to Hardware Fixtures of the doors.
(i) This Invention caters the present day needs of Society in a better manner over the present day practice relating to door bolts. Firstly, the present day professionals in this line, did not properly attend the Area of Making the door bolts with proper design Standards to cater to the Public,. The present Invention takes care of the actual Consumer to folly cater to his total requirements. The Consumer of the Conventional door bolt system is highly unhappy and feels unaesthetic Conditions in his dwelling or Office. This Special Door Bolt System provides a Comprehensive Technical Design which has many technical features like concealing the nut bolts and the total mechanism is given a different fa?ade by hiding the guides of the door bolt and provision of locking arrangement at the end of the sliding door bolt itself and the sliding door bolt rod is specially designed to cater to the technical needs and the assembly of the total door bolt is done following a specific strategy of assembly of different parts or components of the Special Door Bolt System. The specially bent up sheet is provided with multiple features consisting a specially designed Plate attached at the end of the concealed sheet to facilitate locking with a pad lock and having a door handle thereby reducing no of visual items on the door fa9ade. The regular door bolt system is a set of components of the door bolt which shall be assembled by the Carpenter where as the present special door bolt is a pre assembled total door bolt system wherein the marking of the drill holes for fixing the nut bolts is provided with a marking card and the drill holes are marked with the help of the hole marking card and the nut bolts are inserted at once and fixing of the special door bolt is done with ease. Hence, we require a highly efficient Product, over the conventional door bolt and shall also be cost-effective when compared with traditional or conventional type Door Bolts for use in Hardware Fixtures. The Present Invention is made Possible by using high-technical Skills in designing the final New Product.

(XIII) THE DIFFERENT COMPONENTS OF THE "SPECIAL DOOR BOLT" ARE AS FOLLOWS WHICH ARE APPLICABLE IN CASE OF A METAL DOOR BOLT:-
1) A Specially lathed Door Bolt Rod that slides in between the Tubular or Ring Guides or Plate Guides, with a Central Ridge that is created by lathing Or otherwise provided using a known Technique.
2) Tubular Pieces having a diameter slightly higher than the diameter of the Special Door Bolt Rod Or Rings with required strength Or Guide Plates that are to be inserted on to the Special door bolt rod that are fixed at Strategic places within the Semi-Circular Bend of the encasing specially bent up sheet that is designed to encase the mechanism of special door bolt system and the guides are welded to the encasing specially bent up sheet.
3) Specially Bent Up Sheet which is designed to cater to conceal guides nut bolt heads bent up as per the View Sheets having Cut-Slots behind to assist fixing of the Special Door Bolt to the Door wherein the sheet may be decorated with carving designs by hot pressing the Sheet or otherwise decorated by known means Or bent in a specially designed ways to achieve better aesthetics of the Special Door Bolt.
4) End Plate, that slides along with the Special Door Bolt Rod, which is designed to suit the other End Plate with or without a Built-in Door Handle which is fixed at the Locking End of the Encasing Specially Bent Up Sheet wherein the End plate is designed, to facilitate Locking with a Pad-Lock and as per the View Sheets and inserted and fixed to the Special Door Bolt Rod at the locking End which is specially lathed to suit to the End Plate.
5) A Stationary End Plate that is welded or riveted or otherwise fastened to the end of the Specially Bent Up encasing Sheet with a Built In Handle to handle the door and which allows a Locking Facility using a Regular Pad Lock, as per the attached View-Sheets.

6) Catching End Piece, with or without a built in door handle to cater to Double doors, which allows bolting the door by allowing the Special Door Bolt, get inserted into the Catching End Piece Guides, when pushed at the locking end using the end plate to complete bolting the door, which is fixed to the other door in case of Double Door and which is provided with Specially Bent up Sheet having tubular or Ring guide or guides Or Guide Plates provided into the semicircular Bend of the specially bent up sheet, Or at strategic place or places as per the View Sheets.
