Title of Invention

GLYCINE-SUBSTITUTED THIENO [2,3-d]PYRIMIDINES WITH COMBINED LH AND FSH AGONISTIC ACTIVITY
Abstract The present invention resides in glycine substituted thieno[2,3-d]pyrimidine derivatives according to general Formula I, (Formula I) or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof, as claimed in claims and a pharmaceutical composition comprising the same. The compounds of the invention have LH as well as Fsh receptor activating activity and can be used in fertility regulating therapies.
