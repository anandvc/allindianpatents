FIELD OF THE INVENTION
This invention relates on an improved vacuum/thermal container. More particularly this invention relates to a vacuum container for storing food products or the like, under the airless or vacuum condition to keep the food healthy and safe and preserving its taste for 5 a longer period.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
The hygroscopic food products like coffee, tea etc, stored in a normal container are prone to absorb moisture from the air when exposed to atmosphere, resulting into growth to 10 fungus on the food product thereby spoiling the product making it totally unsuitable for consumption.
The food product like crisp fried snacks such as wafers, banana-chips, namkins, papads, chakali etc. And dry baked or roasted food products such as biscuits, non-khatais,
15 roasted papads, cheeselings etc. dry fruits like cashewnuts, almonds, pistas, walnuts etc. powdered food products like flours, spices like chillies, turmeric and other masalas, processed food products like baby foods, milk powders, malted health drinks, sweets, doughs and some cooked foods when stored in normal containers get exposed to atmospheric air and lose their crispness and other inharent qualities in a short period
20 thereby making them tasteless as well as deteriorating their quality.
To overcome these problems and drawbacks of a normal container for storing food products, some vacuum containers have been developed.
25 The existing vacuum container known in the prior art are generally made of metals and fitted with mortised vacuum pumps. These vacuum containers being cumbersome and costly could not be popularized for domestic use. Some vacuum containers, known as vacuumizers, are made in plastics but the construction of these containers also is very complecated and cumbersome.

5 Another object of this invention is to provide an improved vacuum/thermal container which permits an easy and instant opening of the lid, for adding or taking out any product stored therein.
A further object of this invention is to provide an improved vacuum/thermal container 10 wherein a filtering means is provided to prevent even a very fine particle of the food product coming out with the air during vaccumization.
A further object of this invention is to provide improved vacuum /thermal container which is cheaper in initial cost, has no cost for use, no maintainance and is easy to clean and 15 store.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE PREFERRED EMBODIMENTS
Although following examples shows the operation of specific embodiments, many modifications and variations will readily occur to those skilled in the art, accordingly it is not intended to limit the scope of the invention. 5
Referring to fig. 1 to 3, the improved vacuum container (1) according to an embodiment of this invention comprises a hollow body (2), open at top end (3) and adopted to be closed by a lid (4) in leak proof manner. The body (2) in preferably, a jar or bowl shaped made of food grade plastic material selected by taking into consideration properties like
10 durability, working feasibility and aesthetics. The open top end (3) of the body (2) is provided with a shoulder (5) which is preferably tapering outwardly to provide strength in a substantially flat seat (6) with a raised collar (7). The inner surface of the open open top end (3) is kept straight and smooth. The lid (4) at its perephery is provided with an inner collar (8) depending downwardly from the lower top surface (9) and fitting
15 smoothly but tightly at the top open end (3) of body (2). A peripheral groove (10) for accommodating therein an "0" ring seal (11) is provided outside of the said inner collar (8). The groove (10) is in alignment and exactly matching with the collar (7) of the body. A preferably flat seat (12) is provided at the outer periphery of the groove, matching with the said seat (6) of the body. The arrangement provides strong airtight sealing means
20 for closing the lid on the body in a leak proof manner and sealing the same to maintain the vacuum. A vacuum/suction pump (13) consisting of a piston (14) having a handle (15) for operating manually is provided in the lid (4) in the space between upper top surface (16) and lower surface (9) of the lid. The piston (14) is provided with longitudinal slot (17) for evacuating out the air from the container. The lower surface (9) is provided
25 with the opening (18) which is covered by a filter (19) kept is placed with the help of a cap (20). A valve (21) with push button (22) is provided in top surface (16) of the lid (4) which when pressed release the vacuum hereby allowing the lid (4) to be removed from the body (1) preferably silicon rubber ring are used at the meeting junction of the body and lid. Rubber washer to withstand the vacuum pressure as well the atmospheric
30 pressure are used at the junction of piston and lid around the pussed of the vacuum release valve. The manual pumping according to this invention makes the vacuum container compact, easy to manufacture and use at the same time vacuum is created quickly. The valve with push button gives easy and instant release of vacuum and
