Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED CAMLOCK WITH REVERSIBLE KEY
Abstract A camlock / budget lock with reversible key comprises a housing (9) to accomdate a cylinder {3) having a key way slot rotatably mounted in the housing (9) said housing & cylinder have plurality of radial matching holes to accommodate operating pins (4,5,6,17,19); driver pins (7, 18) connected through individual operating baising means to operate said pins, by means of a reversible key (1) ; said reversible key having circular / oval indentation on either side to operate the lock through the key way; in the locking position the operating pins & driver pins bridge the radial hole of housing & cylinder; in the unlocking position the operating pins & driver pins are pushed away from bridging line thus allowing the cylinder to turn by means of reversible key thereby rotating a cam / bolt mounted on cylinder from locking position to unlocking position & vice versa; a key slot provided in the cylinder is slightly deeper than the cylinder face to accommodate key shoulder of key for permitting more torque during operation which prevent twisting of key blade. The requirement of more torque is expected during misalignments which commonly occur during fitment.

AN IMPROVED CAMLOCK WITH REVERSIBLE KEY
This invention relates to door lock and particularly lock of budget lock / cam lock type wherein the locking and unlocking operation is performed by reversible key of varied non-interchangeable combinations which swings a pivoted tongue by 90 / 18 0° into a slot in the locking plate to serve as deadbolt. Heretofore, it was known to use the one sided key for the same operation with limited non-interchangeable combination which makes the key insertion difficult especially in the dark / areas not easily accesible. This has made the inventor to invent the said improved camlock to make the key insertion easier and enhance the security level of the application.
Now there is provided by a present invention, a reversible key, there is also provided an arrangement to change the locking cam position through 90° to take care of the different requirements, such as left hand and right hand opening of the door. Therefore, the object of the invention is to provide reversible key for easier insertion of the key even in the dark / not easily accessible areas and enhance the security level of the application.
Another object of the invention is to provide an arrangement to change the position of locking cam through 90° to take care of the different requirements. Such as left hand and right hand opening of the door / clockwise or anti clockwise movements of bolt.

It is another object of the invention is to provide improved budget lock / camlock with double row of pin tumbler mechanism to enhance the security level by increasing number of non -interhangeable key combinations.
It is still further object of the invention to make improved budget lock / camlock system of smaller size , to increase the versatility of application.
It is still further object of the invention to make an improved budget lock / camlock, which is easier for mounting.
Still further object of the invention to make the camlock capable of master key arrangement of more levels and more variants.
Another obj ect of the invention is to provide the lock as aforesaid which is safe and Practical in use and yet easily fabricated and inexpensive construction while being aesthetically acceptable when door mounted.

WE CLAIM:
1. A camlock with reversible key comprises a housing (9) to accommodate a cylinder (3) having a key way slot rotatably mounted in the housing (9) said housing & cylinder have plurality of radial matching holes to accommodate operating pins (4,5,6,17.19); driver pins (7,18) connected through individual operating biasing means to operate said pins, by means of a reversible key (1); said reversible key having circular/ oval indentation on either side to operate the lock through the key way; in the locking position the operating pins & driver pins bridge the radial hole of housing & cylinder; in the unlocking position the operating pins & driver pins are pushed away from bridging line thus allowing the cylinder to turn by means of reversible key thereby rotating a cam / bolt mounted on cylinder from locking position to unlocking position & vice versa; a key slot provided in the cylinder is slightly deeper than the cylinder face to accommodate the shoulder of the key.
2. A camlock with reversible key as claimed in claim 1, wherein the said cam/bolt is mounted on the cylinder through slot provided in the cylinder with matching projection in the cam to locate the cam in its proper position and then fitted on the cylinder by means of spring washer & nut.
3. A camlock with reversible key as claimed in claim 1, wherein the cam is provided with a stopper pin which moves within the slot provided in the housing so that it can move 90° with respect to housing & the said stopper


pin is provided on either side of the cam; the side of the cam is interchangeable to provide clockwise and anticlock wise movement.
4. A camlock with reversible key as claimed in claim 1 comprises of a locking cam rotatably by 90° with reference to the housing, further comprising of the rotatable cylinder, housing the operating pins, driver pins and springs, the said cylinder has a receiving slot for key to operate the said locking cam from the locking position to unlocking position and vice versa.
5. A camlock with reversible key as claimed in claim 1, the locking member is a locking cam.
