Title of Invention

A CAPSULAR BAG SUPPORTING DEVICE OR INSTRUMENT
Abstract This invention relates to a capsular bag supporting device for supporting the capsule during phaco emulsification comprising a shaft extending into a shank through an intermediate bend member and a round plate member provided at the terminating end of the shank.

FILED OF INVENTION
This invention relates to a capsular bag supporting device or instrument. In
particular, this invention relates to a supporting instrument or device supporting
the capsule during phaco emulsification.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
For treatment of cataract, it is known to make a circular opening in the anterior
capsule and emulsifying protein material by an ultrasound driven needle. Such a
procedure is known as phaco emulsification. If zonules are weak or broken, the
crystalline lens may then drop back leading to complications. Thus, the capsule
ought to be supported during the procedure of phaco emulsification in cases of
zonular weakness.
Such a support was hitherto provided by an instrument known as Iris hook to
support the capsule. However, such an instrument was not satisfactory as area
and shape of support was not ideal.
Such a hook was modified so as to support the capsule. Thus, an angle was
imparted to the J shaped retractor and length of hook increased so as to support
the capsule upto the fornix of the capsule. However, such a modified retractor
was still attended with the disadvantage that it supported only a single point of
the fornix. Yet another disadvantage is that it had a pointed end, which could
penetrate and damage the capsule.

OBJECTS OF INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a capsule
bag supporting device or instrument.
Another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a
capsule bag supporting device or instrument which obviates the disadvantages
ieferred to hereinabove.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a
capsule bag supporting device or instrument which maintains the position of the
capsule during the procedure of phaco emulsification.
Still another object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a
capsule bag supporting device or instrument which supports the fornix as well as
the capsulorhexis rim, so that the peripheral capsule isn't attracted towards the
phaco tip.
A further object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a
capsule bag supporting device or instrument which avoids the problem of the
capsule supporting device trapping cortex.
A still further object of this invention is to propose an improved construction of a
capsule bag supporting device or instrument which makes it possible to perform

BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided a capsular bag supporting
instrument or device comprising an elongate member extending at the distal end
into an angular member and being generally of a V-shaped member, the free
end of the V-shaped member extending into a hook, characterized in that the
open loop at the end of the hook is in the same plane as the anterior capsule.
in accordance with this invention, the instrument comprises an elongate
member. The distal end of the elongate member extends into an angled
member, said member being generally of a V-shape. The free end of the V-
shaped or angled member has an open ended loop at the tip.
in accordance with this invention the hook is angled to be in the plane of the
anterior capsule. As a result, it's easy to get a firm and reliable grip on the rim

of the fornix of capsule. Further the loop at the tip of the hook is rounded to
prevent any accidental damage to the peripheral capsule while placing the hooks
in the capsular bag. The round tip also provides a larger support area per hook
thereby reducing the number of hooks required in each surgery. Once the hooks
are placed in position to support the capsule, an endocapsular ring can be
inserted
