Title of Invention

"SEPARABLE AND ASSEMBLEABLE OUTER CASE OF HOME APPLIANCE"
Abstract A separable and assembleable outer case of electric home appliance adapted for easy assembly work during mass production and being replaceable when sold in parts and for being order-manufactured according to colors and 5 heights (sizes) desired by consumers, the separable and assembleable outer case of electric home appliance, the home appliance having an outer case nit forming an external appearance thereof, the outer case unit comprising: a plurality of plate members separable in predetermined sizes to form an external appearance of the home appliance; and a plurality of connecting members for 10 facingly connecting tip ends of the plurality of plate members by way of hitching method to cover gaps thereamong lest the gaps are exposed.
Full Text BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to, in particular, a separate and assembleable outer case of electric home appliances, and generally to an outer case for entire lines of electric home appliances.
DESCRIPTION OF THE PRIOR ART
For the sake of explanation, an embodiment of washing machine out of many electric home appliances is described here.
As illustrated in Figure 1 of the accompanying drawings, a washing machine is mounted with an outer case 10 for forming lateral circumference (by way of example, front side, both sides, rear side, etc.) by being bent at predetermined angles and sizes in order to define an external appearance, and the outer case 10 is coupled thereon with a top cover 12. The top cover 12 is installed at an upper central opening (not shown) thereof with a door 14 hinged at, by way of example, a rear side thereof for opening and closing. The top cover 12 is provided at a front upper side thereof with a controller 16 for controllably operating the washing machine and at a rear upper side thereof with a plurality of water supply holes 18 for connecting to hot/ cold water faucets via a water supply hose.
At this location, as illustrated in Figure 2, of the accompanying drawings, the outer case 10 is made of one piece high-strength steel plate member 10a, painted in one-toned colour. The steel plate member 10a is formed at a predetermined place thereof with a plurality of imaginary lines 10b for easy folding in three-dimensional shape
-1A-

according to formal design of the home appliance.
In other words, when a one-piece flattened plate member 10a is folded along the imaginary line 10b, the member 10a is transformed into an outer case 10 having a front side, lateral sides and rear side, as illustrated in Figure 3.
However, there is a problem in the outer case 10 thus constructed according to the prior art in that sizes and height thereof cannot be varied according to desires of consumers because of its production in one piece steel plate member 10a in established dimensions according to size and kind of home appliance products. There is another problem in that processing work on the outer case 10 is not that easy. There is still another problem in that it is impossible to replace the outer case 10a when an after sales service is in need.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
The present invention is disclosed to solve the aforementioned problems and it is an object of the present invention to provide a separable and assembleable outer case of electric home appliance adapted to adjustably manufacture size or height of the outer case unit according to need and desire of consumers to make it easy for the outer case unit to be processes and to be varied, m size thereof. It is another object of the present invention to enable replacement thereof in case of after sales service.
In accordance with the objects of the present invention, there is provided a separable and assembleable outer case of electric home appliance, the home appliance having an outer case unit forming an external appearance thereof, the outer case unit comprising:
a plurality of plate members separable in predetermined sizes to form an external appearance of the home appliance; and
a plurality of connecting members adapted to face connecting tip ends of the

An outer case unit 50 according to the present invention includes a plurality of plate members 60 having a front surface, left surface, right surface and two rear surfaces, in case of unfolded or separated and a plurality of connecting members 70 for connecting the plurality of plate members 60 to an assembly by way of hitching method, as illustrated in Figure 4.
In other words, the plurality of plate members 60 are made of high strength of steel plate or* injected material and can be painted in different colors according to desire of consumers.
Furthermore, the plurality of plate members 60 are respectively formed with bent surfaces 61, each having an angle of approximately 270 degrees by way of bending working toward the plate member 60 along a vertical direction relative to both ends thereof so as to be connected by the plurality of connecting members 70.
At this time, a plate member 60 frontally faced among the plurality of plate members 60 is preferred to be bent at right angle at both tip ends thereof so as to show a beautiful facade of design when connected by the connecting member 70 to other plate members comprising the left surface and right surfaces, such that connecting parts thereof are not exposed to a front surface of the other case unit 50.
Meanwhile, the plurality of connecting members 70 are centrally and

Furthermore, each connecting member 70 serves not to contact each facing boundary part when the two piate members 60 are correspondingly connected and to cover the boundary part so as not to be exposed to outside when viewed from inner/outer sides of the outer case unit 50.
The plurality of connecting members 70 may be painted in the same color as that of the plurality of plate members 60 to render an integral feeling or in contrasting color to be distinguished from the outer case unit 50.
Now, operational "effect of the first embodiment of the present invention thus constructed will be described.
First of all, the front surface, left surface, right surface and two rear surfaces of the plurality of plate members 60 are arranged to mutually face at each end thereof, while each connecting member 70 is vertically inserted through upper side or lower side of bent surfaces 61 respectively formed at mutually facing ends, as illustrated in Figure 4.
At this time, the pair of grooves 71 formed at both sides of each connecting member 70 are vertically and correspondingly fitted by the connecting member 70 along bent surfaces 61 respectively formed at tip ends of two plate members 60 for assembly between the plate member 60 and the connecting member 70, as illustrated in Figure 6.
When the plurality of plate members 60 and the plurality of connecting members 70 are continuously assembled according method thus described, a

finished outer case unit 50 can be produced as illustrated in Figure 5.
The outer case unit 50 is then coupled at an upper end thereof to the top cover 12 according to the prior art while at a lower end thereof to a base panel (not shown) to thereby enable to securely maintain an assembled state between the plurality of plate members 60 and the connecting members 70.
Therefore, the outer case unit 50 according to the present invention is easy in assembly work during mass production thereof, and replaceable during repair work in case sold per part. The outer case unit 50 can be order-made in different sizes (height) and colors according to consumer's desire. In other words, the outer case unit may be assembled with mutually different colors of plural plate members 60 to provide variety to the entire design of the product.
Furthermore, a plurality of plate members, by way of example, two or ore than two plate members 60 are produced in different heights (sizes) and co-used to allow appropriate installation according to heights of consumers and easy application to complex kitchen system.
