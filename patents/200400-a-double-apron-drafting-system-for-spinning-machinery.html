Title of Invention

A DOUBLE APRON DRAFTING SYSTEM FOR SPINNING MACHINERY
Abstract This invention relates to a double apron drafting system for spinning machinery. A pneumatic condenser device is arranged between the front rollers and delivery device. A perforated apron and a suction device which extends on the side of the apron for drawing air through the fibre structure is provided. The apron has a series of perforations crosswise to the direction of fibre transport. The perforations are provided with extensions crosswise to the direction of fibre transport which is larger than that in the direction of fibre transport.
