Title of Invention

INTAKE AMOUNT CONTROL SYSTEM FOR ENGINE
Abstract To provide an intake amount control system for an engine which makes it possible to concurrently perform the machining of a throttle body and the manufacture of an assembly of an idle air control value,an actuator and a throttle sensor,and which can contribute to enhancement of productivity. A synthetic resin made casing 10 seperate from a throttle body 1 is fitted to the throttle body 1 on one end side of a value shaft 4 of a throttle valve 3,and an idle air control valve 25,an actuator 26 and a throttle sensor 37 are disposed in the casing 10.
