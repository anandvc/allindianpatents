Title of Invention

COLLAPSIBLE HEADGEAR
Abstract The invention relates to a Collapsible Head Qear which designed to open in operative position and to fold in in-operative position. The said Collapsible Head Qear comprises of a plurality of fan-shaped segments which can be super imposed one on the other. The said seven segments arrangement which made up of high density polyethylene fibre glass are hinged at two points. Each segment has a spherical internal radius to form a normal size of the skull and also includes a spring-loaded plunger mechanism on the sides to achieve the Lock-in position. Two ends of each of the said segments are fixed to each one of the two circular plates. The said circular plates are connected in a super imposed position by means of a pivot. The said spring disposed in a sleeve of pivot comprises of two ends - one end fixed to hold-on member and its other end to an adjustable-member extending into said sleeve, whereby the tension of the said spring can be adjusted. The successive position of the said segments from collapsed position can be obtained by means of aperture portion of said circular plates and a complementary hook member. Thus the Collapsible Head Qear as per the invention prevents the two wheelers from skull injuries and provide safe travel, comfortable and easy to carry.
