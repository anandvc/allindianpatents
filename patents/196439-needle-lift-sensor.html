Title of Invention

NEEDLE LIFT SENSOR
Abstract The invention in general relates to a Needle Lift Sensor having an integral non-metallic bobbin and sleeve inside the nozzle holder of an injector to measure the beginning and end of fuel injection in fuel injection pump and therebz reduce emission and smoke and improves the engine performance. A non-metallic bobbin formed bz injection moulding wound with thin copper wire including output leads, forms a bobbin insert; which bobbin insert undergoes injection mould so as to form a single integral unit having a non-metallic bobbin and sieeve set.
