Title of Invention

A METHOD OF GENERATING A PSEUDORANDOM DATA SEQUENCE AND A GENERATOR THEREOF
Abstract The invention relates to a generator (1) of a pseudorandom data sequence (3) comprising searching means (5) for searching at least one search pattern (7) in an initial data sequence (9) of N bits.
