Title of Invention

TRANSMITTER CONFIGURED TO MEASURE A PROCESS VARIABLE OF AN INDUSTRIAL PROCESS AND A METHOD THEREFOR
Abstract A transmitter configured to measure a process variable of an industrial process, includes a pressure sensor (10) configured to couple to a pressure of a fluid. An acoustic detector (18) receives an acoustic signal from the fluid. Measurement circuitry (20) coupled to the pressure sensor (10) and the acoustic detector (18) provides an output (24) related to pressure of the fluid.
