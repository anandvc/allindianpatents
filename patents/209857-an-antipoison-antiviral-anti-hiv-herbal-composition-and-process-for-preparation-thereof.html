Title of Invention

"AN ANTIPOISON, ANTIVIRAL & ANTI HIV HERBAL COMPOSITION AND PROCESS FOR PREPARATION THEREOF"
Abstract The present invention discloses an antipoison, antiviral & anti HIV herbal composition useful for the treatment of diseases caused by the viruses, poisons, toxins, autotoxins, comprising the following ingredients in a ratio as mentioned therewith : Kesar from the Nelumbo Nucifera 10 % ,Wood powder of Santalum Album 45 % ,Dried plant of Sassurea Lappa 45 % and also a process for the preparation of an antipoison, antiviral & anti HIV herbal composition comprising the following steps,Collecting the saiud ingredients,Washing with pure water for 5 mins.,Keeping the ingredients for drying in the sunlight,Mixing in WAV ratio 10%, 45%, 45% respectively and then grinding all the ingredients into a fine powder to obtain the desired herbal composition.
