Title of Invention

A DEVICE FOR THE FASTENING OF A CLOTHING ON A CYLINDER OF A ROTARY PRINTING PRESS
Abstract The invention relates to a fixture for fixing a cover (1) on a cylinder (2) of a rotary web printing machine with at least one axis parallel channel (3) in the cylinder (2), which forms a slot (4) for push-in and pull-out of ends of a cover (1) and a clamp (5) arranged in the channel (3), the clamp is pressed radial in the direction of the slot (4) by means of several compression springs (6) for fixing cover (1), wherein at the ends of the cylinder (2) adjusting elements (7) are arranged, through which the clamp (5) is guided as being radialy push able, to enable push-in and pull-out of the ends of the cover (1). According to the invention inside of the channel (3) near the clamp (5) at least one support element (8) is arranged, which grabs in at least one cavity (9) in the clamp (5), wherein through a movement of adjusting elements (7) a relative movement is caused between support element (8) and clamp (5).
