Title of Invention

A UNIQUE SYNERGISTIC COMPOSITION FOR TREATMENT OF DANDRUFF AND A METHOD OF PREPARING THE SAME
Abstract This invention relates to a unique synergistic composition for treatment of dandruff and related bacterial infections and to a method of preparing the same. The composition comprises of at least one antifungal agent such as miconazole or ketoconazole in combination with an antibacterial agent such as metronidazole or tinidazole; an astringent such as zinc sulphate; a cooling - soothing agent such as Menthol; and optionally an anti inflammatory agent in a phannaceutically acceptable vehicle. The antifungal agent and antibacterial agents are present in the range of upto 10% w/v of the composition. The composition controls dandruff and related bacterial infections and prevents recurrence of dandruff for considerably longer period.

Dandruff is a popular collective name signifying a scaly flaking scalp condition. It occurs when the scalp sheds dead epithelial cells in large clumps, usually due to primary flmgal infection, which is often affected by secondary bacterial infection. Dandruff scales appear dry, white grayish, appearing as small, unsightly patches especially on top of the head. It is often accompanied with one or other form of skin inflammation sometimes with too much of oily secretions known as seborrhea. The most common cause is probably due to fungus, previously known as "Pityrosporum ovale" Malassezia ovalis. The fungus is found naturally on the skin surface of both healthy people and those with dandruff
Presently, many types of products in the form of shampoos / drugs are available in the market for the treatment of dandruff.
However, the above mentioned conventional dandruff treatment shampoos / drugs have many disadvantages, some of which are mentioned below.

Although conventional dandruff medication is based on forms of therapy targeted mainly at fungal infection, they do not treat fungi lilce T.podis, T.oppora. T.rubrum, etc.
Currently used antifungal based products are not satisfactory against secondary bacterial infection, which is very common in dandruff. The possible causative organism of seborrheic dermatitis is Pitryosporum ovalie. The conventiona] dandruff treatment drugs are also not effective against anaerobic bacteria.
The object of the present invention is to develop a uniqije synergistic composition for treatment of dandruff and plated infections) which could overcome the disadvantages of the conventional dandruff treatments and provide a better alternative.
The composition of the present invention not only shows good clinical efficacy in the treatment of dandruff but also prevents recurrence of dandruff within a reasonably short time.
The composition, according to this invention, is also effective against flingal infection, especially from fungi like T.podis, T.oppora. T.rubrum, etc. It is also effective against secondary bacterial infections, mcluding anaerobic bacterial infections.
