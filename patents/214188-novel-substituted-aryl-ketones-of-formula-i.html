Abstract "NOVEL SUBSTITUTED ARYL KETONES OF FORMULA (I)* The invention relates to benzocyclohexandiones of general formula (I), a method for the production and use thereof as herbicides and plant growth regulators. In said general formula (I), Rl, R2, R3, R4, R5, R6 and R7 represent various radicals, L is an alkylene chain and Y and Z are a monoatomic bridging element.
