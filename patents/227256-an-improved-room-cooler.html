Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED ROOM COOLER"
Abstract This invention relates to a humidity and temperature control room cooler comprising: a casing having any two opposite sides open, an exhaust fan fixed in front of one open side and the other opposite side has suction windows, slots are provided in one side of said casing, a number of cooling pads are arranged in said slots between said exhaust fan and said suctions windows, a water spraying system on said cooling pads with control release of water for each said pad, and a water tank with pump is provided at the bottom of said casing.
Full Text This' invention relates to humidity, temperature and breeding of mosquitoes control room cooler.
BACKGROUND
The room coolers available in the market have a metal/plastic rectangular frame having openings at three sides and fan fixed on the fourth side. The said fourth side may have louvers before it to change the direction of the air-flow. The three open sides are covered with frames having cooling pads. The said frames are removable. In these coolers the air is sucked from three sides by the suction fan ad the water flow is continuously sprinkled on the said three cooling pads. When the air is sucked from three sides it gets cooled and wet and is thrown from the front fourth side to make the room cool.
The drawbacks of these cooler;, are.
• There is no humidity control
• There is no temperature control
The water droplets come out from the front side, which makes the room or articles lying in the room wet
• The water tank at the base of the body of the cooler is open,
causing mosquitoes to breed and thereby causing diseases.
The object of this invention is to control the humidity and temperature of the air, which is sucked and is thrown into the room and also preventing breeding of mosquitoes.
To achieve the said objective this invention provides a humidity, temperature and breeding of mosquitoes control room cooler comprising:
a casing having any two opposite sides open,
an exhaust fan fixed in front of one open side and the other
opposite side has suction windows,

slots are provided in one side of said casing,
a number of cooling pads are arranged in said slots between said
exhaust fan and said suctions windows,
a water spraying system on said cooling pads with control release
of water for each said pad, and
a water tank with pump is provided at the bottom of said casing.
The said cooling pads are arranged parallel to each other.
Louvers are provided on said one open side for changing the direction of cooled air-flow from said cooler.
The said water spraying system consists of a pump for pumping water from the water tank to the water spraying means having control valves for water spra\. said water spray control system is provided under the roof of said casing over said cool ing pads.
The water released from the spraying system on said cooling pads is controlled by water flow valves for each pad so that the cooling pad before said suction window receives maximum amount of water and the cooling pad before said exhaust fan receives waterjust to moist.
The said tank at the base of said casing is covered with a metal sheet slanting towards said suction window end to prevent suction of water from a cooling pad before the suction fan. This slanting metal sheet also prevents mosquitoes to breed in and help not to spread diseases.
The said metal sheet is provided with water collection hole covered with a net towards said suction window.

The number of cooling pads can be increased or decreased depending upon the cooling temperature required in the room.
The frames for holding the cooling pads is provided with hooks to prevent the cooling pads from shrinking
Casing of said frame is made of metal or plastic.
The number of blades of said exhaust fan can be increased subject to desired cooling.

WORKING
The air from the said suction windows is sucked by the exhaust fan and it passes through the cooling pads which are wet in varying degree and said sucked air loses heat on said cooling pads and finally reaches the moist pad and loses its moisture. The air which is blown out of the said cooler is comparatively humidity and temperature controlled. The temperature of the room is controlled by the number of cooling pads provided in the cooler.



Claim :
1. An improved room cooler comprising:
a casing (1), an exhaust fan (EF) and water tank (2) with a pump at the bottom of said casing characterized in that
- said casing has two opposite sides (01, 02) open,
- said exhaust fan (EF) being fixed in front of one open side and the other opposite side has suction windows (4),
- slots (S1,S2,S3,S4,S5...) being provided in one side of said casing,
- a number of cooling pads (P) are arranged in said slots between said exhaust fan (EF) and said suction windows (4) and
- a water spraying system on said cooling pads (P) with control release of water for each said pad.

2. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1 wherein said cooling pads (P) are placed parallel to each other.
3. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein louvers are positioned on one open side for changing the direction of cooled air flow.
4. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein said water spraying system consists of a pump water from the water tank (2) to the water spraying means having control valves (V1, V2..) for water spray, said water spray control system is provided under the roof of said casing (1) over said cooling pads (P).
5. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein the water released from the spraying system on said cooling pads (P) is controlled by water flow valves (V1, V2,...) for each pad so that the cooling pad (P) before said exhaust fan (EF) receives water just to moist.

6. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein said tank (2) at the base of said casing (1) is covered with a metal sheet slanting towards said suction window end to prevent suction of water from a cooling pad (P) before the suction fan thereby preventing breeding of mosquitoes and spreading of diseases.
7. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein said metal sheet is provided with water collection hole (7) covered with a net (N) towards said suction window.
8. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, having a provision of increasing/decreasing the number of cooling pads (P) depending upon the desired room temperature.
9. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein frames for
holding the cooling pads (P) is provided with hooks to prevent the
cooling pads from shrinking.
10. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 9, wherein casing (1) of
said frame is made of metal or plastic.
11. An improved room cooler as claimed in claim 1, wherein the blades in said exhaust fan are increased or decreased subject to desired cooling.
12. An improved room cooler substantially as herein described with reference to accompanying drawings.
