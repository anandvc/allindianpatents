Title of Invention

HYDROGEN GAS KIT
Abstract A Hydrogen gas generator for Internal combustion (I.C.) engines, comprising stainless steel tank-A, adopted for generating Hydrogen gas by chemical reaction between a mild acid or an alkqli and ferraus/Non-ferrous metal scrap, connected with pressure gauge, for indicating the pressure of the gas, safety valve for the safety of gas pressure control, inlet with cap for feeding above said materials, battery operated solenoid valve for switching on/off gas supply, Hydrogen gas pipe-line, non-jacketed regulator for controlling the gas supply to the I.C. engines.

Background of the prior art :-
State-of-the-art technology known comprises C.N.G./L.N.G./L.P.G. compressed cylinder kit, fitted to the floor of the boot of the car or an automobile and in continuation, gas pipe-line from the cylinder, fitted with a battery operated solenoid valve, a main valve, a regulator, a pressure gauge and In continuation, the pipe-line, being fitted to the air cleaner of the engine of the car, through the main valve being fitted to the chassis of the engine of the car, etc., the handle of the main valve being fitted to the accelerator of the engine of an automobile, the engine can be accelerated as required.
The established prior art, however, has many imperfections and design defects which are clearly pinpointed as follows :-
1. Compressed gas cylinder of C.N.G./L.N.G./L.P.G.
A highly compressed fuel gas cylinder is obviously unsafe, and at times dangerous, in case of an accident, causing loss of human lives and property, whereas this kit or invention, incorporates only a tank in which Hydrogen gas, as a fuel, is produced as and when required i.e., Hydrogen gas on demand. The gas is produced for simultaneous or on-line use; no storage of compressed Hydrogen gas is necessary; it is thus much safer and better inall respects. However, Hydrogen gas kit works on non-compressed Hydrogen gas fuel technology. Practical advantages are very obvious.
2. Leakage : A highly compressed gas cylinder and the
entire kit, Is susceptible or liable to leakage, but
this Invention or the kit being a non-pressure device,
is not liable to leakage, hence safer.


3. Pollution : C.N.G./L.P.G., being a Hydro-carbon, when used as a fuel, Invariably produces or exhausts carbon-dioxide and to some extent, carbon-mono-oxide- a poisonous gas, sulphur-dioxide, nitrogen oxides and particulate matter, which are all health hazards and cause atmospheric pollution; however, there Is no pollution, when Hydrogen gas is used as a fuel, as the product of combustion or exhaust is water :- 2H202 a 2H20.
4. Heating pad : There Is a provision for heating L.P.G. cylinder which consumes power of the automobile battery; however, the tank of the kit, does not need a heating pad, the boiling point of Hydrogen being -252#75* C or 20.lfOBK.
5. Corrosion t L.P.G./C.N.G. cylinder Is made of general
purpose steel, which is susceptible or liable for atmospheric corrosion or rusting, however, the kit comprises a stainless steel tank, which is not liable to rusting, hence safer and better.
6. Ignition point : The ignition point of L.P.G./C.N.G. Is much lower than that of Hydrogen gas, hence Hydrogen gas is safer.
7. Technology : L.P.G./ C.N.G. works on four - stroke engine but Hydrogen gas works on two-stroke engine also, with efficiency,hence, practically double power Is available without pollution, when the existing four-stroke engines will be converted to two-stroke ones.
8. Calorific value : Hydrogen has about 2.50 times more Calorific value than L.P.G./C.N.G.; this means 1 M.T. of Hydrogen gas is needed, instead of 2.50 M.T. of L.P.G./C.N.G.,
9. Design of main valve : C.N.G./L.P.G. main valve has built-in design or jacket for warming or heating the gas; however, Hydrogen gas kit embodies a simpler and less complicated valve without heating jacket or design, hence not liable
to leakage or maintenance etc., because of less fittings and simpler design. 10. Safety t C.N.G./L.P.G. cylinder has neither a pressure gauge nor a safety valve but the tank of the Hydrogen gas kit embodies them, hence much safer.
