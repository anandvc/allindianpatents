Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED PROCESS FOR PREPARATION OF A PYROTECHNIC FAVOURED CASTABLE COLOUR SMOKE COMPOSITION"
Abstract This invention relates to a process for the preparation of a pyrotechnic favoured castable colour smoke composition. According to the process a binder is prepared by mixing polyester resin, an accelerator and catalyst. A smoke composition is prepared by mixing of an oxidizer, sublimable dyes and fuel and the binder and smoke composition is mixed. Further this invention relates to an improved pyrotechnic favoured castable colour smoke composition. An improved composition has a binder of polyester resin, an accelerator and a catalyst and a smoke composition of oxidizer, sublimable dyes and fuel
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
The present invention relates to an improved process of preparing pyrotechnic favoured castable coloured smoke which is suitable for generating coloured smoke using coloured dyes which sublime into vapour directly from solid state.
PRIOR ART
When a Smoke Composition utilizes the heat of chemical reaction between oxidizer and fuel, to vaporize the volatile ingredients present in the composition, it is known as Pyrotechnic Smoke Composition. In a Colour Smoke Composition the colour dye ingredients vaporize and subsequently the dye vapour condenses in atmosphere, giving Coloured Smoke. Some of the Pyrotechnic Colour Smoke Composition known in the art are made with binder or without binder. When a binder is used as a constituent of smoke composition, it is known as a Castable Smoke Composition. Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition have been used for signaling, marking, tracking, spotting and distress signaling.
One of the Pyrotechnic Castable Colour Smoke Composition known in the art comprises by weight of 10 to 25% of alkali metal chlorate as oxidant, selected from the group consisting of Potassium Chlorate and Sodium chlorate, 10 to 30% of Guanidine Nitrate, 10 to 35% of synthetic-aliphatic epoxy resin, 1 to 15% of organic plasticizer, 0 to 3% of hardener, 25 to 50% of a sublimable organic dye, 0 to 5% of atleast one metal oxide and the carbazate.
One of the disadvantages of the above is, that this composition has to be processed at elevated temperature of 60° C and above.
Another disadvantage of the above composition is that, the binder is also to be prepared separately at elevated temperature of 60° C and above.
Another Pyrotechnic Colour Smoke Composition known in the art. contains 1.4-benzenedicarboxylic acid between about 7 and 35% by weight, solvent red dye between about 30 and 50% by weight, fuel between about 13 and 25% by weight, potassium chlorate between about 17 and 30% by weight, sodium bicarbonate or magnesium bicarbonate between about 5 and 20% by weight,
One of the disadvantages of the above composition is use of acetone in the preparation or the composition by the wet mixing method, as acetone is a fire hazard. This may result into injurv to workers and accidental fire during the course of its manufacture.
Another disadvantage of the above composition is the use of special process equipment's required for its production/pelletisation
Still another disadvantage of the above composition is that the density of the peilet being
lower, production of smoke vis-a-vis per unit volume of the pellet is less.
Still another disadvantage of the above composition is the that it poses dust hazard during processing due to the absence of binder.
OBJECTS OF THE PRESENT INVENTION
The primary object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition does not decompose when ignited in confinement under pressure of 0.5 to 0.6 Kg/Cm2
Another object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition uses a synthetic aromatic polyester binder which is cheap and also abundantly available.
Still another object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition does not cause dust hazard during the process of manufacture.
Yet another object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition can be pelleted-out at lower pressure while achieving higher pellet densities.
Further object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition favours the emission of smoke for longer period.
Still further object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition retains its colouring property while sublimating into vapour state.
Yet further object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition produces smoke which is visible from a greater distance, more particularly produces Orange colour smoke, which is distinctively visible.
Even further object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition produces voluminous smoke which hovers over the spot of its launch in calm wind conditions
Yet further object of the present invention is to have a Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition produces smoke which, does not rise very high to avoid loss of visibility due to dispersion
Still further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition can be produced without using special process equipment.
Yet further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition has higher density and produces copious colour smoke per unit volume of the Composition
Still further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition does not utilise hazardous substances for its production.
Yet further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition can he easily ignited .
Still further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition is economical to produce.
Even further object of the present invention is to have Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition and process for preparation thereof wherein the composition which can be produced at ambient temperature.

The composition is prepared as per the following steps:-
i) Required quantity of ingredients (excluding polyester resin which has been utilised for the preparation of binder) are mixed in an aluminium tray and sieved three times using preferably 600 micron IS sieve and then mixed for one hour using mechanical (drum mixer) means to get uniform mix.
ii) Polyester resin prepared separately as per step(a) is added slowly to the smoke composition in an Aluminium tray and mixed for around thirty minutes using mechanical means like drum mixer, to get uniform mix.
(iii) The composition is allowed to stabilise by retaining in the tray for about twenty four hours, thereafter the composition is pelletised.
Filling Method
230 gms Pyrotechnic Favoured Castable Colour Smoke Composition is pressed at 530 kg/cm2 using 69 mm dia plunger to give perforated pellet. Four such pellets when assembled in a suitable container & ignited using priming composition or primed cambric and squib give Orange smoke for about 90 sec.
Due to the Synergic effect of the constituents of the composition, a stable copius Orange coloured smoke is produced. However no smoke is produced by the constituents when ignited individually.
