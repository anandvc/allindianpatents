Title of Invention

BRUSHWARE
Abstract TITLE: BRUSHWARE. THE INVENTION RELATES TO A PRODUCT WITH BRISTLES, COMPRISING A BRISTLE SUPPORT AND BRISTLES CONNECTED THERETO. AT LEAST SOME OF THE BRISTLES ARE COMBINED IN BRISTLE GROUPS IN WHICH THE DISTANCE BETWEEN THE BRISTLES IS LESS THAN THE DISTANCE BETWEEN THE GEOMETRICAL CENTERS OF ADJACENT BRISTLE GROUPS. A PRODUCT WITH BRISTLES OF THIS TYPE IS CHARACTERIZED IN THAT THE DISTANCE OF THE GEOMETRICAL CENTRE OF AT LEAST ONE BRISTLE GROUP FROFM THE GEOMETRICAL CENTRE OF ATLEASDT ONE ADJACENT BRISTLE GROUP IS ESSENTIALLY THE SAME AS OR GREATER THAN THE PRODUCT OF THE AVERAGE NUMBER OF BRISTLES IN THE TWO BRISTLE GROUPS AND THE DIAMETER OF THESE BRISTLES. THIS CONFIGURATIO AND ARRANGEMENT ENABLE THE WORKING ENDS OF THE BRISTLES OF THESE BRISTLE GROUPS TO BE ARRANGED LINEARLY IN THE DIRECTION OF BRUSHING AND PENETRATE NARROW GAPS AND RECESSES, INDEPENDENTLY OF THEIR FIXING SURFACE ON THE BRISTLE SUPPORT.
