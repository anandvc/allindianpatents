Title of Invention

"A COMPACT HIGH-VOLTAGE INCANDESCENT LAMP"
Abstract This invention relates to a compact high-voltage incandescent lamp, in particular, a high-voltage halogen incandescent lamp, having a lamp bulb(1;12) and a luminous element (4) which is made from a triply wond incandescent wire, which is arranged inside the lamp bulb (1;12) and is connected in an electrically conducting manner on to two supply leads (9a, 9b) leading outward. The lamp bulb (1;12) and the supply leads (9a,9b) defining a longitudinal axis of the lamp, and in that the luminous element being a double helix (4) which is formed from a coiled-coil filaments (4a-4c).
