Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A NOVEL HERBAL ANTI-MAGGOT AND TOPICAL CARE COMPOSITION
Abstract ABSTRACT A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A NOVEL HERBAL ANTI-MAGGOT AND TOPICAL CARE COMPOSITION The present invention comprises taking the distillates of Cedrus, Eucalyptus and Firms and mixing with emulsifier thoroughly. To this mixture the extract of Curcuma is added which is solublised in a solvent using excipients and mixed thoroughly. To the resulting mixture is then added a propellent base (L.P.G) or a cream base or a lotion base, in order to obtain an antimaggot aerosol spray or a cream or a lotion, respectively. The resulting composition has powerful anti-maggot, anti-microbial and wound healing (vulnerary) activities.
