Title of Invention

A SYNERGISTIC INSECTICIDAL COMPOSITION CONTAINING IMIDACLOPRID CYPERMETHRIN
Abstract The invention disclosed in this application relates to synergistic insecticidal composition containing Chloronicotynyle and Pyrethroids compounds, which comprises mixing thoroughly, one or more Chloronicotynyle compound, in an amount ranging from 0.1 to 5 % by weight of the composition, one or more compounds falling within the group of Pyrethroids compounds, in an amount ranging from 1.0 to 60 % by weight of the composition and 98.90 to 35 % by weight of conventional agriculturally acceptable carrier(s) and excepient(s).
