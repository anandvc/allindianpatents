Title of Invention

A COMPOSITION FOR ENHANCING NITROGEN FIXATION IN LEGUMES
Abstract A composition to enhance and assure Nitrogen Fixation in Legumes thereby increasing yield and enrich the soil for Agriculture and Horiculture comprising the first step of taking 3 kilos Ferrous Sulphate Hepta Hydrate (FeSO4 7 H2O) and sequestering it or chelating it with 3.547 Kilos of Hydroxy Ethyledene di Phosphonic acid or 4.033 Kilos Di sodium Salt of Ethylene diamine tetra acetic aci with sufficient De Mineralized water and allowing to react completely and then adding 200 grams Ammonium Molybdate or 253.66 gram sodium Molybdate or 236.36 gram iron Molybdate and 20kg Ammonium Sulphate and 5 kg vitex negundo extract and 5 kilos water dispersible granular sulphur and 1 kg succinic acid and prime it to 200 litres with De mineralized water.
Full Text The invention relates to a preparation for enhancing nitrogen fixation in Legumes. The invention specifically relates to Leguminous crops improvement. Hither to thinking goes that only bacterial support is needed for nitrogen fixation in Legumes. But chemical ion exchanges are essential where in sulphur and iron ions are acting by the force of smaller protein which supply electrons. This Fe and S ions are pushed by bigger protein electrons and ultimately goes into Molybdenum ion forming Fe, S, Mo cluster. Then from atmosphere two atoms of nitrogen enters plant system in Fe, S5 Mo Cluster with exchange of NH3 and H2 from the plant system to atmosphere. Thus two atoms of Nitrogen comes in and one Nitrogen atom goes out in plant system.
This chemical reaction induced us to invent a suitable formula to help Legumes, perfectly to do the job of Nitrogen fixation.
Increasing the yield in pulses and oil seeds like groundnut and soyabean and increasing the soil fertility by increasing Nitrogen content in soil is the aim of the invention. This invention is specific to Legumes.
Legumes needs nitrogen in two ways 1) for growth and 2) for protein synthesis and Amino Acids.

Increasing the Nitrogen contents in Root nodules and increasing yield in Black gram, green gram, Red gram, Bengal gram, Dolichos Beans of different variety of Legumes, cow pea, peas, Rajma beans, Groundnut, French bean, Spring bean, Cluster bean, Soyabean, and other pulses and oilseeds and incidentally enriching the soil to enrich the yield of crops followed by pulses is the role of this product.
There is no prior art in enhancing and stabilysing nitrogen fixation from Atmosphere and storing it in root nodules by legumes. In fact if the elements iron, sulphur, Mobyldenumn, Nitrogen are not there adequately the nitrogen fixation will suffer. Now with this composition it is assured if applied on plants in time. Further to prevent interaction of chemicals, iron is sequestered fully. We have added vitex Negundo extract to help flowering, to prevent pests, and also to mitigate disease entry and succinic Acid is added as Electron transporter. This composition is first time introduced in the world for Legumes.

The primary object is to increase yield, increase quality, increase oil content in pulses, groundnut, and soyabean and also ensure protein content in all Legumes & reduce shrivelled grains and to increase soil fertility.
It is environmental friendly and the composition is totally water soluble and no effluent is left The metallic ions acts effective, after being charged electronically by protein molecules in the plants and plays a vital role in the transfer chemistry by taking two atoms of Nitrogen inside and relea$ing one atom of Nitrogen. Net result one Nitrogen atom is gained by the crops (Legumes) in every reaction.
The composition herein proposed will be made by taking 3 kg FeSO4 7H2O (OR) 3 kg FeSO4 H2O and dissolved in DM. water and it is sequestered respectively with 3.547 kg Hydroxy Ethyledene Di-Phosphenic acid for FeSO4 7H2O or 6.347 kg Hydroxy Ethyledene Di-Phosphenic acid for FeSO4 H2O (And if it is Na EDTA it should be used respectively at 4.033 for Hepta Hydrate Iron Sulphate or 7.218 kg for Mono Hydrate iron sulphate)
After this chelation is over 200 gram Ammonium Molybdate or 253.66 gram sodium Molybdate or 236.36 gram Iron Molybdate is added every time adding De - Mineralized water and stirred well and 5 kg WDG

sulphur is added 1 kg succinic acid is added and 5 kg V. Negundo extract and Ammonium Sulphate 20 kg is added and primed to 100 litres and if needed filter it to get a clear solution.
The composition is made as a soluble powder by mixing 6 kg FeSO4 7H2Q (Hepta Hydrate Iron Sulphate) or 6 kg FeSO4 H2O (Mono Hydrate Iron Sulphate) with 4.433 kg Na2 Hydroxy Ethyledene di Phosphonic acid for FeSO4 7H2O and 7.934 kg Na2 HEDP for FeSO4 H2O (or) Na2 EDTA 8.067 kg for FeSO4 7H2O and 14.436 kg Na2 EDTA for FeSO4 H2O and once the chelation is over powder is mixed well. Add 400 gram Ammonium Molybdate, (or) 507.32 gr sodium Molybdate (OR) 472.72 gr Iron Molybdate and 1 kg succinic acid, 200 gram V. negundo crystal and 20 kg Ammonium Sulphate is added and 17.964 kg to 27.967 kg Calcium carbonate 20 kg MgSO4-Magnisum sulphate, 20 kg KCL-MURIATE OF POTASH as fillers required quantity to make it up to 100 kgs is added and mixed thoroughly and ball milled to fine powder and 100 kilos product is ready.
