Title of Invention

PLASTIC FILM FOR MEDICAL LIQUID CONTAINERS
Abstract A plastic film for medical liquid containers which comprises a laminate composed of at least three layers each containing no non-elastomeric block copolymer of an olefin having no benzene ring and propylene, characterized by comprising a first layer consisting mainly of a propylene homopolymer, a second layer comprising a polypropylene resin, a polyethylene resin, and a thermoplastic elastomer, and a third layer consisting mainly of a copolymer of propylene and other monomer and serving as a seal layer, the three layers being superposed without through an adhesion layer so that the second layer is an interlayer.
