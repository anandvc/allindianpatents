Title of Invention

FUEL EFFICIENT BIOMASS STOVE AND A METHOD OF OPERATING THE STOVE
Abstract Disclosed herein is a class of fuel efficient biomass based stoves that provide efficiencies that are the highest amongst the stoves designed or built anywhere in the world and also applications of said stove for heating water, LPG stoves and the like. The fuel efficient biomass stove of the present invention has high efficiency due to its optimized diameter to height ratio of the fuel storage with the provision for complete combustion towards the top portion of the stove. Additionally, using high-density biomass to reduce the distance between the heat release zone and the vessel to which the heat is transferred through a significant component of radiant heat transfer. By the use of fan based air supply, the appropriate air-to-fuel ratio is managed during the combustion process. This also facilita~es clean combustion and therefore, reduced emissions of carbon monoxide and oxides of nitrogen.
