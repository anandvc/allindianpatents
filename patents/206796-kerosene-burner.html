Full Text

This invention relates to Kerosene Burner, which converts the kerosene fuel into heat energy and also converts the cold fuel into hot fuel.
In the known all types of kerosene burners are having one 'U' shaped pipe which is welded to the top combustion chamber and two pipe's are welded to the top combustion chamber and other sides welded to the fuel burner inlet (which can be called as base, having the threads and nut).
The draw back of the known type of kerosene burners are having the number of parts are welded together.
The draw back of the known type of kerosene burner is difficult to manufacture in bracing of number of parts.
The draw back of the known type of kerosene burners is, the fuel is not converting into heat energy fully.
The draw back of the kerosene burner's combustion chamber is getting blocked due to PLUS Type holes connected to fuel line to accumulate carbon particles when the kerosene bumer is used for long period.
The draw back of the kerosene burners is producing high noise.
The draw back of the kerosene burners is consumption of kerosene is high.
The draw back of the kerosene burners is low heat and low efficiency.
The draw back of the kerosene burners is the combustion chamber is highly heated with the flames of burner.
The draw back of the kerosene burner is, the fuel is heated to a very high temperature directly.
The draw back of the kerosene burners is servicing is difficult, to remove the carbon particles from the combustion chamber and the number of pipes which are welded to the base of the burner and combustion chamber.

The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in kerosene burner is to eliminate the number of pipes, which are braced to the combustion chamber and base.
The object of this invention is to propose an Inventive step in kerosene burner is to covert the kerosene or any fuel in to heat energy through uniform cross section.
The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in kerosene burner is having the single pipe bended in loops and at its center(in hole) the nipple threaded base is braced. The said pipe both ends are braced to the base of the burner(fuel inlet).
The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in kerosene burner is to eliminate the number of bracings by introducing the single pipe, which is bended in continuous loops and having the nipple at its center.
The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in kerosene burners by reducing the noise.
The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in fuel burners, where in decrease the pollution by increasing the efficiency of the burner by super heating the fuel.
The object of this invention is to propose an inventive step in kerosene burner by reducing the manufacturing cost.
The object of this invention is to provide a trouble, explotion free and re-serviceable bumer with maximum efficiency to save fuel.
According to this invention, the kerosene burner is described herein comprising a kerosene fuel inlet nozzle, continuous looped metal tube, bent with 2 loops at least, combustion gas outlet nozzle holder, combustion gas out let nipple, a top flame spreader, flame ring and burner ring.
The improved and modified kerosene burner's fuel inlet nozzle at least 5 milli meter diameter hole at bottom side and two holes at least 3 milli meter diameter at top, having the external tiireads(at bottom) with nut as single part. The said inlet nozzle is braced to the two ends of continuous looped tube, which is bent to form at least with 2 loops. The said continuous tube diameter is at least 3 milli meters. A combustion gas outlet nozzle holder is braced in upward direction at the bottom of centre looped pipe, which is having the internal threads, in which the combustion gas outlet nipple is fitted by

screw fitment. The flame spreader is a sheet metal piece in curved shape, pressed to form as braced on the top of burner to reduce the obstructions to the combustion gas path and to guide the hot flames. A burner ring is braced at externally, center of the metallic pipe loops to position the metallic flame ring characterised by the comprises of single continuous loop shaped bend metallic tube (1). This metaiiic flame ring may be made in ceramic, metal, so that it will not absorb or dissipate heat and loss of heat can be avoided. This aspect increases the effective heat transfer.
In order to more clearly, the said inlet nozzle purpose is to fit the burner in the stove with its threads and nut support. The purpose of the said continuous tube Is to supply the kerosene and convert the kerosene into gas form by the heat of the burner flames. The converted kerosene gas will come out from the nozzle. The purpose of the said flame spreader, which is on the top is to spread the flame equally around the bent loops and also to transfer the flame heat to the pressurised kerosene fuel, which is in the bent loop shaped pipe. The purpose of the burner ring is to hold the flame ring. The purpose of the said flame ring is to distribute the flame in circle shape equally In upward direction, around the metallic pipe bent loops top portion and to guide the flames for transferring the flame's heat covering loops.
The working procedure of the improved and modified burner is as follows.
The kerosene burner is ignited as usually like existing bumers igniting methods. The kerosene stove is to be fitted with this bumer and filled the kerosene, pressurise the kerosene stove container until the fuel come out from the nozzle( 4 ) of the burner. This kerosene is to be ignited with the extemal flames. After few minutes the bent looped pipe( 1 ) will be heated up and also the kerosene, which is in the bent looped pipe( 1 ) also heated up. Due to these phenomena the kerosene converts in to gas form and come out through the nozzie( 4 ) of the burner due to increase in pressure in the bent looped pipe(l). These kerosene fumes will be ignited by the extemal flames and the burner get ignited on Its own without external flames. After bumer ignited independently, the kerosene stored container of the stove should be pressurised by the air pump as required or up to the maximum. With this air pressure of the stove, the kerosene will come out from the stove tank and entered in to the improved & modified burner through its inlet nozzle( 2 ). The pressurised kerosene, which Is passing through the bent looped pipes(l), is heated up by the burner's flames heat and also through the flame spreader(5). The said flame spreader( 5 ) is heated up by the burner's flames. Due to this phenomenon, the pressurised fuel

convert Into gas form and exit through the nozzle(4), which is at bottom of the second bent ioop(l) in upward direction.
