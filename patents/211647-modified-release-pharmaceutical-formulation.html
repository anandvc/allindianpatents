Title of Invention

MODIFIED RELEASE PHARMACEUTICAL FORMULATION
Abstract The use of amoxycillin and clavulanate in the manufacture of a medicament for treating bacterial infections in humans, such that administration of the medicament provides from 1900 to 2600 mg of amoxycillin and an amount of potassium clavulanate such that the weight ratio of amoxycillin to potassium clavulanate is from 2:1 to 20:1, at intervals of about 12h.
