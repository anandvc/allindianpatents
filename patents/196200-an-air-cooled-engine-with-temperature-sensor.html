Title of Invention

AN AIR COOLED ENGINE WITH TEMPERATURE SENSOR.
Abstract The invention discloses an air cooled engine with temperature sensor the engine being provided with a crankcase (12), a cylinder block (13), a cylinder head (14) a valve mechanism (22) inside the cylinder head (14), and a lubricating system for lubricating the valve mechanism (22), the lubricating system comprising : Oil suppply passageways (L1-L6) for supplying oil from an oil pan (46) provided at a bottom of the crankcase (12) and an oil tank to the cylinder head (14); an oil return passageway (L7) for returning oil from the cylinder head (14) to the oil pan (46) and to the said oil tank; and an oil pump (45) provided in the oil supply passageways (L1-L6), characterized in that , a temperature sensor (48) for detecting the temperature of oil is provided in the oil return passageway (L7).
