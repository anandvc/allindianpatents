Title of Invention

FABRICATED ARTICLES MADE BY ROTOMOLDING OR SLUSHMOLDING FROM A POLYOLEFIN COMPOSITION
Abstract ABSTRACT FABRICATED ARTICLES MADE BY ROTOMOLDING OR SLUSHMOLDING FROM A POLYOLEFIN COMPOSITION There is disclosed fabricated articles made by rotomolding or slushmolding from a polyolefin composition, said composition comprising (a) from 99% by weight to 99.999% by weight of (i) a polyolefin, or (ii) a polyolefin composition comprising from 50% by weight to 99% by weight of a first polyolefin and from 1% by weight to 50% by weight of a second polymer, which is selected from the group consisting of polyamide, copolyamide, a second polyolefin different from the first one, copolymers of ethylene and vinyl acetate (EVA), copolymers of ethylene and vinyl alcohol (EVOH), polystyrene, polycarbonate and polyvinyl chloride (PVC); (b) from 0.001 % by weight to 1% by weight of a polyethylene glycol as a densification aid in the rotomolding/slushmolding; and (c) optionally from 0.025 % by weight to 0.500 % by weight of one or more UV-stabilizers, wherein the polyolefin has a density in the range from 0.925 g/ml to 0.945 g/ml (measured according to ASTM D 1505 at 23°C) and is a homo- or copolymer of ethylene prepared with a metallocene catalyst system.
