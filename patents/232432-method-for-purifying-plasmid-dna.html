Title of Invention

"METHOD FOR PURIFYING PLASMID DNA"
Abstract The present invention provides methods for the purification of piasmid DNA that includes removal of host cell impurities, such as endotoxins, RNA, proteins, and chromosomal DNA. from an aqueous solution containing piasmid DNA and methods for separation and purification of supercoiled piasmid DNA from an aqueous solution containing a mixture of supercoiled and nicked or relaxed piasmid DNA using hydrophobia interaction chromaiography supports.
