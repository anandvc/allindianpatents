Title of Invention

A CONDOM VENDING MACHINE
Abstract A condom vending machine comprising a housing provided with a lockable door, the housing accommodating a shelf provided for storage of condom packs thereon ; characterized by a tumahle knob, located on the exterior of the housing and having a slit on its body for insertion of a coin of the desired size therein, the said body also incorporating a catch which normally arrests any movement of the knob, but which is actuated by the coin, when inserted in the slit, to release the knob; a push arm connected to the body of the knob such that whenever the knob, in its released state, is turned, the coin in the slit falls, through a gap in the body of the knob, into the housing, while, simultaneously, the push arm is constrained to push the bottom most pack off the shelf to Ml on to the floor of the housing, for manually being removed there from through an outlet, the arm, along with the knob reverting to normal state thereafter. This invention relates to a condom vending machine.
The machine proposed herein does not require power for operating it. It is manually operable; at the same time it enables condom packs to be dispensed without requiring the services of a storage attendant. The operation involves the insertion of a coin and the turning of a knob.
While the machine is simple in construction, it is effective in operation.
