Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED DEVICE TO SUPPLY WATER LIQUID OR FLUID
Abstract This invention is an improved device for to supply clear and filtered water from the available centre itself; that is from the well or from the deep well or from the bore well itself without electric facilities such as motor; so that electric failure or motor failure is not a problem at all. This invention is very simple, economic and efficient. Once fixed, it can be used for several years and maintenance will be very negligible. Due to the pressure of the pressing, water liquid, on fluid flows up and released out through the out let pipe.
Full Text This invention is an improved device for to supply water from one place to the other end. This invention can supply water from the well or from the deep well or even from the borewell; without electric power or electric facilities (such as motor) and without any bucket pulleying. Water, liquid, fluid or anything those are in the viscous nature can supply from one place to the other end with this invention.
The field to which this invention pertain is mainly supply of water from the well or from the borewell. Anything those have viscous nature can supply by the help of this invention. As it is working without the aid of electric power, both elctricity and money can be saved. Save electricity is same as to create electricity and more than that it will be very helpful to the farmers and as a result agricultural prosperity can be ensured by its use.
In the known art, pulleying bucket; and motor pumping is the usual method. Pulleying the bucket with water is a tough work. Lot of pulleying is also needed. Pumping water by the use of motor is also not so dependable because electric failure or motor failure may create problems. It is expensive also.
But this invention can solved all these problems. By a single use it supply water as much as that of the containers capacity (which is immerced in the water as in fig). Once fixed it can be used regularly for years and maintence will be very negligible in normal case. Another advantage is that clear and filtered water can be ensured from the well itself. Its object is to ensure clean water supply with less effort and with less expense and without electricity and can ensure prosperity in the agricultural field by helping farmers with its use.
In short, this invention is simple, economic and efficient. It is much dependable as it is working without the aid of electric power. By a single use as much as water needed can ensure (depends on the quantity or capacity of the container).

I Claim: -
1. An improved device for to supply filtered clear water from the well on from
the deep well or from the bore well or from anywhere, where water is
available without electric power or electric facilities and this invention
comprises a container, which is immersed in the water, with outer and inner
filtered opening or hole and on the top of the outer container brokened
portions are arranged for to release out unused water and wastes it any, will
send away with the help of shades arranged and in inside of the outer
container the inner presser or main portion present and through its top
tapered hollow region water release out to the other end and the string is
arranged for to press the inner main portion to downward when at work and
spring is for to pull upwards for to stand in the normal condition and pulley is
for to smoothening the work and flexible tube at the releasing and for to
release out water and at the joints, frictionless joints helps to reduce friction.
2. An improved device for to supply water as claimed in claim one, can supply
anything those have viscous nature (water, liquid or fluid) from one place to
the other end.
3. An improved device for to supply water and other viscous natured substances
from one place to the other end as here in described and illustrated in the
accompanying drawings.
