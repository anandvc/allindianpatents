SANJEEVANI STRETCHER (S.S.) Useful in any type of emergency for rescue operation in
water or on ground before victims get proper medication or hospitalization.
It is a very unique concept for carrying victims or patients as well as for general use. Non
woven fabric material of spun bond (150 gsm )or any suitable cloth is used for the Sanjeevani
stretcher . This stretcher is designed for multipurpose use, mainly to carry the patient and
protect him from infection . As this material is porous, it also allows for respiration while
keeping patient inside.
Sanjeevani stretcher is one of the best carriers for patient. It is made up of 6 parts as shown in
fig 1 & 2.
Part No 1) - Stretcher -6 feet 6 inches in length and 3 feet in width made of. non woven fabric material (spun bond) (150 gsm) for top and bottom.
Part No 2 ) -Handle - 6 inches in length and 1.5 inches in width ( broad). It is made of . non woven fabric material, ( spun bond) in two layers and 2 cotton strips in two layers are stitched inside the handle to increase its strength .Total eight handles are stitched on the bottom side of the stretcher for lifting the patient so that the weight of the patient is distributed equally all over and one can carry a patient easily with the help of 2 or 4 persons, depending on the weight of the patient .as shown in fig 3 .
Part No 3 ) -ZIP - 9 feet 6 inches long , 8 No. size , double opening /closing system ( 2 runners for chain movement and locking system).
Part No 4 ) -ZIP - 1 foot long , 5 No .size , attached on top side of the stretcher to enable viewing and recognizing the patient ( or dead body) kept inside the stretcher . It is also useful for providing additional ventilation while using it as a sleeping bag .
Part No 5 ) -Sheet of Hitlon (Polyurethane Foam), 25 mm in thickness , 2 feet 3 inches in width and 6 feet 3 inches in length for making the stretcher float. .Hitlon Foam gives plane surface to the stretcher so that the patient gets relaxation to his back or injured spine get proper support. Also it may be used as a mattress when used as a sleep-in-bag .
Part No 6 ) - Strips (4 NOs) - 7 inches in width and 3 feet in length , made of non woven fabric material ( spun bond) and cotton cloth strips stitched at a distance of 3 inches from both ends of the bottom part of the stretcher to bear the weight of the patient , giving extra support to handle and also to insert the Hitlon foam inside the strips so that the Foam may not move from inside.

SANJEEVANI STRETCHER (S.S)
A) A Compact Stretcher (Useful in rescue from narrow space) for carrying any
patient or person from any narrow space. If any accident or any mishap occurs on ground floor,
or on road, the rescue is easy ; but if it is in any apartment in highrise building , it is difficult to
help the patient.
In congested city areas, with narrow lanes, and hindrances caused by various obstructions problems are created in carrying patient to hospital. And if he is staying on higher floor and if there is no lift or lift is not working, it is very dangerous to carry such a patient, who is not in a position to walk or move. Also most of the traditional Stretchers are rigid they can not bend while the patient is being carried through the staircase and if the passage is very narrow, it is more difficult to maintain the balance and time consuming, too. In such a case S.S is really a SANJEEVANI (ultimate remedy) for patients. . .It is very flexible and durable it can not be torn easily.
B) A Floating Stretcher- Many times we find that in rainy season water gets collected (water
logged) in the buildings which are situated near the riverside and it becomes risky to go
through the water with patients . With the help of SS we can easily rescue the patient in this
situation..
C )For injured patient-
A Proper support for injured patient of an accident
Many times , in an accident, the victim is injured badly -some times he gets fractures or deep wounds which may start bleeding profusely.. At such times it is hard to carry the patient carefully . And here S.S comes to help him .
As SANJEEVANI Stretcher is designed specially for the comfort of the patient, it really gives him relief ,while he is being carried to the hospital, because of the Hitlon sheet and keeping him inside , safely covered to avoid infection , movements and horrible sights for rescuers,
As the patient is getting support from all four sides . Many time due to pain he tries to move , and movement of his body may be the cause of any critical injury ,. If he gives movement to his body may be some of the injured bones or neck may be dislocated. And in this situation patient can get better support of Stretcher . When we carry a patient on the open Stretcher ( Regular Iron or Canvas ) there is not any support, and patient moves on it , but in S.S he is having support from all side so he may not move more, and even to carry such a patient is also easy to the assistants.
D) For carrying burn patient
SS is design to use every where as a multipurpose Stretcher in all type of accidents. Carrying properly to burn patient is always a difficult task. A burn patient is trying to move all the time
