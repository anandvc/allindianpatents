Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED METHOD FOR ORGANIZING AND PRESENTING COMPLEX, DETAILED INFORMATION STORED IN ELECTRONIC FORM AND A SYSTEM THEREOF
Abstract A system including a document repository is provided. The system determines, automatically, a level of similarity between two of a plurality of discrete elements stored in the document repository. The system tehn stores then stores data representative of a link between the elements based in-part on the level of similarity. In another embodiment, the system datermines a relation between documents by retrieving a plurality of documents form a document repository. The system segment at least two document of teh plurality of conceptually meaningful segments. The system determines if a segment of one document is related to a segment of another document and stores data representative of the relationship.
