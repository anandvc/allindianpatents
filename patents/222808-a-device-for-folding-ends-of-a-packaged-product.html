Field of the Invention:

The present invention relates to a device for folding the ends of a packaged product. The packaged products flowing out of the flow wrap machine are transferred in such a way that the ends of the packaged product gets pre-folded during the transfer and by applying heat, the ends are set in the folded position.
Background and Prior Art Description:
During the process of packaging a product, the packets flowing through a wrapping machine will have its sealed ends perpendicular to the surface of the product. Figure 1 (a) shows the packaged packet flowing out of the wrapping machine having perpendicular sealed ends. The sealed ends of the packet are folded or iron downed so that they lie flat along the sides. Figure 1 (b) shows the packet having its sealed ends folded such that they lie flat along the sides.
The main purpose of folding the ends of a packet are as follows:
1. End folding will facilitate easy and convenient placement of packets in cartons and at retainer shelves.
2. End folding imparts better look and increases product appeal.
3. End folding makes the side panel of the packs available for display purpose.
4. End folding restricts the movement of the product in the packet thereby improving the protection against damage during transportation.
Till date the sealed ends of packaged products have been folded by a laborious manual process which is time consuming. In order to overcome the disadvantages faced during the manual process, the applicants have now devised a novel device for folding the ends of a packaged product.

In the drawings accompanying the specification,

The invention is further described in detail with reference to the accompanying drawings, which are given by way of illustration and hence, should not be construed to limit the scope of the invention in any manner.
Referring to figure 2, the end folding device of the present invention is mainly categorized in to two units viz. (a) End Folding Unit and (b) Heating Unit.
The End folding unit comprises of a conveyor means (1) rotating means (4)
and a pre-end folding means (3). The conveyor means further comprises of a conveyor belt (12) and a product transfer chain with carry lugs (13). The packaged product coming out of the wrap machine are first taken on the conveyor means. The carry lugs of the product chain carry the packs from the conveyor belt and transfer them to the rotating means. The rotating means is designed such that the packs are received horizontally from

the conveyor belt and are turned by an angle of 90째 such that the packets are oriented vertically and the first and second end flaps (6 and 7) are at the top and bottom of the packs or vise versa. The packaged product is then transferred to the pre-end folding means which comprises of a top and a bottom guide (8 and 9). The top and the bottom guides pre-fold the first and the second end flaps such that they lie flat along the top and the bottom surface of the product.
The packets are then transferred to the heating unit where they are heated so that the end flaps remain in the folded position. The heating unit comprises of a top heating unit (10) and a bottom heating unit (11) which further comprise of a top heating plate and one or more heating coils.
