Title of Invention

EXHAUST GAS FILTER COMPRISING AT LEAST ONE FILTER LAYER AND METHOD FOR THE PRODUCTION OF A FILTER LAYER
Abstract This invention relates to an exhaust gas filter (7) for cleaning an exhaust gas of an internal combustion engine, the exhaust gas filter (7) comprising at least one strip-shaped filter layer (1) made of a material through which a fluid can at least partly flow, said material through which a fluid can at least partly flow having a thickness (D); said at least one filter layer (1) having a length (L) in a longitudinal direction and a width (B) in a transverse direction; said at least one filter layer (1) having a metallic reinforcing region (2) at least in a partial region; said metallic reinforcing region (2) having a width (VB) and a length (VL); and at least one of said width (VB) of said reinforcing region (2) being less than said width (B) of said filter layer (1), or said length (VL) of said reinforcing region (2) being less than said length (L) of said filter layer(I); and said reinforcing region being connected to said filter layer (1) by a process using a joining technique.
