Title of Invention

FRONT LIGHTING STRUCTURE FOR MOTOR SCOOTER TYPE VEHICLE
Abstract To reduce in size a front lighting structure provided at a handle cover. A front lighting structure 11 provided at a front of a handle cover covering a handle is formed to be laterally elongate, a head light portion 60 is disposed at the center of the front lighting structure 11, while position light portions 61 are disposed on the left and right sides of the front lighting structure 11, these are integrated, and the position light portions 61 are retracted from the head light portion 60. A reflector 64 is integrally provided with flat surface portions 64d on both sides of a head light portion reflector 64a, to be reflectors of the position light portions 61. The head light portion reflector 64a reflects the light from a head light bulb 65 disposed at the center, thereby illuminating the front side of the tip ends of the flat surface portions 64d, whereas the flat surface portions 64d and position light bulbs 66 cast light to lateral sides. A lens 62 integrally comprises a head light portion lens 62a and position light portion lenses 62b, and is curved to be protuberant to the front side, and the position light portion lenses 62b are turned round to both lateral sides beyond the head light portion reflector 64a.
