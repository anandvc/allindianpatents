Title of Invention

METHOD OF DELIVERING MULTIMEDIA MESSAGING SERVICE (MMS) NOTIFICATIONS
Abstract The invention explains a method and system for MMS feeds for MMS value added services where the said method avoids unnecessary notification and its response mechanism in case of MMS Value Added Services by incorporating changes in (MM7), the protocol suit used to connected between MMS Proxy/Relay and a Value Added Service Provider and in (MM1), the set of protocols used for communication between a MMS Proxy/Relay and a client Terminal.
