Title of Invention

AN INHALER
Abstract Inhalers used for Asthmatics from kids to aged are difficult to coordinate in stress leading to poor relief. An alerting Inhaler helps timing of spray always. The new inhaler has an air inlet with a cover, with a hole to develop deep, longer inspiration. A whistle behind the air inlet gives sound alert for spray trigger. The whistle leads to spray rod of a drug cannister with a fine hole to back spray into an adjustable mouthpiece. The mouthpiece directs the spray into wind passages and lungs for good effect. The inhaler is kept in mouth, air is sucked in through the air inlet, the whistle gives an alert, the spray cannister is pressed to release a sprays at the back of mouthpiece which travels to lungs for better effect. The inhaler is better bee use of alert, slow, deeper inspiration, adjustable mouth piece and air inlet for an individual variation.
