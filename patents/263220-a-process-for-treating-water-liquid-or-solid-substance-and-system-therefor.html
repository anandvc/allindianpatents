FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to treatment of water, liquid and solid substances for elimination and reduction of harmful contamination and ,in particular, to a process and a system of treating flowing water to make it po table, to suppress undesirable substances present in water and further to energies the water to impart healing characteristics to the water. The process and the system would be simple and cost effective and directed to favour wide scale application and use for a relatively low cost benefit for people desiring portable water and safe guard the health concerns. Importantly, the invention is directed to dynamisation of substances through its contents and in the process make it contamination free and / or impart desired characteristics for the benefit of the end user / consumer.
BACKGROUND ART
It is well known that many undesirable man made substances contaminate raw water which are the sources for po table waters that are bottled for human consumption. Reverse osmosis as well as activated carbon filtration are widely used processes to remove heavy metals and pesticides from raw waters. However, owing to a natural characteristics of the activated carbon, bacteria can easily infest the carbon filter, causing bad taste, bad odour and risk bacteriological contamination. This is one of the reasons why activated carbon is not as widely used, as it should have been. Being of relatively low cost, its utilization could be more widely extended for the benefit of large population and wide section of the society. It is important to have means to improve the elimination and / or degradation of organic contamination such as due to pesticides and heavy metals. Importantly, it is found that even the taste and life charastistics of water get affected due to the presence of organic and inorganic contaminants which usually contaminate water.

SUMMARY OF THE PRESENT INVENTION
Thus according to the basic aspect of the present invention there is provided a process for treating water, liquid or solid substance for elimination and/or degradation of organic /inorganic contaminants comprising:
subjecting the said water/liquid/solid to high frequency waves or bio waves to energize the substance /contents therein and in the process treat the water, liquid or solid substance to free it of harmful contaminants and/or impart desired characteristics therein.
the above process thus involves high frequency waves under action of oscillating circuits, ultrasonic, electric, electromagnetic energies, artificial or natural, tellurian, cosmic or combinations thereof. The process can be used to preferably submit water or any specific liquid or solid substance to combined action of artificial and natural vibrations, generated either from an antenna or from the electrical main line working as an antenna preferably filtered and amplified for effective action.
The substance being treated are submitted to a concentrated high frequency wave and transmitted to the substance through the intermediary of an electrode. The concentrated high frequency wave is obtained by an action of oscillating a filter condenser in which the dielectric is mainly composed of beeswax connected to an antenna and excited by a rotating condenser or any frequency generating device similar to the pulse accelerator.
The electrode used in the above process is preferably made of pure metal, metalloid, crystals or natural or biochemical compounds combined or not with the metal or metalloid.

Preferably, the high frequency waves are transferred to whichever material the water is in contact with selected from sand , silica, activated carbon or any other natural or artificial compounds.
The activated carbon as a filtration substrate is thus energized or dynamised by high frequency bio waves such that the dynamism of the activated carbon enhances the capacity of the activated carbon to absorb and degrade water contaminating substances and/or impede the built-up of bacteria colonies onto the filtration material. The said dynamism of the activated carbon is transmitted to water which acquires special energized properties/characteristics.

As apparent from the above disclosure the invention utilizes high frequency waves to energize the carbon and avoid bacterial contamination. These high frequencies are also known as biowave which can be advantageously and effectively utilize to improve the elimination and degradation of organic and inorganic contamination such as for instance resulting from pesticides and heavy metals. Moreover, it is found that by way of such energizing involving high frequency waves and biowave there is also improvement in water taste and life charastistics.

According to the invention water is subjected to action of oscillating circuits, ultrasonic, electric, electromagnetic energy, artificial or natural, tellurian, cosmic or else whereby water is imparted with special properties which can be advantageously utilized in various fields.
The combined action of artificial and natural vibration is preferable collected either from an antenna or from the electrical mainline, working as an antenna and filtered and amplified by the system.
Advantageously, high frequency waves and biowave can be concentrated before transmitting the same to the substance to be treated. For such purpose the substance to be treated is preferably submitted to the action of oscillating a filter condenser in which the dielectric is mainly composed of beeswax connected to an antenna and excited by a rotating condenser or by a frequency generating device similar to a pulse accelerator. The biowave thus concentrated by the above process are transmitted to the substance to be treated through the intermediary of an electrodes.
The electrode used is preferably obtained of pure metal, metalloid, crystals or natural or biochemical compounds or combination thereon.
The high frequency waves or biowave are transmitted to water for the media to be treated through material which is in direct contact with the water or the substrate to be treated. Such materials in contact with water / media to be treated can be preferably selected from sand, silica, activated carbon or other natural artificial compound. Importantly, moving water into the filtering media such as the energize activated carbon increased considerably the dynamising capacity of the treated media and in the process avoid its unwanted contamination.
The system for the generation of biowave and treatment of water in accordance with invention as apparent from the above basically involves the water to be treated to be filled by inlet into a vessels containing the activated carbon. One or several electrodes preferably pure metal rods are maintained plunged into the activated carbon. The electrode is connected to a filter condenser preferably composed of two multi blade sections separated by a space filled mainly with natural beeswax as a

dielectric. This condenser is further operatively connected to a second condenser or a series of condenser which can be driven by a motor or any revolving condenser. An equipment or electronic device adapted to produce wide range of high frequencies or quartz pistons could be used in place of the rotating condenser. Wide range of harmonics further produce which amplifies selectively grass harmonic produced by the beeswax condenser. The connection of the system to the electrical grid is made in such a way that the electrical mainline works as an antenna with a screw driver tester lighting when the right phase is connected. Importantly the substance to be treated should never in contact with metals other than the electrodes.
The system of the invention thus is adopted to generate high frequency waves or biowave which are transmitted to the water and the mass of activated carbon in contact with water by means of electrodes. Such high frequency waves generated by the system which are preferably of the order of about 1040 hertz, display on the oscilloscope, numerous high frequency impulsions which super impose onto the usual curve, a considerable number of harmonics known as "GRASS HARMONICS"
