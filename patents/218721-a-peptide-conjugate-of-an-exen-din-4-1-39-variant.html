Title of Invention

A PEPTIDE CONJUGATE OF AN EXEN DIN-4(1-39) VARIANT
Abstract A peptide conjugate of an exendin-4 (1-39)variant comprising a deletion of between 1 and 5 amino acid residues at positions corresponding to positions 34-38 of exendin-4, wherein the exendin-4 variant is couples via its C-terminus to an amino acid sequence Z, where Z comprises from 1 to 7 Lys amino acid residues.
