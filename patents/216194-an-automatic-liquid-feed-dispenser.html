Title of Invention

AN AUTOMATIC LIQUID FEED DISPENSER
Abstract This invention relates to an automatic liquid feed dispenser characterized in that a tank (1), liquid feed dispenser (2) secured therewith at the bottom surface, inside tank (1), an air carrying tube (4) being provided periphery of the liquid feed dispenser (2), such that to be connected with feed reservoir (6) provided above said dispenser (2), a feed carry tube (7) connected to the bottom end of the said reservoir (6) being provided such that to supply the feed into said reservoir (6) for supplying the same into the tank (1), a sight glass (9) is provided at one side of said reservoir (6) for the observation of the level of the liquid feed contained in the reservoir (6) and a drain pipe is provided at the bottom end of said reservoir for cleaning purposes as and when required.
Full Text This invention relates to an automatic liquid feed dispenser- An automatic feed dispenser may be used as an automatic feeding utensil for animals and birds.
The animals and birds drink/eat the water/liquid feed from a container and which has to be filled with the water/liquid feed manually which is a very cumbersome job and the user has to remain vigilant whole day and niqht so as to provide water/liquid feed to the animals-
Therefore the main object of this invention is to provide an automatic liquid feed dispenser which obviates the disadvantages associated with the prior art.
Anotfier object of this invention is to provide an automatic liquid feed dispenser for use for feeding animals/birds.
Still another object of this invention is to provide an automatic liquid feed dispenser which is maintenance free and no operator is required day and night to keep the device in operation.

According to this invention there is provided an automatic liquid feed dispenser characterized in that a tank, liquid feed dispenser secured therewith at the bottom surface, inside tank, an air carrying tube being provided periphery of the liquid feed dispenser, such that to be connected with feed reservoir provided above said dispenser, a feed carry tube connected to the bottom end of the said reservoir being provided such that to supply the feed into said reservoir for supplying the same into the tank, a sight glass is provided at one side of said reservoir for the observation of the level of the liquid feed contained in the reservoir and a drain pipe is provided at the bottom end of said reservoir for cleaning purposes as and when required.
In accordance with this invention an automatic liquid feed dispenser has a feed tank having a vessel secured therein at the bottom surface thereof. An air carrying tube secured at the top end of the vessel is secured at the top end of a reservoir provided for containing the

liquid feed material therein and for supplying the air into said reservoir during the filling operation of said tank with the liquid feed. A liquid feed tube secured with the bottom end of the feed reservoir is secured at the top end of the vessel such that to supply the liquid feed material into the tank as and when required automatically. Control valves are provided with the feed supplying tube and air carrying tube for controllinq the amount of supply. A sight glass is provided at one side of the reservoir to observe the level of the liquid feed contained in the reservoir. An air tight lid is provided at the inlet of said reservoir provided at the top end thereof. A drain pipe is secured at the bottom end of said reservoir for cleaning the reservoir as and when required. The reservoir is kept on a stand to keep the reservoir above the height of the liquid feed dispenser.
An automatic liquid feed dispenser according to a preferred embodiment is herein described and illustrated in the accompanying drawings wherein Fig- 1 shows the plan view of the liquid feed dispenser.

I CLAIM:
1. An automatic liquid feed dispenser characterized in that a tank (1), liquid feed dispenser (2) secured therewith at the bottom surface, inside tank (1), an air carrying tube (4) being provided periphery of the liquid feed dispenser (2), such that to be connected with feed reservoir (5) provided above said dispenser (2), a feed carry tube (7) connected to the bottom end of the said reservoir (6) being provided such that to supply the feed into said reservoir (6) for supplying the same into the tank (1), a sight glass (9) is provided at one side of said reservoir (6) for the observation of the level of the liquid feed contained in the reservoir (6) and a drain pipe is provided at the bottom end of said reservoir for cleaning purposes as and when required.
2. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein an air supplying channels (4) are provided at the periphery of said reservoir (6) such that to supply air into said reservoir (6) when the level of the liquid feed goes down from the openings of said channels.
3. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein said air carrying tube and feed supplying tube (7) are connected between said vessel and said reservoir by means of joints Jl, J2, J3, J4 provided near said vessel and reservoir.
4. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein a control valve (8) is provided with feed supplying tube (6) for controlling the supply of feed into said tank (1).

5. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein an air tight lid (11) is provided at the top end of said reservoir (6) for supplying the feed into said reservoir (6).
6. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein an elivation (13) is provided at the bottom of said reservoir so as to stop the storage of liquid feed therein.
7. An automatic liquid feed dispenser as claimed in claim 1 wherein said reservoir is placed on a stand (12) so as to keep said reservoir (6) at a height and above said vessel.
8. An automatic liquid feed dispenser substantially as herein described and illustrated.
