Title of Invention

MOBILE RICE PLANTING MACHINE
Abstract The invention provides for a mobile rice transplanting machine for transplanting rice seedlings in the fields that provides for a saving in time and also addresses the problem of labor shortage and in-efficient production, especially during peak season.
Full Text This invention relates to a mobile rice planting machine for planting any type of rice seedlings in the field.
BACKGROUNB
The transplant of the rice seedling is normally done manually which consumes considerable time. For instance, 14 labours can transplant only 1 acre of seedling in a day. Furthermore, during peak season there is labour shortage and it is difficult to transplant rice seedling resulting in less production.
The object of this invention is to transplant rice seedling in the field during the peak season faster and with less expenditure.
To achieve the said objective this invention provides a mobile rice transplanting machine for transplanting rice seedlings in the fields which comprises an engine for generating power, the said engine is coupled to the transmission system and the planting mechanism and are mounted on a frame having wheels, characterized in that the said planting mechanism consists of a seedling boxes containing number of seedling per hill, the seedling pusher means for pushing the rice seedlings in the boxes to the guiding grooves, adjusting mechanism for pushing the number of seedling per hill through the said guide grooves, finger means for planting seedlings in the soil and means for adjusting the depth for rice seedlings in the soil.
The engine provided in the mobile rice transplanting machine is preferably gasoline engine 4 H,P. at 3000 RPM.
The transmission system consist of a clutch coupled to reduction gear and speed change gear and transmission case. The clutch is simple belt driven and the gear boxes are fitted with high quality gears, shafts and bearings. The planting mechanism is driven through the transmission case of transmission system for planting the rice seedlings in the field.

The means for adjusting the depth consists of a float having adjusting lever connected to depth adjusting bolt, a check and set pin for setting depth adjusting pin. The said float is made of long flat piece of timber wood chemically treated to stand water and insects' attacks.
The seedling pusher means for pushing the rice seedling consists of a rocking arm connected to adjusting screw and pusher bar and the means for pushing the seedling is connected to the said pusher bar.
An adjusting mechanism for pushing the number of seedling per hill placed in the seedling box consists of an installation plate, adjusting bolt and nut and setting finger and a screen and bush.

Working of the Machine
The drive from the engine (1) is transmitted to the speed change gear box (4) through the belt drive clutch (3). The speed change gear box (4) provides two speeds for the machine i) Traveling Speed ii) Working Speed. The drive to the front wheel (10) is transmitted through the reduction gear box (11), and gear box (4). Drive to the planting apparatus (C) is transmitted through the transmission case which is driven by propeller shaft^from speed change gear box (4).
The rice seedlings are kept by hill in the seedling boxes (SB) and are pushed by seedling pushers shown in figure 4, through the guide grooves to ensure the planting of set number of

rice seedlings. The mechanism for adjusting the number of rice seedlings per hill & depth of planting in the soil, distance between bill and position angle of planting is shown in figure 5. The whole operation of rice seedling planting is controlled by engaging a lever fitted to transmission case. In figure 6 planting operation is shown. The machine enters to the paddy field without performing planting work through 'El' and enters the planting area 'E2'. The said machine turns through turning area 'E3' without performing planting work. The machine exits the paddy field after finishing the work through exit 'E5'.
