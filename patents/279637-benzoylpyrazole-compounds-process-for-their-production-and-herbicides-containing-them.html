TECHNICAL FIELD

DISCLOSURE OF THE INVENTION
OBJECT TO BE ACCOMPLISHED BY THE INVENTION
Heretofore, herbicides which have excellent herbicidal activities against weeds and which are safe to crop plants, have been desired for labor saving in the operation of controlling weeds and for improvement of productivity of agricultural and horticultural plants. In development of new herbicides in future, it is desired to develop compounds capable of exhibiting desired herbicidal activities while their dosages are controlled to be low. Further, it is desired to develop compounds
