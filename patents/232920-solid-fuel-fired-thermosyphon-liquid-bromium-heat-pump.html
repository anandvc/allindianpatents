Title of Invention

SOLID FUEL FIRED THERMOSYPHON LIQUID BROMIUM HEAT PUMP
Abstract There is proposed to be a solid fuel fired thermosyphon on vapour absorption machine comprising: a) a solid fuel combustor for burning solid fuel b) a thermosyphon unit housed within the solid fuel combustor and having a heat exchanger with fluid supply connections c) a vapour absorption unit connected to said heat exchanger in said thermosyphon and held at a level above said thermosyphon d) a return pipe unit connecting said vapour absorption machin and said exchanger for transfer of the heat depleted heat exchange fluid of the said heat exchanger from the vapour absorption machine to the heat exchanger, and e) an exhaust gas processing unit and attached to the solid fuel combustor.
