Title of Invention

"A DEVICE TO PREVENT DRAINAGE OF EXCRETION (WASTE) ON RAILWAY TRACK"
Abstract A device to prevent drainage of excretion (waste) on railway tracks. Characterised in that a hemispherical top cover with a vent pipe, which is to be joined to toilet drainage pipe of the train, a circular concave plate is provided with a plurality of blades thereunder, held on a bearing arrangement which is mounted on a bottom stand and the said stand is attached to the hemispherical top cover.
Full Text Field of Invention:-
This invention relates to a device to prevent drainage of excretion (waste) on railway track and make platforms look neat & clean and create hygienic conditions on railway station.

In accordance with this inventions it is a device to prevent drainage of excretion (waste) on railway track. This device is an arrangement consists of a hemispherical top cover provided with a banded pipe which is to be joined to toilet drainage pipe provided with, a vent for escape of bad air and a concave circular plate with blades under it, held on a bearing arrangement. The bearing arrangement is placed on the top cover.
When the train is standstill in a station the waste from the toilet is directed through the banded pipe on to the concave circular plate. As the train start moving and attains a certain speed the plate get start rotating with the passage of high speed air. The waste accumulated on the plate get spilled on to the inner side of hemispherical top cover (fig.2) due to centrifugal action of plate rotation. The wet material dryup there and fall down on the earth. The arrangement will work in either direction of train movement.
Presently such waste from railway compartments is being allowed to fall directly on the track is causing very tedious awkward situation for the passengers waiting on the platforms. Involving very tedious job in cleaning the tracks in railway stations with a lot of wastage of water for cleaning. Causing unhygienic conditions in railway stations as the food items are sold and also passengers have to have these food items on the same platforms. Involving high amounts of money in keeping cleanly atmosphere in stations. With the proposed system the waste will not be drained directly on the track, so it will not be visible at all.
Hence track will remain clean from this waste, and will not cause any inconvenience to passengers in the railway stations. No cleaning effort are require since no waste is present on the track and eliminates wastage of water which is presently being used for cleaning the tracks. This system will improve hygienic conditions in railway stations. The proposed system is very simple arrangement and can easily be installed and does not require any major modifications in the existing arrangement. And once the system is installed no servicing and maintenance is require.
