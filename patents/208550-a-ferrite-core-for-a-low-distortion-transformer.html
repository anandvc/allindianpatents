Title of Invention

A FERRITE CORE FOR A LOW-DISTORTION TRANSFORMER.
Abstract S FOR TRANSFORMERS, HAS A MIDDLE BLEB (MB)WITH AN OVAL CRISS-SECTION OR FLATTENED OVAL CROSS-SECTION, WHEREBY THE LONGITUDINAL AXIS (L)OF THE MIDDLE BLEB (MB) IS ORIENTED PARALLEL TO THE ATTACHMENT PLANE (BE) OF THE TRANSFORMER AND THE LONGEST AXIS OF THE OVAL CROSS-SECTION OR FLATTENED OVAL CROSS-SECTION IS ORIENTED VERTICALLY TO THIS ATTACHMENT PLANE (BE). THE CORE IS SYMMETRICALLY STRUCTURED WITH RESPECT TO A MIRROR PLANE(SE), WHICH CONTAINS THE LONGITUDINAL AXIS (L)OF THE FERRITE CORE (FK) AND WHICH RESIDES VERTICALLY TO THE ATTACHMENT PLANE (BE), AND IS PARTICULARLY LOW IN DISTORTION.
