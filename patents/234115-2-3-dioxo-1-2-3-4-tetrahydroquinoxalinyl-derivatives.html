Title of Invention

2,3-DIOXO-1,2,3,4-TETRAHYDROQUINOXALINYL DERIVATIVES
Abstract The present invention relates to 2,3-dioxo-l,2,3,4-tetrahydroquinoxalinyl derivative. The pharmaceutical composition comprising the compound of the present invention are suitable for the prophylatic and therapeutic treatment of pathological conditions that are responsive to blocking of AMP A Kainate and/or glycine binding sites of the NMDA regulator.
