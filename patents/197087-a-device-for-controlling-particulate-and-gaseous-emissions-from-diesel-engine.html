Title of Invention

"A DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING PARTICULATE AND GASEOUS EMISSIONS FROM DIESEL ENGINE"
Abstract This invention relates to a device for controlling particulate and gaseous emissions from diesel engines. The device has a container for containing a scrubbing liquid therein. An inlet pipe adapted to be connected to the exhaust of the engine is connected to said container, A sparger is connected to the bottom end of the body such that to be submerged into the scrubbing liquid. A plurality of chevron plates disposed spacedly within the top end of the body are provided to remove the scrubbing liquid particles from the emissions and to produce artificial rain within said body. An outlet chamber is provided at the top end to the body for facilitating the exit of the exhaust gases from the said container. Water make-up means are provided with said container to maintain the level of the scrubbing liquid in the container.
Full Text This invention relates to a device for controlling particulate and gaseous emissions from diesel engine.
Diesel engines emit a lot of particulate matters and hazardous gases as the exhaust of the vehicle. The main disadvantages of vehicle emissions having particulate matter are as follows:
1. It contains carcinogenics;
2. It causes lung congestions;
3. It causes reduction in visibility; and
4. It affects nervous system.
There are various techniques for controlling the particulate matter in the exhaust from diesel engines like modifications in the engine, use of alternative fuels etc. However, these techniques are expensive to be useful practically for Indian low cost diesel engines.
Therefore, the main object of the present invention is to provide a device to control particulate and hazardous gaseous emissions from diesel engines.
Another object of this invention is to provide a device which is of low weight and is small in size and which does not affect the diesel engine at all.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a device which is of low cost and maintenance thereof is very easy.
Still another object of this invention is to propose a device which can be used as the silencer and reduces the noise from the engine.
A further object of this invention is to propose a device which reduces gaseous oxides of sulpher and nitrogen from exhaust of the engine.
According to this invention, there is provided a device for controlling particulate and gaseous emissions from diesel engine comprising: a container (1) for containing a scrubbing liquid selected from water or a mixture of water and lime, an inlet pipe (2) connected to the said container adapted to be connected with the exhaust pipe of the engine, a sparger (3) secured to the bottom end of a body (4) lower end of which is contained within the said container such that the said sparger is submerged in the said scrubbing liquid, a plurality of chevron plates (5) comprising zig-zag plates disposed spacedly within the said compartment, a .baffle plate (9) provided at the top end of the said compartment, an outlet chamber (6) secured at the top end of the said compartment, a cover (7) with an outlet (8) mounted on the top of the said outlet chamber to facilitate the exit of exhaust gases from the said container body, and water make up means provided to maintain level of scrubbing liquid in the said container.
