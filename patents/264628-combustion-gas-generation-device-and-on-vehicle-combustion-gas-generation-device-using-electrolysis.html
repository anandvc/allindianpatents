Title of Invention

“COMBUSTION GAS GENERATION DEVICE AND ON-VEHICLE COMBUSTION GAS GENERATION DEVICE USING ELECTROLYSIS”
Abstract It is possible to effectively generate a large amount of a gas mixture of hydrogen and oxygen for a long time. A positive electrode and a negative electrode are immersed in an electrolytic cell filled with electrolytic liquid and DC alternating voltage is applied across the both electrodes. A plurality of intermediate electrodes are arranged between the both electrodes. The electrolytic cell is sealed by a sealing cover. The gas mixture of hydrogen and oxygen generated by electrolysis is extracted from an exhaust opening arranged in the sealing cover.
