Title of Invention

A GARDEN CHAIR
Abstract THE SITTING ARRANGEMENT (1) MAY BE A CHAIR (2) OR A BENCH LIKE CONSTRUCTION (3). THE BACK REST (4) OF WHICH IS EXTENDED UPWARDS END PROVIDED WITH A HOODLIKE CONTRAPTION (5) WHICH MAY BE A FIXED STRUCTURE OR MAY BE HINGED OR MAY BE PROVIDED IN SUCH A MANNER THAT IT IS FLEXIBLE AND ITS ANGLE CAN BE VERIED AS PER REQUIREMENT. THE HOED PORTION COMPRISES MODULE (6) LOCATED AT THE DORSAL SIDE OF THE SAID HOOD (5), THE ELECTRICITY THUS GENERATED WILL BE STORED AND RECHARGEABLE BATTERIES (8) PLACED UNDERNEATH OR VENTRAL SIDE (9) OF THE SAID HOOD (5). THE ENERGY THUS STORED WILL BE OF D.C, TYPE AND CAN BE DIRECTLY CONNECTED TO THE D.C. BULB OR BULBS.
