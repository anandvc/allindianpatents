FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to production of charcoal by using coconut shell. This charcoal plant is unique, for ft is only of its kind for producing charcoal without air pollution. More particularly this invention relates to charcoal production without affecting the environment i.e,. plants, animals or human beings.
OBJECT OF INVENTION:
This invention is to control and to avoid pollution and polluting the air, the ozone layers
during the production of charcoal.
NOVELTY:
When charcoal is produced the
environment get polluted leading !o various problems, !n my novelty the polluting smoke
is burnt and the environmental pollution is controlled.
SUMMARY:
For decades, the production of charcoal Is by way of using coconut sheil. while converting the coconut shell into charcoal, 2/3 of its weight goes out as the white fumes (smoke) and 1/3 of it weight obtained as charcoal. The smoke that comes out pollute the environment in large scale by way of deposition of smoke over the leaves, plants which contains oily ingredients while inhaling such smoke mixed in the air causes various breathing problems with the humans and animals.
The Inventor has made with great effort to provide a method which meets the requirements of the Industry and over come the problems associated in the prior art.
The prior art is nothing but, mixing the smoke directly in the air which pollutes the environment & atmosphere at great levels.
