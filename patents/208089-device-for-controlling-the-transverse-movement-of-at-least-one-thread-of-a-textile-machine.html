Title of Invention

DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING THE TRANSVERSE MOVEMENT OF AT LEAST ONE THREAD OF A TEXTILE MACHINE
Abstract controlling the transverse movement of at least one thread of a textile machine" The invention includes a driver part (2) for driving a thread (4) which is to be moved transversely and is clamped on both sides in a frame (10) by means of springs (6, 8). The device forms a system vibrating freely at the natural frequency. Holding devices (12, 14) serve to hold the driver part in the extreme positions temporarily in an adjustable fashion.
