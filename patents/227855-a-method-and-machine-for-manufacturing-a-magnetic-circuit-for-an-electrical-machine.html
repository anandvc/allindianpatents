Title of Invention

A METHOD AND MACHINE FOR MANUFACTURING A MAGNETIC CIRCUIT FOR AN ELECTRICAL MACHINE
Abstract A method of manufacturing a magnetic circuit for an electrical machine, the method comprising: using a cutting station (112) to cut out at least one strip (12) of sectors (20) that are interconnected by deformable connections (16); and using a winding station (200) to wind the strip of sectors around a mandrel (201); the method being characterized by the face that between the cutting station and the winding station, there is a zone (300) configured to enable the strip to deform so as to strip at the outlet from the cutting station and its speed at the inlet to the winding station.
