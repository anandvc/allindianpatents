Title of Invention

N-(INDOLECARBONYL) PIPERAZINE DERIVATIVES.
Abstract The invention relates to compounds of formula (I) in which R1, R2, R4and R5 have the meanings indicated in Claim 1, are potent 5-HT2A-antagonists and are suitable for the treatment of psychosis, schizophrenia, depression, neurological disorders, memory disorders, Parkinson's disease, amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, Alzheimer's disease, Huntington's disease, eating disorders, e.g. nervous bulimia and anorexia, and premenstrual syndrome and/or for positively influencing compulsive behaviours (obsessive-compulsive disorder, OCD).
