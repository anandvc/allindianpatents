Title of Invention

GENE 62 OF OKA VACCINE VIRUS
Abstract The present application provides the gene 62 of Oka vaccine virus which comprises at least with the following base substitutions&nbsp;(a) to (d) in the nucleotide sequence of SEQ ID No.1: (a) the base A at position 2110 is G; (b) the base A at position 3100 is G; (c) the base T at position 3818 is C; and (d) the base A at position 4006 is G; and a method for identifying the Oka vaccine virus and an attenuated varicella-zoster virus strain acceptable as virus strain for live attenuated varicella vaccine by using the gene 62.
