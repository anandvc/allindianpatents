Title of Invention

A PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION SUITABLE FOR ORAL ADMINISTRATION
Abstract A Pharmaceutical composition suitable for oral administration, in form of an emulsion pre-concentrate, comprising i)one or more NO-releasing NSAID (S); ii)one or more surfactants; iii)optionally an oil or semi-solid far; said composition forming an in-situ oil in water emulsion upon contact with aqueous media such as gastroinestinal fluids.
