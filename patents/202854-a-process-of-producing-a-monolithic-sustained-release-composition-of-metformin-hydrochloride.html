Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF PRODUCING A MONOLITHIC SUSTAINED RELEASE COMPOSITION OF METFORMIN HYDROCHLORIDE
Abstract Monolithic pharmaceutical composition containing metforming hydrophobic polymer and/or other hydrophobic material process of producing a sustained release of the composition that includes granulating metformin hydrochloride and hydrophobic polymer and/or other hydrophobic material by hot melt granulation or by extrusion and drying the granulated product.
