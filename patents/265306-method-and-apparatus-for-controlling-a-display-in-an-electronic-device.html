Title of Invention

METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR CONTROLLING A DISPLAY IN AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE”
Abstract A novel method and apparatus for controlling a display of an electronic device is provided in an embodiment the device is a portable electronic device with the combined features of wireless email and telephony. The portable electronic device also includes a camera.The device includes a trackball which can be used to control the image shown in the viewfinder when the device is being used as a camera
