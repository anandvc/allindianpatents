Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A QUICK COOKING RICE
Abstract A novel quick cooking rice with unique properties is obtained by subjecting a rice to a heat treatment in a hot gas stream while being in an annular, fluidised, swirling, preferably toroidal bed with horizontal and vertical velocity components
