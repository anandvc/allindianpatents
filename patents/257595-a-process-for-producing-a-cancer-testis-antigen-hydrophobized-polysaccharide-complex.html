Title of Invention

“A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING A CANCER-TESTIS ANTIGEN-HYDROPHOBIZED POLYSACCHARIDE COMPLEX”
Abstract Disclosed is a process for the production of an encapsulated protein antigen, which comprises the step of encapsulating a protein antigen having two or more cysteine residues in the molecule in a hydrophobic polysaccharide. The protein antigen is purified before or after the encapsulation of the protein antigen in the hydrophobic polysaccharide. Also disclosed is an encapsulated protein antigen characterized in that the protein antigen can be purified properly while reducing the denaturation of the antigen as much as possible.
