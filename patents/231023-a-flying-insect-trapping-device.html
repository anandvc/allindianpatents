Title of Invention

"A FLYING INSECT TRAPPING DEVICE"
Abstract This invention relates to a flying insects/mosquito trap. The insects/mosquito has a chamber having a plurality of transparent flaps. The transparent flaps are provided in the middle of the sides of the chamber such that to be opened into the chamber upon switching on the induced draft fan. The induced draft fan is provided at the bottom end of the chamber. A mosquito net is provided near the bottom end so as to retain the trapped mosquitoes inside the chamber. The violet lamps are provided within the chamber near the transparent flaps to attract the mosquitoes. An openable top cover is provided on the other end of the chamber for releasing the trapped mosquitoes in the outside environment.
