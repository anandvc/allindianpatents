Title of Invention

NON-INVASIVE ULTRASONIC BODY CONTOURING
Abstract A methodology and system for lysing adipose tissue including directing ultrasonic energy (10) at a multiplicity of target volumes (12) within the region, which target volumes (12) contain adipose tissue, thereby to selectively lyse the adipose tissue in the target volumes (12) and generally not lyse non-adipose tissue in the target volumes (12) and computerized tracking (44) of the multiplicity of target volumes (12) notwithstanding movement of the body.
