Title of Invention

TRANSMISSION OF SPEECH INFORMATION IN A PACKET ORIENTED NETWORK
Abstract Mobile terminal for a packet-oriented network, particularly UMTS, which transmits voice information on a conncetion-oriented basis as standard, having a module, which can preferably be installed later, which intercepts the voice information before it is transmitted on a connection-oriented basis in order to break it down into packets which are then transmitted on a packet-oriented basis.
