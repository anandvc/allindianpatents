Title of Invention

A DEVICE OF INDEPENDENT ROTARY DRIVE (IRD) SYSTEM FOR SKYTOP MODEL MAST & SUBSTRUCTURE
Abstract In order to install IRD system in skytop type mast and substructure, the substructure configure was redesigned keeping the mast identical to accommodate the single skid IRD system. The whole structure was analysed on STAAD S/W and integrity of mast and substructure was checked. A single skid independent Rotary drive (Single speed. Horizontal split gearbox) was developed to suit this redesigned skytop model Mast and substructure. It consist of a Motor coupled with a single speed reduction gear box which is connected to rotary through chain coupling. All the three are mounted on a single skid thereby avoiding dismantling and assembly of different components during every rig move.

FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The invention relates to a device of Independent Rotary drive system (IRD) for
sky top model mast and substructure to reduce wear and tear of draw works.
BACLKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
In oil rigging operation, generally the draw works power is used for supporting
the drill pipe and also giving the torques to the drilling stem is provided by a
rotary table that gets drives from draw works. This has a disadvantage as the
draw works has to run all through the drilling operation there by the life of the
bearing, shafts and other supporting equipment gets drastically reduced.
To overcome this practical operational hazard; an independent rotary drive (IRD)
system is being developed and installed in branham type of mast and
substructure.
This IRD system can not be installed in sky top type of mast and substructure
due to limitation of working space between welll center and the mast.
Hence, a device of an Independent Rotary Drive system for sky top type of mast
and substructure was developed to address the above problem to reduce wear
and tear of draw works.

OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
It is therefore, an object of the present invention to propose a device of
inpendent rotary drive system for sky top model mast and substructure which
eliminates the disadvantages of prior Art.
Another object of the present invention is to propose a device of Independent
Rotary Drive System for sky top model mast and substructure which constitutes
an integrated assemble of independent rotary Drive System to generate torque
to drill stem.
A further object of the present invention is to propose a device of independent
rotary drive system for sky top model mask and substructure which increased
the life of the bearing, shafts and other supporting equipments.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
In order to install IRD system in skytop type mast and substructure, the
substructure configure was redesigned keeping the mast identical to
accommodate the single skid IRD system. The whole structure was analysed on
STAAD S/W and integrity of mast and substructure was checked. A single skid
independent Rotary drive (Single speed. Horizontal split gearbox) was developed
to suit this redesigned skytop model Mast and substructure. It consist of a Motor
coupled with a single speed reduction gear box which is connected to rotary
through chain coupling. All the three are mounted on a single skid thereby
avoiding dismantling and assembly of different components during every rig
move.

DETAILS DESCRIPTION OF THE PREFERRED ENBODIMENT OF THE
INVENTION
As shown in Fig - 1 A drill floor plan of redesigned drill rig for supporting the
drilling machinery and drilling loads and to accommodate IRD unit comprising a
set back speader (1), it transfers set back load of 250 Ton to the ground through
the casing set back columns. In order to accommodate the IRD (2) unit, the
front beam (A) of set back was redesigned considering of the load capacity; it
also provides a front floor support Box (3) which is a critical component of
substructure which transfers set back load and loads from A frame to the ground
through columns. The inside beam (B) was connected to strong back beam in
prior art. For installation of IRD unit. Supporting beam (B) and supporting
sheave (C) was modified.
The assembly also provides a Rotary Beam (4) which accommodates a Rotary
table, being a rugged machine to rotate drill stem and supports its weight. This
also transfers horizontal loads of set back to strong back beam (6). This was
also redesigned to accommodate IRD unit.

5
A Rotary floor spreader (5) (off driller side) which gives support to rotary floor
and also takes IRD unit load by proper setting of position.
The assembly also includes a strong back beam (6) which is main component of
substructure which transfers casing loads, draw work's load and lateral loads of
set back and Draw works. Present invention modified this beam to suit redesign
rotary beam, front floor support box and rotary spreader.
As shown in Fig - 2, the assembly comprising a IRD skid (7) which is a rugged
structure to support Rotary table (8) and IRD component like gearbox (9) motor
(10) clutch (11) and brake (12). The IRD components comprises a motor (10)
which is an independent motor (10) which gives the drilling torque to rotary
through gearbox (9). The gear box (9) having a single speed reduction gear
which increases to drilling torque by reducing the drilling speed. A flexible
coupling (12) transfer the drives from motor (10) to gear box (9). A chain
coupling (13) is attached to the gear box (9), transfer the drive from gear box
(9) to rotary (14). In case of failure of IRD unit, it transfer drive drawn from
draw works to rotary table through an emergency sprocket. An Air flex clutch
(11) is also attached in is conjunction of motor and motor coupling adapter
which is a balloon type air operated brake to stop IRD system.
