Title of Invention

"A BODY STRUCTURE OF VEHICLE"
Abstract A body structure of a vehicle (10) comprising, a body cover (23) disposed on the lower side of a tandem seat (22), and passenger steps (37) for the passenger seated on a rear portion of said tandem seat to put his feet on provided on the lower side of said body cover (23), characterized in that recessed portions (23a) are provided at side surfaces of said body cover (23) on the upper side of said passenger steps (37).
Full Text [Detailed Description of the Invention] [0001] [Technical Field of the Invention]
The present invention relates particularly to a vehicle body structure of a vehicle such that the passenger can ride thereon comfortably. [0002] [Prior Art]
As a vehicle body structure of a vehicle, there has been known, for example, the one described in Japanese Patent Laid-open No. Hei 6-48343 "Motor Scooter Type Vehicle".
In Figs. 2 and 3 of the above publication, there is shown a vehicle in which auxiliary foot rests 96, 96 (the symbols used in the publication are used here and hereinafter) are provided at side surfaces of a rear
cover 94 covering side portions of a rear portion of the vehicle body, and recessed portions 114, 114 are provided respectively along the auxiliary foot rests 96, 96 in the vicinity of the auxiliary foot rests 96, 96.
By the presence of the recessed portion 114, the gap between the recessed portion 114 and a power unit 32 on the inside of the rear cover 94 is reduced, resulting in that hot air from an exhaust pipe 44 would not easily enter into the rear cover 94.
[0003]
[Problems to Be Solved by the Invention]
In the running of a vehicle, for example, where it is necessary to run at a low speed in a city street in a summery season, the running airflow may not sufficiently come into contact with the riders and the riders may feel unpleasant. This is more so particularly for the passenger seated on the rear seat. In such a case, the unpleasant feeling can be reduced if the running airflow can be caused to come into contact with the body of the passenger as efficiently as possible by utilizing a part of the vehicle body.
The position of the recessed portion 114 of the vehicle as mentioned above is located roughly at the same height as the auxiliary foot rest 96, just corresponding
to the position of the shoe of the passenger. Therefore, it is difficult to control the running airflow so as to bring the airflow into contact with the body of the passenger.
[0004]
In view of the above, it is an object of the present invention to improve the vehicle body structure of a vehicle and thereby to enable particularly the passenger to ride comfortably.
[0005]
[Means for Solving the Problems]
In order to attain the above object, claim 1 is characterized in that, in a vehicle comprising a body cover disposed on the lower side of a tandem seat and passenger steps for the passenger seated on a rear portion of the tandem seat to put his feet on provided on the lower side of the body cover, recessed portions are provided at side surfaces of the body cover on the upper side of the passenger steps.
[0006]
With the recessed portions provided at side surfaces of the body cover on the upper side of the passenger steps, airflows can be passed through the recessed portions, and the airflows can be sufficiently
brought into contact with the inside of the legs of the passenger seated on the rear portion of the tandem seat, so that the passenger can feel cool and can ride comfortably. [0007]
Claim 2 is characterized in that the recessed portions have a triangular shape with a width reduced toward the rearwardly upward side in side view.
With the recessed portions having a triangular shape with a width reduced toward the rearwardly upward side in side view, the appearance property of the body cover can be more emphasized, and a sharp impression can be provided. [0008]
Claim 3 is characterized in that rearmost portions of the recessed portions are connected to edge lines provided in a rearwardly upward form on the body cover.
Since the triangular recessed portions are connected naturally to the edge lines and the edge lines set off the recessed portions to advantage, the appearance property of the body cover can be further enhanced. [0009]
Claim 4 is characterized in that a tail lamp unit
is disposed at a rear end portion of the body cover, and the rear end of the tail lamp unit is formed in a V shape with a width reduced toward the rearwardly upward side.
[0010]
It is possible to respectively emphasize the V-shaped rear ends of the recessed portions and the V-shaped rear end of the tail lamp unit, and to harmonize the V-shaped rear ends of the recessed portions with the V-shaped rear end of the tail lamp unit, so that the appearance property of the side surfaces of the vehicle body can be made to be more attractive.
[0011]
Claim 5 is characterized in that the body cover is provided at its rear end with a triangular portion with a width reduced toward the rearwardly upward side, and the triangular portion is so formed as to lap over bulbs of winkers provided in the tail lamp unit in side view.
[0012]
It is possible to emphasize the triangular portion of the body cover, and to synchronize the triangular portion of the body cover with the V-shaped rear ends of the recessed portions and the V-shaped rear end of the tail lamp unit, so that it is possible to provide a better impression design with the feeling of unity as
side surfaces of the vehicle body. In addition, the bulbs of the winkers can be protected by the triangular portion of the body cover.
