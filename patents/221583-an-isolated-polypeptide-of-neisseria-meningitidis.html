Title of Invention

AN ISOLATED POLYPEPTIDE OF NEISSERIA MENINGITIDIS
Abstract THE INVENTION RELATES TO TREATMENT AND DIAGNOSIS OF BACTERIAL MENINGITIS CAUSED BY NEISSERIA MENINGITIDIS. PROVIDED ARE NOVEL SURFACE POLYPEPTIDES FROM NEISSERIA MENINGITIDIS AS DESCRIBED IN FIG. 7 AND IMMUNOGENIC GRAGMENTS OF THESE POLYPEPTIDES, AS WELL AS ENCODING NUCLIIC ACIDS AS DESCRIBED IN THE SEQUENCE LISTING. ALSO PROVIDED ARE ANTIBODIES WHICH BIND THE POLYPEPTIDES. PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS AND VACCINES CONTAINING THE POLYPEPTIDES AND NUCLEIC ACIDS OF THE INVENTION ARE ALSO DISCLOSED,WHICH ARE USEFUL IN THE TREATMENT OF N. MENINGITIDIS INFECTION. ALSO PROVIDED ARE KITS AND DIAGNOSTIC METHODS THAT USE THE POLYPEPTIDES, IMMUNOGENIC FRAGMENTS, ANTIBODIES TO THE POLYPEPTIDES AND/OR ENCODING NUCLEIC ACIDS.
