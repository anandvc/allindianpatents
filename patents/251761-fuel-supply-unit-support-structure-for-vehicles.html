Title of Invention

FUEL SUPPLY UNIT SUPPORT STRUCTURE FOR VEHICLES
Abstract [Object] To increae the vibration resistance of a fuel supply unit without increasing the number of components in a fuel supply unit support strcture for a vehicle. [Solving Means] A fuel supply unit support structure for a vehicle, having a fuel supply unit 20 mounted on a ceiling wall 15a of a fuel tank 15 and having a fuel pump assembly 31 immersed in a fuel in the fuel tank 43 is mounted on the outer circumference of the fuel pump assembly 31, and the fuel tank 15 has a plurality of support walls 51 through 53 integrally formed therewith which are arranged in the circumferential direction of the fuel pump assembly 31 for supporting thr circumference of the fuel pump assembly 31 through the damper member 43.
