Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A URETHANE TETRAMETHACRYLATE RESIN MATRIX(UTMA)
Abstract A process for the preperation of a radiopaque dental paste comprisisng ther steps of mixing urethane tetramethacrylate (UTMA) with a thinner to obtain a resin mixture, blending 20-40 parts by weight of said resin mixture with 60-80 parts by weights of a filler and a photo initiator to obtain the radiopaquedental paste.
