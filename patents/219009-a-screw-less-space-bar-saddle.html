FIELD OF THE INVENTION:
The present invention relates generally to a space bar saddle, particularly to a screw less space bar saddle. More particularly, the present invention relates to a screw less space bar saddle having unique slide and lock technique of holding the base and the top of the space saddle together without any other fastening means.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION :
Space bar saddles are traditionally used for fixing the elongated members like PVC pipeline on wall or ceiling and to prevent them from falling or sliding hence keeping it fixed at the same position without letting it to loose grip.
Traditional space bar saddle with screws is currently in use for fitting PVC pipes on wall and ceiling. This space bar saddle has many limitations and disadvantages to itself.
Limitations of traditional space bar saddle:
In traditional space bar saddle its very tiring to fix the screws every now and
then.
Some times while tightening the screws of saddle the material breaks and a
saddle with base or top becomes useless, resulting in total wastage of the
product.
Once the saddle is mounted, its become very difficult to mount it the next time, as
the sizes of the holes provided for the screws changes resulting in loose screw fitting.
Hence preventing it from been used for the second time.
2

OBJECT OF THE INVENTION:
It is therefore an object of the invention to obviate the above-mentioned
drawbacks in the prior art.
It is an important object of the invention to provide a screw less space bar
saddle.
It is an object of the invention to provide a better space bar saddle which is
easier to use ,fix and mount without the usage of screws.
It is also an object of the invention to avoid the use of screw drivers during
dismantling of the saddle for maintenance purpose.
It is also an object of the invention to provide a screwless space bar saddle
which comes with a unique 4 way locking system that locks the top and base
of the saddle without the help of any other fastening means. This unique
locking system prevents the top and base to get misplaced amongst
themselves to move either way or even slide down when fixed vertically.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
In order to achieve the above objectives and others, the present invention provides a screw less space bar saddle comprising: -
a top part comprising a curved portion with an "u" shaped cross section defining a space for the elongated object to pass through it and having two outwardly stretching flat portion at both ends and perpendicular to the limbs, two mutually perpendicular ridges emerging from the lower edge of each of the said flat portions,

In another embodiment the grooves in said walls of the base extends from one side to the substantially nearer to the end of other side, thus forming another lock to prevent top portion from slipping or falling in the direction of insertion.

LOCKS:
There are total 4 locks designed especially to prevent any kind of slipping or falling of the top from the base. These locks are technically designed. These locks are named Lock A, B & C visible on front side of the top and A1, B1 & C1 on the front side of the base. The technique is explained in the fig.4.
The fourth lock is designed on the base of the product. This lock is named Lock 'D'. This lock stops the top to slide downward further in the direction of insertion when the product is fixed in vertical position. These locks (front view) are as illustrated in fig.5.

Locks TV &'A1'
When the product is fixed upside down on the ceiling, the primary locks that
comes into act are 'A' & 'AT which are designed to prevent the top from
falling down. The lock A1 acts as stopper when the top is fixed on the base in
upside down position on the ceiling as shown in fig. 10. This lock has been

The secondary locks that comes into act are lock 'B', 'B1\ 'C & 'C1'. These
locks are designed in such a manner that they prevent the top to slide either
way horizontally or vertically when it is fixed on the ceiling.
Method of using the screw less space bar saddle:

Step 2: Hold the top in line with the base

Advantages of screwless space bar saddle
Extremely user friendly and easy to fix as there are no screws involved.
No need to mount screws on the top to keep it fixed with the base thus
saving time and energy.
Top and base of the product can be fixed with just sliding .thus resulting in no
wastage or breakage of material while mounting as there is no usage of
screws to fix the top and base.
4 way locking system that uniquely hold the top and base together
irrespective of any angle in which the product is fixed.
