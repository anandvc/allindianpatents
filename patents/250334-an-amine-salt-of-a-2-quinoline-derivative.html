Title of Invention

AN AMINE SALT OF A 2-QUINOLINE DERIVATIVE
Abstract The invention provides a novel amine salt, composed of specific quinoline derivative [2-(4-chlorobenzoylamino)-3-(2-quinolon-4-yl) propionic acid] and specific amines and the solubility of the salts is unexpectedly higher than that of the base-free compounds. The invention provide good water solubility of the amine salts of 2-quinoline derivative which is useful for the preparation in the form of solution and is used as drugs for treating various diseases, especially as aqueous formulations due to the superior water solubility and the superior pharmacologic effects.
