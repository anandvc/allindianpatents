A BICYCLE AND HANDLE FOR THE SAME
Abstract A bicycle comprising; a handle; an operation body comprising an operating lever detachably fitted to the handle and a wire operated by said operating lever; clamp means fastening and fixing said operating body to said handle ; a cam mechanism portion for opening and closing said clamp means; and positioning means provided in said clamp means so as to position said operating body at a normal use position, characterized in that a tip end of hand is provided with a longitudinal groove (GL) and a circumferential groove (GR) connected to the longitudinal (GL), wherein said grooves (GL,GR) are adapted for engaging the positioning means, and wherein the circumferential groove (GR) extends in the circumferential direction over an angle (a) of about 180Â°.

The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed:


The present invention relates to a bicycle and handle for the same.
[0001]
[Technical Field of the Invention]
The present invention relates to a handle lever of bicycle, and particularly to a handle lever of bicycle which can make it easy to divide a vehicle body frame in the case where the vehicle body frame can be divided into front and rear portions.
[0002]
[Prior Art]
A mo tor -assisted bicycle which comprises a human driving system for transmitting a tread force applied to pedals to a rear wheel and a motor driving system capable of adding auxiliary power to the human driving system according to the tread force has been known. For example, Japanese Patent Laid-open No. Hei 10-45081 discloses a motor -assisted bicycle in which a vehicle body frame divided into front and rear portions can be assembled with bolts or connected by a hinge.
[0003]
[Problems to be Solved by the Invention]
Where a motor - assisted bicycle is divided into front and rear portions as mentioned above, there is the following problem. An operating portion for a rear brake and an operating portion for a speed change gear are provided in a front portion of a vehicle body, and


mechanism portions of the rear brake and the speed change gear are provided in a rear portion of the vehicle, namely, on the rear wheel or in the vicinity of the rear wheel. The operating portions and the mechanism portions are connected by a rear brake wire and a speed change gear wire. Namely, the vehicle body rear portion and the vehicle body front portion are connected by these wires, so that when the vehicle body frame is separated, there is the inconvenience that in order to transport the separated vehicle body frames, the separated vehicle body frames must be moved as one body.
[0004]
In order to dissolve the inconvenience, it may be contemplated to divide the wires and connect them through, for example, a joint using screws. In that case, however, a play may be generated at the joint portion, or structure may be complicated in order to ensure that connection of the joint is not forgotten.
[0005]
In consideration of the problem in the prior art, it is an object of the present invention to provide a handle lever of bicycle which facilitates the division and ensures that divided frames can be separately transported..


[0006]
[Means for Solving the Problems]
In order to attain the above object, the present invention is firstly characterized in that there are provided an operating body comprising an operating lever fitted to a handle, and a wire operated by the operating lever; a clamp means fastening and fixing the operating body to the handle; a cam mechanism portion for opening and closing the clamp means; and a positioning means provided in the clamp means so as to position the operating body at a normal-use position. According to the first characteristic feature, the clamp means can be easily fitted to and detached from the handle by the cam mechanism portion. Particularly, the operating body can be easily positioned at the normal-use position by the positioning means.
[0007]
In addition, the present invention is secondly characterized in that the clamp means is constituted to be fittable and detachable in the longitudinal direction of the handle, at a position where the clamp means is turned by a predetermined angle from the normal-use position of the operating body in the circumferential direction of the handle. According to the second

characteristic feature, at the fitting and detaching position of the clamp means, the operating body is staggered from the normal-use position by the predetermined angle, so that incomplete fitted condition can be easily recognized on an appearance basis.
[0008]
In addition, the present invention is thirdly characterized in that the clamp means comprises a sleeve fitted over the handle, and the cam mechanism portion and a handle grip is integrated with the sleeve. According to the third characteristic feature, the handle grip can be fitted to and detached from the handle as one body with the clamp means.
[0009]
In addition, the present invention is fourthly characterized in that the clamp means comprises split halves openably and closably coupled to each other by a hinge, and an opening portion located on the opposite side of the hinge portion is fastened by the cam mechanism portion, whereby the clamp means is fixed to the handle. According to the fourth characteristic feature, the clamp means constituted to be openable and closable at the hinge portion can be fastened and unfastened by the cam mechanism portion provided on the


opposite.side of the hinge portion, so that the operating body can be easily fitted to and detached from the handle.
[0010]
Further, the present invention is fifthly characterized in that the vehicle body frame comprises a front frame on which the handle is provided, and a rear frame on which a rear wheel is provided; the front frame and the rear frame are separatably connected to each other; and the wire is either one of a wire for operating a rear brake fitted to the rear frame and a speed change gear wire. According to the fifth characteristic feature, when the vehicle body frame is separated into front and rear portions or when the separated vehicle body frames are coupled to each other, the operating body connected to the rear frame by the wire can be easily fitted to and detached from the handle.
