Title of Invention

A SIX SIDED, THREE DIMENSIONAL PUZZLE
Abstract The invention consists of a six sided, three dimensional puzzle, comprising of n3 even sized, six sided blocks, where n is any integer >2. All the sides of the blocks are equal dimension. The blocks are in an assortment of n2 attributes, such that when all the blocks are assembled together to form one large six sided block of equal sides, no attribute is repeated on any one face of the block.
