Title of Invention

"DEVICE FOR BENDING STEEL PLATE FOR A DOOR OF A REGRIGERATOR"
Abstract A device for bending steel plate for a door of a refrigerator having a roller and metals placed to be close to the roller to make the steel plate placed therebetween be bent, wherein there are provided a plurality of metala, slopes with different inclination angles are formed at the sides of the metals facing the roller, and an approaching unit for selectively approaching the metals to the roller is provided to selectively adjust the bending angle of the steel plate.
Full Text DEVICE FOR BENDING STEEL PLATE FOR A DOOR OF A REFRIGERATOR
(1) Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to a device for bending steel plate for a door of a refrigerator, and more particularly to a device for bending ateel plate for a door of a refrigerator which can easily change the bending angle by selectively sticking a plurality of metals with different inclination angles fast to a roller and then bending the ateel plate.
(2) Discussion of Related Art
In general, a door of a refrigerator ie comprised of a steel plate, an inner case and an insulating material filling therebetween. Both ends of the steel plate is bent to inside and the inner case is combined to the end part of the steel plate.
Fig. 3 is a diagram showing a conventional bending device for bending the both ends of a steel plate. Ae shown in Fig. 3, a door steel plate 1 is provided bet%*e*n a clamp 2 and a roller 3 to be pressed and bent. A slope 2a which ie inclined by a predetermined angle is formed at one side of the clamp 2 facing the roller 3 to make the door steel plate 1 be bent along the slope 2a. The door steel plate 1 inserted between the clamp 2 and the rcller 3 is bent along the slope 2a at the rotation of the roller 3.
However, such a conventional bending device has a problem that it is difficult to change the bending angle of the door steel plate because the clamp must be replaced to change the
bending angle.
Accordingly, the present invention is directed to a device for bending steel plate for a door of a refrigerator that substantially obviates one or more of the problems due to limitations and disadvantages of the related art.
An object of the preaent invention is to provide a device for bending steel plate for a door of a refrigerator which can easily change the bending angle of the steel plate.
Additional features and advantages of the invention will be set forth in the description which follows, and in part will be apparent from the description, or may be learned by practice of the invention. The objectives and other advantages of the invention will be realized and attained by the structure particularly pointed out in the written description and claims hereof as well as the appended drawings .
To achieve these and other advantages and in accordance with the purpose of the present invention, as embodied and broadly described, a device for bending steel plate for a door of a refrigerator having a roller and metals placed to be close to the roller to make the door steel plate placed therebetween be bent, is characterized in that there are provided a plurality of metals, slopes with different inclination angles are formed at the sides of the metals facing the roller, and an approaching unit for selectively approaching a plurality of metals to the roller is provided to selectively control the bending angle of the ateel plate.

DETAILED DESCRIPTON OF PREFERED _EMBODIMENT
Reference will now be made in detail to the preferred embodiments of the present invention, examples of which are illustrated in the accompanying drawings.
Fig. 1 depicts a steel plate for a door of a refrigerator both €:nds of which are bent by a bending device of the present invention. As shown in Fig. 1, the door comprises a steel plate 10 both ends of which are bent, an inner caae (not shown) the both Sides of which are combined to the bending parts of the
steel plate 10, and an insulating material (not shown) filled between the steel plate 10 and the inner case.
The both ends of the steel plate 10 are bent to be obtuse-angled and then are bent by 90° three times. The present invention relates to a device for bending both ends of the door steel plate 10 to be obtuse-angled. Fig. 2 illustrates the bending device according to the present invention. As shown in Fig. 2, the door steel plate bending device comprises four metals
21, 22, 23, 24 having slopes with different inclination angles
at the outer sides thereof, a roller 30 placed to be rotatable
at the outline of the metal of the metals 21, 22, 23, 24 which
is placed at the upper side and an approaching unit for
approaching one of the metals 21, 22, 23, 24 to the roller 30.
Four metals 21, 22, 23, 24 are arranged to be concentric and a connecting member 40 having outer aides diverged from a center to be connected to the metals 21, 22, 23, 24. respectively is placed at the center thereof. The approaching unit has a motor 50 combined to the center of the connecting member 40 by interposing a rotary axis therebetween. That is, the motor 50 rotates the metals 21, 22, 23, 24 through the connecting member 40 and changes the metals to be close to the roller, thus adjusting the bending angle. Slopes 20a which are inclined by different angles are formed at the outer sides of the metals 21,
22, 23, 24.
A cylinder 60 wherein an operating axis 61 rises and falls
is prcvided at a lower portion of the connecting member 40.The
upper portion of the operating axis 61 is combined to the
connecting member 40 to lift and drop the metals 21, 22, 23, 24,
thereby enabling the bent steel plate 10 to be separated from the roller and the metals.
The operation of the bending device of the present invention as described above will be discuased hereinbelow.
The end part of the door steel plate 10 is placed between the roller 30 and one of the metals 21, 22, 23, 24 and the cylinder 60 is operated to press the meta1 to the roller 30. Thereafter, the roller 30 rotates along the outer side of the metal, and thus the and part of the door steel plate 10 ie bent along the slope of the metal.
in order to change the bending angle, the motor 50 is rotated by 90o, 180o or 270°, thus approaching the metals 21, 22, 23, 24 with different inclination angle to the roller 30.
As described above, the device for bending the steel plate for a door of a refrigerator according to the present invention has an advantage of eaaily changing the bending angle of the door steel plate by rotating the metals by using a motor.
