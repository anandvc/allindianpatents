Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED BODY FOR ELECTRICAL / ELECTRONIC SWITCH
Abstract An improved electrical switch (1) having a front surface (2) and a bottom surface (3) and a knob member (4) which placed between the central grooves provided at the back of front surface so that the knob member fitted from the back will protrude at the front surface through the grooves of the front surface; the said grooves is oval shaped; the said knob member of oval shape, boat like structure having two projections at its outer flanges which seats in the grooves of the front surface from the back; the said knob member is provided with two spring member inside the recess; the said spring member is held by v-shaped member which is pivoted on a brass piece; the said brass piece is also having projections at two ends and centrally placed in the grooves of the bottom member which are fitted tiltably; said bottom member is assembled along with knob member from the back on the front surface by screwing means the brass pieces are connected to the bottom member thereby providing a electrical switch with long lasting knob. AN IMPROVED ELECTRICAL SWITCH
The invention relates to an improved electiical switch. More particularly the invention relates to an improved electrical switch having centrally hinged switch knobs of oval shaped made from plastic mouldable material.
The conventional switches consisting of a body portion and a switch knob. The OFF/ON position of the knob is not working smoothly and thereby the spring inside the knob becomes ineffective and thereby the switch is damaged requires frequent replacement. The inventor has done a lot research on this and found there is a need to develop an improved electrical switch having improved knob which does not damage the spring easily.
The primary object of this invention is therefore to provide an unproved electrical switch wherein the switch knob is not damaged easily. Another object of this invention is to provide an improved electrical switch wherein the spring provided inside the knob is long lasting, safe and economic.
Accordingly there is provided an improved electiical switch having a front surface and a bottom surface and a knob member which placed between the cential grooves provided at the back of front surface so that the knob member fitted from the back protiude at the front surface through the grooves of the front surface; the said grooves is oval shaped; the said knob member of oval shape, boat hke structure having two projections at its outer flanges which sits in the grooves of the front surface from the back; the said knob member is provided with two spring member inside the recess; the said spring member is held by v-shaped member which is pivoted on a brass piece; the said brass piece is also having projections at two ends and centially placed in the grooves of the bottom member which are fitted tiltably; said bottom member is assembled along with knob member from the back on the front surface by screwing means the brass pieces are connected to the bottom member thereby providing a electiical switch with long lasting knob.
