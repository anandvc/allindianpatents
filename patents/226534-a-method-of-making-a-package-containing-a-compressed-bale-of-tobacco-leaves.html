Title of Invention

A METHOD OF MAKING A PACKAGE CONTAINING A COMPRESSED BALE OF TOBACCO LEAVES
Abstract Tobacco leaves are collected from the farm. These tobacco leaves are collected and put in the cloth to make it bundle. The leaves in bundle form transported to the place of manufacture. This way of packing and moving spoils the quality of tobacco leaves. According to the invention leaves are packed in compressed form of bales which gives quality packing and moving of tobacco eaves without spoiling the quqlity of leaves.
