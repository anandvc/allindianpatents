Title of Invention

AN AUTOMATIC PLATE WASHING MACHINE
Abstract This invention relates to an automatic plate washing machine. The automatic plate washing machine has a revolving stand adapted to be revolved by means of a set of gears which are rotated by a motive means. An equipment holder, mounted onto the revolving stand, is provided for holding different means like blade carrier, plate holder, soap water sprayer, washing brushes, hot water sprayer for carrying out washing operation of the plates in the washing chamber. An upper holder is provided on the top end of the equipment holder to keep the above means in its respective positions. Soap water tank and hot water tank are mounted on the top of the plate washing machine. An air pump is provided to spray hot water onto the plates to clean them. An electricity supplier is provided to supply electricity to the different means operated by electricity. Feeding means and delivery means, adapted to be operated by another motive means, are provided for feeding the dirty plates to the washing machine and delivering the cleaned plates from the washing machine.

This invention relates to an automatic plate washing machine for cleaning the plates.
A plate washing machine is known in the prior art. The machine comprises a chamber having stand disposed therein for holding the plates thereon. A soap water container having spray means is provided on the top of the machine such that to provide the soap water spray on to the plates in order to clean the plates. Another container with spray means is also provided on the top of the machine for providing clean water for washing the plates finely There are disadvantages associated with the prior art One of the main disadvantages is that the plates are not cleaned completely as the means to air the plates are not provided inside the cleaning chamber of the washing machine.
Therefore, the main object of this invention is to provide an automatic plate washing machine, which obviates the disadvantages associated with the prior art.
Another object of this invention is to provide an automatic plate washing machine with the means to rub the plates so as to clean the plates completely.
According to this invention there is provided an automatic plate washing machine comprising a revolving stand adapted to be revolved by means of a set of gears being rotated by a motive means, an equipment holder mounted onto said revolving stand being provided for holding different means for example plate holder, soap water sprayer, washing brushes, hot water sprayer etc thereon being provided for carrying out washing operation by the machine, an upper holder being provided on the top end of said equipment holder to keep said components in its respective positions, soap water tank and hot water tank being mounted on the top of said plate washing machine, an air pump being provided to spray hot water onto said plates to clean said plates, an electricity supplier being provided to supply electricity to the different means operated by electricity, feeding means and delivery means adapted to be operated by another motive means being provided for feeding the dirty plates to the washing machine and delivering the cleaned plates from the washing machine

Tn accordance with this invention the plates washing machine has a main chamber for accommodating the means used for washing the plates A revolving stand having a plurality of plate carrier is disposed centrally in the washing chamber. Means such as soap water sprayer, washing brushes and hot water sprayer are provided with each plate carrier such that to effect the cleaning of the plates during the washing operation of the machine. Soap water container and hot water container are provided on the top of the washing chamber of the machine Means such as conveyer belts are provided for feeding the dirty plates to the plate carrier and for delivering the cleaned plates from the plate carrier. Motive means for example an electric motor is provided below the bottom of the washing chamber such that to rotate the revolving stand by means of the gears. Electric means are provided to supply the electricity to the water heater and other equipment used in the machine for different purposes.
When the power is supplied to the machine, the air pump starts to pump air through the air pipe for a powerful spray of water Simultaneously, the water heater is switched on to heat water and supply the same to the hot water spraying device
