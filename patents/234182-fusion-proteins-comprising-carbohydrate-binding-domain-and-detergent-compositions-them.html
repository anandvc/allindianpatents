Title of Invention

FUSION PROTEINS COMPRISING CARBOHYDRATE BINDING DOMAIN AND DETERGENT COMPOSITIONS THEM
Abstract There is provided a fusion protein comprising a Carbohydrate Binding Domain and a domain having a high binding affinity for a melamine-type polymer. Also provided is a detergent composition comprising one or more surfactants and a fusion protein according to the invention and micro-particles comprising a melamine-type polymer, which contain a benefit agent selected from the group consisting of a fabric softening agents, fragrances, perfumes, polymeric lubricants, photoprotective agents, dye fixative agents, antioxidants, insecticides, soil repelling agents or a soil release agents. The fusion protein is advantageously used in a detergent composition to deliver benefit agents to a fabric.
