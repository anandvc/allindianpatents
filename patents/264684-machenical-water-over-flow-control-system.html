INSTALLATION OF THE MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW CONTROL SYSTE1 .
The over-flow of the water is a very great problem and the people are facing this problem daily and everybody fails to solve this problem. We solve this problem after the invention of the MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW CONTROL SYSTEM. The details of MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW CONTROL SYSTEM are as below: -
The MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW CONTROL SYSTEM install on the top at outside of a Water Tank and it binds with two nuts. An insulator Ball install into the water inside the Water Tank with a insulator rod for the working of this System. When, the Water Tank fully filled with water, then, the aforesaid insulator Ball pushes the equipment and thereafter this equipment cut the power supply of the Electric Motor. The Electric Motor cannot be restart automatically until we do not start the same. A Four-Cour Cable connects with System and N phase of the Electric Motor and it performs the main part in stopping the electric Motor after fully filling the Water Tank. This system works as a Two-way Switch by which we can start our Electric Motor. There is a Panel Board in the MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW CONTROL SYSTEM and a Signal Light is also installed in the Panel Board which signals about the position of the Water Tank whether it is full or not.

It is a very cheap system and this system has no expenditure in its running as it works on the pressure of the water which available with any cost. It stops the over-flow of water from Water Tanks and solves the problem of stores of undesirable water and helps in controlling the diseases, which occurs due to the storage of undesirable water. This System also helps in saving the invaluable water and electricity. There is no danger in use of this System. This System also saves the very valuable time of consumers, which they waste in waiting the fully filling of Water Tank. There are few chances of problem in the System because it has a very small Circuit. This System works as a two way switch. The Electric Motor can be operate by this System which install on the top at outside of Water Tank and at the time of cleaning the Water Tank, the consumer have no need to go down to switch on/off the electric Motor, because by this System the Electric Motor can be switch on/off on the roof of house where generally the Water Tank locates. The MECHANICAL WATER OVER-FLOW SYSTEM requires a very short time for repairing and installation.
