Title of Invention

A MACHINE FOR BACKING CARPET
Abstract TITLE; A MACHINE FOR BACKING CARPET. A MACHINE FOR BACKING CARPET COMPRISES: BASE PLATES, HEATERS, AN INDUCTION MOTOR, AND A GEAR BOX ASSEMBLY; WHEREIN THE BASE PLATE COMPRISES A TOP PLATE AND A BOTTOM PLATE WHICH ARE FIXED TO THE FRAMES AND A CENTER PLATE WHICH IS MOVEABLE IN THE VERTICAL DIRECTION IN BETWEEN THE TOP PLATE AND BOTTOM PLATE; THE HEATERS ARE FITTED TO THE TOP PLATE; AND THE SAID INDUCTION MOTOR AND SAID GEAR BOX ASSEMBLY ARE FITTED TOTHE BOTTOM PALTE TO MOVE THE CENTER PLATE IN THE VERTICLE DIRECTION.
Full Text Present invention relates to a machine for backing carpet.
More particularly, in the present invention a carpet or such
thick fabric like mattresses, blankets, etc. is backed, With
polymer sheet and textile fabric, if necessary, and the Said
backing is done by the machine for backing carpet or such thick
fabric material.
In the conventional process for backing of carpet or such
thick fabric materials like mattresses, blankets, etc. are bonded
manually with multiple backing textile fabrics by means of
chemical coatings such as latex. Carpets or such thick fabric
materials like mattresses, blankets, etc. are also bonded with
polymer sheet. Carpets or such thick fabric materials are laid,
such that the back surface of the carpet or such thick fabric
materials like mattresses, blankets, etc. is on top and polymer
sheet is spread on the backside of the carpet or such thick
fabric materials like mattresses, blankets, etc. Optionally, the
polymer sheet may also be covered with textile fabric. The
polymer sheet is then bonded manually on the backside of the
carpet by a heated roller rolled over the polymer sheet which
softens under heat and gets bonded to the back side of the
carpet.
This conventional manual process of bonding polymer sheet to
the carpet is tedious because of the involvement of manual labour
and at least 7 to 8 hours is required for proper bonding of the
carpet with polymer sheet.
An object of the present invention is to introduce a machine
which will heat the polymer sheet prior to bonding the same on to
the back surface of the carpet or such thick fabric like
mattresses, blankets, etc.
The machine for backing carpet has three plates of which the
upper plate and the bottom plate are fixed to the iron frames at
four corners of the machine. The position of the center plate is
adjustable in between the top plate and bottom plate by a screw
rod arrangement attached with the center plate. The position of
the center plate is adjustable in the verticle direction along
the frames of the machine by the screw rod mechanism which is
moved up and down by an induction motor through a bevel assembly
which is attached to the gear box assembly. The screw rod, fixed
to the center plate, is meshed with an internal gear of the bevel
assembly. At every corner edges of the center plate , a ball
bearing is fixed . The bevel gears are rotated by the gear box
assembly which is driven by the induction motor through a motor
pulley and a v-belt coupled inbetween the said gear box assembly
and the induction motor. Heater(s) are also fixed to the upper
surface of the top plate for heating the top plate to a
temperature not more than 280 deg. C. The heated top plate heats
the polymer sheet for proper bonding of the polymer sheet onto
the back surface of the carpet. The upper surface of the top
plate is covered with glass wool for minimizing heat loss. A
thermocouple is used for controlling the temperature of the top
plate. A digital timer is also attached with the centre plate
for monitoring the dwelling time of the top plate on the polymer
sheet for proper bonding of the same on the backside of the
carpet. The temperature and the dwelling time required for
bonding the polymer sheet depends on the size of the carpet.
According to the present invention there is provided :
A machine for backing carpet or such thick fabric material
comprises:
base plates, heaters, an induction motor, and a gear box
assembly ; wherein
the base plate comprises a top plate and a bottom plate
fixed to the frames and a center plate which is moveable in the
vertical direction in between the top plate and bottom plate ;
the heaters are fitted to the top plate ; and
the said induction motor and said gear box assembly are
fitted to the bottom plate.

The machine for backing carpet comprises four frames{3D at
four corners of the machine, base plates (5,6,7), heaters(8),
induction motor(lO), gear box assembly(14) and a reversible
switch(3). The base plates comprises of top plate(7), center
plate(6) and bottom plate(5). Heaters(8) are attached to the top
plate(7) which is fixed to the frames(31). Heaters heat the top
plate to a maximum temperature of 280 deg. C for proper bonding
of the polymer sheet with the carpet. However, the temperature
required for bonding the polymer sheet and the dwelling time of
the top plate on the polymer sheet of the carpet depends on the
size of the carpet . A digital timer(1) is used for measuring the
dwelling time. The top plate is covered with steel wool to
minimize heat loss from the heated top plate.
Preferably, but not essentially, four strip heaters of 750 W
each are used to attain the requisite temperature for heating the
polymer sheet and bonding the same with the carpet. The number of
strip heaters and the power of each heaters depends on the size
of the carpet. Moreover, various types of heaters, other than the
strip heaters, may be used without departing from the scope of
the invention.
The center plate(6) comprises four ball bearings(45) fixed
at the corner edges of the center plate such that the ball
bearings(45) graces the frame(31) surfaces of the machine. A
screw rod(15) is also fixed to the center plate.
A 3-phase induction motor (10) and a gear box assembly(14)
are supported by the bottom plate(5). A reversible switch (3), a
thermocouple (4) , and a switch for the thermocouple (2) are fixed
to one of the side plate situated near the bottom plate(5) of the
machine . The induction motor(10) drives a motor pulley(11) which
in turn induces drive to the gear box pulley(13) of the gear box
assembly(14) by means of a v~belt(12) placed over the motor
pulley(11) and gear box pulley(13). The gear box assembly(14)
subsequently rotates a bevel assembly(30). The bevel assembly is
preferably, but not essentially, 18T/48T. However, other teeth
ratio of bevel assembly can also be used without deviating from
the scope of the invention. An internal gear(32) is placed in the
housing of upper bevel(i.e. 48T). The other end of the said screw
rod(l5) is meshed with the internal gear(32) of the said upper
bevel . As the bevel gear rotates, it makes the screw rod(15) to
move up and down and inturn the center plate also moves up and
down in the vertical direction in between the top plate and
bottom plate. This direction of movement of the screw rod(15) is
controlled by the reversible switch(3).
Carpet(42) with polymer sheet on the back surface is placed
on a trapezoid frame(40), provided with pin fabric gripper(41)
which permits stretching cum orientation of the carpet. The
trapezoid frame is subsequently placed on the center plate, such
that polymer sheet is exposed towards the top plate and on top of
the polymer sheet a textile material covering the polymer sheet
may also be placed. The center plate is subsequently raised, by
activating the induction motor and selecting the proper position
of the reversible switch, till the surface of the polymer sheet
presses against the heated top plate. The heated top plate melts
the polymer sheet and the polymer sheet gets bonded with the
carpet under heat and pressure. The temperature of the top plate
is monitored by a thermocouple (4) . Once the predetermined
temperature of the top plate is reached, the thermocouple
disconnects the heater. The polymer sheet is kept pressed for a
predetermined duration for proper bonding. The reversible switch
position is changed and the center plate is subsequently lowered
by rotating the bevel assembly in the opposite direction by means
of induction motor and the trapezoid frame with the carpet is
taken out of the machine for backing carpet and a hand roller is
rolled over the backing cloth or the polymer sheet for uniform
bondage of the polymer sheet over the entire cross-section of the
carpet.
It is evident that the invention relating to the machine for
backing carpet with polymer sheet as described above may be
modified in order to achieve the same result without departing
from the scope of the invention.
