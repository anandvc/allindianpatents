Title of Invention

A CATALYST COMPRISING PALLADIUM FOR THE GAS-PHASE OXIDATION OF ETHYLENE AND ACETIC ACID TO VINYL ACETATE AND A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING THEM
Abstract TITLE: CATALYSTS FOR THE GAS-PHASE OXIDATIO OF ETHYLENE AND ACETIC ACID TO VINYL ACETATE, A PROCESS FOR PRODUCING THEM AND THEIR USE. A catalyst comprising palladium, at least one alkali metal compound and, if desired, one or more promoters ona porous support, obtainable by loading the porous support, which comprises a TiO2 produced through the flame hydrolysis of TiCl4 with at least one palladium compound, subsequently carrying out a reduction at a temperature of 300-500 C and additionally applying at least one alkali metal compound and, if desired, one or more promoters before or after the reduction.
