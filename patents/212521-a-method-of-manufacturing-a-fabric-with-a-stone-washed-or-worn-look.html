Title of Invention

A METHOD OF MANUFACTURING A FABRIC WITH A STONE-WASHED OR WORN LOOK
Abstract A method of manufacturing a yarn, fabric or garment with a stone wash or worn look, said method comprises a) coating the yarn, fabric or garment with a solution of xyloglucan polymer; b) dyeing the coated yarn, fabric or garment; and c) treating the dyed yarn, fabric or garment with an effective amount of an enzyme in an aqueous medium wherein said enzymes is capable of degrading the xyloglucan polymer, and has no or only a low activity towards insoluble cellulose.
