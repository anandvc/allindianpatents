BACKGROUND OF INVENTION WITH REGARD TO THE DRAWBACK OF ASSOCIATED WITH KNOWN ART.
A common technique of making floating water vehicle:
Shape of the floating water vehicle is developed in such way that huge buoyant force can be achieved and it floats in water easily. If a hole is found accidentally inside the floating water vehicle then the bottom portion of the floating water vehicle is becoming full of water and by which it sinks into water gradually as its weight is more than the displaced water or necessary buoyant force.
But if inside of the bottom of the floating water vehicle is filled with the light solid substance like thermocol then water will not be able to occupy its place by removing the solid substance. Ly this way the water displaced by the floating water vehicle will almost remain same whatever may be the condition, which will arise at the time of any type of calamities.
