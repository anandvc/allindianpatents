Title of Invention

SUSPENDED AXLE WITH SIDE AND OSCILLATION CONTROL LINKAGE.
Abstract The invention relates to an axle and suspension system for suspending an axle (16) relative to a vehicle frame (18), having an axle body (22s), a thrust arm (24), which extends in the longitudinal direction between axle body (22) and frame (18) and is mounted on the frame by means of a self-aligning bearing (26), land having a suspension means (48), which couples the axle body (22) and the frame (18) together, a scissors linkage (32) is provided, the scissors linkage (32) having a first and a second link (34, 38) which extend substantially in the longitudinal direction, the first link (34) being coupled pivotably at an upper pivot axis (36) by means of upper left and right pivot bearing (52, 54) to the frame (18) and at a central pivot axis (40) by means of central right and left pivot bearings (68, 70) to the second link (38) and the second link (38) being coupled pivotably at a lower pivot axis (42) by means of lower right and left pivot bearings (74, 76) to a trunnion bearing (46), the scissors linkage extending vertically between the axle body (22) and the frame (18) and being coupled by the trunnion bearing (46) to a trunnion (44) of the axle body (22) in such a manner that loscillation movements of the axle body (22) about a longitunal axis (28) are possible, and the pivot bearings (52, 54, 68, 70, 74, 76) being configured as support bearings in order to prevent lateral movements of the axle body (22).
