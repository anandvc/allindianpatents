Title of Invention

"AN IMPROVED ADVERTISEMENT BOX PANEL FOR A BUS QUE SHELTER"
Abstract This invention relates to an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter comprising a box provided with a plurality of tube lights therein and fixedly mounted on a support underneath wherein the box is fixed/hinged to a frame provided with c-channel on its atmost three sides for sliding and supporting the advertisement flex therein in which the flex is inserted into the frame from top/side.
Full Text FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to an improved advertisement box panel used particularly for a bus que shelter.
PRIOR ART
Advertisement box panel is as such known in the art in which the entire box is fabricated followed by installation of tube lights therein. Thereafter, flex of the advertisement is stretched and mounted on the front side of the box by using rivets and adhesive bond. The advertisement box thus obtained is lifted using strong ropes and placed on the top of bus shelter. Thereafter, it is bolted or tied with binding wire on the roof of bus que shelter .
However, the said advertisement box is associated with the following disadvantages:
1. Maintenance of the tube lights requires opening of the entire
advertisement printed flex, which requires remounting and
restretching of the flex all over again. Therefore, it is tedious.
Also, in this process the flex gets damaged and torn since it is
riveted and stuck with adhesive bond and this leaves the flex
completely unusable again.
2. Consumption of considerable time for fabrication and mounting
such a heavy box over the roof of bus que shelter.
3. Requirement of considerable man power for installation.
4. It is heavy and expensive.
5. Poor aesthetic value as the box hangs out of the dimensions of
the bus que shelter.
6. Over and above it can cause severe damage and accident to the
public standing in and around the bus que shelter and the
labourers who are handling such a heavy box.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of the invention is to propose an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter which overcomes all disadvantages associated with the prior art.
Another object of this invention is to propose an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter which is simple in construction.
Further object of this invention is to propose an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter which is having good aesthetic value and which is easy for use.
Still another object of this invention is to propose an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter which makes possible frequent alteration and changing of the advertisement printed flex.
Yet another further object of this invention is to propose an improved advertisement box panel used for a bus que shelter wherein method of installation is completely safe unlike prior art and maintenance of electrical fittings hardly consumes time.
STATEMENT OF INVENTION
According to this invention there is provided an improved advertisement box panel for a bus que shelter comprising a box provided with a plurality of tube lights therein and fixedly mounted on a support underneath characterized in that a frame is fixed/hinged to the box comprising of c-channel on its atmost three sides for sliding and supporting the advertisement flex therein in which the flex is inserted into the frame from top/side.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION WITH REFERENCE TO ACCOMPANYING DRAWINGS;
Reference may be made to fig .1 and 2 wherein the advertisement box panel comprises a box fabricated using preferably mild steel tubes, which is fixedly mounted on the bus que shelter. The said box is covered with galvanized iron sheet on its each side besides front side. Further, the box is covered with a frame made out of tubes, on its front which acts as an outer frame to the box. Inside of the frame is disposed with c-channel on its atmost three sides for sliding and supporting the advertisement flex mounted on the frame.
A plurality of tube lights are provided inside the box fixed to the frame. The flex mounted on a frame made of preferably mild steel tubes can be inserted inside the frame from top or left/right hand side.
The above constitutes first embodiment of the invention.
The outer frame (as shown in fig. 3) may be hinged to the box at the bottom/top and on both sides so as to make it openable wherein top/bottom of the frame, which is not provided with hinges is attached with the box on its back by suitable means. In a similar way, the outer two sides of advertisement box may be provided.

Such variant embodiments employing the concepts and features of this invention are intended to be within the scope of the present invention, which is further set forth under the following claims:-



I CLAIM;
1. An improved advertisement box panel for a bus que shelter comprising a box provided with a plurality of tube lights therein and fixedly mounted on a support underneath characterized in that a frame is fixed/ hinged to the box comprising of c-channel on its atmost three sides for sliding and supporting the advertisement flex therein in which the flex is inserted into the frame from top/side.
2. An improved advertisement box panel as claimed in claim 1 wherein the box is covered with a sheet on its all sides except front side.
3. An improved advertisement box panel as claimed in claim 1 or 2 wherein the frame is hinged to the box at its bottom/top or on both sides.

4. An improved advertisement box panel as claimed in claim 3 wherein top or bottom of the frame, which is not provided with hinges is attached with the box on its back by suitable means.
