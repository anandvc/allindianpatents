Title of Invention

A TAMPER EVIDENT SECURED SEAL ALONGWITH ELECTRICAL CONDUCTIVE PATH AND METHOD OF MANUFACTURING THE SAME
Abstract The present invention relates to a tamper evident secured seal and method of manufacturing the same. A sheet of a polyvinyl copolymer material with adhesive coating, said sheet is divided in to three sections, in which first section bears a manufactures identification along with bar code containing certain information, middle section for accommodating the hologram over it and third section for bearing the safety instructions; an open looped electrically conductive path being placed on said polyvinyl copolymer sheet for detecting the tampering of the seal, both ends of said open looped electrically conductive path are visible on either first section and/or third section of said polyvinyl copolymer sheet; a hologram being placed in the middle section of said polyvinyl copolymer sheet, said hologram is placed over the middle section of said polyvinyl copolymer sheet in such way that the conductive path is in between said hologram and said polyvinyl copolymer sheet; wherein when said seal is tried to be tampered, then said tampering is detected by said conductive path just by checking the electrical conductivity across the ends of said conductive path.

A Tamper Evident Secured Seal and Method of Manufacturing the Same
Field of the Invention:
The present invention relates to a tamper evident secured seal and method of manufacturing the same.
Prior Art:
Presently in marketing and selling of any product, labels, stickers and seals plays very important role in providing the information of the manufacturer, product as well as sealing to the particular product. Typically the application area includes file cabinets, air craft hatches, entry doors, envelops, cartons, electronic components etc.
Presently large variety of labels, stickers and seals are available in the market. But they all are suffered by following problems:
i. They are not water proof,
ii. They are not tamper evident.
iii. They can be removed from any surface with the help of solvent, petrol, etc.
iv. They can also be removed by heating.
v. The printing provided on them is not permanent and not of secured type,
vi. The seal provided by the customer can be easily destroyed and made unnoticed,
vii. They do not contain any secured media like hologram, barcode for providing
extreme level of security.
Hence there is a need to provide a new seal which will obviate all above mentioned problems and provide the high level of security.
Object of the Invention:
The object of the present invention is to provide a secured type of seal which obviates all the problems associated with the prior art labels, stickers and seals.

Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a tamper evident secured seal in which if it is tried to be destroyed then it can be easily detectable.

Now referring to figure 1 the tamper proof secured seal (1) of the present invention is shown. It comprises a polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2) with adhesive coating. The adhesive coating is of solvent free synthetic rubber based pressure sensitive adhesive. The reason of selecting polyvinyl copolymer material with coating for making the seal is to make the seal waterproof and non-tearable. This sheet (2) is divided in to three sections (S1, S2, S3). The first section (S1) bears a manufactures identification (8) along with bar code (7) containing certain information, middle section (S2) accommodates the hologram (6) over it and third section (S3) bears the safety instructions (9). The bar code can contain any information like serial number of product, company's code number etc. safety instruction may any printed warning regarding non tampering of the seal.
The tamper proof secured seal (1) of the present invention further includes an open looped electrically conductive path (3). This conductive path (3) is used for detecting the tampering of the seal (1). This conductive path (3) consists of two ends (4, 5). The conductive path (3) is placed on the polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2). The conductive path (3) is placed in such way that both ends (4, 5) of an open looped electrically conductive path (3) are visible on either first section (S1) and/or third section (S3) of said polyvinyl copolymer sheet. If somebody tries to tamper the seal (1) at the middle section (S2), same can be easily detectable just by
6

checking the electrical conductivity across the both ends (4, 5) of the conductive path (3). If the conductivity is there between both ends (4, 5) that means seal (1) is ok and if conductivity is not found between both ends (4, 5) that means seal (1) is tampered.
Seal (1) also consist of a hologram (6). The hologram (6) is placed in the middle section of the polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2). The hologram (6) is placed over the middle section of the polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2) in such way that the conductive path (3) is in between the hologram (6) and polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2). The portion of the conductive path (3) which is under the hologram (6) is not visible.
The main purpose of using hologram (6) is to provide the high level of the security. Also following advantages are provided by the hologram:
i. It can not be optically scanned by computer
ii. It can not be reproduced with a photocopier.
iii. It minimizes the document tampering.
iv. It provides quick and easy optical validation.
v. Hologram can contain embedded and hidden images.

Now we will see how the tamperproof seal (1) of the present invention is manufactured. For this first a sheet (2) of a polyvinyl copolymer material is taken. Then this sheet is coated with the adhesive coat of solvent free synthetic rubber based pressure sensitive adhesive. The coating is done by roll to roll hot coating process. Thereafter sheet (2) is cut in a proper
7

dimension as per the need by guillotine machine. Then this sheet (2) is divided in three sections (S1, S2, S3) in which first section (S1) bears a manufactures identification (8) along with bar code (7) containing certain information, middle section (S2) for accommodating the hologram (6) over it and third section (S3) for bearing the safety instructions (9). Then an open looped electrically conductive path (3) is placed on polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2) by screen printing process. The conductive path (3) is placed in such way that both ends (4, 5) of the open looped electrically conductive path (3) are visible on either first section (S1) and/or third section (S3) of the polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2). The conductive path (3) is made up of conductive silver based ink. Then hologram (6) is laminated over middle section (S2) of the polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2) and finally bar coding and numbering is done on the first section (S1) of polyvinyl copolymer sheet (2).
Industrial Applicability:
The tamper evident secured seal are often used to detect tampering with the objects containing valuables, confidential information, or calibration/ equipment settings. Typical applications includes file cabinets, air craft hatches, entry doors, envelops, cartons, electronic components. More particularly it is used in energy meters i.e. electric meters provided by electricity board and electricity distribution companies.
