Title of Invention

A DEVICE FOR THE MANUFACTURE OF CORE YARN
Abstract With a method for the manufacture of core yarn(8),whereby a core thread(6) is conducted to a fibre sliver(1),before the fibre sliver(1) is consolidated by rotation,the core thread(6) is intened to be monitored before joining to the fibre sliver(1),in particular for breakage.
