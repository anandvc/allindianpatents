Title of Invention

"A HERBAL COMPOSITION USEFUL FOR THE TREATMENT OF WHOOPING COUGH, REDUCING CHOLESTROL & BODY FAT"
Abstract The present invention discloses a novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of whooping cough, reducing cholesterol & body fat, comprising the following ingredients : (a) Extract of Euphorbia Neriifolia (b) Extract of Cassia Occidentalis (c) Extract of Pistacia Integerrima mixed with each other thoroughly in a ratio of 1:1:1 optionally mixing with the sugar to effect sweetness in the composition, any essence and thymol as per requirement may be mixed. This process also discloses a process for the preparation of the said herbal composition.
Full Text Field of the invention
This invention relates to a novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of whooping cough, reducing cholesterol & body fat. The composition of the present invention reduces the cholesterol therefore it treats the arteriosclerosis and therefore treats the cardiac artery diseases.
Background & Prior Art for the invention
So far in the prior art neither any ayurvedic nor any allopathic or any homeopathic medicines are available which can treat three diseases i.e. whooping cough, fatty ness, cholesterol unbalancing and Cardiac artery diseases. For whooping cough the effective remedy is only vaccination otherwise there is practically no kind of medicine is available which can treat the disease and in many instances it is a killer disease. The fattyness is also difficult to treat without expenditure of heavy amount of the money by going into the slimming centers. No effective medicines are available for solving this problem. No effective medicines are available in homeopathy & ayurveda. Allopathic medicines as usual exert side effects. Similar is the position with the treatments of unblanced cholesterol levels . no effective homeopathic & ayurvedic medicines available. Allopapathic medicines are available but inherent side effects of such medicines make it difficult to use.
No medicines are available for the treatment of CAD. Only bypass surgery
or angioplasty are the effective techniques for the treatment of CAD. These
treatments are very costly and in case of failures are life threatening.
Objects of the present invention:
The object of the present invention is to provide an effective treatment for
whooping cough, reducing cholesterol, body fat and cardiac artery disease.
Further objective of the invention is to provide a single herbal composition
which can treat effectively all the diseases . Still further objective of the
invention is to provide process for the preparation of such herbal
composition.
Disclosure of the invention:
The present invention discloses a novel herbal composition useful for the
treatment of whooping cough, reducing cholesterol & body fat, comprising
the following ingredients :
(i) Extract of Euphorbia Neriifolia (ii) Extract of Cassia Occidentalis (iii) Extract of Pistacia Integerrima
mixed with each other thoroughly in a ratio of 1:1:1 are mixed. This invention also discloses a process for the preparation of the novel herbal composition useful for the treatment of whooping cough, reducing cholesterol & body fat, comprising the following steps;
Collecting the following plants Euphorbia Neriifolia
Cassia Occidentalis
Pistacia Integerrima
Washing with water, drying for 4-5 days in sunlight, grinding to a fine
powder, mixing each separately with 8 times by weight of water, keeping dipped in water for there or four days, filtering the same separately to remove residues and reducing the solution to l/3rd and mixing each extract in a ratio of 1:1:1 and then optionally mixing with the sugar to effect sweetness in the composition, any essence and thymol as per requirement may be mixed. Statement of Synergism
The herbal composition of the present invention alone can be used for the treatment of whooping cough, reducing cholesterol & body fat and also of the treatment of cardiac Artery Disease. This herbal composition is a cost effective remedy for all the above said diseases. In this way the composition of the present invention is not a mere admixture but is synergistic composition and having unexpected & enhanced sum total of the properties as compared to the properties of the individual ingredients. Dosage:
The composition may be prepared in the form of tablet and capsules. The capsules and tablets may be prepared in 500 250 & 100 mg dosage form.
In cases of whooping cough 3 capsules of 500 mg may be administered in a day for an adult.
For children from 8 to 13 years 250 mg for two or three times may be given.
For children from 5 to 8 years 100 mg for two or three times
may be given
The composition may also be prepared in the form of an
injection. Injection of 250 mg & 500 mg for adults may be
prepared.
For pediatric dosage 20 to 100 mg dosage may be prepared. The
patient may be given I.V and I.M. administration generally 2
times a day.
The injection are supposed to be very effective in cases of
arteriosclerosis.
Syrups of the composition may also be prepared in the known
manners.
