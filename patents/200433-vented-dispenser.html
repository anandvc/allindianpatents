Title of Invention

VENTED DISPENSER
Abstract A vented dispenser for use with liquid products has a deformable body, a first valve assembly and a second valve assembly. The first valve assembly permits liquid to exit the dispenser if pressure within the deformable body is greater than external pressure. The second valve assembly permits air to enter the dispenser if pressure within the deformable body is less them external pressure. The liquid exits the bottle through the first valve assembly when an external pressure is applied to the deformable body. The first valve assembly closes when the external pressure is no longer applied. The deformable body, however, made from an elastic material having memory, will try and attain its original shape, thereby causing pressure inside the vented dispenser to decrease below the ambient pressure. The decrease in the internal pressure will cause the second valve assembly to open and air will enter the bottle, thus equalizing internal pressure to ambient pressure. The control of the internal pressure results in enabling substantially all of the fluid to leave the bottle.
