Title of Invention

ECOLOGICAL FILTERING SYSTEM AND A PROCESS OF PURIFYING CONTAMINATED WATER
Abstract The invention relates to a process of purifying contaminated water comprises treating the said contaminated water by subjecting it to undergo the following steps. a step of physical filtration another step of biological filtration and treating the water with spiral as neutralizing agent 2. Further this invention relates to a system of purifying contaminated water comprises a tank having an inlet for receiving contaminated water, a bed consisting of different sizes of spiral shaped filter made of resin, an outlet for discharge of purfies water and a provision at the outlet containing neutralizing agent.
