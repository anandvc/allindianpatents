Title of Invention

"A THERMAL PRINTER AND A METHOD FOR THERMAL PRINTING THEREWITH"
Abstract A thermal printer (160) comprises: a plurality of thermal print heads (216,218, 220) ,each being operable to print a district one of a plurality of colors: and Dot size varying means for varying sizes perceived levels of color printed by the thermal printer by varying sizes of dots printed by the thermal print heads., Wherein a first thermal print head has a first number of thermal elements energizable at a first rate, and a second thermal print head has a second number of thermal elements energizable at a second rate, the first number being different than the second number, and the first rate being different from the second rate.
