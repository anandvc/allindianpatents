Title of Invention

MULTI-PURPOSE DISPLAY SYSTEM
Abstract The invention relates to a compact multipurpose display system which is enclosed in a fully openable envelope. (1) It consists of ( i) a quadrate sleeved base,(3) having a similarly sized flap end,(5) foldable around a bridge(7) and flap (9), and an oppositely located insert end (11) to receive the flap, and the sleeve open at both ends(13). (ii) An inwardly placed fixed loop (15) which is located at insert end of the base, parallel to the sleeves; (iii) A similarly sized sleeved and looped display card(17), which is slidably connected to the base, through the loop of the base, and the sleeve of the display card. The display card in turn is slid ably connected similarly to one or more display cards (18) through the loop and the sleeve of the subsequent display card.

FIELD OF INVENTION: The invention relates to a compact multipurpose display system, which is enclosed in a fully openable envelope.
BACK GROUND OF INVENTION: Several types of display systems are currently available. However most of mem are for single purpose and do not allow flexibility. Not many of them are not sufficiently compact. The present invention describes a compact multipurpose display system, ideally suited for use as a shade card, swatch card , or disc holder or could be used in various types of teaching aids. When used as a shade card or swatch display, it allows easy comparison of fabric or shades. The display system provides its own cover and can be secured for easy use. The invention has the potential of finding application in various industries, in addition to various types of packaging industry.
