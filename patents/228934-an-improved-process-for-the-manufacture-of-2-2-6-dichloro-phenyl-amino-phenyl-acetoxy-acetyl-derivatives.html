Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED PROCESS FOR THE MANUFACTURE OF 2-[(2,6-DICHLORO PHENYL) AMINO] PHENYL ACETOXY ACETYL DERIVATIVES
Abstract An improved process of manufacture of Aceclofenac, an NSAID, which essentially involves modification of reaction parameters such as temperature and pressure, selection of non-reactive and solvents as the reaction medium, and optimization of regent quantities in the debenzylation of aceclofenac benzylester. This modified process of the present invention eliminates mostly the drawbacks of the prior art and yields pure aceclofenac with a substantial increase in the overall yield and thereby best suited to the manufacturing conditions.
