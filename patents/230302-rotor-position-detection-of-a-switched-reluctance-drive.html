Title of Invention

ROTOR POSITION DETECTION OF A SWITCHED RELUCTANCE DRIVE
Abstract A switched reluctance drive is controlled without using a physical rotor position dector.The control method estimates the standing flux-linkage associated with the phase and uses this estimate to improve its improve of rotor position.the method works robustly regardless of whether the current is continuous or discontinus.
