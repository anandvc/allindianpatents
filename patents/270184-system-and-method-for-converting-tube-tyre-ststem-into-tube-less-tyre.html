Title of Invention

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR CONVERTING TUBE-TYRE STSTEM INTO TUBE-LESS TYRE
Abstract The present invention relates to a method and system for converting tube-tyre system into tube-less tyre and/or slowing vehicle tyre deflation. The system of the present invention comprises a conventional rim; a tyre air seal made of rubber for rim and having a hole to adapt a inflation valve; a tubeless inflation valve; a soluble substance of fibre, fillers or rubber composition to be injected between the tyre and the rim for maintaining the pressure and temperature within the chamber; wherein the tyre air seal in combination with tyre and rim forms a compartment for air and the soluble substance maintains moisture within the compartment maintaining the pressure and temperature within the chamber thereby actuating transfer of force on sidewalls of the tyre instead of tread area and minimizing friction, force and stress between the rim and the tyre. Full Text FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention particularly relates to an improved inflation, operation and protection (IOP) process for all types of Tube Type Tyre' those comprising of Tyre Air Seal' (TAS),' Tubeless Inflation Valve' and 'Soluble Fibres composition' also 'Air / Inflation Pressure' as in conventional modes and more specifically for all those tyres those are to be mount with 'Conventional Rim unit' particularly known as Cylindrical Bead Seat Rim use with spokes, Divided Type Rim, One Piece Rim, Two Pieces and Three Pieces (Semi Drop Centre only) Rim and also Four Pieces Rim in which Tube Type Pneumatic Tyre' allow removal of 'Inner Rubber Tube' and introducing new improved process for inflating them.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
Modern pneumatic tyres for vehicles are of two types as shown in Figure 1. The first of these is so called Tube Types Tyre' as shown in Figure 1A which is designed to be mounted directly upon 'Conventional Rim unit' by means of a separate 'Inner Rubber Tube' having an integral inflation device which projects through the wheel rim valve hole. Conventional Tube Type Tyres may be 'Bias (Diagonal / Cross / Nylon) Ply' or 'Radial Ply'. The second type of these is so called Tubeless Tyre' as shown in Figure 1B which has an inner lining of rubber having a low permeability to air and which is designed to be mounted directly on to the Tubeless Rim' to make the wheel of vehicle and inflated by means of an separate inflation device which is so called as Tubeless Inflation Valve' inserted on to the wheel rim valve hole.


It is well known that tyre life and vehicle safety depend largely on tyres being maintained at the recommended inflation pressure, which varies with the vehicle-tyre combination, tyre location on the vehicle and also operating and weather conditions.
The tube type tyres those inflate with the help of inner rubber tube suffer the problems of rubber porosity, generation of high heat, uneven distribution of weight of assembly and sudden deflation in case of puncture. Also the tubeless tyres those inflate only upon Tubeless rim (single piece or mono block) with the help of extra rubber lining on bead of tyre and hump lock whether flat or round which is provided on taper seat of rim suffer the problem of repairs of even smaller puncture area with sharp object, irregular air pressure, porosity leaks, bead leaks and also not capable to inflate on conventional rims like divided type rim, cylindrical bead seat rim, two and three pieces rim.
The prior art of tyre inflation process suffer certain disadvantages. One of the major disadvantages is ride characteristics do not match the comfort ability because tyre works on the basis of tread area because of inner rubber tube and also suffers the problem of weather.
Furthermore, while 'Conventional Tube Type Tyres' explode in case of puncture or Cross cuts, the so-called "Tubeless" too, get flabby at zero level quite soon, also explode in case of particular stress and for what concerns lowered the life of tyres, also irregularity in air pressure so damage the rim when it hits certain obstacles or runs onto terrains.


SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
The present invention describes a system and method for converting tube-tyre system into tube-less tyre and/or slowing vehicle tyre deflation.
The method of the present invention comprises the steps of providing a tyre air seal made of rubber having a hole to adapt a tubeless inflation valve; mounting the tyre air seal over a conventional rim; fitting the tubeless inflation valve on the rim for inflating the tyre; mounting the tyre over the tyre air seal; injecting soluble substance of fibre, fillers or rubber composition through the inflation valve; and inflating the tyre with air through the inflation valve; wherein the tyre air seal in combination with tyre and rim forms a compartment for air capable to hold and retain air pressure within chamber with the help of hydro dynamical properties i.e. moisture, drops or layer of substance of fibres, filler and rubber within inner periphery of tyre; and wherein the soluble substance maintains moisture within the compartment maintaining the pressure and temperature within the chamber thereby actuating transfer of force on sidewalls of the tyre instead of tread area and minimizing friction, force and stress between the rim and the tyre.
The system of the present invention comprises a conventional rim; a tyre air seal made of rubber for rim and having a hole to adapt a inflation valve; a tubeless inflation valve; a soluble substance of fibre, fillers or rubber composition to be injected between the tyre and the rim for maintaining the pressure and temperature within the chamber; wherein the tyre air seal in combination with tyre and rim forms a compartment for air and the soluble substance maintains moisture within the compartment maintaining the pressure and temperature within the chamber


thereby actuating transfer of force on sidewalls of the tyre instead of tread area and minimizing friction, force and stress between the rim and the tyre.
DEFINITIONS
• Tyre' mean - a hollow of rubber filled with air with aim to make smoother and comfortable ride. It is a casing and tread assembly that is mounted on a wheel rim to provide pneumatically cushioned, contact and traction with the road. Tyres are closely associated with automobiles.
• 'Rim' mean - the outer often curved or circular edge or border of something where the inner part of a wheel joined to the hub which is removable and outer metal band on a wheel to which the tyre is attached.
• Tyre Air Seal' (TAS) mean - made of natural / foam / synthetic rubber material and blend thereof those utilize as protective sheathe on inner periphery of rim and having a hole to adapt inflation valve and / or also utilize with aim to provide an extra safety hump lock to join together the tyre bead with bead seat of rim which is further known as TAS.
• 'Soluble Substance of Fibre, Filler and Rubber' mean - a soluble fine divided particles of fibres whether vegetables or synthetic along with rubber, Polyester, Silicone, Ethylene base an improved composition, eligible to regulate prime condition of tyre mean prevent hardening of inner surface of tyre and fix a permanent fill or seal automatically as and when tyre needs to fill and seal the holes from where air can escape which is further known as 'Soluble Fibre Substance'.
• 'Rubber Tube' mean - a fully sealed rubber tube attached a valve to control flow of air in and out. This one is a critical part of wheel assembly because it can crease or fold over itself and fails suddenly due to chaffing and thinning of the rubber which is a bad review of ability and workability of tyre.


• Tubeless inflation Valve' mean - a component which is fitted upon valve hole of rim. The housing of valve has an internal passage threaded at one end to accommodate a valve core.
• 'Bead of Tyre' mean - the part of a tyre which is so shaped as to fit on to the rim and rests on the rim bead seat, made of steel wires, wrapped and reinforced by the plies of the tyre.
• Taper Seat of Rim' mean - a portion of the wheel rim below the 'rim flange' providing radial support to the 'Bead of Tyre'. The taper must be so designed to lock the tyre onto the rim and provide a good seal.
• 'Side Wall of Tyre' mean - a wall that forms the side of something mean a side surface of tyre between the bead seat and shoulder of tyre.
• Tread of Tyre' mean - The tread refers to the rubber on its circumference that makes contact with the road. Tread is so designed to provide rib and groove for traction, tyres are regularly used so tread is worn off, limiting its effectiveness in providing traction on road.
• 'Puncture' mean - To cause sudden Loss of air pressure in a tyre when a hole is made by some sharp object mean a perforation and loss of pressure in a pneumatic tyre.
• 'Puncture Sealant' mean - a latex free composition of fine divided particles of fibres of vegetable or synthetic, tacky adhesives, surfactant, silica, glycol, ethylene etc. by which puncture holes caused in a tyre can fill and seal the holes within inner periphery of tyre from where air can escape.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION:
This new model comprises of an improved process by which 'Conventional Tyre' to fit out with 'Conventional rim unit' without the use of inner rubber tube so the air pressure troubles reduced with this invention also in exercise allow maintenance of ambient inflation pressure and identical temperature within tyre at optimum level, more safety and possibility of using very soft mix.
The process of 'Fibre Touch Tyre Inflation System' has been designed and formulated to stimulate and improve the drive and ride characteristics of conventional tyres. By application of new improved process tyres possess several advantages particularly slower deflation characteristics and high gripping properties on road upon sudden application of brakes, high cushioning capabilities because tyre works on the basis of sidewalls instead of tread area as

well as safety too. Also the proposed invention successfully solves the other problems, because it provides us a low cost technique as well as trouble free operation at optimum level.
