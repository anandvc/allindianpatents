Title of Invention

SOLID-ELECTROLYTE SECONDARY BATTERY
Abstract A solid-electrolyte secondary battery, comprising: a positive electrode; a negative electrode; and a solid electrolyte provided between the electrodes; the solid electrolyte containing as a matrix polymer a fluorocarbon polymer from 550,000 to 1,000,000 in weight-average molecular weight.
Full Text A solid-electrolyte secondary battery, comprising:
a positive electrode;
a negative electrode; and
a solid electrolyte provided between the electrodes; the solid electrolyte containing as a matrix polymer a fluorocarbon polymer from 550,000 to 1,000,000 in weight-average molecular weight.
