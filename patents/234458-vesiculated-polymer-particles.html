Title of Invention

VESICULATED POLYMER PARTICLES
Abstract Vesiculated polymer particles and a method of manufacture thereof, are suitable for the beneficial replacement of titanium pigments and extenders amongst others, and have improved opacity whiteness, scrub resistance and water resistance due. The manufacturing process exhibits effective control of particle size and particle size distribution.
