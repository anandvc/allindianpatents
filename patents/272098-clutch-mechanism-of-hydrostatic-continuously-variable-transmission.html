Title of Invention

CLUTCH MECHANISM OF HYDROSTATIC CONTINUOUSLY VARIABLE TRANSMISSION
Abstract [Problem] To provide a clutch mechanism for a hydrostatic continuously variable transmission in which a hydraulic circuit comprised of a high pressure oil path and a low pressure oil path is constituted between a hydraulic pump and a hydraulic motor in a casing rotated by a driving source within a cylinder integral with a transmission shaft, a clutch valve arranged in the transmission shaft is slid by a centrifugal governor, the high pressure oil path and the low pressure oil path are shortened to change-over a transmittance of power, wherein there is provided a constitution of the spring sheet of which manufacturing and assembling are simple and the number of component parts is less. [Solving Means] This clutch mechanism for a hydrostatic continuously variable transmission is comprised of a cam plate member arranged at the end part of the transmission shaft, a roller engaged with the cam plate member and moved in a diametrical outward direction with a centrifugal force, a roller bearing member slid axially through an outward motion of the roller, a spring member for biasing the roller bearing member toward the roller, a spring sheet member for supporting the spring member, and this is characterized in that the spring sheet member is held by said cam plate member and said casing and supported. [Title of the Invention] CLUTCH MECHANISM OF HYDROSTATIC CONTINUOUSLY VARIABLE TRANSMISSION [Technical Field] [0001]
This invention relates to a clutch mechanism of a hydrostatic continuously variable transmission and more particularly a constitution of a spring sheet of a coil spring for pressing a roller bearing member of a centrifugal type governor clutch of the hydrostatic variably transmission. [Background Art] [0002]
In the prior art centrifugal type governor clutch, the side part of a cam plate arranged near the end part of a transmission shaft was axially extended to form a cylindrical part and the cylindrical part was directly attached and fixed to a casing of the transmission with bolts. A ring-like spring sheet of an independent component formed as a separate element was fixed to the cylindrical part with a ring-like grip (for example, refer to Patent Document 1) . The number of component elements was increased because the ring-like sheet of such a small parts as described above was troublesome in its manufacturing and assembling and a fixing clip was needed. [0003]
[Patent Document 1] JP-A 070331/2004 (Fig.l) [Disclosure of the Invention] [Problem to be Solved by the Invention]

This invention overcomes the aforesaid problems of the prior art and provides a constitution of the spring sheet of which manufacturing and assembling are simple and the number of component elements is less.
[Means for Solving the Problem]
[0005]
This invention overcomes the aforesaid problem and the invention according to claim 1 relates to a clutch mechanism for a hydrostatic continuously variable transmission in which a hydraulic circuit comprised of a high pressure oil path for feeding working oil from a hydraulic pump to a hydraulic motor and a low pressure oil path for feeding working oil from said hydraulic motor to said hydraulic pump is constituted between said hydraulic pump and said hydraulic motor in a casing rotated by a driving source within a cylinder integral with a transmission shaft, a clutch valve arranged in the transmission shaft is slid by a centrifugal governor, said high pressure oil path and said low pressure oil path are shortened to change-over a transmittance of power characterized in that the same is comprised of a cam plate member arranged at the end part of said transmission shaft, a roller engaged with said cam plate member and moved in a diametrical outward direction with a centrifugal force, a roller bearing member for receiving a roller pressing force caused by an outward motion of said roller and axially slid, a spring member for biasing said roller bearing member toward said roller, a spring sheet member for supporting said spring member, and said spring sheet member being held by said cam plate member and said casing and

supported.
[Effect of the Invention]
[0006]
In this invention, the cylindrical part formed by extending the side part of the cam plate member axially is substituted such that the cylindrical part is made a separate member different from the cam plate member, the spring sheet is integrally formed at the cylindrical part to make the cylindrical spring sheet member, the spring sheet member is held between the cam plate member and the casing of transmission and fixed with bolts. Accordingly, manufacturing and assembling of the clutch mechanism are easy because there is not provided the spring sheet of a small independent parts and the number of component parts is decreased because the fixing clip is not required.
