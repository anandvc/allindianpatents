Title of Invention

A METHOD OF REDUCING POWER CONSUMPTION IN A COMMUNICATION DEVICE.
Abstract The present invention relates generally to digital communication. More particularly, the present invention relates to a method and apparatus for reducing power consumption in a spread spectrum communication system such as a code division multiple access (CDMA) cellular telephone system.
