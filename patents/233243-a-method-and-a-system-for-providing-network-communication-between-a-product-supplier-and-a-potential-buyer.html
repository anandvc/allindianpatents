" A METHOD AND A SYSTEM FOR PROVIDING NETWORK COMMUNICATION BETWEEN A PRODUCT SUPPLIER AND A POTENTIAL BUYER"
Abstract A system for providing network communication between an offerer (14) of a product (4) and a potential interested party (potential buyer) (1) with regard to the product (4), comprising at least one announcement channel (2, 15, 17) directed to the public, to a group of potential buyers (1) or directed individually, and in which channel the offerer places an approach (3) regarding the product (4), and a network by means of which the potential interested party (1) establishes communication with the offerer (14) by means of a special network address associated with the offerer, wherein a unique perceptual element (10) that the offerer incorporates in said approach (3) with permission from a proprietor of at least one special network domain name, and a network central (13) belonging to said proprietor, operative for computerized interpretation of addresses and onward routing of network calling from a potential interested party (1) on the basis of a network address stated by said potential interested party (1), which network address (12) is an approach-specific intuitive net address part together with the special domain name, said onward routing then being made directly to an address with the offerer (14) or associated with the offerer, which address is relevant with regard to the announced product (4) or with regard to one argument (11) among a number of arguments in said approach.
