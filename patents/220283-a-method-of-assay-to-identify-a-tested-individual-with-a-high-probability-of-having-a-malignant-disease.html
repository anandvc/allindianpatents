Title of Invention

A METHOD OF ASSAY TO IDENTIFY A TESTED INDIVIDUAL WITH A HIGH PROBABILITY OF HAVING A MALIGNANT DISEASE
Abstract The invention discloses a method of assay for identifying a tested individual with a high probability of having a malignant disease comprising: (a) contacting a body fluid sample obtained from said individual with at least one DNA and amino lacid sequence of monoclonal antibodies (mAbs) as defined in the specification. (b) determining the extent of binding of said mAbs to T-cells within said sample; and (c) comparing the extent of (b) to the extent of binding of the mAbs to T-cells in a sample obtained from a healthy individual, a significant difference between the above two extents of binding indicating that said tested individual has a high probability of having a malignant disease.
