Title of Invention

A MIXING APPARATUS AND A SLURRYING APPARATUS
Abstract The present invention relates to a mixing apparatus for mixing fine particles and liquid, comprising a cylindrical body having a lower opening and an upper opening and a part of which diameter decreases in the direction toward the lower opening from the upper opening, and a dispersing pipe connected to the lower opening of the cylindrical body; a liquid feeding opening is provided on an upper side of the cylindrical' body for injecting the liquid along the peripheral direction of inner wall surface of the cylindrical body to make the liquid fall on the inner wall of the cylindrical body while swirling; and the upper opening of the cylindrical body is a c.harging port for charging the fine particles toward the lower opening; characterized in that a dispersing means for dispersing the fine particles in the liquid is disposed inside the dispersing pipe. The invention also relates to a slurrying apparatus for slurrying fme particles and liquid by mixing and storing slurry.
