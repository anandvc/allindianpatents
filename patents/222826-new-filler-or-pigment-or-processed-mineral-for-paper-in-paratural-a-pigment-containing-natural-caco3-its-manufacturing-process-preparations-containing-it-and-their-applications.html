Title of Invention

NEW FILLER OR PIGMENT OR PROCESSED MINERAL FOR PAPER, IN PARATURAL A PIGMENT CONTAINING NATURAL CACO3, ITS MANUFACTURING PROCESS, PREPARATIONS CONTAINING IT AND THEIR APPLICATIONS
Abstract Aquerous suspension of one or more pigments, fillers or miners or minerals, Which may contain a dispersent polymer to stabilise the rheology of the suspension, the said pigments enabling the weight of the paper at constant surface area to be reduced, characterised by the fact that: a)it contain a natural carbonate and the reaction product or products of the said carbonate With gaseous CO2 and the reaction product product or products of the said carbonate With one or more medium-strong H3O+ ion-providers, and b)it has a pH greater than 7.5 measured at 20 degree C
