Title of Invention

CEREAL GRAIN KERNELS FORTIFIED WITH METAL AMINO ACID CHELATES
Abstract A cereal grain kernel coated with a metal amino acid chelate wherein said coating is comprised of a stabilizer and a metal amino acid chelate having a ligand to metal molar ratio from 1:1 to 4:1.
