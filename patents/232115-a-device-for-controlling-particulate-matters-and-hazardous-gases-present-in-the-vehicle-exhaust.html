Title of Invention

"A DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING PARTICULATE MATTERS AND HAZARDOUS GASES PRESENT IN THE VEHICLE EXHAUST"
Abstract This invention relates to a device for controlling the particulate matters and hazardous gases present in the vehicle exhaust comprising a container (1) for containing a scrubbing liquid therein, an inlet pipe (2) connected to the exhaust of the engine connected to the outlet chamber (3) provided onto said container to facilitate the separation of liquid from the exhaust and exit of the same therefrom, a sparger (4) connected near the bottom end of said container submerged into said liquid, said sparger connected to the lower end of said inlet pipe.
