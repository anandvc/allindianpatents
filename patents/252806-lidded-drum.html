Title of Invention

LIDDED DRUM
Abstract A lidded drum of synthetic material includes a clamping ring of metal for fastening the lid on the upper drum rim, wherein the clamping ring is U-shaped in cross section and engages in the closed state with its upper leg over the lid rim and the lower leg engages under an annular contact surface of a border of the drum wall. The contact surface extends perpendicularly or obliquely relative to the longitudinal axis of the drum. The contact surface is arranged below the upper drum rim and constitutes the upper limitation of an indentation in the upper portion of the drum wall. The lid rim formed by an inner rim, an outer rim, and a connecting ring, has a U-shaped cross sectional profile which is open toward the bottom and includes a sealing bed for placing or foaming a lid seal therein, wherein the lid seal is pressed in the closed state of the drum lid by the clamping force of the clamping ring against the upper lid rim. An annular bead is integrally formed on the connecting leg of the upper and lower legs of the clamping ring, wherein the annular bead is directed radially inwardly and, when the clamping ring is closed, the annular bead rests in a positively engaging manner against the outer rim of the lid for securing the lid on the upper lid rim.

BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
1. Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to a lidded drum of synthetic material with a clamping ring of metal for fastening the lid on the upper drum rim, wherein the clamping ring is U-shaped in cross section and engages in the closed state with its upper leg over the lid rim and the lower leg engages under an annular contact surface of a border of the drum wall which contact surface extends perpendicularly or obliquely relative to the longitudinal axis of the drum. The contact surface is arranged below the upper drum rim and constitutes the upper limitation of an indentation in the upper portion of the drum wall. The lid rim formed by an inner rim, an outer rim, and a connecting ring, has a U-shaped cross sectional profile which is open toward the bottom and includes a sealing bed for placing or foaming a lid seal therein, wherein the lid seal is pressed in the closed state of the drum lid by the clamping force of the clamping ring against the upper lid rim.
2. Description of the Related Art
In lidded drums of this type as known from U.S. Patent 5,301,853, the sealing effect of the drum lid is effected by an axial pretensioning of the lid seal which is placed on the

upper lid rim, wherein the axial pretensioning force is produced during the closing of the clamping ring over the two inclined legs of the clamping ring which slide over the lid rim and the drum border. In lidded drums which are permitted to be used for storing and transporting dangerous liquids, certain demands are made with respect to the storage and transport safety which are tested by means of prescribed registration examinations, in which the lidded drums are subjected to a lateral drop onto the wall, a diagonal drop onto the lid rim, a static internal pressure measurement, and a stacking load.
In the lidded drum according to U.S. Patent 5,301,853, the necessary strength of the ring is achieved by using a relatively great thickness of the sheet metal of the clamping ring, wherein, when the drum drops, for example, from the loading surface of a truck, leaks are prevented in the drum lid and, when the drum drops from a greater height, a separation of the lid from the drum due to deformations of the clamping ring are prevented. The disadvantages of this known lidded drum are the weight of the clamping ring because of the relatively large material thickness of the ring and the high price of manufacturing the ring.

SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
It is the primary object of the present invention to further develop the lidded drum described above with respect to the tightness of the drum lid when the drum drops from middle or large heights.
In accordance with the present invention, the above object is met by providing an annular bead integrally formed on the connecting leg of the upper and lower legs of the clamping ring, wherein the annular bead is directed radially inwardly and, when the clamping ring is closed, the annular bead rests in a positively engaging manner against the outer rim of the lid for securing the lid on the upper lid rim.
The lidded drum of synthetic material according to the present invention with a clamping ring of metal for fastening the drum lid on the upper drum rim has compared to the known lidded drums the advantage of an improved strength with respect to drops and a clamping ring with reduced material thickness leading to the reduction of the weight of the clamping ring and to a reduced manufacturing cost of the clamping ring because of a reduced use of material.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
The lidded drum 1 manufactured of synthetic material as shown in Fig. 1 is composed of a drum wall 2, a lower bottom 3, a drum lid 4, as well as a clamping ring of metal for fastening the lid 4 on the upper drum rim 6.
In both embodiments of the drum illustrated in Figs. 2 and 3, the clamping ring 5, which has a U-shaped cross sectional profile, engages with its upper leg 7 over the lid rim 8 and engages with its lower leg 9 on the annular contact surface 11 of a border or projection 12 at the drum wall 2, wherein the annular contact surface 11 extends obliquely relative to the longitudinal axis 10 of the drum, and wherein the border 12 is arranged underneath the upper drum rim 6 and forms the upper limitation of an indentation 13 in the upper portion of the drum wall 2. The drum rim 8 formed by an inner rim 14, an outer rim 15, and a connecting ring 16 has a downwardly open U-shaped cross sectional profile with a sealing bed 17 for placing or foaming a lid seal 18 therein, wherein, in the closed state of the cover 4, the lid seal 18 is pressed as a result of the clamping force of the clamping ring 5 against the upper drum rim 6.

In the first embodiment illustrated in Fig. 2 of a clamping ring closure of the drum lid 4, an annular bead 2 0 is integrally formed on the connecting leg 19 of the upper and the lower legs 7, 9 of the clamping ring 5, wherein the annular bead 20 is directed radially inwardly and, when the clamping ring 5 is closed, the annular bead 20 engages in an engagement groove 21 formed in the outer rim 15 of the lid 4 for securing the lid on the upper drum rim 6.
When the lidded drum 1 is dropped from a height onto the lid rim 8, the clamping ring 5 is upset and the annular bead
20 of the clamping ring is pressed into the engagement groove
21 in the outer rim 15 of the lid rim 8. In this manner, the drum lid 4 is better secured on the drum rim 6 and the strength of the drum with respect to drops is increased, such that the material thickness of the clamping ring can be reduced.
The second embodiment of the clamping ring closure of the drum lid 4 according to Fig. 3 is characterized in that, when the clamping ring 5 is closed, the annular bead 2 0 engages on the upper drum rim 6 against a support ring 22 integrally formed on the outer rim 15 of the lid for securing the drum lid 4 on the upper drum rim 6.

While specific embodiments of the invention have been shown and described in detail to illustrate the inventive principles, it will be understood that the invention may be embodied otherwise without departing from such principles.

2. The lidded drum according to claim 1, wherein the
outer rim of the lid has an engagement groove for the annular
bead of the clamping ring.
3. The lidded drum according to claim 1, comprising a
lower support ring for the annular bead of the clamping ring
which acts on the support ring, wherein the support ring is
integrally formed on the outer rim of the lid rim.
