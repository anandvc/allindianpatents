Title of Invention

ORAL PHARMACEUTICAL TABLET COMPOSITION AND PROCESS OF MAKING THE SAME
Abstract This invention generally relates to various pharmaceutical compositions for oral administration comprising of antiplatelet drug. The present invention provids for an improved film coated tablet wherein coating solution comprises of polyvinyl alcohol. It also describes process of preparation of such tablets. The process of preparation of capsules, effervescent tablets comprising of antiplatelet drug is also disclosed in the present invention.
