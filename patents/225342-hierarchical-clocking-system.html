Title of Invention

HIERARCHICAL CLOCKING SYSTEM
Abstract A method of synchronizing a circuit comprising the steps of synchronising the circuit globally using a high&#8722;frequency clock signal, further synchronising at multiple lower frequencies by cooperative short&#8722;range state machines clocked by the high&#8722;frequency clock, and synchronising the state machines to each other by exchanging rollover signals between them.
