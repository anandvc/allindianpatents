Title of Invention

ELECTRIC-MOTOR THREE-WHEELED VEHICLE
Abstract An electric-motor three-wheeled vehicle is provided that can reduce power loss during power transmission by devising the arrangement of drive-system electric components and that can enable satisfactory body layout. [Solving Means] In an electric-motor three-wheeled vehicle including a motor 24a for applying a drive force to drive wheels WR; a motor control unit for controlling the motor 24a; a secondary battery 85 which stores the electric power generated by a fuel cell 18; a rear body lb supporting a pair of left and right rear wheels WR; and a connecting mechanism 23 which connects the rear body lb with a front body la so as to tilt the front body la while the rear wheels WR are brought into contact with the ground; the secondary battery 8 5 is housed in the inside of a luggage carrier 22 above the rear wheels WR, the motor 24a and the motor control unit composed of a motor driver 50 and a main CPU 53 are mounted between the left and right rear wheels WR. The voltage converter 52 which increases or decreases the voltage of the electric power supplied from the fuel cell 18, and control drivers 54 and 55 which control a supercharger 16 of reaction gas and a electric pump 19 of cooling water, respectively, are adjacently arranged between the left and right rear wheels WR. [Title of the Invention]
Electric-motor Three-wheeled Vehicle
[Technical Field]
[0001]
The present invention relates to electric-motor three-wheeled vehicles, and more particularly, to an electric-motor three-wheeled vehicle that can reduce power 10ss during power transmission by devising the arrangement of drive-train electric components and that can enable satisfactory vehicle body layout.
[Background Art]
[0002]
There have heretofore been known various electric vehicles each having a motor as a drive source. Such electric vehicles need various drive-train electric components, such as a drive motor, a secondary battery and a motor control unit for driving the motor, which are not included in an engine vehicle. Fuel cell-powered vehicles include a fuel cell which generates electric power through the chemical reaction between hydrogen as fuel gas and oxygen contained in reaction gas (air) and drive a motor with the electric power supplied from the fuel cell. The fuel cell-powered vehicles need to

incorporate a supercharger for reaction gas, a motor control driver for a fuel cell-cooling water pump, a voltage converter for increasing or decreasing the voltage of electric power supplied from the fuel cell. In particular, for straddle type vehicles with a small excess space, a method of arranging such electric components has a large influence on vehicle body layout.
[0003]
Patent Document 1 disc10ses a straddle type fuel cell-powei'ed motorcycle configured as be10w. An almost rectangular parallelepipedic fuel cell is disposed in the 10wer portion of a vehicle body so as to orient its 10ngitudinal direction in the back and forth direction of the vehicle body. An almost cylindrical hydrogen cylinder is disposed above the fuel cell. In addition, electrical components are concentratedly installed rearward of the seat and above a rear wheel. [Patent Document 1] Japanese Patent Laid-Open No. 2005-28988
[Disc10sure of the Invention]
[Problem to be Solved by the Invention]
[0004]
However, in the configuration of Patent Document 1,
since the electric components are disposed remote from

the fuel cell, the drive motor and the like, wiring used to connect them is liable to e10ngate. In addition, the vehicle body configuration of the electric-motor vehicle may be a scooter type three-wheeled vehicle that has a 10w f10or type foot rest portion disposed between a steering handlebar and a seat and is provided with two rear wheels. Such a case needs an arrangement method different from that of the motorcycle as in Patent Document 1.
[0005]
It is an object of the present invention to solve the problem of the conventional technique mentioned above and to provide an electric-motor three-wheeled vehicle that can reduce power 10ss during power transmission by devising the arrangement of drive-train electric components and that can enable satisfactory vehicle body layout.
[Means for Solving the Problem]
[0006]
To achieve the object, the present invention is firstly characterized in that in an electric-motor three-wheeled vehicle including: a motor for applying a drive force to drive wheels; a motor control unit for con-rolling the motor; a secondary barrery which stores

electric power; a rear body rotatably supporting a pair of left and right rear wheels as the drive wheels; and a connecting mechanism which swingably connects the rear body with a front body so as to tilt the front body from side to side while the rear wheels are brought into contact with the ground; the secondary battery is disposed above the rear wheels, the motor control unit includes at least a motor driver and a CPU, and the motor and the motor control unit are provided between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body.
[0007]
The invention is secondly characterized by including a fuel cell which generates electric power resulting from chemical reaction between fuel gas and reaction gas, and in that the fuel cell is provided in the front body, and the secondary battery stores the electric power generated by the fuel cell.
[0008]
The invention is thirdly characterized by further including a voltage converter which increases or decreases the voltage of the electric power supplied from the fuel cell and in that the voltage converter is disposed between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body.

[0009]
The invention is fourthly characterized by further including an actuator at least including a supercharger which supplies the reaction gas to the fuel cell and an electric pump which supplies under pressure cooling water of the fuel cell; and a control driver which controls the actuator; and in that the control driver is disposed between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body.
[0010]
The invention is fifthly characterized in that the motor control unit, the voltage converter and the control driver are concentratedly arranged adjacently to each other.
[0011]
The invention is sixthly characterized in that the rear body is provided with a protection member that externally surrounds at least a portion of electric components mounted between the left and right rear wheels.
[Effect of the Invention]
[0012]
According to the first characteristic, the secondary battery is disposed above the rear wheels, the motor control unit includes at least the motor driver and the CPU, and the motor and the motor control unit are

provided between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body. Thus, the drive-train electric components and the secondary battery are concentratedly arranged c10se to the rear wheels, which can reduce the length of wiring connecting the electric components with each other. This can reduce power 10ss during power transmission from the secondary battery. In addition, since the motor and the motor control unit are disposed between the left and right rear wheels, the body layout having satisfactory left-right weight balance of the vehicle body can be provided. Further, since the motor and the motor control unit are provided in the rear body that is not tilted even during turn-traveling, they can be protected against lateral swing. [0013]
According to the second characteristic, the fuel cell is provided which generates electric power resulting from chemical reaction between fuel gas and reaction gas, the fuel cell is provided in the front body, and the secondary battery stores the electric power generated by the fuel cell. The drive-train electric components and the secondary battery are concentratedly arranged c10se to the rear wheels and the fuel cell is disposed in the front portion of the vehicle body. Thus, the fuel cell-

powered three-wheeled vehicle having satisfactory anteroposterior weight balance of the vehicle can be provided.
[0014]
According to the third characteristic, the voltage converter is provided which increases or decreases the voltage of the electric power supplied from the fuel cell and it is disposed between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body. Thus, the drive-train electric components are concentratedly arranged to need short wiring connecting the electric components with each other, which can reduce power 10ss during power transmission. In addition, since the voltage converter tending to be heavy is disposed between the left and right rear wheels, the body layout of the satisfactory left-right weight balance of the vehicle body can be provided.
[0015]
According to the fourth characteristic, provided are the actuator at least including the supercharger which supplies the reaction gas to the fuel cell and the electric pump which supplies under pressure cooling water of the fuel cell; and the control driver which controls the actuator; and the control driver is disposed between the left and right rear wheels in the rear body. Thus,

the electric components are further concentratedly arranged, which needs the short wiring connecting the electric components with each other, making it possible to reduce power 10ss during power transmission. In addition, since many drive-train electric components are mounted to the rear body, the front portion of the vehicle body causes an excess space, which can increase the flexibility of the body layout. [0016]
According to the fifth characteristic, since the motor control unit, the voltage converter and the control driver are concentratedly arranged adjacently to each other, the volume occupied by the electric components can easily be reduced and power 10ss during power transmission can further be reduced. In addition, since the plurality of electric components are concentratedly arranged, assembly manhours can be reduced compared with the configuration where the plurality of electric components are installed in a distributed manner. [0017]
According to the sixth characteristic, the rear body is provided with the protection member that externally surrounds at least a portion of the electric components mounted between the left and right rear wheels.

Thus, the electric components such as the motor control unit and the voltage converter can be protected. [Best Mode for Carrying Out the Invention] [0018]

Therefore, it is easy to access the inside of the rear frame, which facilitates the maintenance of the drive-train electric components. [0031]
In the embodiment as described above, the luggage carrier 22 housing the secondary battery 85 is disposed above the rear wheels WR and the drive motor 24a and the motor control unit are disposed between the left and right rear wheels WR of the rear body lb. Thus, the

electric components can concentratedly be arranged c10se to the rear wheels WR. This needs short wiring extending from the secondary battery 85 to the motor control unit and drive motor 24a via the electric components, whereby power 10ss can be reduced during power transmission. Since the drive motor 24a and the motor control unit are disposed between the left and right rear wheels WR, the body layout having satisfactory left-right weight balance of the vehicle body can be provided. Further, since the rear body lb is not banked during turn-traveling, the drive motor 24a and the motor control unit can be protected from the swing of the left-right direction. [0032]
With the arrangement configuration described above, since many drive-train electric components are concentratedly mounted, the wiring connecting the electric components with each other can be reduced in length, thereby reducing power 10ss during power transmission. In addition, since many electric components are mounted in the rear body, the flexibility of the body layout can be increased on the front side of the vehicle body. In the present embodiment, the motor control unit (the motor driver 50 and main CPU 53), the voltage converter 52, and the control drivers 54, 55 are

concentratedly arranged adjacently to each other. This makes it easy to reduce the installation space for the drive-train electric components and can further reduce the power 10ss during power transmission. Additionally, assembly manhours can be reduced compared with the configuration where many electric components are arranged in a distributed manner. [0033]
The pair of left and right sub-frames 34 is connected to each other by the connecting pipe 3 5 as a protection member that extends vehicle-widthwise to protect the electric components from above. This further increases the rigidity of the rear frame 47. Since the protection member composed of the sub-frames 34 and the connecting pipe 35 is arranged between the rear wheels WR so as to surround the electric components including the motor control unit, it functions to protect them. A stay 35a which extends toward the front side of the vehicle body and supports the DC-DC converter 51 is joined to the connecting pipe 35. The rear lateral end of the power unit 24 is disposed to slightly project rearward from the framework of the base frames 32. However, if the rear wheels WR are attached, the rear ends of the rear wheels WR are 10cated slightly rearward of the rear lateral end

of the power unit 24. Thus, it is less probable that the power unit 24 comes into contact with foreign matter or the like. [0034]
