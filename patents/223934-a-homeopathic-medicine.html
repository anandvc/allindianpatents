THE FOLLOWING SPECIFICATION PARTICULARLY DESCRIBES THE INVENTION AND THE MANNER IN WHICH IT IS TO BE PERFORMED.


The present invention relates to "Homeopathic medicine".
The present invention relates generally to methods for the treatment of Immunological medicated disorders in extreme stage, i.e. when the condition of patient is very serious for such situation a micro dose of HIV-Type-I virus is given to the patient.
The homneopathically Potentized (micro dose) HIV-Type I and HIV-TYPE-II virus used for the treatment of immunologically medicated disorders of the respiratory system such as sarcoidosis, asthma, and lung cancer, for treatment of allergic disorder such as atopic dermatic and eczema and for Psertains, as well as for the treatment of prevention of infections diseases.
DESCRIPTION OF EXISTING PRODUCT:-
Tuberculosis is a chronic, infections disease that is caused by infection. Although the infection may be asymptomatic for a considerable period of time, the disease is most commonly manifested as a chronic inflammation of the lungs resulting in fever & respiratory symptoms if left untreated may cause death.
Although tuberculosis can generally be controlled using extended antibiotic therapy, such treatment is not sufficient to prevent the spread of the diseases. Vaccination is also other method for immunological diseases. The most common mycobacterium employed for this purpose is Bacille Calmetic-Guerin. Another mycobacterium that has been used for immunotherapy for tuberculosis and also leprosy is Mycobacterium Vaccal (M. Vaccal).. But it is not effective vaccine.

The first objective is to develop a product, which will work effectively in practice and should be used for wide range of diseases and disorder.

SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
According to this invention, there is provided a homeopathic medicine comprising 1 ml of the serum, obtained by known pathological methods, of a patient detected for HIV-I virus mixed with 99 drops of ethyl alcohol duly potentized by serial dilutions with a suitable medium and striking with powerful strokes with a mechanical device.
Typically, the suitable medium is ethyl alcohol, distilled water or saline or a combination of any of them.
Typically, the potentization is affected by striking a receptacle field with the medicine with at least 15 strokes.
3

Typically, the serial dilutions are serially lc, 2c, 3c, 30c, ...50c,
...100c, ....200c, ....500c, and the like, every "c" standing for a dilution of 100 times.
Details Description :-
HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) infection has two major type HIV Type-I which are found in the serum of Patient or carriers who are HIV Positive. For the preparation of Homeopathically Potentized (micro-dose) HIV Type - I Virus militer of serum (which contain the virus) Patient who is detected positive for HIV virus is mixed with about 99 drops of ethyl alcohol, which is used as vehicle. This mixture is allow to undergo a process of potentization in which serial dilution is done with powerful strokes with the help of a mechanical device to arrive to Potentized (micro-dose) HIV-Type-I virus 1C i.e. potency. I.e. HIV Type I-1C.
Vehicle is a medium comprises of ethyl alcohol distilled water, normal saline or any such neutral medium.
About 1ml of HIV-Type IIC is mixed with about 99ml of ethyl alcohol to undergo the same process of Potentization to arrive to HIV-I-2C potency. Similarly, the process is carried out to achieve 3c, 4c, ...30c, —50c, — 100c, 200c, 1000c lmillion and above for clinical use the proportion of original substances (serum) to vehicle may vary from 1:99 to 50:50.

CASE STUDIES:
This is the case of Mr. S.T. (Patient Ref No. L-6731) 27 years old male patient, who reported to clinic with complaints of Psoriasis since 4 years. The complaints had increased since 1 month. He had taken allopathic as well as Homeopathic treatment in the past for a long time but did not benefit much from there. The patient was prescribed a remedy called HIV-1 which was prescribed in increasing potencies of 50c, 100c and 150c over a period of time. Within 4 months of treatment his complaints were better by more than 90%. He rashes which were present all over his body and almost disappeared and there was no itching anymore. He did get a few new spots on the arms during the course of the treatment but these disappeared with the medicines within a short time.
The present invention have some advantages as compared to the conventional treatment.
The present medicine has been used effectively for a wide range of diseases and disorders as a therapeutic agent.
The present medicine seems to be working as a good immunodulator and curative for several diseases conditions such as
(a) Skin diseases: Psoriasis, Urticaria, Lichen Planus, warts, vitiligo,
eczema, and other allergic or immunalogically mediated disorders
proposed to use for the patients who are HIV Positive.
(b) For recurring viral infections and other opportunities infections.
Due to its potentized form, it is free from live virus but it is presumed to retain the antigenic properties of the virus.
