Title of Invention

COMPOSITION COMPRISING A PDE4 INHIBITOR AND A PDE5 INHIBITOR
Abstract The invention relates to the combined administration of PDE4 inhibitor and a PDE5 inhibitor for the treatment of a disease in which phophodiesterase 4 (PDE4) and / or phosphodiesterase 5 (PDE5) activity is detrimental.
