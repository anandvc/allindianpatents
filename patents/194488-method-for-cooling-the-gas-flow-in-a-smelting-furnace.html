Title of Invention

METHOD FOR COOLING THE GAS FLOW IN A SMELTING FURNACE
Abstract The present invention relates to a method for cooling the gas flow exiting through the uptake of a smelting furnace such as a flash-smelting furnace with a finely dispersed liquid sprays, which prevents the solid matter particles contained within from coalescing and sintering
