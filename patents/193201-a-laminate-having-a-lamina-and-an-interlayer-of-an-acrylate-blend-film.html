Title of Invention

A LAMINATE HAVING A LAMINA AND AN INTERLAYER OF AN ACRYLATE BLEND FILM
Abstract This invention relates to a laminate consisting of at least one lamina and at least one interlayer useful as safety glasses and architectural applications.The lamina may be glass and the interlayer is made of a film produced by blending ethylene methyl acrylate copolymer and ethylene butyl acrylate copolymer. The laminate may be a bilayer or multilayer laminate.
