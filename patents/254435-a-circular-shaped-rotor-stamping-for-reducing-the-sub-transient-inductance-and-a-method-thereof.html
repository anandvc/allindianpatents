Title of Invention

A CIRCULAR SHAPED ROTOR STAMPING FOR REDUCING THE SUB-TRANSIENT INDUCTANCE AND A METHOD THEREOF
Abstract This invention refers to a type of electromatnetic rotating machine used as a powr source called a COMPULSATOR, in the area of electrical power engineering. In the present invention a method of manufacturing a drop in field winding with squirrel cage type of compensating winding, which ensures minimum sun-transient inductances for short pulse generation is claimed.
