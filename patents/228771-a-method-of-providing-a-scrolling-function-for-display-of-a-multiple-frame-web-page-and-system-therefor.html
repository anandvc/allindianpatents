Title of Invention

A METHOD OF PROVIDING A SCROLLING FUNCTION FOR DISPLAY OF A MULTIPLE FRAME WEB PAGE AND SYSTEM THEREFOR
Abstract A scroll device (20) may be provided on a processor-based system (48) to simultaneously scroll both of at least two frames (12, 16) in two separate windows (24). Thus, each frame (12,16) is scrolled until a first frame reaches its beginning or end. At that point, the scrolling of the first frame stops automatically while the other frame continues to scroll.
