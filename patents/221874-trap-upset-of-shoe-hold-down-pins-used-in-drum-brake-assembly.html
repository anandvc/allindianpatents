Title of Invention

TRAP UPSET OF SHOE HOLD DOWN PINS USED IN DRUM BRAKE ASSEMBLY
Abstract This invention of Trap upset of Shoe hold down pins denotes the process inside closed two open dies. The function of the Trap upset is to enlarge the portion. Steel in coil form fed into an automatic Cold forging machine to cut preform and final form as per the profile of pre-part. The pre-part is manually fed into another machine with two split dies and a punch. The Punch moves forward while the product is gripped between the two dies and the moving pin trap upsets the profile and return back and stops.When the two split dies automatically move out, the product is taken out as a final part.

Details of process:
Process I:
Heading of blanks using an ordinary single die double blow header
1. Input to machine: Cold heading quality annealed wire of suitable diameter.
2. Input to die: Wire cut-off
3. Output from the die: Co(3 headed pre part.
The above process Is equivalent to any notmal cold heading process done with a single die and two punches to get the headed profile.

Process - II
Profile formation using Rod Header.
6. Input to the machine: Cold headed part fed manually to the machine.
7. Machine consists of two split dies with profile on each side matched and lapped to suit wire size.
8. Trap pin acts as the front stopper for the input part.
9. The part is placed between the two split dies and the trap pin moves forward during ttie stroke of the machine.
10. As the two split dies are gripped, cold forming taken place and the die cavities are filled thus producing the part.
