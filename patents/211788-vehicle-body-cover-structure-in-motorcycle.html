Title of Invention

VEHICLE BODY COVER STRUCTURE IN MOTORCYCLE
Abstract A motorcycle in which an engine unit arranged below a main frame is suspended from a body frame having a main frame extending down backward from a head pipe, in which the main frame is covered at its two sides with a pair of left and right main frame side covers, and in which the main frame is covered at its upper portion with an upper cover. At least part of the front upper ends of the paired main frame side covers (31L, 31R) supported by the body frame (F) are joined at the back of the head pipe (12), and the front portion of the upper cover (34) is fastened to the joined portions of the two main frame side covers (31L, 31R). Therefore, a stay for mounting at least the front portion of the upper cover on the body frame side can be eliminated to reduce the number of parts.

DESCRIPTION
VEHICLE BODY COVER STRUCTURE IN MOTORCYCLE FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to a motorcycle which comprises a vehicle body frame comprising a main frame extended downwardly rearward from a head pipe, an engine unit disposed on the lower side of the main frame and hung from the vehicle body frame, a left-right pair of main fram6 side covers covering both lateral sides of the main frame, and an upper cover covering the upper side of the main frame, and particularly to an improvement in a vehicle body cover structure including the main frame side covers and the upper cover, BACKGROUND ART
Hitherto, a structure in which main frame side covers and an upper cover are fitted to stays provided on a vehicle body frame has been known, as, for example, disclosed in Japanese Patent Application Laid-open No. 10-16851.
However, in the structure according to the prior art as above, the number of stays provided on the vehicle body frame is increased, leading to the increase in number of
component parts*
The present invention has been made in consideration of the above situations, and it is an object of the present invention to provide a vehicle body cover structure for

motorcycle which makes it possible to reduce the number of component parts. DISCLOSURE OF INVENTION
In order to attain the above object, the present invention is firstly characterized in that, in a motorcycle which comprises a vehicle body frame comprising a main frame extended downwardly rearwards from a head pipe, an engine unit disposed on the lower side of the main frame and hung from the vehicle body frame, a left-right pair of main frame side covers covering both lateral sides of the main frame, and an upper cover covering the upper side of the main frame, at least portions of the upper ends of front portions of the left-right pair of main frame side covers supported by the vehicle body frame are overlapped with each other on the rear side of the head pipe, and a front portion of the upper cover is fastened to the overlapped portions of the main frame side covers.
According to the constitution of the first characteristic feature, a stay for fitting at least a front portion of the upper cover to the vehicle body frame side is unnecessary, the number of component parts can be reduced by reducing the number of stays to be provided on the vehicle body frame, and the layout of the cables and the like can be facilitated by eliminating a stay restricting the degree of freedom in laying out cables and the like disposed in the vicinity of the head pipe. Besides, appearance can be enhanced by good matching of the

upper ends of front portions of the left-right pair of main frame side covers and a front portion of the upper cover.
In addition to the constitution of the first characteristic feature, the present invention is secondly characterized in that at least portions of front portions of a left-right pair of rear side covers covering both sides of a rear portion of the vehicle body frame on the lower side of a rider's seat and supported by the vehicle body frame are overlapped with each other on the lower side of a front portion of the rider's seat, and a rear portion of the upper cover is fastened to the overlapped portions of the rear side covers. According to this constitution, it is unnecessary to provide the vehicle body frame with a stay for fitting the rear portion of the upper cover to the vehicle body frame side, so that the number of component parts can be more reduced, and a space for laying out component parts such as electrical equipments can be secured between the rear portion of the upper cover and the vehicle body frame.
In addition to the second characteristic feature, the present invention is thirdly characterized in that the upper cover comprises a front upper cover and a rear upper cover disconnectably connected to each other, and a rear portion of the front upper cover is fastened to the main frame side covers. According to this constitution, a portion near the center in the front-rear direction of the upper cover can be fixed to the main frame side covers, it

is unnecessary to detach the upper cover as a whole at the time of maintenance of an ignition unit or the like, and it is possible to detach only the rear upper cover, whereby maintenance work can be facilitated.
In addition to the third characteristic feature, the present invention is fourthly characterized in that a front portion of the rear upper cover is engaged with a rear portion of the front upper cover while covering the location of fastening of the rear portion of the front upper cover to the main frame side covers. According to this constitution, the appearance can be enhanced, fitting and detaching of the rear upper cover is facilitated, and maintenance work can be more facilitated by covering the location of fastening of the rear portion of the front upper cover to the main frame side covers.
In addition to the constitution of the first characteristic feature, the present invention is fifthly characterized in that the upper ends of the front portions of the main frame side covers are engaged with each other so as to maintain the overlapped condition of the main frame side covers. According to this constitution, at the time of fastening the front portion of the upper cover to the overlapped portions of the main frame side covers, fastening work can be facilitated through maintaining the overlapped condition of the main frame side covers, and the fastened conditions of the upper ends of the front portions of the main frame side covers and the front portion of the

upper cover can be made rigid.
In addition to the first characteristic feature, the present invention is sixthly characterized in that the upper side of fastened portions of upper portions of the main frame side covers to the main frame is covered by the upper cover. According to this constitution, the fastened portions of the upper portions of the main frame side covers to the main frame are hidden by the upper cover, whereby appearance can be enhanced.
In addition to the first characteristic feature, the present invention is seventhly characterized in that the outside surfaces of a left-right pair of rear side covers covering both sides of a rear portion of the vehicle body frame on the lower side of a rider's seat and supported by the vehicle body frame are each demarcated into a coated zone and non-coated zones. According to this constitution, without composing each rear side cover of a plurality of cover parts, a part or parts of the rear side cover are made to be non-coated covers, whereby an excellent color effect on a design basis can be obtained while preventing an increase in the number of component parts and contriving a reduction in cost, and it is possible to ensure that scratches and stains will not be conspicuous. In addition, the demarcation between the coated zone and non-coated zones can be freely changed.
In addition to the seventh characteristic feature, the present invention is eighthly characterized in that

portions of the rear side covers located on the lower side of a front half portion of the rider's seat are each set to be the non-coated zone. According to this constitution, the non-coated zone is disposed at the portion with which the rider' s feet are liable to come into contact, whereby it is possible to ensure that scratches and stains on the rear side covers will not be conspicuous.
Further, in addition to the seventh characteristic feature, the present invention is ninthly characterized in that lower end portions of front portions of the rear side covers are each set to be the non-coated zone. According to this constitution, the non-coated zone is disposed at the portion with which the rider's feet are liable to come into contact, whereby it is possible to ensure that scratches and stains on the rear side covers will not be conspicuous, BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF DRAWINGS

Next, the action or effect of this embodiment will be described. In the vehicle body cover 30 covering the vehicle body, at least one part of the upper ends of front portions (in this embodiment, the whole parts of the upper ends * of front portions) of the left-right pair of main frame side covers 31L, 31R supported on the vehicle body frame F while covering both lateral sides of the main frame 13 are overlapped with each other on the rear side of the head pipe 12, and a front portion of the upper cover 34 covering the upper side of the main frame 13 is fastened to the overlapped portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R.

Therefore, a stay for fitting at least the front portion of the upper cover 34 to the side of the vehicle body frame F is unnecessary, the number of stays to be provided on the vehicle body frame F is reduced, hence the number of component parts can be reduced, and stays which would reduce the degree of freedom in laying out cables such as a throttle cable 95 and a carburetor choke cable 96 (See Fig. 4) in the vicinity of the head pipe 12 are eliminated, thereby facilitating the layout of the cables and the like. In addition, appearance can be enhanced by good matching of the upper ends of the front portions of the left-right pair of main frame side covers 31L, 31R and the front portion of the upper cover 34,
Besides, the upper ends of the front portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R are engaged with each other so as to maintain the overlapped condition of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R; therefore, at the time of fastening the front portion of the upper cover 34 to the overlapped portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R, the fastening work can be facilitated by maintaining the overlapped conditions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R, and the fastened condition of the upper ends of the front portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R and the front portion of the upper cover 34 can be made rigid.
In addition, the upper side of the fastened portions of the upper portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R onto the main frame 13 is covered by the upper cover 34,

so that the fastened portions of the upper portions of the main frame side covers 31L, 31R onto the main frame 13 are hidden by the upper cover 3 4, whereby appearance can be enhanced.
