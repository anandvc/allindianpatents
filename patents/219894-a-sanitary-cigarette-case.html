Title of Invention

" A SANITARY CIGARETTE CASE"
Abstract A package for cigarettes, in particularly, a sanitary cigarette case which can avoid filter tips from being contacted by fingers, so avoiding germs on finger from spreading into mouth through filter tips, includes a case body and a case cover and is characterized in that the opening surface of the case body is parallel with the longitudinal axis of cigarettes, the opening end of the case body is covered by the case cover, which has a concavity in its longer face. The advantage of this cigarette case is that it is designed in such a configuration so that person can only put the middle of cigarette when they pick cigarette out of cigarette case, thereby avoiding filter tips from being contacted by fingers, so preventing germs on fingers from spreading into mouth through filter tips. Therefore, this cigarette case has sanitary, convenience, elegant operation, simple process and low cost.
Full Text TECHNICAL FIELD OF THE INVENTION
The present invention relates to sanitary cigarette package, especially relates to a sanitary cigarette case which can avoid filter tips of cigarettes therein from being contacted by fingers so as to prevent germs on fingers from spreading into mouth through the filter tips.
BACKGROUND ART OF THE INVENTION
At present, the known high-grade cigarette package is generally a chipboard, cigarette case with the cover turned up, in which generally accommodates twenty cigarettes, there are also cases for accommodating the cigarette whose number is nine, ten and sixteen etc, an opening is provided at the short side of the rectangle cigarette case, the opening surface is disposed transversely with the longitudinal axis of the cigarette. A defect of this kind of cigarette case having been used for mpny years is that the cigarette is taken out from the cigarette case only by holding the filter tip with hand, while the filter tip. having contacted with the finger is to be contacted with lip while smoking, which leads to germs on fingers spreading into mouth through filter tip as a result of making the unhealthy smoking habit more insanitary, sometimes, the smoker shall invite other person to have the cigarette by nipping the filter tip, this is not only insanitary but also increasing the infecting source of illness, and the awkward is often occurred; the function of the filter tip is initially for filtering the harmful substance to the human body in the cigarette in order to greatly protect people's health, but the existing cigarette case structure makes the filter tip to be the medium inducing the harmful substance into the human body. In order to avoid this conflict, some smokers would like to take the cigarette by nipping the end with exposed tobacco leaf by opening the bottom of the cigarette case in stead of taking the cigarette from the opening, although, this way is sanitary,
but which is easy to make the tabacco leaf loose by nipping the end to which the tobacco leaf is exposed the cigarette is easy to fail out, thus affecting the appearance of the cigarette case and the cigarette quality, and also the operation is complex.
SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
Concerning the above, the inventive purpose of the present invention is to provide a kind of sanitary, convenient package structure, which can make people only take the middle portion of cigarette when they pick cigarette out of cigarette case, thus not only avoiding insanitary and infectivity while taking cigarette tip, but also avoiding cigarette leaf loose while taking the end to which cigarette leaf is exposed, therefor , this cigarette case has simple structure and convenience elegant operation.
The present invention is originated from the inventor's years practical experience of different kinds of package research and repetitious hard work.
In regard to the disadvantages of the prior art, the purpose of this invention is to provide a sanitary cigarette case, which can make people self-conscious avoiding fingers . from contacting with filter tip, thus preventing germs on fingers from spreading into mouth through filter tip.
