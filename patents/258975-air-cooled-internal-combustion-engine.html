Title of Invention

AIR-COOLED INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE
Abstract To improve air-cooling effect in a cooling air passage and to provide an ignition plug at such position that enables easy mounting and dismounting while enabling the effective air-cooling thereof. In an air-coolinged internal combustion engine including a vlue gear mechanism proviede above a combustion chamber, and a cooling air passage extending in the fore-and-aft direction between the aforementioned combustion chamber and the aforementioned value gear mechaism along an exhaust colling air passage in communication with the aforementioned cooling air passage extending in the fore-and-aft direction between the aforementioned exhaaust port and the intake port, an ignition plug provided in the laterally extending cooling air passage, and a cooling fin curved from the aforementioned cooling air passage extending in the fore-and-aft direction toward the aforementioned laterally extending cooling air passage. Furthermore, a centerline of the fore-and-aft direction toward the aforementioned laterally extending colling air passage. Furtheremore, a centerline of the aforementioned exchaust port is inclined with resepect to the fore-and-aft direction of an internal combustion engine so as to broaden the front opening of the aforementioned cooling air passage extending in the fore-and-aft direction.
