Title of Invention

"A DEVICE FOR CLEANING TEETH AND MESSAGING GUMS"
Abstract A device for cleaning teeth and massaging gums. It comprises a soft tubular structure. One end of the tube is covered and other end is open so that a finger or a thumb can be inserted tightly into it. Certain areas of the outer side of it have striations in patterns. These striations give mild but effective rubbing on the surface of the teeth to remove food particles stains stuck on the surface of the teeth. These striations also help in pulling out fibers that get stuck between the teeth. The tube is of soft elastic material and can be bent by the finger to reach interior of the teeth easily. Suitable toothpaste can he applied on the striations. The gums can be massaged in suitable direction by the finger to keep the gum fit and healthy. The finger to the extent required applies the pressure.
Full Text TITLE :This invention relates to a device for cleaning teeth and massaging gums.
FIELD OF INVENTION : Particularly this invention relates to a device for reducing formation of cavities in teeth and messaging the gums and to keep the gums in proper shape.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION WITH REGARDS TO THE DRAWBACK OF ASSOCIATED WITH KNOWN ART : The teeth need to be cleaned thoroughly reguiarly so that not a spec of food particle or food fiber remains in the teeth. Gum needs to be messaged so that it remains in its position and continues to give full support to the teeth. Existing device is a toothbrush having bristles for cleaning teeth. The fine bristles in the toothbrush tend to get in between the teeth and the gums. This tends to poke and lift the gums and the gums tend to recede backwards. This receding of the gums exposes fine pits between teeth where food particles can easily settle in. These food particles if not removed can create cavities. Some time food fiber gets stuck in between the teeth and does not come out easily. This food fiber does not come out easily by brushing with toothbrush. This entangled food fiber starts rotting which can create cavities. Another practice is using toothpick or small sticks or pins to remove the fiber. This practice damages the gum, as it tends to poke or lift the gum from its place. Conventional toothbrush have draw backs in removing food particles and food fiber completely and easily thus resulting in formation of cavities and gaps even after regular use of toothbrush. The toothbrush used presently has another problem of reaching interiors of the teeth and gum. The inner side of the teeth and teeth situated at the ends can not be reached easily. The inner side of the teeth formation is concave in nature where as the toothbrush is straight. As a result many of the teeth in the interior remains stuck with food particles. The sticking of the food particle and food fiber are some of the cause of cavity formation and teeth decay. The cavity in the teeth after some time causes further problem to the teeth. Thus the problem starts with interior teeth easily. It is very common that most of the people after some time starts having problem with

teeth situated at the interior of the mouth. There have been attempts in improving the toothbrush design by giving various bends, spring action, swivel joints. But the above shortcoming in the toothbrush is not overcome. It is not possible to massage the gum with toothbrush. Messaging of the gums is very necessary to renew the position of the gums to its original position so that gum covers up the exposed pits and gaps between teeth. Messaging the gum also help in removing food particles which may have got in between the gum and the teeth. Messaging also help the gum to sit tightly on to the gum and thus stopping food particles entering between gum and he teeth.
OBJECT OF THE INVENTION: Object of this invention is to offer a much superior yet simple alternative to toothbrush for cleaning teeth and also help in massaging gums simultaneously. Thrust is towards reaching cleaning action to nook and corner of the teeth profile and reducing the chances of damaging the gum. Thrust is also to improving the cleaning efficiency and simultaneously massaging the gum thus ensuring that the gum is messaged regularly along with cleaning of the teeth in a short time in one operation. Messaging the gum is very essential so that gum maintains its profile and do not recede backwards away from the tooth.
A SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION: According to this invention there is provided a device for cleaning teeth and massaging gums which comprises a soft elastic tubular structure and one end of the soft elastic tubular structure is covered and other end is open so that a finger or a thumb can be inserted tightly into it and
the device to have suitable size so as to fit tightly on to a finger or a thumb and the finger can apply the required pressure on the gum through the device so as to massage the gum and help the device to reach nook and corner of the teeth profile for cleaning teeth and
certain areas of the outer surface of it have fine raised striations in patterns to give mild but very effective rubbing action required for dislodging food particles and food fiber from the teeth as well as from the fine pits between teeth and

that these striations are in certain patterns to help in pulling out food fiber that get stuck between the teeth and reduce damage to the gum and
that the tube is of soft elastic material and can be bent by the finger to reach interior of the teeth easily and
that suitable toothpaste can be applied on the striations and the gum can be massaged in suitable direction by the finger to keep the gum fit and healthy and
the device to have suitable thickness to improve longevity and to protect the gum from not being hit by hard portion of the finger and the nail.
