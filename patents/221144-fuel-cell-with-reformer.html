Title of Invention

FUEL CELL WITH REFORMER
Abstract The fuel cell with a reformer according to the present invention comprises a solid electrolytic layer 56 of proton conductor or oxygen ion conductor disposed among a reforming Layer 52 containing a catalyst 50 for reforming a hydrogen-content substance, a hollow layer 60 through which air containing oxygen flows and a reforming layer 52; a first electrode layer 54 formed on the surface of the solid electrolytic layer 56 opposed to the reforming cell 52; and a second electrode Layer 581 formed on the surface of the solid electrolytic layer 56 opposed to the hollow layer 60. Whereby electric energy is outputted form the- first electrode layer 54 and the second electrode layer 56, Thus, the present invention can provide a fuel cell with, a reformer which has a simple system and can solve problems of hearing.
