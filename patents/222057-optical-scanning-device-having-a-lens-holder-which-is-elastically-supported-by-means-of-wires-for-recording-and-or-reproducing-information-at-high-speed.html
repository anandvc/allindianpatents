Title of Invention

OPTICAL SCANNING DEVICE HAVING A LENS HOLDER WHICH IS ELASTICALLY SUPPORTED BY MEANS OF WIRES FOR RECORDING AND/OR REPRODUCING INFORMATION AT HIGH SPEED
Abstract This invention relates to an optical scanning device having a lens holder (LH) which is elastically supported by means of wires (SW), for recording and/or reproducing information at high speed, said lens holder (LH) being formed of a ho0llow body in which side walls (SF) have a depression for accommodating a focus coil (FC) and webs (ST) formed by the depression and whose side walls (SF) are arranged such that they run at an angle to the wires (SW) when the lens holder (LH) is in the neutral position. The optical scanning device has a lens holder (LH) which is formed by a hollow body having side walls (SF) forming a depression for accommodating a focus coil (FC) and forming webs (ST) formed by the depression and which are connected via a circuit board (PCB) having connection points for the elastic support and coils arranged on the lens holder (LH).
