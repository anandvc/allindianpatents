Title of Invention

A SHAVING IMPLEMENT.
Abstract A shaving implement comprises a shaving head member (SH) and a rubberized composite long handle member (60), the said shaving head member comprises: a top cap member (20) having projected pips, a lubricating strip (LS) attached on said top cap member (20), a top blade (30), a bottom blade (40), a spacer member (50) for maintaining the said top blade and said bottom blade in a spaced parallel relationship, the said rubberized composite long handle member (60) comprises a blade support portion (BSS)A platform (PF), a guard portion (C) and swarf slots which is a combination of guard portion and handle portion moulded integrally as a single part and rubberized grip portion (ER) is over moulded on composite handle in the injection moulding machine wherein all the components of the said shaving head member (SH) and the rubberized composite handle member (60) are placed one over the other on the machine and the said projected pips (PP) of the said top cap (20) are riveted by means of cold form riveting so that shaving handle with blades cannot be detached.
Full Text Field of the Invention
The present invention relates to a shaving implement and more
particularly to a shaving implement wherein shaving head member and
rubberized composite long handle member cannot be detached.
Description of the prior art
The disposable razor which is available in the market is embedding the
blades in a plastic shaving head. It has been discovered that a twin blade
shaving system in which geometrical relationships like blade tangent angle,
protrusion and SPAN are maintained with critical limits, provide superior
shaving characteristics. In such a shaving system, the two cutting edges are
successfully active with respect to hair elements being cut during single shaving
stroke. A guard structure with platform known as seat as an integral part of the
handle has a fixed relation to the cutting edges. The top cap attached with
lubricated strip portion which have pins on the body to locate and cover the
blades revetted to the seat part to complete the shaving head. Lubra strip
manufactured out of water soluble resin gives lubricating effect during shaving
and improves comfort.
The shaving implement of the present invention differs from
conventional shaving implement in such a way that the shaving efficiency is
improved and this is achieved that the shaving head assembly is made out in
conjunction with the handle cap with lubricated strip and a rubber grip as an
integral part of the handle.

Summary of the Invention
It is therefore an important object of the present invention to provide a"
shaving implement which is novel in its construction, easy to manufacture and
obviates all disadvantages and drawbacks associated with the shaving systems
existing in the art.
A further object of the present invention is to provide a shaving
implement wherein the handle cannot be replaceable or removable from the
shaving head.
Yet the another object of the present invention is to provide a shaving
implement wherein lubricated strip is attached to top cap and thermo plastic
elestomer grip is provided to avoid slipping of handle during shaving and give
extra grip.
According to the present invention there is provided a shaving
implement comprises a shaving head member and a rubberized composite long
handle member, the said shaving head member comprises: a top cap member
having projected pips, a lubricating strip attached on said top cap member, a
top blade, a bottom blade, a spacer member for maintaining the said top blade
and said bottom blade in a spaced parallel relationship, the said rubberized
composite long handle member comprises a blade support portion, a platform,
guard portion and swarf slots and a rubberized grip portion wherein all the
components of the said shaving head member are placed one over the other on
the assembly machine and the said projected pips of the said top cap member
are revetted on to the said long handle member so that shaving head member
and rubberized composite long handle member cannot be detached.

Detailed Description of the Preferred Embodiments
Referring to the accompanying drawings of Figures 1 to 10,13 and 14, the
present invention, thus provides a shaving implement comprising a rubberized
Composite long handle member(60) which accommodates shaving head (SH)
components such as top cap member(20) having projected pips (PP), a top blade
(30) having four square holes in the same pitch of top cap pips and swarf slots to
clear the swarf, a bottom blade(40) having four square holes similar to top blade and
a spacer member (50) having four round holes in the similar pitch of top blade for
maintaining the said top and bottom blades in a spaced parallel relation ship wherein
the above components are placed one over the other locating on the top cap pips on
the machine and the said projected pips (PP) of top cap are riveted by means of cold
form riveting so that the shaving head (SH) with blades cannot be detachable.
-6-
The rubberized composite handle member 60 has blade support portion
BSS, guard portion C and swarf slots SL and rubber grip ER moulded as an
integral part on injection moulding machine. The bottom blade 40 is having
first cutting edges CEB and top blade 30 is having second cutting edge CET
and the top and bottom cutting edges each having a blade tangent angle BTA
in the range of 18-30 degree and span in the range of 0.05 to 2.00mm. The
bottom blade 40 is having protrusion PROT-1 of 0.33+/-0.03 and the top
blade 30 is having protrusion PROT-II of 0.07+/-0.07. The over moulded
composite long handle 60 is provided with round neck N attached to the
platform PF one side and long handle H with grooves on the other side also
facilitates easy nesting when it is kept one over the other during packing. The
rubberised composite long handle is a handle combined with a platform guard
portion which are moulded integrally having specific holes for accommodating
all components and also having curved ribs CR on entire length of hollow
portion to give rigidity for the handles 60.
Among the important geometrical relationships blade tangent angle
BTA, protrusion PROT and span S1,S2 are the important parameters in the
twin shaving systems. The above parameters are maintained with critical limits
to provide superior shaving characteristics. In this shaving systems two blades
30 and 40 are placed parallel to each other and maintain a gap of 0.4 to 0.6mm
by spacing spacer member 50 in between the top and the bottom blades. Both
the blades are placed in such a way that each blade having a tangent angle
BTA1,BTA2 in the range of 18-30 degree and span S1 & S2 in the range of 0.5
to 2.00mm. The cutting edges are supported by spacer teeth point TP "FIG.6"
maintained a parallel relations to one another.
-7-
A guard structure C is provided on the platform of the rubberized
composite long handle member 60 in a fixed relation to the cutting edges to
define desired geometrical relationship include the above referred blade tangent
angle protrusion and span.
Referring to figure 11 this is illustrated the manner in which the span S is
determined. The span S of bottom blade 40 "S1" is the distance between the
bottom blade cutting edge CEB and tangent point C of immediately proceeding
skin engaging surface i.e. guard portion. The span S of top blade 30 "S2" is the
distance between the top blade cutting edge GET and tangent point of
immediately skin engaging surface i.e. bottom cutting edge. The span S1 and S2
fall within the range of 0.5 — 2.00mm.
Referring to figure 12 this is illustrated the manner in which the
protrusion PROT is determined. The protrusion of bottom blade 40 PROT-1
is determined by extending a line C-e between the tangent points immediately
proceeding the following top cutting edge, i.e. from the guard tangent point C
to the top blade cutting edge and parallel to the line C-e. The perpendicular
distance between the lines C-e and h-h define PROT-1. The PROT-1 falls
within the range 0.03-0.03mm.
The protrusion of top blade 30 PROT-2 determined by extending a line
a-o between the tangent point immediately proceeding the following bottom
cutting edge that is from the top cap tangent point o to the bottom blade
cutting edge. A line 1-j is then extended tangent to the top blade cutting edge
and parallel to line a-o. The perpendicular distance between the lines a-o and
1-j defines PROT-2. The PROT-2 falls within the range of 0.03+/-0.03mm.
