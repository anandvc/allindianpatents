Title of Invention

AN OLIGONUCLEOTIDE COMPRISING A NON-HOMOPOLYMERIC SEQUENCE OF NUCLEOSIDE SUBUNITS
Abstract The present invention relsate to an oligonucleotide comprising a non-homopolymeric sequence of nucleoside subunits joined by at least one inter-subunit linkage that is a N3 - P5' thiophosphoramidate, wherein the oligonucleotide can inhibit or reduce telomerase enzyme activity.
