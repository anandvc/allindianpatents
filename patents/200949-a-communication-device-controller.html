Title of Invention

A COMMUNICATION DEVICE CONTROLLER.
Abstract A communication device controller (1)comprising: a signal receive / send (13) unit with a SIM socket used for mobile communication system; a signal process unit connected to a plurality of phone lines and said signal receive / send unit; and a power supply unit (15) providing stable power for said signal receive / send unit and said signal process unit; whereby said communication device controller (1) been settled in the field where electrical signal is more stronger and using telephones to send / receive cellular phone could achieve the functions of using cellular phones instead of telephones in the field where hard to set up wires of telephones and amplifying a plurality of extensive phones indoor.

Field of the Invention
The present invention relates a communication device controller andmore specially to a communication device been settled in the field where electrical signal is more stronger and using telephones to send / receive cellular phone could achieve the functions of using cellular phones instead of telephones in the field where hard to set up wires of telephones and amplifying a plurality of extensive phone indoor.
Description of the prior art
Following the technology changing with each pa.ssing day, communicative skill advancing day-by-day and government policy liberating, the communicative quality of cellular phone has been improved by way of setting station widely. Populations of using cellular phone increase by exponentially rate, meanwhile the fee cost of cellular phone relatively lower than that was.

home or individual user must apply to the communication system providers for wiring telephones lines from station or divider to the user terminal. In the city, the telephones net has been developed preferably, so that to set telephones lines is easily and quickly. Nevertheless, in the wild area, especially in secluded area, it will cost long period to wire telephones lines and connect the user terminals and the communication system providers.
After developing cellular phones, for sake of convenient and wireless characteristics, it attracts many persons quickly. Especially, in contrast to conventional telephones, mobile system overcomes the defects of long-distance wire and set up of telephones system. However, one cellular phone do not have extensive function for other users.
Furthermore, the mountains, high buildings and other wave generators usually influence the communicative signal of cellular phones from the station.
US 5333177 provides a universal physical and electrical connection to a plurality of different kinds of hand-held cellular telephone units. A selectable adapter cable coding permits determination of the unique identity of the type of attached phone thereby allowing a data processing unit or the like to establish appropriate electrical interfacing support operations.
Unlike US 5333177 the present invention relates to a communication device controller which is capable of signal processing and their communication to various outlets including computers and telephones and is provided with signal receive/send unit as well as signal processing unit. The device of present invention also has power supply unit to provide it with stable power for locations where constant power supply is not easy to avail.
