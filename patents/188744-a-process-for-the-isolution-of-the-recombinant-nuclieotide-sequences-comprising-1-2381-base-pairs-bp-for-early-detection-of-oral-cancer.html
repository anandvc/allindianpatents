Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE ISOLUTION OF THE RECOMBINANT NUCLIEOTIDE SEQUENCES COMPRISING 1-2381 BASE PAIRS (BP) FOR EARLY DETECTION OF ORAL CANCER
Abstract Recombinant nucleotide sequences which comprise 1-2381 base pairs (bp) used for early detection of oral cancer. This is isolated from a recombinant gene cloned from oral cancer tissue DNA by subcloning and nucleotide sequencing followed by identification of the bp sequences in the nucleotide. The nucleotide containing the bp sequences along with a buffer system, a nucleotide mix and thermophilic polymerase enzyme is used as a kit for early detection of oral cancer.
