Title of Invention

DETERGENT COMPOSITION
Abstract WE CLAIM: 1) A detergent composition comprising. (i) from 3 to 60% by weight of at least one detergent active; and (ii) from 0.1 to 10% by weight of a polyethylene glycol of molecular weight in the range 400 to 5000.
