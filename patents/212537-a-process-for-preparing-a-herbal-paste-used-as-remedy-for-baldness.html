Title of Invention

"A PROCESS FOR PREPARING A HERBAL PASTE USED AS REMEDY FOR BALDNESS"
Abstract This application deals with a process for preparation of a herbal paste, used as a Remedy for baldness, The paste has the following special characteristics; Application of the paste on the hairless head of a person (bald) causes the re growth Of the hairs which starts in about an month . The paste has to be applied on the whole Head for about two hours daily. This paste is to be used for two months, only, I.e., a Complete course for a bald person, After two months, the growth of new hairs will Be a normal process and there is no need to use the paste any more. It has also been Noticed that there is no any bad cffect of the paste on human hairs or body as the Paste is pure herbal. It also checks the fall of hairs [all of hair as well as it makes The older hairs very shinning and strong.
Full Text An aqueous paste of the leaves of a plant was noticed to have the following special characteristics :
(a) Application of the paste on the hairless head of a person ( bald ) caused the
growth of hairs with in a month.
(b) After two hours of its application daily on the whole head , it was washed
with fresh water while taking bath.
(c) The growth of hairs was started after 15 days which reached the length of
2.0 cm. in two months.
(d) The effect of the paste on the residual older hairs was tremendous i.e., it
made the hairs silky, shining and strong. This indicated to check the fall of
hairs also.
(e) It had no bad effect on human hairs or body.
The leaves of Trichosanthes dioica plant (100 g) were washed with water. The leaves were made in the form of a paste in a mixi or grinder after addition of 25 ml water. The prepared green coloured paste could be used for two or three days . The half of the paste was applied on the head of a person who was bald for the last 15-20 years . The person was to wait for two hours , then to take a bath and wash his head properly with fresh water . After repeating this process ( daily one time ) for two months , a pronounced growth of hairs (about 2.0 cm. in length ) was noticed on his head . This experiment was tested on four bald persons and the results were cent percent successful.





I claim
A process for preparation of a herbal paste which can be used as a remedy to baldness comprising washing the leaves of Trichosanthes dioica plant ( 100 g) and then making its paste with 25 ml. water after grinding in a mixi, the green coloured paste so prepared can be used as a remedy to baldness.
In this invention, I claim a method for preparing a herbal paste and the procedure to apply the paste for removing baldness.
