Title of Invention

STAND-UP POUCH
Abstract A both side gusseted stand up flexible pouch comprising an operative front face portion of flexible laminate material, an operative rear face portion of flexible laminate material side sealed with said front face portion on side vertical edges characterized in that an operative top horizontal gusset is side sealed with the side horizontal edges of rear face portion and front face portion , and an operative bottom horizontal gusset with or without a flexible opening for product filling is provided, the edges of the said bottom horizontal gusset being sealed with the side horizontal edges of said front face portion and rear face portion of said pouch.

THE FOLLOWING SPECIFICATION PARTICULARLY DESCRIBES THE INVENTION AND THE MANNER IN WHICH IT IS TO BE PERFORMED:-

This invention relates to a stand-up pouch.
Normal stand-up pouches are able to be placed in a stand up position in only one side.
See for instance, US published patent applications 20040136620, 20040091183 and 20050069230 [no Indian equivalent] which disclose stand up pouches which can be made to stand up on one side only.
What is envisaged in accordance with this invention is a Both Side Stand-up Pouch with top and bottom gusset - with or without Flexible Filling Valve for product filing and sealing. These pouches can stand upright on both the sides.
According to this invention there is provided a both side gusseted stand up flexible pouch comprising an operative front face portion of flexible laminate material, an operative rear face portion of flexible laminate material side sealed with said front face portion on side vertical edges characterized in that an operative top horizontal gusset is side sealed with the side horizontal edges of rear face portion and front face portion, and an operative bottom horizontal gusset with or without flexible fill valve for product filing is provided , the edges of the said bottom horizontal gusset being sealed with the side horizontal edges of said front face portion and rear face portion of said pouch.
As envisaged in accordance with this invention the said pouch stands upright operatively vertically on both top and bottom sides.
2

In accordance with a preferred embodiment of the invention, the said pouch is made of flexible laminate rnaterial. The said flexible laminate material is made up of at least two substrates selected from a group of substrates consisting of Pet, Bopp, Poly, CPP, Al-foil, and paper.
The said flexible laminate material is made by the combination of two or more material together by adhesive bonding or extrusion lamination.
The pouch may be performed or made on a pouching machine or made on a form fill seal machine and with due rotation of 90 degree thereafter.
The pouch when filled up to/with brim give rise to unique brick shape.
Various modifications are envisaged without departing from the nature and scope of this invention. For instance, the width of said fill opening extends upto the width of said pouch or the said fill opening positioned on the top or bottom gusset has a variable width and height as per predetermined requirements
Again, the said filling valve is sealed and tucked inside the operative bottom gusset.

The Both side stand up pouch can be either pre-formed on a pouching machine or can be made on a form fill seal machine and due rotation of 90 degrees thereafter. This format of pouch will have two horizontal gussets instead of two vertical side gussets that is there in a conventional stand up pouch. The biggest advantage is that when filled upto the brim, it gives rise to a unique brick shape. It can have the following specifications,
1. 10-12 micron Polyester plain or chemically coated film 12- 3gsm adhesive/ 40- 200 micron natural PE.
2. 15 -25 micron BOPP 12- 3gsm adhesive/ 40- 200 micron natural PE.
3. 10-12 micron Polyester plain or chemically coated film/2-3 gsm adhesive/7-12 micron foil/2- 3gsm adhesive/ 40- 200 micron natural PE.
4. 10-12 micron Polyester plain or chemically coated film/2-3 gsm adhesive/22- 40 gsm paper/3 gsm adhesive/ 40- 200 micron natural PE.

The pouches when supplied as pre-formed will have a filling valve that can be, as big as the width of the pouch. After filling, this valve is sealed & tucked inside the bottom gusset. The width, height, gusset depth & the fill valve position & width can be tailor- made to suit the product/customer requirement
When supplied in roll form to be made on a FFS machine, the panel positioning is done in such manner that readability is achieved only after the pouch is formed & held/ viewed perpendicular to the direction of the forming.
