Title of Invention

DEVICE FOR RECEIVING AND HOLDING AN IDENTIFICATION PROVIDER, SUCH AS AN ELECTRONIC KEY, ESPECIALLY FOR AN IGNITION-STARTER SWITCH
Abstract The device has in its housing receptacle for insertion of an identification provider .A securing positioned within the interior of the receptacle engage in this connection a lateral ward of the identification provider. The opening of the receptacle is usually closed by a spring -loaded cover element .For realizing a functionally safe,cost-saving design,it is suggested to configure the cover elements as a pivotable fla=p which can be pivoted by the identification provider from the outer pivot position covering the opening of the receptacle.The flap has at its exposed side a projection provider .For the movement of the flap into the inner pivot position a recess is arranged adjacent to the receptacle .Upon insertioni and removal of the identification provider the porjection of the flap contacts the contour of the identification provider and temporarily pivots it farther back into an over-pivoted position.A profile control surface is transferred into an active locking position which delimits the space for the inner pivot position of the flap and thus secures the engagement of the projection of the flap in the ward of the identification provider.
