Title of Invention

A PDE IV INHIBITING PYRIDINE COMPOUND
Abstract The present invention concerns pyridine derivatives having formula(I), the pharmaceutically acceptable addition salts and the stereochemically isomeric from thereof wherein L is hydrogen; C1-6alkyl; C1-6 alkyl carbonyl; C1-6alkyloxycarbonyl; substituted C1-6alkyl; C3-6alkenyl; substituted C3-6alkenyl; piperidyl; C1-6alkylsulfonyl or arylsulfonyl;-A-B-is-CR4=CR5-or-CHR4-CHR5-; D is O or NR6 is a hydrogen or C1-4alkyl; R2 is hydrogen; halo; c1-6alkyl; trifluoromethyl; or NH-R10; R3 is hydrogen halo, hydroxyl, C1-6 alkyl or C1-6alkyl or R2 and R1, or R2 and R3 taken together may form a bivalent radical; Q is disubstituted pyridine, R7 and R8 each independently are hydrogen; C1-6alkyl; difluoromethyl; trifluoromethyl; C3-6cyclo alkyl; a saturated 5-, 6- or 7-membered heterocycle containing one or two heteroatoms selected from oxygen, sulfur or nitrogen; indanyl; 6,7-dihydro-5h-cyclopentapyridyl; bicycle[2.2.1]-2-heptenyl; bicycle C1-6alkylsulfonyl; arylsulfonyl; or substituted C1-10alkyl; having PDE IV and cytokine inhibiting activity. The invention also relates to process for preparing the compounds of formula (I), pharmaceutical compositions thereof and their use as a medicament.
