Title of Invention

A PRIMER FOR DETECTING MYCOBACTERIUM INTRACELLULARE
Abstract Disclosed are: an oligonucleotide which comprises a part or the entire of the nucleotide sequence depicted in SEQ ID NO:1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8 or a part or the entire of a complementary sequence thereof and which is capable of hybridizing with the nucleotide sequence for a Mycobacterium intracellulare (M. intracellulare) gene; a primer or probe for use in the detection of M. intracellulare, which comprises the oligonucleotide; and a method for detection of M. intracellulare by using the primer and/or the probe. The detection method enables to eliminate any false positive result in diagnosis and to detect or diagnose M. intracellulare with higher accuracy and preciseness in a specific manner compared to a conventional diagnostic method employing a cell culture assay or a PCR assay. The method also enables to quantify a microbial cell.
