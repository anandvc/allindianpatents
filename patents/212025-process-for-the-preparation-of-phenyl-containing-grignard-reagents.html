Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF PHENYL-CONTAINING GRIGNARD REAGENTS
Abstract 1. A process for the preparation of phenyl-containing Grignard reagents, the process comprising contacting magnesium metal with a phenylhalide, where the halide is chloride or bromide, in the presence of a co-solvent comprised of a mixture of dialkyl ether and toluene.
