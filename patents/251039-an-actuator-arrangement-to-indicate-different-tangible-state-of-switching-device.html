FIELD OF INVENTION
The present invention relates to an actuator arrangement denoting different tangible state of switching device comprising a handle for actuating the switching device. More particularly, the invention relates to an integral arrangement of components within the actuator (herein indicated as handle coupler unit) adapted to denote different tangible states -of switching device "ON", "OFF" and/or "TRIP" by means of sensing module means operatively connected with indicator means comprising one or more colors indications in consonance with physical position of the handles indicating each state. The handle coupler unit finds its application in circuit breakers, switch fuse disconnector and the like.
Background And Prior Art
Switching devices and their combinations with associated operative control, measuring, protection and regulating equipments are conventionally known as Switchgear. in particular , Switchgears usually make or break the current in one or more electric circuits by various methods including use of mechanical switching means which include separable contacts. Such mechanical switches can be operable by toggle of knob attached with these switching device or by an arrangement of mechanism and extension shaft wherein these are operated by rotary motion of the handle coupler unit.
Under these conditions, a rotable operating shaft with pin, and mechanism suitably adapted, actuates such switching devices, which is housed inside the enclosure commonly called as panel. It is important to note that keeping in view the safety requirements of such devices, it is extremely important

The devices used in practice till date are different, mostly electromechanical. Knobs with single colour glows denoting the "ON" state are already existing but the combination of emitting signal in all three state of switching device is unique as described under present invention.
In the present invention the indicating means is governed by the circuit sensing the power hence glowing accordingly. This is achieved by physically sensing the position and connecting the same to an electrical supply for glowing the light emitting electronic device having electrodes or terminals in three different states harmonizing the glow with the physical movement of the handle.
The present inventors have found that a handle coupler unit arrangement comprising a sensing module electronically configured and operatively connected with an indicating means denotes different tangible states of switching device giving accurate physical indication of pointer means.
OBJECTS OF INVENTION
Thus one of the objects of the present invention is to provide safe indication of the switching device.
Another object of the present invention is to provide variant glowing colour indication on the handle of the switching device indicating each state of the switching device with particular colour.
Yet another object of the current invention is to give clear indication of the state of switching mechanism particularly

"on" "off" and "trip" by directly and electrically sensing, transmitting and displaying the state of switching device.
The other object of the said invention, the handle serves the dual role of a actuator knob and an indicator thus saving on component and complex wiring arrangement, adding an elementary safety since an operator and machine interact through a single media i.e. handle.

DETAILE DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
The present invention is achieved by an integral arrangement of handle coupler unit, sensing module, indicator means comprising plurality of electrodes or terminals and auxiliary contact blocks of ' the switching device. The invent
5 10 NOV 2005

integrates the function of the three indication lamps into one set of indicator means having three electrodes or terminals, having multi-colour glow. The handle coupler unit comprising transparent handle adapted to serve dual role of an actuator knob and an indicator thereby saving on component and reducing complexity in wiring arrangement, adding an elementary safety since an operator and machine interact through a single media i.e. the handle. It is actuated while it denotes the state. The said handle is mounted outside the panel means of the switching device. The handle coupler unit and the switching device are operatively interconnected by extension mechanism provided in predetermined cavities of the switching device such that to take feed back of the state of the breaker by directly sensing its contact position. The said extension mechanism may also be fitted externally to the switching device.
The contact sensing module comprises plurality of switching means having normally open (NO) and normally close (NC) contacts. The switches are micro-switches operatively housed in housing referred as contact sensing module. One of the micro-switches within the contact sensing module being provided in a manner that senses the drive shaft position of switching device differentiating between "ON" and "OFF" position, while in "TRIP' position, the drive shaft position remains the same as "OFF" state. The other micro-switch senses the position of switch operating mechanism and latch plate, which gets detached while in "TRIP" state which invariably leads the lever of the switch to sense positional imbalance. The combination of the two switches provides the sensing of the imbalance positional signals.
10 NOV 2005

Thus contact sensing module of the invention provides two independent signals of ON/OFF and/or TRIP.
The said contact sending module is operatively connected with circuit means comprising of printed circuit board having diode means and current regulator means. The said diode means comprises of light emitting diodes (LEDs) adapted to indicate each state of the device. The current regulator means comprises plurality of resistors having predetermined ohmic values adapted to regulate the amount of current fed in the diode terminals and also the colour of glow of LEDs.
In an embodiment of the invention, the transparent handle is provided such that the light emitting diodes are encapsulated in a manner that glowing of diode at a time will generate corresponding light, which is thus visible from outside the handle. The plurality of light emitting diodes comprises plurality of terminals such that varying current flow in the terminals generate predetermined red, green and amber colour diode glowing for ON, OFF and TRIP respectively.
In one embodiment of the invention, the handle coupler unit is provided with a cavity ergonomically designed to accommodate printed circuit board. The handle is screw fitted on the base of the handle coupler unit. The said base is provided with markings corresponding to ON/OFF/TRIP. The printed circuit board unit is sandwiched between the handle and the base and the said handle is rotatable against the base.

Further referring to the figure 3, TRIP sensing module (4) comprises terminals (Bl, B2 and B3). Upon actuation terminals (Bl and B3) gets disconnected and contact is formed with terminals (Bl and B2).
So, this micro switch gets actuated in TRIP condition and here terminals (Bl and B2) get connected while otherwise terminals (Bl and B3) remain connected.
