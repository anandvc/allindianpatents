The system according to the invention allows the implementation of the flexible prodiiclion of a plurality of types of moulded products which differ from each other,
For this purpose, the initial input station SI transfers or writes to the memory devices 4 of each mouid M operating information which comprehensively identifies the specific products to be formed in the mould and the corresponding forming operations. On the biisia of this operating information, the subsequent stations are or are not activated, and carry out operations appropriate to the types of product which are to be formed in each mould.
Production can therefore be adapted in real time, in a highly flexible way, to the requirements of production and of the producing company, thus overcoming the rigidity and inefRciency of conventional systems of production in successive uniform batches.
In the flexible production system described up to this point, each mould can be used to form a specific product, in the sense that the same product is formed on each occasion in all the housings or cells of a mould.
In a variant embodiment, at least some of the moulds M can allow the forming of different products, in other words products diversified within a single mould, in predetermined iqsecific areas of the mould. In this case, the input station SI is designed to transcribe into the memoty devices 4 associated with these moulds the infomiation indicating the specific products to be formed in each of the said areas, and the corresponding forming operations.
In this way, the flexibility of production can be increased further.
