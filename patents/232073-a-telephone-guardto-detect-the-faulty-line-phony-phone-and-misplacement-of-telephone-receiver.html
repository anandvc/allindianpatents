Title of Invention

"A TELEPHONE GUARDTO DETECT THE FAULTY LINE, PHONY PHONE AND MISPLACEMENT OF TELEPHONE RECEIVER"
Abstract This invention relates to a telephone guard to detect the faulty line, phony phone and misplacement of telephone receiver characterized in that means to detect the faulty line (1) being connected to the own phone detection means (2), the output of own phone detection means being connected to phony phone detection means (3) the output of which being connected to a signal analyser circuit (8), means (4) to detect the speech signal being connected to said signal analyser circuit through a timer circuit (5), a signal killer circuit (7) being connected to said speech signal means (4) the outputs of the faulty line means and phony phone means being connected to said signal analyser circuit, a square wave generator (6) connected to the outputs of phony phone and timer circuit being connected to a pulse modulator (10) and also to an AND gates (9) connected to the outputs of said signal analyser circuit, the inputs of said faulty line means, own phone means, own phone means, speech signal means and signal killer circuit being connected to the main phone line through a resistance (15).
