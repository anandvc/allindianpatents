Title of Invention

BROADBAND SINGLE VERTICAL POLARIZED BASE STATION ANTENNA
Abstract Abstract BROADBAND SINGLE VERTICAL POLARIZED BASE STATION ANTENNA An antenna (10) for receiving and/or transmitting electromagnetic signals is disclosed. The antenna (10) includes a ground plane (28) with a length and having a vertical axis along the length, and a dipole radiating element (18. 20) projects outwardly from a surface of the ground plane (28). The radiating element includes a feed section (20), and a ground section (18).
