Title of Invention

A PROCESS OF PRODUCING O-XYLENE-AIR MIXTURES FOR THE PRODUCTION OF PHTHALIC ANHYDRIDE
Abstract A process of producing an o-xylene-air mixture for the production of phthalic anhydride, characterized in that o-xylene is completely evaporated at a temperature of atleast 175degreeC in the absence of oxygen, is superheated thereafter and then mixed with the process air, and this mixture is supplied to the PA reactor.
