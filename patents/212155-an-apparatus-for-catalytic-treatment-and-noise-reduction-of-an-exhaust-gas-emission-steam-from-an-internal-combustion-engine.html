Title of Invention

AN APPARATUS FOR CATALYTIC TREATMENT AND NOISE REDUCTION OF AN EXHAUST GAS EMISSION STEAM FROM AN INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE.
Abstract AN APPARATUS FOR CATALYTIC TREATMENT AND NOISE REDUCTION OF AN EXHAUST GAS EMISSION STREAM FROM AN INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE. AN APPARATUS FOR CATALYTIC TREATMENT AND NOISE REDUCTION OF AN EXHAUST GAS EMISSION STREAM FROM AN INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE, COMPRISING A CANISTER HAVING AN INLET AND AN OUTLET AND A PLURALITY OF PERFORATED TUBES HAVING AN EMISSIONS CATALYST COATED THEREON DISPOSED INSIDE SAID CANISTER WHEREIN, SAID PERORATED TUBES ARE ARRNAGED IN AN ALIGNED RELATION IN A STAGGERED RELATION IN AN ANGLED RELATION, OR IN ANY COMBINATION THEREOF.
