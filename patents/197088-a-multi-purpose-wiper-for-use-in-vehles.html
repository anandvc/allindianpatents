Title of Invention

"A MULTI PURPOSE WIPER FOR USE IN VEHLES"
Abstract This invention relates to a multi purpose wipers. The wiper has a compressor adapted to be secured at the bottom surface of the chasses of the vehicle to provide the compressed air. An air conveying pipe is secured to the outlet of the compressor so as to convey the compressed air to a hot chamber provided around the silencer of the vehicle. Hot air conveying pipe is secured to the outlet of the hot chamber for conveying the hot compressed air to the nozzles connected to the other end of the hot air conveying pipe such that to spread hot compressed air onto the wind shield glasses.(Fig.1)
Full Text This invention relates to a multipurpose wiper for use in vehicles.
The use of wipers is as such known in the art for the removal of the water or dust from the front wind shield glass of the motor vehicle. The conventional wipers comprises mechanical wipers. There are several disadvantages associated with conventional mechanical wipers.
One of the main disadvantage is that the conventional wipers cause hindrance in the vision during its operation.
Another disadvantage is that substantial amount of condensed moisture sticks on the front wind shield resulting into poor visibility.
Yet another disadvantage is that the conventional wipers create scratches on the front wind shield glass which cause blurring in the night vision.
Yet another disadvantage of the conventional mechanical wipers is that after sometime the noise is produced by the wiper motor which causes irritation during driving.

Yet another disadvantage of the conventional wipers is that these wipers are useless during the winter season for defogging purposes.
Therefore the main object of the present invention is to propose a multi purpose wiper in order to obviate the above mentioned disadvantages.
Another object of the present invention is to propose a wiper which does not cause hindrance in the vision.
Yet another object of the present invention is to porpose a multi purpose wipers, wherein no condensation of moisture takes place.
Still another object of the present invention is to propose a wiper which is useful effectively during the winter season for defogging purposes.
Another object of the present inventon is to propose a noiseless wiper.
According to this invention there is provided a multipurpose wiper for use in vehicles comprising :-
(a) a compressor adapted to be secured to the chassis of the vehicle;

(b) an air conveying pipe secured to the outlet of said compressor conveying ambient air through an inlet to the hot chamber provided around the silencer/exhaust of the vehicle;
(c) a hot air conveying pipe secured to the outlet of the hot chamber surrounding the said silencer/exhaust wherein the said hot air conveying pipe is adapted to convey the hot air to the wind shield glasses of vehicle through nozzle being connected to the other end of said hot air conveying pipe, wherein said nozzle comprises a hollow body having an inlet secured therewith and plurality of holes/continuous slit provided at the bottom surface of the said hollow body which are adapted to direct the hot air over the said wind shield glasses to remove water/dust/fog therefrom.
