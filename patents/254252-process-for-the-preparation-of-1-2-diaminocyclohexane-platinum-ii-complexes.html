Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF 1,2-DIAMINOCYCLOHEXANE PLATINUM(II) COMPLEXES
Abstract A process for the preparation of diaminocyclohexane-platinum(II)-dicarboxylates has the follow- ing steps: B Conversion of K2PtX2 with 1 ,2-diaminocyclohexane (DACH) to Conversion of (2) with too little quantity of silver salt AgnA to and removal of the resulting AgX precipitate. Conversion of (3) with a dicarboxylate to Isolation of the product (1), whereby R1 and R2 together form a dicarboxylato group, X stands for CI or I, A for a 1-2-valent anion of a mineral acid and n stands for 1 or 2.
