Title of Invention

PH-SENSITIVE POLYMER
Abstract The present invention relates to a conjugate or complex of an pH-sensitive polymer with a pharmaceutically effective natural or synthetic biomolecule or an active pharmaceutical ingredient pH-sensitive polymer, characterized in that the pH- sensitive polymer is a (meth)acrylate copolymer composed of 20 to 65% by weight methacrylic acid units and 80 to 35% by weight units of C<SUB>1</SUB>- to C<SUB>18</SUB>-alkyl esters of (meth)acrylic acid, which has a molecular weight in the range from 1 000 to 50000 g/mol, and brings about at least 60% haemolysis at pH 5.5, and less than 5% haemolysis at pH 7.4, in a concentration of 150 µg/ml in a cytotoxicity test with human red blood cells. This invention also relates to a pH-sensitive polymer and a process to prepare the pH-sensitive polymer.
