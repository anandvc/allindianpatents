Special type of rollers. Such projections and Depressions will be formed in an alternative manner in each Slot and also in an alternative manner with respect to each Slots throughout its length so that the Cut Section of the Rod at any point will have 4 Webs, 4 Slots out of which the 2 Opposite Slots will have the Diamond shaped Projections with Square shaped depressions and the other 2 Slots will have the Diamond Shaped Depressions with Square shaped Projections throughout its entire length and for this Steel Rod the surface area of contact with the Cement Concrete is increased by 25%, the strength and bending Moment is increased by 25% and the Grip between the Steel Rod and Cement concrete is increased.
