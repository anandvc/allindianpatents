Title of Invention

REAR HUB ASSEMBLY FOR BICYCLE
Abstract The present invention provides a rear hub assembly for bicycle, comprises an axle (1), a hub shell (12), a shaft cup (5) and a shaft stop (3), wherein, at one side of the hub shell (12), a slidable ball stop (11) is installed between the shaft cup and a ball cup, and the flywheel mount (6) is mounted on the axle(1) by the slidable ball stop(11) and the ball cup located at its two sides. With this rear hub assembly, the axial clearance of the rear hub may be adjusted from either side of the rear hub conveniently, so as to be easy for assembling and repairing the bicycle, to significantly reduce the diameter and the teeth of the flywheel, and to improve the transmission ratio of the bicycle.
Full Text Technical Field
The present invention relates to a device for adjusting and locking the axial clearance in
the rear hub assembly of a bicycle, and to a device for connecting the flywheel with the hub
assembly.
Background of the Invention
In the prior art, adjusting and locking the axial clearance in the rear hub assembly of a
bicycle is by means of adjusting the gap between the two shaft stops each located at one of the
two ends of the hub shell, where it is necessary to respectively adjust both of the left side and
right side shaft stops and then lock each of the shaft stops by means of a locknut. Therefore, it is
not convenient for operation. Moreover, as the flywheel plane axially overlaps with the
revolving plane of the steel balls, the flywheel of the conventional bicycle has bigger diameter,
more teeth and smaller transmission ratio.
Summary of the Invention
The present invention is aimed to provide a rear hub assembly for bicycle, which enables to
adjust the axial clearance conveniently from either side of the rear hub, so as to be easy for
assembling and repairing the bicycle, and to significantly reduce the diameter and the teeth of
the flywheel.
The rear hub assembly for bicycle according to this invention comprises an axle, a hub
shell sleeved on said axle and a flywheel mount located at one end of said hub shell, wherein
said hub shell has a locknut, an axle cup and steel balls at both of its two ends, and at the side
where said flywheel mount is located, a slidable ball stop and a ball cup mounted on said axle
are further provided, wherein said slidable ball stop is pressed against multiple steel balls in
said axle cup, said flywheel mount is mounted on said axle between said slidable ball stop and
said ball cup via steel balls at both of the two sides of said flywheel mount, and said ball stop is
fixed by said locknut.
According to the rear hub assembly for bicycle of this invention, since a slidable ball stop
is installed between an axle cup and a ball stop at one said of the hub shell, when the gap at any
end of the axles gets larger, the hub shell will move along the axle, i.e., the slidable ball stop will
combine the gaps at the two ends of the axle, and the gap at one end will be transferred into the
gap at the other end, therefore, the gap at both ends of the axle may be adjusted from either side
of the hub shell. When adjusting the shaft stop at either end of the axle with the locknut at the
other end fixed, the shaft cup, flywheel mount and the slidable ball stop are pressed in turn, so
as to adjust the total axial clearance. At the same time, the flywheel mount is clearance fit with
the axle, both ends of the flywheel are provided with a slidable or rolling bearing, the flywheel
and the revolving plane of the steel balls are axially separated, thereby, the diameter and the
teeth of the flywheel are reduced, and the transmission ratio of the bicycle is improved.

Detailed description of the Preferred Embodiment
Embodiment 1:
As shown in Fig. 1, a hub shell 12 is sleeved on the axle 1, the hub shell 12 has a shaft cup
5 at both ends of the hub shell, a shaft stop 3 connected with the axle 1 via screw is pressed
against multiple steel balls 4 in the shaft cup 5, a spacer 2 is mounted at the outer side of the
shaft stop 3, and at the side of the hub shell where a flywheel is installed, a slidable ball stop 11
is installed between the shaft cup 5 and the ball cup 8. The flywheel mount 6 is mounted on the
axle 1 between a slidable ball stop 11 and a ball cup 8 via steel balls 10,7 at both of its two sides.
In order to improve the bearing capacity of the steel balls 10 at one side of the flywheel mount 6,
a jack 9 is sleeved outside the flywheel mount 6, so as to enlarge the diameter and improve the
bearing capacity of the steel balls 10.

stop(11) and the ball cup located at its two sides. With this rear hub assembly, the
axial clearance of the rear hub may be adjusted from either side of the rear hub
conveniently, so as to be easy for assembling and repairing the bicycle, to
