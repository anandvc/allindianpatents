Title of Invention

INFORMATION STORAGE MEDIUM ,METHOD AND APPARATUS OF RECORDING AND/OR REPRODUCING DATA.
Abstract An information storage medium and a method of recording data on hand/or reproducing data form the information storage medium, includes an information storage medium having at least one information storage layer. Data is recorded in the form of protruding or indented pits in the entire or partial area of the information storage medium, and information regarding the protruding or indented pits is recorded. Smooth tracking can be achieved using the information regarding the protruding or indented pits, that is, oush-pull polarity information.
