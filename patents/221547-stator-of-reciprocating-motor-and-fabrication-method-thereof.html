STATOR OF RECIPROCATING MOTOR AND FABRICATION METHOD THEREOF
Abstract Disclosed is a stator of reciprocating motor comprising a stator including two lamination assemblies formed ac- cordingly as a plurality of lamination sheets are stacked as a cylindrical shape or a circular are shape and a mover inserted into an air gap between said two lamination assemblies and linearly reciprocating. In the stator, the lamination sheets constituting the lam- ination assembly are thicker at outer circumference side of the lamination assembly than at an inner circumference said thereof. According to this, a path of a flex applied to the lamination assembly is maximized a resistance of the flux.
