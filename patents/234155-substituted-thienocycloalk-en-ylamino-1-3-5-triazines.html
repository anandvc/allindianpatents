Title of Invention

SUBSTITUTED THIENOCYCLOALK(EN) YLAMINO-1,3,5-TRIAZINES
Abstract The invention relates to substituted thienocycloai(en)ylamino-1,3,5-triazines of the gemeral formula(1) in which Z,R1,R2 and R3 are as defined in the descrimption,and to processes for their preparation, to their us as herbicides and to the intermediates required for their preparation, including their preparation processes in the case of the preparred compounds.
