Furthermore, it is difficult to purify by distillation because a tetrahydropyranyl group, as a protective group, is easily eliminated by heat.
Pharmaceutical products are obligated to be produced under severe quality control in accordance with Good Manufacturing Practice (hereinafter abbreviated to GMP) so as to secure safety.
