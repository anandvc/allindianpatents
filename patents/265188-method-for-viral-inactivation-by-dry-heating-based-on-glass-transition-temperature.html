Title of Invention

“METHOD FOR VIRAL INACTIVATION BY DRY HEATING BASED ON GLASS TRANSITION TEMPERATURE”
Abstract The invention concerns a method of viral inactivation by dry heating of a virus present or potentially present in a biological product that has been dried according to the glass transition temperature. In a first step, the glass transition temperature of the dried product is determined. In a second step, the product is heated to a temperature not less than the determined glass transition temperature.
