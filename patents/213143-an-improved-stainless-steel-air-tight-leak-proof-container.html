Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED STAINLESS STEEL AIR TIGHT & LEAK PROOF CONTAINER
Abstract An improved stainless steel air tight & leak proof container comprises a conical base (1) & upper lid (2) closet by multiple locking means (3); the said locking means comprises a bow springs (4) which inits open position will be removed from the notch of the lid which is generally a curved flange position (5) which is position over the outwardly flunge circumference of the upper edge of the conical container; the said bow spring is held at the curve position of the lid & urge against the bottom spring portion which is secured attached in the flunge portion of the bottom conical base (6) thereby fastening the lid against the container top in air tight & leak proof manner. By means of flunge rubber gasket lined inside the lid so as to cover the rim of the stainless steel air tight & leak proof container; their being provided with spring loaded locking means secured attached to the rim of the container for locking the lid with the container for locking the lid with the container alongwith lever with handle fitted on the container to lift the lid by pushing by the lever.
