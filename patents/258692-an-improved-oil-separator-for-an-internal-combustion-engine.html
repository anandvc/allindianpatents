Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED OIL SEPARATOR FOR AN INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINE
Abstract This invention relates to an oil separator for an internal combustion engine. Oil separator which conventionally uses impact technology for the separation of oil particles from the blow by gases of the internal combustion engine. Due to the impact of high velocity blow by gases with an impact plate through small holes, kinetic energy of the blow by gases is reduced which separates oil from the blow by gases. In the present invention, three offset plates have been used as baffles. The two of them act as impact plates to fully utilize the space available. All three in total act as baffles to divert the fluid flow directions. In place of the constant diameter housing of the oil separator, a divergent type housing is used which helps to further reduce the velocity of the gases which facilitates oil separation. This in total improves the oil separation efficiency. Blow by gases are drawn from cylinder head cover into the inlet side of oil separator. The separated oil drains back into the oil sump below oil level. Substantially oil free gas is then introduced to the air induction system of the engine.

FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention relates to an internal combustion engine and more particularly it relates to a novel oil separator for Internal Combustion Engine which separates entrained lubricating oil from the blow by gases.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
An oil separator for the blow by gases is provided in an internal combustion engine for the purpose of separating entrained lubricating oil from blow by gases and thus significantly reducing the amount of oil going to the air intake system of the internal combustion engine. If oil is not effectively separated the performance of turbocharger & intercooler gets adversely affected furthermore the un-separated oil burns in the combustion chamber hampering engine performance and emission. Conventionally oil separation for the blow by gases is achieved using cyclonic separation principle, impact separation principle, expansion principle or multiple baffle plates to separate the entrained lubrication oil from blow by gases . The subject invention uses a novel combination of impact and expansion principle in conjunction with multiple baffle plates.
OBJECTS OF INVENTION
The main object of the invention is to provide a novel oil separator for internal combustion Engine,
Another object of the present invention is to provide a novel oil separator for internal combustion engine which separate the entrained lubricating oil from blow by gases.


Yet another object of the present invention is to provide a novel oil separator for an internal combustion engine with simple construction, compact size and light weight intended for ease of manufacturing.
STATEMENT OF INVENTION
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine comprises; a divergent type housing mounted on bracket fitted on cylinder head for separating entrained lubricating oil from the blow by gases in an internal combustion engine, strainer plate mounted on the said divergent housing for increasing the velocity of blow by gases, at least two impact plates to reduce the velocity of blow by gases, baffle plate to change the flow direction of blow by gases, said impact plates and baffle plate are mounted on the said divergent housing, rear cover having holes for providing atmospheric pressure on one side of diaphragm which is provided between said rear cover and said housing , wherein said divergent type housing comprises; inlet of oil separator for blow by gas , outlet for oil separator for oil free blow by gases, oil outlet for separated lubricating oil, resilient means provided between said diaphragm and said outlet for oil separator for oil free blow by gases.
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said strainer plate comprises orifices through which high velocity blow by gases are allowed to hit on impact plates.
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said outlet for oil separator for oil free blow by gases comprises holes.


A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said holes are inclined to change the suction direction away from the centre of oil outlet for separated lubricating oil.
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said divergent housing comprises sealing means to prevent the leakage of blow by gases to the atmosphere.
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said sealing means is a ring.
A novel oil separator for Internal Combustion engine, in accordance with this invention, wherein said impact plate comprises drain hole to drain the separated oil.
BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF INVENTION
In accordance with the present invention, A novel oil separator for internal combustion engine comprises; divergent housing (06), inlet (01) of oil separator for blow by gas, outlet (09) for oil separator for oil free blow by gases, oil outlet (10) for separated oil. According to present invention, blow by gas is directed through said inlet (01) and impacts on strainer plate (02). The blow by gas then flows through small orifices of said plate (02) with high velocity and hits the impact plates (03, 04). The separated oil drains through holes (13) provided on said plate (02) and impact plate (04) to said oil outlet (10). The said impact plates (03,04) and baffle plate (05) also reduce the velocity of blow by gas due to change in flow direction of the same. The said impact plates (03, 04) also act as baffle plates. This helps further to separate entrained oil from the blow by gas.


Finally the said divergent type housing (06) also helps to reduce velocity head of blow by gases along the flow direction thereby further separating oil from blow by gases. The separated oil drains to said oil outlet (10) through said oil holes (13) on said strainer plate (02) and impact plate (04). The divergent type housing (06) also helps to drain oil towards oil outlet (10). Thus oil free blow by gases pass through (09) towards air induction system.

The general arrangement of the oil separator in internal combustion engine includes cylinder head cover, oil separator, and oil sump and air intake system. To remove the entrained lubricating oil from blow by gases, oil separator is mounted as close to cylinder head cover as possible. Blow by gases coming from the cylinder head are made to pass through the oil separator which separates oil from them and oil free blow by are again supplied to the air induction system of the engine. Oil which has been taken out from the blow by gases is again send back to the oil sump.
