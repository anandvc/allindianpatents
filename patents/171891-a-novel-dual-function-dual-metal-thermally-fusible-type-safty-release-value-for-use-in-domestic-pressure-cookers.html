Abstract A novel, dual functioning, dual metal, Safety Release Valve which comprises, a fusible metal alloy material held within the bore of a •s^eetrd metallic retainer member, the stem of the said metallic retainer being adapted to be loosely and angularly held at an angle of 10° to 25° to the vertical axis of the cooker by a holding member to an opening in the lid of the pressure cooker, the ratio of the diameter of the hole to the diameter or hole of the stem being 1.05 to 1.20the said stem also having a thin washer made of non-metallic resilient material and having a lift distance of 2 to 5 m.m adapted to seal the opening in the lid of the pressure cooker automatically after substantially all the air is vented and due to the generation of steam.
