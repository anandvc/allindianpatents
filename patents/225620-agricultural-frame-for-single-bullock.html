Title of Invention

AGRICULTURAL FRAME FOR SINGLE BULLOCK
Abstract The agricultural frame for single bullock is capable of being used with a single bullock. More over, this device can be used for cultivation in between rows of big trees, where two bullocks can not enter. The device is used alike as a traditional frame with a pair of bullocks in agricultural fields. Gorru, plough, putler, buck scraper, blade harrow, danti etc are the general cultivation devices usable with this multi-utility frame. The length of the frame can be adjusted using adjustments of the device to set the device for different bullocks and environments (natures of agricultural fields).

'COMPLETE SPECIFICATION'
AGRICULTURAL MULTI-INSTRUMENTAL FRAME FOR SINGLE BULLOCK
FIELD OF INVENTION: This invention is a multipurpose agricultural instrument, usable with a single bullock.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION : This instrument is developed basically with a plough attached to the frame shown in figure, for the purpose of ploughing in between rows of big trees. Ploughing between rows of trees becomes difficult with the tradition of using a pair of bullocks for field applications, as two bullocks can not pass through rows of trees in fields. Though machines or direct man power can be employed in such places, such things are not preferred by formers due to high cost of those methods. This new design (invention) of plough enables ploughing in between rows of trees with a less cost of one bullock employment.
OBJECTIVES: • Enabling ploughing and other such agricultural activities easy between rows of trees at a less cost of single bullock over a pair of bullocks.
• Providing the frame with a trally (designed especially for use with it) for taking small weights around some 1 OOkgs into agricultural fields with the help of bullocks, instead of using big carts or manpower to do such medium transportation; which seems to be a best method of agricultural transportation into fields.
• Providing all agricultural instruments used traditionally with a pair of bullocks, with a corresponding changes and developments to set with the new device for ordinary field applications, where ever a single bullock can be used, which reduces cost of cultivation.
STATEMENT OF THE INVENTION:
This invention is an agricultural multi-instrumental frame usable with a single bullock. The instruments are trally, plough, gorru, putler, blade harrow, buck scraper, danti, etc..

SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION:
This Agricultural multi-instrumental frame is usable with a single bullock for ordinary farm field applications as with a pair of bullocks or buffellows. The advantage is using a single cattle instead of two in fields, where ever a single bullock(cattle) can do well and easily. We can attach any of the instruments used with traditional "pair of bullocks" method, at the axial rod end of frame with few changes provided in them to set with a single bullock environment; along with a trally.
