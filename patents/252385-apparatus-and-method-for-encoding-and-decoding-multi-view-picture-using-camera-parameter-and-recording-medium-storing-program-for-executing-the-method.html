Title of Invention

APPARATUS AND METHOD FOR ENCODING AND DECODING MULTI-VIEW PICTURE USING CAMERA PARAMETER AND RECORDING MEDIUM STORING PROGRAM FOR EXECUTING THE METHOD
Abstract An apparatus and method for encoding and decoding a multi-view picture using a camera parameter are provided. The multi-view moving picture encoding apparatus includes: a multi-view picture receiver which receives the multi-view picture and a plurality of camera parameters; a camera parameter information setting unit which selects a camera parameter to be used for encoding the multi-view picture from among the plurality of the received camera parameters, and sets information which indicates the selected camera parameter; and an encoder which encodes the multi-view picture on the basis of the set information. Therefore, it is possible to enhance multi-view picture encoding efficiency.
