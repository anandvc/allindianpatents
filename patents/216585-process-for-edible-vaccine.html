PROCESS FOR EDIBLE VACCINE
Abstract "FACING THE DISEASES- THE OTHER WAY-PHYTOIMMUNOPHARMACOKINETICS." In study of diseases one observes that few unfortunate ones succumb to disease, some develop sub-clinical infection, some develop & overcome the disease with or without medicaments, but fortunate ones do not show any sign of exposure. It is said that this is because of different immunological status. The persons who develop disease are having lowered immunity and others have good immunity. If one identifies the dietary factors that imparts immunity and give these factors to diseased persons and if their ailment process is relieved then one has to investigate. Antigen like material will give active immunity and antibody like material give passive immunity. (Dr. MADHAV PANDHARINATH KAMATKAR) The aim of this invention is to to identify the process for differentiating dietary articles having those properties from the remaining dietary articles those do not posses these properties.

In study of diseases one observes that few unfortunate ones succumb to disease, some develop sub-clinical infection, some develop & overcome the disease with or without medicaments, but fortunate ones do not show any sign of exposure. It is said that this is because of different immunological status. The persons who develop disease are having lowered immunity. The persons who do not develop disease are having good immunity. After invasion of America, Australia & other countries the natives disappeared from this world to much extent. Indians at that time & today also are highest in population.The then Government & medical society gave stress for new inventions like new microorganisms, new diseases, new therapy & new vaccines. Enough attention was not given to why Indians do not suffer from disease in spite of exposure to or harboring dreadful virulent organisms.
Being most populous country with poorest sanitation facilities there is maximum number of infective diseased persons in India. It is said that Indians live amidst Bay of organisms. It is no wonder few unfortunate ones develop disease. It is more wonder that, many do not suffer from disease. One has to remember that there are no wonders in Science & one has to understand rationale behind them. In modern times Indian diseases are thoroughly investigated & presented. However, Indian immunity is not investigated properly. In spite of presence or exposure to the dreadful organisms if Indians are not developing disease it should be investigated. Before advent of modern anti Koch's treatment manf patients were dying miserably. But at that time also occasional patient was fully recovering from the disease. It is already known that diet gives immunity. The Indian diet mainly composed of cereals, pulses, vegatables & fruits. All of thsese come from plant kingdom. In diet also fresh fruits are advised to boost immunity while studing tuberculosis & other diseases. So in the present scenario persons who are dying miserably are eating fresh fruits & persons who are not developing disease are also eating fresh fruits. Further persons who have developed the disease & are recovering from disease are also eating fresh fruits. So at present no method is available to say positively that this fruit gives immunity. All are said to be nonspecific ones. Attempts are not made to identify the process to differentiate the fruits those are having immuno-therapeutic properties & others not having these properties.
Most of the human pathogenic microorganisms usually do not cause diseases in plants. Plants are unsuitable hosts for these pathogenic organisms. To decrease the virulence of pathogenic microorganism, it has to be passed through unsuitable host. Even though the organism is present in vascular system and in other parts of plants the plants are having immune system working against these organisms. Antibody like material of plant origin should be used for passive immunity. Less virulent or avirulent pathogenic microorganism should be identified, isolated and cultivated for active immunity.
