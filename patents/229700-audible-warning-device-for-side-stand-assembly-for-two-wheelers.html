AUDIBLE WARNING DEVICE FOR SIDE STAND ASSEMBLY FOR TWO WHEELERS

FIELD OF INVENTION
The present invention relates to an "Audible warning device for side stand assembly of two wheelers".
PRIOR ART DISCLOSURE
Following description explain various technologies known in the art, the problem associated with prior art and the solution offered by the invention to circumvent the problem associated with it.
In any two-wheeler, normally side stand is provided for parking the vehicle. In the normal circumstances the rider releases the side stand using his feet and start the vehicle for moving forward. If side stand is not released, there is every possibility of the vehicle meeting with an accident during the forward motion of the vehicle. This is overcome by providing a switch warning system. The device consists of a plunger type switch operated by the side stand being connected to the ignition switch and a bulb on the speedometer panel by electrical means. When the ignition switch is "ON" and if the side stand is not released the circuit is closed and current flows to the bulb making it glow cautioning the rider. Most of the time this caution is unnoticed by the rider especially during the daytime making it less safe. This is overcome by another known art in which, an audible piezo ceramic beeper is used to caution the rider. When the ignition switch is ON and the side stand is in the ON position, current is drawn by the piezo electric beeper and makes an audible sound, which can be heard by the rider. When the side stand is in the OFF position or released position no current is drawn by the beeper and no sound will be heard.
However in the above mentioned system with beeper, there is a draw back. The side stand and the switch operated by the stand are fixed on the lower most part of the vehicle. These assemblies are prone to water entry during rainy season while riding on potholes and during water wash at the time of servicing. Due to this the switch can malfunction since water may conduct electricity. So even when the side stand is in the OFF position, due to the electric conduction of water a very low current is sufficient to activate the beeper and will give false alarm to rider while riding. Though it is possible to provide a leak proof joint and enclosure to

avoid water entry, these solutions are not cost effective and not fool proof. It is therefore an object of this invention to provide a foolproof system, which is capable of solving this problem.
SUMMARY OF INVENTION
For solving the aforementioned problem, according to the present invention, there is provided a reed relay, which is electrically connected to the 12V supply and'the beeper through the side stand switch. The reed relay will close the circuit only when the required voltage is supplied to the system causing the beeper to activate and makes an audible sound. Any leakage caused by the water entry will not operate the reed relay and therefore the beeper will not give any sound.

Detailed description of preferred embodiment
Figure 1 - Electrical circuit diagram of the proposed invention
The Figure 1 consists of a side stand switch SW2, encapsulated housing H which houses a beeper B and reed relay R connected through a reed switch RE. The 12V supply is fed to the reed relay R through Ignition switch SW1 and the side stand switch SW2.
When the Ignition switch SW1 is ON, and when the stand is in the ON position, the stand switch SW2 is closed and current flows to the reed relay R and the reed switch RE will close.
Through the reed switch RE, the required 12V is supplied to the beeper B that in turn will give audible sound. Any amount of leakage through the side stand switch SW2 in case of water entry, will not operate the reed relay R since the relay requires more current than the leakage current. Thus false alarm of beeper is eliminated.
