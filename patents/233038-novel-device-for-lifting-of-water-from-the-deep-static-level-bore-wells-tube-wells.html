Title of Invention

NOVEL DEVICE FOR LIFTING OF WATER FROM THE DEEP STATIC LEVEL BORE WELLS /TUBE WELLS
Abstract A novel pump for lifting of water from the deep water level bore wells/Tube wells. Comprising, a cylindrical bucket Fig. (1,2) arranged vertically in a bore well fig.(1) (2)characterized in that a flap type valve (3) is provied at the end of the circular or cylindrical bucket, with an opening of 80 mm dia at the bottom of the bucket which allows water to enter into the bucket when bucket reaches at depth, further from its outer peripheral of the valve at centre an extended edge projecting out side to some length, which helps in discharing water when rests on movable sliding plate (9) water poured into a water chamber (12), A wire rope(4) having been tied at the top of the bucket, and its another end be tide up with a rotatably fitted drum (5), to wind the wire mounted on a shaft (6) housed with two bearings (8) are mounted in the rigid plates, welded to the m.s pipe(10) further having a square m.s base plate is welded to seal this casing from upper side through which a wire rope inserted to fix with, a small pulley (11) another end tied to on the top of the bucket, a drum having been attached with wire is fixed with help of a clamp, to wind up the wire rope, a handle (7) is mounted to the shaft (6) for applying effort, an effort wheel (7a) is provided to reduce efforts, a fabricate water tank (12) in square an arrangement is made to a movably fitted steel plate (9), a sliding plate piece is provided to cover bore well, and a log handle is provided to the slide plate to rotate, a speed control device (13) with a free wheel mounted on the drum shaft and a sprocket spring loaded roller to control the speed of the bucket is fixed to baring holder plate (8) a water tank (12) is provided with 4 in built bolts on the top plate and 4 on bottom plate for fastening arrangement with the help of nuts.
