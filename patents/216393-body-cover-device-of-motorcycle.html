Title of Invention

"BODY COVER DEVICE OF MOTORCYCLE "
Abstract A body cover device of a motorcycle with a muffler (22) arranged on a lower side of a body cover (11) characterized in that the body cover (11) is made of resin and has a portion in correspondence with an upper side of a side portion of the muffler (22), and the portion of the body cover (11) has a chipped portion (11d) to which a muffler (22) protector (123) made of steel is fitted and integrally attached.
Full Text [DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION]
[Industrial Field of Utilization]
The present invention relates to a body cover structure of a motorcycle.
The present invention relates to an improvement in a body cover of a motorcycle arranged with a muffler on a lower side of one side portion of a Vehicle body.
[Prior Art]
Conventionally, in the case of a scooter type motorcycle, there is frequently adopted a structure of a unit swing type in which a power unit comprising an engine and a transmission case is supported by a vehicle body swingably in the movement of the power unit and there is frequently provided a structure in which a rear wheel is supported at a rear portion of the power unit and a rear cushion unit is interposed between the power unit and the vehicle body.
Fig. 5 is a sectional view viewing a body cover on a side having a muffler from a rear side and a rear wheel 54 supported by a power unit 55 is arranged at center C in a direction of vehicle width. The power unit 55 is arranged to shift to one side of the rear wheel 54 and is installed in the forward and rearward direction of the vehicle body.
In the meantime, a muffler 52 arranged to the power unit 55 is arranged to shift to a side opposed to the power unit 55 such that it does not interfere with the rear wheel 54.
Meanwhile, the muffler 52 is arranged on an outer side of the rear wheel 54 to interpose a predetermined space therebetween in consideration of prevention of thermal influence on the rear wheel 54 and a body cover 51 is installed on an outer side of the muffler 52 by securing a predetermined space therebetween in consideration of prevention of thermal influence from the muffler.
Therefore, the muffler 52 is arranged from one side of the rear wheel 54 in an outer side direction by interposing a space S2 therebetween, further, a portion of the body cover 51 in correspondence with the muffler 52 is arranged in an outer side direction from the muffler 52 by interposing a space SI therebetween.
By the above-mentioned reason, the muffler 52 is arranged on one side of the rear wheel 54 and on an outer side of the rear wheel 54 in the vehicle width direction by interposing the space S2 and in respect of the body cover 51 constituting
an outlook of the motorcycle, a portion thereof in correspondence with the muffler 52 is formed as a bulged portion 51a significantly bulging in the outer side direction of the vehicle width direction in comparison with other portions of the body cover for avoiding interference between the body cover 51 and the muffler 52 and avoiding thermal influence from the muffler' 52 as mentioned above.
Further, numeral 53 designates a muffler protector arranged on the upper side of the muffler 52 and arranged with a gap between it and the bulged portion 51a of the body cover 51. The space SI is provided between the muffler 52 including the muffler protector 53 and the bulged portion 51a of the body cover 51 as mentioned above and the muffler 52 and the muffler protector 53 are disposed on an inner side of the bulged portion 51a of the body cover 51, holds the space SI therebetween and is moved upwardly and downwardly integrally with the swinging movement of the power unit 55 as shown by an arrow mark in the drawing.
Accordingly, there is provided a body cover structure of a motorcycle with a muffler (22) arranged on a lower side of a body cover (11) characterized in that the body cover (11) has a portion in correspondence with an upper side of a side portion of the muffler (22), and the portion of the body cover (11) has a chipped portion (1 Id) to which a muffler protector (123) made of steel is integrally attached.
[Problem to Be Solved by the Invention]
The conventional body cover 51 is molded integrally by resin or the like to include the bulged portion 51a on the side having the muffler 52 and when the body cover 51 is formed of a left and right dividing type, in respect of a half body cover on one side (hereinafter, described as "body cover hair), a total of the body cover half is integrally molded to include the bulged portion 51a.
In the meantime, the bulged portion 51a of the body cover 51 on the side having the muffler 52, is obliged to bulge to an outer side direction in comparison with other portions of the body cover owing to the reason.
Accordingly, there is a possibility in which the bulged portion 51a is brought into contact with an obstacle in parking or in moving it by pushing it by the hand or the like. Further, in the case of parking, when the motorcycle is erroneously fallen down, the bulged portion 51a may be grounded, impaired, or destructed.
In the case of a scooter type motorcycle, the body cover 51 constitutes directly outlook of the vehicle body and accordingly, impairment or destruction of the bulged portion 51a of the body cover 51 mentioned above is not preferable.
Conventionally, when the bulged portion 51a is impaired or destructed as mentioned above, a total of the body cover or a total of the body cover half including the bulged portion 51a needs to interchange.
When the total of the body cover or the total of the body cover half is interchanged, the interchanging operation becomes hard, a burden on a user in view of the cost is considerable and an improvement therefor is requested.
[Means for Solving the Problems]
In order to resolve the problem, according to Claim 1, in a motorcycle arranged with a muffler on a lower side of
a body cover, a portion of the body cover in correspondence with an upper side of a side portion of the muffler is integrally attached with a muffler protector separate from the body cover.
According to Claim 1, a muffler protector constituting the portion of the side portion of the body cover in correspondence with the upper side of the side portion of the muffler can be integrated to constitute the portion of the body cover by constituting the body cover by a body separate from the side portion of the body cover. Accordingly, only the muffler protector constituting the one side portion of the body cover can be interchanged. That is, the total of the body cover or the total of the body cover half needs not to interchange.

[Effect of the Invention]
According to the present invention as described above, the following effect is achieved.
According to Claim 1, in a motorcycle arranged with a muffler on a lower side of a body cover, a muffler protector separate from the body cover is integrally attached to a portion of the body cover in correspondence with an upper side of a side portion of the muffler.
Therefore, according to the invention, the muffler protector constitutes a portion of the body cover and can be
installed integrally with the body cover to fit in. Therefore, the promotion of appearance quality can be achieved as a total of a motorcycle.
Further, according to the invention, a portion of the muffler protector constituting a portion of the body cover mostly projected in the vehicle width direction, can be formed by a member separate from the body cover, the structure is integrated with the body cover and accordingly, only the muffler protector can be interchanged.
Therefore, in the case in which when the motorcycle is parked, or moved by the hand or the like, the muffler protector which is a portion of the body cover bulged to the outer side is brought into contact with an obstacle or in parking the motorcycle, the motorcycle is erroneously fallen down and the muffler protector constituting the bulged portion of the body cover is impaired or destructed, only the impaired or destructed muffler protector may be interchanged since it is a member separate from the body cover although it constitutes a portion thereof.
In the interchanging operation, only a muffler protector may be detached from a body cover and a new one of the muffler protector may be screwed or the like and accordingly, the interchange operation is extremely facilitated.
Further, a total of the body cover or a total of the body cover half needs not to interchange, a destructed part can be interchanged or repaired inexpensively and a burden on a user is largely reduced which is extremely advantageous in
repair or interchange.
Further, the muffler protector is attached integrally with the body cover. The muffler protector needs not to attach to a power unit and accordingly, a stay exclusive for the muffler protector is dispensed with, simplification of the structure of the power unit and a reduction in a number of parts can be achieved. Further, the weight reduction can also be achieved thereby.
Further, the muffler protector is installed integrally with the body cover on the outer side of the vehicle body and accordingly, in running, high cooling effect of the muffler protector can be achieved by running wind.
Further, the muffler and the muffler protector are
separate bodies. The muffler itself is not surrounded by the
muffler protector, moved upwardly and downwardly in a naked state in running and exposed to running wind. Accordingly, further high cooling effect of the muffler itself can be achieved.
