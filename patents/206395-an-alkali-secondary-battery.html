AN ALKALI SECONDARY BATTERY
Abstract An alkali secondary battery comprises a cathode current collector (397), a cathode active material and an electrolytic solution, a separator (391) that permits passage of an ion but does not permit passage of an electron, an anode active material and an electrolytic solution (394), and an anode current collector (396) which are placed in this order, wherein metal carbide or a mixture of metal carbide and the metal is used as the anode active material.
