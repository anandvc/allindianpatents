Title: A Cubicl or Capsule bed with air-conditioning system (Cubirator)
Field of the invention: The present invention relates a cubicle bed with air conditioning system more particularly relates to a field of furniture construction, adapted for use in a residence hall or other institutional setting.such as hospital, Nurshing \M
Back Ground:
In the past, furniture used in residence halls or other similar institutional settings has consisted of floor mounted bed unit, a desk area associated drawers, and bed may be mounted on rollers for movement towards and away from the wall to which the storage unit is mounted.
In view of the above, there exists a need for flexible similar settings, in which the furniture components can be easily moved, repositioned or configured according to the requirements.
In view of the above the prior are relates a furniture system up to No. 5970537 comprising first and second post assemblies each of which includes a series of posts, each post having a substantially vertical longitudinal defined by two or more walls.
Where as in the instant application comprises a cubicle bed with an air conditioning system, which is new and novel, a similar or equivalent publications are not reported & air conditioning System is unique with using refrigeration for air conditioning & beat is transferred by means of without or with fan and condensing unit, which keep it apart from other available systems.

Summery of the invention:
The present invention relates to a cubicle bed with air conditioning system, a bed manufacture4 by using MS/GI material powder coated or fibre coated or fibre, wooden material cuts in different sizes like 3'X6' or 6'X6' & others as per the requirement and added invention of cubicle of same length and width, the walls of the cub are made up of insulated material like fibre wood or glass or combination thereof, all with one or two doors for entry and exit, being taking care of leak proof arrangements, further the invention comprises the cubicle bedroom system having features of temperature control and dust free environment by putting an air conditioning device on the wall of cub With other facilities like fresh air system, voice conduction, reading light etc.
Description:
The present invention relates to a cubirator more particularly relate to a cubicle bed with air conditioning system, a construction of the cubicle bed comprising a four corner each of having a series of spaced verticals slots having a sizes 3'X6', 6'X6' with height of 1 1/2 of bed & other variable size & length of wood, MS/GI pipes or fibre with well insulated cubicle with height of 3 to 6 feet made up of material having quality of poor conductor for heat like fibre sheet, glass or wood.
The entire cubicle bed is leak proof having one or two door for entrance or exit a person shall move on the hinges. The unique feature of the bed shall be a air conditioning system is fixed on the head or foot side of the wall containing coils are made-up of copper and aluminium with fan coil unit to throw the air in the cubicle bed system. The temperature of the cubicle bed is being controlled by thermostat which is arranged at the wall below coils to control a temperature, a valve is arranged in line with electrical panel for control of cooling & heating in cubirator & thermometer to show temperature arranged inline with electrical penal for control of cooling and


heating in cubirator and thermometer to show the temperature.
The temperature of cooling coil will go in minus. So it will give comparatively low temperature cool air & because on cooling coil temperature will go in minus, it will give better control on humidity.
The condenser of unit will be designed of 3 types;
1. the Compressor & Condenser will be placed under the bed with suitable capacity. It will
contain a compressor, relay & condenser. The condenser will be made up of iron / copper
pipes with heat transmitting bars attachment. The size of condenser & cooling coil will
depend on the size of cubicle bed & type of bed like single bed or double bed.
2. In this design, the condenser is kept under the bed with suitable capacity of compressor &

fan coil unit having condenser, fan motor & fan blade to radiate the heat with suitable capacity.
3. In this design, the condensing unit will designed as a separate piece having condenser, condenser fan, compressor, electrical parts, etc. It will be connected to evaporator coil of bed via electrical wiring & gas tubes.
The gas & compressor will be HCFC & CFC free. It is environment friendly which will not harm the ozone layer of atmosphere.
