Field of Invention
This invention pertains to human necessities. More particularly and precisely this invention relates to protective gear for human beings. This invention focus on solar Ultra violet protective cap produce a cap which can protect humanbeings from the hazardous solar ultra violet radiation. The cap is provided with a strap at the bottom, which is also UV protective. This strap helps for proper fixing of cap on the head and to overcome the major disadvantage of cap flying away during riding of two wheelers and during strong breeze. The cap and the strap is provided with air holes such that air can pass but not UV rays.
The object of the Invention is to manufacture the cap, fabric is coated with UV protective chemicals. This fabric is cut and made in to cap with necessary pads and accessories. Strap is fixed at the bottom of the cap. Holes are provided in the cap and a cover for holes with opening on both sides is provided for air to pass through but not UV rays.
There is no adequate protective head gear from solar ultra violet rays, this invention fulfill the requirement needed by the society.
