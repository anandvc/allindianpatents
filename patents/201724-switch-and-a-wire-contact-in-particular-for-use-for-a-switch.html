Title of Invention

"SWITCH AND A WIRE CONTACT ,IN PARTICULAR FOR USE FOR A SWITCH"
Abstract Switch,containing a base plate,which has stationary contacts,and an operating part which can be moved approximately at right angles relative to the bawe plate between at least two positions and has a conductor arrangement which can be engaged with the stationary contacts,and during movement of the operating part,switches a first predetermined electrical connection between the stationary contacts and a second predetermined electrical connection,characterized in that the stationary contacts are in the form of wire contacts,one of whose ends are attached to the base plate and whose other ends have a sliding region, and the electrical conductor arrangement is formed by a circuit board (8)which ,in both positions of the operating part electrically connects the sliding brackets,which are elestically prestressed such that they rest against it,of the wire contacts to one another in a predetermined manner.
