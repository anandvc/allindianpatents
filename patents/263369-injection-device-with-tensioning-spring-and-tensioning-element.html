Title of Invention

INJECTION DEVICE WITH TENSIONING SPRING AND TENSIONING ELEMENT
Abstract The invention relates to an injection device with a receiving device for a substance to be dispensed, in particular with an ampoule holder (2), which is movable relative to the injection device or a part thereof, with a tensioning element, in particular a spring (6), which can bear on a part of the injection device, and with a coupling element (5) for coupling the receiving device (2) to the spring (6) such that, during an insertion movement of the receiving device (2) into the injection device, the spring (6) can be tensioned. The invention also relates to a method for preparing an injection device for dispensing a substance from an ampoule (3), wherein a discharging spring (6) of the injection device is tensioned by introduction of the ampoule (3) into the injection device.
