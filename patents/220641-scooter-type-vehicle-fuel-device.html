Title of Invention

SCOOTER TYPE VEHICLE FUEL DEVICE
Abstract An engine 21 is provided below a seat, a step floor 33 as a foot-rest is provided in front of the engine 21, an undercover 35 is provided below the step floor 33, a fuel pump 43 is provided between the undercover 35 and the step floor 33, and a heat insulation plate 45 is provided between the fuel pump 43 and the engine 21. The heat insulation plate 45 is integrally formed with the undercover 35 via a flexible bend portion 71 and bent in the bend portion 71, thereby protecting the fuel pump 43. The heat insulation plate and the undercover can be integrally formed. As integral formation is made, attachment of the heat insulation plate can be easily made.
