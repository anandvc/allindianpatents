Title of Invention

A PROCESS AND DEVICE FOR SELECTIVE RECOVERY OF COBALT AND MANGANESE CATALYSTS FROM PURGE STREAM IN THE MANUFACTURE OF POLYCARBOXYLIC ACIDS
Abstract A process for selective recovery of cobalt and manganese catalysts from purge stream in the manufacture of polycarboxylic acids. It comprises precipitating cobalt and manganese from the purge stream with a selective precipitating agent at 40-100`c. The molar ratio of the precipitating agent to the cobalt and manganese in the purge stream is 0.7-0.95 : 1. The purge stream i filtered to eparate the precipitate are recovered by acidification and from the filtrate are recovered by running the filtrate through an ion exchange bed comprising a chelaig ion exchange resin and eluting the ion exchange bed with a mineral acid. A device for carring out the above process comprising a precipitation vessel (2) provided wih heating means (2a) and connected to a filtration vessel (5)which in turn is connected to a dissolution vessel (6)and an ion exchange column (7)comprising an ion exchange bed of chelating ion exchange resin (8).
