Field of Invention

This invention generally relates to Manual Sea Weed Algae Juice Extractor used for the purpose of extracting juice from Sea Weed Algae named Kappaphycus alvarezii without Electric Power. This extractor can also be run by hydraulic unit.

This invention is mainly concentrated on the extraction of juice from the Sea Weed Algae named Kappaphycus alvarezii without Electric Power as well as increase the productivity. Though this invention is explained with respect to the extraction of juice from the Sea Weed Algae named Kappaphycus alvarezii, this invention will also be useful in extraction of juice from Sea Weed Algae such as Gracilaria edulis, Gracilaria arcuata etc and in various applications where in extraction is required.

To fabricate the Manual Sea Weed Algae Juice Extractor the components required are Power press set up with male die, female die, Column with spring. Filter, Collector and other fabrication items. Hydraulic unit in case the extractor is run using hydraulic power.

Prior Art

Prior to this invention after cultivating the Sea Weed Algae, it is directly supplied to the manufacturers, and then the manufacture extracts the juice from SeaWeed Algae and uses that juice in their products. This Process increases extra work load for getting less quantity of Sea Weed Algae juice.

Hence to avoid the above problem, this invention i.e. Manual Sea Weed Algae Juice Extractor Machine, which can be easily installed at the farming place itself so that the juice can be extracted quickly before it gets dry, by this process the quantity and quality of juice extracted will be increased as well as the fanner will get good selling price.

Accordingly what is needed; in the art is an improvement that overcomes the above-mentioned deficiencies of the prior art.

Figure 1: Shows the block diagram of the MANUAL SEA WEED ALGAE JUICE EXTRACTOR.
1. Hydraulic cylinder
2. Male Die
3. Female Die
4. Tank with filter
5. stroke handle
6. Fabricated bed
7. Filter
8. Collector tank
9. column with spring

The sea weed algae are placed in the tank (4), which to be mounted on the female die (3). The Hydraulic cylinder (1) mounted on the underside table; the Hydraulic cylinder stroke arm is fixed under the juice extracting tank. The male die (2) is fixed on top portion which is in between H type column. When hydraulic cylinder is operated through stroke handle (5) the female die tank with sea weed algae moves upside and press with male die. Due to pressure the juice is extracted from see weed algae and collected the juice in collector tank (8). The return stroke is automatic due to gravity and spring action.
