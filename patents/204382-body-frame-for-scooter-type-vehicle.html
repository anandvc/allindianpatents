(Prior Art]
The body frame of this type has been conventionally disclosed, for example, in Japanese Patent Laid-open No. Hei 10-'218 0 65..

The above-described prior art body frame, however, has a problem. Since a rear end of a front frame is fastened to a front end of a rear frame along fastening planes thereof extending substantially in the horizontal direction, on the projection of a head pipe and the fastening planes of the rear end of front frame and the front end of the rear frame to a plane parallel to the fastening planes, the fastening planes are disposed at positions apart from a lower end of the head pipe. As a result, when there occurs an error in assembling the rear frame to the front frame,"a variation in position of the head pipe with respect to the center line of the rear frame in the width direction becomes relatively large.
In view of the foreg"bing, the present invention has been made, and an object of the present invention is to provide a body frame for a scooter-type vehicle, which is capable of making relatively small a variation in position of a head pipe with respect to the center line of a rear frame in the width direction, even if there occurs an error in assembling the rear frame to a front frame.
