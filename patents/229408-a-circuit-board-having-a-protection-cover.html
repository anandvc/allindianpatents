Title of Invention

A CIRCUIT BOARD HAVING A PROTECTION COVER
Abstract The invention relates to a circuit board having a protection cover, comprising: a board body 10, 100 having a circuit mounting surface lOa; a box-like protection cover 20, 120 for covering the circuit mounting surface of said board body entirely and made of an insulating material; and an incision 21 selectively formed in said protection cQver; characterized in that said box-like protection cover comprises a first portion and a second portion, said first portion and said second portion on either side of said incision abut each other when closed during non-measurement of said circuit board; said board body comprises at least one of a measurement pad 11, 111 and a circuit adjusting component 112 formed on the circuit mounting surface; an said incision is formed to correspond to one of the measurement pad and the circuit adjusting component on the circuit mounting surface.
