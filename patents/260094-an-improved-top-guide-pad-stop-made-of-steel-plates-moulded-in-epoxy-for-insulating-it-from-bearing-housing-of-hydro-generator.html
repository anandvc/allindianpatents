Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED TOP GUIDE PAD STOP MADE OF STEEL PLATES MOULDED IN EPOXY FOR INSULATING IT FROM BEARING HOUSING OF HYDRO GENERATOR
Abstract In the new design of guide stop (Ref Fig. No.02), main consideration were to increase circulation of oil, proper bonding of insulation material and to eliminate complicated machining. In the new design instead of machining from a heavy block of steel it is made from six thick steel plate, which is trimmed and bent at both the ends to get the required size and shape of pad stop, without any machining. The pad stop made by bending the steel plate is moulded with epoxy fiber glass insulation to make it a monolithic mass. There is no danger of knocking down of the insulating from pad stop during working condition.

FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention is related to an improved epoxy moulded type top guide pad stop
to restrict the circumferential movement of guide pad and also to keep the top
guide pad insulated from bearing housing of Hydro Generator.
BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION
Top guide bearing is a main part of Hydro Generator which guides and supports
the rotating shaft (Ref. Figure No.03) and pad stop is provided to restrict the
circumferential movement of guide pads The guide pad stops are designed
such that these do not create any obstruction in the oil circulation path inside the
bearing housing, so as to maintain the temperature of guide bearing pads within
permissible limits. Further, to eliminate the circulation of shaft currents through
bearing pads and to avoid electrical pitting and damages of bearing pads, only
Top guide bearing pads needs to be insulated from the bearing housing. This
necessitates the provision of insulated guide pad stop for Top guide bearing of
Hydro Generators.
In the existing, pad stop having extremely complicated shape is machined from a
solid steel block number of machining operations to achieve desired shape of pad

3
stop. For insulating it from bearing housing, insulated strips are glued to it on
both the sides of the guide pad stop (Ref Fig. No. 01).
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
It is therefore, an object of the present invention to propose an improved epoxy
moulded top guide pad stop to restrict circular movement of guide pad and to
keep the top guide pad insulated from bearing housing of Hydro Generators,
which eliminates the disadvantages of existing system (i.e. complicated
machining and gluing of insulation of pad stops).
Another object of the present invention is to propose an improved epoxy
moulded top guide pad stop which improves oil circulations and to keep the
uniform temperature gradient inside the housing.
A further object of the present invention is to propose an improved epoxy
moulded top guide pad stop which improves the bonding strength of insulation
with pad stop to avoid any chance of knock down of the insulation from the pad
stop during operation.

A still further objection of the present invention is to propose an improved epoxy
moulded top guide pad stop which is easy to manufacture and less cost.
SUMMARY OF TH E INVENTION
In the new design of guide stop (Ref Fig. No.02), main consideration were to
increase circulation of oil, proper bonding of insulation material and to eliminate
complicated machining. In the new design instead of machining from a heavy
block of steel it is made from six thick steel plate, which is trimmed and bent at
both the ends to get the required size and shape of pad stop, without any
machining. The pad stop made by bending the steel plate is moulded with epoxy
fiber glass insulation to make it a monolithic mass. There is no danger of
knocking down of the insulating from pad stop during working condition.
