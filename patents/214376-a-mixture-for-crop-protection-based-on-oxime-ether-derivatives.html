Title of Invention

A MIXTURE FOR CROP PROTECTION BASED ON OXIME ETHER DERIVATIVES
Abstract Mixtures for controlling harmful fungi and insects, which comprise, as active components, A) oxime ether derivatives of the formula I in which the substituents are as defined below: X is NH or oxygen; R<sup>1</sup> and R<sup>3</sup> independently of one another are 20 hydrogen, cyano, alkyl, cyclopropyl or haloalkyl; R<sup>2</sup> and R<sup>4</sup> independently of one another are hydrogen, alkyl, alkenyl, alkynyl, haloalkyl, haloalkenyl or haloalkynyl; and B) the compound of the formula II in a synergistically effective amount, and methods for controlling harmful fungi using mixtures of the compounds I and II are described.
