Title of Invention

A CARD READER PAYMENT TERMINAL
Abstract The present invention relates to the invention concerns a card reader (ICC) payment terminal (I) comprising an electronic circuit formed by at least a printed circuit board whereon are connected electronic components and in particular a microcontroller (UC) designed for controlling various peripherals (RFI, KB, DU). The invention is characterized in that said micro controller (UC) is connected to an associated microprocessor (IC), said microcontroller (UC) using said associated microprocessor (IC) to provide programmes such as arithmetic calculations, said microprocessor (IC) being borne by a support (MIC) with SAM-type micro-smart card format and comprising a connector compatible with the normalized connector of said micro-smart cards to operate the connection to said microcontroller (UC).
