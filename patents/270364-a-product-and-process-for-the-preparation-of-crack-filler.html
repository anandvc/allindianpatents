FIELD OF THE INVENTION:-
The present invention relates to a product and process for the preparation of the readymade putty powder. More particularly present invention relates to a product and process for the preparation of the readymade putty powder useful to fill the Gaps, Holes, Cracks in Wood, Sculpture Art, Plywood And Cracks in the Walls, and the like..
PRIOR ART:-
ln Prior Art furniture makers were using wood powder or whiting or fly ash or plaster of paris, chalk powder, tamarind seed powder with suitable adhesive ie. White glue, M-seal, Metal paste, Wax are also used as a crack filler. There are certain drawbacks and deficiencies, which are as follows:
1. When wood powder mixed with adhesive and filled the cracks, it shows colour difference since the dried wood powder becomes darker in shade than natural wood.
2. When whiting mixed with adhesive and filled in the cracks of wooden surface, it cracks after drying and comes out from the wood.
3. When plaster of paris is mixed with adhesive and filled in the cracks, it can also come out from the wood.
4. When fly ash is mixed with adhesive and filled in the cracks, it shows colour difference.
5. When chalk powder or tamarind seed powder mixed with adhesive, it also cracks and comes out from the wood.
6. When M-seal, metal paste is used as filler which is generally available in two pack system, it has short shelf life and pot life. It is not suitable for wood.
7. When wax is used as filler, it melts down in summer.

OBJECT OF THE PRESENT INVENTION:-
The object of the present invention aims at developing a product and process for
the preparation of the readymade putty powder.
It is also the object of the present invention to develop a product which can stick
up with wood
Yet another object of the present invention is to develop the crack filler for wood
having excellent holding capacity with wood and the product which shall not fall
off from the wood after drying.
Yet another object of the present invention is to fulfill the requirements of the
furniture makers and plywood manufacturers by developing a product which fills
the cracks does not fall down from the filled surface.
It is also an object of the present invention to develop a product, which does not
form cracks on the applied area and which shrinks very less when used as filler.
Yet another object of the present invention is to fill the surface which can be
carved with wood.
it is also an object of the present invention to develop a product, which can
match with the wood colour and also cost effective.
It is also the object of the present invention to eliminate the short falls and
drawbacks of prior art.
According to this invention, therefore, a readymade putty powder comprising of filler, strengthening material and binder in the ratio of 85 to 70 as to 15 to 5 as to 15 as to 5, by volume respectively with or without fast drying material and/or fly Ash or whiting and suitable colours.
Further according to this invention, A process for the preparation of the readymade putty powder comprising the steps of i) mixing by any conventional method predefined amount by volume of filler, strengthening material and binder

to form the product crack filler ii) adding predefined volume of water to the said product crack filler to form the thick paste of the product crack filler iii) adding the suitable colours to formed the paste of step (ii) and iv) applying the said paste of suitable colour to the cracks in the sufficient quantity.
Fast Drying Materials like Anhydrous Magnesium Sulphate Or Calcium Sulphate (POP)(Particle Size 45 to 75 Microns) 2.5 to 20 Parts by Volume can be added. Smooth Paste Makers like Fly Ash Or Whiting (Particle Size 45 to 75 Microns) 2.5 to 20 Parts by Volume can be added.
The invention will now be described with reference to the examples which are illustrations of the present invention and in no way construed as the limitation of the present invention. Suitable changes can be made in the invention without changing the scope of the invention.
PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF CRACK FILLER PRODUCT:-
1. Taking BaS04 (Barium Sulphate) about 63 microns.
2. Taking ZnO (Zinc Oxide) about 45 microns.
3. Taking Gelatin powder about 75 microns.
4. Thoroughly mixing the said Barium Sulphate with Zinc Oxide and Gelatin powder in the ratio of 70: 10: 10 parts by volume respectively by any conventional method to form the product crack filler.
5. Suitable colours can be added to make the various colours in putty.
6. Mix this crack filler powder in a water to make a thick paste and this paste can be applied on the cracks of wood, damaged edges of wood, hole in the wood, edge of plywood, the face veneer of plywood , etc.
7. Finer grade can also be obtained by taking more fine partical size of the ingredient.

FEATURES OF THE PRESENT INVENTION:-
1. It does not crack when filled to the wooden surface.
2. It works as a Hole Filler, Crack Filler, Knot Filler, Edge Filler and Grain Filler for wood and plywood.
3. It will not fall out during sanding or after finishing.
4. After filling to wood, the filled surface can also be carved.
5. The present invention is cost effective compared to wax or two pack system fillers.
6. This crack filler can be used to fill the gaps of sculpture, cracks in wall, etc.
7. This product is useful for wooden furniture manufacturers, toy manufacturers, plywood manufacturers, painters etc.

I CLAIM:-
1. A readymade putty powder comprising of filler, strengthening material and binder in the ratio of 85 to 60 as to 15 to 5 as to 15 as to 5, by volume respectively with or without fast drying material and/or fly Ash or whiting and suitable colours.
2. A readymade putty powder as claimed in claim 1 wherein the filler material is selected from Barium Sulphate or Aluminium Oxide or Silicon Di Oxide having the particle size in the range of 45 to 75 Microns.
3. A readymade putty powder as claimed in claim 1 wherein the strengthening material is Zinc Oxide having the particle size in the range of 45 to 75 Microns.
4. A readymade putty powder as claimed in claim 1 wherein the binder is selected from Gelatin Powder or re-dispersibie Powder Polymer Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) having the particle size in the range of 45 to 75 Microns.
5. A readymade putty powder as claimed in claim 1 wherein the fast drying materials is Anhydrous Magnesium Sulphate or Calcium Sulphate (POP) having the particle size in the range of 45 to 75 Microns.
6. A process for the preparation of the readymade putty powder comprising the steps of i) mixing by any conventional method predefined amount by volume of filler, strengthening material and binder to form the product crack filler ii) adding predefined volume of water to the said product crack filler to form the thick paste of the product crack filler iii) adding the suitable colours to formed the paste of step (ii) and iv) applying the said paste of suitable colour to the cracks in the sufficient quantity.

7. A readymade putty powder as claimed in the above claims and herein described with reference to the examples of the accompanying specification.
