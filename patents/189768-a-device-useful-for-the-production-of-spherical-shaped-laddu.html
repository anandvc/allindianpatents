Title of Invention

"A DEVICE USEFUL FOR THE PRODUCTION OF SPHERICAL SHAPED "LADDU"
Abstract A device useful for the production of spherical shaped "Laddu" such as herein described which comprises a fixed hemispherical hollow die-cup (2) and a movable hemispherical hollow die-cup (3), the said die cups (2&3) being movably fixed in a hollow cylindrical rotatble channel ( 1) in such a manner so that the hollow concave surfaces of the said die-cups face each other, the said fixed die-cup (2) being provided at the center of its convex side with a spring actuated spindle ejector (5), the said movable die-cup (3) being connected at its convex side with means of a hand lever (4) for enabling it to move and form a sphere with the said fixed die-cup (2), the said cylindrical rotatable channel (1) being fitted onto a stand (10) in such a memner so as to allow tilting/rotation of the said assembly by means of a ball lever (6) and stopper (7).
Full Text This invention relates to a device useful for the production of spherical shaped "Laddu"
The device particularly produces products of definite size, weight and bulk density out of granular, pasty and semi-solid food materials.
A number of traditional foods are formed into spherical shapes before they are processed and consumed, some examples being, Laddus from Boondi mix, Besan ,Soji, Ragimuddi (dough) products.
The sweets like Laddu and other sweet products that are presently prepared in Indian temples are generally of spherical shape and they have become very popular throughout the Country. For instance, boondi Laddus are regularly prepared by thousands of sweet meat shops and many religious institutions such as The Tirupathi Tirumala Devastanam (TTD) every day by manual operation using bare hands. Very little effort has gone into the development of such Gadgets and machinery for the production of such spherical foods for mass consumption in a hygienic manner.
The present practice adopted for production of Laddus using hands has the following drawbacks :
1. The production rate is very low.
2. The operation is labour intensive.

The main object of the present invention, is to provide a device useful for forming spherical shaped food items such as Laddu and like products.
Another objective of the present invention is to provide a shnple hand operated device which can be conveniently used for fonning Laddu, Ragimudde and similar spherical shaped foods in a hygienic condition without using human hands for forming.
Yet another objective of the present invention is to provide a device to produce spherical shaped toods having uniform consistancy in shape, size and bulk density in the entire batch of production.
Still another objecthe of the present invention is to provide a device to increase the production rate and to form spherical shaped products even when the foods are at higher temperatures where human hands cannot be used.

Example-1
150 gms of loose grannular boondi mix with all ingredients at a forming temperature (50 degree C) is fed into the cut opened portion of the cylindrical pipe (1) Figure of 1&2, which is placed in horizantal position.
