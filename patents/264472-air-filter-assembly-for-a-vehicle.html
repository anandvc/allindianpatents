Title of Invention

AIR FILTER ASSEMBLY FOR A VEHICLE
Abstract The present invention relates to an improved air assembly for an automobile, the assembly comprising- a) a housing consist of bottom & top part with a filter made of flexible porous cellular plastic (FPCP) media, located inside the housing and having a rubber hose inserted and clamped on the inlet tube of the filter for connecting it to the front grill area of vehicle.
