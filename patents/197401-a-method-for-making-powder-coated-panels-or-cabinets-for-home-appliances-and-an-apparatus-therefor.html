Title of Invention

"A METHOD FOR MAKING POWDER COATED PANELS OR CABINETS FOR HOME APPLIANCES AND AN APPARATUS THEREFOR"
Abstract The present invention relates to a process and appartus for the preparation of the powder coated panel for home appliances especially for the refrigerators wherein the coating of the refrigerator panel and door are of incosistent dry film thickness and apparatuds thereof.
Full Text Field of the invention:
The present invention relates to a process and the apparatus for the preparation of powder coated panel for home appliances.
More specifically, it relates to a process and the apparatus used for powder coating the substrate prepared galvanised steel sheet to be used in the home appliances.
Prior Art
The powder coated conventional panels have bare CRCA (cold rolled cold annealed) sheet resulting in the rusting of sheets during monsoon. The process also produces lots of effluent and the pollution level also is high. The paint quality specification in terms of corrosion resistance and paint adhesion was not good. The adhesion of the paint to the substrate applied panel and powder coated panel did not show satisfactory results. The said sheet was susceptible to scratching giving a a worn out look to the appliance after some time in turn decreasing the market value of the product.
Thus there is a need to develop a process which overcomes all the above mentioned drawbacks of the conventional process.
It is an object of the current invention to develop an improved process of powder coating to produce better quality paint having corrosion resistance properties.
It is an object of the present invention to develop a process of powder coating which reduces the effluent produced in the process decreasing the pollution.
It is also an object of the present invention to develop a process of powder coating to produce the paint of quality specification (physical and chemical
both) having good adhesion, flexibility, resistance to scratch and water and fast colour.
Summary of the invention:-
The present invention relates to a process and apparatus used for powder coating the substrate prepared galvanised steel sheet The substrate preparation is done prior to powder coating of the galvanised steel sheet to be used in home appliances. The substrate preparation is a 2-step novel process which involves degreasing and rinsing of the galvanised steel sheet.
The novel process of substrate preparation producing better quality substrate is explained in our co-pending Indian Patent Application No. 533/D EL/2002.
The substrate prepared galvanised steel sheet is taken to the water drying oven. The degreased, rinsed and dried galvanised steel sheet is then taken and placed in the cleaned class-l chamber provided with the overhead conveyor belt system for holding the said sheet for powder coating.
The process for powder coating involves the spray drying the Epoxy polyester hybrid powder having a particular particle size distribution. The powder is filled in the main feed hopper having the pneumatically operated transfer pumps. The powder is sprayed on the galvanised steel sheet by means of plurality of automatic guns having a corresponding said transfer pump and a control unit. The transfer pumps are connected to the automatic spray guns by means of powder hoses. The number of automatic guns depends on the size of the appliance and accordingly the number of transfer pumps. Each automatic gun has different powder flow having a uniform air pressure, diameter and current. The different powder flow of the guns depends on the pneumatic pressure applied on the corresponding transfer pump of each automatic gun. Each automatic gun has a corresponding control unit which supplies current to the gun and thereby charges the
powder. The said powder is sprayed by means of the said automatic guns on the substrate. The manual gun is also used during the process having a particular air pressure and a current to ensure proper powder coating on the appliance. The said powder coated galvanised steel sheet moves to the curing oven for baking oven at a particular baking temperature for melting of the powder.

The degreased , rinsed substrate prepared galvanised steel sheet moving on the conveyor belt system is taken to the water drying oven and dried which is further taken and placed in a cleaned class I chamber provided with the overhead conveyor belt system for holding the said sheet for powder coating to be used for home appliance.
The said conveyor belt system moves at a speed of 3.3 meters/minute. The speed is verified by means of the stop watch and frequency of verification is once per month.
The said process involves the spray drying of epoxy polyester hybrid powder on the said sheet. The powder has the particle size distribution of The cleaned class I chamber is cleaned by vacuum cleaner and automatic spray guns , powder hoses are cleaned properly by means of compressed air
before starting the process. The actual charge at the gun tip is checked with the help of KV meter for all the automatic spray guns.
After spraying of powder on the cabinet of the appliance, the powder is taken to the curing oven for baking and melting of the opowder at a temperature of 220 +/- deg C. the temperature is verified by means of the temperature gauge and frequency of verification is once per shift.
