SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION
According to this invention, there is provided a homeopathic medicine comprising 1 ml of the serum, obtained by known pathological methods, of a patient detected for HIV-II-virus mixed with 99 drops of ethyl alcohol duly potentized by serial dilutions with a suitable medium and striking with powerful strokes with a mechanical device.
Typically, the suitable medium is ethyl alcohol, distilled water or saline or a combination of any of them.
Typically, the potentization is affected by striking a receptacle field with the medicine with at least 15 strokes.
3

Typically, the serial dilutions are serially lc, 2c, 3c, 30c, ...50c,
...100c, ....200c, ....500c, and the like, every "c" standing for a dilution of 100 times.
Details Description :-
HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) infection has two major type HIV Type-II which are found in the serum of Patient or carriers who are HIV Positive. For the preparation of Homeopathically Potentized (micro-dose) HIV Type - II Virus militer of serum (which contain the virus) Patient who is detected positive for HIV virus is mixed with about 99 drops of ethyl alcohol, which is used as vehicle. This mixture is allow to undergo a process of potentization in which serial dilution is done with powerful strokes with the help of a mechanical device to arrive to Potentized (micro-dose) HIV-Type-II virus 1C i.e. potency. I.e. HIV Type II-1C.
Vehicle is a medium comprises of ethyl alcohol distilled water, normal saline or any such neutral medium.
About 1ml of HIV-Type IIIC is mixed with about 99ml of ethyl alcohol to undergo the same process of Potentization to arrive to HIV-II-2C potency. Similarly, the process is carried out to achieve 3c, 4c, ...30c, — 50c, —100c, 200c, 1000c 1 million and above for clinical use the proportion of original substances (serum) to vehicle may vary from 1:99 to 50:50.
