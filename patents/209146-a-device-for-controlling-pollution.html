Title of Invention

"A DEVICE FOR CONTROLLING POLLUTION"
Abstract This invention relates a device for controlling pollution caused by the suspended particulate matter and noise of the vehicle. The device has a housing consisting of top and bottom half portions adapted to be secured with each other. An inlet port is provided near the top end of the top half and an outlet is provided in said top half at the opposite sides thereof. Filtering elements are secured at the bottom end of the top half. A reinforcement having a reinforcement plate is provided in the middle of the lower half. Brackets are provided at the inlet as well as outlet port for securing the device with the chassis of the vehicle.
Full Text This invention relates to an device for

controlling pollution and noise caused by motor vehicles and generator sets.
The problem of pollution is a great problem in our country. The efforts are being made to reduce pollution caused by motor vehicles generator sets etc.

There are devices known in the prior art for reducing The chemical pollutant like carbon mono oxide etc. produced by the vehicles but no device is as such known for controlling the pollution caused by the suspended
particulate matter (SPM) present in the exhaust of the
motor vehicles as well as generating sets. Further, no
device except silencer is as such known for reducing the noise pollution caused by the motor vehicle as well as generator sets.
Therefore, the main object of this invention is to propose a device for controlling pollution caused by the suspended particulate matter present in the exhaust/smoke of the vehicles and generator sets.
Another object of this invention is to propose a device for controlling the pollution caused by the suspended particulate mattery as well as noise of the vehicles and generator sets.
According to this invention there is provided at device for controlling pollution caused by the suspended particulate matter and noise of the vehicle comprising a housing consisting of top and bottom half portions adapted to be secured with each other, an inlet being provided near the top end of said top half and an outlet being provided in said top half at the opposite sides thereof, a filtering elements being secured at the bottom end of said top half, a reinforcement having a reinforcement plate being provided in the middle of said lower half, brackets being provided at the inlet as well as outlet port for securing the device with the chassis of the vehicle.
In accordance with this invention the device for controlling the pollution caused by the suspended

particulate matter as well as noise of the vehicles and generator sets has a housing having top and lower half portions thereof. Said top and lower half portions are adapted to be secured with each other by means of three toggle clamps. A partition is provided in the said top half portion of the housing so as to divide the same into upper and lower chambers. A filtering element is provided at the bottom end of the lower chamber of said top half by means of a flange and gasket. An inlet, is provided in one side of the upper chamber of said top half for allowing the insertion and it is supported by the partition provided in the top half. An outlet pipe is provided in the other side of the upper chamber of said top half for holding an exhaust pipe therewith. The bottom half of the housing comprises upper and lower chambers adapted to be secured with each other by welding or other securing means. A reinforcement plate having a plurality of holes is secured at the bottom end of the upper portion of said lower half for supporting a reinforcement thereon having a plurality of holes in flow communication with the holes of reinforcement plate. Clamps are provided
with the inlet as well as the outlet pipes for securing the device with the chassis of the vehicle.
