FIELD OF INVENTION
This invention more particularly relates to improved venous reservoir with integral cardiotomy reservoir for blood oxygenator.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
Venous Reservoirs are disposable devices more often used with Blood Oxygenators in open heart surgeries to collect, defoam and temporarily store the venous and cardiotomy blood from the patient. The input to the device is the raw venous and cardiotomy blood from the patient. The output of the device is the filtered and defoamed blood.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose an improved venous reservoir with integral cardiotomy reservoir for collection of venous blood and collection defoaming and filtering of cardiotomy blood.

Another object of this invention is to propose an improved venous reservoir with integral cardiotomy reservoir for collection of venous blood and collection, defoaming and filtering of cardiotomy blood which is easy to fabricate, sterilise and store for specified period without any impairment of efficiency.
