Title of Invention

A COMPOSITION IN FORM OF A SOLUTION FOR USE IN AN AEROSOL INHALER AND AN AEROSOL INHALER THEREOF
Abstract A composition in formof solution for use in an erosol inhaler, the composition comprising an active materials, a propellant contaning a hydrofluoroalkane (HFA), a coosolvent, optionally alowe volatility component characterized in that the propellant consisits of a mixture of HFA 227 and HFA 134a.
