Title of Invention

A WIND TURBINE NACELLE HANDLING SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR VERTICAL DISPLACEMENT OF WIND TURBINE NACELLE THEREOF
Abstract The invention relates to a handling system (11) for a wind turbine nacelle (3) in connection with self-loading or self-unloading of the nacelle (3) to or from a vehicle. The system comprises two or more lifting means (15), where the lifting means (15) are engaged with lifting areas (14) of a load carrying structure (10) of the nacelle (3). The lifting areas (14) are a part of the load carrying structure (10) or are integrated in the load carrying structure (10), and where the lifting means (15) lifts by applying linear force to the load carrying structure (10) of the nacelle (3). The invention further relates to a method for vertical displacement of a wind turbine nacelle (3) and a wind turbine nacelle (3) prepared for self-loading or self-unloading to or from a vehicle.
