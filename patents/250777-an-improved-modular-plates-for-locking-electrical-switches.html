Title of Invention

AN IMPROVED MODULAR PLATES FOR LOCKING ELECTRICAL SWITCHES
Abstract An improved modular plates (1) for locking electrical switches (2) having a bottom plate (3) with plurality of apparature/grooves (4) ranging from 1 to 18 module provided for accommodating switches with in-built projection inwardly slunting projection (5) for locking the modular switch having matching slots (6) in a press fit manner. The said bottom plate having screw fitting holes (7) on its flanges for fitting the bottom plate on the wall or on wooden frames in around the grooves portion of the switching box. The said bottom plate is provided with longitudinal flanges (8) on its periphery and a top cover of the matching grooves (9) of the bottom plate is press fit on the inner flanges by means of hole (10) and projection (11) and locked at top and bottom by means of projection on the top cover and grooves at the bottom cover with mono or different colour of flape member thereby giving a multicolour effect on the switch board.

3. PREAMBLE TO THE DESCRIPTION
The following specification particularly describes the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed : -

TRUEI
An improved modular plates for locking electrical switches.
The invention relates to modular plates for fixing the electrical switches in a consealed manner.
The conventional switch boxes is made from wood or metal has its inherent problems such as fire hazards, electrical shock and moisture prone rusting therefore those plates has been replaced by polycarbonate plastic modular switch boxes for accommodating and fixing modular switches.
Principal object of this invention is to provide an improved modular plate for locking multiple electrical switches in a push-fit manner.
Another object of this invention is to provide an improved modular plate for locking multiple electrical switches in the consealed switch box wherein replacement of the switches is easy and without opening the switch box thereby user's friendly.
Still further object of this invention is to provide an improved modular plate for locking multiple electrical switches which gives a good asthetic look and shockproofness.
Still further object of this invention is to provide an improved modular plate for locking multiple electrical switches which gives un-interrupted services.
Accordingly there is provided an improved modular plates (1) for locking electrical switches (2) having a bottom plate (3) with at least one apparature/grooves (4) ranging from 1 to 18 module provided for accommodating switches with in-built projection inwardly slunting projection (5) for locking the modular switch having matching slots (6) in a press fit manner. The said bottom plate having screw fitting

The bottom plate (3) having multiple appartures the said appartues for accommodating modular switches in a push fit manner. The mechanism of locking is established by in-built moulded inwardly slunting projections (5) provided below the bottom plate and the grooves (6) provided on the both ends of the modular switch near the base. The said bottom plate (3) having screw fitting hole (7) for engaging the bottom plate on the wall or on wooden frames. The said bottom plate is covered by the top plate with matching grooves so as to lock the top plate with peripherial flanges in grooves and projection means in top and the bottom plate to conseal the screw fitting (7). The top plate having peripherial flanges made from polycarbonate plastic may have mono colour or multi-colour effect depending upon the colour of the peripherial flange of the top cover.
The bottom plate made from polycarbonate plastic mouldable material having multiple grooves to accommodate modular switches in a push-fit manner by means of slunting projection at the lower part of the bottom plate and the grooves provided on both side of the modular switch at their base said bottom plate having screw fitting holes at four corners for fixing switching box for consealed wiring. The said bottom plate having grooves on its edges for locking the top flange members in a push-fit manner for consealing metal screws.
