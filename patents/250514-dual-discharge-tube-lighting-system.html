Title of Invention

DUAL DISCHARGE TUBE LIGHTING SYSTEM
Abstract .Power saving, in the order of power of one discharge lamp, out of the two lamps connected in series with necessary axillaries are proved by this innovative way of connections. The power factor of the discharge lamp system also improves to unity from 0.6. These, when effected to the national grid, there will be considerable amount of power saving and grid system improvement due to power factor improving and attendant pollution mitigation and eco system improvement.
