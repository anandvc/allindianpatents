Title of Invention

A METHOD FOR RESERVING ISOCHRONOUS RESOURCES ON A WIRELESS NETWORK FOR CONNECTION SET UP.
Abstract TITLE: METHOD FOR ISOCHRONOUS RESOURCE MANAGEMENT IN A NETWORK BASED ON HIPERLAN 2 TECHNOLOGY‚Äù. The invention concerns a method for reserving isochronous resources on a wireless network of the Hiperlan 2 type for connection set-up, said network comprising an isochronous resource manager, said method comprising the steps of: identification of a talker de vice and a listener device by a connection controller; acquisition by the isochronous resource manager of the list of the devices to be part of the connection; determination of the bandwidth required for connecting the talker and the listener by the isochronous resource manager, as a function of the list of devices to be part of the connection; set-up of a multicast group including the talker device and the listener device if said bandwidth is available.
