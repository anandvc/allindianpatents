Title of Invention

A MOBILE KITCHEN
Abstract A mobile kitchen comprising a box for containing cooking apparatus, the box being provided with a lid, the lid being extensible to provide a table top; a source of fuel for the cooking apparatus attached to the box; a wheeled stand provided for the box, said stand being detachably fixed to the box; a canopy structure with telescoping columns and a canopy for being raised or lowered over the box.
Full Text

This invention relates to a mobile kitchen for preparing and dispensing various items of food, such as, sweet corn, fries and other edibles, and which can be moved about from place to place for dping business.
This invention provides mobility for doing business in the said edibles from one location to another.
It also provides means for carrying, among other things, a stove, utensils, water, disposable cups, plates, apart from provisions for preparing such edibles.
This invention enables the aforementioned articles to be carried about without any damage while being mobile.
This invention can be parked in stable condition while doing business and enables the said articles tp be used easily and effortlessly.
Various other features of this invention will be apparent from the following further description.
The mobile kitchen, Regarding to this invention, Comprises a box for containing cooking apparatus, the box being provided with a lid, the lid being extensible to provide a table top; a source of fuel for the cooking apparatus attached tp the box a wheeled stand provided for the box, said stand being detachably fixed to the box; a canopy structure with telescoping columns and a canopy for being raised or lowered oyer the box.
