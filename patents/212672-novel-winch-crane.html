Title of Invention

NOVEL WINCH CRANE
Abstract This invention relates to NOVEL WINCH CRANE. This machine can be assembled as per the equirement on the floor within few minutes. This machine is mobile and useful in lifting the building materials from the ground up to the required floor depending on the requirement of the rise or height of the building. The boom machine of the present invention can move 360 degree round.
