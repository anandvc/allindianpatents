In particular this invention, relates to the loaded vehicles, road wheel rotating with out friction by the provision of vehicles lower body road wheels rotatable shaft with its pinions and upper bodys fixed shaft with its ratchet gear meshing in 75° above to 90° below, for anti clock wise rotation of the road wheels, the ratchet outer gear rotate clock wise at that time anti clock wise rotation of the another ratchet gear rotate as idle gear by the lever operation and for clock wise rotation of the road wheels, another ratchet gear meshing with its pinion is in 90° above to 135° below the ratchet outer gear rotates anti clocks wise at that time clock wise Rotation of the other ratchet gear rotate as idle gear by the lever operation lower body and the upper body connected with pin and bush with springs.
In the prior made vehicles road wheels rotating with rolling friction due to weight and the rotating capacity of the road wheels relates to the in put power, further it has been found that considerable amount of fuel energy is wasted in the previously made vehicles.
In the proposed invention, vehicles lower body road wheels rotatable shaft with its pinions is separated, from the upper body with its fixed shafts and its ratchet gear meshing like neutral equilibrium arrangement and the upper body and the lower body connected with pin and bush in horizontal plane to have sliding motion and resulting inclined plane mechanical advantage for road wheels rotation with out friction.
