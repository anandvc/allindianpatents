Title of Invention

AUTOLOCK SLIDER FOR SLIDE FASTENER
Abstract The present subject matter describes an Auto lock slider plastic slider for a slide fastener. According to one embodiment of the present invention the slider comprises a slider body having one or more slots formed therein. A puller means having a cam provision is integrated with the slider body. In another embodiment, the puller means is attached to the sliderbody and not an integrated part thereof. further, a resilient locking member having a first end and a second end is attached to tghe slider body so that the one end gets arrested in one of the slots in the slider body when assembled. Additionally, the resilient locking member is adapted to cooperate with the cam provision to affect engagaing and/r disengaging of the another end of the resilient locking member with respect to the slide fatener through another slot in the slider body thereby locking or unlocking the slide fastener.
