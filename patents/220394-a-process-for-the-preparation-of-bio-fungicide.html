Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF BIO-FUNGICIDE
Abstract (57) Abstract: This invention relates to a process for preparation of a bio-fungicilde for management of nursery diseases, which cause severe losses to mulberry plantation. Presently a chemical fungicide is known only for- the control of one the nursery disease namely stem-canker. The present invention discloses a process of a bi-fungicide for management of nursery diseases wherein the process comprises fermentation of a Trichoderma " pseudokoningii on a carrier substrate comprising lignite-molasses-wheat bran. FIELD OF IHVENTION
This invention relates to a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide for protection against nursery diseases in mulberry plants.
BACKGROUND OF INVENTION
The diseases namely, stem-canker fit die-back {Botryodiplodia (=Lasiodiplodia) theobromae}, collor-rot (Phoma sorghina/P. mororum) and cutting-rot (Fusarium solani), which are commonly known as nursery diseases, cause severe losses to mulberry plantation by affecting its initial establishment in nurseries as well as in main fields.
There is no alternative bio-product available for the management of these nursery diseases. The method is available only for the control of one of the nursery diseases i.e., stem-canker, that is the use of a chemical fungicide, Bavitin (Carbendazim 50% WP i.e., Methyl-2-benzimidazole carbamte). However, chemical control is more expensive and short term effective, besides being ecologically unsafe and farmers' unwillingness in the use of chemical fungicide due to the apprehensions about its toxicity on silkworms.
OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
An object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide for an effective management of nursery diseases, such as stem-canker, die-back, collor-rot and cutting-rot in mulberry.

Another object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide, which is ecofriendly and cost effective and has effective application for mulberry plant.
Still another object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide, which provides a high survival and growth of mulberry saplings for better and uniform establishment and growth of mulberry plantation, which ensures the high quality leaf yield and ultimately the silk production.
Yet another object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio- fungicide, which does not contain any hazadous chemical, and thereof, the use therefore is ecologically safe, as it does not cause any sort of environmental hazard and provides healthy leaves for silkworm feeding.
A further object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide, which kills the pathogens in soil and thus makes the soil disease-suppressive, and remains active in soil for very long period, as it grows on organic matter present in soil.
A still further object of this invention is to propose a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide, which stimulates the plant growth and vigour, thus, improves leaf yield and quality for healthy silkworm rearing.
STATEMENT OF INVENTION
According to this invention, there is provided a process for preparation of a bio-fungicide for protection against nursery diseases in mulberry plants wherein the process comprises inoculating of Lignite-Molasses-Wheat bran mixture with an isolate of fungal biocontrol agent Trichoderma pseudokordngii, incubating at 28± 4°C for 12-14 days, harvesting the materials, diying at room temperature followed by mild blending to break lumps, wherein said Lignite-Molasses-Wheat bran mixture is prepared by impregnating 15% v/v molasses solution, having pH of 5.5± 2 and containing 3g/! of each of KNO3 and KH2PO4, with heat sterilized powdered lignite for 15-20 hours at 80-100% solution, mixing thorughly impregnated lignite thus obtained with wheat bran at 5-8g per lOOg of said powdered lignite wherein further said powdered lignite contains fixed carbon and ash with calorific value of 2600-2800 K Cal/kg.
