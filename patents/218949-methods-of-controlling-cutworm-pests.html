Title of Invention

METHODS OF CONTROLLING CUTWORM PESTS
Abstract The present invention relates to a method for controlling a cutworm pest wherein said method comprising applying a CrylF toxin to soil or plants to prevent cutworm damage to plants where cutworms are known or suspected of being present
Full Text WE CLAIM:
1. A method for controlling a cutworm pest wherein said method comprising
applying a CiylF toxin to soil or plants to prevent cutworm damage to plants where
cutworms are known or suspected of being present
2. The method as claimed in claim 1 wherein said toxin is applied to V 1 plants.
3. The method as claimed in claim 1 wherein said toxin is applied to V 2 plants.
4. The method as claimed in claim 1 wherein said cottons are thin instar or
greater larvae.
5. The method as claimed in claim 1 wherein said toxin is applied to seedlings.
6. The method as claimed in claim 1 wherein said toxin is applied as a bait
formulation.
7. A polynucleotide for expressing aerie 1 Gene Jan V 1 plant.
8. A polynucleotide for expressing a cry 1 F gene in a V 2 plant.
9. A polynucleotide for expressing a city 1 F gene in a seedling.
