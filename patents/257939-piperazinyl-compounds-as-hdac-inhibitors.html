Title of Invention

PIPERAZINYL COMPOUNDS AS HDAC INHIBITORS
Abstract The present invention relates to novel compounds of the general formula (I), their derivatives, analogs, tautomeric forms, stereoisomers, polymo hs hydrates, solvates, pharmaceutically acceptable salts and compositions. The present invention more particularly provides novel HDAC inhibitors of the general formula (1).
