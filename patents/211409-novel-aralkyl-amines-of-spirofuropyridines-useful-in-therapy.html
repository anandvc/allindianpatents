TECHNICAL FIELD
This invention relates to novel substituted amines of spirofuropyridines or pharmaceutically acceptable salts thereof, processes for preparing them, pharmaceutical compositions containing them and their use in therapy. A further object is to provide active compounds, which are potent ligands for nicotinic acetylcholine receptors (nAChR's).
