Title of Invention

AUTOMATED SCREEN PRINTING SYSTEM
Abstract The system comprises an ink source that delivers printing ink to an ink carrier (M). The ink carrier (M) receives and thereafter transfers the received ink asd a fine layer, to an image carrier (B) through a screen mesh. The image formed thereon the image carrier (B) is then transferred onto a sheet of material e.g. paper to be printed.
