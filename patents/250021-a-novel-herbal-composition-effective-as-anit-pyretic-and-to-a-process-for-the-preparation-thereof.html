Abstract This invention relates to a novel herbal composition effective as anti pyretic comprising of Zeera sufaid (Cuminum cyminum L) in an amount of 95-125 mg, Dar-e-filfil (Piper longum L) in an amount of 95-125 mg, Maghz-e-karanjwa (Caesal pinia bonducella) in an amount of 95-125 mg, Barg-e-babool (Acacia arabica wild) in an amount of 95- 130 mg and Kushta gardanti (Calx gypsum) in an amount of 50-70 mg and also this invention relates a process for preparation of a novel herbal composition effective as anti pyretic comprising steps of filtration of extract of zeera sufaid, Dar-e-filfil, Maghz-e-karanjwa and Barg-e-babool and evaporating all filtrates to the cone viscous mass, extraction of filtrate is carried out using purified water, addition of kushta gaudanti the diluent such as light kaolin in the range of 25-40% and preservative such as Sodium Benzoate in the range of 0.1-0.15% followed by drying in a fluid bed drier at 90째-105째C for 3-4 hrs.

OBJECTS OF THE INVENTION
The principal object of the present invention is to propose a novel therapeutic composition capable of offering an effective treatment for Fever and to a method of preparation thereof.
Another object of the present invention is to propose a novel therapeutic composition capable of offering an effective treatment for Fever and to a method of preparation thereof prepared entirely from herbal and mineral commonly available drugs.
Still another object of the present invention is to propose a novel therapeutic composition capable of offering an effective treatment for Fever and to a method of preparation thereof which has no toxicity or untoward effect after prolonged administration in children and also in pregnant women.
