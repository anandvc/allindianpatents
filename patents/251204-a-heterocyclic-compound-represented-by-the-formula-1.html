DISCLOSURE OF THE INVENTION
An object of the present invention is to provide an antipsychotic drug which has a wider treatment spectrum, less side effects and excellent tolerability and safety as compared with well-known typical and atypical antipsychotic drugs.
The present inventors have conducted intensive studies on the above-described problem and consequently succeeded in synthesizing a novel compound which has dopamine D2 receptor partial agonist activity (D2 receptor partial agonist activity) , serotonin 5-HT2A receptor antagonist activity (5-HT2A receptor antagonist activity) and adrenalin α1 receptor antagonist activity (α1 receptor antagonist activity) and further has serotonin uptake inhibitory effect (or serotonin reuptake inhibitory effect) together in addition to these effects.
