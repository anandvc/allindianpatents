Title of Invention

DYEABLE FLUOROPOLYMER FIBERS AND FILMS
Abstract This invention relates to fluoropolymer such fibers and films which are receptive to cationic modifying agents such as dyes, whiteners, antistatic agents and flame retardants, and the fibers or films formed by treatment of the receptive fibers or film of the invention therewith. Such fibers and film are useful in various consumer goods where the products are desirably colored, whitened, antistatic or flame retardant.
