Title of Invention

FLOCCULANT, MANUFACTURING METHOD THEREFORE, AND FLOCCULATION METHOD USING FLOCCULANT
Abstract A coagulant with which service water and suspensions such as wastewaters, e.g., the waters in tap water works and in sewage works and industrial wastewaters, can be easily, safely, and speedily treated. The coagulant comprises a silicon sol which gels upon dilution and coagulates suspended substances simultaneously with the gelation.
