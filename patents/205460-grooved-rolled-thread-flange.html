GROOVED ROLLED THREAI FLANGE
The invention relates to an improved Grooved Rolled Thread Flange fitted on the drum top as a closer.
Conventional drum top closer are having flat flange member with or without gasket which are leak proof but when drum is damaged same are not sufficiently leakproof. During transportation, the liquid contained in the drum are leak through the drum top due to a sudden accidental impact and consequent damage.
The prior art flat rolled threaded flanges are provided with a flat flange bottom (1) which supports and press the inner gasket (2) in between flat flange bottom and the drum top. Due to the sudden impact Drum Sheet mould leave gasket surface thus releasing pressure on the gasket then from gap through which the liquid contained in the drum will flow.
The inventor with constant research has found effective solution. To obviate such drawbacks he made a grooved rolled threaded flange which is leakproof.
Accordingly there isprovided a groove provided in rolled threaded flange (101) consisting of a grooved flange outwardly extended within this Groove a gaskets (102) is housed which is placed between the drum top (103) and the grooved flange the gaps (104) towards the top of neck is


filled up by the gaskets as it moves upward while curling and then in case of any jurk thereby not loosen or keep pressed between the flange with the drum top.
The principle object of this invention therefore is to provide a grooved rolled threaded flange for preventing a leakage of liquid contained in a container due to sudden impact.
Further object of this invention is to provide a better threading with cost effective and strong, easy to handle.
Still further object of this invention is to provide a leakproof flange which can be adopted to existing drums with out even slight modifications.

PRESENT SYSTEM :
I) Conventional flanges have flat surface on octagonal face, toward cylindrical wall of the flange, the flange gasket is fittign on this side.
II) Conventional flange which has a radius, which is formed during press operation (cupping); has a characteristic or effect to shift (slide) the gasket upward;
III) When flange is inserted by pressing the flange in drum top this gasket can flow in both directions, i.e. horizontally towards octagonal edge and or upward along the cylindrical wall i.e. neck of drum.
IV) Portion of the gasket which flows in the horizontal direction has a little contribution towards preventing leakage.
V) The portion of gasket going upward alon the cylindrical portion is desired to climb upto portion where neck of drum top and flange neck i.e. cylindrical portion which does not curl are straight and nearly touching each other.

VI) While drop testing the drum is hold such that the chime portion which is near flange is facing downward, and by impact drum top it tries to bend and the flat octagonal portion of the flange and drum top sheet try to go away from each other since drum top gets bend inward; this creates a situation where flange gasket loose pressure and its sealability as it is no more filling the space between flange and drum top.
VII) This can be achieved only if gasket is prevented from expanding horizontally and thereby forcing the seal to move upwards along the cylindrical portion.

DESCRIPTION OF THE INVENTION
A grooved rolled threaded flange (101) consisting of a grooved flange outwardly extended within this Groove a gaskets (102) is housed which is placed between the drum top (103) and the grooved flange the gaps (104) towards the top is filled up by the gaskets while it moves upward in case of any jerk thereby tightening the flange with the drum top. A threaded flange having a straight vertical/height portion and angular base portion extended outwardly which accomodates a elongatated gasket which is sandwich between the height of the flange and the outer covering of the drum top. The drum top is projected inwardly to press against the gasket. The flange angle is an acute angle after insertion. The acute angle is from 0 to 15 inside under the use. The gasket climb upward while insertion instead of going horizontally which makes assembly leakproof. The flange and the drum top made from material particularly of metals and non-metals. The gasket is made from elastomer material, resins material or type of material which elongates due to pressure force.
