Such tumours comprise colon tumours which have a high expression ofthe mdr 1 gene (multiple drug resistance gene). Taxoids are generally known to be strongly recognized by experimental tumours such as P388/DOX, a cell line selected for its resistance to doxorubicin (DOX) which expresses mdr l.
