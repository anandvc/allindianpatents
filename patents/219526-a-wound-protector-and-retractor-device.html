Title of Invention

A WOUND PROTECTOR AND RETRACTOR DEVICE
Abstract There is disclosed a wound protector and retractor device comprising: a longitudinal axis; a distal member (3); a proximal member(4); a sleeve (2) extending at least between the distal member (3) and the proximal member (4); and a guide member for a proximal portion of the sleeve (2); said sleeve (2), extending, distally from the proximal member (4) to the distal member (3), and around and proximally from the distal member (3), said sleeve having a return section, extending proximally between the guide member and the proximal member (4), said sleeve (2) being fixed to the proximal member (4) at a first end portion and being axially slidable over the proximal lmember (4) at the return section, said return section providing a proximal gripping portion for pulling said sleeve (2) upwardly to shorten an axial extent located between the distal member (3) and the proximal member (4).
