Title of Invention

MASTER TRACK SYSTEM FOR MOVIE TROLLEY TRACK IN CINE FIELD
Abstract ABSTRACT This invention relates to a newly invented Master Track System which is used for movie trolley tracks for cine field. This Master Track System has a pair of main tracks. The sleeper is connected to the main track. The main track has steel buttons to which screws through the sleeper will be connected to main track in order to provide necessary width adjustment. The main track is made of Aluminium extrusions section. The sleepers are specially created in the form of specially designed C shape so as to take care of complete load. This system provides highly optimized jerk free motion of the cine camera trolleys, cranes and dollies etc providing variable width adjustments, contributing to space savings and easy transportation.
Full Text The Master Track System according to this invention is for movie trolley tracks for cine field. The present tracks used in the cine field are made of steel pipes welded and provide a jerky motion at all points. The tracks are heavy and do not provide with an adjustment of width as they are welded. The master track system according to the invention eliminates all the above problems as it is a highly sophisticated new invention.
The joints have been made of stainless steel, CNC Machined so as to enable a very perfect joint to ensure smooth and jerk - free motion.
The salient feature of the invention is that the width of the Master track system can be changed so as to suit individual equipmental requirements. This is a special and highly novel feature of this new Master track system. Such a system has never been in existence before. By this invention transportation space requirements are very much reduced.
Sleepers of the Master Tack System have been specially developed and designed with Aluminium so as to bear the burden or weight of the movie trolley, cranes and dollies etc. In the convention and existing system the sleeper is made of steel square or rectangular pipe. In this novel construction it is made of a special C shape illustrated in the drawings. This construction ensures taking of complete load up to 45 feet movie cranes.
It is the primary object of the invention to design a novel master track system which is unique.
It is another object of the invention to design and develop a master track system which is simple in construction, efficient in operation and easy to maintain.
Further objects of the invention will be clear from the following description.
A master track system consisting of pair of main tracks, sleeper being connected to main track wherein main track has steel knobs to which screws through the sleeper will be connected to main tack to provide the necessary width adjustments.

The construction of the main track is specially designed and developed. The method of construction is by way of extrusion. This eliminates the process of welding. The welding results in fixed width construction of main track. By means of this novel process the width is adjustable. By simple hand twisting the width between main tracks or rails is

The Master Track System has got bits or pieces of standard lengths of 2-1/2 feet, 5 feet and 7-1/2 feet. These bits are connected to one another to get the required length of the track.
The track width is adjustable to meet the individual equipmental requirements. This is the novelty of this system never in existence before.
