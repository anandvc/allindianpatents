Title of Invention

COATED KRAFT PAPER
Abstract The invention relates to paper and paper oriented products by coating the kraft paper with the coating resolution as more fully described in the Invention. It Is possible to get a glossy paper, which is sturdy and cost effective. This invention Is a method for manufacturing a novel coated kraft paper. The coating is made of pigments like KCG LUMPS, CaCo3, NUCLAY 90 and HYDROCARB 90 with binders like DL 930 and PVA 325 to these are added CAUSTIC SODA, POLYSALT "S'. SORBITAL, DYE CCL IS GRADE BLUE and DYE CCL & VIOLET to which water is added in the required quantity to make the coating slurry. And coating slurry is coated on the kraft paper as described in this specification to make an economical attractive low GSM weight coated kraft paper which is also eco friendly.
Full Text This invention in general relates to paper and paper-oriented products. This invention is more concerned with Kraft Paper. More pertinently this Invention relates to a novel method of manufacturing coated Kraft paper. This invention Is also concerned with the machinery employed in manufacturing coated Kraft paper.
Using the materials in Table 1 below, the coating slurry will be made by mixing and dissolved in water to prepare coating solution. This coating solution consists of pigments and binders. The coating solution is supplied to coating machine in the applicator tray. A rotating roll made of steel with chrome plating having uniform smoothness also known as applicator roll fitted partly submerged in coating solution, picks-up coating solution on its circumstance. The Kraft paper to be coated is passed over the top surface picks-up coating solution. Any excess picked up solution by the Kraft paper is whipped out with the help of air knife. The wet coated Kraft paper passes through hot air chamber for drying and dried Kraft paper is getting rewind at the end of the coating machine. This coated Kraft paper will be passed through calendar machine for required glossiness and then it will cut into required size into sheets through cutters or will be made into reels as per requirement by passing through re-winder machine. These into bundles or reels for dispatch to the market.
Today the practice In the market is to print on top of Kraft and Duplex Board as top ply of master carton to enable the manufacturer to print the Information regarding the product and information regarding the company it belongs to . Duplex Board, as a top ply has the following disadvantages.
a. Forces the consumer to use much higher GSM thus increasing the weight
of the carton.
b. The Burst Factor of carton comes down thus forces to use higher Burst
Kraft in other layer or increase ply of boxes to reach the desired level.
c. It cracks on fold thus giving ugly look to carton.
These disadvantages make an innovator to think what can be done next to get the desired goal without increasing the cost. The corporate worid is looking for building brand equity and it is very important to see how the packaged product looks while getting delivered, or put on point of display. Customer's decisionÂ¬making becomes faster in case if the packaging is colorful and more attractive.
By coating the Kraft paper with the coating solution a described in the invention it would be possible to get a glossy paper which will be sturdy and at the same time cost effective. This also enables the companies wanting to make their cartion attractive and create to switch over to multi colored boxes to give better look to the cartons, which make the customer feel good.
