Title of Invention

PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF A SYNTHETIC BULK LAXATIVE
Abstract A synthetic bulk laxative which comprises a crosslinked graft polymer. It is formed of a hydrophilic monomer partially neutralised upto 75%, a polysaccharide gum upto 3% by weight and crosslinker upto 2% by weight.
