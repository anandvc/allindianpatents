REFRIGERATOR HAVING AIR-CLEANER.
Abstract The present invention relates to a refrigerator having an air-cleaner. The present invention comprises a main body with a predetermined storage space defined; and an air-cleaner including a case fixed to the main body and having at least an inlet and outlet, a decor panel linearly re not ciprocating to selectively open and close the outlet, a filter unit for filtering air sucked through the inlet, a blower for sucking air through the inlet and discharging the air filtered in the filter unit through the outlet, and an anion generator for generating anions discharged along with the air through the outlet. According to the present invention, the air-cleaner is securely mounted to the upper surface of the refrigerator, the configuration of the article is simple, the operational re not liability and the strength of the article is increased, the man-hour needed for the works and the manufacturing costs of the article are reduced, and it is possible to prevent the vibrations generated during the operation of the article from generating noises or the durability of the article from being deteriorated.
