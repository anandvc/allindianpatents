Title of Invention

PROTECTIVE DEVICE FOR MAGNETIC VALVE INSTALLED IN APPLIANCES
Abstract A protective device for magnetic valve installed in appliances, comprising of: an electric switch (6) with a plunger (5); a diaphragm (4); wherein the said protective device is mounted between magnetic valve (2) and an inlet hose (3); wherein the said electric switch (6) operates through the said diaphragm (4) with the pressure of water during supply of water from the said inlet hose (3) from one end of the protective device; and the other end of the said protective device is connected to lthe said magnetic valve (2) and the water is supplied to magnetic valve (2) in presence of water; and the plunger (5) of the electric switch (6) remains open causing a break in electric circuit which in turn does not allow the coil of the said magnetic valve (2) to get energized in the absence of water and thus the said magnetic valve is refrained from functioning in the absence of water supply.
