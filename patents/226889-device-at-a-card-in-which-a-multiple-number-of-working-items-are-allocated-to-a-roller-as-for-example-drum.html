Title of Invention

DEVICE AT A CARD, IN WHICH A MULTIPLE NUMBER OF WORKING ITEMS ARE ALLOCATED TO A ROLLER AS FOR EXAMPLE DRUM .
Abstract In the device at a card, in which in the pre-and/or post carding area a multiple number of working items,, as for example, fixed carding items, suction equipments, eliminating knife and the equivalent lying opposite to each other and - seen in the circumferential direction of the roller - adjacent to each other are assembled to a roller as for example drum a module is formed out of at least two working items. In order to make possible a change or rather influencing of waste composition (eliminated foreign bodies) and/or the removal of neps, in the pre-and/or post carding zone at least two modules are present, which are provided for the elimination of foreign bodies (impurities) like trash and equivalent and/or for the elimination of neps.
Full Text Device at a card, in which a multiple number of working
items are allocated to a roller as for example drum.
The invention refers to a device at a card, in which in pre
and/or post carding area a multiple number of working items,
as for example fixed carding items,, suction equipment,
eliminating knife, and the equivalent lying opposite to each
other and - seen in circumferential direction of the roller
adjacent to each other are assembled to a roller wherein out
of at least two working items one module is formed.
In a known device (DE 3855776A1) modules are provided with
two and more working items and are fixed on card side
frame. A cleaning device in shape of a suction unit is
present before which a guide rod and after which a measuring
rod are assembled. It is disadvantageous that this device
does not make possible an influencing of the composition of
eliminated foreign items (waste composition) and a removal
of neps-
The assignment therefore lies at the base of the invention,
to procure a device of the type described at the outset,
which avoids the mentioned disadvantages, which permits in
particular a change or rather influencing of waste
composition and the removal of neps.
The solution of this assignment takes place through the
distinguishing features of claim 1.
Through the measures as per invention it turns out well, to
improve considerably the elimination of troubling foreign
materials and/or neps. A special advantage exists therein,

that a multifunctional assembly of working items is made
possible. Through it,, a flexible matching to fibre materials
in order to meet high quality requirements in the materials
under production, turns out well, in particular on ground of
module design and the uniform angle stretching to reduce
considerably time expenditure for the exchange of worn out
working items at higher precision of the assembly of
replaced items.
The claims 2 to 17 H^tve in content advantageous further
developments of invention.
