Title of Invention

A METHOD AND SYSTEM FOR TEST STRIP QUALIFICATION.
Abstract A method of test strip qualification, said method comprising: providing a test strip comprising an assay reaction area, a first control reaction area and a second control reaction area; obtaining PT results for each reaction area; comparing results from said first control area to first control qualification criteria and results from second control area to second control qualification criteria, wherein said first control qualification criteria comprise an upper limit and a lower limit, said upper limit being at least partially dependent upon assay reaction area PT results; and outputting a message to a user indicating test strip reliability.
