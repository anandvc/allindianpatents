Title of Invention

A MULTI PURPOSE GEOMETRICAL EDUCATIONAL APPARATUS
Abstract A multi purpose geometrical educational apparatus, which comprises at least one circular disc, a plurality of scales, a plurality of arrows which can be removably fitted to the scales , at least one protractor and a plurality of nuts and bolts for removably fitting the scales to each other and to the disc and to the protractor to configure the apparatus into different geometric representations for teaching and learning purposes.

The following Specification particularly describes the nature of this invention and the manner
in which it is to be performed :-

This invention relates to a multipurpose Geometrical Educational apparatus to help students or teachers with learning or teaching problems.
To the knowledge of the inventor no similar apparatus is available which can help students or teachers learn or teach the geometrical parts in the text -books from STD-4th to STD- 10th
It is envisaged that with the help of this apparatus in accordance with this invention, the following parts can be explained.
Line, Line segment, Ray, Angle, Type of Angle, Triangle & its types Quadrilateral & its types Quadrilateral & its types, Circle, Radius, Diameter Chord, Secant, tangent, arc & its types, intercepted arc, inscribed angle, central angle, angle inscribed in a semi circle is a right angle, cyclic quadrilateral & its properties, inscribed angle theorem
The measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of its intercepted arc can be shown practically with the help of this apparatus.
According to this invention therefore there is provided a multi purpose geometrical educational apparatus, which comprises at least one circular disc, a plurality of scales, a plurality of arrows which can be removably fitted to the scales , at least one protractor and a plurality of nuts and bolts for removably fitting the scales to each other and to the disc and to the protractor to configure the apparatus into different geometric representations for teaching and learning purposes..

The apparatus 10 can be used as a teaching aid in the study of geometry & is very easy to handle & operate. It is also useful to understand original concepts in geometry & in self-study.
A single scale without arrow represents a line segment
A single scale having an arrow at one end represents a ray .
By adjusting two scales connected at the ends types of angles can be represented.


A Triangle can be represented by connecting three scales with three nut bolts and by adjusting the scales types of triangles can be shown.
A Quadrilateral can be represented by connecting 4 scales with 4 nut bolts and by adjusting the scales with the help of slits quadrilateral & its types can be made.
A Circle is represented by the disc and with the help of the scales the radius, diameter, chord, secant, tangent can be shown.
Various theorems can be proved with the help of the apparatus. Thus the measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of an angle at the circumference can be shown practically. Similarly, the angle made in a semi circle is always a right angle can be shown practically. At the same time arc types of arc & its measure can be shown directly .The measures of central angle can be shown directly with the help of circular protractor
This apparatus is useful in teaching & learning. Geometry fro STD IVth to STD Xth & gives a practical knowledge of geometry. A Taecher can save his time by having to avoid drawing figures on the blackboard while teaching and a student can learn geometry practically.
