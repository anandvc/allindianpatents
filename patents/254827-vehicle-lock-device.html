Title of Invention

VEHICLE LOCK DEVICE
Abstract [Problem] To provide a vehicle lock device capable of unlocking two locks in a short time with a single motor, with a simple circuit structure having low power consumption. [Means Of Solution] A structure comprise a motor 50, a cam 55 driven by the motor 50 and having a cam surface displaced according to a rotation angle, a first lever 56a having a contact section for contacting the cam surface, a shutter lock pin 59 operated according to displacement of the first lever 56a and putting a key shutter 69 in a locked state, and a seat wire 61 operated according to displacement of the second lever 56b.
