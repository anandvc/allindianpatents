The following Examples illustrate the invention.


EXAMPLE 1

This Example illustrates a process for the preparation of4,6-dichloro-2-(propylthio)pyrimidin-5-aniine.

A Pt[VIC catalyst (available from Degussa; about 3% Pt and 0.6% V adsorbed on charcoal, 30g) was charged to a vessel and the vessel was purged with nitrogen. 4,6-Dichloro-5-nitro-2-(propylthio)pyrimidine (302g) dissolved in tert-butyl methyl ether (31) was charged to the vessel and agitation was started. The resulting mixture was heated to an initial temperature of 300C and then the vessel was pressurised with hydrogen to 3 bar for 3 hours. After the completion of the hydrogenation the catalyst was filtered off. The filtrate was concentrated under reduced pressure to provide the title compound (254 g).
