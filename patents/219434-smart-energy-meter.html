TITLE SMART ENERGY METER
This invention relates to invention of a device for measuring electric energy consumed, controlled and computed by an external memory, smart card technique.
BACKGROUND OF THE PROBLEM
Currently the practice of metering electrical energy is by means of analogue and digital devices. This process comprises of the cumbersome procedure of meter reading and then billing for the no of units consumed. This process has been posing great difficulties the highest and greatest difficulty is securing the reading for units. There is also wastage of energy.

PRIOR ART
There are equipments which are used by energy metering industry which convert electromechanical meter to more accurate solid-state meters. The power-system designers have also incorporated new features for easy meter reading.
There are solid-state meters that measure energy more accurately than electromechanical meter and are capable of being read remotely by the utility company. Also in China there is a prepaid meter system.
SUMMARY
The above problems are overcome by the present invention. The present Smart Energy Meter is a device, which is based on Smartcard Technology. This device comprises of a Meter-controller chip (integrated circuit), unit counting circuit, miniature circuit breaker (MCB), a display (LCD), battery and a safety guard. This device; is capable of reading data, editing the data per unit and allows the energy of the balance amount inside the smart card. This device works as prepaid smart card energy meter.
