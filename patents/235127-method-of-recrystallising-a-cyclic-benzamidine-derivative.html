METHOD OF RECRYSTALLISING A CYCLIC BENZAMIDINE DERIVATIVE
Abstract "METHOD OF RECRYSTALLISING A CYCLIC BENZAMIPINE DERIVATIVE" This invention relates to a method for recrystallizing a cyclic benzamidine derivative (C), wherein the method comprises the steps of dissolving a compound represented by formula (XIII) (wherein, Me represents a methyl group, and Et represents an ethyl group), or the salt thereof, in a mixed solvent comprising a methanol and water, or ethanol and water or a mixed solvent comprising a tetrahydrofuran and water, wherein the volume ratio of water to methanol or ethanol, or water to tetrahydrofuran in the mixed solvent is 10:90 to 30:70; and after dissolution, adding additional water to precipitate crystals of the compound represented by formula (XIII), or the salt thereof, wherein the temperature at which crystals precipitate is 50 DegC or lower.
