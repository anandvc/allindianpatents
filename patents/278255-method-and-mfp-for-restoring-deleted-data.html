Title of Invention

METHOD AND MFP FOR RESTORING DELETED DATA
Abstract The present invention explains a method and a Multi Functional Peripheral (MFP) for restoring deleted data. The MFP includes a trash can. The trash can is capable of storing and restoring the deleted data based on choice of the user of the MFP. The storage caopacity of the trash can be configured by the user of the MFP based on the storage capacity of the MFP and user's needs. The trash can feature is optional.
