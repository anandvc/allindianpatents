Title of Invention

CARRIAGE SYSTEM FOR AN AUTOMATIC RUBBER COT ARBOUR GRINDING OR BUFFING MACHINE.
Abstract A carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours for processing in a automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine comprising a plate mounted on a linear slide driven by a prime mover, a bridge track unit assembly provided at each end of the said plate, each of the said bridge track unit provided with a stopper, each of the said bridge track unit being provided with an adjusting means for adjusting central space according to the size/gauge of the arbour.

This invention relates to carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours in automatic rubber cot
grinding/buffing machine comprising the same.
More particularly this invention relates to a carriage system in which unfinished rubber cot arbour either directly from the unfinished rubber cot arbour unit or from the measuring unit is carried to the grinding head through the centre less holding and guiding device

and the finished rubber cot arbour after grinding is carried to the finished rubber cot arbour storage unit/magazine through finished rubber cot arbour conveyer unit, the unfinished/finished rubber cot
arbour are carried by the carriage simultaneously and smoothly
ithereby making the working of automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine quite a smooth and increasing its productivity
Rubber cot arbour mainly comprises a steel spindle or axle provided
with one or two rubber cots used as top rollers in Ring Frame,
Frame, Combers, etc. of cotton/synthetic yarn Speed Frame, Draw
spinning mills, in the textile industries.
The arbours or top rollers used in Draw Frames or Combers are provided with single long cot up to 400 mm in length in the middle of the axle portion, while the arbours or top rollers used in Ring
2

Frames or Speed Frames are provided with a pair of cots, one each at the two ends of the axle. The cots are preferably made of rubber. A very large number of top rollers or rubber cot arbours are used in a Spinning mill/machine, which work continuously at a high speed.
Regular grinding of the rubber cot arbours is required for ensuring quality of yarn by reducing thread breaks and minimizing lap formation. Generally centreless grinding is used for rubber cot arbours of Ring Frames and Speed Frames and with centre grinding' is used for Draw Frames and Comber Cots.
Carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours are also known and used in the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding machines sold in the market under the brand name 'BERKOL' Â® manufactured by 'HUBER + SUHNER'. But the existing carriage system is having numbers of branching of pneumatic cylinders and liner device which makes the construction of existing carriage complicated. Also the carriage has to travel two times one
for picking the unfinished rubber cot arbour from the arbour holder

and again to deliver the unsuitable rubber cot arbour to the rejection tray and in the mean time grinding head remains waiting for feeding the unfinished rubber cot arbours: for grinding thereby

reduction the productivity of the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine.
In the existing carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbour is not very smooth.
In the existing carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours is occupy more space.
The main object of this invention is to obviate the above mentioned drawbacks and limitation of the existing carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours in the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding machine and to provide carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours in the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding machine in which the carriage is able to carry unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours is smoothly and simu hereby making the working of the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine very smooth and increasing its productivity.
Another object of this invention is to provide carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours in the automatic rubber cot arbour grinding machine, which is quite compact and occupying less space.

Further object of this invention is to provide carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours, in which the branching of pneumatic cylinders and liner device avoided thereby reducing the maintenance cost and break down time thereby increasing the productivity at low cost.
Accordingly this invention provides carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours for processing in a automatic rubber cot arbour grinding/buffing machine, comprises of a plate mounted on a linear slide, driven by a prime mover, a bridge track unit assembly provided at each end of the said plate, each of the said bridge track unit provided with a stopper, each of the said bridge track unit being provided with an adjusting means for
adjusting central space according to the size/gauge of the arbours,

Referring to figs. 2 to 4 the carriage system for carrying unfinished/finished rubber cot arbours comprises a plate (16), preferably made of metal such as steel or aluminium, mounted on a linear slide (17), which is driven by a prime mover such as an electric motor or a pneumatic/hydraulic means. The plate (16) of each end is provided with a bridge track unit assemblies (18 & 19) each having a stopper (20 & 21) which is a pneumatic/hydraulic or mechanical stopper. In order to carry different,size of arbours each of the bridge track unit assemblies (18 & 19) is made adjustable for
central space and provided with adjusting means (22 & 23) which
can be a screw and nut type or Rack and pinion type adjusting
means and can be operated manually or by an electric motor or the
like. The bridge track unit assemblies (18 & 19) are provided on the
two opposite sides of the arbours holder unit (9) or grinding wheel
head unit (11). One assembly (18) is provided on measuring unit
side to convey one by one unfinished rubber cot arbour (3) to the

rubber cot arbour holder unit (9) when found suitable for grinding,
or otherwise to the rejection tray, and the other is provided towards
finished rubber cot arbour unloading magazine (4) for unloading the
finished rubber cot arbour (5), after grinding. The feeding of
unfinished arbour (3) to arbour holder unit (9) and unloading of
finished arbour (5) after grinding is carried out simultaneously in a
smooth sequential operation, without wasting any time, resulting
into higher productivity of the automatic rubber cot arbour
grinding/buffing machine.
