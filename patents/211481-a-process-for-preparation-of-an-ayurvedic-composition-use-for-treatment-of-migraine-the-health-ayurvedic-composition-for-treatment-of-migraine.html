Title of Invention

A PROCESS FOR PREPARATION OF AN AYURVEDIC COMPOSITION USE FOR TREATMENT OF MIGRAINE & THE HEALTH AYURVEDIC COMPOSITION FOR TREATMENT OF MIGRAINE
Abstract The present invention discloses an ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of migraine, composed, of aqueous extract of dried powder of fresh leaves of Psidium guava and aqueous extract of dried powder of fresh leaves of Eucalyptus Camel dulensis plant in a ratio of 1:1 the same is prepared by drying the leaves in shadowed area, crushing the leaves to convert said leaves into powder, mixing both the ingredients in 200 ml. of water and heating up to 100째C on mild flame till the volume of the water reduces to half optionally mixing with sweetener to make the composition sweet for comfortable oral administration to obtain the said composition.
Full Text Field of the invention
This invention relates to an ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of migraine. This invention also relates to the ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of migraine.
Prior Art for the invention
Various allopathic medicines are available for the treatment of the migraine so far, however none can treat the migraine permanently. No ayurvedic or homeopathic medicines as yet are known which can treat this problem permanently. The allopathic medications include sedatives and painkillers used during the pain of migraine or attack of the migraine. All the medicines used in allopathic have inherent side effects. Therefore long use of the medicines inspite of it's consistent need become painful and harmful. No ancient medicinal literature like charak sanhita discloses any composition as is disclosed in the foregoing invention for the treatment of the migraine.
Object of the invention
The object of this invention is to provide a novel ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of migraine. This invention also relates to a process for the preparation of ayurvedic composition useful for the treatment of migraine. Further object of the invention is to provide a method for the treatment of the patient suffering from the migraine. Still further object of the invention is to provide a permanent treatment of the migraine.

Detailed description of the invention
The ayurvedic composition of the present invention is useful for the treatment of migraine. This gives permanent remedy for the migraine after treatment. The composition of the present invention is composed, of aqueous extract of dried powder of fresh leaves of Psidium guava and aqueous extract of dried powder of fresh leaves of Eucalyptus Camel dulensis plant in a ratio of 1:1. Optionally any sweetener like sugar may be added to make the taste of the composition comfortable to the patient. The number of the leaves of Psidium guava and Eucalyptus Camel dulensis, which is used in the preparation of the aq. Extract may be increased or decreased as per the requirement of the concentration of the medicinal composition. The process for the preparation of the abovesaid composition is as under:
(i) Collecting of fresh leaves of Psidium guava
(ii) Collecting of fresh leaves of Eucalyptus Camel dulensis plant,
(iii) drying the leaves in shadowed area,
(iv) crushing the leaves to convert said leaves into powder,
(v) mixing powdered leaves of Psidium guava and Eucalyptus Camel dulensis plant in a ratio of 1 :1
(vi) mixing 1 mg to 20 gm of mixture in 200 ml. of water
(vii) heating up to 100째C on mild flame till the volume of the water reduces to half
(viii) Optionally mixing with sweetener to make the composition sweet for
comfortable oral administration
