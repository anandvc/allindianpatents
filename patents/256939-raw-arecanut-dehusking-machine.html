Title of Invention

RAW ARECANUT DEHUSKING MACHINE
Abstract This Invention is in respect of RAW ARECA NUT DEHUSKING MACHINE which can be used to dehusk the areca nut fruits and separate the fruits from the bunch. It is fully automated, cost effective husking machine for areca nut growers with capacity to process 95 Kgs. Per hour, at a safe and comfortable pace of working. I claim that the usage of this dehusking machine will result in substantial saving in time, labour and cost of the areca nut growers and also increases their overall efficiency.

This invention relates to Raw Areca nut Dehusking machine in order to dehusk the areca nut fruits which is plucked from the trees and remove the nuts from the fruits. These dehuskers are fully automted, cost effective and finds advantageous application for areca nut growers in an easy, economical manner.
It is the object of invention to device dehuskers with this equipment it is possible to dehusk the areca nut at least 95 kgs per hour, at a safe and comfortable pace of working, easy and safe husking of areca nut with high productivity and the manpower requirement is single person.
